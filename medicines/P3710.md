# EN
### 💊 투엑스비엠연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supply of vitamins D, E, B1, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, reduced physical strength during or after illness (during illness or recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness and coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet), neuralgia, muscle pain, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the oral cavity), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 투엑스비엠연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 투엑스비엠연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication or to soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, abnormal lipid metabolism (e.g., diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis), require careful administration of lipid emulsions, are at special risk due to fat overload, have a thrombotic predisposition, have folic acid deficiency, or have a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
