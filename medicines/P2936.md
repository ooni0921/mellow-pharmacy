# EN

### 💊 속콜기가연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for phlegm or cough caused by phlegm.

### 🕒 속콜기가연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years and older take 2 capsules per dose. Children aged 8 to 14 years take 1 capsule per dose. Administer 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 속콜기가연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to it, gastric/duodenal ulcer, or are a child under 8 years of age. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, an elderly person, an individual with a predisposition to conditions such as urticaria, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis (coryza), migraine, or food allergies, someone who has experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), a patient with high fever, heart disease, liver disease, or severe kidney disease, or someone with a history of gastric/duodenal ulcer. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
