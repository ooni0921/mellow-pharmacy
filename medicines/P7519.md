* 만 8세 이상 어린이 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 벤포큐정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, D, E and zinc in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after illness leading to physical debility, during growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for poor bone and teeth development, prevention of rickets, relief of symptoms such as beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 벤포큐정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 8 years and older, and adults, should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 벤포큐정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (excessive calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, have a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to soybean oil, or are hypersensitive to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, have hyperoxaluria (excessive oxalate excretion in urine), are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding, are a premature infant or young child, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones or a history thereof, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a thrombotic predisposition (tendency for blood clots) or folic acid deficiency.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar in various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
