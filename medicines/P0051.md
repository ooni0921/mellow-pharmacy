#EN
### 💊 일양노이겔현탁액(규산알루민산마그네슘) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for its antacid effect and to improve symptoms of hyperacidity, gastritis, and duodenitis.

### 🕒 일양노이겔현탁액(규산알루민산마그네슘) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1/2 to 1 sachet (0.5 to 1 g) once, 3 to 4 times a day on an empty stomach. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age or symptoms.

### ⚠️ 일양노이겔현탁액(규산알루민산마그네슘) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with liver impairment, cardiac dysfunction, or hypermagnesemia should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
