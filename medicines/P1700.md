#EN
### 💊 엘시콤정 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for brittle hair, growth and nutritional disorders of fingernails/toenails, and as an adjunct in the treatment of corneal injuries.

### 🕒 엘시콤정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: Take 4 tablets daily, with meals.

### ⚠️ 엘시콤정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have severe renal impairment, hepatic coma, cystinuria, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, glucose-galactose malabsorption, or other genetic problems of this nature. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
