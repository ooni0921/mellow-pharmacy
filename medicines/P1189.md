#EN
### 💊 판토가캅셀 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for damaged hair, non-infectious nail growth disorders, and as an adjunctive treatment for hair loss.

### 🕒 판토가캅셀 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 1 capsule three times a day after meals.
*   Children aged 12 years and older: Take 1 capsule once or twice a day after meals.
*   The average duration of treatment is 3 to 6 months.

### ⚠️ 판토가캅셀 - Precautions
*   No information
*   **Do not take** this medication if you have hypersensitivity to its components, are under 12 years of age, have hair loss due to scarring, androgenetic alopecia, or male pattern baldness.
*   **Before taking** this medication, pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   服用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期女性应咨询医生或药师。

#JP

### 🕒 판토가캅셀 - 用法・用量

*   平均治療期間は3〜6ヶ月です。
