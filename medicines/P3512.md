### 💊 Shinshin Alex Lotion - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammation for sprains, bruises, muscle pain, joint pain, fracture pain, lower back pain, stiff shoulders, neuralgia, and rheumatic pain. It is also used for skin itching, insect bites, and frostbite.

### 🕒 Shinshin Alex Lotion - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ Shinshin Alex Lotion - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine should not use it.

Do not use on or around the eyes, mucous membranes, eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, wound areas, or sensitive skin.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness/hyperemia, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances) due to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, have damp or severely eroded/macerated skin, circulatory disorders, diabetes, are a child under 12 years old, are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

When using for children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine is for external use only; do not take orally or inhale.

Be careful not to get it in your eyes; if it does get into your eyes, wash with water.

Wash off any medicine liquid that gets on your hands thoroughly.

Do not cover or wrap the applied area, and do not expose it to external heat or hot water.

If water touches the applied area after using this medicine, you may feel a strong irritation.

Patients with particularly sensitive skin should not use this medicine continuously on the same area.

Do not use for a long period.
