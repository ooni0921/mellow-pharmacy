#EN
### 💊 쎈비큐정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness, growth periods, and in old age.
*   Through its contained vitamins, this medicine is used to alleviate symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, stiff shoulders, other joint pains, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.
*   Through its contained vitamins, this medicine is used for beriberi, eye fatigue, and poor bone or tooth development.
*   This medicine is used for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 쎈비큐정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years or older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 쎈비큐정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, have hypercalcemia, kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, an elderly person, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, hypoproteinemia, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, a history of kidney stones, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or require cautious administration of lipid emulsions.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   Please strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
