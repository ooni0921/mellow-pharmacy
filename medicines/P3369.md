#EN
### 💊 Rubidoll Plus Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 Rubidoll Plus Capsule - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Rubidoll Plus Capsule - Precautions
* No information
* Do not administer this medicine to children under 15 years of age.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to medicines, cosmetics, or eggs, or if you have an allergic constitution.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for more than 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 루비돌플러스캡슐 - 用法用量

* 无信息
