#EN
### 💊 콘드로빅300연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is low during illness or convalescence (during or after recovery from illness), for the supplementation of Vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in old age, for peripheral circulatory disorders, for the relief of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands/feet (cold hands/feet) during menopause, for neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, relief of symptoms of dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 콘드로빅300연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, including adults, take 1 capsule at a time, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 콘드로빅300연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient for whom lipid emulsions should be administered cautiously, a patient with special risk expected from fat overload, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition, a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow, which could affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
