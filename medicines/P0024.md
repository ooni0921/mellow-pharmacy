# EN
### 💊 키모랄에스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the relief of inflammatory edema after surgery and trauma, and in cases of galactostasis (milk stasis).

### 🕒 키모랄에스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2 tablets once, 4 times a day on the first day, and then take 1 tablet once, 4 times a day as a maintenance dose.

Many aspects of its mechanism of action in the body are yet to be elucidated, and the dose-effect relationship is not clear; therefore, this drug should not be administered without a specific purpose.

### ⚠️ 키모랄에스정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this drug.

Before taking this drug, patients with blood coagulation disorders, severe liver or kidney impairment, and women who are pregnant or may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
