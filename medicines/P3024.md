### 💊 Hanpoong Cold Liquid - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 Hanpoong Cold Liquid - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 15 years or older: Take 1 pouch (90 mL) three times daily.
*   Children aged 11 years to under 15 years: Take 2/3 pouch (approx. 60 mL) three times daily.
*   Children aged 7 years to under 11 years: Take 1/2 pouch (approx. 45 mL) three times daily.
*   Children aged 3 years to under 7 years: Take 1/3 pouch (approx. 30 mL) three times daily.
*   Children aged 2 years to under 3 years: Take 1/4 pouch (approx. 22.5 mL) three times daily.
*   Administer 30 minutes after meals. If necessary, it can be taken at bedtime. In principle, the dosing interval should be 4 hours.

### ⚠️ Hanpoong Cold Liquid - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

    Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, its components, other antipyretic analgesics, or cold medicines that caused asthma, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age or a child under 2 years of age.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, a child under 2 years of age (only if absolutely necessary), a person with a predisposition to hives, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraine, food allergies, etc., a patient who has previously experienced allergic symptoms to medication, a person with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, edema, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma, difficulty urinating, an elderly person, a frail person, a person with high fever, a person with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or with ulcer or bleeding problems, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a smoker, or a person with asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than one week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    Do not use continuously for a long period.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
