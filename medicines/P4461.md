#EN
### 💊 슈페젠정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 슈페젠정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 슈페젠정 - Precautions
* No specific information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; an infant or young child aged 3 months to under 2 years; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant or is breastfeeding; a person with a weakened body; an allergic constitution, or a history of allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); or if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, high fever, heartburn, gastric discomfort, stomach pain or other gastrointestinal problems, ulcers, bleeding problems, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, cough that lasts for more than 1 week or recurs, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
