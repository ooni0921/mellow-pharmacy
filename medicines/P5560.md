### 💊 비메진정 - 효능 및 효과
* 이 약은 육체피로, 임신·수유기, 병중·병후(병을 앓는 동안이나 회복 후)의 체력저하시의 비타민 B1, B6 의 보급 및 신경통, 근육통, 관절통(요통, 어깨결림 등), 구각염(입꼬리염), 구순염(입술염), 구내염(입안염), 설염(혀염), 습진, 피부염, 각기, 눈의 피로의 완화에 사용합니다.

### 🕒 비메진정 - 용법 및 용량
* 만 8세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정, 1일 2회 또는 1회 2정, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 비메진정 - 주의사항
* 정보 없음

이 약을 복용하기 전에 의사의 치료를 받고 있는 환자는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 비메진정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1 and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and decreased physical strength during and after illness (during illness or after recovery), and for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain) (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 비메진정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 8 years of age and older should take 1 tablet twice a day, or 2 tablets once a day.

### ⚠️ 비메진정 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, infants under 3 months of age, or those with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.

Patients currently receiving medical treatment should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
