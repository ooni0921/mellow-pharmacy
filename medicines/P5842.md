### 💊 Bimax Ever Premium Tablet - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to supplement vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or periods of physical decline after recovery, during growth, and in old age.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medication is used to relieve symptoms of peripheral circulatory disorders and, during menopause, shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medication is used to relieve symptoms of joint pain such as neuralgia, myalgia, lower back pain, or shoulder stiffness.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medication is used for the relief of dry eyes, night blindness, poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

This medication is used to supplement zinc.

### 🕒 Bimax Ever Premium Tablet - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older and adults, take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ Bimax Ever Premium Tablet - Precautions
*   If pregnant women take more than 5,000 IU of vitamin A per day, it may cause congenital malformations. Women in the first three months of pregnancy or those who may be pregnant should not take this medication.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months, or have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, premature infant, infant, elderly, or have hyperoxaluria, gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, cardiovascular dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones or a history thereof, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously.

If symptoms do not improve after about 1 month of taking this medication, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with a thrombotic predisposition or folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Since the vitamin A contained in this medication is sufficiently supplied through a normal diet, ensure that the dose for adjunctive therapy does not exceed 5,000 IU per day.

This medication may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
