#EN
### 💊 비타비스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical weakness during illness or convalescence (during or after recovery from illness), and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 비타비스정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 2 tablets at a time, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 비타비스정 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to any component of this medicine, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), gout or kidney stones, have a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), or have folic acid deficiency.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine may interfere with blood sugar detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 비타비스정 - 功效及效果
* 本药用于缓解以下症状：身体疲劳、妊娠/哺乳期、病中/病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、老年期维生素 E、B1、B2、B6、C 的补充，以及神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮肤炎、脚气病、眼睛疲劳。

### 🕒 비타비스정 - 用法及用量
* 8岁以上儿童及成人：每次2片，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 비타비스정 - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 对本药过敏者、未满3个月的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、痛风或肾结石患者、有血栓（血管阻塞）倾向的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
* 本药在进行各种尿液检查时可能会干扰血糖检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查值。
* 如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
