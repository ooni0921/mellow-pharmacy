#EN
### 💊 큐어코프에이연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 큐어코프에이연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older should take 2 capsules three times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 큐어코프에이연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or under 2 years of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, have experienced allergic reactions (such as fever, rash, joint pain, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty urinating, are elderly, are frail or have a high fever, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, or have a cough (due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   For infants and young children aged 7 years or younger, administer a syrup formulation.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

*   本药用于治疗咳嗽和咳痰。

### 🕒 큐어코프에이연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   15岁以上成人及青少年，每日3次，每次2粒，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 큐어코프에이연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   对本药过敏者、未满3个月的婴儿（新生儿）及未满2岁的儿童请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，如果您有以下情况，请咨询医生或药剂师：过敏体质者、有药物过敏反应史（如发热、皮疹、关节痛、瘙痒等）、肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、青光眼（如眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者、老年人、体弱者或高热患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、以及以下情况的咳嗽患者（吸烟引起的咳嗽、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽）。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   7岁以下的婴幼儿（新生儿）、幼儿请服用糖浆剂。
*   服用后可能会出现困倦，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

### 🕒 큐어코프에이연질캡슐 - 用法・用量

*   7歳以下の乳児（新生児）、幼児にはシロップ剤を服用させてください。
*   服用後に眠気が現れることがあるため、運転や機械操作を行う際は注意してください。
