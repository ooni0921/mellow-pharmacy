#EN

### 💊 테라젠비타골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilosis), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis. Additionally, it helps prevent beriberi, eye fatigue, poor development of bones and teeth, and rickets, and provides zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 테라젠비타골드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 12 years of age or older and adults, take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 테라젠비타골드정 - Warnings and Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, premature baby, infant/young child, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), impaired cardiac or circulatory function, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or have experienced kidney stones, have a thrombotic predisposition (tendency for blood clots), have folic acid deficiency, or have hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.
*   If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately one month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 💊 테라젠비타골드정 - 功效与作用
*   本药品用于补充体力疲劳、妊娠及哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降、发育期、老年期的维生素B1、B2、B6、C、D、E。并用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。此外，还用于预防脚气病、眼睛疲劳、骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病，以及补充锌。

### 🕒 테라젠비타골드정 - 用法用量
*   12岁以上儿童及成人，每次1片，每日1次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 테라젠비타골드정 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药品过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态）、结节病（不明原因的全身炎症性疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本药品。
*   服用本药品前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症（尿液中草酸盐排出过多的状态）、心血管系统功能障碍者、肾脏功能障碍者、低蛋白血症者、痛风或有肾结石病史者、有血栓（血管堵塞）倾向者、叶酸缺乏者、对黄色4号色素过敏或有过敏史者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守用法用量。
*   本药品在进行各种尿液检查时可能干扰血糖检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查结果。
*   若服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
