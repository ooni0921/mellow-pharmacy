### π’ μ•„μ„Έν‹΄μΊ΅μ(μ•„μ„Έν‹Έμ‹μ¤ν…μΈ) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for difficulty in sputum expectoration in diseases such as acute and chronic bronchitis, bronchial asthma, laryngitis, sinusitis, and cystic fibrosis.

### π•’ μ•„μ„Έν‹΄μΊ΅μ(μ•„μ„Έν‹Έμ‹μ¤ν…μΈ) - Dosage and Administration
* For acute diseases: Adults take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, 3 times daily. Children aged 6-14 take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, 2 times daily. Take before meals with a small amount of water.
* For chronic diseases: Adults take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, 2 times daily. Children aged 6-14 take 1/2 capsule (100 mg) once, 3 times daily. Take before meals with a small amount of water.
* For cystic fibrosis: Individuals aged 6 years and older take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, 3 times daily. Take before meals with a small amount of water.

### β οΈ μ•„μ„Έν‹΄μΊ΅μ(μ•„μ„Έν‹Έμ‹μ¤ν…μΈ) - Precautions
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastric or duodenal ulcers, are an infant under 2 years of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Pregnant or potentially pregnant women, and breastfeeding mothers, should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
