# EN
### 💊 판그로캡슐 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for damaged hair, non-infectious nail growth disorders, and as an adjuvant therapy for hair loss.

### 🕒 판그로캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day. Children aged 12 years and older take 1 capsule once, 1 to 2 times a day after meals. The average treatment period is 3 to 6 months.

### ⚠️ 판그로캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, a child under 12 years of age, have hair loss due to scarring, androgenetic alopecia, or male-pattern baldness. Before taking this medicine, pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
