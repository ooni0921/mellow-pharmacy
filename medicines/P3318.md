#EN
### 💊 실비도정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for tinnitus.

### 🕒 실비도정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older should take 2 to 3 tablets once, 3 times a day, after meals with water.

### ⚠️ 실비도정 - Precautions
*   No specific information.
*   Do not administer this medication to children under 15 years of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are hypersensitive to other antihistamines, motion sickness medications, cold medications, or antitussive-expectorants, or if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, have urinary disorders such as prostatic hypertrophy, glaucoma, or a history of heart disease.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
