#EN
### 💊 메가원플러스정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C in cases of physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, during/after illness, growth period, and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis; for the prevention of poor bone/tooth development and rickets; and for beriberi, eye fatigue, and zinc replenishment.

### 🕒 메가원플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 메가원플러스정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), renal disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or have severe renal failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who receives ample sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant (general term for young child), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a history of kidney stones.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose in various urine tests and may affect clinical test results by turning the urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

本品在各种尿液检查中可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查结果。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
