#EN
### 💊 파워스탑스키니플라스타(케토프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder joint, tendinitis, tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 파워스탑스키니플라스타(케토프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* After removing the release liner from the patch, apply it to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 파워스탑스키니플라스타(케토프로펜) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity or photosensitivity to this drug, aspirin asthma (asthmatic attacks caused by NSAIDs, etc.) or a history thereof, hypersensitivity to components that may cause cross-hypersensitivity reactions (tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, oxybenzone), if you are pregnant for 6 months or more, if you are under 15 years of age, or on the eyes, mucous membranes, athlete's foot, or ringworm. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are applying it to damaged skin, eczema, or rashes. During use and for up to 2 weeks after use, regardless of the weather, avoid outdoor activities and cover the application area with clothes, sunglasses, hats, sunscreen, etc., to prevent exposure to ultraviolet rays when going out. Do not use occlusive dressings. If symptoms do not improve after approximately one week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
