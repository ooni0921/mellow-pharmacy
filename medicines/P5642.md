#EN
### 💊 씨엠지마그액 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B2 and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, or when physical strength is depleted during or after illness.
*   Through its contained vitamins, this medicine is used to relieve symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.
*   This medicine is used for muscle cramps caused by magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 씨엠지마그액 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 20 ml once, twice daily, between meals (i.e., between mealtimes).

### ⚠️ 씨엠지마그액 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal insufficiency.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, or if you have or have experienced caramel hypersensitivity.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately stop taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
