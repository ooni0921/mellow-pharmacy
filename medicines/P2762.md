### 💊 하원아스피린장용정100mg - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used to inhibit thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, to inhibit thrombus formation after Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) or Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA), and to reduce cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, and diabetes).

### 🕒 하원아스피린장용정100mg - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once daily. This medication is an enteric-coated tablet, so it can be taken before meals with plenty of water.

### ⚠️ 하원아스피린장용정100mg - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or antirheumatic drugs, peptic ulcer, aspirin asthma or a history thereof, hemophilia, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency, or if you are concomitantly taking methotrexate at a dose exceeding 15 mg/week, or if you are in the third trimester of pregnancy. Do not take this medication during the first and second trimesters of pregnancy unless absolutely necessary. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (such as renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, dehydration, major surgery, sepsis, or significant bleeding events), hepatic impairment, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are a pre-surgical patient, an infant under 3 years of age, an elderly person, a patient with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a patient under 14 years of age with chickenpox or influenza infection, or a patient with gout. Inform your doctor that you are taking this medication if you are undergoing surgery (including dental surgery) due to the risk of bleeding. Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medication.
