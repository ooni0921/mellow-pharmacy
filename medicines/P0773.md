### EN
### 💊 Cetoma Soft Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamin E in old age, relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorder, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness and coldness in hands and feet, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 Cetoma Soft Capsule - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ Cetoma Soft Capsule - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or soybean oil, soybeans, peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal insufficiency.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have heart or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatic inflammation, or other lipid metabolism disorders, or if you are a patient who needs to be carefully administered lipid emulsions, or if you are a patient at special risk due to lipid overload, or if you have a thrombotic (vascular blockage) predisposition.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
*   When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
