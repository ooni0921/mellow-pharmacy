#EN

### 💊 콘맥스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness, growth periods, and old age.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medication is used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain or stiff shoulders), poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

This medication is used for the supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 콘맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 19 years and over: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 콘맥스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, or suffer from hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, or kidney stones.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or potentially pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with renal impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, or have a history of kidney stones.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medication may cause urine to turn yellow and affect clinical test results.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
