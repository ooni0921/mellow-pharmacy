급성전신발진고름물집증과 같은 중증 피부 이상반응이 나타나는 경우 복용을 중단하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

### 💊 킥노즈에스연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, chills (symptoms of feeling cold and shaking), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 킥노즈에스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: Take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 킥노즈에스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

    Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. Discontinue use immediately if skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication.

    Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are under 2 years of age, are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, or if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) appear in relation to the pseudoephedrine component. Also consult if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, are elderly, are frail or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts or recurs for more than a week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication; exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
