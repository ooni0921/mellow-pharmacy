#EN

*   This medication is used for nutritional supply in ulcerative diseases of the cornea and conjunctiva due to nutritional deficiency, and for micro-damage to the cornea and conjunctiva caused by factors such as contact lens wear.

### 🕒 아이리스케어점안액(폴리데옥시리보뉴클레오티드나트륨)(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Instill 2-3 drops at a time, 2-4 times a day.
*   Discard any remaining solution and the container after instillation.

### ⚠️ 아이리스케어점안액(폴리데옥시리보뉴클레오티드나트륨)(1회용) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not use it.
*   Patients with significant corneal damage should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medication.
*   If symptoms do not improve after about 2 weeks of use, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When used in children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication is for ophthalmic use only.
*   When using for the first time, discard the first 1-2 drops without instilling them.
*   Use this medication only once after opening.
*   If the tip of the container touches the eyelid or eyelashes, the solution may become contaminated or cloudy (turbid) by eye discharge or fungi, so be careful. Also, do not use if the solution is cloudy (turbid).
*   To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the medication if possible.
