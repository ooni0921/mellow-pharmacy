### 🕒 알카펜네이잘연질연질캡슐(수출명:Alkafen-N Soft Caps., Alkafen Cough Soft Caps.) - Dosage and Administration

### ⚠️ 알카펜네이잘연질연질캡슐(수출명:Alkafen-N Soft Caps., Alkafen Cough Soft Caps.) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. If severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, are physically weak, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), or have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, high fever, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or urination difficulties. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
