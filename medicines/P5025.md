#EN
### 💊 무피신연고(무피로신) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for most bacterial skin infections caused by Staphylococcus aureus (including methicillin-resistant strains), other staphylococci, and streptococci; gram-negative bacteria such as E. coli and Haemophilus influenzae; and bacterial skin infections such as impetigo, folliculitis, furunculosis, infected eczema, and bacterial skin infections in traumatic injuries and burns.

### 🕒 무피신연고(무피로신) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply a small amount to the infected area 2-3 times a day for 10 days.

The treated area may be dressed or occluded if necessary.

### ⚠️ 무피신연고(무피로신) - Precautions
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or its ingredients, or to other ointments containing polyethylene glycol. Also, do not use if you have kidney disease, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

Be careful not to get it in your eyes.

For external use only.

Do not use this medication on mucous membranes such as the eyes or nose.

Do not use this medication on areas where central venous catheterization will be performed.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after 3 to 5 days of use, stop using the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 무피신연고(무피로신) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗由金黄色葡萄球菌（包括甲氧西林耐药菌株）、其他葡萄球菌和链球菌等引起的大部分皮肤感染，以及由大肠杆菌和流感嗜血杆菌等革兰氏阴性菌引起的感染，和脓疱病、毛囊炎、疖病、感染性湿疹等细菌性皮肤感染，以及外伤和烧伤后的细菌性皮肤感染。

### 🕒 무피피신연고(무피로신) - 用法用量
* 每日2-3次，将少量药膏涂抹于感染部位，持续10天。

必要时，可对涂药部位进行敷料包扎或密封。

### ⚠️ 무피신연고(무피로신) - 注意事项
* 对本药或其成分，或含有聚乙二醇的其他软膏剂过敏的患者、肾脏疾病患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的妇女、哺乳期妇女请勿使用本药。

注意避免接触眼睛。

本药仅供外用。

请勿在眼睛、鼻子等粘膜表面使用本药。

请勿在即将进行中心静脉置管的部位使用本药。

使用3-5天后若症状无改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
