# EN
### 💊 헥시클렌브러쉬액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염 액) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for hand disinfection by surgeons and healthcare professionals during surgery.

### 🕒 헥시클렌브러쉬액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염 액) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply a sponge saturated with this medication's solution to the hands and up to the elbows, then scrub thoroughly by hand for 3 minutes. Pay particular attention to scrubbing the nails, cuticles, and areas between the fingers. If necessary, use a brush for scrubbing. Rinse with warm water, then repeat the scrubbing process for another 3 minutes. After rinsing thoroughly with water, dry completely.

### ⚠️ 헥시클렌브러쉬액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염 액) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to it, or on mucous membranes such as the brain, spinal cord, ears, vagina, bladder, oral cavity, eyes, eroded (chafed) areas, or areas with suppuration. Patients with hypersensitivity to other medications, allergic diseases like asthma, or a history or family history thereof, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, or lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medication is for external use only; do not administer orally or by injection. Be careful not to let it get into your eyes, as contact may cause corneal damage. If it does get into your eyes, rinse thoroughly with clean water immediately. This medication is adsorbed by fibers and fabrics (cotton, gauze, wool, rayon, etc.), so when using it by soaking, if the effective concentration is not maintained, supply a new solution. The diluted solution used during hand washing should be replaced with a new solution at least daily.

# CN
### 💊 헥시클렌브러쉬액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염 액) - 功效及效果
* 本药品用于手术时，对手术者及保健卫生从业人员的手部进行消毒。

### 🕒 헥시클렌브러쉬액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염 액) - 用法及用量
* 将吸收有本药品药液的海绵应用于手部至肘部，用手搓洗3分钟。特别注意搓洗指甲、表皮和手指间的部位。如有需要，可使用刷子搓洗。用温水冲洗干净，然后再次重复搓洗3分钟，再用清水彻底冲洗干净后，完全擦干。

### ⚠️ 헥시클렌브러쉬액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염 액) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药品有过敏史者，以及脑、脊髓、耳、阴道、膀胱、口腔等粘膜表面、眼睛、糜烂（擦伤）、有化脓的部位，请勿使用。对其他药品有过敏症、哮喘等过敏性疾病及其病史或家族史的患者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，或哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药品仅供外用，请勿口服或注射。接触眼睛可能导致角膜损伤，因此请注意不要进入眼睛。万一进入眼睛，请立即用清水彻底冲洗。本药品会被纤维・布料（棉、纱布、羊毛、人造丝等）吸附，因此在浸泡使用时，如果未达到有效浓度，请补充新的溶液。手部清洗过程中使用的稀释液至少应每天更换新的溶液。
