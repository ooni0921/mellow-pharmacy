### 💊 Bipainjeong - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical weakness during or after illness, growth periods, and old age; for poor development of bones and teeth; for beriberi, eye fatigue, and prevention of rickets; for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as low back pain, stiff shoulders); for angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation inside the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis; and for the replenishment of zinc.

### 🕒 Bipainjeong - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ Bipainjeong - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, soy, or peanuts; if you have experienced an allergic reaction to soybean oil; if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood); sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause); kidney disease; kidney stones; severe renal failure; or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcers, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis; have heart or circulatory system dysfunction; have kidney impairment or kidney stones or a history thereof; have hypoproteinemia; have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis (inflammation of the pancreas); or if you are a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsions, or a patient for whom a special risk is anticipated due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medication may turn urine yellow and can affect clinical test values.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of use, immediately discontinue the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
