# EN
### 💊 아인쿨점안액(수출명:아이쿨점안액) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for eye fatigue, conjunctival hyperemia, discomfort after swimming or when dust or sweat enters the eye, inflammation of the eye caused by ultraviolet and other light, blepharitis (sore eyelids), discomfort when wearing hard contact lenses, itchy eyes, and blurred vision (e.g., when there is a lot of eye discharge).

### 🕒 아인쿨점안액(수출명:아이쿨점안액) - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops at a time, 5-6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 아인쿨점안액(수출명:아이쿨점안액) - Precautions
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe eye pain, a history of allergic reactions to eye drops (e.g., eye redness, itching, swelling, rash, hyperemia leading to redness), glaucoma, or hypersensitivity to propylene glycol. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. Use this medicine only for ophthalmic instillation. Do not use as a soft contact lens solution or while wearing soft contact lenses. To prevent contamination, avoid co-use whenever possible. Do not administer if the expiration date has passed, and use opened containers as soon as possible.

# CN

* 本药用于缓解眼睛疲劳、结膜充血、游泳后眼部不适或灰尘汗水入眼、紫外线及其他光线引起的眼部炎症、眼睑糜烂、佩戴硬性隐形眼镜时的不适感、眼睛瘙痒、视力模糊（如眼屎较多时等）。

### 🕒 아인쿨점안액(수출명:아이쿨점안액) - 用法用量
* 每次2~3滴，每日5~6次滴眼。

### ⚠️ 아인쿨점안액(수출명:아이쿨점안액) - 注意事项
* 在使用本药前，如您有严重的眼部疼痛、对眼药水有过敏症状（例如：眼部充血、瘙痒、肿胀、皮疹、充血变红等）的经历、青光眼、对丙二醇过敏或有相关过敏史，请咨询医生或药剂师。请严格遵守规定的用法用量。本药仅供滴眼使用。请勿作为软性隐形眼镜的护理液使用，或在佩戴软性隐形眼镜时使用。为防止污染，请尽量避免共同使用。请勿使用超过有效期的产品，已开封的产品请尽快使用。
