#EN
### 💊 포린정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamin B6 during pregnancy and lactation, and during periods of physical exhaustion due to illness or recovery from illness. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 포린정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 포린정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, an infant under 3 months of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age and patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
