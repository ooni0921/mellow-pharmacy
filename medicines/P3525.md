#EN
### 💊 크록쎈연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of mild to moderate pain such as muscle pain, neuralgia, post-traumatic and post-operative pain, headache, toothache, and earache.

### 🕒 크록쎈연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 to 2 capsules (125-250 mg) per dose, three times a day.

### ⚠️ 크록쎈연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have peptic ulcer, gastric or duodenal disturbance caused by medication, hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma, urticaria or allergic reactions to aspirin or other NSAIDs (including COX-2 inhibitors), are undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are pregnant or may be pregnant, have severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe heart failure, or inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you have a history of peptic ulcer, are a child or elderly, have hypertension, fluid retention or heart failure, bronchial asthma, hepatic impairment or a history thereof, or renal impairment or a history thereof.
