# EN
### 💊 메코민캡슐(메코발라민) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for peripheral neuropathy.

### 🕒 메코민캡슐(메코발라민) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 3 capsules (1,500 ㎍) daily, divided into 3 doses.

### ⚠️ 메코민캡슐(메코발라민) - Precautions
* No information provided (This refers to a lack of further subheadings, the following points are precautions.)
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, or with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, should not take this drug. Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve or worsen after approximately 1 month of administration, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
