### 💊 센스가드정 - Indications & Effects
*   This medicine is used for contraception and for the treatment of moderate acne in women who can take this medicine.

### 🕒 센스가드정 - Dosage & Administration
*   Take one tablet daily for 21 consecutive days, following the order indicated, followed by a 7-day tablet-free interval.

For more detailed information, please refer to the full prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 센스가드정 - Warnings & Precautions
*   Women who use oral contraceptives should not smoke because smoking increases the risk of serious cardiovascular side effects such as thrombosis. Women over 35 years of age who smoke should not use this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, estrogen-dependent tumors (e.g., endometrial cancer), breast cancer (or suspicion thereof, or a history of it), thrombophlebitis or thromboembolism (or a history of it), deep vein thrombosis, congenital or acquired thrombophilia, cerebrovascular or coronary artery disease (especially myocardial infarction), undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding, moderate to severe hypertension, moderate to severe hepatic impairment, jaundice, hepatitis, benign or malignant liver tumors (or a history of them), severe diabetes mellitus with vascular complications, severe metabolic disorders such as severe hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, and porphyria, persistent pruritus during pregnancy, herpes gestationis, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, have pituitary tumors, hyperprolactinemia, valvular heart disease, diabetes mellitus with vascular lesions, pancreatitis, rare hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, congenital or acquired hypercoagulopathy, or are a smoker over 35 years of age.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are a smoker, have hypertension, are obese, are over 40 years of age, have epilepsy, have migraines, have a family history of arterial disease or a history of varicose veins, have systemic lupus erythematosus, have a family history of breast cancer, have or have a history of depression, have cardiac or renal failure, have atypical hyperplastic mammary disease, have a history of breast nodules, have or have a history of valvular heart disease or atrial fibrillation, have a family history of hyperlipidemia, have a history of gallbladder disease and gallstones, have endometriosis, asthma or chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorders, diabetes mellitus, or migraines without aura.

This medicine does not protect against HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases.

In cases of severe gastrointestinal disorders, absorption may be incomplete, so additional contraceptive methods should be used.

This medicine is not indicated for pre-menarcheal women or post-menopausal women.

For detailed warnings and precautions, please refer to the full prescribing information.
