#EN

### 💊 콜민코연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 콜민코연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years of age and older: Take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 콜민코연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Regular drinkers of three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

    Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). Exceeding this may cause liver damage. Do not take concomitantly with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

    Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are: an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza (including children under 2 years of age); have an allergic constitution; have a history of allergic reactions (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric or duodenal ulcers, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcers or bleeding issues; are pregnant, possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding; smoke; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting more than 1 week or that recurs, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
    If symptoms do not improve even after 5-6 doses, immediately stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 💊 콜민코연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解感冒的各种症状（流鼻涕、鼻塞、打喷嚏、喉咙痛、寒战、发热、头痛、关节痛、肌肉痛）。

### 🕒 콜민코연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   15岁以上及成人，一次2粒，一日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 콜민코연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒超过三杯的常规饮酒者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。

    极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。如果在服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。

    扑热息痛每日最大剂量（4,000毫克）请勿超量服用。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与其他含有扑热息痛的产品一同服用。

    如果出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等严重皮肤不良反应，请停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
*   对本药过敏者、服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾出现哮喘者、3个月以下的婴儿、重度或未受控制的高血压患者请勿服用本药。

    在服用本药之前，请咨询医生或药师：如果感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿及15岁以下儿童（包括2岁以下儿童），过敏体质者，有过敏反应（发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）史者，患有肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者，老年人，体弱者或高热者，胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性及哺乳期妇女，吸烟者，患有哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽，持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽，慢性咳嗽，伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽者。

    服用后可能会出现困倦，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
    服用5-6次后症状仍未改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

### ⚠️ 콜민코연질캡슐 - 注意事項
*   毎日3杯以上飲酒する習慣のある方は、本剤または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する前に、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。
