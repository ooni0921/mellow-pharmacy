#EN
### 💊 엔디펜알파정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for neuralgia, lumbago (lower back pain), and stiff shoulders.

### 🕒 엔디펜알파정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets at a time, twice daily, when pain or symptoms occur.

### ⚠️ 엔디펜알파정 - Precautions
* No information provided
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), are an infant (nursing baby), young child, or child (under 14 years old) infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, are physically weak, have a high fever, are elderly, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication several times, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
