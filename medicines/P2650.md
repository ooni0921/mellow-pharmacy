#EN
### 💊 비타포린정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supplementation of Vitamin B6 during pregnancy and lactation, or when experiencing physical weakness during or after illness. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 비타포린정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 8 years or older, take 1 tablet per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 비타포린정 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication, infants under 3 months of age, and patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication.

Before taking this medication, infants under 1 year of age and patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
