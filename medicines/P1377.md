# EN
### 💊 아나탁심정(나프록센나트륨) - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), ankylosing spondylitis, tendinitis, acute gout, dysmenorrhea, bursitis, musculoskeletal disorders (sprains, contusions, trauma, lumbosacral pain), postoperative pain, migraine, and post-extraction pain.

### 🕒 아나탁심정(나프록센나트륨) - Dosage & Administration
*   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, and ankylosing spondylitis, adults should take 1 to 2 tablets (275 to 550 mg) once, twice daily (every 12 hours). For acute gout, an initial dose of 3 tablets (825 mg) should be taken, followed by 1 tablet (275 mg) every 8 hours until the attack subsides. For musculoskeletal disorders, postoperative pain, post-extraction pain, dysmenorrhea, tendinitis, and bursitis, an initial dose of 2 tablets (550 mg) should be taken, followed by 1 tablet (275 mg) every 6 to 8 hours, ensuring that the total daily dose does not exceed 5 tablets (1,375 mg). For migraine, an initial dose of 3 tablets (825 mg) should be taken, and if necessary, an additional 1 to 2 tablets (275 to 550 mg) may be taken daily, 30 minutes after the initial dose.

    The total daily dose should not exceed 5 tablets (1,375 mg).

### ⚠️ 아나탁심정(나프록센나트륨) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

    Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal, cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may occur.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are pregnant or breastfeeding, are an infant under 2 years old, have peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe renal impairment, severe hypertension, hypersensitivity to non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) such as aspirin, or have experienced asthma, rhinitis, nasal polyps, urticaria, or allergic reactions due to aspirin or other NSAIDs (including COX-2 inhibitors). Also, do not use for pain management before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

    Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, children, the elderly, those with a history of peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver impairment, renal impairment, cardiac dysfunction, hypertension, bronchial asthma, systemic lupus erythematosus, and mixed connective tissue disease should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 아나탁심정(나프록센나트륨) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于治疗类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎（退行性关节疾病）、强直性脊柱炎、肌腱炎、急性痛风、月经困难症、滑囊炎、肌肉骨骼疾病（扭伤、挫伤、外伤、腰骶部疼痛）、术后疼痛、偏头痛和拔牙后疼痛。

### 🕒 아나탁심정(나프록센나트륨) - 用法与用量
*   对于类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎，成人每次服用1-2片（275-550 mg），每日2次（每12小时一次）。对于急性痛风，首次剂量为3片（825 mg），之后每8小时服用1片（275 mg），直至症状消失。对于肌肉骨骼疾病、术后疼痛、拔牙后疼痛、月经困难症、肌腱炎、滑囊炎，首次剂量为2片（550 mg），之后每6-8小时服用1片（275 mg），每日总剂量不得超过5片（1,375 mg）。对于偏头痛，首次剂量为3片（825 mg），如有需要，可每日追加服用1-2片（275-550 mg），在首次剂量服用30分钟后服用。

    每日总剂量不得超过5片（1,375 mg）。

### ⚠️ 아나탁심정(나프록센나트륨) - 注意事项
*   如果每日饮酒三杯以上且正在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药，请咨询医生或药师。本药可能引起胃肠道出血。

    服用本药可能增加致命性心血管血栓事件、心肌梗死和中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
*   以下患者请勿服用本药：对本药过敏者、孕妇、哺乳期妇女、2岁以下婴儿、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重心功能不全、严重肾功能障碍、严重高血压患者、对阿司匹林等非甾体抗炎药（NSAIDs）过敏者，或因阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药（包括COX-2抑制剂）引发哮喘、鼻炎、鼻息肉、荨麻疹、过敏反应者，以及冠状动脉旁路移植术前后疼痛治疗的患者，或患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者。

    服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、儿童、老年人、有消化性溃疡病史者、有血液异常病史者、有出血倾向的患者、有肝功能障碍病史者、有肾功能障碍病史者、有心功能障碍、高血压、支气管哮喘、系统性红斑狼疮和混合性结缔组织病患者，请咨询医生或药师。

### 🕒 아나탁심정(나프록센나트륨) - 用法及び用量
*   関節リウマチ、変形性関節症、強直性脊椎炎の場合、成人は1回1～2錠（275～550 mg）を1日2回（12時間ごと）服用します。急性痛風の場合、初回量として3錠（825 mg）を服用し、発作が消失するまで8時間ごとに1錠（275 mg）を服用します。筋骨格系障害、術後疼痛、抜歯後疼痛、月経困難症、腱炎、滑液包炎の場合、初回量として2錠（550 mg）を服用した後、6～8時間ごとに1錠（275 mg）ずつ服用し、1日の総用量が5錠（1,375 mg）を超えないようにします。片頭痛の場合、初回量として3錠（825 mg）を服用し、必要に応じて1日1～2錠（275～550 mg）を追加服用でき、初回量服用後30分後に服用します。

    1日の総用量が5錠（1,375 mg）を超えないようにしてください。
