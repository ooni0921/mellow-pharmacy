# EN

### 💊 Serind Ointment - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for eczema, heat rash, skin itching, allergic skin conditions, burns, and frostbite.

### 🕒 Serind Ointment - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area (diseased part) 2-3 times a day.

### ⚠️ Serind Ointment - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use on affected areas with chickenpox, athlete's foot, ringworm, or suppuration, on mucous membranes such as the conjunctiva, or on extensive areas of the face. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of allergies to medications or cosmetics, if the affected area is extensive, wet, or severely festering, or if you are pregnant or may be pregnant. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. This medication is for external use only; do not ingest.
