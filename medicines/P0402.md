##EN
### 💊 Q-zyme Tab. - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for loss of appetite (anorexia), abdominal distension, indigestion, overeating, stomach upset, nausea, vomiting, promotion of digestion, and abdominal distension caused by indigestion.

### 🕒 Q-zyme Tab. - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years of age and older should take one tablet per dose, three times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Q-zyme Tab. - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not administer this medicine to children under 7 years of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with allergic constitution, hypertension, the elderly, patients with renal impairment (kidney disorder), or edema (swelling) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking the medicine for approximately 2 weeks, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

##CN
### 💊 큐자임정 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于食欲减退（食欲不振）、胃部胀满、消化不良、过食、积食、恶心、呕吐、促进消化，以及因消化不良引起的胃部胀满。

### 🕒 큐자임정 - 用法用量
*   成人及15岁以上儿童，一次1片，一日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 큐자임정 - 注意事项
*   暂无信息
*   7岁以下儿童请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，过敏体质患者、高血压患者、老年人、肾功能障碍患者、水肿患者应咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
