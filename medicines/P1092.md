#EN
### 💊 Rhinocold Syrup - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the temporary relief of sneezing, runny nose, nasal congestion, and watery eyes due due to cold or allergic and vasomotor rhinitis, and nasal congestion due to sinusitis.

### 🕒 Rhinocold Syrup - Dosage and Administration
*   Children 12 years and older and adults: Take 10 mL every 4 hours, not to exceed 60 mL in 24 hours.
*   Children 6 years and older but under 12 years: Take 5 mL every 4 hours, not to exceed 30 mL in 24 hours.
*   Children 2 years and older but under 6 years: Follow physician's instructions.

### ⚠️ Rhinocold Syrup - Precautions
*   None provided.
*   Do not take Rhinocold Syrup if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine or to amine-type sympathomimetics, antihistamines, or if you have an idiosyncratic constitution, are a nursing mother, or have severe hypertension, severe coronary artery disease, narrow-angle glaucoma, urinary retention, peptic ulcer, asthma, emphysema, chronic pulmonary disease, or shortness of breath or respiratory distress.
*   Before taking Rhinocold Syrup, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with difficulty breathing due to chronic bronchitis, heart disease, hypertension, thyroid disorders, diabetes, glaucoma, difficulty urinating due to prostatic hypertrophy, or if you are a child.
*   Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery after taking this medicine.

#CN
### 💊 Rhinocold Syrup - 功效与作用
*   本品用于暂时缓解感冒、过敏性鼻炎和血管运动性鼻炎（鼻炎）引起的打喷嚏、流鼻涕、鼻塞、流泪，以及鼻窦炎引起的鼻充血（鼻塞）。

### 🕒 Rhinocold Syrup - 用法用量
*   12岁及以上儿童和成人：每4小时服用10毫升，24小时内不得超过60毫升。
*   6岁及以上至12岁以下儿童：每4小时服用5毫升，24小时内不得超过30毫升。
*   2岁及以上至6岁以下儿童：请遵医嘱。

### ⚠️ Rhinocold Syrup - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本品或胺类拟交感神经药、抗组胺药过敏者、特异体质者、哺乳期妇女、严重高血压、严重冠状动脉疾病、闭角型青光眼、尿潴留（小便潴留）、消化性溃疡、哮喘、肺气肿、慢性肺疾病、气短或呼吸困难的患者，请勿服用Rhinocold Syrup。
*   服用Rhinocold Syrup前，患有慢性支气管炎等呼吸困难、心脏病、高血压、甲状腺功能疾病、糖尿病、青光眼、前列腺肥大引起的排尿困难的患者以及儿童，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   服药后驾驶或操作机械时请注意安全。
