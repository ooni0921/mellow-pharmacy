#EN
### 💊 조인트콤비800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is low during or after illness, during growth periods, and in old age.
*   Through the contained vitamins and other ingredients, this drug is used for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, back pain, joint pain such as shoulder stiffness, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.
*   Through the contained vitamins and other ingredients, this drug is used for bone and teeth developmental disorders, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 조인트콤비800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 조인트콤비800정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, or kidney stones should not take this drug.
*   Before taking this drug, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, premature infants, infants, and individuals with a history of kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this drug for about one month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This drug may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
