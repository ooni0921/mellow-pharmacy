#EN
### 💊 칼시맥스500정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, for the prevention of poor bone and teeth development, rickets, and for calcium deficiency and other calcium supplementation.

### 🕒 칼시맥스500정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 8 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 칼시맥스500정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, or hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, or hypoproteinemia, a patient currently taking cardiotonic glycosides, or a person with a history of kidney stones.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
