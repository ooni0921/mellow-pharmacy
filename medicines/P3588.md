#EN
### 💊 아니스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of fever and pain associated with colds, headaches, toothaches, muscle pain, backaches, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 아니스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years and older and adults should take 2 tablets every 8 hours. Do not exceed 6 tablets within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 아니스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other fever-reducing analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, asthma experienced due to aspirin or other NSAIDs, have taken barbiturate drugs or tricyclic antidepressants, have consumed alcohol, or are under 12 years of age.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.
* Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist.
* As this medication is a sustained-release formulation, swallow the tablets whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving them.

#CN
### 💊 아니스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解发热、感冒引起的疼痛以及头痛、牙痛、肌肉痛、腰痛、月经痛和关节痛。

### 🕒 아니스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 用法与用量
* 12岁及以上的儿童和成人每8小时服用2片。24小时内不要超过6片。

### ⚠️ 아니스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯或以上的人士，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能导致肝损伤。
* 极少数情况下，曾报告出现可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征和中毒性表皮坏死松解症。如果在服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。
* 每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量不要超过最大剂量（4,000毫克）。这可能导致肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品一起服用。
* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液异常患者、严重肝功能障碍患者、严重肾功能障碍患者、严重心功能不全患者、曾因阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药引起哮喘的患者、正在服用巴比妥类药物或三环抗抑郁药的患者、饮酒者以及12岁以下儿童请勿服用本药。
* 在服用本药之前，肝功能障碍、肾脏（肾）功能障碍、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、心功能异常、有过敏史者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 未经医生或药剂师指示，成人止痛服用不超过10天，儿童止痛服用不超过5天，发热服用不超过3天。
* 本药为缓释剂型，请勿压碎、咀嚼或溶解药片，应整片吞服。
