#EN
### 💊 파마메코발라민정(메코발라민) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for peripheral neuropathy.

### 🕒 파마메코발라민정(메코발라민) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 3 tablets (1,500 µg) daily, divided into 3 doses.

### ⚠️ 파마메코발라민정(메코발라민) - Precautions
*   No specific information.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to it, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve or worsen after approximately one month of administration, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
