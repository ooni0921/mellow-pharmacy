# 토코엔지연질캡슐

##EN
### 💊 토코엔지연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamin E in old age, relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness and coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet) during menopause, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 토코엔지연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 12 years or older, take 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 토코엔지연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months, or if you have severe renal failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, a breastfeeding mother, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload, or a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage).

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
