*   This medicine is used for dry eyes (discomfort, stinging, irritation) or eye fatigue caused by wind, smoke, pollution, dust, dry heat, air conditioning, air travel, or prolonged computer use.

### 🕒 오에수트레할로스점안액(트레할로스수화물)(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Instill 1 drop into each eye several times a day, as needed.

    Gently pull down the lower eyelid and instill one drop into the lower conjunctival sac while looking upwards.

### ⚠️ 오에수트레할로스점안액(트레할로스수화물)(1회용) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients hypersensitive to this medicine should not use it.

    Before using this medicine, patients with severe eye pain, those with a history of allergic reactions to eye drops (e.g., eye redness, itching, swelling, rash, hyperemia), or glaucoma patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    If symptoms worsen or persist for more than 3 days, stop use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

    This medicine is for ophthalmic use only.

    Wash hands thoroughly before use.

    For single-use products, discard the first 1-2 drops without instilling them during initial use. This is to remove container fragments from opening.

    As this is a single-use preparation, use it only once after opening, and discard any remaining solution and the container after instillation.

    Be careful not to let the tip of the container touch the eyelid or eyelashes, as this may contaminate or cloud (blur) the solution with eye discharge or mold. Do not use if the solution is turbid (blurred).

    To prevent contamination, avoid sharing as much as possible.

    This medicine can be used while wearing contact lenses.

    本剤を使用する前に、目の痛みがひどい患者、点眼薬によるアレルギー反応（目の充血、かゆみ、腫れ、発疹、充血して赤くなるなど）の経験者、緑内障患者は、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
