### 💊 Exeralmin N Tab. - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical weakness during illness or recovery, growth phases, and old age.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used to relieve symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, stiff shoulders, other joint pains, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 Exeralmin N Tab. - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ Exeralmin N Tab. - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, have heart or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
