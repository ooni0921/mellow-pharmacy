#EN
### 💊 마그오캡슐250mg(산화마그네슘) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the antacid action and improvement of symptoms in gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity, as well as for constipation.

### 🕒 마그오캡슐250mg(산화마그네슘) - Dosage and Administration
*   For gastric/duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity, take 2 to 4 capsules (0.5 to 1 g) of magnesium oxide daily, divided into several doses. For constipation, take 8 capsules (2 g) daily, divided into 1 to 2 doses.
*   However, for the initial dose, take the minimum amount and gradually increase or decrease the dose while observing the shape and condition of the stool.

### ⚠️ 마그오캡슐250mg(산화마그네슘) - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not take this medication if you have renal impairment (kidney disorder) or diarrhea.
*   Before taking this medication, patients with cardiac dysfunction, hypermagnesemia, or elderly individuals aged 65 or older should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 마그오캡슐250mg(산화마그네슘) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于胃、十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多症的制酸作用及症状改善，以及便秘症。

### 🕒 마그오캡슐250mg(산화마그네슘) - 用法用量
*   胃、十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多症时，以氧化镁计，每日服用2~4粒胶囊（0.5~1克），分多次服用；便秘症时，每日服用8粒胶囊（2克），分1~2次服用。
*   但是，初次服用时应从小剂量开始，并根据粪便的形状和状态，逐渐增量或减量。

### ⚠️ 마그오캡슐250mg(산화마그네슘) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   肾功能障碍（肾脏疾病）、腹泻患者请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，心功能障碍患者、高镁血症患者、65岁及以上老年人应咨询医生或药师。
