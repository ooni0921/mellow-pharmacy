#EN
### 💊 비메타비정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for the supplementation of Vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of physical weakness during or after illness, growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, and for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue, as well as for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 비메타비정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비메타비정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, a history of soy oil hypersensitivity or allergic reactions, or soy or peanut hypersensitivity.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug if you are currently undergoing medical treatment, have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or young child, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, a history of kidney stones, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or require cautious administration of lipid emulsions.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This drug may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.
