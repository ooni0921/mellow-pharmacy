*   This medication is used for the temporary relief of discomfort, burning, and itching associated with hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 프라렉신크림(프라목신염산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults apply directly to the affected area up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 프라렉신크림(프라목신염산염) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to it. Before using this medication, children under 12 years of age, pregnant women, or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. This medication is for external anal use only and should not be used ophthalmically.
