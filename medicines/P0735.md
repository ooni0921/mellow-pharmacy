# EN
### 💊 Minulet Tab. - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for contraception.

### 🕒 Minulet Tab. - Dosage and Administration
*   Take one tablet daily for 21 consecutive days in the indicated order, followed by a 7-day tablet-free interval. It is advisable to take the tablet at the same time each day, if possible. Menstruation usually occurs during the 7-day tablet-free interval, typically starting 2 to 3 days after the last active tablet. If you continue taking this medication, start a new pack of tablets after the 7-day tablet-free interval; menstruation may not have ended before starting the new pack. For further details, please refer to the detailed information in the prescribing information.

### ⚠️ Minulet Tab. - Precautions
*   Oral contraceptive users should avoid smoking due to an increased risk of serious cardiovascular events such as thrombosis. Smokers over 35 years of age should not take this medication.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, tumors (e.g., breast cancer, endometrial cancer, pituitary tumor), thrombophlebitis, thromboembolism, cerebrovascular or coronary artery disease, undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding, severe hypertension, severe liver impairment, Dubin-Johnson syndrome, Rotor syndrome, jaundice, cholestasis, hepatitis, sickle cell anemia, severe diabetes mellitus, hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, porphyria, metabolic disorders, persistent pruritus during pregnancy, herpes gestationis, pemphigoid gestationis, otosclerosis, if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, galactorrhea, hyperprolactinemia, migraine with aura such as amaurosis or scintillating scotoma, pancreatitis, headaches accompanied by focal neurological symptoms such as aura, hepatitis C, congenital or acquired hypercoagulability disorders, if you are a smoker over 35 years of age, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker, have high blood pressure, obesity, are a woman over 40 years old, have epilepsy, migraine, a history of arterial disease or varicose veins, systemic lupus erythematosus, a history of breast cancer, depression or a history of depression, heart or kidney failure, atypical hyperplastic mastopathy, a history of breast nodules, heart valve disease, atrial fibrillation, a history of hyperlipidemia, gallbladder disease and cholelithiasis, endometriosis, asthma or chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorders, diabetes mellitus, or migraine without aura.
*   This medication does not protect against HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases.
*   If you have severe gastrointestinal disorders, absorption may be incomplete, so use an additional contraceptive method.
*   This medication is not applicable to premenarcheal women and postmenopausal women. For detailed precautions, please refer to the detailed information in the prescribing information.

*   本药物用于避孕。

### 🕒 Minulet Tab. - 用法与用量
*   每日服用1片，连续服用21天，按包装上指示的顺序服用，然后停药7天。最好每天在相同的时间服用。在7天的停药期间，月经会出现，通常在服用最后一颗药片后的2~3天内开始。如果继续服用本药物，在7天停药后开始服用新包装的药片；在新包装开始之前，月经可能尚未结束。详细信息请参考药品说明书。

*   在服用本药物之前，吸烟者、高血压患者、肥胖者、40岁以上女性、癫痫患者、偏头痛患者、有动脉疾病或静脉曲张史者、系统性红斑狼疮患者、有乳腺癌史者、抑郁症患者或有抑郁症史者、心肾功能不全患者、非典型增生性乳腺病患者、有乳腺结节史者、心脏瓣膜病患者、心房颤动患者、有高脂血症史者、胆囊疾病和胆结石病史者、子宫内膜异位症患者、哮喘或黄褐斑患者、周围血液循环障碍患者、糖尿病患者、无前兆偏头痛患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
