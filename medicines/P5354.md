#EN
### 💊 판시자임정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, gastric distress, promoting digestion, and abdominal distension caused by indigestion.

### 🕒 판시자임정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults aged 15 years or older, take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 판시자임정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, or if you are a child under 7 years of age.
* If you have an allergic constitution, consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately stop taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN
### 💊 판시자임정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗消化不良、食欲不振（食欲减退）、过食、胃胀、促进消化，以及因消化不良引起的腹胀。

### 🕒 판시자임정 - 用法用量
* 15岁以上者，每次2片，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 판시자임정 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，以及7岁以下儿童，请勿服用本药。
* 过敏体质者在服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师。
* 服用约2周后症状无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守用法用量。

#JP

### 🕒 판시자임정 - 用法・用量
* 15歳以上の方は、1回2錠を1日3回、食後に服用してください。
