아세트아미노펜으로 일일 최대 용량(4,000 mg)을 초과하여 복용하지 마십시오. 간손상을 일으킬 수 있습니다. 아세트아미노펜을 포함하는 다른 제품과 함께 복용하지 마십시오.

* 이 약에 과민증 환자 및 경험자, 다른 해열진통제, 감기약 복용 시 천식 경험자, 만 3개월 미만의 영아, 중증 또는 조절되지 않는 고혈압, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN

### 💊 아웃콜코정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills (shivering sensation), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 아웃콜코정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 15 years or older should take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 아웃콜코정 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

    Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or a history of it, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant (newborn baby) or a child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) appear in relation to the pseudoephedrine component; if you have an allergic constitution, or a history of allergic reactions (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, edema (swelling), gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or dysuria (difficulty urinating); if you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have a history of ulcers or bleeding problems; if you are pregnant or likely to be pregnant, or are breastfeeding; if you have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm; if you have a cough lasting or recurring for more than one week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache; or if you are a child under 2 years of age.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

*   每日饮酒三杯以上且经常饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。

    若出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等重度皮肤不良反应，请停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

    给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下进行。

    服用后可能出现困倦，因此驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
