### 💊 Champ Cold Syrup - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for the alleviation of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 Champ Cold Syrup - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, take 3 sachets per dose; for those aged 11 years and older to under 15 years, take 2 sachets per dose; for those aged 7 years and older to under 11 years, take 1½ sachets per dose; for those aged 3 years and older to under 7 years, take 1 sachet per dose; for those aged 2 years and older to under 3 years, take ¾ sachets per dose. Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals and at bedtime if necessary, maintaining an interval of 4 hours between doses.

### ⚠️ Champ Cold Syrup - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker (3 or more alcoholic drinks daily) and taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
*   Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. Discontinue use immediately if a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication.
*   Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are: an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; an individual with allergic diathesis or a history of allergic reactions (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); an individual with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or dysuria; an elderly individual, a debilitated individual, or one with a high fever; an individual with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or those with ulcer or bleeding issues; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a breastfeeding woman; an individual receiving treatment from a doctor or dentist (e.g., those taking other medications such as antidiabetic drugs, gout medications, arthritis medications, anticoagulants, or steroids); or an individual with a cough (cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, or excessive phlegm; cough lasting more than 1 week or recurring; chronic cough; or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).

*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

*   Drowsiness may occur during use, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

*   Caution is advised for patients receiving barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, and alcohol, as their ability to metabolize large amounts of acetaminophen may be reduced, potentially increasing the plasma half-life of acetaminophen.

### 🕒 챔프콜드시럽 - 用法用量
*   15岁以上：每次3包；11岁以上至15岁以下：每次2包；7岁以上至11岁以下：每次1½包；3岁以上至7岁以下：每次1包；2岁以上至3岁以下：每次¾包。每日3次，饭后30分钟及必要时睡前服用，每次服用间隔为4小时。

### ⚠️ 챔프콜드시럽 - 注意事项
*   若每日饮酒三杯以上且正在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起肝损伤。
*   极少数情况下报告有致命性的严重皮肤反应，如急性全身性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。

*   服用本药前，以下情况请咨询医生或药剂师：患有或疑似患有水痘或流感的婴儿及15岁以下儿童；过敏体质者或有过敏反应（发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）史者；肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难等患者；高龄者、体弱者或高热者；持续或反复出现胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期女性；正在接受医生或牙医治疗者（正在服用糖尿病药、痛风药、关节炎药、抗凝血剂、类固醇等其他药物者）；或咳嗽患者（吸烟、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽）。

*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

*   服药期间可能出现嗜睡，因此驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

*   对于正在使用巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药和酒精的患者，由于其代谢大量乙酰氨基酚的能力下降，可能导致乙酰氨基酚的血浆半衰期延长，因此请注意。

*   バルビツール酸系薬物、三環系抗うつ薬およびアルコールを投与された患者は、大量のアセトアミノフェンを代謝する能力が低下し、アセトアミノフェンの血漿半減期を増加させる可能性があるため、注意してください。
