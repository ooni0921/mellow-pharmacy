#EN

### 💊 미놀노즈에스점비액 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of symptoms such as nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy-headedness caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 미놀노즈에스점비액 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 7 years and older, and adults: Spray once into each nostril, twice daily.

Use at intervals of 10 to 12 hours.

Do not exceed 2 administrations within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 미놀노즈에스점비액 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not use this medication in children under 7 years of age, infants under 2 years of age, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to this medication.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medications including nasal sprays, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, or have a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, or glaucoma.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have smoking habits, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that persists or recurs for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Use this medication only as a nasal spray, meaning for application inside the nose. Do not use it in the eyes or ears.

Do not share for communal use to prevent contamination.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, stop use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Drowsiness may occur during use. Be careful when driving a car or operating machinery.

Children should use this under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly. Excessive use may actually cause nasal congestion.

Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.

#CN

*   本药用于缓解由普通感冒、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流涕、打喷嚏、头重等症状。

每隔10至12小时使用一次。

24小时内不要超过2次。

### ⚠️ 미놀노즈에스점비액 - 注意事项
*   无相关信息
*   7岁以下儿童和2岁以下婴儿、对本药有过敏史或过敏经验的患者请勿使用本药。

在使用本药前，如果您是过敏体质、曾对包括鼻腔喷雾剂在内的药物出现过敏症状、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、对丙二醇过敏或有相关经验的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

在使用本药前，如果您患有高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾病、青光眼，请咨询医生或药师。

在使用本药前，如果您有吸烟史、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过量痰的咳嗽、持续或反复超过1周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热和皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽，请咨询医生或药师。

本药仅限作为鼻腔喷雾剂，即用于鼻内喷洒。请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。

为防止污染，请勿与他人共用。

如果使用3天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。

使用期间可能会出现嗜睡。驾驶汽车或操作机械时请注意。

儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。过度使用反而可能引起鼻塞。
