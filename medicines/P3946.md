### 💊 락토필캡슐300밀리그램(락토바실루스아시도필루스균) - 효능 및 효과
* 이 약은 정장, 변비, 묽은 변, 복부팽만감, 장내 이상발효에 사용합니다.

### ⚠️ 락토필캡슐300밀리그램(락토바실루스아시도필루스균) - 주의사항

#EN
### 💊 락토필캡슐300밀리그램(락토바실루스아시도필루스균) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for bowel regulation, constipation, loose stools, abdominal bloating, and abnormal fermentation in the intestines.

### 🕒 락토필캡슐300밀리그램(락토바실루스아시도필루스균) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years or older: Take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 1 to 3 times a day. Children aged 8 years or older and under 12 years: Take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 1 to 2 times a day.

### ⚠️ 락토필캡슐300밀리그램(락토바실루스아시도필루스균) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have a compromised immune system (e.g., Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome (AIDS), leukemia, malignant tumors, organ transplantation, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, after long-term high-dose steroid treatment), if you are a child under 1 year of age with high fever or bloody diarrhea, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Patients with hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
