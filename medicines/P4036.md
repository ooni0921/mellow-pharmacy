### 💊 쎄스콘미니정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for contraception.

### 🕒 쎄스콘미니정 - Dosage and Administration
* Take 1 tablet daily for 21 days, then take a 7-day break before starting a new pack. Tablets should be taken around the same time each day, with water, and in the order indicated on the package. Withdrawal bleeding usually occurs during the 7-day break, typically starting 2-3 days after the last tablet is taken. If you continue taking this medicine, start a new pack after the 7-day break; menstruation may not have ended before starting the new pack. For detailed information on other cases, such as forgetting to take a dose or changing your cycle, please refer to the detailed information in the prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 쎄스콘미니정 - Precautions
* Women taking oral contraceptives should refrain from smoking due to an increased risk of serious cardiovascular events such as thrombosis. Smokers aged 35 years or older should not take this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, or if you have or have experienced venous thrombosis (deep vein thrombosis, pulmonary embolism), arterial thrombosis (myocardial infarction, cerebrovascular accident), or prodromal symptoms of thrombosis (e.g., transient ischemic attack, angina pectoris). Also, do not take if you have a predisposition to venous or arterial thrombosis such as activated protein C resistance, antithrombin-III deficiency, protein C deficiency, protein S deficiency, hyperhomocysteinemia, or antiphospholipid antibodies. Do not take if you have a history of migraine with focal neurological symptoms, severe metabolic disorders such as severe diabetes with vascular lesions, severe hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, and porphyria. Do not take if you have severe venous or arterial thrombosis or multiple risk factors, major surgery with prolonged immobilization, pancreatitis due to severe hypertriglyceridemia, severe liver disease with liver function not yet returned to normal, liver tumors (benign or malignant), pituitary tumors, known or suspected malignant tumors influenced by sex steroids (e.g., in reproductive organs or breasts), undiagnosed vaginal bleeding, are pregnant or suspect pregnancy, are breastfeeding, have experienced persistent itching during pregnancy, herpes gestationis, pemphigoid gestationis, or otosclerosis. Do not take if you are receiving direct-acting antiviral therapy for hepatitis C. Do not take if you have congenital or acquired hypercoagulability, are a smoker aged 35 or older, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker, have high blood pressure, are obese, are a woman aged 40 or older, have liver disease, migraine, a history of arterial disease or varicose veins, systemic lupus erythematosus, a history of breast cancer, depression or a history of depression, heart failure or renal failure, atypical hyperplasia of the mammary gland, a history of breast nodules, heart valve disease, atrial fibrillation, a history of hyperlipidemia, a history of gallbladder disease and gallstones, endometriosis, asthma or chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorders, diabetes, or severe gastrointestinal disorders.
* This medicine does not prevent HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
