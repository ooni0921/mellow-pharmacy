#EN
### 💊 피록하이겔(피록시캄) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for analgesia and anti-inflammation in cases of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder joint, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, humeral epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), muscular pain (e.g., myositis, fasciitis), and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 피록하이겔(피록시캄) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Apply 1g (approximately 3cm) to the affected area 3-4 times a day, gently rubbing it in to ensure good absorption.

### ⚠️ 피록하이겔(피록시캄) - Precautions
*   Do not use this medicine in patients with or with a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, patients with or with a history of aspirin asthma, patients who have experienced urticaria, rhinitis, and angioedema due to aspirin or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), children under 14 years of age, pregnant or breastfeeding women, on skin with lesions (hemorrhagic skin diseases, eczema, infected lesions, burns, wounds), or on eyes and mucous membranes.

*   Before using this medicine, patients with bronchial asthma, patients with or with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, children, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   Do not use occlusive dressings.
