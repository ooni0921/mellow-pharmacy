#EN
### 💊 Kwangdong QDac - Efficacy and Effects

*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness when physical strength is weakened, and in old age, and for the relief of symptoms such as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 Kwangdong QDac - Dosage and Administration

*   Adults take one bottle once daily.

### ⚠️ Kwangdong QDac - Precautions

*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to it.
*   Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, and infants should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 💊 光东QD液 - 功效与作用

*   本药用于补充维生素E、B1、B2、B6，适用于身体疲劳、妊娠及哺乳期、病中病后体力下降时和老年期，并用于缓解口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

### 🕒 光东QD液 - 用法用量

### ⚠️ 光东QD液 - 注意事项

*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   本药可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检查结果。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
