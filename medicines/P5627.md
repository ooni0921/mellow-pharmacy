* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

* 이 약 또는 대두유·콩·땅콩에 과민증 환자, 고칼슘혈증(혈액중에 칼슘이 과잉으로 존재하는 상태), 유육종증(원인을 알 수 없는 전신 염증질환), 신장(콩팥)질환 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 신장(콩팥)결석 환자, 심한 심부전 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

1개월 정도 복용하여도 증상의 개선이 없을 경우 복용을 즉각 중지하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 벤포바이타나이스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical weakness during illness or convalescence (during or after recovery from illness), growth period, and geriatric period. It is also used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, alleviation of neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain) (such as back pain, stiff shoulders), angular cheilitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 벤포바이타나이스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 12 years of age and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 벤포바이타나이스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or soybean oil, soybeans, peanuts, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or have severe heart failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, have or have a history of kidney stones, have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, have a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), have folic acid deficiency, or are expected to have special risks due to fat overload.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

It may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
