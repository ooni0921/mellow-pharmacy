#EN
### 💊 캐롤쿨링더블파워카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for analgesia and anti-inflammation of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, humeral epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), muscle pain, and post-traumatic swelling/pain.

### 🕒 캐롤쿨링더블파워카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Remove the release liner from the patch surface and apply to the affected area (diseased part) twice a day.

### ⚠️ 캐롤쿨링더블파워카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not use this drug in patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, patients with aspirin asthma or a history thereof, patients with hypersensitivity to tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, or oxybenzone, pregnant women in the last trimester (6 months or more into pregnancy), and children under 15 years of age.
*   Do not use this drug on the eyes, mucous membranes, damaged skin and mucous membranes, eczema, rash areas, tinea pedis (athlete's foot), or tinea corporis (ringworm).
*   Before using this drug, patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Photosensitivity may occur, so avoid ultraviolet light during use of this drug and for up to 2 weeks after use.
*   Do not use occlusive dressing methods.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
