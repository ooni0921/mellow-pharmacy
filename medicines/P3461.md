# EN

### 💊 멘소래담딥릴리프겔 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory action in sprains, contusions, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 멘소래래담딥릴리프겔 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply a thin layer of approximately 1-4 cm to the affected area three times a day, and gently rub it in until well absorbed.
    Unless directed by a doctor, the treatment period should not exceed 5 days.

### ⚠️ 멘소래담딥릴리프겔 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this product, hypersensitivity to aspirin or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) (e.g., asthma attacks, hives, rhinitis), are under 12 years old, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or have gastric or duodenal ulcers.

Do not use on eyes or around the eyes, mucous membranes, eczema, dermatitis caused by poison ivy/oak, etc., wounds and inflamed areas, sensitive skin, hemorrhagic skin conditions, infected skin, or burned areas.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma, kidney disease, an allergic predisposition, severe wetness or erosion/chafing, circulatory disorders or diabetes, or if you have experienced hypersensitivity to propylene glycol.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medication is for external use only; do not take internally.

Be careful not to get it in your eyes; if it gets into your eyes, rinse immediately with water.

Do not use occlusive dressing (sealed bandage) methods.

When used in children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

# CN

*   本药用于扭伤、挫伤、肌肉痛的镇痛及消炎。

### 🕒 멘소래담딥릴리프겔 - 用法与用量
*   每日3次，每次取约1-4厘米，薄薄地涂抹于患处，并轻轻揉搓以促进吸收。
    若无医生指示，治疗周期不得超过5天。

### ⚠️ 멘소래담딥릴리프겔 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏者，对阿司匹林或非甾体抗炎药（如哮喘发作、荨麻疹、鼻炎）过敏者，12岁以下儿童，孕妇及哺乳期妇女，以及胃十二指肠溃疡患者请勿使用本药。

请勿在眼睛或眼睛周围、粘膜、湿疹、因漆树等引起的皮炎、伤口及炎症部位、敏感肌肤、出血性皮肤病、感染的皮肤或烧伤部位使用。

使用本药前，若患有支气管哮喘、肾脏疾病、过敏体质、湿疹或糜烂（破溃）严重者、循环系统障碍或糖尿病患者，或对丙二醇过敏或有相关经验者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

本药仅供外用，请勿内服。

注意不要进入眼睛，万一进入眼睛，请立即用水冲洗。

请勿使用密封包扎法。

若给儿童使用，请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
