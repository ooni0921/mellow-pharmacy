#EN
### 💊 글로덱시정300mg(덱시부프로펜) - Indications & Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful edema or inflammation following trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 글로덱시정300mg(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet (300 mg) at a time, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 tablets (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 글로덱시정300mg(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist when a regular drinker who consumes three or more alcoholic beverages daily takes this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine increases the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this drug, have or have a history of gastrointestinal ulcers, bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular, or other sites, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, have experienced asthma, hives, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), are a patient undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are pregnant for 6 months or more, are breastfeeding, have inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous NSAID treatment.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, hepatic impairment or a history thereof, renal impairment or a history thereof, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, a history of hypersensitivity, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, are a child aged 6 or older, have stomach cancer, are an alcoholic, are pregnant in the first or second trimester, or are a woman who may be pregnant, have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss, or have blood coagulation disorders or are receiving anticoagulants.

Considering the importance of drug administration, either discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue drug administration.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

For long-term use in acute diseases, it should generally not exceed 5 days.
