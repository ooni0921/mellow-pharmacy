#EN
### 💊 스로쿨트로키(레몬향)(플루르비프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the short-term symptomatic relief of sore throat.

### 🕒 스로쿨트로키(레몬향)(플루르비프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults, children aged 12 years and over, and adolescents should take one lozenge and allow it to dissolve slowly in the mouth.

Move the lozenge around in the mouth while dissolving to avoid local irritation.

If necessary, take a maximum of 5 lozenges per day, with an interval of 3 to 6 hours between doses, and use for a maximum of 3 days.

### ⚠️ 스로쿨트로키(레몬향)(플루르비프로펜) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, aspirin, or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); peptic ulcer, bleeding; asthma (including asthma attacks), urticaria, bronchospasm, rhinitis caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, or a history thereof; severe cardiac insufficiency; gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation caused by NSAIDs or anti-rheumatic drugs; severe colitis; severe blood disorders; unexplained hematopoietic disorders; severe hepatic impairment; severe renal impairment; pregnant women in the last trimester (final 3 months); or patients with hereditary fructose intolerance, glucose-galactose malabsorption, or sucrose-isomaltase deficiency.

Before taking this medication, patients with bronchial asthma or a history thereof, cardiac insufficiency, renal impairment, hepatic impairment, hypertension, blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendency, elderly patients, peripheral arterial occlusive disease, cerebrovascular disease, hyperlipidemia, diabetes mellitus, smokers, hay fever, nasal polyps, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, pregnant women in the first and second trimesters, fluid retention, or heart failure should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Dizziness and visual disturbances may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

#CN
### 💊 스로쿨트로키(레몬향)(플루르비프로펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于短期缓解咽喉痛症状。

### 🕒 스로쿨트로키(레몬향)(플루르비프로펜) - 用法用量
* 成人、12岁及以上儿童和青少年，将本药1粒含在口中使其缓慢溶解。

为避免局部刺激，请在口中含服时转动。

必要时，每隔3~6小时服用1粒，每日最多服用5粒，最长使用3天。

### ⚠️ 스로쿨트로키(레몬향)(플루르비프로펜) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药、阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药（NSAIDs）过敏的患者，患有消化性溃疡、出血、阿司匹林或其他NSAIDs引起的哮喘（包括哮喘发作）、荨麻疹、支气管痉挛、鼻炎或有相关病史者，患有严重心功能不全、NSAIDs或抗风湿药引起的胃肠道出血或穿孔、严重结肠炎、严重血液异常者，有不明原因的造血功能障碍、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍的患者，妊娠晚期3个月的孕妇，遗传性果糖不耐受、葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良、蔗糖-异麦芽糖酶缺乏症患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，支气管哮喘患者或有相关病史者，心功能不全患者以及肾脏、肝功能障碍、高血压、血液异常或有相关病史者，有出血倾向的患者，老年人，周围动脉闭塞症、脑血管疾病、高血脂症、糖尿病、吸烟者，花粉症、鼻息肉、慢性阻塞性肺疾病患者，妊娠早期和中期的孕妇，体液潴留、心力衰竭患者，请咨询医生或药师。

服用本药后可能会出现头晕和视力障碍，因此驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

必要に応じて、3～6時間間隔で1日最大5個を服用し、最大3日間使用してください。
