# EN
### 💊 Zerova - Indications and Usage
*   This drug is used for decreased saliva secretion due to drug treatment, radiation therapy near the mouth or throat, salivary gland infection, mouth or throat infection, dental or oral surgery, fever, or emotional factors.

### 🕒 Zerova - Dosage and Administration
*   Spray directly into the mouth or throat 1-2 times as needed to maintain oral moisture.

### ⚠️ Zerova - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this drug if you are hypersensitive to it. Do not spray into eyes.
