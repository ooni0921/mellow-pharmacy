#EN
### 💊 코코스탑나잘스프레이 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heaviness in the head caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 코코스탑나잘스프레이 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 7 years of age and older, spray once into each nostril, 3 times a day.

When using, maintain an interval of at least 3 hours between each dose.

### ⚠️ 코코스탑나잘스프레이 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you are under 7 years of age, an infant under 2 years of age, or a patient who has experienced hypersensitivity to this medicine.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicines including rhinitis nasal sprays, are pregnant, possibly pregnant, have experienced or have propylene glycol hypersensitivity, hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, or glaucoma.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have smoking habits, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Use this medicine only as a rhinitis nasal spray, i.e., for spraying inside the nose. Do not use in eyes or ears.

To prevent contamination, do not share this medicine.

Drowsiness may occur during use. Be careful when driving a car or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, stop use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.

Children should use this under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Excessive use may actually cause nasal congestion.

#CN

* 本药用于缓解由普通感冒、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流鼻涕、打喷嚏、头重等症状。

### 🕒 코코스탑나잘스프레이 - 用法与用量
* 7岁以上儿童及成人，每次向两侧鼻腔内各喷一次，每日3次。

每次使用时，请间隔至少3小时。

### ⚠️ 코코스탑나잘스프레이 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 7岁以下儿童、2岁以下婴儿，以及对本药有过敏史或过敏反应的患者请勿使用本药。

使用本药前，如您有过敏体质、曾因包括鼻炎喷雾剂在内的药物引起过敏症状、孕妇、可能怀孕的女性、曾对丙二醇过敏或有过敏反应的患者、高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾病、青光眼患者，请咨询医生或药师。

使用本药前，如果您有吸烟习惯、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续或反复发作超过1周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发烧、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽，请咨询医生或药师。

本药仅限用作鼻炎喷雾剂，即用于鼻腔内喷雾。请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。

为防止污染，请勿共用。

使用期间可能会出现嗜睡。驾驶汽车或操作机械时请注意。

如果使用3天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。

请勿连续使用超过7天。

儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。过度使用反而可能引起鼻塞。
