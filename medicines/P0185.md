# EN
### 💊 하노백연질캡슐1000아이유(비타민이) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to alleviate symptoms such as vitamin E deficiency, peripheral circulatory disorders, and in menopause: shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness and coldness of extremities (hands and feet), intermittent claudication (occasional limping), and frostbite.

### 🕒 하노백연질캡슐1000아이유(비타민이) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 50 IU to 1,000 IU of vitamin E per day.

### ⚠️ 하노백연질캡슐1000아이유(비타민이) - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this drug, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are breastfeeding. Avoid high-dose use of this medicine during pregnancy. Before taking this medicine, pregnant women, patients with a thrombotic predisposition, individuals with a history of bronchial asthma, rash, hives, etc., patients with hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or other fat metabolism disorders or patients with expected special risks due to fat overload, children taking iron supplements, and patients hypersensitive to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 하노백연질캡슐1000아이유(비타민이) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解维生素E缺乏症、末梢循环功能障碍以及更年期时出现的肩颈僵硬、手足发麻（手脚麻木）、手足冰冷（手脚冰凉）、间歇性跛行（偶尔跛行）、冻伤等症状。

### 🕒 하노백연질캡슐1000아이유(비타민이) - 用法用量
* 成人每日服用维生素E 50 IU～1,000 IU。

### ⚠️ 하노백연질캡슐1000아이유(비타민이) - 注意事项
* 对本药、大豆油、大豆或花生过敏的患者，以及哺乳期妇女请勿服用本药。妊娠期间请避免高剂量使用本药。服用本药前，孕妇、有血栓形成倾向的患者、有支气管哮喘、皮疹、荨麻疹等病史的患者、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症和胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常或因脂肪过载预计有特殊风险的患者、正在服用铁剂的儿童、对黄色5号过敏的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
