# EN
### 💊 씨엠지알마게이트정500mg(알마게이트) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for its antacid action and improvement of symptoms such as gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, hyperacidity, heartburn, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and acid belching.

### 🕒 씨엠지알마게이트정500mg(알마게이트) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age and older: Chew and take 2 tablets (1 g) per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes to 1 hour after meals.
*   If necessary, one more dose can be taken before bedtime.

### ⚠️ 씨엠지알마게이트정500mg(알마게이트) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, Alzheimer's disease, hemorrhoids, fluid retention (swelling), preeclampsia, diarrhea, undiagnosed gastrointestinal bleeding, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or genetic problems such as glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment (kidney disorder), are physically debilitated, are on a low-phosphorus diet, have malabsorption disorders, are elderly, have symptoms of gastrointestinal bleeding such as hematemesis or black stools, are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are nursing.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
