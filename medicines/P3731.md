### 🕒 보니타정 - 용법 및 용량
* 1일 1정씩 21일간 복용하고, 7일간 휴악한 후 새로운 포장을 시작합니다.정제는 매일 대략 같은 시간에, 물과 함께, 포장에 표시된 순서대로 복용해야 합니다.7일의 휴약기간 동안 소퇴성 출혈이 나타나며 대개 정제의 마지막 복용 후 2~3일 내에 시작됩니다.이 약을 계속 복용할 경우 7일간 휴약 후 새 포장의 정제로 복용을 시작하며 새로운 포장을 시작하기 전에 월경이 끝나지 않을 수도 있습니다.기타 복용을 잊은 경우, 주기를 변경하는 경우 등 자세한 사항은 허가사항 상세정보를 참고하십시오.

### 💊 Bonita Tablets - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for contraception.

### 🕒 Bonita Tablets - Dosage and Administration
*   Take one tablet daily for 21 days, followed by a 7-day tablet-free interval, after which a new pack should be started. Tablets should be taken at approximately the same time each day, with some water, in the order indicated on the package. Withdrawal bleeding usually starts 2 to 3 days after the last tablet in the 7-day tablet-free interval. If you continue to take this medication, start a new pack after the 7-day tablet-free interval, even if menstruation has not finished before starting the new pack. For detailed information regarding forgotten doses, cycle changes, and other situations, please refer to the detailed information in the product's approved labeling.

### ⚠️ Bonita Tablets - Precautions
*   Women taking oral contraceptives should refrain from smoking, as it may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular events such as thrombosis. Smokers aged 35 or older should not take this medication.
*   This medication should not be taken by patients with hypersensitivity to this drug; those with or a history of venous thromboembolism (deep vein thrombosis, pulmonary embolism) or arterial thrombosis (myocardial infarction, cerebrovascular accident) or prodromal symptoms of thrombosis (e.g., transient ischemic attack, angina pectoris); those with a predisposition to venous or arterial thrombosis, such as activated protein C resistance, antithrombin-III deficiency, protein C deficiency, protein S deficiency, hyperhomocysteinemia, antiphospholipid antibodies; those with a history of migraine with focal neurological symptoms; patients with severe metabolic disorders such as severe diabetes with vascular complications, severe hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, and porphyria; those with severe or multiple risk factors for venous or arterial thrombosis; those undergoing major surgery requiring prolonged immobilization; those with pancreatitis due to severe hypertriglyceridemia; those with severe liver disease where liver function has not returned to normal; patients with liver tumors (benign or malignant); those with pituitary tumors; patients with known or suspected malignant tumors influenced by sex steroids (e.g., in the reproductive organs or breasts); patients with undiagnosed vaginal bleeding; pregnant women or those suspected of being pregnant; breastfeeding mothers; those with a history of persistent pruritus during pregnancy, herpes gestationis, pemphigus, or otosclerosis; patients undergoing treatment with direct-acting antiviral agents for hepatitis C; those with congenital or acquired hypercoagulability; smokers aged 35 or older; patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker, have high blood pressure, are obese, are a woman aged 40 or older, have liver disease, migraine, a history of arterial disease or varicose veins, systemic lupus erythematosus, a history of breast cancer, depression or a history of depression, heart or kidney failure, atypical hyperplastic mastopathy, a history of breast nodules, heart valve disease, atrial fibrillation, a history of hyperlipidemia, gallbladder disease or gallstones, endometriosis, asthma, chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorders, diabetes, or severe gastrointestinal disorders.
*   This medication does not prevent HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases.
*   Please follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
