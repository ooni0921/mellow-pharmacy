#EN
### 💊 쎄레손크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin diseases (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, chronic lichen simplex, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis) and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 쎄레손크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area (diseased part) 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 쎄레손크림 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, if you have bacterial (e.g., tuberculosis, syphilis), fungal (e.g., candidiasis, tinea), spirochetal, yeast, viral (e.g., herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia), or animal (e.g., scabies, pubic lice) skin infections, eczematous external otitis with a perforated eardrum, ulcers (excluding Behçet's disease), second-degree deep burns or frostbite or worse, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, rosacea, or if you are hypersensitive to aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or to bacitracin.

Before using this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, infants and children, and patients hypersensitive to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.

Do not use for purposes other than treatment after makeup or shaving.

Do not use occlusive dressing therapy without a doctor's supervision.

Do not use for a long period.
