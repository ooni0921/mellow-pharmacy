#EN
### 💊 코벤시럽 - Indications & Effects
*   This medicine is used for the temporary relief of sneezing, runny nose, nasal congestion, and watery eyes caused by the common cold or allergic and vasomotor rhinitis, and nasal congestion due to sinusitis.

### 🕒 코벤시럽 - Dosage & Administration
*   Children 12 years and older, and adults: Take 10 mL every 4 hours, not to exceed 60 mL in 24 hours.
*   Children 6 years to under 12 years: Take 5 mL every 4 hours, not to exceed 30 mL in 24 hours.
*   Children 2 years to under 6 years: Consult a doctor.

### ⚠️ 코벤시럽 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, sympathomimetic amines, or antihistamines; individuals with specific constitution; breastfeeding women; patients with severe hypertension, severe coronary artery disease, narrow-angle glaucoma, urinary retention, peptic ulcer, asthma, emphysema, chronic pulmonary disease, or shortness of breath/respiratory distress should not take this medicine.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients and children with respiratory difficulty such as chronic bronchitis, heart disease, hypertension, thyroid disorders, diabetes, glaucoma, or difficulty in urination due to prostatic hypertrophy should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery after taking this medicine.

#CN
### 💊 코벤시럽 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于暂时缓解由感冒、过敏性鼻炎或血管运动性鼻炎引起的打喷嚏、流鼻涕、鼻塞、流泪，以及鼻窦炎引起的鼻充血（鼻塞）。

### 🕒 코벤시럽 - 用法及用量
*   12岁以上儿童及成人：每4小时服用10毫升，24小时内不得超过60毫升。
*   6岁以上至12岁以下儿童：每4小时服用5毫升，24小时内不得超过30毫升。
*   2岁以上至6岁以下儿童：请遵医嘱。

### ⚠️ 코벤시럽 - 注意事项
*   信息缺失
*   对本药、胺类交感神经兴奋剂或抗组胺药过敏的患者、特异体质者、哺乳期妇女、重度高血压、重度冠状动脉疾病、狭窄性青光眼、尿潴留、消化性溃疡、哮喘、肺气肿、慢性肺病、气短或呼吸困难的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，患有慢性支气管炎等呼吸困难、心脏病、高血压、甲状腺功能障碍、糖尿病、青光眼、因前列腺肥大引起的排尿困难的患者及儿童，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   服用后驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

*   6歳以上12歳未満の小児：4時間ごとに5 mL服用し、24時間で30 mLを超えないでください。
*   2歳以上6歳未満の小児：医師の指示に従ってください。
