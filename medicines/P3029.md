#EN
### 💊 디소론캡슐(디오스민) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for the improvement of symptoms associated with venous insufficiency (such as leg heaviness, pain), as an adjunctive treatment for disorders caused by capillary fragility, and for the treatment of signs related to hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 디소론캡슐(디오스민) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule (300 mg) twice daily (with lunch and dinner).

In cases of recurrence or worsening of hemorrhoids, up to 4 to 6 capsules (1,200 to 1,800 mg) can be taken daily.

Dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 디소론캡슐(디오스민) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to it or if you are a breastfeeding mother.

Pregnant women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.

Do not administer for a prolonged period.

If symptoms do not improve quickly, a proctological examination and re-evaluation of treatment are necessary.
