* 정보 없음
* 이 약 또는 대두유, 콩, 땅콩에 과민증 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 심한 증상의 신부전 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

# EN

### 💊 마그토닉연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamin E in old age, alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands/feet, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그토닉연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 12 years of age and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그토닉연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or soybean oil, soy, or peanuts; if you are an infant under 12 months of age; or if you have severe renal failure.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant; if you have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional (localized) colitis; heart or circulatory system dysfunction; kidney dysfunction; hypoproteinemia; lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, or patients who need careful administration of lipid emulsions; patients with a thrombotic predisposition; or patients with or who have experienced hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
