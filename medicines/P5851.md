#EN

### 💊 이브400원큐에스연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for fever and pain due to the common cold, low back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), postoperative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 이브400원큐에스연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 200-600 mg per dose, 3-4 times daily. The maximum daily dose is 3,200 mg.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight per dose, divided into 3-4 doses daily.

For mild to moderate pain and common cold, adults take 200-400 mg per dose, 3-4 times daily. The dosage can be adjusted as appropriate depending on age and symptoms.

For migraine, adults take 200-400 mg per dose. Do not exceed 400 mg within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 이브400원큐에스연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily and intend to take this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
Taking this medication may increase the risk of fatal serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes mellitus, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulcer, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, liver impairment, kidney impairment, heart failure, hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors), pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are in the last trimester of pregnancy, or are receiving high-dose methotrexate as part of anticancer therapy.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis, liver impairment or a history thereof, kidney impairment or a history thereof, heart failure or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) or mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly or a child, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are taking low-dose aspirin for the prevention of myocardial infarction or stroke.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery after taking this medication.

Do not take this medication for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) or for fever for more than 3 days without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.
