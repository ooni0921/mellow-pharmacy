#EN
### 💊 속크린에스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, loss of appetite, dyspepsia, overeating, promotion of digestion, and stomach bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 속크린에스정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 15 years and older, take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 속크린에스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are undergoing dialysis, are a child under 7 years old, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have kidney impairment, an allergic constitution, or are on a sodium-restricted diet.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
