#EN
### 💊 베로타정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach, promoting digestion, and stomach bloating caused by indigestion.

### 🕒 베로타정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 tablets once, three times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 베로타정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not administer to patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, patients with hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 (Tartrazine), and infants/children under 7 years of age. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
