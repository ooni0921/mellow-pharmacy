#EN

### 💊 펜타셀100마이크로그램퍼오랄정(아셀렌산나트륨오수화물) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to supplement selenium in patients with selenium deficiency that cannot be compensated for by nutritional intake.

### 🕒 펜타셀100마이크로그램퍼오랄정(아셀렌산나트륨오수화물) - Dosage and Administration
* Take 1 tablet (100 μg as selenium) once daily. The dose can be increased up to a maximum of 2 tablets (200 μg as selenium) per day. This medication should be swallowed whole with water without chewing.

### ⚠️ 펜타셀100마이크로그램퍼오랄정(아셀렌산나트륨오수화물) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, an infant under 12 months of age, a patient with selenium toxicity, hereditary fructose intolerance, glucose-galactose malabsorption, or sucrase-isomaltase deficiency. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, or have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, or are a child under 12 years of age. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

### ⚠️ 펜타셀100마이크로그램퍼오랄정(아셀렌산나트륨오수화물) - 注意事项
