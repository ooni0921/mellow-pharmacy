#EN
### 💊 파로네프캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal therapy.

### 🕒 파로네프캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 파로네프캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are a child under 15 years of age or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, have experienced hypersensitivity to cosmetics or eggs (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), or have an allergic constitution. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
