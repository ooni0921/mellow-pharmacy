#EN
### 💊 클리어딘연질캡슐(로라타딘) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the temporary relief of symptoms of allergic rhinitis (sneezing, runny nose, itching, burning eyes) and for the relief of symptoms of chronic idiopathic urticaria.

### 🕒 클리어딘연질캡슐(로라타딘) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age or older should take 1 capsule (10 mg) once daily. Children aged 6 to under 12 years who weigh 30 kg or more should take 1 capsule (10 mg) once daily.

### ⚠️ 클리어딘연질캡슐(로라타딘) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are under 6 years of age, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Patients with liver impairment or renal failure should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

#CN

*   本药用于暂时缓解过敏性鼻炎症状（打喷嚏、流鼻涕、瘙痒、眼睛灼热感）以及缓解慢性特发性荨麻疹症状。

### 🕒 클리어딘연질캡슐(로라타딘) - 用法与用量
*   成人及12岁以上儿童每日一次，每次服用1粒胶囊（10毫克）。6至12岁以下体重30公斤以上的儿童每日一次，每次服用1粒胶囊（10毫克）。

### ⚠️ 클리어딘연질캡슐(로라타딘) - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药过敏者、6岁以下儿童、孕妇及哺乳期妇女、以及患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本药。肝功能障碍或肾功能不全患者在服用本药前请咨询医生或药师。
