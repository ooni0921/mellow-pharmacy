### 💊 티엔코연질캡슐 - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 티엔코연질캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
*   For adults and children 15 years and older: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 티엔코연질캡슐 - Precautions & Warnings
*   Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson Syndrome, and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of a hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have discontinued them within the past 2 weeks, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, an infant or young child between 3 months and under 2 years old, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant or a nursing mother, an elderly person, a person with a weak constitution, a person with an allergic constitution, a person who has experienced allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), a person with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), dysuria, high fever, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, ulcers, bleeding problems, smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting more than 1 week or recurring, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache, or a person with a hypersensitivity to or experience with Yellow No. 5.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
