# EN
### 💊 에코파워600정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamins B1, B2, B6, and D during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during illness or convalescence, growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, poor bone and teeth development, prevention of rickets, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 에코파워600정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 12 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 에코파워600정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or a kidney stone patient.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding mother, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, or a patient with folic acid deficiency.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and could affect clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 에코파워600정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于补充维生素B1、B2、B6、D，适用于身体疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、发育期、老年期。本药还可用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）等症状，预防脚气、眼睛疲劳、骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病，以及补充锌。

### 🕒 에코파워600정 - 用法用量
* 12岁及以上成人，每日1次，每次1片，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 에코파워600정 - 注意事项
* 高钙血症、结节病、肾脏疾病、对本药过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、肾结石患者请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、心血管系统功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、有肾结石病史者、叶酸缺乏症患者请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

本药可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检测结果。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
