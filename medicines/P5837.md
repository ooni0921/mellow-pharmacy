#EN
### 💊 렛잇비프로원정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after illness when physical strength is decreased, during growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, as well as for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 렛잇비프로원정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 렛잇비프로원정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to soybean oil, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, kidney stones or a history of them, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or require careful administration of lipid emulsions.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Patients with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage) or folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
