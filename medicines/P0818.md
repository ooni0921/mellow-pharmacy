# EN
### 💊 케토크린카타플라스마(케토프로펜)(수출용) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis, tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (such as tennis elbow), muscle pain, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 케토크린카타플라스마(케토프로펜)(수출용) - Dosage and Administration
* Remove the release liner from the patch and apply it to the affected area twice daily.

### ⚠️ 케토크린카타플라스마(케토프로펜)(수출용) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity or photosensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma induced by aspirin or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), a history of hypersensitivity to drugs with cross-reactivity with this medicine (tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, oxybenzone), are pregnant for 6 months or more, or are under 15 years of age.
* Patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant women, and nursing mothers should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medicine.
* While using this medicine and for up to 2 weeks after use, regardless of the weather, avoid outdoor activities. When going out for daily errands, cover the application area of this medicine with clothing, sunglasses, hats, sunscreen, etc., to prevent exposure to ultraviolet light.
* Do not use on eyes, mucous membranes, damaged skin, eczema, rash areas, athlete's foot, or tinea.
* Do not use occlusive dressing.

# CN

### ⚠️ 케토크린카타플라스마(케토프로펜)(수출용) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药有过敏症及光过敏症经验者、使用阿司匹林或非甾体抗炎镇痛药时有哮喘经验者、对本药及交叉过敏反应药物（硫普罗酸、非诺贝特、苯扎贝特、环丙贝特、奥西苯宗）有过敏症经验者、妊娠期超过6个月的孕妇、以及15岁以下的儿童请勿使用本药。
