#EN
### 💊 락토그린정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for diarrhea, diarrhea accompanied by abdominal pain, indigestion, loose stools, and vomiting.

### 🕒 락토그린정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents aged 15 years and older should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.
* Ensure a minimum dosing interval of 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 락토그린정 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medication if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a neonate, a child under 7 years of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4, a history of drug-induced allergies (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), diarrhea accompanied by fever, are elderly, have bloody stools or persistent mucous stools, or have conditions such as gastric or duodenal ulcers.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.
* Do not take for more than 3 consecutive days.
* If symptoms do not improve after several doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 락토그린정 - 用法用量
* 15岁以上及成人，一次2片，一日3次，饭后服用。
* 服用间隔应为4小时以上。

* 无信息。
* 孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、新生儿、7岁以下儿童、患有半乳糖不耐受症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题的患者请勿服用本药。

* 服用期间请勿饮酒。
* 请勿连续服用3天以上。
* 若多次服用后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
