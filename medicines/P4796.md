#EN
### 💊 엘카딘정(L-카르니틴) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, or myocardial metabolic disorders due to ischemic heart disease such as angina pectoris and acute myocardial infarction.

### 🕒 엘카딘정(L-카르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 2~3 g daily, divided into 2~3 doses.

The dosage may be adjusted appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘카딘정(L-카르니틴) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug should not take it.

Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug.

#JP

### ⚠️ 엘카딘정(L-카르니틴) - 注意事項
