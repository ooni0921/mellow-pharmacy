#EN
### 💊 콘조탑골드600정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain, including lumbago, stiff shoulders, etc.), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 콘조탑골드600정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 콘조탑골드600정 - Precautions
*   No specific information provided.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 3 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, or kidney stones.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a person with a history of kidney stones, or a patient with folic acid deficiency.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and can affect clinical test values.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after taking for more than 1 month, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
