# EN
### 💊 아이포레점안액(트레할로스수화물)(1회용) - Efficacy and Effect
*   This medicine is used for dry eyes (discomfort, stinging pain, irritation) or eye fatigue caused by wind, smoke, pollution, dust, dry heat, air conditioning, air travel, or prolonged computer use.

### 🕒 아이포레점안액(트레할로스수화물)(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Instill 1 drop into each eye several times a day as needed. Discard any remaining solution and the container after use.

### ⚠️ 아이포레점안액(트레할로스수화물)(1회용) - Precautions
*   Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to it.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medicine if you have severe eye pain, a history of allergic reactions to eye drops (e.g., eye congestion, itching, swelling, rash, redness), or glaucoma.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medicine is for ophthalmic use only.
*   Wash hands thoroughly before use.
*   Do not share to prevent contamination.
*   Discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist if symptoms worsen or persist for more than 3 days.

*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   本药仅供滴眼使用。
*   使用前请彻底洗手。
*   为防止污染，请勿与他人共用。
