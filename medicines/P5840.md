#EN
### 💊 콜즈록나잘스프레이 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and head pressure caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 콜즈록나잘스프레이 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 7 years of age and older: Spray once into each nostril, twice a day.
Allow 10-12 hours between doses, and do not exceed 2 doses within a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 콜즈록나잘스프레이 - Warnings
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or a history of it, if you are a child under 7 years of age, or an infant under 2 years of age.
Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicines including nasal sprays, have hypersensitivity to propylene glycol or a history of it, are pregnant or may be pregnant, or have high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, or glaucoma.
Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts for more than a week or recurs, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, discontinue this medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
This medicine is for nasal spray use only (for spraying into the nose); do not use it in the eyes or ears.
Do not share to prevent contamination.
Drowsiness may occur during use. Exercise caution when driving a car or operating machinery.
Do not use excessively. It may cause rebound nasal congestion.
Children should use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.
Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

### 💊 콜즈록나잘스프레이 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解普通感冒、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流涕、打喷嚏、头重感等症状。

### 🕒 콜즈록나잘스프레이 - 用法用量
* 7岁及以上儿童和成人：每次每个鼻孔喷1下，每日2次。
每次用药间隔10-12小时，24小时内用药次数不得超过2次。

### ⚠️ 콜즈록나잘스프레이 - 注意事项
* 对本药有过敏史或过敏反应者，7岁以下儿童以及2岁以下婴儿请勿使用本药。
在使用本药前，如您有过敏体质、曾因包括鼻喷剂在内的药物引起过敏症状、对丙二醇过敏或有过敏史、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、患有高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾病、青光眼者，请咨询医生或药师。
在使用本药前，如您有吸烟史、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续一周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽，或伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽，请咨询医生或药师。
若使用3天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用本药并咨询医生或药师。
本药仅供鼻腔喷雾使用（即喷入鼻腔的药物），请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。
为防止污染，请勿与他人共用。
使用本药期间可能会出现困倦。请注意驾驶汽车或操作机械。
请勿过量使用。过量使用可能引起反跳性鼻塞。
儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
请勿连续使用超过7天。
请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
