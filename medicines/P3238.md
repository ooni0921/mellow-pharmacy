#EN
### 💊 쎌렉스킨지크림 - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medication is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin conditions (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis) and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 쎌렉스킨지크림 - Dosage & Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times daily.

### ⚠️ 쎌렉스킨지크림 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or bacitracin, or have a history of such hypersensitivity. Also, do not use if you have skin infections (tuberculosis, syphilis, candidiasis, tinea, herpes zoster, herpes simplex, varicella, vaccinia, scabies, pubic lice), eczematous external otitis with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (except Behçet's disease), second-degree deep burns or higher and frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, or rosacea.
*   Before using this medication, pregnant or potentially pregnant women, nursing mothers, infants, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
*   Do not use for purposes other than treatment after makeup or shaving.
*   Do not use occlusive dressing therapy without a doctor's supervision.

*   无信息。
