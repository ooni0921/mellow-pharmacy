#EN
### 💊 크로쎈연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Indications & Effects
*   This drug is used for the relief of mild to moderate pain such as muscle pain, neuralgia, post-traumatic and post-operative pain, headache, toothache, and earache.

### 🕒 크로쎈연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults take 1 to 2 capsules (125-250 mg) once, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 크로쎈연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this drug if you have peptic ulcers, gastric/duodenal disturbances caused by drugs, hypersensitivity to this drug, a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors), are being treated for pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are pregnant or may be pregnant, have severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe heart failure, or inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis.
*   Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of peptic ulcers, are a child or an elderly person, have hypertension, fluid retention, or heart failure, have bronchial asthma, have or have a history of hepatic impairment, or have or have a history of renal impairment.
