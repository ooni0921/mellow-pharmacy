# EN

### 💊 엘라스에이액(트록세루틴) - Indications and Efficacy
* This medication is used for symptoms associated with venous and lymphatic circulatory disorders, such as heaviness in the lower limbs (legs), pain, restless legs, and various symptoms related to hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 엘라스에이액(트록세루틴) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 ampoule (7 mL) once daily.
It is recommended to take this medication with a glass of water during meals.

### ⚠️ 엘라스에이액(트록세루틴) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are in the first 3 months of pregnancy, breastfeeding, have liver disease, are an alcoholic, have epilepsy, or are a child.
Before taking this medication, patients with hypersensitivity (e.g., rash, redness, itching) to this medication, pregnant women, or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
This medication is for oral use only; do not use it for injection.
