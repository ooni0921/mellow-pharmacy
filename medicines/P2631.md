*   Instill 1 to 3 drops at a time, 3 to 6 times a day.

### ⚠️ Shindotopcool Eye Drops - Precautions
*   No information
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe eye pain or have experienced allergic symptoms due to sulfonamides and eye drops.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
*   Do not use continuously for a long period.
*   This medicine is for ophthalmic use only.
*   Do not use as a solution for soft contact lenses or while wearing soft contact lenses.
*   Be careful not to let the tip of the container touch your eyelids or eyelashes, as the medicinal solution may become contaminated or turbid (cloudy) by eye discharge or fungi; do not use if it is turbid (cloudy).
