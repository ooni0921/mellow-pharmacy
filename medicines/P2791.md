#EN
### 💊 메가트루골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical weakness during illness or convalescence (during or after illness), growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the relief of dry eyes, night blindness, poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 메가트루골드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once, 1 to 2 times a day.

### ⚠️ 메가트루골드정 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day may cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medicine if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy.
*   Patients with hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this medicine, infants under 12 months of age, kidney stones, or severe renal failure should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or young child, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, biliary obstruction, copper deficiency, gout, a history of kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Since Vitamin A contained in this medicine is sufficiently supplied through a normal diet, ensure that the dosage for supplementary therapy does not exceed 5,000 IU per day.

This medicine may turn urine yellow, affect clinical test results, or interfere with glucose detection during urine tests.
