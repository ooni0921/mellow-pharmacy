#EN
### 💊 록소케어카타플라스마(록소프로펜나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of osteoarthritis, muscle pain, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 록소케어카타플라스마(록소프로펜나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply one patch to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 록소케어카타플라스마(록소프로펜나트륨) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, or have aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by NSAIDs, etc.) or a history thereof.

Do not apply to eyes and mucous membranes, damaged skin and mucous membranes, areas with eczema or rash, athlete's foot, tinea, etc.

Before using this medicine, patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant and nursing mothers, premature infants, neonates, infants (suckling babies, newborns), toddlers, children, and elderly patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not use occlusive dressing.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### ⚠️ 록소케어카타플라스마(록소프로펜나트륨) - 注意事项
* 信息缺失
* 对本药有过敏症的患者，或患有阿司匹林哮喘（由非甾体类抗炎药等引起的哮喘发作）或有相关病史者，请勿使用本药。

请勿用于眼睛和粘膜、受损皮肤和粘膜、湿疹或皮疹部位、脚癣、体癣等。

请勿使用密封敷料法。

若使用约1周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药剂师。
