#EN
### 💊 득생활혈산통고 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammation in cases of contusions, stiff shoulders, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 득생활혈산통고 - Dosage and Administration
* Clean and dry the affected area, then apply one patch once daily.
* Secure with bandages, adhesive tape, etc., if necessary.

### ⚠️ 득생활혈산통고 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not use on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, or on wounded areas.
* Before using this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity to medicines or cosmetics, those with allergic constitution, or those with severe wetness (moisture retention) or erosion (soreness) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine is for external use only.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 득생활혈산통고 - 注意事项

* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
* 儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
* 本品仅供外用。
* 若使用5-6天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
