#EN

### 💊 타미노펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 타미노펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 2 capsules (650 mg) once, 3-4 times a day (every 4-6 hours) as needed. Do not exceed a maximum daily dose of 12 capsules (4,000 mg).

### ⚠️ 타미노펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker of three or more alcoholic beverages daily when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. Rare but serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of a hypersensitivity reaction occur after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of 4,000 mg of acetaminophen, as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe renal impairment, severe heart failure, aspirin asthma (induced by NSAIDs) or a history of it, if you have taken barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, or if you have consumed alcohol. Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you have liver impairment, renal impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, heart dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Consult a doctor or pharmacist if pain or fever symptoms persist or worsen, or if new symptoms appear.

#CN

### 💊 타미노펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、扭伤痛、牙痛、关节痛以及风湿性疼痛。

### 🕒 타미노펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 用法用量
* 12岁及以上儿童和成人：需要时，每次服用2粒胶囊（650毫克），每日3-4次（每4-6小时一次）。每日最大剂量不超过12粒胶囊（4,000毫克）。

### ⚠️ 타미노펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒量超过三杯的常饮者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能导致肝损伤。极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征和中毒性表皮坏死松解症。如果服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状，请立即停止服用。对乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量请勿超过4,000毫克，这可能导致肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全患者、阿司匹林哮喘（由非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘发作）患者或有相关病史者、服用巴比妥类药物或三环抗抑郁药的患者以及饮酒者，请勿服用本药。在服用本药之前，肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、心功能异常患者、有过敏史者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。如果疼痛或发热症状持续或恶化，或出现新症状，请咨询医生或药师。
