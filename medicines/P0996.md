* 정보 없음

#EN

### 💊 Zeolara S Liquid - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for eczema, dermatitis, prickly heat, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, itching, insect bites, and urticaria.

### 🕒 Zeolara S Liquid - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ Zeolara S Liquid - Precautions
* Do not use this medicine in infants aged 30 months or younger.
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced hypersensitivity reactions (such as rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances) to medicines or cosmetics, if you have an allergic constitution, if you have severe moist eczema (wetness) or erosion (soreness), or if you are a child.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine is for external use only; do not take internally.
* Be careful not to get it in your eyes. If it gets into your eyes, rinse immediately with water.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 days of use, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 💊 Zeolara S Liquid - 功效与作用
* 本药物用于治疗湿疹、皮炎、痱子、漆树等引起的皮炎、瘙痒、蚊虫叮咬和荨麻疹。

### 🕒 Zeolara S Liquid - 用法用量
* 每日数次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ Zeolara S Liquid - 注意事项
* 30个月以下的幼儿请勿使用本药物。
* 使用本药物前，如您曾对药物或化妆品有过敏史（如皮疹、发红（充血发红）、瘙痒、漆树等引起的皮炎等），或属于过敏体质患者，或有严重湿润（潮湿）或糜烂（溃烂）的患者，以及儿童，请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
* 本药物仅供外用，请勿内服。
* 请注意避免入眼，如不慎入眼，请立即用水冲洗。
* 使用5～6天后，若症状未见改善，请停止使用并咨询医生或药剂师。

* 本剤は外用のみに使用し、内服しないでください。
* 目に入らないように注意し、万一目に入った場合には、直ちに水で洗い流してください。
* 5～6日間使用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、使用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
