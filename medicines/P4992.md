#EN
### 💊 시크린원스점안액(1회용) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for eye fatigue, as a tear supplement, for discomfort while wearing hard or soft contact lenses, and for blurred vision.

### 🕒 시크린원스점안액(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 1 to 2 drops at a time, 4 to 5 times a day.
Discard any remaining solution and the container after instillation.

### ⚠️ 시크린원스점안액(1회용) - Precautions
* No specific information provided.
* Before using this medication, patients experiencing severe eye pain, individuals who have previously experienced allergic symptoms to eye drops (such as conjunctival hyperemia, itching, swelling, rash, redness due to congestion), or patients with glaucoma should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When used by children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medication is for ophthalmic use only.
* Use immediately after opening for a single application, and always discard any remaining solution and the container.
* Do not use if the solution is cloudy (turbid).
* To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the medication as much as possible.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
