#EN
### 💊 아노덱스골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical debility during illness or recovery (while suffering from illness or after recovery), growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the alleviation of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia, and supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 아노덱스골드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 아노덱스골드정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, or if you have hypercalcemia (a condition in which there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hyperkalemia, hemochromatosis (a disorder of iron metabolism where iron is deposited in the liver and pancreas), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet; a pregnant woman, a woman who may be pregnant, or a lactating woman; a premature infant; an infant; a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition in which excessive amounts of oxalate are excreted in the urine); gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis; cardiovascular or circulatory dysfunction; renal impairment; hypoproteinemia; alkalosis; gout; hyperlipoproteinemia; patients with lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic dyslipidemia and pancreatitis, or patients who need to be administered lipid emulsions carefully; those with a history of kidney stones; patients for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload; patients with a thrombotic predisposition; or patients with folic acid deficiency.
    Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
    This medicine may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.
    If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   本药物用于补充在身体疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、发育期、老年期所需的维生素 A、B1、B2、B6、C、D、E。同时，也用于缓解眼睛干涩、夜盲症（夜间视力不佳）、骨骼和牙齿发育不良、预防佝偻病，以及预防和治疗缺铁性贫血，并补充锌。

### 🕒 아노덱스골드정 - 用法与用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人：一次1片，一日1次服用。

*   无信息。
*   对本药物、大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，患有高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高）、结节病（原因不明的全身性炎症疾病）、肾脏疾病、未满12个月的婴儿、高钾血症、血色素沉着症（铁代谢异常导致铁沉积在肝脏和胰腺的疾病）、含铁血黄素沉着症、非缺铁性贫血、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药物。在服用本药物前，如果患者是：接受充足阳光照射且饮食正常的儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿；患有高草酸尿症（尿液中草酸盐过量排泄的状态）；患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病；心血管循环系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、碱中毒、痛风、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高血脂症和胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需谨慎使用脂质乳剂的患者；有肾结石病史者；预计因脂肪过量负荷而有特殊风险的患者；有血栓形成倾向的患者；叶酸缺乏症患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。本药物可能在各种尿液检查中干扰血糖检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检测值。如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
