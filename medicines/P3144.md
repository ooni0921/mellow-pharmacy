#EN

### 💊 프리아틸정600mg(아세틸시스테인) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for difficulty in expelling sputum in conditions such as bronchitis, bronchial asthma, laryngitis, sinusitis, and cystic fibrosis.

### 🕒 프리아틸정600mg(아세틸시스테인) - Dosage and Administration
*   Take the following doses with a small amount of water before meals.
*   For acute conditions: Children aged 12 years and older, and adults should take 1 tablet (600 mg) once daily.
*   For cystic fibrosis: Children aged 6 years and older should take 1 tablet (600 mg) once daily.

### ⚠️ 프리아틸정600mg(아세틸시스테인) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug, an infant under 2 years of age, or a patient with gastric or duodenal ulcer, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or genetic problems such as glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
