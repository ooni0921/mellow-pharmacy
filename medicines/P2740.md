# EN
### 💊 유카인크림(리도카인) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used to reduce the tactile sensitivity of the male genitalia.

### 🕒 유카인크림(리도카인) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply 0.25~0.66 g (23~62 mg as lidocaine) of the cream 5~15 minutes before sexual intercourse. Dosage and application time vary by individual; use the lowest effective dose and do not exceed a maximum of 2 g (184 mg as lidocaine) within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 유카인크림(리도카인) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not use this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or other amide-type local anesthetics, trauma or inflammation at the application site, or if your partner is pregnant. Before using this drug, patients with liver or kidney disease, or hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. For external use only. Do not use on eyes or nose. It is advisable not to apply continuously to the same area.
