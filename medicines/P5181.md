#EN
### 💊 지노칼골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, for the development and growth of bones and teeth, and for the prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 지노칼골드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 지노칼골드정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.

*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, a patient with a history of kidney stones or currently having them, or a patient receiving cardiac glycosides.

*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 지노칼골드정 - 用法・用量

*   本剤に過敏症の患者、高カルシウム血症（血液中にカルシウムが過剰に存在する状態）の患者、サルコイドーシス（原因不明の全身性炎症性疾患）の患者、腎疾患、腎結石、重症の腎不全患者、生後12ヶ月未満の乳児は、本剤を服用しないでください。

*   定められた用法・用量を厳守してください。

*   小児に服用させる場合には、保護者の指導監督のもとに服用させてください。

*   約1ヶ月服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
