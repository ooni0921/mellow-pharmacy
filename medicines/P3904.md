# EN
### 💊 메가골드플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness when physical strength is low, during growth, and in old age. It is also used to alleviate symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye strain.

### 🕒 메가골드플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 8 years or older and adults should take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 메가골드플러스정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, an infant under 3 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a state where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, or kidney stones.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with kidney stones or someone with a history of them, or a patient with folic acid deficiency.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

It may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after taking for about 1 month, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   以下患者请勿服用本品：对本品过敏者、未满3个月的婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态）、结节病（病因不明的全身炎症性疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石患者。
