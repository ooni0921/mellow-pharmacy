#EN
### 💊 디카듀오정100/1000 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, and for the prevention of poor bone and tooth development and rickets.

### 🕒 디카듀오정100/1000 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 8 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 디카듀오정100/1000 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets ample sunlight exposure and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking the medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
