#EN
### 💊 스파토민캡슐(디시클로민염산염) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve spasms associated with gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, colitis, diverticulitis, and spastic constipation.

### 🕒 스파토민캡슐(디시클로민염산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 to 2 capsules (10 to 20 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times daily.

### ⚠️ 스파토민캡슐(디시클로민염산염) - Precautions
* This drug should not be taken by patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, glaucoma, dysuria due to prostatic hypertrophy, severe cardiac disease, obstructive uropathy, gastrointestinal obstruction (such as pyloric stenosis), paralytic ileus, intestinal atony, unstable cardiovascular status in acute hemorrhage, infants under 6 months of age, nursing mothers, severe myasthenia gravis, severe ulcerative colitis, megacolon, reflux esophagitis, or hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug if you have autonomic neuropathy, liver or kidney disease, hypertension, hyperthyroidism, prostatic hypertrophy, cardiac dysfunction (e.g., coronary artery disease, congestive heart failure, arrhythmia), are in a high-temperature environment, have ulcerative colitis, are elderly, pregnant, pediatric, or have a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

This drug may cause visual accommodation disorder and drowsiness, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 스파토민캡슐(디시클로민염산염) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解胃十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、结肠炎、憩室炎、痉挛性便秘的痉挛。

### 🕒 스파토민캡슐(디시클로민염산염) - 用法用量
* 成人每次服用1～2粒胶囊（10～20毫克），每日3～4次。

### ⚠️ 스파토민캡슐(디시클로민염산염) - 注意事项
* 对本药过敏的患者、青光眼、前列腺肥大引起的排尿障碍、严重心脏病、阻塞性尿路疾病、幽门狭窄等胃肠道梗阻患者、麻痹性肠梗阻患者、肠弛缓患者、急性出血期心血管状态不稳定患者、6个月以下婴儿、哺乳期妇女、重症肌无力、重症溃疡性结肠炎、巨结肠患者、反流性食管炎、以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，患有自主神经病变、肝肾疾病、高血压、甲状腺功能亢进症、前列腺肥大、心功能障碍（如冠状动脉疾病、充血性心力衰竭、心律失常）、处于高温环境、溃疡性结肠炎、老年人、孕妇、儿童、对黄色5号有过敏史或过敏症的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

本药可能引起视觉调节障碍和嗜睡，因此驾驶和操作机械时请注意。

如果服用约2周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
