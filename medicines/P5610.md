#EN
### 💊 눈앤큐점안액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for various eye discomforts such as eye fatigue, conjunctival congestion, eye discomfort after swimming or when dust or sweat enters the eye, eye inflammation caused by ultraviolet light and other light rays, eyelid soreness, discomfort when wearing hard contact lenses, itchy eyes, and excessive eye discharge.

### 🕒 눈앤큐점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 1 to 3 drops into each eye, 3 to 6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 눈앤큐점안액 - Precautions
* No information.
* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe eye pain, glaucoma, or a history of allergic symptoms such as eye redness, itching, swelling, or rash caused by eye drops.

* If eye discomfort does not improve with the use of this medication, or if symptoms do not improve after several days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* This medication is for ophthalmic use only; it should only be instilled into the eyes.

* Do not let the tip of the container touch your eyelids or eyelashes. If it touches, the solution may become contaminated or cloudy due to eye discharge or mold. Do not use cloudy solution.

* To prevent contamination, do not share this medication with others.

* Do not use this medication as a soaking solution for soft contact lenses, nor while wearing soft contact lenses.

* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Excessive use may actually cause conjunctival congestion.

* If the solution accidentally stains clothing, wash it off with water.

* Use the opened container as soon as possible, and do not use the medication beyond its expiration date.
