#EN
### 💊 다이락스쎈정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of constipation and associated symptoms such as loss of appetite, abdominal distension, abnormal intestinal fermentation, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 다이락스쎈정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 15 years and older: 1 to 2 tablets once daily at bedtime (on an empty stomach).
*   For individuals 11 years to under 15 years: 2/3 to 1 and 1/3 tablets once daily at bedtime (on an empty stomach).
*   For individuals 7 years to under 11 years: 1/2 to 1 tablet once daily at bedtime (on an empty stomach).

However, for the initial dose, take the minimum amount and gradually increase or decrease the dosage while observing the stool appearance and condition.

### ⚠️ 다이락스쎈정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medication if you have acute abdominal diseases (such as appendicitis, intestinal bleeding, ulcerative colitis, etc.), intestinal obstruction, are a child under 7 years of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, are taking other medications, or have a history of rectal bleeding or bowel dysfunction.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When used in children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

Children under 10 years of age should avoid taking this medication unless absolutely necessary.

Do not chew the tablet when taking it.

*   本药用于缓解便秘及其引起的食欲不振（食欲减退）、腹部胀满、肠内异常发酵和痔疮。

*   15岁及以上：一次1~2片。
*   11岁及以上至15岁以下：一次2/3片~1又1/3片。
*   7岁及以上至11岁以下：一次1/2片~1片。
*   每日1次，睡前（空腹时）服用。

首次服用时请服用最小剂量，并根据排便的形状和状况，逐渐增加或减少剂量。

### ⚠️ 다이락스쎈정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   急性腹部疾病（如阑尾炎、肠出血、溃疡性结肠炎等）患者、肠梗阻患者、7岁以下儿童、以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如患有严重腹痛、恶心、呕吐，或为孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，或正在服用其他药物，或有直肠出血或肠功能不全病史者，请咨询医生或药师。

服用时请勿咀嚼药片。

#JP
### 💊 다이락스쎈정 - 効能・効果
*   本剤は、便秘および便秘に伴う食欲不振（食欲減退）、腹部膨満、腸内異常発酵、痔の緩和に使用します。

### 🕒 다이락스쎈정 - 用法・用量
*   15歳以上：1回1～2錠。
*   11歳以上15歳未満：1回2/3錠～1と1/3錠。
*   7歳以上11歳未満：1回1/2錠～1錠。
*   1日1回、就寝時（空腹時）に服用してください。

ただし、初回は最小量を服用し、便の形状と状態を見ながら徐々に増量または減量してください。

### ⚠️ 다이락스쎈정 - 注意事項
*   情報なし。

定められた用法・用量を厳守してください。

服用時に錠剤を噛まないでください。
