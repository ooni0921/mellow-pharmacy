* This medicine is used for fever and pain (ache) due to common cold, headache, neuralgia, myalgia, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia, and rheumatic pain.

### π•’ μ½λ€μ›ν‚¤μ¦νμ‹λ½(μ•„μ„ΈνΈμ•„λ―Έλ…Έν) - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 12 years or younger, take the recommended single dose every 4 to 6 hours as needed. This medicine should be taken at the lowest effective dose for the shortest possible duration, and should not exceed 5 doses per day (75 mg/kg). Refer to the approved product information for age-specific recommended doses.

### β οΈ μ½λ€μ›ν‚¤μ¦νμ‹λ½(μ•„μ„ΈνΈμ•„λ―Έλ…Έν) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorder, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history thereof, or if you have taken barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol.
