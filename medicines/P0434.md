#EN
### 💊 트라몰정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain associated with the common cold, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 트라몰정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, take 300 to 1,000 mg per dose, 3 to 4 times a day. A maximum of 4,000 mg per day may be administered. Dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 트라몰정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult your doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body. Patients with phenylketonuria, who need to restrict phenylalanine intake, should not take this medicine. Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac insufficiency, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by NSAIDs) or a history thereof, or if you have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol. Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, a tendency to bleed, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, if you are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

* 本药用于感冒引起的发热及疼痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、扭伤痛、牙痛、关节痛、风湿性疼痛。

### 🕒 트라몰정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - 用法与用量
* 成人每次服用 300~1,000 毫克，每日 3~4 次。每日最高可服用 4,000 毫克。可根据年龄、症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 트라몰정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒量超过三杯的规律饮酒者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药师。可能会引起肝损伤。本药含有阿斯巴甜，其在体内代谢为苯丙氨酸，因此苯丙酮尿症患者（需要限制苯丙氨酸摄入）请勿服用。极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症等严重皮肤反应。服用本药后，如果出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状，请立即停药。请勿超过对乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000 毫克）。可能会引起肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液疾病患者、严重肝功能障碍患者、严重肾功能障碍患者、严重心功能不全患者、阿司匹林哮喘（非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘发作）或有其病史者、服用巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药、酒精者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，肝功能障碍或有其病史者、肾功能障碍或有其病史者、消化性溃疡病史者、血液疾病或有其病史者、有出血倾向的患者、有心功能障碍的患者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药师。
