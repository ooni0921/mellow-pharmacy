#EN
### 💊 현대물파스에프 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of sprains, contusions, muscle pain, joint pain, fracture pain, lower back pain, stiff shoulders, neuralgia, and rheumatic pain, as well as for skin itching, insect bites, and chilblains.

### 🕒 현대물파스에프 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area once to several times a day.

### ⚠️ 현대물파스에프 - Precautions
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, on infants under 30 months old, around the eyes, on mucous membranes, on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, on wounds, or on sensitive skin.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine or cosmetics, those with an allergic constitution, patients with severe dampness or erosion (soreness), patients with circulatory disorders or diabetes, children, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
*   For external use only.
*   Shake well before use.
*   Be careful not to get it in your eyes. If it gets into your eyes, rinse thoroughly with clean water.
*   Do not cover or wrap the application area, and do not expose it to external heat or hot water.
*   Avoid contact with objects that may dissolve in alcohol (e.g., spectacle frames, synthetic fibers).
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 5 to 6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   本药用于扭伤、挫伤、肌肉痛、关节痛、骨折痛、腰痛、肩颈僵硬、神经痛、风湿痛的镇痛消炎，以及皮肤瘙痒、蚊虫叮咬、冻疮。

### 🕒 현대물파스에프 - 用法与用量

### ⚠️ 현대물파스에프 - 注意事项
*   对本药过敏者、30个月以下的婴儿、眼周、粘膜、湿疹、漆树等引起的皮炎、伤口部位、敏感肌肤，请勿使用本药。
*   对本药或化妆品过敏者、过敏体质者、湿润或糜烂（溃烂）严重者、循环系统障碍或糖尿病患者、儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
*   仅限外用。
*   使用前请充分摇匀。
*   注意避免药液进入眼睛，万一进入眼睛，请用清水彻底冲洗。
*   请勿覆盖或包扎使用部位，切勿暴露于外部热源或热水。
*   请避免接触可能溶于酒精等物质的物品（如眼镜架、化学纤维等）。
*   如果使用约5~6天后症状仍无改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
