### 💊 화이투벤큐연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve various symptoms of common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 화이투벤큐연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 15 years or older, take 2 capsules at a time. For children aged 7 years or older but under 15 years, take 1 capsule at a time. Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 화이투벤큐연질캡슐 - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can very rarely be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months old, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or are beyond 30 weeks of pregnancy.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age; an infant or child under 15 years suspected or infected with chickenpox or influenza; experiencing symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) related to pseudoephedrine; have an allergic constitution; have a history of allergic symptoms to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision); have difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; are breastfeeding; or if you have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration instructions.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after taking 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
