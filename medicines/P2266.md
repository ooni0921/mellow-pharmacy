# EN
### 💊 엔비100정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and physical debility during or after illness (convalescence), and for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), angular cheilitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 엔비100정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 엔비100정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, pernicious anemia, primary or secondary anemia, or folic acid deficiency.
*   Pregnant and lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and may affect clinical test results.

# CN
