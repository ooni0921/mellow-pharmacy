### ⚠️ 슬라드린정 - 주의사항
* 매일 세잔 이상 정기적 음주자가 이 약 또는 다른 해열진통제를 복용할 때는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오. 간손상을 일으킬 수 있습니다.

#EN
### 💊 슬라드린정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (cough, phlegm, sore throat, chills (shivering symptoms), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 슬라드린정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, take 1 tablet at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals. The dosing interval should be 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 슬라드린정 - Precautions
* Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, if you are an infant under 3 months of age or a child under 2 years of age, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if: it is for a child under 2 years of age (only if absolutely necessary), you have a history of hypersensitivity to tartrazine (Yellow No. 4), you are an infant or child under 15 years of age suspected or confirmed to have chickenpox or influenza, you are an infant or toddler aged 3 months to under 2 years, you have an allergic constitution, liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, edema (swelling), gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), dysuria, you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, you are pregnant or may be pregnant, you are breastfeeding, you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not take continuously for a prolonged period.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for 5-6 doses, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
