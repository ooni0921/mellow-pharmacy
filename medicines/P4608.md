#EN
### 💊 에스알파정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for stomach discomfort, stomach bloating, stomach pain, hyperacidity, heartburn, sour belching, overeating, indigestion, difficulty digesting, promotion of digestion, loss of appetite, nausea, and vomiting.

### 🕒 에스알파정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years and older: Take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.
*   Children aged 8 to under 15 years: Take 1 tablet per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 에스알파정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not administer this medication to children under 7 years of age.
*   Breastfeeding women should not take this medication, or should avoid breastfeeding if taking this medication.
*   Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, patients with gastrointestinal disorders, patients with kidney impairment, patients with heart disorders, patients with glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision) or urination disorders, patients with thyroid dysfunction or the elderly, patients with hypermagnesemia, individuals with an allergic constitution, or those who have experienced allergic reactions to medications (e.g., rash, redness, itching) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Taking this medication may make breast milk secretion difficult.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
