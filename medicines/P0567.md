#EN
### 💊 셀마겐정(알마게이트) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for antacid action and improvement of symptoms such as gastric/duodenal ulcer, gastritis, hyperacidity, heartburn, nausea, vomiting, stomachache, and sour belching.

### 🕒 셀마겐정(알마게이트) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years and older: Chew and take 2 tablets (1 g) once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes to 1 hour after meals.
*   If necessary, an additional dose may be taken once before bedtime.

### ⚠️ 셀마겐정(알마게이트) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, Alzheimer's disease, hemorrhoids, fluid retention (edema), pre-eclampsia, diarrhea, undiagnosed gastrointestinal bleeding, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, are physically weak, are on a low-phosphorus diet, have malabsorption, are elderly, have symptoms of gastrointestinal bleeding such as hematemesis or black stools, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   必要时，可在睡前额外服用一次。

### ⚠️ 셀마겐정(알마게이트) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏者、阿尔茨海默病、痔疮、体液潴留（水肿）、妊娠期毒血症、腹泻、未确诊的消化道出血患者，以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，如有肾功能障碍、身体虚弱、实行低磷饮食者、消化吸收障碍者、老年人、有呕血或黑便等消化道出血症状的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。

*   成人および12歳以上の小児は、1回2錠(1 g)を1日3回、食後30分～1時間に噛んで服用します。
*   必要に応じて、就寝前にもう1回服用できます。

*   約2週間服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
