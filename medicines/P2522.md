#EN
### 💊 덱시푸루펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Indications and Usage
* This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation after trauma and surgery, and infectious diseases accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 덱시푸루펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) per dose, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 capsules (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 덱시푸루펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

When taking this medication, the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke is increased. Severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to it, have a history of asthma, hives, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other NSAIDs, have gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal, cerebrovascular, or other bleeding, severe blood dyscrasias, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are pregnant in the third trimester (6 months or more), are breastfeeding, have inflammatory bowel diseases such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation.

Before taking this medication, patients with blood dyscrasias or bleeding tendencies, liver impairment, kidney impairment, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), elderly patients, children aged 6 years or older, women in early pregnancy or who may be pregnant, stomach cancer, alcoholics, ischemic heart disease, peripheral artery disease, cerebrovascular disease, patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), hepatic porphyria, patients with blood coagulation disorders or those receiving anticoagulants, and those with a history of gastrointestinal ulcers due to NSAIDs should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

After taking this medication, dizziness or fatigue may occur, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

For long-term use in acute conditions, it should generally not exceed 5 days.

#CN
### 💊 덱시푸루펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于辅助治疗慢性多发性关节炎、风湿性关节炎、关节病、强直性脊柱炎、外伤及手术后疼痛性水肿或炎症，以及伴有炎症、疼痛及发热的感染症。

### 🕒 덱시푸루펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 用法用量
* 成人每次服用1粒（300毫克），每日2~4次。但每日总量不得超过4粒（1,200毫克）。

### ⚠️ 덱시푸루펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯以上的规律饮酒者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药师，因为这可能会引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药时，可能会增加致命性严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死、中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
* 对本药过敏的患者，有阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘、荨麻疹或过敏反应史者，有胃肠道溃疡、胃肠道或脑血管或其他部位出血、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、严重高血压、支气管哮喘、冠状动脉（心脏动脉）旁路移植术（CABG）前后出现疼痛的患者，怀孕6个月以上的孕妇，哺乳期妇女，患有克罗恩病或溃疡性结肠炎等炎症性肠病患者，以及有胃肠道（肠）出血或穿孔（破裂）病史者，不得服用本药。

服用本药前，有血液异常或出血倾向的患者，肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、体液潴留或心力衰竭患者，高血压、系统性红斑狼疮（SLE）和混合性结缔组织病（MCTD）患者，老年人，6岁以上儿童，妊娠初期或可能怀孕的女性，胃癌患者，酒精中毒者，缺血性心脏病、周围动脉疾病、脑血管疾病患者，有心血管疾病危险因素的患者（例如，高血压、高脂血症、糖尿病、吸烟），肝性卟啉症患者，有血液凝血障碍或正在接受抗凝剂治疗的患者，以及过去因非甾体抗炎药引起消化道溃疡的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

服用本药后可能会出现头晕或疲劳，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请小心。

治疗急性疾病时，如果需要长期服用，原则上应在5天以内。
