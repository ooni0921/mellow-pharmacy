#EN
### 💊 바이타비플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins B1 and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and physical weakness during illness or convalescence (during or after recovery from illness). It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular cheilitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation in the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 바이타비플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 8 years and older and adults should take 1 tablet at a time, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 바이타비플러스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, an infant under 3 months of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after taking the medicine for about one month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
