#EN

### 💊 맥스콘드로이틴1200경구용겔(콘드로이틴설페이트나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for mild to moderate osteoarthritis and degenerative arthritis.

### 🕒 맥스콘드로이틴1200경구용겔(콘드로이틴설페이트나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 sachet once daily with meals, either as is or diluted in water.

### ⚠️ 맥스콘드로이틴1200경구용겔(콘드로이틴설페이트나트륨) - Precautions
*   No additional information.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, have acute joint pain, or are under 18 years of age.

Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, patients with impaired cardiac or renal function, and patients who require sodium intake restriction should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Dizziness may occur after taking this medication. It may affect your ability to drive or operate machinery.
