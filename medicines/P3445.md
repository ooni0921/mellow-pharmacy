정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

3일 이상 계속하여 복용하지 마십시오.

수일(여러차례)간 투여하여도 증상의 개선이 없을 경우 복용을 즉각 중지하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 로페도에프캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for diarrhea, diarrhea accompanied by abdominal pain, indigestion, loose stools, and vomiting.

### 🕒 로페도에프캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2 capsules once, three times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 로페도에프캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a newborn, a child under 7 years of age, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching) to medication, have persistent diarrhea with fever, bloody stools, or mucous stools, are elderly, have gastric or duodenal ulcers, or are allergic to or have experienced hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for more than 3 days.

If symptoms do not improve after several days of administration, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
