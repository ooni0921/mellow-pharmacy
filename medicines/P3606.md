* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 2회 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 비나플러스연질캡슐 - 주의사항
* 정보 없음
* 이 약 또는 대두유, 콩, 땅콩에 과민증 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 심한 증상의 신부전 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.이 약을 복용하기 전에 임부 또는 임신하고 있을 가능성이 있는 여성 및 수유부, 미숙아, 유아, 심장·순환기계기능 장애, 신장장애, 저단백혈증, 고지단백혈증, 당뇨병성고지질혈증 및 췌장염 등 지방대사 이상 환자 또는 지질성 유제를 신중히 투여해야 하는 환자, 지방과부하로 특별한 위험이 예상되는 환자, 혈전성 소인이 있는 환자, 황색5호에 과민증 환자 또는 경험자는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

# EN
### 💊 비나플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is reduced during or after illness, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder/neck stiffness during menopause, numbness and coldness in hands and feet, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 비나플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 12 years and older, take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 비나플러스연질캡슐 - Warnings
* Do not administer this medication to patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, infants under 12 months of age, or patients with severe renal dysfunction.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, premature infants, infants, patients with cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders (such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis) or patients who require careful administration of lipid emulsions, patients with a special risk due to fat overload, patients with a thrombotic predisposition, or patients with a history of or current hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
