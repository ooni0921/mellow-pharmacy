# EN
### 💊 부루펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to common cold, backache, dysmenorrhea, postoperative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, myalgia, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 부루펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults should take 1/2 to 1.5 tablets (200-600 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day. The maximum daily dose is 3,200 mg.

    For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or common cold, adults should take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day.

    Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    Adjust the dose appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 부루펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and plan to take this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
    Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious and potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, active or history of gastrointestinal ulcers or bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or history thereof, hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors) or history thereof, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, pregnant women in the last trimester (final 3 months), patients receiving high-dose methotrexate for cancer chemotherapy, or patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of blood disorders, a tendency to bleed, liver cirrhosis, liver impairment or history thereof, kidney impairment or history thereof, cardiac dysfunction or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, elderly patients, children, pregnant women, nursing mothers, chickenpox, or dehydration.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

    年齢、症状により適宜増減してください。

### ⚠️ 부루펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - 注意事項
*   毎日3杯以上のアルコールを定期的に摂取している方が本剤または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する際は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。胃腸出血を引き起こす可能性があります。
    本剤服用により、致死的となりうる重篤な心血管系血栓症、心筋梗塞、脳卒中のリスクが増加し、胃腸出血、潰瘍および穿孔などの重篤な胃腸系副作用が現れることがあります。

    本剤を服用する前に、血液異常またはその既往歴がある患者、出血傾向のある患者、肝硬化、肝障害またはその既往歴がある患者、腎障害またはその既往歴がある患者、心機能不全または心疾患、高血圧、全身性エリテテマトーデスおよび混合性結合組織病の患者、潰瘍性大腸炎、クローン病、高齢者および小児、妊婦、授乳婦、水痘、脱水患者は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。

    服用後、眠気が現れることがあるため、車の運転や機械の操作には注意してください。
