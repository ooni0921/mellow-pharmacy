#EN

### 💊 네오신정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is indicated for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and for general debility during or after recovery from illness.

### 🕒 네오신정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 네오신정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not administer this drug to patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, soy, or peanuts, patients with or with a history of hypersensitivity to soybean oil, or infants under 3 months of age.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria, gout, or kidney stones, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, patients for whom lipid emulsions must be administered cautiously, or patients with fat overload.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue this drug immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This drug may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical laboratory values by causing the urine to turn yellow.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
