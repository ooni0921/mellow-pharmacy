#EN
### 💊 벤포칸정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for neurological disorders due to vitamin B1 and B6 deficiency.

### 🕒 벤포칸정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 벤포칸정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to it, or if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of use, immediately stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 벤포칸정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于因维生素 B1、B6 缺乏引起的神经系统疾病。

### 🕒 벤포칸정 - 用法用量
* 成人每次服用 1 片，每日 1 次。

### ⚠️ 벤포칸정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者，以及患有半乳糖不耐受症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
* 孕妇及哺乳期妇女在服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 服用约 1 个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
