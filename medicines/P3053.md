### ⚠️ 싸이타민플러스정 - 주의사항

#EN
### 💊 싸이타민플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical weakness during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the alleviation of dry eyes, nyctalopia (difficulty seeing at night), prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness and coldness in hands and feet due to peripheral circulatory disorders and during menopause, alleviation of symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), alleviation of symptoms of beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and supplementation of selenium.

### 🕒 싸이타민플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years or older: Take 2 tablets once a day, or 1 tablet twice a day.

### ⚠️ 싸이타민플러스정 - Precautions
* Taking more than 5,000 IU of vitamin A per day can cause congenital malformations. Do not take this drug if you are within 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy.
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have kidney stones.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant or infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 5), folic acid deficiency, or a thrombotic predisposition.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This drug may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests, and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
