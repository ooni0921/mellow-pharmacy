#EN

### 💊 이부로엔연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, back pain (lumbago), menstrual pain (dysmenorrhea), rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), postoperative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 이부로엔연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults with rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, or psoriatic arthritis, the usual dose is 1/2 to 1.5 capsules (200-600 mg) once, 3 to 4 times daily, up to a maximum of 8 capsules (3,200 mg) per day.
*   For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, the daily dose is 30-40 mg per kg of body weight, administered in 3-4 divided doses. For mild to moderate pain or common cold, adults take 1/2 to 1 capsule (200-400 mg) once, 3 to 4 times daily.
*   For migraine, adults take 1/2 to 1 capsule (200-400 mg) once, and the dose should not exceed 1 capsule (400 mg) within 24 hours.
*   Do not exceed the recommended dose.
*   The dose may be adjusted appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 이부로엔연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular consumer of three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
*   Taking this medicine increases the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction (heart attack), and stroke, and may cause serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe renal dysfunction, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors) or a history thereof, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, or if you are in the last trimester of pregnancy.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction or a history thereof, renal dysfunction or a history thereof, cardiac insufficiency or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, elderly patients, children, pregnant women, or breastfeeding women.
*   Do not exceed the recommended dose.

*   对于幼年型类风湿性关节炎，每日按体重每公斤30~40 mg分3~4次服用。对于轻度及中度疼痛或感冒，成人每次服用1/2至1粒胶囊（200~400 mg），每日3~4次。
*   对于偏头痛，成人每次服用1/2至1粒胶囊（200~400 mg），24小时内不得超过1粒胶囊（400 mg）。
*   请勿超过推荐剂量服用。
*   请根据年龄、症状适当增减剂量。
