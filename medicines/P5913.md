#EN
### 💊 메가벤포큐정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the following: supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth period, and old age; prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, and rickets; relief of symptoms such as beriberi, eye strain, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 메가벤포큐정 - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 메가벤포큐정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney (renal) disease, an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney (renal) stones, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergy to soybean oil, or have hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), are pregnant or possibly pregnant or lactating, are a premature infant, an infant (general term), have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis (colitis appearing in a specific area), have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney (renal) impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney (renal) stones or a history thereof, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or require cautious administration of lipid emulsions.

Patients with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage) and patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test results by turning the urine yellow.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 메가벤포큐정 - 用法与用量

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师：未满1岁的婴儿，经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童，高草酸尿症（尿液中草酸盐排出过多的状态），孕妇及可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，早产儿，婴幼儿，患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性（特定部位出现）结肠炎等胃肠道疾病者，心血管系统功能障碍者，肾脏功能障碍者，低蛋白血症者，痛风患者，有肾结石或曾有肾结石病史者，高脂蛋白血症者，糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要谨慎给予脂质乳剂的患者。

有血栓形成倾向（血管阻塞）的患者和叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

本药在各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变为黄色，从而影响临床检查结果。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
