#EN
### 💊 파인콜정 - Indications & Efficacy
* This medication is used to relieve various cold symptoms such as runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 파인콜정 - Dosage & Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, take 2 tablets at a time. For individuals aged over 7 years and under 15 years, take 1 tablet at a time. Administer 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 파인콜정 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN) (Lyell's syndrome), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, stop taking it immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this medication or its components, have a history of asthma after taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, or are under 7 years of age.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, experience symptoms of ischemic colitis such as sudden abdominal pain or rectal bleeding in relation to pseudoephedrine, have a history of allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due to medication, are elderly, frail, or have a high fever.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma, or dysuria; if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or if you have ulcer or bleeding problems; if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than one week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking this medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take continuously for a long period.
* Drowsiness may occur while taking this medication. Avoid driving a car or operating machinery.
* Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
