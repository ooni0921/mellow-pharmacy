#EN
### 💊 유한비타민씨정1000mg - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the prevention and treatment of scurvy, for conditions where vitamin C requirements are increased such as physical fatigue and debilitating conditions during pregnancy, lactation, illness, or convalescence, for capillary hemorrhage including gum bleeding, epistaxis (nosebleed), and hematuria, for pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, and in cases suspected of vitamin C deficiency, such as malabsorption.

### 🕒 유한비타민씨정1000mg - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1/2 to 1 tablet (500 to 1,000 mg) once daily, or in divided doses several times a day. Take with an adequate amount of water.

### ⚠️ 유한비타민씨정1000mg - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to it, hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), thalassemia, gout, cystinuria, Glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, or kidney oxalate stones.
* Before taking this drug, pregnant or lactating women, smokers, and chronic alcohol consumers should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Prolonged contact with the esophageal mucosa can cause esophagitis, so do not lie down immediately after taking the drug.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* It may interfere with the detection of blood glucose in various urine glucose tests.

#CN

* 本药物用于预防和治疗坏血病，以及在妊娠期、哺乳期及病中病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降、身体疲劳时维生素C需求量增加的消耗性疾病，牙龈出血、鼻出血（流鼻血）、血尿等毛细血管出血，由阳光、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑），以及吸收不良等疑似维生素C缺乏的情况。

### 🕒 유한비타민씨정1000mg - 用法用量
* 成人每次服用1/2片至1片（500～1,000毫克），每日一次或分多次服用。请用足量水送服。

### ⚠️ 유한비한비타민씨정1000mg - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药物过敏者、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、地中海贫血、痛风、胱氨酸尿症、葡萄糖-6-磷酸脱氢酶（G6PD）缺乏症、肾脏草酸结石患者请勿服用本药物。

* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
* 本药物可能干扰各种尿糖检测中血糖的检测。
