#EN
### 💊 디어미순정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for contraception.

### 🕒 디어미순정 - Dosage and Administration
* Take 1 tablet daily for 28 days following the indicated order (yellow tablets (active pills) for 24 days, followed by white tablets (placebo pills) for 4 days).
* It is preferable to take the medicine at the same time each day, if possible.
* For more details, please refer to the detailed information in the prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 디어미순정 - Precautions
* Smoking increases the risk of serious cardiovascular adverse reactions due to oral contraceptives, and this risk increases with age and smoking amount (more than 15 cigarettes per day), especially prominent in women over 35 years of age. Therefore, women taking oral contraceptives should refrain from smoking.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, estrogen-dependent tumors or suspected or experienced them, thrombotic arteriovenitis, thromboembolism, deep vein thrombosis or experienced them, thrombotic valvular heart disease, thrombotic cardiac arrhythmias, congenital or acquired thrombophilia, cerebrovascular or coronary artery disease, patients at high risk of venous or arterial thrombosis, undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding, moderate to severe hypertension, liver dysfunction, Dubin-Johnson syndrome and Rotor syndrome, jaundice, cholestasis, hepatitis and benign or malignant liver tumors or experienced them, sickle cell anemia, severe diabetes with vascular degeneration, severe dyslipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia and severe metabolic disorders such as porphyria, persistent itching during pregnancy, gestational herpes, pemphigoid or otosclerosis experienced, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant and nursing mothers, pituitary tumors, hyperprolactinemia that may accompany galactorrhea, experienced pancreatitis or severe hypertriglyceridemia, headache with focal neurological symptoms such as aura, undiagnosed vaginal bleeding, concomitant use of direct-acting antiviral agents for hepatitis C, congenital or acquired hypercoagulability, smokers over 35 years of age, patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker, have high blood pressure, obesity, are over 40 years old, have epilepsy, migraine, a family history of arterial disease or experience varicose veins, systemic lupus erythematosus, a family history of breast cancer, depression or experience it, heart failure/renal failure, atypical hyperplastic mammary dysplasia, breast nodules experienced, heart valve disease, atrial fibrillation or experienced it, a family history of dyslipidemia, gallbladder disease and gallstones experienced, endometriosis, asthma or chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorders, or diabetes.
* For other detailed information, please refer to the detailed information in the prescribing information.
