#EN
### 💊 하디콜맥스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various common cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 하디콜맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 15 years and older: Take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals. Children 7 to under 15 years old: Take 1 tablet per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 하디콜맥스정 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of Acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing Acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma after taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5), have a history of drug allergies, are a child aged 3 months to under 2 years, are an infant or child under 15 years of age who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric or duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), urination difficulty, are elderly, are frail or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are a nursing mother, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting more than 1 week or a recurrent cough, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
