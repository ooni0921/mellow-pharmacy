#EN
### 💊 캐롤비노즈연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle aches).

### 🕒 캐롤비노즈연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults aged 15 years and older, take 2 capsules once. For children aged 8 years to under 15 years, take 1 capsule once. Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 캐롤비노즈연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist when a regular drinker who consumes three or more alcoholic beverages daily takes this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it before, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure, or are in the 30th week of pregnancy or later.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years suspected of or infected with chickenpox or influenza; an infant under 2 years of age; a toddler; have an allergic constitution; have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver, kidney, heart, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; are breastfeeding; smoke; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be cautious when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after taking 5-6 doses, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
