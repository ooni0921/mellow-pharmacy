# EN
### 💊 이지정(디시클로민염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of spasms in gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, colitis, diverticulitis, and spastic constipation.

### 🕒 이지정(디시클로민염산염) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, take 1 to 2 tablets (10 to 20 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 이지정(디시클로민염산염) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with glaucoma, urinary obstruction due to prostatic hypertrophy, severe heart disease, obstructive uropathy, gastrointestinal obstruction such as pyloric stenosis, paralytic ileus, intestinal atony, unstable cardiovascular status in acute hemorrhage, hypersensitivity to this drug, infants under 6 months, nursing mothers, severe myasthenia gravis, severe ulcerative colitis, toxic megacolon, reflux esophagitis, or hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, patients with autonomic neuropathy, hepatic or renal impairment, hypertension, hyperthyroidism, prostatic hypertrophy, cardiac dysfunction (coronary artery disease, congestive heart failure, arrhythmia, etc.), those in a high-temperature environment, patients with ulcerative colitis, the elderly, pregnant women, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

As visual accommodation disorder and drowsiness may occur, exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

# CN

* 本药用于缓解胃及十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、结肠炎、憩室炎、痉挛性便秘的痉挛。

若服用约2周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

* 无信息
* 青光眼、因前列腺肥大引起的排尿障碍、症状严重的心脏病、阻塞性泌尿道疾病、幽门狭窄等胃肠道梗阻、麻痹性肠梗阻、肠弛缓症、急性出血期心血管状态不稳定的患者、对本药过敏的患者、6个月以下的婴儿、哺乳期妇女、症状严重的重症肌无力、症状严重的溃疡性结肠炎、巨结肠患者、反流性食管炎，以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
