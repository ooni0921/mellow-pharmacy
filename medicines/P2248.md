#EN
### 💊 간모아액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for liver diseases.

### 🕒 간모아액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 10-30 mL daily, divided between meals. Dilute 10 mL of this medicine in about half a glass of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 간모아액 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with kidney disease, myocardial infarction, or a history of such conditions should not take this medicine.
* This medicine cannot be used as an antidote for alcohol poisoning.
* If there is no improvement after approximately one month of use, immediately discontinue taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medicine is for oral use only and should not be used for injection.
