#EN
### 💊 메가트루파워정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the supply of vitamins D, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness, developmental period, and old age; for poor development of bones and teeth; prevention of rickets; relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; and for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 메가트루파워정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 메가트루파워정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this drug if you have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or severe renal failure.

*   Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or young child, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, a history of kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency.

*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

*   This drug may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values or interfering with glucose detection during urine tests.

*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 메가트루파워정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解身体疲劳，妊娠期和哺乳期，病中或病后体力下降，发育期，老年期时补充维生素D、B1、B2、B6、C，以及骨骼、牙齿发育不良，预防佝偻病，缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩周炎等）症状，脚气病，眼睛疲劳，口角炎，唇炎，口腔炎，舌炎，湿疹，皮肤炎，并补充锌。

### 🕒 메가트루파워정 - 用法与用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人：每次1片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 메가트루파워정 - 注意事项
*   高钙血症、结节病、肾脏疾病、对此药过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、肾结石患者、重度肾功能不全患者请勿服用本药。

*   服用本药前，以下人群请咨询医生或药师：未满1岁的婴儿，经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童，高草酸尿症患者，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女，早产儿，婴幼儿，患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病者，心血管功能障碍者，肾功能障碍者，低蛋白血症患者，痛风患者，有肾结石病史或现有肾结石患者，叶酸缺乏症患者。

*   请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。

*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

*   本药可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查值，或干扰尿液检查中的血糖检测。

*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
