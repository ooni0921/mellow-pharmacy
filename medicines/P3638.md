* 정보 없음

# EN
### 💊 이지굿정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of constipation, hemorrhoids, abdominal bloating, abnormal intestinal fermentation, headache/heavy feeling in the head, facial flushing, and loss of appetite (anorexia).

### 🕒 이지굿정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults, take 1 to 3 tablets once daily, before bedtime (or on an empty stomach).

    Initially, take the minimum dose, and gradually increase or decrease it depending on bowel movements.

    As this medicine is an enteric-coated tablet, swallow it whole with water without chewing or crushing.

### ⚠️ 이지굿정 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Patients with acute abdominal diseases (such as appendicitis, intestinal bleeding, ulcerative colitis, etc.) or intestinal obstruction should not take this medicine.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant, may be pregnant, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic reactions to medicines, have severe abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, rectal bleeding, a history of intestinal failure, or hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Yellow No. 5.

    If you are breastfeeding, stop taking this medicine or avoid breastfeeding while taking it.

    If symptoms do not improve after 1 week of taking the medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

# CN
### 💊 이지굿정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解便秘、痔疮、腹胀、肠道异常发酵、头重感、面部潮红、食欲不振（食欲减退）。

### 🕒 이지굿정 - 用法与用量
*   15岁及以上人群和成人，每次服用1~3片，每日1次，睡前（或空腹时）服用。

    初次服用时请服用最小剂量，并根据排便情况逐渐增加或减少剂量。

    本药为肠溶片，请勿咀嚼或压碎，直接与水一同服用。

### ⚠️ 이지굿정 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   急性腹部疾病（如阑尾炎、肠出血、溃疡性结肠炎等）或肠梗阻患者请勿服用本药。

    服用本药前，如果您是孕妇、可能怀孕的人、过敏体质者、有药物过敏反应史者、有剧烈腹痛、恶心、呕吐、直肠出血、肠功能不全病史者，或对黄色5号有过敏反应或过敏史者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

    如果您正在哺乳，请停止服用本药，或在服用本药期间避免哺乳。

    如果服用1周后症状没有改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。

    请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
