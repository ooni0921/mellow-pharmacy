# EN

### 💊 졸리민정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the temporary relief of headaches, pain accompanied by discomfort and insomnia, and fever.

### 🕒 졸리민정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age and older should take 2 tablets once daily before bedtime. Do not exceed the recommended dose.

### ⚠️ 졸리민정 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medication if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity reactions (rash, redness, itching, swelling (e.g., larynx, eyelids, lips)) to this drug, or if you have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 14 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, if you have an allergic predisposition, liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, if you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, if you are pregnant or likely to be pregnant, a nursing mother, or if you have asthma, glaucoma, or prostatic hypertrophy. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

# CN

### 💊 졸리민정 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于暂时缓解头痛、伴随不适和失眠的疼痛以及发热。

### 🕒 졸리민정 - 用法及用量
*   成人及12岁以上儿童，每日一次，睡前服用2片。请勿超过推荐剂量。

### ⚠️ 졸리민정 - 注意事项
*   若每日饮酒三杯以上且规律饮酒者服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起肝损伤。请勿超过对乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000毫克）。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
*   对本药有过敏反应（皮疹、发红、瘙痒、肿胀（如喉部、眼睑、嘴唇等））的患者及有相关经历者，以及服用其他解热镇痛剂、感冒药时曾出现哮喘者，请勿服用本药。在服用本药前，若婴儿或14岁以下儿童感染或疑似感染水痘或流感，或有过敏体质、肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、老年人、体弱者或高热者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、哮喘、青光眼、前列腺肥大患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。服药后可能出现嗜睡，因此驾驶和操作机械时请注意。

### 🕒 졸리민정 - 用法及び用量
