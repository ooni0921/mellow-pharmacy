#EN

### 💊 프리미움리도센크림(리도카인) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to reduce the sensitivity of the male genital tactile sensation.

### 🕒 프리미움리도센크림(리도카인) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply 0.25-0.66 g (23-62 mg as lidocaine) of the cream 5-15 minutes before intercourse. Dosage and application time vary depending on the individual, and the lowest effective dose should be used, but do not exceed a maximum of 2 g (184 mg as lidocaine) within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 프리미움리도센크림(리도카인) - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not use this product if you are hypersensitive to this medication or other amide-type local anesthetics, if you have trauma or inflammation at the application site, or if your partner is pregnant. Before using this medication, patients with hepatic or renal disease or hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. For external use only. Do not apply to the eyes or nose. It is advisable not to apply continuously to the same area.
