#EN
### 💊 라라올라액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjuvant therapy for symptoms of mental and physical functional asthenia, and as an adjuvant therapy during the recovery period (amino acid deficiency).

### 🕒 라라올라액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - Dosage and Administration
* Adolescents aged 12 years and older and adults should take 5 g once daily. It is best to take it in the morning, but it can also be taken before bedtime. It can be taken with a small amount of water.

### ⚠️ 라라올라액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, have severe renal impairment or hepatic impairment, or have experienced myocardial infarction. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have urinary retention problems, are pregnant or potentially pregnant or breastfeeding, plan to take it continuously for more than 15 days, or have a history of or hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5). Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If any unusual symptoms occur while taking this medication, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

* 本药品用于精神和身体功能衰弱症状的辅助治疗，以及恢复期（氨基酸缺乏状态）的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 라라올라액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - 用法用量

### ⚠️ 라라올라액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - 注意事项
* 无信息。

#JP
### 💊 라라올라액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - 効能・効果
* この薬剤は、精神的・身体的機能低下症状の補助療法、および回復期（アミノ酸欠乏状態）における補助療法として使用されます。

### 🕒 라라올라액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - 用法・用量
* 12歳以上の青少年および成人は、1日1回5gを服用してください。朝に服用するのが望ましいですが、就寝前にも服用可能です。少量の水と一緒に服用できます。
