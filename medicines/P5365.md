#EN
### 💊 하이타민골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supply vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is decreased during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the relief of hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight, skin diseases, etc., the prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds), and the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 하이타민골드정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 12 years of age or older: 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 하이타민골드정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or kidney stones, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, or an infant.

If symptoms do not improve after taking the medicine for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test results by turning urine yellow.

* 无信息
