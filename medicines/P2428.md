* 만 15세 이상 및 성인은 1회 2캡슐씩, 만 8세 이상 만 15세 미만은 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 3회 식후 30분에 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 오메코프에스캡슐 - 주의사항
* 매일 세잔 이상 정기적 음주자가 이 약 또는 다른 해열진통제를 복용할 때는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오. 간손상을 일으킬 수 있습니다.

매우 드물게 치명적일 수 있는 급성전신발진고름물집증, 스티븐스-존슨증후군, 독성표피괴사용해와 같은 중대한 피부반응이 보고되었고 이 약 복용 후 피부발진 또는 다른 과민반응의 징후가 나타나는 경우 즉시 복용을 중단하십시오.

급성전신발진고름물집증과 같은 중증 피부 이상반응이 나타나는 경우 복용을 중단하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

5~6회 복용 후에도 증상의 개선이 없을 경우 복용을 즉각 중지하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

# EN
### 💊 오메코프에스캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (symptoms of feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 오메코프에스캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: 2 capsules per dose. For individuals aged 8 years and older but under 15 years: 1 capsule per dose. Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 오메코프에스캡슐 - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause liver damage.

    Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: children under 2 years of age, infants and children under 15 years of age who have or are suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, experiencing symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding), have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicines (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, elderly, frail individuals or those with high fever, individuals with persistent or recurring gastrointestinal issues such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or those with ulcer or bleeding problems, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, individuals who smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting more than 1 week or recurring, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

# CN
### 💊 오메코프에스캡슐 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于缓解感冒的多种症状（流鼻涕、鼻塞、打喷嚏、咽喉痛、咳嗽、咳痰、寒颤（发冷发抖的症状）、发热、头痛、关节痛、肌肉痛）。

### 🕒 오메코프에스캡슐 - 用法及用量
*   15岁及以上成人，每次2粒；8岁及以上未满15岁儿童，每次1粒；每日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 오메코프에스캡슐 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒超过三杯的长期饮酒者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。

    非常罕见地报告了可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状，请立即停止服用。

    每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量请勿超过最大剂量（4,000 毫克）。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与其他含有对乙酰氨基酚的产品同时服用。

    如果出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等严重皮肤不良反应，请停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
*   对本药有过敏史或过敏经历者、服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾有哮喘经历者、未满3个月的婴儿、重度或未受控制的高血压患者、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。

    服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师：未满2岁的儿童；患有或疑似患有水痘或流感的婴儿及未满15岁的儿童；出现缺血性结肠炎症状（如：突发腹痛、直肠出血等）者；过敏体质者；曾因药物出现过敏症状（如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）者；患有肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡者；老年人；身体虚弱者或高热者；胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题持续或反复出现，或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；吸烟者；患有哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽者；咳嗽持续1周以上或反复发作、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽者。

    服用5-6次后症状仍未改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

    请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

    儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
