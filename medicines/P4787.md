#EN
### 💊 하벤코프에스액 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 하벤코프에스액 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years or older, take 1 sachet (20 mL). For individuals aged 11 years or older and under 15 years, take 2/3 sachet. For individuals aged 7 years or older and under 11 years, take 1/2 sachet. For individuals aged 3 years or older and under 7 years, take 1/3 sachet. For individuals aged 2 years or older and under 3 years, take 1/4 sachet. Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

    Do not administer to infants under 2 years of age.

### ⚠️ 하벤코프에스액 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

    Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

    This medication contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body. Therefore, patients with phenylketonuria who need to regulate phenylalanine intake should not take this medication.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma after taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child aged 3 months or older and under 2 years, an infant or child under 15 years suspected of or infected with chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have experienced drug-induced allergies, have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, are elderly, are frail or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, or if you experience symptoms such as smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    Do not take continuously for a prolonged period.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 하벤코프에스액 - 用法用量

### ⚠️ 하벤코프에스액 - 注意事项
*   每天饮酒超过三杯的常饮者，在服用本药或其它解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能导致肝损伤。

    极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的急性全身性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症等严重皮肤反应。服用本药后，若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状，请立即停止服用。

    本药含有阿斯巴甜，其在体内会代谢为苯丙氨酸，因此需要限制苯丙氨酸摄入的苯丙酮尿症患者请勿服用。
*   对本药有或曾有过敏反应者，服用其他解热镇痛剂或感冒药时曾引起哮喘者，未满3个月的婴儿（新生儿），正在服用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）或在停用MAO抑制剂后2周内的人，请勿服用本药。

    服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果出现以下情况：3个月以上2岁以下的儿童，患有或疑似患水痘或流感的婴儿及15岁以下的儿童，过敏体质者，有过药物过敏史者，患有肝脏、肾脏、心脏、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者，老年人，身体虚弱或有高热者，持续或反复出现胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠道问题或有溃疡、出血问题者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女，吸烟者，哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有大量痰液的咳嗽，持续或反复超过1周的咳嗽，慢性咳嗽，伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽等症状。

    给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下进行。

    服用后可能会出现嗜睡，因此驾驶车辆或操作机械时请注意。
