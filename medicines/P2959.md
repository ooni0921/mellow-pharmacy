# EN

### 💊 코리투살노즈시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 코리투살노즈시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and individuals aged 15 years or older: 20 mL per dose.
* Individuals aged 11 years or older to under 15 years: 13.33 mL per dose.
* Individuals aged 7 years or older to under 11 years: 10 mL per dose.
* Individuals aged 3 years or older to under 7 years: 6.67 mL per dose.
* Individuals aged 2 years or older to under 3 years: 5 mL per dose.
* Take 3-4 times a day, 30 minutes after meals and at bedtime.
The dosing interval should be 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 코리투살노즈시럽 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and intend to take this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug or have experienced it before, have a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: infants or children under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; pregnant or possibly pregnant women or breastfeeding women; the elderly; physically weak individuals; individuals with an allergic constitution; those with a history of allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); individuals with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, high fever, or gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, ulcers, bleeding problems; smokers; individuals with asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting for more than one week or recurring, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache; or children under 2 years of age.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
