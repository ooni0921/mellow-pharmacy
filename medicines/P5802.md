#EN
### 💊 비스콘틴엠800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, B6, and D in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or recovery, during growth, and in old age.
*   Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used to relieve symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, shoulder stiffness, and other joint pains, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and to prevent beriberi, eye fatigue, poor bone and teeth development, and rickets.

### 🕒 비스콘틴엠800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years or older should take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 비스콘틴엠800정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney stones, kidney disease, severe renal failure, genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have heart and circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should immediately discontinue this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
