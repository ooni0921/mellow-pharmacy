# EN
### 💊 콘티박스디연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and when physical strength is depleted during or after illness. It is also used to relieve symptoms of neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 콘티박스디연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years and older should take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 콘티박스디연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, and infants under 3 months of age should not take this medication.
*   Before taking this medication, infants under 1 year of age, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, infants (toddlers), patients with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or patients who should be administered lipid emulsions cautiously, patients for whom a special risk is anticipated due to fat overload, and patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 콘티박스디연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于补充维生素B1、B2、B6，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠/哺乳期以及病中/病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降的情况。同时，也用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）、脚气病、眼睛疲劳、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

### 🕒 콘티박스디연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   成人及8岁以上儿童，每次1粒，每日2次服用。

### ⚠️ 콘티박스디연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿，请勿服用本药。
*   在服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需要慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者、因脂肪过量负荷而预计有特殊风险的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者，请务必咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
*   本药可能使尿液变为黄色，并可能影响临床检查结果。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。

# JP

### 🕒 콘티박스디연질캡슐 - 用法・用量

*   約1ヶ月服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
