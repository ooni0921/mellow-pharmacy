# EN
### 💊 후시덴연고(퓨시드산나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for impetigo, infectious eczematous dermatitis, acne vulgaris, folliculitis, furuncles and furunculosis, suppurative hidradenitis, impetiginous eczema caused by Staphylococcus, Streptococcus, Corynebacterium, Clostridium, and secondary infections following burns, trauma, surgical wounds, and skin grafts.

### 🕒 후시덴연고(퓨시드산나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
* Cleanse the affected area, then apply an appropriate amount directly to the affected area 1 to 2 times a day, or spread it thinly onto sterile gauze and apply. Limit the duration of administration to about 1 week. Do not apply thickly.

### ⚠️ 후시덴연고(퓨시드산나트륨) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication in patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, or in patients with infections caused by non-susceptible bacteria (especially Pseudomonas aeruginosa). Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, premature infants, and newborns should consult with a doctor or pharmacist. Due to its high percutaneous absorption, do not use this medication for long periods. Do not use for ophthalmic purposes. Do not use on the inner parts of the face, such as the eyes or eyelids.

# CN
### 💊 후시덴연고(퓨시드산나트륨) - 功效及效果
* 本药用于治疗由葡萄球菌、链球菌、棒状杆菌、梭状芽孢杆菌引起的脓疱疮、感染性湿疹样皮炎、寻常痤疮（普通痤疮）、毛囊炎、疖和疖病、化脓性汗腺炎、脓疱性湿疹，以及烧伤、外伤、缝合伤、植皮伤引起的继发感染。

### 🕒 후시덴연고(퓨시드산나트륨) - 用法及用量
* 清洁患处，每日1~2次，取适量直接涂抹于患处，或均匀涂布于无菌纱布上再贴敷。给药期间限为约1周。请勿厚涂。

### ⚠️ 후시덴연고(퓨시드산나트륨) - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 对本药过敏的患者，以及由非敏感菌（特别是铜绿假单胞菌）引起的感染患者请勿使用。在使用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿及新生儿应咨询医生或药师。本药具有高渗透吸收性，请勿长期使用。请勿用于眼科。请勿用于眼部或眼睑等面部内侧。
