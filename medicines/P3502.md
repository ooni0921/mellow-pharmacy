* 만 8세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN

### 💊 Bibek Power Tablet - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, and periods of physical debility during or after illness. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 Bibek Power Tablet - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ Bibek Power Tablet - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to any component of this product, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalate is excreted in the urine), heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

* 无信息。
* 对本品过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本品。
