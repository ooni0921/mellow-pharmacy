# EN
### 💊 리드판액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for liver diseases.

### 🕒 리드판액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 10-30 mL daily, divided between meals (between meal times). Dilute 10 mL of this medicine in about half a glass of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 리드판액 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with kidney disease or myocardial infarction, or those with a history thereof, should not take this medicine. Patients with a history of or hypersensitivity to caramel should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medicine is for oral use only and should not be used for injection. If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 리드판액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于肝病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 리드판액 - 用法与用量
* 成人每日服用 10-30 毫升，分次于两餐之间服用。将本药 10 毫升稀释于约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 리드판액 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 肾脏疾病、心肌梗死患者或有相关病史者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，对焦糖过敏或有相关病史者，请咨询医生或药剂师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药仅供口服，不可用于注射。若服用约一个月后症状仍未改善，请立即停药并咨询医生或药剂师。
