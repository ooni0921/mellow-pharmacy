#EN
### 💊 타스멘정(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain associated with colds, headache, neuralgia, myalgia, dysmenorrhea, sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 타스멘정(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 300-1,000 mg per dose, 3-4 times daily. The maximum daily dose is 4,000 mg. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 타스멘정(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body. Do not take this medicine if you have phenylketonuria, which requires regulation of phenylalanine intake. Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. Discontinue use immediately if skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medicine. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of 4,000 mg of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks triggered by NSAIDs) or a history thereof, or if you have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment or a history thereof, kidney impairment or a history thereof, a history of peptic ulcer, blood disorders or a history thereof, a bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

### 🕒 타스멘정(아세트아미노펜) - 用法用量
*   成人每次服用300~1,000毫克，每日3~4次。每日最高剂量不超过4,000毫克。可根据年龄、症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 타스멘정(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上的定期饮酒者，在服用本品或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。本品含有阿斯巴甜，其在体内代谢为苯丙氨酸。苯丙酮尿症患者（需要限制苯丙氨酸摄入者）请勿服用。极罕见情况下，曾报告过可能致命的急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症等严重皮肤反应。服用本品后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状时，请立即停药。每日乙酰氨基酚最大剂量（4,000毫克）请勿超量服用。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
*   对本品过敏患者、消化性溃疡、严重血液异常患者、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、阿司匹林哮喘（非甾体抗炎药诱发的哮喘发作）或有相关病史者、服用巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药、酒精者请勿服用本品。服用本品前，肝功能障碍或有相关病史者、肾功能障碍或有相关病史者、有消化性溃疡病史者、血液异常或有相关病史者、有出血倾向的患者、有心功能异常的患者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药师。
