* 만 8세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 식후에 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 셀비다정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamins D, B1, B2, B6, C, and Zinc in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical decline during illness or recovery, growth period, and geriatric age. It is also used for the poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), relief of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 셀비다정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 셀비다정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, kidney disease, an infant under 12 months of age, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, a patient with or a history of hypersensitivity to soybean oil, a patient with hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child with significant sun exposure and a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, or a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload.

If you are a patient with folic acid deficiency, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders (such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis), or a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsion, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose in various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
