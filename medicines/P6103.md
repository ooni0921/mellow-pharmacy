### 💊 헤파코엔액티브정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after recovery from illness, growth periods, and in old age.
*   Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used to alleviate symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia such as lower back pain or stiff shoulders, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.
*   Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue.
*   This medicine is used for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 헤파코엔액티브정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults 19 years of age or older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 헤파코엔액티브정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybeans, or peanuts; if you have hypersensitivity to or a history of hypersensitivity to soybean oil; if you are an infant under 12 months of age; or if you have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, renal disease, kidney stones, or severe renal insufficiency.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or infant, have hyperoxaluria, gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis (Crohn's disease), cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, have kidney stones or a history thereof, have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, require cautious administration of lipid emulsions, or have conditions related to fat overload.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue this medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with a thrombotic predisposition or folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
