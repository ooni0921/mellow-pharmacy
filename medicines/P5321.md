#EN

### 💊 큐어코연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the alleviation of various cold symptoms such as runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, chills with cold shivering, fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 큐어코연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 큐어코연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. Discontinue this medicine immediately if skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking it.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine or its components, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 2 years of age, an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, have experienced symptoms of ischemic colitis such as sudden abdominal pain or rectal bleeding related to pseudoephedrine, have a history of allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching caused by medicine, are elderly, are frail or have a high fever, or have hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF).

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer, glaucoma, or dysuria; if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; or if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, immediately stop taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

Drowsiness may occur during administration. Avoid driving a car or operating machinery.

Adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
