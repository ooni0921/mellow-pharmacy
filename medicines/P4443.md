* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 2캡슐씩, 1일 1회 또는 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 2회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 바스힐캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins C and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness (convalescence), and in old age. It also helps relieve hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight, skin diseases, etc., prevent gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds), and alleviate symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet (peripheral circulatory disorders and during menopause).

### 🕒 바스힐캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years and older should take 2 capsules once daily, or 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 바스힐캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition in which an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or other fat metabolism disorders; or patients who require careful administration of lipid emulsions; patients for whom special risks are anticipated due to lipid overload; patients with a predisposition to thrombosis; or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 本药用于缓解身体疲劳，妊娠ㆍ哺乳期，病中ㆍ病后（患病期间或恢复后）体力下降时，老年期维生素C、E的补充，以及由阳光、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑），预防牙龈出血、鼻出血（流鼻血），并缓解外周血液循环障碍以及更年期时肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木、手脚冰凉（手脚发凉）的症状。

### 🕒 바스힐캡슐 - 用法及用量
* 12岁以上及成人，一次2粒，一日1次；或一次1粒，一日2次服用。

### ⚠️ 바스힐캡슐 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，以及未满12个月的婴儿，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师，如果您是孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、高草酸尿症患者（尿液中排出过量草酸盐的情况）、胃肠道疾病患者如消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等、心血管系统功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、痛风患者、肾结石患者、高脂蛋白血症患者、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要慎重服用脂质乳剂的患者，或因脂肪过载预计有特殊风险的患者，或有血栓形成倾向的患者，或对日落黄（黄色5号）有过敏史或经历的患者。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
* 本药可能会干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测。
* 如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
