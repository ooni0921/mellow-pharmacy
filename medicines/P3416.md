# EN
### 💊 아이란정75밀리그램(라니티딘염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, and sour belching.

### 🕒 아이란정75밀리그램(라니티딘염산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 16 years and older, take 1 tablet (75 mg) once, up to 2 tablets (150 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 아이란정75밀리그램(라니티딘염산염) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to it. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you experience difficulty swallowing food or pain when swallowing, have symptoms such as vomiting blood, bloody stools, or heartburn that persist for more than 3 months, experience dizziness, cold sweats or lightheadedness, chest or shoulder pain accompanied by shortness of breath, frequent chest pain, unexpected weight loss, nausea, vomiting, gastrointestinal pain, kidney impairment, liver impairment, are elderly, have acute porphyria, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, have chronic lung disease, diabetes, or a weakened immune system. Do not take this medicine for more than 14 days.

# CN

*   本药用于胃酸过多、胃灼热、酸性嗳气。
