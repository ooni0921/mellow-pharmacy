#EN

### 💊 헤모글루탑연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia.
*   This medication is used to supplement vitamins B6 and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and weakness during or after illness.

### 🕒 헤모글루탑연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 8 years of age and older: take 1 capsule once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 헤모글루탑연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Keep out of reach of children. Iron-containing preparations can cause fatal poisoning if accidentally overdosed by children under 6 years of age.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, an infant under 12 months of age, a patient with hemochromatosis (iron deposition in the liver, pancreas, etc. due to iron metabolism abnormalities), hemosiderosis, or non-iron deficiency anemia.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: an infant under 1 year of age, pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiovascular dysfunction, kidney impairment, hyperoxaluria, gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatic abnormalities such as pancreatitis, or patients who require careful administration of lipid emulsions, patients with fat overload, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF) and Red No. 40 (Allura Red AC).
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue this medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

### 🕒 헤모글루탑연질캡슐 - 用法用量

*   对本药品过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、因铁代谢异常导致铁沉积于肝脏、胰腺等的血色素沉着病患者、含铁血黄素沉着症患者、非缺铁性贫血患者请勿服用本药品。
*   在服用本药品前，以下人群应咨询医生或药师：未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、心血管功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、高草酸尿症患者、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、低蛋白血症患者、痛风或肾结石患者、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症、胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需谨慎使用脂质乳剂的患者、脂肪超载相关患者、对黄色5号（日落黄FCF）和红色40号（诱惑红AC）过敏或有相关经历的患者。

*   叶酸缺乏患者请咨询医生或药师。
*   儿童应在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
*   本药品可能会干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
