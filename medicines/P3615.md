#EN
### 💊 히루도이드포르테겔(뮤코폴리사카리드폴리설페이트) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for symptoms caused by varicose veins such as pain, heavy sensation, and lower limb edema, as well as for blunt traumas caused by accidents or sports, including hematoma, contusion (bruise), sprain, swelling, and pain in muscles and ligaments.

### 🕒 히루도이드포르테겔(뮤코폴리사카리드폴리설페이트) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should apply approximately 3 to 5 cm of the gel to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 히루도이드포르테겔(뮤코폴리사카리드폴리설페이트) - Precautions
* No additional information.
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, a history of hypersensitivity reaction to heparin, a bleeding tendency, heparin-induced thrombocytopenia, or if you are a child.
* Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and individuals with a history of propylene glycol hypersensitivity or allergic reactions should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* This medication is for external use only; do not take internally.
* Do not apply to wounds, mucous membranes, around the eyes, or to purulent infected areas.
* Be careful not to get it in your eyes. If it gets into your eyes, rinse immediately with water.
* Do not apply over a wide area.
* Avoid exposing the treated area to sunlight or other light sources.
* Do not use with occlusive dressings.
