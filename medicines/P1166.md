# EN
### 💊 ポスストンG액 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, and decreased physical strength during or after illness, and for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder pain), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 ポスストンG액 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 bottle per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ ポスストンG액 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to its ingredients, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, and infants should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
*   This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow, which may affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking the medicine for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 포스톤G액 - 功效与作用
*   本品用于补充体力疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中或病后体力下降时的维生素B1、B2、B6，以及缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩痛等）、脚气、眼睛疲劳、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮肤炎等症状。
