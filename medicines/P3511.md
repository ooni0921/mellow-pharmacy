# EN

### 💊 엘카르닌정(L-카르니틴) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and for myocardial metabolic disorders caused by ischemic heart diseases such as angina pectoris and acute myocardial infarction.

### 🕒 엘카르닌정(L-카르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 2-3 g daily, divided into 2-3 doses. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘카르닌정(L-카르니틴) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug should not take it. Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult with a doctor or pharmacist.
