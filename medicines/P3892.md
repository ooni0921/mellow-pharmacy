#EN
### 💊 Ichinon Cream - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for itching associated with dry skin (xerosis in adults and the elderly, dry skin in children).

### 🕒 Ichinon Cream - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area once to several times a day.

### ⚠️ Ichinon Cream - Precautions
* Do not use this medication in children under 5 years of age, patients with hemorrhagic blood disorders (hemophilia, thrombocytopenia, purpura), patients for whom even minor bleeding is expected to have serious consequences, or patients with symptoms of atopic dermatitis.
* Do not use on mucous membranes such as around the eyes or lips, on wounds, red and swollen areas, erosions, or severely cracked skin.
* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a hypersensitivity to medicines or cosmetics, or if you have severe weeping or erosion.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after 2 weeks of use, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Be careful not to let it get into your eyes. If it does get into your eyes, rinse immediately with water or lukewarm water.
* For external use only.
* When administering to children, use under the supervision of a guardian.
* Do not touch your eyes or mucous membranes with hands that have applied this product.

* 5歳以下の小児、出血性血液疾患（血友病、血小板減少症、紫斑病）の患者、わずかな出血であっても重篤な結果が予想される患者、アトピー性皮膚炎の症状がある患者は本剤を使用しないでください。

* 小児に使用する場合は、保護者の指導監督のもとに使用させてください。
