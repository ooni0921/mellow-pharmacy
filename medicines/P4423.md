#EN
### 💊 파비시드정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and acid belching.

### 🕒 파비시드정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 15 years of age or older should take 2 tablets at a time. Children aged 8 to under 15 years should take 1 tablet at a time. Take 3 times a day between meals (between mealtimes) and at bedtime. Maintain an interval of at least 4 hours between doses.

### ⚠️ 파비시드정 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients undergoing dialysis and children aged 7 years or younger should not take this medication. Before taking this medication, patients with renal impairment or those on a sodium-restricted diet should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

#CN
### 💊 파비시드정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于胃酸过多、胃灼热（烧心）、胃部不适、胃胀、消化不良、恶心、呕吐、胃痛、酸嗳气。

### 🕒 파비시드정 - 用法与用量
* 15岁及以上成人一次2片，8岁至15岁以下儿童一次1片。一日3次，餐间（两餐之间）及睡前服用。服用间隔应至少4小时。

### ⚠️ 파비시드정 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 正在接受透析治疗的患者及7岁及以下儿童请勿服用本药。在服用本药之前，肾功能障碍患者或正在进行钠限制饮食的人请咨询医生或药剂师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
