# EN
### 💊 이엠지연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for vitamin E supplementation in old age, alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and coldness in hands and feet associated with peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 이엠지연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 12 years of age or older and adults, take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 이엠지연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine or soybean oil, soybeans, peanuts; an infant under 12 months of age; or a patient with severe renal impairment.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are:
*   pregnant or possibly pregnant, or lactating
*   a premature infant or an infant
*   a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis
*   a patient who needs to carefully administer lipid emulsions
*   a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload
*   a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage).

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
