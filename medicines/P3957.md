#EN
### 💊 리액타민연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins B1, B2, B6, D, and zinc in cases of physical fatigue, decreased physical strength during pregnancy/lactation, illness or convalescence (during or after illness), growth period, old age, poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis (inflammation of the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 리액타민연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 capsule at a time, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 리액타민연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you are allergic to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts; an infant under 12 months of age; or have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, or kidney stones.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, patients who need to be carefully administered with lipid emulsions, patients at special risk due to fat overload, patients with folate deficiency, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
