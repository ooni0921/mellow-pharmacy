#EN
### 💊 훼스탈슈퍼자임정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, dyspepsia, promotion of digestion, stomach distension, hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and sour belching.

### 🕒 훼스탈슈퍼자임정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 15 years of age or older: take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, 3 times daily.
*   For children 8 years of age to under 15 years of age: take 1 tablet per dose, 3 times daily.

### ⚠️ 훼스탈슈퍼자임정 - Precautions
*   None.
*   Do not administer this medication to children 7 years of age or younger.

Patients with kidney impairment or allergic predisposition should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
