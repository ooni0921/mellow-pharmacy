#EN
### 💊 타세놀8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Indications & Effects
* This medication is used for fever reduction and the relief of pain associated with colds, headache, toothache, muscle pain, backache, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 타세놀8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage & Administration
* Children aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 2 tablets every 8 hours. Do not exceed 6 tablets in a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 타세놀8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker (three or more alcoholic drinks daily) and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe heart failure, a history of asthma caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, are taking barbiturate-containing drugs or tricyclic antidepressants, have consumed alcohol, or are a child under 12 years of age. Patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, a tendency to bleed, heart dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without consulting a doctor or pharmacist. This medication is an extended-release formulation; therefore, do not crush, chew, or dissolve the tablet, but swallow it whole.

### 🕒 타세놀8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 用法及用量
* 12岁及以上的儿童和成人，每8小时服用2片。24小时内不要超过6片。

* 每日饮酒超过三杯的常饮酒者，在服用本药或其它解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能会引起肝损伤。极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性全身发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征和中毒性表皮坏死松解症。如果服用本药后出现皮疹或其它过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量不应超过最大剂量（4,000 mg），否则可能导致肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其它产品同时服用。
