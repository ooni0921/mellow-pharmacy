#EN
### 💊 마그롤연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness when physical strength is low, and in old age. It also helps relieve symptoms of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness of hands and feet (cold hands and feet), neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그롤연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age and older should take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 마그롤연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or a patient with severe renal failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult with a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant or infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsions, or a patient with a history of or hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF), or a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload.

Adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

For administration to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult with a doctor or pharmacist.
