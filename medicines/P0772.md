체중이 30 kg 미만인 어린이는 1일량이 500 mg을 초과해서는 안되며 공복시 복용은 피하는 것이 바람직합니다.

권장용량을 초과하여 복용하지 마십시오.

조절되지 않는 고혈압, 울혈심부전증, 확립된 허혈성 심장질환, 말초동맥질환, 뇌혈관질환을 가진 환자는 고용량 이부프로펜(1일 2,400 mg) 복용을 피해야 하며, 심혈관계 위험 요소(고혈압, 고지혈증, 당뇨병, 흡연 등)를 가지고 있는 환자는 고용량 이부프로펜(1일 2,400 mg) 복용이 필요한 경우 이 약을 복용하기 전에 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.
* 이 약에 과민증 환자, 위장관궤양, 위장관 출혈 또는 경험자, 심한 혈액이상, 심한 간장애, 심한 신장장애, 심한 심장기능부전, 심한 고혈압, 기관지천식 또는 경험자, 아스피린이나 다른 비스테로이드성 소염진통제(COX-2 저해제 포함)에 천식, 두드러기, 알레르기 반응과 같은 과민 증 또는 경험자, 관상동맥(심장동맥) 우회로술 전후 통증발생 환자, 임신 말기 3개월 기간에 해당하는 임부, 항암요법으로 고용량 메토트렉세이트를 복용중인 환자, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

이 약을 복용하기 전에 혈액이상 또는 경험자, 출혈경향이 있는 환자, 간경화, 간장애 또는 경험자, 신장장애 또는 경험자, 심장기능부전 또는 심질환, 고혈압, 전신성홍반성루푸스 및 혼합 결합조직질환 환자, 궤양성대장염, 크론병, 고령자 및 소아, 임부, 수유부, 황색5호 과민증 환자는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

# EN
### 💊 아루펜정400밀리그람(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to the common cold, lower back pain, dysmenorrhea, postoperative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 아루펜정400밀리그람(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults should take 1/2 to 1.5 tablets (200-600 mg) per dose, 3-4 times a day, with a maximum daily dose of 3200 mg.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, administer 30-40 mg per kg of body weight per day, divided into 3-4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or the common cold, adults should take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) per dose, 3-4 times a day.

For children aged 11-14, administer 200-250 mg per dose; for 7-10 years, 150-200 mg per dose; for 3-6 years, 100-150 mg per dose; and for 1-2 years, 50-100 mg per dose, 3-4 times a day orally.

For children weighing less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid taking the medication on an empty stomach.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

### ⚠️ 아루펜정400밀리그람(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
* Do not take this medication if you have: hypersensitivity to this drug; gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding, or a history thereof; severe blood disorders; severe liver impairment; severe renal impairment; severe cardiac dysfunction; severe hypertension; bronchial asthma or a history thereof; a history of hypersensitivity reactions (such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions) to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors); pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; are pregnant in the last trimester (last 3 months); are taking high-dose methotrexate as chemotherapy; or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have: blood disorders or a history thereof; a bleeding tendency; liver cirrhosis, liver impairment, or a history thereof; renal impairment or a history thereof; cardiac dysfunction or heart disease; hypertension; systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease; ulcerative colitis; Crohn's disease; are elderly or a child; are pregnant; are breastfeeding; or have hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
