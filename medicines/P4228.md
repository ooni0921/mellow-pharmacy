#EN
### 💊 래피콜코프플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 래피콜코프플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older and adults: 2 capsules per dose. For individuals aged 8 years and older but under 15 years: 1 capsule per dose. Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 래피콜코프플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

    Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; if you have an allergic constitution; have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are breastfeeding; or have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

    Do not take continuously for a long period.

    Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
