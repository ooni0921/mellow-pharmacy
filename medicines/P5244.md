#EN
### 💊 히팅플러스정(카페인무수물) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for drowsiness.

### 🕒 히팅플러스정(카페인무수물) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 2 to 6 tablets (100 to 300 mg) orally, 1 to 3 times daily.
    The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 히팅플러스정(카페인무수물) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5), patients with or a history of gastric ulcer, patients with heart disease or glaucoma, women who may be pregnant or are breastfeeding, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
