#EN
### 💊 삼남아세트아미노펜정 - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for fever and pain due to colds, headache, neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia (joint pain), and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 삼남아세트아미노펜정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 300 to 1,000 mg per dose, 3 to 4 times a day. A maximum of 4,000 mg can be administered per day. Dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 삼남아세트아미노펜정 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Rare but potentially fatal severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN) have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe heart dysfunction, or a history of aspirin asthma (asthma attack caused by NSAIDs), or if you are taking barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, or have consumed alcohol. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have had liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding tendency, heart dysfunction, hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, or if you are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

#CN
### 💊 삼남아세트아미노펜정 - 功效与作用
* 此药用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、扭伤痛、牙痛、关节痛以及风湿性疼痛。

### 🕒 삼남아세트아미노펜정 - 用法与用量
* 成人每次服用 300~1,000 毫克，每日 3~4 次。每日最大剂量为 4,000 毫克。可根据年龄和症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 삼남아세트아미노펜정 - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯或以上者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药师。可能导致肝损伤。极罕见报道有严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症，这些反应可能致命。若服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。乙酰氨基酚每日最大剂量（4,000 毫克）不可超量服用。可能导致肝损伤。请勿与含有乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液异常患者、严重肝功能障碍患者、严重肾功能障碍患者、严重心功能低下患者、阿司匹林哮喘（非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘发作）或有相关病史者、服用巴比妥类药物者、三环类抗抑郁药者、饮酒者，请勿服用本药。在服用本药前，肝功能障碍或有相关病史者、肾（脏）功能障碍或有相关病史者、有消化性溃疡病史者、血液异常或有相关病史者、有出血倾向的患者、有心脏功能异常的患者、有过敏史者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。
