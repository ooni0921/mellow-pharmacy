#EN
### 💊 폴락스산4그램(폴리에틸렌글리콜4000) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medicine is used for the treatment of constipation symptoms in children.

### 🕒 폴락스산4그램(폴리에틸렌글리콜4000) - Dosage & Administration
* For infants aged 6 months to 1 year, take 1 sachet (4 g) once daily. For children aged 1 to 4 years, take 1 to 2 sachets (4-8 g) once daily. For children aged 4 to 8 years, take 2 to 4 sachets (8-16 g) once daily. Dissolve each sachet in 50 mL of water before administration. If taking 1 sachet per day, administer in the morning. If taking more than 1 sachet per day, divide the dose into morning and evening administrations. The effect of this medicine typically appears within 1 to 2 days (24-48 hours) after administration.

### ⚠️ 폴락스산4그램(폴리에틸렌글리콜4000) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not administer this medicine to patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, inflammatory bowel disease (such as ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease), toxic megacolon, intestinal perforation, risk of intestinal perforation, intestinal obstruction, suspected intestinal obstruction, intestinal stricture, abdominal pain of unknown cause, or fructose intolerance. Before administering this medicine, patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist if they are children under 2 years of age, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, patients with acute constipation not due to lifestyle changes, acute constipation accompanied by pain, fever, or abdominal distension, patients with irritable bowel syndrome, or patients with hypersensitivity to macrogol (polyethylene glycol) or sulfur dioxide.

#CN
### 💊 폴락스산4그램(폴리에틸렌글리콜4000) - 功效及效果
* 该药用于治疗儿童便秘症状。

### 🕒 폴락스산4그램(폴리에틸렌글리콜4000) - 用法及用量
* 6个月至1岁的婴儿，每日服用1袋（4克）；1至4岁的儿童，每日服用1至2袋（4-8克）；4至8岁的儿童，每日服用2至4袋（8-16克）。每袋溶解于50毫升水中服用。如果每日服用1袋，应在早晨服用；如果每日服用超过1袋，则应分早晚两次服用。本药的效果通常在服药后1至2天（24-48小时）内显现。

### ⚠️ 폴락스산4그램(폴리에틸렌글리콜4000) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者，患有炎症性肠病（如溃疡性结肠炎、克罗恩病等）、中毒性巨结肠、肠穿孔、有肠穿孔风险的患者，肠梗阻、疑似肠梗阻的患者，肠狭窄、原因不明的腹痛患者，以及果糖不耐受的患者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，2岁以下的儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期女性、非生活习惯改变引起的急性便秘、伴有疼痛、发热、腹胀的急性便秘患者、肠易激综合征患者，以及对马可罗（聚乙二醇）、二氧化硫过敏的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

* 生後6ヶ月から満1歳までは1日1包（4 g）、満1歳から4歳までは1日1～2包（4～8 g）、満4歳から8歳までは1日2～4包（8～16 g）を服用します。1包あたり50 mLの水に溶かして服用してください。1日1包を服用する場合は朝に服用し、1日1包を超える量を服用する場合は朝と夕方に分けて服用します。本剤の効果は、服用後1～2日（24～48時間）以内に現れます。
