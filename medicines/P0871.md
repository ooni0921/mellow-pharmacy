#EN
### 💊 쎌스톤크림 - Efficacy and Effect
* This medicine is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin diseases (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis), and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 쎌스톤크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 쎌스톤크림 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or bacitracin; or if you have skin infections (tuberculosis, syphilis, candidiasis, tinea, herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia, scabies, pubic lice); eczematous otitis externa with tympanic membrane perforation; ulcers (except Behçet's disease); second-degree deep burns or higher, or frostbite; perioral dermatitis; acne vulgaris; or rosacea.
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, an infant or child, or if you have a history of or hypersensitivity to propylene glycol.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* For administration to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
* Do not use for purposes other than treatment after applying makeup or shaving.
* Do not use occlusive dressings without medical supervision.

#CN
### 💊 쎌스톤크림 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于继发感染的过敏性或炎症性皮肤病（湿疹、接触性皮炎、脂溢性皮炎、特应性皮炎、光变应性皮炎、慢性单纯苔藓、擦烂、剥脱性皮炎、瘙痒、银屑病），以及一度烧伤。

### 🕒 쎌스톤크림 - 用法用量
* 每日1~3次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ 쎌스톤크림 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 对本品或链霉素、卡那霉素、庆大霉素、新霉素等氨基糖苷类抗生素或杆菌肽过敏的患者，有皮肤感染症（结核、梅毒、念珠菌病、癣、带状疱疹、单纯疱疹、水痘、牛痘、疥疮、阴虱），鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳道炎，溃疡（白塞病除外），二度深层以上烧伤及冻伤，口周皮炎，寻常痤疮，酒渣鼻患者请勿使用本药。
* 在使用本药之前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、婴幼儿、对丙二醇有过敏史或过敏者请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
* 儿童用药请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
* 请勿用于眼科用途。
* 请勿在化妆或剃须后用于治疗以外的目的。
* 未经医生指导，请勿使用密封敷料法。
