#EN
### 💊 SsenBQMT-Jung - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, for physical weakness during or after illness, for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in old age, for the relief of peripheral circulatory disorders and climacteric symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands/feet, for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (lumbago, shoulder stiffness, etc.), for beriberi, eye fatigue, and for the relief of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 SsenBQMT-Jung - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ SsenBQMT-Jung - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity to soybeans or peanuts, individuals with a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, patients with severe renal failure, and infants under 12 months of age should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, premature infants, infants, patients with cardiac/circulatory dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or patients who must be carefully administered lipid emulsions, or patients at special risk due to fat overload should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow, affecting clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
