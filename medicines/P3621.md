#EN
### 💊 치아탄캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the adjunctive treatment of gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 치아탄캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 치아탄캡슐 - Precautions
* No information.
* Children under 15 years of age should not take this medicine. Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, those with a history of hypersensitivity (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to cosmetics or eggs, and individuals with an allergic constitution should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
