# EN
### 💊 뉴란스플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is low during or after illness, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis. Additionally, it helps relieve beriberi, eye fatigue, pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, and is used for the prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds), as well as for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 뉴란스플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 2 tablets once daily, or 1 tablet twice daily.

### ⚠️ 뉴란스플러스정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant/young child, or a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, a thrombotic predisposition, folic acid deficiency, or hypersensitivity to or a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, which could affect clinical test values.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 뉴란스플러스정 - 功效与作用
*   本药品用于补充因体力疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中或病后体力下降以及老年期所需的维生素 E、B1、B2、B6、C。同时，它还用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。此外，它还可缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳、由阳光或皮肤病引起的色素沉着（如黄褐斑、雀斑），以及预防牙龈出血、鼻出血（鼻血）和补充锌。

### 🕒 뉴란스플러스정 - 用法与用量
*   12岁以上及成人，每次2片，每日1次；或每次1片，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 뉴란스플러스정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本品过敏的患者以及未满12个月的婴儿请勿服用本品。
*   服用本品前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症患者（尿液中草酸盐排出过多的状态）、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心血管功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、痛风患者、肾结石患者、有血栓形成倾向的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者、对黄色5号过敏或有相关过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下进行。
*   本品可能会干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
