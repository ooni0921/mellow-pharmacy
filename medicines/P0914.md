* 1일 1~3회 환부(질환분위)에 적당량을 바릅니다.

### 💊 세스틴크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin diseases (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis), and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 세스틴크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area (diseased site) 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 세스틴크림 - Precautions
* Do not use this medicine if you have:
    * Hypersensitivity to this medicine, or a history of hypersensitivity to aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or bacitracin.

    * Ulcers (except Behcet's disease).

* Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
