#EN

### 💊 맥스타민콘드로800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is low during or after recovery from illness, during growth periods, and in old age.
*   Through its contained vitamins, this medicine is used to relieve symptoms of joint pain such as neuralgia, myalgia, lower back pain, or stiff shoulders, as well as for beriberi and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 맥스타민콘드로800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years or older should take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 맥스타민콘드로800정 - Precautions
*   No specific information provided.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, infants under 3 months of age, patients with hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.
*   Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, premature infants, infants, and individuals with a history of kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use of this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folate deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and may affect clinical test results.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
