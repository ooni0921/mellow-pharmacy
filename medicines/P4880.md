#EN

### 💊 메가트루맥스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, alleviation of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, and for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 메가트루맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 메가트루맥스정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, or have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment (kidney dysfunction), hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and may affect clinical test results.
