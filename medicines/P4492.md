#EN
### 💊 리놀에스트로키(플루르비프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the short-term symptomatic relief of sore throat.

### 🕒 리놀에스트로키(플루르비프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults, children aged 12 years and over, and adolescents should slowly dissolve one lozenge in the mouth.

To avoid local irritation, move the lozenge around in the mouth while dissolving.

If needed, take one lozenge every 3 to 6 hours, up to a maximum of 5 lozenges per day, for a maximum of 3 days.

### ⚠️ 리놀에스트로키(플루르비프로펜) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, aspirin, or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); peptic ulcer, bleeding; asthma (including asthmatic attacks), urticaria, bronchospasm, rhinitis caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, or a history thereof; severe cardiac insufficiency; gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation caused by NSAIDs or antirheumatic drugs; severe colitis; severe blood disorders; unexplained hematopoietic disorders; severe liver impairment; severe kidney impairment; if you are pregnant in the last trimester (final 3 months); if you are breastfeeding; if you have hereditary fructose intolerance, glucose-galactose malabsorption, or sucrose-isomaltase deficiency; or if you are a child under 12 years of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma or a history thereof; cardiac insufficiency; kidney or liver impairment; hypertension; blood disorders or a history thereof; a tendency to bleed; if you are elderly; if you have peripheral arterial occlusive disease, cerebrovascular disease, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, or are a smoker; if you have hay fever, nasal polyps, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease; if you are pregnant in the first or second trimester; if you have fluid retention or heart failure.

Dizziness and visual disturbances may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
