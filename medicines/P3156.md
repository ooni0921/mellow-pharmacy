#EN
### 💊 마그엔지연질캡슐 - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (convalescence), and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet) during menopause, neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain) (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, oral stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis. Additionally, it is used for beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그엔지연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adolescents aged 12 years and older and adults: Take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 마그엔지연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal insufficiency.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory dysfunction, renal (kidney) impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or a patient who requires cautious administration of lipid emulsions, a patient for whom a special risk due to fat overload is anticipated, a patient with a thrombogenic predisposition (blood clot in vessels), or a patient with hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) or those with a history of such.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
