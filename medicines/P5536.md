#EN
### 💊 노바멜라인장용정100밀리그램(브로멜라인) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of inflammatory symptoms accompanied by swelling and edema after injury or surgery.

### 🕒 노바멜라인장용정100밀리그램(브로멜라인) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age and older should take 1 tablet once, twice daily, 30 minutes before meals, with water.

### ⚠️ 노바멜라인장용정100밀리그램(브로멜라인) - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medication if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, a child under 12 years of age, have severe blood coagulation disorders such as hemophilia, severe liver disease, severe kidney disease such as in hemodialysis patients, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are sensitive to or have a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
