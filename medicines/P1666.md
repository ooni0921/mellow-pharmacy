# EN
### 💊 카비드츄어블정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to supplement Vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, to prevent poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, and to supplement calcium in cases of calcium deficiency and other calcium needs.

### 🕒 카비드츄어블정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 8 years of age or older: take 1-2 tablets once daily. Children aged 36 months to under 8 years: take 1/2-1 tablet once daily. Dissolve or chew in the mouth.

### ⚠️ 카비드츄어블정 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or severe renal insufficiency. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
