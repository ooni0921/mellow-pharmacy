#EN
### 💊 종근당조인트콘드로정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical debility during illness or convalescence (during or after recovery from illness), developmental stage, and old age.
*   This medicine, through its contained vitamins, is used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, as well as for the prevention of rickets and stunted bone/tooth development, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 종근당조인트콘드로정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 종근당조인트콘드로정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient undergoing medical treatment, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with renal impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient currently taking cardiac glycosides, or a patient with a history of kidney stones.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow, which may affect clinical test results.
