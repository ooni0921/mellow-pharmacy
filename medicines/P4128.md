#EN
### 💊 피록스겔(피록시캄) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for analgesic and anti-inflammatory relief of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, humeral epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia (e.g., myositis, myofascitis), and swelling/pain after trauma.

### 🕒 피록스겔(피록시캄) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should apply approximately 3 cm (1 g) thinly to the affected area 3-4 times a day and gently rub until well absorbed.

### ⚠️ 피록스겔(피록시캄) - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not use this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug or piroxicam preparations, have or have experienced aspirin asthma (asthma attacks caused by NSAIDs, etc.), are a child under 14 years of age, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or are a lactating woman. Before using this medication, patients with bronchial asthma and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not use on lesional skin (hemorrhagic skin diseases, eczema, infected lesions, burns, wounds), eyes, mucous membranes, etc. Do not use occlusive dressings.

* 本药用于缓解退行性关节炎（骨关节炎）、肩周炎、肌腱炎/腱鞘炎、腱周炎、肱骨外上髁炎（如网球肘）、肌肉痛（如肌炎、筋膜炎）以及创伤后肿胀、疼痛的镇痛和消炎。

* 成人每日3~4次，每次约3厘米（1克）薄薄地涂抹于患处，并轻轻揉搓使其充分吸收。

### ⚠️ 피록스겔(피록시캄) - 注意事项
* 无可用信息
* 对本药或吡罗昔康制剂过敏的患者、阿司匹林哮喘（因非甾体抗炎药等引起的哮喘发作）患者或有相关病史者、14岁以下的儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女请勿使用本药。在使用本药前，支气管哮喘患者和老年人应咨询医生或药师。请勿用于病变皮肤（出血性皮肤病、湿疹、感染病变、烧伤、创伤）、眼睛和粘膜部位等。请勿使用密封敷料法。
