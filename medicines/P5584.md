#EN
### 💊 Selen Vita Tab. - Indications & Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamin D in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and during illness or physical debility after recovery.
*   This medicine is used to supplement zinc and selenium.

### 🕒 Selen Vita Tab. - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults aged 12 years or older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ Selen Vita Tab. - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, patients with cardiovascular dysfunction, kidney impairment, or hypoproteinemia should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue this medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
