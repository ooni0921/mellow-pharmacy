* 정보 없음

이 약을 투여 후 투약 전과 신체 기능에 변화가 있는 경우 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.
증상의 개선이 없을 경우 복용을 즉각 중지하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.
이 약은 14일 이상 복용하지 마십시오.

##EN
### 💊 Famotop Tab. (Famotidine) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for excessive stomach acid, heartburn, and acid indigestion (sour burping).

### 🕒 Famotop Tab. (Famotidine) - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 16 years and older, take 1 tablet (10 mg) once, up to 2 tablets (20 mg) daily.

### ⚠️ Famotop Tab. (Famotidine) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication or H2 receptor antagonists, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you experience difficulty or pain when swallowing food, vomiting blood (hematemesis) or bloody stools, heartburn symptoms lasting more than 3 months, dizziness or drowsiness accompanied by cold sweats, chest or shoulder pain accompanied by shortness of breath, frequent chest pain, unexpected weight loss, nausea, vomiting, gastrointestinal pain, have a history of hypersensitivity to medications, have kidney impairment, heart disease, liver impairment, are elderly, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, or are undergoing hemodialysis.
After administering this medication, if there are changes in your physical function compared to before administration, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
If symptoms do not improve, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
Do not take this medication for more than 14 days.

##CN
### 💊 파모탑정(파모티딘) - 功效及效果
* 本药用于胃酸过多、胃灼热、酸性嗳气。

### 🕒 파모탑정(파모티딘) - 用法及用量
* 16岁以上者可一次服用1片（10毫克），每日最多服用2片（20毫克）。

### ⚠️ 파모탑정(파모티딘) - 注意事项
* 没有信息
* 对本药及H2受体拮抗剂过敏者，或有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如果您出现吞咽困难或吞咽时疼痛，呕血或血便，胃灼热症状持续3个月以上，伴有头晕或嗜睡、盗汗，伴有呼吸急促的胸部或肩部疼痛，频繁出现胸痛，意外体重减轻，恶心、呕吐、胃肠道疼痛等症状，有过药物过敏史，有肾功能障碍、心脏病、肝功能障碍，老年人，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，血液透析患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
服用本药后，如果身体机能与服药前发生变化，请咨询医生或药剂师。
如果症状没有改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
请勿连续服用本药超过14天。
