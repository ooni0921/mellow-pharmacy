#EN

### 💊 맥스케어액트정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical decline during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of bone and teeth maldevelopment and rickets, and for the relief of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis, as well as for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 맥스케어액트정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 맥스케어액트정 - Precautions
* Not available
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, premature infant, infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a history of kidney stones.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
