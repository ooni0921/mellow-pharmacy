* 만 15세 이상은 1회 24 mL씩, 만 11세 이상~만 15세 미만은 1회 16 mL씩, 만 7세 이상~만 11세 미만은 1회 12 mL씩, 만 3세 이상~만 7세 미만은 1회 8 mL씩, 만 2세 이상~만 3세 미만은 1회 6 mL씩, 1일 4회 매 식후 및 필요한 경우에는 취침 전에 복용합니다.

필요에 따라 약 4시간마다 1일 6회까지 복용할 수 있습니다.

#EN
### 💊 베비맥시럽(딸기향,포도향) - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, watery eyes, sore throat, and heavy head due to common cold (acute rhinitis), allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 베비맥시럽(딸기향,포도향) - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older: 24 mL per dose.
*   For individuals aged 11 years and older but under 15 years: 16 mL per dose.
*   For individuals aged 7 years and older but under 11 years: 12 mL per dose.
*   For individuals aged 3 years and older but under 7 years: 8 mL per dose.
*   For individuals aged 2 years and older but under 3 years: 6 mL per dose.
*   Take 4 times a day, after each meal and, if necessary, before bedtime.

If needed, it can be taken up to 6 times a day, approximately every 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 베비맥시럽(딸기향,포도향) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not administer this medication to children under 2 years of age, or to individuals who are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have discontinued them within the past 2 weeks.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you: have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have high blood pressure, heart disease, kidney disease, edema, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), dysuria, thyroid disease, diabetes, are physically weak or have a high fever, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, are elderly, or if you have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts longer than a week or recurs, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

*   情報なし。
