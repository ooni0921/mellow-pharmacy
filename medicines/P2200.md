#EN
### 💊 이젠이캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the adjunctive treatment of gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 이젠이캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 이젠이캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not administer this medication to children under 15 years of age. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to medications, cosmetics, or eggs, or if you have an allergic constitution. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after taking the medication for more than one month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
