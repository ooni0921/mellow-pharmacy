#EN
### 💊 제감노즈캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 제감노즈캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, take 2 capsules once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 제감노즈캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

In very rare cases, serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of a hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant younger than 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, an infant or toddler under 2 years of age, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, elderly (seniors), frail, or have high fever, allergic constitution, a history of allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, edema (swelling), gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), dysuria (difficulty urinating), heartburn, gastric discomfort, stomach pain and other gastrointestinal problems, ulcers, bleeding problems, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, a cough accompanied by fever, rash or persistent headache, or have a history of or current hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 5 to 6 doses, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
