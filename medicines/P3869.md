### 💊 타세놀8시간이알서방정325밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of fever and pain caused by colds, headaches, muscle aches, menstrual pain, toothaches, lower back pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 타세놀8시간이알서방정325밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years and older and adults should take 4 tablets every 8 hours. Do not exceed 12 tablets in 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 타세놀8시간이알서방정325밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* If you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily, consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this product with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (induced by NSAIDs) or history thereof, those who have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, alcohol, or patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. This medicine is an extended-release formulation, so the tablets should be swallowed whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving.
