# EN
### 💊 하원아시클로버크림(수출명:하시클로크림) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for herpes simplex virus infections, including initial and recurrent genital herpes and herpes labialis.

### 🕒 하원아시클로버크림(수출명:하시클로크림) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply to the affected area 5 times a day, approximately every 4 hours. Treatment should be continued for 5 days, and if the condition does not improve after 5 days of use, it may be used for an additional 5 days.

### ⚠️ 하원아시클로버크림(수출명:하시클로크림) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, are a nursing mother, or are a child. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an immunocompromised patient (e.g., AIDS, bone marrow transplant), pregnant or potentially pregnant, or have experienced hypersensitivity to propylene glycol. Start treatment as early as possible; for recurrent infections, begin at the first sign of prodromal symptoms or lesions. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not use this medication for preventing transmission to others or for preventing recurrent herpes simplex virus infections. Do not dilute this medication or use it as a base to mix with other medications. This medication is for external use only. Do not use for ophthalmic purposes on the cornea or mucous membranes. To prevent the spread of this medication to other parts of the body or transmission to others, apply with a thimble or rubber glove.

*   本药用于治疗单纯疱疹病毒感染（包括原发性和复发性生殖器疱疹及口唇疱疹）。

### 🕒 하원아시클로버크림(수출명:하시클로크림) - 用法用量
*   每日5次，每次间隔约4小时，涂抹于患处。治疗应持续5天，如果使用5天后仍未痊愈，可继续使用5天。

### ⚠️ 하원아시클로버크림(수출명:하시클로크림) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏者、哺乳期妇女和儿童请勿使用本药。在使用本药前，艾滋病患者或骨髓移植等免疫缺陷患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、对丙二醇有过敏症或曾有经验者，请咨询医生或药剂师。治疗应尽早开始，如果是复发性，请在前驱症状或病变首次出现时开始。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药不应用于预防传染给他人或预防复发性单纯疱疹病毒感染。请勿稀释本药或将其用作与其他药物混合的基质。本药仅供外用。请勿用于眼科，勿用于角膜、粘膜。为防止本药扩散到身体其他部位或传染给他人，请戴指套或橡胶手套涂抹。
