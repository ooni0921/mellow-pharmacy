# EN
### 💊 플러비카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for analgesia and anti-inflammatory action in conditions such as osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, humeral epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia (muscle pain), and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 플러비카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Remove the release liner from the patch and apply it to the affected area twice daily.
*   Please use after wiping sweat from the application site.

### ⚠️ 플러비카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, or if you are a patient with or have a history of aspirin-induced asthma (asthma attacks induced by NSAIDs, etc.).
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with bronchial asthma, a patient with or history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, a pregnant woman, a child, an elderly person, have inflammation with infection, or suffer from chronic diseases.
*   Do not use on damaged skin and mucous membranes, eczema, or rash sites.
*   Please use after wiping sweat from the application site.

# CN
### 💊 플러비카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - 功效与作用
*   本药品用于缓解和减轻以下症状的疼痛和炎症：骨关节炎（退行性关节炎）、肩周炎、腱鞘炎/肌腱炎、肱骨外上髁炎（如网球肘）、肌肉疼痛以及外伤后的肿胀和疼痛。

### 🕒 플러비카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - 用法与用量
*   每日两次，撕下药膏表面的离型纸，贴于患处。
*   请在使用前擦干患处的汗水。

### ⚠️ 플러비카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药品过敏的患者，或患有阿司匹林哮喘（由非甾体抗炎药等引起的哮喘发作）或有相关病史的患者，请勿使用本药品。
*   在使用本药品前，支气管哮喘患者、对丙二醇过敏或有相关病史的患者、孕妇、儿童、老年人、伴有感染的炎症患者以及慢性疾病患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请勿用于受损的皮肤、粘膜、湿疹或皮疹部位。
*   请在使用前擦干患处的汗水。
