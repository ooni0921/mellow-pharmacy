# EN
### 💊 파라멜액(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used as an auxiliary treatment for detoxification in severe liver diseases such as liver cirrhosis.

### 🕒 파라멜액(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
*   Take 2 sachets (6 g) twice a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 파라멜액(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴) - Precautions
*   Patients with severe renal impairment (serum creatinine level 3 mg/100 mL or higher) should not take this drug.
*   Before taking this drug, patients with hypersensitivity to acetylsalicylic acid or Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF), or those with a history of such hypersensitivity, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, discontinue the drug and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 파라멜액(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴) - 功效与作用
*   本品用于肝硬化等重症肝病的解毒辅助治疗。

### 🕒 파라멜액(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴) - 用法用量
*   每日2次，每次2袋（6克），饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 파라멜액(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴) - 注意事项
*   严重肾功能不全患者（血清肌酐水平≥3 mg/100 mL）不得服用本品。
*   在服用本品前，对乙酰水杨酸或柠檬黄（Yellow No. 5）过敏或有相关过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
