# EN
### 💊 헬씨큐플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, loss of physical strength during or after illness (during illness or recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the relief of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, relief of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness and coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet) due to peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause, relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, and arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), beriberi, eye fatigue, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 헬씨큐플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 8 years of age or older should take 1 capsule once a day.

### ⚠️ 헬씨큐플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day may cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medicine if you are within 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, hyperkalemia, Wilson's disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, biliary obstruction, copper deficiency, acute or chronic bronchitis, pulmonary edema, alkalosis, peptic ulcer, diabetes, gout, or kidney stones (or have a history of them), a patient with a thrombotic predisposition, a patient with folic acid deficiency, a patient with abnormal lipid metabolism such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs to be administered lipid emulsions cautiously, a patient for whom a special risk is expected due to fat overload, or a patient with hypersensitivity to or a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.
* It may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
