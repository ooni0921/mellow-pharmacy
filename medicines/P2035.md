#EN
### 💊 엑스프리벤정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, B6, and C when experiencing physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, or during and after illness (during illness or after recovery) when physical strength is low. It is also used to alleviate neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and hyperpigmentation (e.g., melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases. Furthermore, it is used for the prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds) and for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 엑스프리벤정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 2 tablets once daily, or 1 tablet twice daily.

### ⚠️ 엑스프리벤정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to any component of this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have severe renal failure, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant or infant, have heart and circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, diabetes, gout, kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 엑스프리벤정 - 用法用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每次2片，每日1次；或每次1片，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 엑스프리벤정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、重症肾功能不全患者、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，如果您患有高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、心脏·循环系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病、糖尿病、痛风、肾结石、叶酸缺乏，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药在进行各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液呈黄色，从而影响临床检查值。
*   如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
