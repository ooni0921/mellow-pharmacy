#EN
### 💊 겔티-포르테연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, relief of dry eye sensation, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), beriberi, eye fatigue, poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, and for the relief of symptoms such as stiff shoulders/neck, numbness in hands/feet, and cold hands/feet (cold hands and feet) during peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause.

### 🕒 겔티-포르테연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 겔티-포르테연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 3 months old, or if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), or kidney stones.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, or a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gout, kidney stones or a history thereof, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or patients who should be administered lipid emulsions cautiously, patients with a predisposition to thrombosis (blood vessel blockage), patients with folic acid deficiency, patients for whom special risks are anticipated due to fat overload, or patients with hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4) and Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) or a history thereof.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

May interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests, and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
