# EN
### 💊 벤포맥스100정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supply of Vitamins B1, B2, B6, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and during or after illness when physical strength is depleted. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., low back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 벤포맥스100정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 12 years or older, take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 벤포맥스100정 - Precautions
* No information provided
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to it or if you are an infant under 12 months of age. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant, are an infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or kidney stones, or have folic acid deficiency. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist. This medication may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may cause urine to turn yellow, affecting clinical test values.

# CN
### 💊 벤포맥스100정 - 功效与作用
* 本药物用于补充体力疲劳、妊娠及哺乳期、病中或病后体力下降时的维生素B1、B2、B6、C，并缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状，以及补充脚气病、眼睛疲劳和锌。

### 🕒 벤포맥스100정 - 用法与用量
* 12岁及以上成人，每次1片，每日1次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 벤포맥스100정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药物过敏的患者，以及未满12个月的婴儿请勿服用本药物。在服用本药物前，如有高草酸尿症、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病、心血管循环系统功能障碍、肾脏功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风、肾结石的患者，以及叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。如果服用约1个月后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。在进行各种尿液检查时，本药物可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能导致尿液呈黄色，影响临床检测值。
