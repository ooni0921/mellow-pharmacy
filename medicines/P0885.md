#EN
### 💊 안티비오캡슐300mg(락토바실루스아시도필루스균)(수출용) - Efficacy & Effects
* This medicine is used for bowel regulation, constipation, loose stools, abdominal bloating, and abnormal fermentation in the intestines.

### 🕒 안티비오캡슐300mg(락토바실루스아시도필루스균)(수출용) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age or older should take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 1 to 3 times a day. Children 8 years of age or older but under 12 years of age should take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 1 to 2 times a day.

### ⚠️ 안티비오캡슐300mg(락토바실루스아시도필루스균)(수출용) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with compromised immune systems (Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome (AIDS), leukemia, malignant tumors, organ transplants, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, or after long-term high-dose steroid treatment), children under 1 year of age with high fever or bloody diarrhea, and patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine. Patients with Yellow No. 5 (tartrazine) hypersensitivity should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
