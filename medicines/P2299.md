# EN
### 💊 덱스쿨연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjuvant treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful edema or inflammation following trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 덱스쿨연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule (300 mg) at a time, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 capsules (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 덱스쿨연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, consult your doctor or pharmacist. This may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, a history of asthma, hives, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), gastrointestinal ulcers, bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular, or other sites, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are in the last trimester of pregnancy (6 months or more), have inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation.

Before taking this medication, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of blood disorders, a tendency to bleed, hepatic impairment, renal impairment, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, a child aged 6 years or older, have stomach cancer, alcoholism, are in the first or second trimester of pregnancy, are a woman who may be pregnant, have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, have risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), have hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss, have blood coagulation disorders, or have a history of gastrointestinal ulcers caused by previous non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

For acute conditions, long-term use should generally be limited to within 5 days.

# CN
### 💊 덱스쿨연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于辅助治疗慢性多发性关节炎、类风湿性关节炎、关节病、强直性脊柱炎、外伤及术后疼痛性水肿或炎症，以及伴有炎症、疼痛和发热的感染。

### 🕒 덱스쿨연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 用法与用量
* 成人每次服用1粒（300毫克），每日2~4次。但是，每日服用量不得超过4粒（1,200毫克）。

### ⚠️ 덱스쿨연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 每日定期饮酒三杯以上者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能会引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药可能会增加致命性严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死和中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
* 对本药过敏者，对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药有哮喘、荨麻疹或过敏反应史者，有胃肠道溃疡、胃肠道或脑血管或其他部位出血、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、严重高血压、支气管哮喘病史或正在患有者，冠状动脉（心脏动脉）旁路移植术（CABG）前后发生疼痛者，怀孕6个月以上的孕妇，克罗恩病或溃疡性结肠炎等炎症性肠病患者，有胃肠道出血或穿孔史者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如果您有血液异常或病史，有出血倾向，有肝功能障碍或病史，有肾功能障碍或病史，有体液潴留或心力衰竭，高血压，全身性红斑狼疮（SLE）和混合性结缔组织病（MCTD），老年人，6岁及以上儿童，胃癌，酒精中毒患者，妊娠早期或中期的孕妇，可能怀孕的女性，缺血性心脏病、外周动脉疾病、脑血管疾病患者，有心血管疾病风险因素的患者（例如：高血压、高脂血症、糖尿病、吸烟），有肝性卟啉症的患者，接受过导致失血的重大外科手术的患者，有血液凝固障碍，或有既往非甾体抗炎药引起的消化道溃疡史，请咨询医生或药师。

服用本药后可能会出现头晕或疲劳，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

对于急性疾病，长期服用原则上应在5天以内。
