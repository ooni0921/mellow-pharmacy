#EN
### 💊 셀디아정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, as well as for the prevention of bone and tooth developmental disorders and rickets.

### 🕒 셀디아정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 셀디아정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or have or have experienced kidney stones.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
