#EN
### 💊 페노크린카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of pain and inflammation in conditions such as osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis · tenosynovitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia, and post-traumatic swelling · pain.

### 🕒 페노크린카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply to the affected area twice daily after removing the release liner from the patch.

*   Wipe away sweat from the application site before use.

### ⚠️ 페노크린카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or a history of it, a history of asthma when using aspirin or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), or on broken skin, mucous membranes, eczema, or rashes.

*   Before using this medicine, patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant women, children, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   Wipe away sweat from the application site before use.

#CN

*   每日2次，撕下药贴表面的剥离纸，贴于患处。

*   使用前请擦干涂抹部位的汗水。

### ⚠️ 페노크린카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本品过敏或有过敏史的患者、使用阿司匹林或非甾体抗炎镇痛药时曾有哮喘病史的患者、以及皮肤和粘膜受损、湿疹或皮疹部位，请勿使用本品。

*   使用本品前，支气管哮喘患者、孕妇、儿童和老年人应咨询医生或药剂师。
