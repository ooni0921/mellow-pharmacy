#EN
### 💊 디페나엘플라스타(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder (shoulder joint periarthritis), tendinitis (tendon inflammation), tenosynovitis (tendon sheath inflammation), peritendinitis (inflammation around a tendon), humeral epicondylitis (epicondylitis of the upper arm bone; e.g., tennis elbow), post-traumatic swelling and pain (swelling and pain after injury; e.g., contusions, bruises, sprains), and muscle pain.

### 🕒 디페나엘플라스타(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 디페나엘플라스타(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication in the third trimester of pregnancy and late-stage pregnancy, individuals with a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, or patients with a history of aspirin asthma.

Before using this medication, pregnant women (first and second trimesters) or women who may be pregnant, women having difficulty conceiving or undergoing infertility treatment, breastfeeding mothers, children, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Before using this medication, patients with bronchial asthma, or those using it for inflammation accompanied by infection and chronic diseases (e.g., osteoarthritis), should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not use on lesioned skin (exudative dermatitis, eczema, infected lesions, burns, wounds, etc.), eyes, or mucous membranes.
