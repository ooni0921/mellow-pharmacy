#EN
### 💊 나인나인정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical decline during or after illness, growth period, and old age. It also helps relieve dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), impaired bone and teeth development, prevent rickets, and prevent and treat iron-deficiency anemia.

### 🕒 나인나인정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years or older: Take 1 tablet once daily, after a meal.

### ⚠️ 나인나인정 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day can cause congenital malformations. Women who are within 3 months of pregnancy or who may be pregnant should not take this medicine. Overdosing by children under 6 years of age can lead to fatal iron poisoning; keep out of reach of children.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, have hyperkalemia, Wilson's disease, hemochromatosis, hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, biliary obstruction, copper deficiency, acute/chronic bronchitis, pulmonary edema, alkalosis, peptic ulcer, diabetes, gout, a history of kidney stones, folic acid deficiency, or thrombotic predisposition.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   The Vitamin A contained in this medicine is adequately supplied by a normal diet, so the dosage for supplementary therapy should not exceed 5,000 IU per day.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow, affect clinical test values, or interfere with the detection of glucose in urine tests.
