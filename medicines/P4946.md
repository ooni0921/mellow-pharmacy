### 💊 WIENTAC-JUNG - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, loss of appetite (anorexia), dyspepsia, overeating, aiding digestion, and stomach bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 WIENTAC-JUNG - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ WIENTAC-JUNG - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a child aged 7 years or younger, a patient undergoing dialysis, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, patients with renal impairment, individuals prone to allergies, or those on a sodium-restricted diet should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
