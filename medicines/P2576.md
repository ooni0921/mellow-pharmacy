### 🕒 인코라민스페셜정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 인코라민스페셜정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or genetic problems such as glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalate is excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or a history of kidney stones, or folate deficiency.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

It may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking the medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 인코라민스페셜정 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 对本药过敏者、12个月以下婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高）、结节病（原因不明的全身炎症性疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石、症状严重的肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如果您是孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐）、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心血管功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风或有肾结石病史者、叶酸缺乏症患者，请咨询医生或药师。

本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检测结果。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
