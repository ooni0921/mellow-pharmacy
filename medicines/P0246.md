# EN
### 💊 신일이부프로펜정400밀리그람(수출명:IFENTab.) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to common cold, back pain, dysmenorrhea, postoperative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, strains), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 신일이부프로펜정400밀리그람(수출명:IFENTab.) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 1/2 to 1.5 tablets (200-600 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day, with a maximum daily dose of 3200 mg. For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or common cold, adults take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day. Children aged 11-14 take 200-250 mg once, 7-10 years take 150-200 mg once, 3-6 years take 100-150 mg once, and 1-2 years take 50-100 mg once, orally 3 to 4 times a day. Children weighing less than 30 kg should not exceed a daily dose of 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid taking it on an empty stomach. Do not exceed the recommended dose.

### ⚠️ 신일이부프로펜정400밀리그람(수출명:IFENTab.) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, a history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors), patients experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, pregnant women in the last trimester of pregnancy, patients taking high-dose methotrexate as part of chemotherapy, or patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders or a history thereof, a tendency to bleed, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction or a history thereof, kidney dysfunction or a history thereof, cardiac insufficiency or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, elderly patients, children, pregnant women, lactating women, or patients with hypersensitivity to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
