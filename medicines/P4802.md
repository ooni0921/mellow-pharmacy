#EN

### 💊 디큐펜프로나이트연질캡슐 - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medication is used for acute nocturnal pain accompanied by temporary insomnia due to pain.

### 🕒 디큐펜프로나이트연질캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults: When experiencing difficulty sleeping due to pain, take 1 to 2 capsules once daily at bedtime.

Do not exceed 1,200 mg of ibuprofen and 75 mg of diphenhydramine in a 24-hour period, including this medication.

Maintain a dosing interval of at least 4 hours.

If the cause of symptoms is not identified, do not take continuously for more than 5 days.

### ⚠️ 디큐펜프로나이트연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication increases the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, are under 15 years of age, have or have a history of gastrointestinal ulcers or bleeding, have or have a history of bronchial asthma, experience respiratory distress due to asthmatic attacks, emphysema, or chronic bronchitis, have or have a history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, hives, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), have severe blood disorders, liver dysfunction, heart failure, hypertension, lower urinary tract obstructive diseases such as glaucoma or prostatic hypertrophy, convulsive disorders such as epilepsy, pheochromocytoma, or experience pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, have chickenpox, have blood disorders, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction, or kidney dysfunction, have a tendency to bleed, have heart failure or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, myasthenia gravis, diabetes, thyroid disease, are an elderly person aged 65 or older, or are a child.

If symptoms do not improve after taking for 5 days or more, immediately stop taking this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not exceed the recommended dose of this medication.

Breastfeeding mothers should either stop breastfeeding or discontinue administration of this medication.

It may affect reaction time. Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   成人：因疼痛难以入睡时，一次服用1~2粒胶囊，每日一次，睡前服用。

服药间隔应至少为4小时。

如果症状原因不明，请勿连续服用超过5天。
