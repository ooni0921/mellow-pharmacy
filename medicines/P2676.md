# EN

### 💊 프라임아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Uses and Effects
*   This medication is used for the inhibition of thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, for the inhibition of thrombus formation after Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) or Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA), and for the reduction of cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with complex risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, and diabetes).

### 🕒 프라임아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 tablet once daily. As this medication is an enteric-coated tablet, it can be taken before meals with plenty of water.

### ⚠️ 프라임아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medication or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or antirheumatic drugs; have a peptic ulcer, aspirin-induced asthma or a history thereof; have hemophilia, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, a bleeding tendency; or are concomitantly administered methotrexate at a dose of 15 mg/week or more; or are a pregnant woman in the third trimester. During the first and second trimesters of pregnancy, do not take this medication unless absolutely necessary. Before using this medication, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have kidney dysfunction, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (e.g., renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, fluid depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major bleeding events), liver dysfunction, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma; are a pre-operative patient, an infant under 3 years old, an elderly person, a patient with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a patient under 14 years old with chickenpox or influenza infection, or a patient with gout. As there is a risk of bleeding, if you undergo surgery (including dental surgery), inform your doctor that you are taking this medication. Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medication.

# CN

### 💊 프라임아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于抑制心肌梗死、脑梗死、不稳定型心绞痛中的血栓形成，抑制冠状动脉旁路移植术 (CABG) 或经皮腔内冠状动脉成形术 (PTCA) 后的血栓形成，以及在高危患者（具有缺血性心脏病家族史、高血压、高胆固醇血症、肥胖、糖尿病等复合危险因素的患者）中降低心血管风险。

### 🕒 프라임아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - 用法与用量
*   成人每次1片，每日1次。本药为肠溶片，可饭前与足量水一同服用。

### ⚠️ 프라임아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   以下患者请勿服用本药：对本药或其他水杨酸制剂、镇痛剂、消炎剂、抗风湿剂过敏的患者；消化性溃疡、阿司匹林哮喘或有其病史的患者；血友病、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全的患者；有出血倾向的患者；同时服用每周15毫克（15 mg/周）以上甲氨蝶呤的患者；妊娠晚期孕妇。妊娠早期和中期，除非绝对必要，否则请勿服用本药。在使用本药之前，如有以下情况，请咨询医生或药剂师：肾功能障碍；心血管循环功能异常（如肾血管疾病、充血性心力衰竭、体液减少、大型手术、败血症或大出血事件等）；肝功能障碍；心功能异常；支气管哮喘；术前患者；3岁以下幼儿；老年人；葡萄糖-6-磷酸脱氢酶（G6PD）缺乏症患者；雷耶综合征；14岁以下患有水痘或流感感染的患者；痛风患者。由于存在出血风险，如果进行手术（包括牙科手术），请告知医生您正在服用本药。请注意不要损坏本药的肠溶衣。
