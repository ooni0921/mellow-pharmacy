*   Adults, children aged 12 years and over, and adolescents should slowly dissolve one lozenge in the mouth.
*   To avoid local irritation, move the lozenge around in the mouth while dissolving.
*   If necessary, take one lozenge every 3 to 6 hours, up to a maximum of 5 lozenges per day, and use for a maximum of 3 days.

### ⚠️ 스트렙실허니앤레몬트로키(플루르비프로펜), 스트렙실오렌지트로키(플루르비프로펜) - Precautions
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine or aspirin, other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); peptic ulcer; bleeding; asthma (including asthmatic attacks), urticaria, bronchospasm, rhinitis induced by aspirin or other NSAIDs, or a history thereof; severe cardiac failure; gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation caused by NSAIDs or antirheumatic drugs; severe colitis; severe blood disorders; unexplained hematopoietic disorders; severe hepatic impairment; severe renal impairment; pregnant women in the last trimester (last 3 months); patients with hereditary fructose intolerance, glucose-galactose malabsorption, or sucrose-isomaltase deficiency should not take this medicine.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with bronchial asthma or a history thereof; cardiac failure; renal impairment; hepatic impairment; hypertension; blood disorders or a history thereof; bleeding tendency; elderly patients; peripheral arterial occlusive disease; cerebrovascular disease; hyperlipidemia; diabetes; smokers; hay fever; nasal polyps; chronic obstructive pulmonary disease; pregnant women in the first and second trimesters; fluid retention; and heart failure patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Dizziness and visual disturbances may occur after taking this medicine, so caution should be exercised when driving or operating machinery.
