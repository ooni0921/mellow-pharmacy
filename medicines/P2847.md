# EN
### 💊 알마티정(알마게이트) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for its antacid effect and for the improvement of symptoms such as gastric/duodenal ulcers, gastritis, hyperacidity, heartburn, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and sour belching.

### 🕒 알마티정(알마게이트) - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 12 years and older, chew and take 2 tablets (1 g) once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes to 1 hour after meals.
*   If necessary, an additional dose can be taken once before bedtime.

### ⚠️ 알마티정(알마게이트) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, Alzheimer's disease, hemorrhoids, fluid retention (edema), pre-eclampsia, diarrhea, or undiagnosed gastrointestinal bleeding.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have kidney impairment, are physically weak, are on a low-phosphate diet, have malabsorption, are elderly, have symptoms of gastrointestinal bleeding such as blood vomiting or black stools, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 알마티정(알마게이트) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于胃/十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多、胃灼热、恶心、呕吐、胃痛、嗳酸等症状的制酸作用及改善。

### 🕒 알마티정(알마게이트) - 用法用量
*   12岁以上及成人，每次2片（1克），每日3次，饭后30分钟至1小时咀嚼服用。
*   必要时，睡前可再服用一次。

### ⚠️ 알마티정(알마게이트) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者、阿尔茨海默病、痔疮、体液潴留（水肿）、妊娠中毒症、腹泻、以及未确诊的消化道出血患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，如果存在肾功能障碍、身体虚弱者、磷含量低的饮食者、消化吸收障碍者、老年人、有消化道出血症状（如呕血或黑便）的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   如果服用约2周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
