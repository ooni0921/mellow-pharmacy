#EN
### 💊 타이로펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain due to the common cold, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain (dysmenorrhea), sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 타이로펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Children 12 years of age and older and adults should take 2 capsules per dose, 3 to 4 times a day (every 4 to 6 hours) as needed.

Do not exceed 12 capsules (4 g) in a day.

This medicine should be taken at the lowest effective dose for the shortest possible duration.

### ⚠️ 타이로펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   If you are a regular drinker of three or more alcoholic beverages daily, consult your doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (asthma attack induced by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history thereof.

Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment or a history thereof, renal impairment or a history thereof, a history of peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities or a history thereof, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

本药应以最小有效剂量服用，并尽可能缩短用药时间。

### ⚠️ 타이로펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
