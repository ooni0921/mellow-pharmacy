#EN
### 💊 유실드더블액션현탁액 - Indications and Usage
*   This medicine is used for the improvement of acid reflux, heartburn, and indigestion due to the reflux of stomach contents (e.g., after meals, during pregnancy, or when accompanied by reflux esophagitis).

### 🕒 유실드더블액션현탁액 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years and older should take 10-20 mL once, four times a day (after meals and before bedtime).

### ⚠️ 유실드더블액션현탁액 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine should not take it.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients who must severely restrict sodium (e.g., those with congestive heart failure, renal impairment), patients with hypercalcemia, nephrocalcinosis, recurrent calcium-containing kidney stones, and children under 12 years of age should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 유실드더블액션현탁액 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于改善胃酸反流、胃灼热、胃内容物反流引起的消化不良（例如饭后、怀孕期间、伴有反流性食管炎的情况）。

### 🕒 유실드더블액션현탁액 - 用法与用量
*   成人及12岁以上儿童，每次服用10~20 mL，每日4次（饭后及睡前）。

### ⚠️ 유실드더블액션현탁액 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者请勿服用本药。
*   在服用本药前，钠摄入量需严格限制的患者（如充血性心力衰竭、肾功能损害等），高钙血症、肾钙沉着症和复发性含钙肾结石患者，以及12岁以下儿童，请咨询医生或药师。
*   如果服用约7天后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
