#EN
### 💊 이바니싹액(디클로페낙) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve inflammation of the oropharynx, such as gingivitis (gum inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), pharyngitis, and inflammation after dental conservative treatment or tooth extraction.

### 🕒 이바니싹액(디클로페낙) - Dosage and Administration
*   Use 15 mL per dose, 2-3 times a day. Rinse your mouth or gargle with the undiluted solution or diluted with a small amount of water, then spit out.

### ⚠️ 이바니싹액(디클로페낙) - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, aspirin, or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), or if you have a history of such hypersensitivity.
*   Before using this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after about one week of use, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

*   本药物用于缓解牙龈炎（gum inflammation）、口腔炎（mouth inflammation）、咽炎等口咽部炎症，以及牙科保守治疗或拔牙后的炎症。

### 🕒 이바니싹액(디클로페낙) - 用法与用量
*   一次15毫升，每日2～3次，可直接使用原液或用少量水稀释后漱口或漱喉，然后吐出。

### ⚠️ 이바니싹액(디클로페낙) - 注意事项
*   无可用信息
*   对本药物、阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药（NSAIDs）过敏的患者以及有过敏史的患者请勿使用本药物。
*   使用本药物前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女以及儿童应咨询医生或药剂师。
*   如果使用约一周后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药剂师。
