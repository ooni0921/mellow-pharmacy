# 비타콘틴800정

##EN
### 💊 비타콘틴800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during illness or convalescence, growth periods, and old age.

*   This medication is used to alleviate symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, shoulder stiffness, and other joint pains, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, through the vitamins it contains.

*   This medication is used to prevent poor bone and tooth development, rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue, through the vitamins it contains.

### 🕒 비타콘틴800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비타콘틴800정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, or kidney stones.

*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have heart and circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.

*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication for more than 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.

*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
