# EN
### 💊 써스펜나이트정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 써스펜나이트정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents aged 12 years and older should take 1 to 2 tablets every 4 hours. Do not exceed 8 tablets per day, combining daytime and nighttime doses.

### ⚠️ 써스펜나이트정 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinsonian drugs, etc.) or within 2 weeks of discontinuing them, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza infection (if there are behavioral changes accompanied by nausea or vomiting, it could be an early symptom of Reye's syndrome, a rare but serious illness, so consult a doctor), if you or your family have a predisposition to develop hives, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraines, food allergies, etc., if you have experienced allergic reactions, if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, if you are elderly, frail, have a high fever, are a child under 12 years old, if you have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, if you are pregnant or likely to be pregnant, if you are breastfeeding, if you are a patient receiving medical treatment (for diabetes, gout, arthritis, anticoagulants, steroids, etc.), or if you have a cough (due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, chronic cough, cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

# CN

*   本药用于缓解感冒的各种症状（流鼻涕、鼻塞、打喷嚏、咽喉痛、发冷、发热、头痛、关节痛、肌肉痛）。

### 🕒 써스펜나이트정 - 用法与用量
*   成人及12岁以上青少年每4小时服用1至2片。每日服用量（日间用药和夜间用药合计）请勿超过8片。

### ⚠️ 써스펜나이트정 - 注意事项
*   每日定期饮酒超过三杯的患者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能会导致肝损伤。
*   对本药有过敏史或曾有过敏反应者，服用其他解热镇痛剂或感冒药时曾发生哮喘者，正在服用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）或停用后不足2周的患者，以及有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师：如果怀疑或患有水痘或流感感染的婴儿及15岁以下儿童（如果出现伴有恶心或呕吐的行为变化，这可能是罕见但严重的疾病雷氏综合征的早期症状，请咨询医生），本人或家族中有易患荨麻疹、接触性皮炎、支气管哮喘、过敏性鼻炎、偏头痛、食物过敏等体质者，有过敏反应史者，患有肝病、肾病、心脏病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者，高龄者（老年人），体弱者，发高烧者，12岁以下儿童，胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题持续或反复发作或有溃疡、出血问题者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女，正在接受医生治疗的患者（糖尿病药、痛风药、关节炎药、抗凝血剂、类固醇等），以及咳嗽患者（吸烟、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续或反复超过一周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽）。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

请勿长期持续服用。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

服用期间请勿饮酒。

服用后可能会出现困倦，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意安全。
