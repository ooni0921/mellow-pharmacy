#EN
### 💊 맥스디스정 - Efficacy & Effects
*   This medicine is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy, lactation, growth period, and old age, for maldevelopment of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, calcium deficiency, and other calcium supplementation.

### 🕒 맥스디스정 - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older: take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 맥스디스정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant (general), have cardiac or circulatory dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, are receiving cardiac glycosides, have a history of kidney stones, or have a hypersensitivity or history of hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4).

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
