#EN
### 💊 아실로크림 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for secondarily infected allergic or inflammatory skin diseases (such as eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis) and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 아실로크림 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times daily.

### ⚠️ 아실로크림 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not use this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to this medicine, aminoglycoside antibiotics (such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin), or bacitracin. Do not use if you have skin infections (such as tuberculosis, syphilis, candidiasis, tinea, spirochete infections, yeast infections, herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia, scabies, pubic lice), eczematous otitis externa with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (except Behçet's disease), second-degree deep burns or higher, frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, or rosacea.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, an infant or child, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to propylene glycol.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
*   When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.
*   Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
*   Do not use for purposes other than treatment, such as after makeup or shaving.
*   Do not use occlusive dressings without a doctor's supervision.

*   本品用于继发感染的过敏性或炎症性皮肤病（如湿疹、接触性皮炎、脂溢性皮炎、特应性皮炎、光敏性皮炎、慢性单纯性苔藓、擦烂、剥脱性皮炎、瘙痒症、牛皮癣）以及一度烧伤。

### 🕒 아실로크림 - 用法用量
*   每日1~3次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ 아실로크림 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本品、链霉素、卡那霉素、庆大霉素、新霉素等氨基糖苷类抗生素以及杆菌肽有过敏史或过敏者；患有皮肤感染症（如结核病、梅毒、念珠菌病、癣、螺旋体感染、酵母菌感染、带状疱疹、单纯疱疹、水痘、牛痘、疥疮、阴虱等）；患有鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳炎；溃疡（贝赫切特病除外）；二度深层（不明显的）以上烧伤·冻伤；口周皮炎；寻常痤疮；酒渣鼻（草莓鼻）的患者，请勿使用本品。
*   在使用本品前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、婴幼儿以及对丙二醇有过敏史或过敏反应者，请咨询医生或药师。

*   给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
*   请勿用于眼科。
*   请勿用于化妆、剃须后等治疗以外的目的。
