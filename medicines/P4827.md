#EN

### 💊 빅코민백골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, and periods of physical debility during or after illness. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (back pain, stiff shoulders, etc.), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, as well as for beriberi and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 빅코민백골드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 빅코민백골드정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in urine), are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, folic acid deficiency, or are at special risk due to fat overload.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
