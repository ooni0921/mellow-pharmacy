# EN
### 💊 바이오파워캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and when physical strength is diminished during or after illness (convalescence). It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., low back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis, as well as for beriberi and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 바이오파워캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 8 years or older and adults should take 2 capsules per dose, once daily.

### ⚠️ 바이오파워캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it or are an infant under 3 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition in which an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gout, or kidney stones, or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.

# CN

*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每次2粒，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 바이오파워캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本品过敏者，以及未满3个月的婴儿，请勿服用本品。
