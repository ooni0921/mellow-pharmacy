#EN
### 💊 이로코딜정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for constipation and associated symptoms such as loss of appetite, abdominal bloating, abnormal intestinal fermentation, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 이로코딜정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older and adults, take 1 to 3 tablets once daily. For individuals aged 11 years or older but under 15 years, take 2/3 to 2 tablets once daily. Take at bedtime or on an empty stomach.
* For the initial dose, start with the minimum amount, and gradually increase or decrease the dosage while observing stool consistency and condition.

### ⚠️ 이로코딜정 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medication if you are a child under 7 years of age, or a patient with acute abdominal diseases such as appendicitis, intestinal bleeding, ulcerative colitis, or intestinal obstruction, or a patient with genetic problems such as Lapp lactase deficiency or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, on a sodium-restricted diet, have kidney impairment, severe abdominal pain or nausea/vomiting, or a history of rectal bleeding or intestinal dysfunction.
* If constipation does not improve after about 1 week of taking this medication, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. Avoid administering to children under 10 years of age unless absolutely necessary.
* Do not chew this medication when taking it.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* 无信息。
