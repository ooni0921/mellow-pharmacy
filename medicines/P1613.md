# EN
### 💊 Maida-jeong - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for constipation and associated symptoms such as loss of appetite (anorexia), abdominal distension, abnormal intestinal fermentation, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 Maida-jeong - Dosage and Administration
* For adults (15 years and older), take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day. For those aged 11-14, take 1 tablet once, 2 times a day. Take before meals (between meals or after meals). The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours. However, for the initial dose, take the maximum amount and gradually increase or decrease the dosage while observing the shape and condition of the stool.

### ⚠️ Maida-jeong - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with acute abdominal diseases (such as appendicitis, intestinal bleeding, ulcerative colitis, etc.) or intestinal obstruction should not take this medication. Before taking this medication, patients with severe abdominal pain or nausea/vomiting, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, patients with renal impairment, patients with hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4) or those with a history of such reactions, and infants under 7 years of age should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
