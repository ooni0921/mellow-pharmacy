##EN
### 💊 마그바이타민연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for physical fatigue; during pregnancy and lactation; physical weakness during or after illness (convalescence); supplementation of Vitamins E, B1, B6 in old age; alleviation of shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness of extremities, and cold hands and feet (peripheral coldness) due to peripheral circulatory disorders and during menopause; beriberi; eye fatigue; neuralgia; myalgia (muscle pain); arthralgia (joint pain) (such as lower back pain, shoulder stiffness); alleviation of angular stomatitis (cheilosis), cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis; and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그바이타민연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age and older: Take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그바이타민연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this drug, hypersensitive to soy or peanuts, have a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, have severe renal insufficiency, or are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, premature infants, infants, patients with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or patients who require careful administration of lipid emulsions, and patients who are at special risk of fat overload should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

##CN
### 💊 마그바이타민연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解身体疲劳，妊娠·哺乳期、病中·病后（患病期间或恢复后）体力下降时，老年期补充维生素E、B1、B6，缓解周围血液循环障碍和更年期引起的肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木、手脚冰凉，脚气病，眼睛疲劳，神经痛，肌肉痛，关节痛（如腰痛、肩周炎等），口角炎，唇炎，口腔炎，舌炎，湿疹，皮炎，以及因镁缺乏引起的肌肉痉挛。

### 🕒 마그바이타민연질캡슐 - 用法用量
* 12岁及以上成人，每次1粒，每日2次服用。

### ⚠️ 마그바이타민연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者，对大豆或花生过敏的患者，有大豆油过敏反应史的患者，严重肾功能不全患者，以及12个月以下的婴儿请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、心脏·循环系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症患者、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需慎重给药的脂质乳剂患者，以及预计有脂肪超负荷特殊风险的患者应咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
