#EN
### 💊 엠지플러스큐연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
* This product is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness, and in old age. It also provides relief from peripheral circulatory disorders, stiff shoulders and neck, numbness and coldness in hands and feet during menopause, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 엠지플러스큐연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents aged 12 years and older should take 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 엠지플러스큐연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this product if you have hypersensitivity to this product, are an infant under 12 months of age, have severe renal impairment, have a history of hypersensitivity to soybean oil or allergic reactions, or have hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.
* Before taking this product, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, or have cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders, or are a patient requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions, or have folic acid deficiency.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This product may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
