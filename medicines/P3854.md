#EN
### 💊 쎈비백정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during illness or recovery (during illness or after recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of rickets and underdeveloped bones and teeth, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as back pain, stiff shoulders), relief of symptoms of angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, and for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 쎈비백정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 쎈비백정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who receives a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, or a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, localized colitis, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, folic acid deficiency, or a thrombotic predisposition.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
