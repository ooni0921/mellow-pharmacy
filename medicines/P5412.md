#EN
### 💊 유한아세트아미노펜정500밀리그램 - Indications and Efficacy
* This medication is used for fever and pain (ache) due to colds, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 유한아세트아미노펜정500밀리그램 - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 12 years and over and adults, take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, 3-4 times a day, every 4-6 hours, as needed.

Do not exceed 4g (8 tablets) per day.

This medication should be taken at the lowest effective dose for the shortest possible duration.

### ⚠️ 유한아세트아미노펜정500밀리그램 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this medication with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe heart dysfunction, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by NSAIDs) or a history thereof, or if you have taken barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding tendency, heart dysfunction, hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, if you are elderly, pregnant or breastfeeding, or if you have a low glutathione reserve.

Do not take this medication for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days without consulting a doctor or pharmacist. If pain or fever symptoms persist or worsen, or if new symptoms appear, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
