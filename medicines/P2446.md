### 💊 테라플루콜드앤코프데이타임건조시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle aches).

### 🕒 테라플루콜드앤코프데이타임건조시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 sachet per dose, 3 times a day. Take only the prescribed minimum amount, with at least 4 hours between doses. Dissolve 1 sachet in approximately 250 mL of hot water (not boiling) and drink within 10-15 minutes.

### ⚠️ 테라플루콜드앤코프데이타임건조시럽 - Precautions
* Regular drinkers of three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, those with a history of asthma due to other cold medicines or antipyretic analgesics, patients currently taking or having taken MAO inhibitors within the last 2 weeks, patients with hypertension, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, epilepsy, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history thereof, diabetes, patients administered barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, and alcohol, children under 18 years of age, patients on a sodium-restricted diet, breastfeeding women, patients with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase deficiency, sucrose-isomaltase deficiency, or genetic problems such as fructose and glucose-galactose malabsorption, patients with phenylketonuria who need to restrict phenylalanine intake due to the aspartame content (which is metabolized into phenylalanine), patients with respiratory insufficiency or at risk thereof (e.g., chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, pneumonia, asthma), and patients with pheochromocytoma should not take this medicine. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, occlusive vascular disease (Raynaud's syndrome), liver impairment, kidney impairment, a history of peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendencies (platelet dysfunction), cardiac dysfunction, emphysema, asthma, chronic bronchitis, thyroid disease, micturition disorder due to prostatic hypertrophy, glaucoma, closed-angle glaucoma, a history of pre-eclampsia, are elderly, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, or hypersensitive to Red No. 40. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Drowsiness, dizziness, visual impairment, cognitive and psychomotor impairment may occur, so avoid driving or operating machinery.
