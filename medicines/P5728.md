#EN
### 💊 마로씬정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for the relief of constipation and symptoms associated with constipation such as loss of appetite, abdominal bloating, abnormal intestinal fermentation, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 마로씬정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 years or older should take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day, before meals.

For the initial dose, start with the minimum amount, and gradually increase or decrease the dose while observing the form and condition of your stool.

### ⚠️ 마로씬정 - Precautions
* No specific information provided.
* Patients with acute abdominal diseases such as appendicitis, intestinal hemorrhage, ulcerative colitis, or intestinal obstruction should not take this drug.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, have severe abdominal pain, nausea or vomiting, kidney impairment, or have a history of rectal bleeding or bowel dysfunction.

If constipation does not improve after about 1 week of administration, immediately stop taking this drug and consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not continue taking this drug for more than 1 week.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
