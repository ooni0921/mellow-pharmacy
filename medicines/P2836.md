# EN

### 💊 원앤데이후레쉬점안액(1회용) - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for eye fatigue, tear supplementation (dry eyes), discomfort when wearing hard or soft contact lenses, and blurred vision (e.g., when there is a lot of eye discharge).

### 🕒 원앤데이후레쉬점안액(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Instill 1 to 2 drops at a time, 4 to 5 times a day.

### ⚠️ 원앤데이후레쉬점안액(1회용) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe eye pain, have experienced allergic symptoms due to eye drops (such as eye redness, itching, swelling, rash, or intense bloodshot eyes), or have glaucoma.

*   Use this medication for ophthalmic use only.

*   If blurred vision does not improve with the use of this medication, or if symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   Be careful not to let the tip of the container touch your eyelids or eyelashes, as the solution may become contaminated or cloudy due to eye discharge or fungi. Do not use if the solution is cloudy.

*   To prevent contamination, avoid sharing with others as much as possible.

*   After opening, use immediately for one application only, and discard any remaining solution and the container.

*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   无相关信息
