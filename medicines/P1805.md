#EN
### 💊 파날디정(덱시부프로펜) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medication is used for the adjunctive treatment of chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation following trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 파날디정(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet (300 mg) per dose, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 tablets (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 파날디정(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may occur.

When taking this medication, serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as bleeding, ulceration, and perforation, which can be fatal, may occur.
* Do not take this medication if you have: hypersensitivity to this drug; gastrointestinal ulcer; bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular region, or other areas; severe blood disorders; severe liver impairment; severe renal impairment; severe heart failure; severe hypertension; bronchial asthma; a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs; are undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; are pregnant beyond 6 months of gestation; are breastfeeding; have Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis; have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation caused by NSAIDs; or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have: blood disorders, bleeding tendency, liver impairment, renal impairment, fluid retention, heart failure, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, stomach cancer, alcoholism; are elderly or a child under 6 years old; are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or breastfeeding; have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, coagulation disorders, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease; have hepatic porphyria; or have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

For acute conditions, long-term use should generally be limited to within 5 days.

#CN

* 本药用于辅助治疗慢性多发性关节炎、类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、外伤及手术后疼痛性肿胀或炎症，以及伴有炎症、疼痛和发热的感染症。

### 🕒 파날디정(덱시부프로펜) - 用法及用量
* 成人一次服用1片（300毫克），每日2～4次。但是，每日不得超过4片（1,200毫克）。

### ⚠️ 파날디정(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯以上的规律饮酒者，在服用本药或其它解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能会引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药可能会增加严重且可能致命的心血管血栓形成反应、心肌梗死、中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。

服用本药时，可能会出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔（穿破）等可能致命的严重胃肠道不良反应。
* 对本药过敏的患者，有胃肠道溃疡、胃肠道、脑血管或其它部位出血的患者，严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心力衰竭、严重高血压、支气管哮喘、有阿司匹林或其它非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏史的患者，心脏动脉旁路移植术前后疼痛治疗的患者，妊娠6个月以上的孕妇，哺乳期妇女，克罗恩病、溃疡性结肠炎患者，有因非甾体抗炎药引起胃肠道出血或穿孔病史的患者，有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如患有血液异常、出血倾向、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、体液潴留、心力衰竭、高血压、全身性红斑狼疮、混合结缔组织病、胃癌、酒精中毒患者；老年人、6岁以下儿童；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女；缺血性心脏病、外周动脉疾病、脑血管疾病、凝血障碍、有心血管疾病风险因素的患者；肝性卟啉病；因失血而接受过重大外科手术的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

服用本药后可能会出现头晕或疲劳，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

对于急性疾病，长期服用原则上应在5日以内。
