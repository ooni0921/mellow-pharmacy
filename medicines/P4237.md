#EN
### 💊 일성알마게이트정500밀리그램 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for antacid action and improvement of symptoms such as gastric/duodenal ulcer, gastritis, hyperacidity, heartburn, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and sour belching.

### 🕒 일성알마게이트정500밀리그램 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age or older should chew and take 1 g of Almagate once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes to 1 hour after meals.
* If necessary, one more dose can be taken before bedtime.

### ⚠️ 일성알마게이트정500밀리그램 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, Alzheimer's disease, hemorrhoids, fluid retention (edema), preeclampsia, diarrhea, undiagnosed gastrointestinal bleeding, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have kidney impairment, physical weakness, are on a low-phosphorus diet, have digestive absorption disorders, are elderly, have symptoms of gastrointestinal bleeding such as hematemesis or black stools, are pregnant or likely to be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
