#EN
### 💊 닥스펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for antipyresis in cases of fever caused by acute upper respiratory tract infection.

### 🕒 닥스펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 6 months or older should take 0.4-0.6 mL/kg (5-7 mg/kg) per dose, as needed, at 4 to 6-hour intervals, not exceeding a maximum of 4 doses (28 mg/kg) per day.

For children weighing 30 kg or less, do not exceed a maximum of 25 mL (300 mg) per day.

Refer to the approved product information for recommended dosages by age group.

### ⚠️ 닥스펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Individuals who regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal, cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, cardiac insufficiency, hypertension, bronchial asthma, asthma, hives, or allergies caused by aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics or have a history thereof, a patient receiving pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, or a pregnant woman, woman who may be pregnant, or breastfeeding woman.

Before taking this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding tendency, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, cardiac insufficiency, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, gastric cancer, Crohn's disease, the elderly, children, or those taking low-dose aspirin for the prevention of myocardial infarction or stroke should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 닥스펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 功效及效果
*   本药用于急性上呼吸道感染引起的发热时的退热。

### 🕒 닥스펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 用法及用量
*   6个月以上的儿童，每次服用0.4~0.6 mL/kg (5~7 mg/kg)，按需每隔4~6小时服用一次，每日最多不超过4次 (28 mg/kg)。

对于体重30 kg以下的儿童，每日最大剂量不超过25 mL (300 mg)。

各年龄段的推荐剂量请参考批准的说明书。

### ⚠️ 닥스펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒超过三杯的常饮者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药可能增加致命性严重心血管血栓事件、心肌梗死、中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
*   对本药过敏的患者、消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全、高血压患者、支气管哮喘、因阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛药引起的哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏患者或有相关病史者、冠状动脉旁路移植术前后疼痛治疗患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，对本药过敏、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全、高血压、系统性红斑狼疮、混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、胃癌、克罗恩病患者、老年人、儿童、以及为预防心肌梗死或中风而服用小剂量阿司匹林的人，请咨询医生或药师。
