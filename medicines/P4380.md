* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1캡슐, 1일 2회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 셀레뉴연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for physical fatigue, decreased physical strength during pregnancy/lactation or during/after illness (during illness or recovery), supplementation of vitamins A, E, C in the elderly, alleviation of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders and stiff shoulders/neck, numbness in hands/feet, and cold hands/feet (cold hands and feet) during menopause, alleviation of pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight/skin diseases, prevention of gum bleeding, nosebleeds (epistaxis), and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 셀레뉴연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 셀레뉴연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, peanuts, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition in which an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or if you are a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload, or a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

It may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
