* 급성 복부질환(충수염, 장출혈, 궤양성 결장염 등) 환자, 장폐색(창자막힘) 환자, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

반드시 필요한 경우 이외에는 만 10세 이하의 어린이는 복용하지 마십시오.

복용 시 정제를 씹지 마십시오.

1주 정도 복용하여도 증상이 좋아지지 않을 경우 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 메이센정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the relief of constipation, loss of appetite (anorexia) due to constipation, abdominal distension, abnormal fermentation in the intestines, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 메이센정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 15 years and older should take 1 to 3 tablets once daily at bedtime (on an empty stomach).
*   However, for the initial dose, take the minimum amount and gradually increase or decrease it while observing the shape and condition of the stool.

### ⚠️ 메이센정 - Precautions
*   None provided
*   Do not take this drug if you have acute abdominal diseases (such as appendicitis, intestinal bleeding, ulcerative colitis), intestinal obstruction, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4), severe abdominal pain, nausea, or vomiting, are pregnant or suspect you may be pregnant, have an allergic predisposition, are on a sodium-restricted diet, or have experienced rectal bleeding or intestinal failure.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not administer to children under 10 years of age unless absolutely necessary.

Do not chew the tablets when taking.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one week of taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 메이센정 - 用法及用量
*   15岁及以上成人，每日一次，睡前（空腹时）服用1至3片。
*   首次服用时请服用最小剂量，并根据排便的形状和状态，逐渐增加或减少剂量。

### ⚠️ 메이센정 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   患有急性腹部疾病（如阑尾炎、肠出血、溃疡性结肠炎等）、肠梗阻、或有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者请勿服用本药物。

服用本药物前，对柠檬黄（黄色4号）有过敏症或过敏史者，患有严重腹痛或恶心、呕吐者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，过敏体质者，正在进行钠限制饮食者，或有直肠出血或肠功能不全史者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

除非绝对必要，否则10岁以下儿童请勿服用。

服用时请勿咀嚼药片。

如果服用约一周后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药师。
