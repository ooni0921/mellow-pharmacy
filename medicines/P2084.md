# EN
### 💊 노펜스트롱카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for analgesia and anti-inflammation in cases of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia (muscle pain), and swelling/pain after trauma.

### 🕒 노펜스트롱카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Remove the release liner from the patch and apply it to the affected area twice a day.

### ⚠️ 노펜스트롱카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug or to tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, or oxybenzone; if you have aspirin-induced asthma or a history thereof; if you are pregnant for 6 months or more; or if you are under 15 years of age.
*   Before using this medication, patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant women, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   While using this medication and for up to two weeks after use, avoid outdoor activities regardless of the weather. When going out, cover the application area with clothing, sunglasses, a hat, sunscreen, etc., to prevent exposure to ultraviolet rays.
*   Do not apply to eyes and mucous membranes, damaged skin and mucous membranes, areas with eczema or rash, athlete's foot, ringworm, etc.
*   Do not use occlusive dressing techniques.

### 🕒 노펜스트롱카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - 用法及用量
*   撕下药面上的离型纸后，每日两次贴于患处。

### ⚠️ 노펜스트롱카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药或硫普罗芬酸、非诺贝特、苯扎贝特、西普贝特、二苯甲酮过敏的患者，患有阿司匹林哮喘或有相关病史者，妊娠期6个月以上的孕妇，以及15岁以下的儿童请勿使用本药。
*   在使用本药前，支气管哮喘患者、孕妇及哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药师。
*   若使用约一周后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
*   在使用本药期间及使用后两周内，无论天气如何，请避免户外活动。日常外出时，请用衣物、太阳镜、帽子、防晒霜等遮盖本药的涂抹部位，避免暴露于紫外线。
*   请勿用于眼睛和黏膜、受损的皮肤和黏膜、湿疹或皮疹部位、脚癣、体癣等处。
*   请勿使用密封敷料法。
