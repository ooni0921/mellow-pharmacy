#EN
### 💊 비타메진캡슐50밀리그램 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for vitamin supplementation when vitamin demand increases and intake from meals is insufficient (e.g., in debilitating diseases, pregnant women, lactating women), and in cases where vitamin deficiency or metabolic disorders are suspected for the vitamins contained in this product, such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia, peripheral neuropathy (alcoholic, diabetic), and peripheral nerve paralysis.

### 🕒 비타메진캡슐50밀리그램 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule once, 1 to 2 times daily.

### ⚠️ 비타메진캡슐50밀리그램 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine. Before taking this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
