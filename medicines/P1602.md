# EN
### 💊 다아펜정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for headache, toothache, joint pain, neuralgia, muscle pain, sprain pain, and menstrual pain.

### 🕒 다아펜정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 to 2 tablets at a time, 3 to 4 times a day, avoiding an empty stomach. The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 다아펜정 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming more than three alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Do not exceed the daily maximum dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity (e.g., rash, redness, itching, swelling of the larynx, eyelids, lips) to this medicine, or if you have experienced asthma attacks when taking other antipyretic analgesics (NSAIDs), cold medicines, or if you have peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, or if you have taken barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol. Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you have an allergic predisposition, have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), are infants or children under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, are frail, have high fever, have or have experienced liver impairment, have or have experienced kidney impairment, have experienced peptic ulcer, have or have experienced blood abnormalities, have a tendency to bleed, have cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are elderly, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

# CN
### 💊 다아펜정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于治疗头痛、牙痛、关节痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、扭伤痛和月经痛（生理痛）。

### 🕒 다아펜정 - 用法用量
*   成人每次服用1至2片，每日3至4次，避免空腹服用。服药间隔应至少为4小时。

### ⚠️ 다아펜정 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒超过三杯的定期饮酒者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能导致肝损伤。对乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000毫克）请勿超量服用。这可能导致肝损伤。请勿与其他含有对乙酰氨基酚的产品同时服用。
*   对本药有过敏反应（如皮疹、充血发红、瘙痒、喉部、眼睑、嘴唇肿胀）的患者及有过敏史者，服用其他解热镇痛药（非甾体抗炎药）、感冒药时发生过哮喘发作或有过敏史者，患有消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全者，以及服用过巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药、酒精者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，如体质易引起过敏、有过敏症状（如发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）史者，感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿及15岁以下儿童，患有甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、身体虚弱者、高热患者、有肝功能障碍或有过肝功能障碍史者、有肾功能障碍或有过肾功能障碍史者、有消化性溃疡史者、有血液异常或有过血液异常史者、有出血倾向的患者、有心功能异常的患者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。
