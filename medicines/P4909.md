#EN

### 💊 콜대원코프큐시럽 - Indications and Efficacy
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms [sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (symptoms of feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain].

### 🕒 콜대원코프큐시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older, take 1 sachet (20 mL). For those aged 11 to under 15 years, take 2/3 sachet. For those aged 7 to under 11 years, take 1/2 sachet. For those aged 3 to under 7 years, take 1/3 sachet. For those aged 2 to under 3 years, take 1/4 sachet. Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

Do not administer to children under 2 years of age.

In principle, the dosing interval should be 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 콜대원코프큐시럽 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity or have experienced it with this medicine, have had asthma attacks when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: an infant or young child aged 3 months to under 2 years; an infant or child under 15 years suspected of or infected with chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic constitution; have experienced drug allergies (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver, kidney, heart, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, etc.; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; are breastfeeding; smoke; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

* 本药用于缓解感冒的各种症状[咽喉痛、咳嗽、痰多、寒战（发冷发抖的症状）、发热、头痛、关节痛、肌肉痛]。

### 🕒 콜대원코프큐시럽 - 用法与用量
* 15岁及以上者每次1袋（20毫升），11岁及以上至15岁以下者每次2/3袋，7岁及以上至11岁以下者每次1/2袋，3岁及以上至7岁以下者每次1/3袋，2岁及以上至3岁以下者每次1/4袋，每日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

2岁以下儿童禁用。

原则上，服用间隔为4小时。

### ⚠️ 콜대원코프큐시럽 - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯或以上者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。

极罕见情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后，若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。

扑热息痛（对乙酰氨基酚）每日服用量不得超过最大剂量（4,000毫克）。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与其他含有扑热息痛的产品同时服用。
* 对本药有过敏史或经历者、服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾引起哮喘者、未满3个月的婴儿（新生儿）、正在服用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪稳定剂、抗帕金森药等）或停药后2周内者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，若为3个月以上且未满2岁的婴幼儿；感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿及未满15岁的儿童；过敏体质者；有过药物过敏史（发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）者；患有肝脏、肾脏、心脏、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡等疾病者；老年人、体弱者或高热者；胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；吸烟者；患有哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰的咳嗽、持续或反复发作一周以上的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽等症状者，请咨询医生或药师。

如果服用5-6次后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。

请勿长期持续服用。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下进行。

服用期间请勿饮酒。

### ⚠️ 콜대원코프큐시럽 - 注意事項
* 毎日3杯以上常飲する方が、本剤または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する際は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。肝機能障害を引き起こす可能性があります。

アセトアミノフェンとして1日最大用量（4,000 mg）を超えて服用しないでください。肝機能障害を引き起こす可能性があります。アセトアミノフェンを含む他の製品と併用しないでください。
* 本剤に過敏症の経験がある方、他の解熱鎮痛剤や風邪薬の服用でぜんそくを起こしたことがある方、生後3ヶ月未満の乳児（新生児）、MAO阻害剤（抗うつ剤、抗精神病薬、気分安定剤、抗パーキンソン病薬など）を服用中または服用中止後2週間以内の方は、本剤を服用しないでください。
