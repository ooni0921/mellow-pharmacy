### 💊 에이리스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for contraception and for the treatment of moderate acne in women for whom this medication is suitable.

### 🕒 에이리스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Take 1 tablet daily for 21 days following the indicated order, then take a 7-day break. It is advisable to take the medication at the same time each day if possible. Menstruation usually occurs during the 7-day break, typically starting 2-3 days after the last tablet is taken. If continuing this medication, start a new pack of tablets after the 7-day break; menstruation may not have ended before starting the new pack. For more details, please refer to the detailed information in the prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 에이리스정 - Precautions
* Women taking oral contraceptives should refrain from smoking due to an increased risk of serious cardiovascular events such as thrombosis. Smokers aged 35 years or older should not take this medication.
* Do not take this medication if you have: hypersensitivity to this drug, tumors (e.g., breast cancer, endometrial cancer, pituitary tumor), thromboembolic phlebitis, thromboembolism, cerebrovascular or coronary artery disease, undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding, severe hypertension, severe liver dysfunction, Dubin-Johnson syndrome, Rotor syndrome, jaundice, cholestasis (bile stasis), hepatitis, sickle cell anemia, severe diabetes mellitus, hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, porphyria, metabolic disorders, persistent pruritus during pregnancy, herpes gestationis, pemphigoid gestationis, otosclerosis, if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding, pituitary tumor, galactorrhea, hyperprolactinemia, migraine with aura such as amaurosis fugax or scintillating scotoma, valvular heart disease complicated by pulmonary hypertension or atrial fibrillation, valvular heart disease with a history of pancreatitis or subacute bacterial endocarditis, diabetes mellitus with vascular lesions, headache with focal neurological symptoms such as aura, are taking direct-acting antiviral agents for hepatitis C, have congenital or acquired hypercoagulopathy, are a smoker aged 35 years or older, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Consult your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are: a smoker, have hypertension, are obese, are a woman aged 40 years or older, have epilepsy, migraine, a history of arterial disease or varicose veins, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE), a history of breast cancer, depression or a history of depression, cardiac or renal failure, atypical hyperplastic mammary disease, a history of breast nodules, valvular heart disease, atrial fibrillation, a history of hyperlipidemia, a history of gallbladder disease or cholelithiasis, endometriosis, asthma or chloasma (melasma), peripheral circulatory disorders, diabetes mellitus, or migraine without aura.
* This medication does not prevent HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases.
* If you have severe gastrointestinal disease, absorption may be incomplete, so use an additional contraceptive method.
* This medication is not applicable to pre-menarcheal women and post-menopausal women. For detailed precautions, please check the detailed information in the prescribing information.
