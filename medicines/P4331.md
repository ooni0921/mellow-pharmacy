*   This medication is used for fever reduction and the relief of pain associated with common cold, headache, toothache, muscle pain, lower back pain, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 아세트엠8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 2 tablets every 8 hours.

Do not exceed 6 tablets within a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 아세트엠8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication or any other fever reducer/pain reliever, as it may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). This may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcers, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, asthma induced by aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics, or if you are taking barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, have consumed alcohol, or are a child under 12 years of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcers, blood disorders, a bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, or are pregnant or breastfeeding.

Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.

This medication is an extended-release formulation; therefore, swallow the tablets whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving them.
