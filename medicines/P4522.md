#EN
### 💊 Sobunal-A Jung - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for acute respiratory disorders accompanied by fever.

### 🕒 Sobunal-A Jung - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, take 1 tablet at a time, 2 to 4 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Sobunal-A Jung - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (asthma attack caused by NSAIDs, etc.) or a history thereof, if you have taken barbiturate drugs or tricyclic antidepressants, if you have consumed alcohol, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment or a history thereof, kidney impairment or a history thereof, a history of peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities or a history thereof, a tendency to bleed, abnormal heart function, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.
