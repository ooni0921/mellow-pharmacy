# EN
### 💊 킥노즈에프연질캡슐 - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, chills (symptoms of feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 킥노즈에프연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 15 years of age and older, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 킥노즈에프연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Individuals who regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age or a child under 2 years of age, are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, or if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) occur in relation to pseudoephedrine, if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicine (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, if you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant or breastfeeding, if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough with excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than one week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
