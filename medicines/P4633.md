#EN
### 💊 Shinshin Compound UDCA Soft Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the improvement of physical fatigue, general debility, and for the supplementation of Vitamin B1 during and after illness.

### 🕒 Shinshin Compound UDCA Soft Capsule - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 15 years or older and adults should take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ Shinshin Compound UDCA Soft Capsule - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not take it.

Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
