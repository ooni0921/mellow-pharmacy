# EN
### 💊 이가탄에프캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 이가탄에프캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 이가탄에프캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are a child under 15 years of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic reactions (such as rash, redness, itching) to medications or cosmetics, allergic reactions (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to eggs, if you have an allergic constitution, or if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5). Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

# CN

### 🕒 이가탄에프캡슐 - 用法与用量
