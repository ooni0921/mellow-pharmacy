#EN
### 💊 리버스액 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for liver diseases.

### 🕒 리버스액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 10-30 mL daily, divided between meals. Dilute 10 mL of this medicine in about half a cup of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 리버스액 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with kidney disease, myocardial infarction, or a history thereof should not take this medicine. This medicine cannot be used as an antidote for alcohol poisoning. If there is no improvement after approximately one month of taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist. Please follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully. This medicine is for oral use only and should not be used for injection.

#CN
### 💊 리버스액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于肝病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 리버스액 - 用法与用量
* 成人每日分次服用10-30毫升，餐间服用。将本药10毫升稀释在约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 리버스액 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 肾病、心肌梗死患者及有相关病史者请勿服用本药。本药不能用作酒精中毒的解毒剂。若服用约1个月后症状无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。本药仅供口服，不可用于注射。
