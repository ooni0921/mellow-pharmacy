#EN
### 💊 부루펜정200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Indications and Usage
* This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to colds, lumbago, dysmenorrhea, postoperative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, myalgia, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injury (sprain, contusion), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 부루펜정200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, take 1 to 3 tablets (200 to 600 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times a day, up to a maximum of 16 tablets (3,200 mg) per day. For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30 to 40 mg/kg of body weight per day, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or cold, adults take 1 to 2 tablets (200 to 400 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times a day.

### ⚠️ 부루펜정200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* Regular drinkers consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
Taking this medicine increases the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe heart failure, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions experienced due to aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics, patients experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, pregnant women in the last trimester (last 3 months), breastfeeding women, patients receiving high-dose methotrexate for chemotherapy, and patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis, liver impairment, kidney impairment, heart failure, heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly, children under 2 years of age, pregnant women, breastfeeding women, individuals taking low-dose aspirin, or have chickenpox.

When taken for a cold, it should generally be taken within 5 days. Do not take for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) for pain, and not more than 3 days for fever, without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 부루펜정200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - 注意事项
