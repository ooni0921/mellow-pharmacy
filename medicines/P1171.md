#EN
### 💊 엔시드8시간이알서방정650mg(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of fever and pain associated with colds, headache, toothache, muscle pain, lower back pain, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 엔시드8시간이알서방정650mg(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Children 12 years of age and older, and adults should take 2 tablets every 8 hours. Do not exceed 6 tablets within a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 엔시드8시간이알서방정650mg(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.
*   Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.
*   Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have: hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe heart dysfunction, asthma experienced due to aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics, if you are taking barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, if you have consumed alcohol, or if you are under 12 years of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have: liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, heart dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, if you are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.
*   Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), and do not take for fever for more than 3 days without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine is an extended-release formulation; therefore, swallow the tablets whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving them.

*   12岁以上儿童及成人每8小时服用2片。24小时内不要超过6片。
