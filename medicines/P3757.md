# EN
### 💊 마그비액티브정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness and coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet), alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (back pain, shoulder stiffness, etc.), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그비액티브정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 12 years and older, take 1 tablet once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 마그비액티브정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, an infant under 12 months old, or a patient with severe renal insufficiency.
* Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, infants, patients with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, or folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.
