#EN
### 💊 제일속엔자임이중정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, stomach upset, aiding digestion, and stomach bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 제일속엔자임이중정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 years or older should take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 제일속엔자임이중정 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you are a child aged 7 years or older, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, individuals with an allergic constitution, pregnant women, or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, immediately discontinue taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 제일속엔자임이중정 - 功效与作用
* 本药品用于治疗消化不良、食欲减退（食欲不振）、过食、积食、促进消化、因消化不良引起的胃部饱胀感。

### 🕒 제일속엔자임이중정 - 用法用量
* 15岁及以上成人，每次1~2片，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 제일속엔자임이중정 - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 7岁及以上儿童，以及患有半乳糖不耐受症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本药品。
* 服用本药品前，过敏体质者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
* 服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。
