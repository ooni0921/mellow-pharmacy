#EN

### 💊 마그오캡슐500mg(산화마그네슘) - Indications & Uses
* This medication is used for its antacid effect and improvement of symptoms in gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity, as well as for constipation.

### 🕒 마그오캡슐500mg(산화마그네슘) - Dosage & Administration
* For gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity: 1 to 2 capsules (0.5 to 1 g) of magnesium oxide daily, divided into several doses. For constipation: 4 capsules (2 g) daily, divided into 1 to 2 doses.

However, for the initial dose, take the minimum amount and gradually increase or decrease the dosage while observing the stool's form and condition.

### ⚠️ 마그오캡슐500mg(산화마그네슘) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with renal impairment or diarrhea should not take this medication.

Before taking this medication, patients with cardiac dysfunction, hypermagnesemia, or the elderly (aged 65 years or older) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medication may affect the absorption and excretion of co-administered drugs due to its adsorptive action or the rise in pH of gastrointestinal fluids, thus it should be administered with caution.

#CN

### 💊 마그오캡슐500mg(산화마그네슘) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于胃十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多症的制酸作用及症状改善，以及便秘症。

### 🕒 마그오캡슐500mg(산화마그네슘) - 用法与用量
* 胃十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多症，以氧化镁计，每日1~2粒胶囊（0.5~1克），分多次服用；便秘症，每日4粒胶囊（2克），分1~2次服用。

但是，初次服用请服用最小剂量，并根据大便的形状和状态，逐渐增加或减少剂量。

### ⚠️ 마그오캡슐500mg(산화마그네슘) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 肾功能障碍、腹泻患者请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，心功能障碍、高镁血症患者、老年人（65岁及以上）请咨询医生或药剂师。

本药的吸附作用或消化道内体液pH值升高可能会影响合用药物的吸收和排泄，因此应慎重给药。
