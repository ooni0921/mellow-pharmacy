# EN

### 💊 카네덤플러스크림 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for superficial fungal infections of the skin (tinea, cutaneous candidiasis, pityriasis versicolor) accompanied by clinical symptoms such as inflammation, itching, and eczema.

### 🕒 카네덤플러스크림 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area (diseased part) once or twice daily (morning or morning and evening).

### ⚠️ 카네덤플러스크림 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you have bacterial or viral skin infections, hypersensitivity to this medicine, eczematous otitis externa with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers, second-degree deep or higher burns/frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, rosacea, syphilis, tuberculosis, or other skin conditions. Do not use if you are a nursing mother, or if you have a skin vaccine reaction. Do not apply to damaged skin or large areas (more than 10% of the body surface area). Before using this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and infants/children should consult a doctor or pharmacist. This medicine is for external use only and should not be taken orally. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. After acute symptoms subside, use a corticosteroid-free medication. In infants and children, avoid long-term use over large areas. Do not use if symptoms do not improve or worsen. If symptoms improve, discontinue use as soon as possible. Do not use occlusive dressing techniques unless directed by a doctor. Be careful as diapers, etc., can have the same effect as an occlusive dressing. When used on the genitals with latex contraceptives (condoms and vaginal suppositories), it may damage the latex contraceptive, thereby reducing the safety and effectiveness of the contraceptive.

# CN

### 💊 카네덤플러스크림 - 功效与作用
*   本品用于伴有炎症、瘙痒和湿疹等临床症状的皮肤真菌病（癣、皮肤念珠菌病、花斑癣）。

### 🕒 카네덤플러스크림 - 用法用量
*   每日1～2次（早晨或早晨、晚上），取适量涂抹于患处（病患部位）。

### ⚠️ 카네덤플러스크림 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   细菌性、病毒性皮肤感染症患者，对本品过敏的患者，鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳道炎患者，溃疡、二度以上深层烧伤/冻伤、口周皮炎、寻常痤疮、酒渣鼻、梅毒、结核等皮肤疾病患者，哺乳期妇女，皮肤疫苗反应者请勿使用本品。请勿涂抹于破损皮肤、大面积（体表面积10%以上）皮肤。孕妇或可能怀孕的妇女、婴幼儿在使用本品前请咨询医生或药师。本品仅供外用，请勿内服。请严格遵守规定的用法用量。给儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。急性症状消失后，请使用不含皮质类固醇的药物。婴幼儿应避免长期在大面积皮肤上使用。症状未改善或恶化时请勿使用。症状改善后，请尽快停止使用。除非医生指示，请勿使用密封敷料法。请注意，纸尿裤等可能产生与密封敷料法相同的作用。与乳胶避孕器具（避孕套和阴道栓）一起用于生殖器时，可能会损坏乳胶避孕器具，从而降低避孕器具的安全性及有效性。

*   1日1～2回（朝または朝、晩）適量を患部（疾患部位）に塗布します。

*   情報なし
