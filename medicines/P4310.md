#EN

### 💊 조인본골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins B1, B6, and D during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of low physical strength during or after illness (while sick or recovering), growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, and joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, poor bone and tooth development, prevention of rickets, calcium deficiency and other calcium supplementation, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 조인본골드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older and adults: Take 1 tablet per dose, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 조인본골드정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or have severe renal failure.
*   Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, infants, patients with cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, patients currently taking cardiac glycosides, individuals with a history of kidney stones, or patients with folate deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
