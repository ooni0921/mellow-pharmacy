#EN
### 💊 마그로민골드프리미엄연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical decline during or after illness (while sick or after recovery), and old age; for the alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders and symptoms like stiff shoulders, neck stiffness, numbness in hands/feet, and cold hands/feet during menopause; for alleviation of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis; and for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그로민골드프리미엄연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 마그로민골드프리미엄연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, soy or peanuts, have a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, are an infant under 12 months, or have severe renal failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatic inflammation, or other lipid metabolism disorders, or patients who should be administered lipid emulsions carefully, or patients at special risk due to lipid overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
