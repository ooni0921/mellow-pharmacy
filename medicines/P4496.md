#EN
### 💊 카보시드정(탄산수소나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for its antacid action and improvement of symptoms in gastric/duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity, improvement of acidosis, promotion of uric acid excretion, and prevention of gout attacks.

### 🕒 카보시드정(탄산수소나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 to 2 tablets (0.5 to 1 g) per dose, with a total daily dose of 3 to 5 g.

The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 카보시드정(탄산수소나트륨) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient who requires sodium restriction (e.g., hypernatremia, edema, pre-eclampsia, bladder stones, hypertension patients), or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, patients with severe gastrointestinal ulcers, renal impairment, cardiac dysfunction, pulmonary dysfunction, electrolyte imbalance such as hypochloremic alkalosis, congestive heart failure, edema or sodium retention, oliguria or anuria should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 카보시드정(탄산수소나트륨) - 功效与作用
*   此药用于胃·十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多的制酸作用及症状改善，酸中毒的改善，促进尿酸排泄和预防痛风发作。

### 🕒 카보시드정(탄산수소나트륨) - 用法用量
*   成人一次服用1~2片(0.5~1 g)，每日总量3~5 g。

可根据年龄、症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 카보시드정(탄산수소나트륨) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   需要限制钠摄入的患者（如高钠血症、水肿、妊娠中毒症、膀胱结石、高血压患者等），以及患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用此药。

服用此药前，患有严重消化道溃疡、肾功能障碍、心功能障碍、肺功能障碍、低氯性碱中毒等电解质紊乱、充血性心力衰竭、水肿或钠潴留、少尿或无尿的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
