#EN
### 💊 콘센비타프리미엄800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement Vitamins D, B1, and B6 for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after recovery from illness when physical strength is decreased, during growth periods, and in old age.
*   This medicine is used to alleviate symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, stiff shoulders, and other joint pains, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, through the vitamins contained within.
*   This medicine is used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue, through the vitamins contained within.

### 🕒 콘센비타프리미엄800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years or older take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 콘센비타프리미엄800정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, or kidney stones.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, are a nursing mother, are a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or have a history of kidney stones.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
