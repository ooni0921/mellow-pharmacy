#EN
### 💊 파워제타비정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical decline during or after illness (while suffering from illness or after recovery), growth, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor bone and teeth development, rickets, and for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 파워제타비정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 19 years and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 파워제타비정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to soybean oil, or have hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, elderly, have hyperoxaluria, gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones or a history of kidney stones, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
