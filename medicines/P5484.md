#EN
### 💊 콘드라맥스800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement Vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after illness when physical strength is low, during growth periods, and in old age.
*   Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used to relieve symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, stiff shoulders, other joint pains, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 콘드라맥스800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years or older should take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 콘드라맥스800정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, an infant under 3 months of age, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or genetic problems such as glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, or have a history of kidney stones.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
