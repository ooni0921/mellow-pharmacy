#EN
### 💊 콘드럭알파정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of low physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth periods, and in the elderly. It also alleviates symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as low back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 콘드럭알파정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and individuals 19 years of age or older, take 1 tablet once a day after a meal.

### ⚠️ 콘드럭알파정 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, infants under 3 months of age, kidney stones, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this drug.

Before taking this drug, infants under 1 year of age, children who get a lot of sunlight and eat a normal diet, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, premature infants, infants, and those with a history of kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this drug, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

This drug may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
