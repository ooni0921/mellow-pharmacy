#EN
### 💊 시메탑정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for stomach pain, heartburn, indigestion, and nausea.

### 🕒 시메탑정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 years to under 80 years should take 2 tablets at a time.

If necessary, 2 tablets can be taken at 4-hour intervals, up to 3 times a day.

However, do not exceed 3 times a day (6 tablets).

Do not continue taking for more than 2 weeks.

### ⚠️ 시메탑정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Patients undergoing dialysis, patients with blood disorders, kidney/liver disease, gastric/duodenal disease, asthma, immune system disorders such as rheumatism, blood abnormalities such as erythrocytopenia, thrombocytopenia, leukocytopenia, individuals with a history of hypersensitivity to histamine H2-receptor antagonists, children (under 15 years of age) and the elderly (80 years of age or older), pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should not take this medication.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity to drugs, are elderly (65 years of age or older), have a sore throat, cough and high fever, unexplained weight loss, persistent abdominal pain, or a history of kidney disease.

Drowsiness may occur during administration, so avoid driving a car or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not continue taking for a long period.

ただし、1日3回（6錠）を超えて服用しないでください。

2週間を超えて継続して服用しないでください。
