#EN
### 💊 디클로하이카타플라스마(디클로페낙나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis and tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, elbow pain such as tennis elbow, myalgia, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 디클로하이카타플라스마(디클로페낙나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply one patch to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 디클로하이카타플라스마(디클로페낙나트륨) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, asthma or hypersensitivity to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), are under 18 years of age, are pregnant beyond 5 months of gestation or in the third trimester of pregnancy, are breastfeeding, or have peptic ulcers.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with bronchial asthma, are pregnant (first and second trimesters), breastfeeding, or potentially pregnant, are a woman who has difficulty conceiving or is undergoing infertility examinations, are elderly, have a history of or known hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, or are an asthma patient with high sulfite sensitivity.

Do not use continuously for more than 2 weeks.

Do not apply to affected skin such as exudative dermatitis, eczema, infected lesions, burns, wounds, or to the eyes and mucous membranes.

Do not use with occlusive dressings without medical supervision.
