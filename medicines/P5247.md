##EN
### 💊 이부브롬연질캡슐 - Efficacy & Effects
*   This medicine is used for pain relief from headaches, toothaches, post-extraction pain, sore throats, earaches, joint pain, neuralgia, lower back pain, muscle pain, stiff shoulders, bruise pain, fracture pain, sprain pain, menstrual pain, and traumatic pain. It is also used as an antipyretic for chills (shivering) and fever.

### 🕒 이부브롬연질캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
*   For adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older: Take 1 capsule per dose, 1 to 3 times a day. Avoid taking on an empty stomach and maintain an interval of at least 4 hours between doses.

### ⚠️ 이부브롬연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication or its components, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months or 2 years of age, or are in the 30th week of pregnancy or later.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant (newborn) or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic predisposition; have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, etc.; are frail or have a high fever; are elderly; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; are breastfeeding; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; or have lost a lot of fluid due to vomiting and diarrhea or are unable to absorb fluids.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

##CN
### 💊 이부브롬연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解头痛、牙痛、拔牙后疼痛、咽喉痛、耳痛、关节痛、神经痛、腰痛、肌肉痛、肩部僵硬、挫伤痛、骨折痛、扭伤痛、月经痛、外伤痛。亦用于寒颤（发冷发抖症状）和发热时的解热。

### 🕒 이부브롬연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   15岁以上及成人：每次1粒，每日1~3次，避免空腹服用，两次服用间隔至少4小时。

### ⚠️ 이부브롬연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒超过三杯的定期饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起胃肠道出血。
*   对本药、本药成分或其他解热镇痛药、感冒药过敏并曾有哮喘病史者，3个月以下及2岁以下婴幼儿，妊娠30周以后者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师：感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿（新生儿）及15岁以下儿童；过敏体质者；有过药物过敏症状（例如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）者；患有肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压等疾病者；体弱者或高热患者；老年人；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠道问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题者；因呕吐和腹泻而大量失水或无法吸收水分者。
*   如果服用5~6次后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
