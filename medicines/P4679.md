#EN

### 💊 브로젠장용정45mg(브로멜라인) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the alleviation of inflammatory symptoms accompanied by edema, and for post-traumatic or post-surgical edema.

### 🕒 브로젠장용정45mg(브로멜라인) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years and older should take 1 to 2 tablets (45 to 90 mg) once, three times a day, 30 minutes before meals with water. Many aspects of its mechanism of action in the body are not yet fully understood, and the relationship between dosage and effect has not been clarified; therefore, do not take it without purpose.

### ⚠️ 브로젠장용정45mg(브로멜라인) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have severe blood coagulation disorders (e.g., hemophilia), severe liver or severe kidney disease (e.g., hemodialysis patients), are taking anticoagulants or antiplatelet agents, are pregnant or breastfeeding, are under 12 years of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, patients with hypersensitivity to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) or those with a history of such hypersensitivity should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
