*   When administering to children, use under the supervision of a guardian.
*   Shake well before use.
*   For external use only.

*   Do not cover or wrap the treated area, and avoid exposure to external heat or hot water.
*   Avoid contact with items that may dissolve in alcohol (e.g., spectacle frames, synthetic fibers).
*   Strong irritation may be felt if water comes into contact with the area where this medication has been applied.
*   Patients with sensitive skin should not use this medication continuously on the same area.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
