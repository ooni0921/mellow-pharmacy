# EN
### 💊 무라졸쿨크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for cutaneous fungal infections (athlete's foot, tinea cruris/jock itch, tinea corporis/ringworm) caused by dermatophytes.

### 🕒 무라졸쿨크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 무라졸쿨크림 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to it, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, or are an infant or child. Do not use on or around the eyes, mucous membranes (mouth, nose, vagina), scrotum, vulva, eczema, wet, eroded, cracked, or severely wounded affected areas, nails, or scalp. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, if the affected area is on the face or is extensive or purulent, or if you have a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not use the medication if the affected area or its surroundings are dirty. Be careful not to let it get into your eyes; if it does, rinse immediately with cold or lukewarm water. This medication is for external use only.

# CN
### 💊 무라졸쿨크림 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于由皮肤癣菌引起的皮肤真菌（霉菌）感染症（脚癣、股癣、体癣）。

### 🕒 무라졸쿨크림 - 用法与用量
* 每日一次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ 무라졸쿨크림 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、婴幼儿和儿童请勿使用本药。请勿用于眼睛或眼睛周围、黏膜（口腔、鼻腔、阴道）、阴囊、外阴、湿疹、湿润（潮湿）、糜烂、龟裂或严重外伤（伤口）的患处、指甲、头皮。在使用本药前，如果您是过敏体质、患处在面部或范围广泛或化脓（化脓）、或对丙二醇过敏或有相关经验，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。请勿在患处或其周围脏污的情况下使用。请注意避免药液进入眼睛，万一进入眼睛，请立即用冷水或温水冲洗。本药仅供外用。
