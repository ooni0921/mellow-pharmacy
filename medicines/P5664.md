#EN

### 💊 알레티브정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Indications & Effects
* This medication is used for the temporary relief of symptoms caused by hay fever or other upper respiratory allergies, such as runny nose, sneezing, itchy and watery eyes, and itching of the nose or throat.

### 🕒 알레티브정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults and adolescents aged 12 years and older should take 1 tablet with water every 12 hours.

Do not exceed 2 tablets within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 알레티브정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to it.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 12 years of age, an elderly person aged 65 years or older, someone unsure if their symptoms are due to allergic rhinitis, a common cold, or other causes, someone with a history of other allergic diseases, someone experiencing severe nasal congestion, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, someone with a history of allergic reactions to drugs, a patient with renal impairment, or a patient with cardiovascular disease.

Avoid breastfeeding while taking this medication.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.
