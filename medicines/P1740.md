# EN
### 💊 콜그린에이캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 콜그린에이캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals. Children aged 8-14 years: Take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 콜그린에이캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have experienced hypersensitivity to this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines (e.g., asthma), if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you are taking MAO inhibitors (e.g., antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinsonian drugs) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks. Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are: an infant or child under 15 years old who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; an infant or toddler aged 3 months to under 2 years; a person prone to allergies; a person who has experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); a person with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty urinating; an elderly person, a frail person, or a person with high fever; a person with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or with ulcers or bleeding issues; a person who has lost a lot of fluids due to vomiting and diarrhea or who cannot absorb fluids; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a breastfeeding woman; a person who smokes, has asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache; or a person hypersensitive to Yellow No. 4 or Yellow No. 5. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

### ⚠️ 콜그린에이캡슐 - 注意事项
* 每日定期饮酒三杯以上者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后，如果出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量请勿超过最大日剂量（4,000毫克）。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
