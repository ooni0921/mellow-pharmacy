#EN
### 💊 더마스킨연고 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for eczema, heat rash, skin itching, allergic skin diseases, burns, and frostbite.

### 🕒 더마스킨연고 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area (diseased part) 2-3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 더마스킨연고 - Precautions
* Do not use on affected areas with chickenpox, athlete's foot, ringworm, or suppuration, mucous membranes such as the conjunctiva, or extensive areas of the face.
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of allergies to medicines or cosmetics, if the affected area is extensive, wet, or severely oozing, or if you are pregnant or may be pregnant.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine is for external use only; do not take internally.

### 🕒 더마스킨연고 - 用法与用量

### 🕒 더마스킨연고 - 用法・用量

* 小児に投与する場合は、保護者の指導監督のもとに投与してください。
* 本剤は外用のみに使用し、内服しないでください。
