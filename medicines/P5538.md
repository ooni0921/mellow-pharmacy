#EN

### 💊 코비메타비정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or recovery), growth period, and old age; for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, and rickets; for the alleviation of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue; and for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 코비메타비정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 12 years of age or older, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 코비메타비정 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, have severe renal insufficiency, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to soybean oil, or have hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are currently receiving medical treatment, have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or young child, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, a history of kidney stones, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, or require careful administration of lipid emulsions.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test results by turning urine yellow.
