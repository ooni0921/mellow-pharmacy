# EN
### 💊 디카맥스에브리원정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supply vitamins D, B1, B2, B6, and C during growth periods, physical fatigue, periods of low physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), old age, pregnancy, and lactation. It is also used for the prevention of poor bone and tooth development, rickets, beriberi, eye strain, and for the alleviation of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 디카맥스에브리원정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age or older should take 1 tablet twice a day, or 2 tablets once a day.

### ⚠️ 디카맥스에브리원정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or have severe renal failure.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who is exposed to a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant/young child, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a history of kidney stones.

    If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

    When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

    This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
