#EN
### 💊 알부타민연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during illness or convalescence, and in old age. It is also used to alleviate symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet caused by peripheral circulatory disorders and during menopause.

### 🕒 알부타민연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 알부타민연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, an infant (general term), a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalate is excreted in the urine), hyperlipoproteinemia, patients with lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or patients who must be cautious when administering lipid emulsions, patients for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload, patients with a predisposition to thrombosis (blood clots), patients with folic acid deficiency, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medication may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, potentially affecting clinical test results.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medication, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 알부타민연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于补充维生素B1、B2、B6、C、E，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠∙哺乳期、病中∙病后体力下降时以及老年期。它还用于缓解由末梢血液循环障碍和更年期引起的肩颈僵硬、手脚发麻、手脚冰凉等症状。

### 🕒 알부타민연질캡슐 - 用法用量
* 成人每次1粒，每日3次。

### ⚠️ 알부타민연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，以下人群请咨询医生或药剂师：未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、幼儿、高草酸尿症患者（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或应慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者、因脂肪超负荷而预计有特殊风险的患者、有血栓（血管堵塞）倾向的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者、对黄色5号过敏的患者或有过敏史的患者。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 进行各种尿液检查时，本药可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变为黄色，从而影响临床检查结果。
* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
* 服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。
