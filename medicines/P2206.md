##EN
### 💊 리버파워포르테연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for chronic liver disease, liver cirrhosis, and toxic liver disease.

### 🕒 리버파워포르테연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 리버파워포르테연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this medication, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you have severe biliary obstruction, or if you are a child under 12 years of age. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or if you are a patient for whom lipid emulsions must be administered cautiously, or if you are a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking the medication, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
