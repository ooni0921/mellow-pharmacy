* 만 15세 이상 소아 및 성인은 1회 3정씩, 1일 3회 식후 또는 식간(식사때와 식사때 사이에)에 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 위제로에스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, gastric discomfort, gastric distention, indigestion (feeling of fullness), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, acid eructation, indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and gastric distention due to indigestion.

### 🕒 위제로에스정 - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 15 years and older and adults, take 3 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, after or between meals.

### ⚠️ 위제로에스정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient undergoing dialysis, a child under 15 years of age, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4), kidney impairment, an allergic constitution, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, or are on a sodium-restricted diet.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
