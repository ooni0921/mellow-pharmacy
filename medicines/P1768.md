* 매일 세잔 이상 정기적 음주자가 이 약 또는 다른 해열진통제를 복용할 때는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오. 간손상을 일으킬 수 있습니다.

매우 드물게 치명적일 수 있는 급성전신발진고름물집증, 스티븐스-존슨증후군, 독성표피괴사용해와 같은 중대한 피부반응이 보고되었고 이 약 복용 후 피부발진 또는 다른 과민반응의 징후가 나타나는 경우 즉시 복용을 중단하십시오.

급성전신발진고름물집증과 같은 중증 피부 이상반응이 나타나는 경우 복용을 중단하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.
* 이 약에 과민증 환자 또는 경험자, 다른 해열진통제, 감기약 복용 시 천식 경험자, 만 3개월 미만의 영아, 중증 또는 조절되지 않는 고혈압 환자, 임신 30주 이후는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

5~6회 복용하여도 증상의 개선이 없을 경우 복용을 즉각 중지하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 그린콜연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effect
* This medicine is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, chills (cold and shivering symptoms), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 그린콜연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 그린콜연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, have experienced asthma when taking other fever reducers/pain relievers or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or are in the 30th week of pregnancy or later.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 2 years of age, an infant or child under 15 years of age who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) appear in relation to the pseudoephedrine component, have an allergic predisposition, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty urinating, are elderly, are frail, have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts for more than a week or recurs, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
