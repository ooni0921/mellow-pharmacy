#EN
### 💊 칼디텍정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy/lactation, growth periods, and old age, for poor development of bones/teeth, prevention of rickets, calcium deficiency, and other calcium supplementation.

### 🕒 칼디텍정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 칼디텍정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, kidney stones, severe renal failure, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or genetic problems such as glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age, children who get a lot of sunlight and eat a normal diet, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, nursing mothers, premature infants, infants, patients with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 칼디텍정 - 用法用量

*   无信息
*   对本品过敏者，高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态），结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病），肾脏疾病，未满12个月的婴儿，肾结石，严重肾功能不全，半乳糖不耐受，Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本品。
