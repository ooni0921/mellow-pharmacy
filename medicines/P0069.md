#EN
### 💊 오로페롤연질캡슐100밀리그램(토코페롤아세테이트) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of symptoms such as Vitamin E deficiency, peripheral circulatory disorders, and menopausal symptoms including shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet, intermittent claudication (limping occasionally), and frostbite.

### 🕒 오로페롤연질캡슐100밀리그램(토코페롤아세테이트) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 50 IU to 1,000 IU of Vitamin E per day.

### ⚠️ 오로페롤연질캡슐100밀리그램(토코페롤아세테이트) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, to soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are a lactating woman. Avoid high-dose use of this medicine during pregnancy. Before taking this medicine, pregnant women, patients with thrombotic predisposition, those with a history of bronchial asthma, rash, hives, etc., patients with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or those for whom special risks are expected due to lipid overload, children taking iron supplements, and patients with hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 or Yellow No. 5, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
