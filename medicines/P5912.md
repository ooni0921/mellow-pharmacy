#EN
### 💊 신신플렉스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for neuralgia, lumbago (lower back pain), and stiff shoulders.

### 🕒 신신플렉스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 tablets once, twice a day, when experiencing pain or an attack.

### ⚠️ 신신플렉스정 - Precautions
* No information.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant (baby) suspected of or infected with chickenpox or influenza; an infant or child under 14 years of age; prone to allergies; have experienced allergic reactions (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver, kidney, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure; are physically weak; have a high fever; are elderly; are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant; or are breastfeeding.
* If symptoms do not improve after several doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
