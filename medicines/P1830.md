#EN
### 💊 콜민-에이시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the temporary relief of sneezing, runny nose, stuffy nose, watery eyes due to common cold or allergic and vasomotor rhinitis, and nasal congestion due to sinusitis.

### 🕒 콜민-에이시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* Children 12 years and older and adults: Take 10 mL every 4 hours, not to exceed 60 mL in 24 hours.
* Children 6 to under 12 years of age: Take 5 mL every 4 hours, not to exceed 30 mL in 24 hours.
* Children 2 to under 6 years of age: Follow physician's instructions.

### ⚠️ 콜민-에이시럽 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, amine-type sympathomimetics, or antihistamines; idiosyncratic reactions; are a nursing mother; or have severe hypertension, severe coronary artery disease, narrow-angle glaucoma, urinary retention, peptic ulcer, asthma, emphysema, chronic pulmonary disease, or shortness of breath or difficulty breathing.
* Before taking this medicine, patients with difficulty breathing due to chronic bronchitis or other conditions, heart disease, hypertension, thyroid dysfunction, diabetes, glaucoma, difficulty urinating due to prostatic hypertrophy, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery after taking this medicine.

#CN
### 💊 콜민-에이시럽 - 功效与作用
* 本药品用于暂时缓解因感冒、过敏性及血管舒缩性鼻炎引起的喷嚏、流鼻涕、鼻塞、流泪，以及因鼻窦炎引起的鼻充血（鼻塞）。

### 🕒 콜민-에이시럽 - 用法与用量
* 12岁及以上儿童和成人：每4小时服用10 mL，24小时内不得超过60 mL。
* 6岁及以上至12岁以下儿童：每4小时服用5 mL，24小时内不得超过30 mL。
* 2岁及以上至6岁以下儿童：请遵医嘱。

### ⚠️ 콜민-에이시럽 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药品、胺类拟交感神经药或抗组胺药有过敏史的患者、特异体质者、哺乳期妇女、患有严重高血压、严重冠状动脉疾病、窄角型青光眼、尿潴留、消化性溃疡、哮喘、肺气肿、慢性肺病、呼吸急促或呼吸困难的患者，请勿服用本药品。
* 在服用本药品前，患有慢性支气管炎等引起的呼吸困难、心脏病、高血压、甲状腺功能疾病、糖尿病、青光眼、因前列腺肥大引起的排尿困难的患者以及儿童，请咨询医生或药师。
* 服用后，驾驶或操作机器时请注意。
