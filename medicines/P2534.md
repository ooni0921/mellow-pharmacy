#EN
### 💊 비타맥스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of physical weakness (during or after illness), growth phases, and old age. It also helps prevent poor bone and tooth development, rickets, and alleviates symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis, and supplements zinc.

### 🕒 비타맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비타맥스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this product, an infant under 12 months old, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, folic acid deficiency, or have experienced hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 and Yellow No. 5.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
