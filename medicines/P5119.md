#EN
### 💊 마그온플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of symptoms such as physical fatigue, vitamin E, B1, B6 supplementation during pregnancy/lactation, physical decline during or after illness, and in old age; peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms including stiff shoulders/neck, numbness in hands/feet, cold hands/feet, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis; and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그온플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years or older and adults should take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그온플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months, have severe renal failure, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to soybean oil, or have hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.
    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient receiving medical treatment, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a person with hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
    Administer with caution to patients with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or to patients for whom lipid emulsions should be administered cautiously.
    Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   本药品用于缓解以下症状：身体疲劳；孕期、哺乳期、病中或病后体力下降时以及老年人补充维生素E、B1、B6；末梢血液循环障碍和更年期引起的肩颈僵硬、手脚发麻、手脚冰凉、脚气病；眼睛疲劳；神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩部僵硬等）；口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等；以及缓解因镁缺乏引起的肌肉痉挛。

### 🕒 마그온플러스연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   12岁及以上儿童和成人，每次1粒，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 마그온플러스연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本品过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、重度肾功能不全患者、对大豆油有过敏或过敏反应史者、对大豆或花生过敏的患者，请勿服用本品。
    服用本品前，正在接受医生治疗的患者、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、心血管功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、对柠檬黄（Sunset Yellow FCF）过敏或有过敏反应史者，请咨询医生或药师。
    对高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需慎重使用脂质乳剂的患者，请慎重给药。
