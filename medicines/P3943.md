# EN
### 💊 엑쎈코연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 엑쎈코연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 15 years and older, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 엑쎈코연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, stop taking it immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) occur, stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or are in the 30th week of pregnancy or later.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; a child under 2 years of age; experiencing symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) in relation to pseudoephedrine; have an allergic constitution or have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, physically weak, or have a high fever; are experiencing persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding; have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after taking the medication 5 to 6 times, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN

### 🕒 엑쎈코연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
* 15岁以上及成人，一次2粒，一日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 엑쎈코연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯以上且规律饮酒者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。

极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征和中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后，如果出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。

请勿超过对乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000 mg）。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。

如果出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等严重皮肤不良反应，请停止服用本药并咨询医生或药师。
* 对本药有超敏反应史者，服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾发生哮喘者，未满3个月的婴儿，患有严重或无法控制的高血压者，以及妊娠30周及以后者，请勿服用本药。

在服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师：感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿及15岁以下儿童；未满2岁的儿童；与伪麻黄碱成分相关出现缺血性结肠炎症状（如：急剧腹痛、直肠出血等）者；过敏体质者，或有药物引起的过敏症状（如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）史者；患有肝病、肾病、心脏病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（如：眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者；老年人、体弱者或高热者；有胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠道问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女；吸烟、患有哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有大量痰的咳嗽、持续或反复发作超过1周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽者。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

服药期间请勿饮酒。

服药后可能会出现困倦，因此驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

如果服用5~6次后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
