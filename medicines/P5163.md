#EN

### 💊 프렌즈아이드롭케어점안액(1회용) - Indications & Effects
* This medicine is used for eye fatigue, conjunctival hyperemia (redness), discomfort in eyes after swimming or when dust or sweat gets into eyes, eye inflammation due to UV rays and other light, blepharitis (sore eyelids), discomfort when wearing hard contact lenses, itchy eyes, blurred vision, and excessive eye discharge.

### 🕒 프렌즈아이드롭케어점안액(1회용) - Dosage & Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops at a time, 5-6 times a day.
* Discard any remaining solution and the container immediately after instillation.

### ⚠️ 프렌즈아이드롭케어점안액(1회용) - Precautions
* No information
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you experience severe eye pain, have a history of allergic reactions to eye drops (e.g., eye hyperemia, itching, swelling, rash, redness), or have glaucoma.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
* When using for children, use under the supervision of a guardian.
* Use this medicine only for ophthalmic instillation.
* Do not use as a soft contact lens wetting solution, or while wearing soft contact lenses.
* Be careful not to let the tip of the container touch your eyelids or eyelashes, as the solution may become contaminated or cloudy due to eye discharge, fungi, etc. Do not use if the solution is cloudy.
* To prevent contamination, avoid sharing this product with others if possible.
* Do not administer if the expiry date has passed, and use as soon as possible after opening.
* If red crystals from the ingredients adhere around the dropper tip and inside the cap, gently wipe them off with a clean gauze before use.
