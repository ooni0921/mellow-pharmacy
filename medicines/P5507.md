#EN
### 💊 베타셀민연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for physical fatigue; supplementation of vitamins A, C, and E during pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness, and old age; relief of dry eyes; night blindness (difficulty seeing at night); relief of hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases; prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds); relief of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in the hands and feet; and supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 베타셀민연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 베타셀민연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Administration of vitamin A exceeding 5,000 IU per day may cause congenital malformations. Do not administer to women within 3 months of pregnancy or those who may be pregnant.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this product, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant or infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or require careful administration of lipid emulsions, or are at special risk of fat overload.
*   If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   It may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests.
