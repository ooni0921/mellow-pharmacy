#EN
### 💊 쌩코에이연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills (shivering sensation), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 쌩코에이연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: take 1 capsule once, 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 쌩코에이연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) appear when taking pseudoephedrine-containing medicines, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it before, if you have experienced asthma reactions when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics and cold medicines, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age; an infant (newborn baby) or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; a person with a predisposition to hives, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraine, food allergies, etc., either personally or in your family; a person who has experienced allergic reactions (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); a person with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or dysuria (difficulty urinating); an elderly person; a person with a weak constitution or high fever; a person with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or with ulcer or bleeding problems; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a nursing mother; a person with a cough (cough due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, cough accompanied by fever, rash or persistent headache); or a person with hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF) and Red No. 40 (Allura Red AC).

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
