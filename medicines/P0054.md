#EN
### 💊 Decakinon Capsule (Ubidecarenone) - Indications and Efficacy
* This medication is used for mild to moderate congestive heart failure.

### 🕒 Decakinon Capsule (Ubidecarenone) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2 capsules (10 mg) at a time, 3 times a day after meals. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ Decakinon Capsule (Ubidecarenone) - Precautions
* No specific information provided.
* Before taking this medication, patients with a history of hypersensitivity or current hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 and Yellow No. 5, or elderly patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
