* 만 8세 이상 및 성인은 1일 1회, 1회 1~3정을 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 비아콘정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the replenishment of vitamins B1, B2, B6, and C when experiencing physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, or during and after illness (while sick or recovering), leading to a decline in physical strength. It is also used for the relief of angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 비아콘정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 to 3 tablets once daily.

### ⚠️ 비아콘정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not administer this medicine to patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, infants under 3 months of age, or patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, premature infants, infants (general term for young children), patients with hyperoxaluria, patients with gout, or patients with kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision and guidance of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

#CN
### 💊 비아콘정 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠・哺乳期、病中・病后（生病期间或恢复后）体力下降时维生素B1、B2、B6、C的补充，以及口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎。

### 🕒 비아콘정 - 用法及用量
*   8岁以上及成人，每日1次，每次服用1～3片。

### ⚠️ 비아콘정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药过敏的患者、未满3个月的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症患者、痛风患者或肾结石患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药可能会干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。
