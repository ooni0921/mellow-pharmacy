### 🕒 디알프레쉬에프점안액(카르복시메틸셀룰로오스나트륨)(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Instill 1 to 2 drops into the affected eye(s) as needed. Discard any remaining solution and the container after use.

### ⚠️ 디알프레쉬에프점안액(카르복시메틸셀룰로오스나트륨)(1회용) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine should not use it.

Before using this medicine, patients with severe eye pain, or those who have previously experienced allergic symptoms due to eye drops (e.g., eye hyperemia, itching, swelling, rash, redness due to congestion) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine is for ophthalmic use only.

Do not reuse after a single use; discard any remaining solution and the container.

To avoid contamination, do not touch the tip of the container to any surface.

Do not use as a wetting solution for soft contact lenses or while wearing soft contact lenses.

Contact lenses should be removed before using this medicine and can be reinserted 15 minutes after instillation.

If using with other eye drops, instill this medicine at least 15 minutes apart from the other eye drops.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

Do not use if the solution changes color or becomes cloudy.

Due to its high viscosity, temporary blurred vision may occur after instillation of this medicine.
