#EN

### 💊 코리투살에프코프시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for cough, phlegm, and asthma.

### 🕒 코리투살에프코프시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 15 years or older: 10 mL per dose.
* Children aged 11 years or older but under 15 years: 6.67 mL per dose.
* Children aged 8 years or older but under 11 years: 5 mL per dose.
* Children aged 5 years or older but under 8 years: 3.33 mL per dose.
* Children aged 3 years or older but under 5 years: 2.5 mL per dose.
* Children aged 2 years or older but under 3 years: 2 mL per dose.
* Take 5 to 6 times a day.
* Administer at intervals of approximately 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 코리투살에프코프시럽 - Precautions
* No specific information.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine and infants under 3 months of age should not take this medicine.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a child aged 3 months or older but under 2 years, elderly, frail, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, high fever, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts for more than 1 week or recurs, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
