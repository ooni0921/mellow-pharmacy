### 🕒 비타케어에프정 - 용법 및 용량
* 만 8세 이상 및 성인 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 비타케어에프정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement Vitamins A, B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of physical weakness during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It also helps alleviate dry eyes, improve night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), prevent poor bone and teeth development, rickets, prevent and treat iron deficiency anemia, and provide zinc.

### 🕒 비타케어에프정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비타케어에프정 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A daily may cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medicine if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy. Keep out of reach of children, as an overdose in children under 6 years of age can lead to fatal iron poisoning.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to any component of this medicine, hypercalcemia (excessive calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, infants under 12 months of age, hyperkalemia, hemochromatosis (a disorder of iron metabolism where iron deposits in the liver and pancreas), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (excessive oxalate excretion in urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, premature infants, infants, patients with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, patients with cardiovascular system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, acute or chronic bronchitis, pulmonary edema, alkalosis, history of peptic ulcer, gout or kidney stones, patients with folic acid deficiency, or patients with thrombotic predisposition. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may change the color of urine to yellow, affecting clinical test values.
