# EN
### 💊 조인트빅300연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the replenishment of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of reduced physical strength during or after illness, and in old age. It also alleviates symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness/tingling in hands and feet, cold hands and feet, neuralgia, myalgia, and arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness). Furthermore, it is used for the alleviation of symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, as well as for beriberi and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 조인트빅300연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 8 years or older should take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 조인트빅300연질캡슐 - Precautions
* None
* Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medication, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: an infant under 1 year of age; pregnant or potentially pregnant, or a nursing mother; a premature infant; an infant/young child (general term); a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, or a patient requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions; a patient for whom special risks are expected due to lipid overload; a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage); a patient with folic acid deficiency; or a patient with or who has experienced hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
