### 💊 센스데이큐정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for contraception and for the treatment of moderate acne in women who can take this medication.

### 🕒 센스데이큐정 - Dosage and Administration
* Take 1 tablet daily for 21 days according to the indicated order, followed by a 7-day tablet-free interval.

For detailed information, please refer to the detailed information in the approved prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 센스데이큐정 - Precautions
* Women taking oral contraceptives should refrain from smoking, as there may be an increased risk of serious cardiovascular events such as thrombosis. Smokers aged 35 or older should not take this medication.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, estrogen-dependent tumors (e.g., endometrial cancer), breast cancer patients or suspected/experienced individuals, thrombophlebitis, thromboembolism patients or experienced individuals, deep vein thrombosis, congenital or acquired thrombophilia, cerebrovascular or coronary artery disease (especially myocardial infarction), undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding, moderate to severe hypertension, moderate to severe liver dysfunction, jaundice, hepatitis, and benign or malignant liver tumor patients or experienced individuals, severe diabetes with vascular lesions, severe metabolic disorders such as severe hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, and porphyria, persistent itching during pregnancy, herpes gestationis, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant and nursing mothers, pituitary tumors, hyperprolactinemia, cardiac valvular disease, diabetes patients with vascular lesions, pancreatitis, patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, patients with congenital or acquired hypercoagulability, and smokers aged 35 or older.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker, have hypertension, are obese, are a woman over 40 years of age, have epilepsy, migraine, a family history of arterial disease or a history of varicose veins, systemic lupus erythematosus, a family history of breast cancer, a history of depression, heart or kidney failure, atypical hyperplastic mammary disease, a history of breast nodules, cardiac valvular disease, atrial fibrillation, a family history of hyperlipidemia, a history of gallbladder disease and gallstones, endometriosis, asthma or chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorders, diabetes, or migraine without aura.

This medication does not prevent HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases.

If you have severe gastrointestinal disorders, absorption may be incomplete, so use an additional method of contraception.

This medication is not applicable to pre-menarcheal and post-menopausal women.

For detailed precautions, please check the detailed information in the approved prescribing information.
