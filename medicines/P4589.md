#EN
### 💊 알카본정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy/lactation, growth periods, and old age, as well as for the prevention of bone and tooth developmental disorders and rickets.

### 🕒 알카본정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 알카본정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.
*   If there is no improvement in symptoms after taking the medication for more than 1 month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
