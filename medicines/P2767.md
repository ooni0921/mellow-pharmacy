# EN
### 💊 세토펜건조시럽(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain associated with colds, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 세토펜건조시럽(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For children 12 years of age or younger, take the recommended single dose every 4 to 6 hours as needed. This medicine should be taken at the lowest effective dose for the shortest possible duration, and should not be taken more than 5 times a day (75 mg/kg). Refer to the approved product information for recommended doses by age group.

### ⚠️ 세토펜건조시럽(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body. Therefore, patients with phenylketonuria who need to restrict phenylalanine intake should not take this medicine. Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by NSAIDs) or a history thereof, or if you have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol. Patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, a history of peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, a child under 2 years of age, or are pregnant or breastfeeding.

### ⚠️ 세토펜건조시럽(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
*   若每日饮用三杯以上酒精饮品的人士服用本品或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能会导致肝损伤。本品含有阿斯巴甜，其在体内代谢为苯丙氨酸，因此，需要限制苯丙氨酸摄入的苯丙酮尿症患者请勿服用本品。极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性全身性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。若服用本品后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量不得超过最大日剂量（4,000 mg）。这可能会导致肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
*   对本品过敏的患者、有消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、阿司匹林哮喘（非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘发作）或有其病史的患者，以及服用巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药、酒精的人士请勿服用本品。患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本品。在服用本品前，肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、有消化性溃疡病史、血液异常、有出血倾向的患者、有心脏功能异常的患者、有过敏史的患者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、2岁以下儿童、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药剂师。
