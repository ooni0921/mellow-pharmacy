#EN
### 💊 치오맥스연고 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 치오맥스연고 - Dosage and Administration
* **For topical application:** Apply directly to the affected area up to 3 times a day.
* **For rectal administration:**
    * Adults and adolescents 15 years of age or older: Insert 2 g rectally up to 3 times a day.
    * Children and adolescents 7 to under 15 years of age: Insert 1 g rectally up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 치오맥스연고 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication if you have a suppurative lesion in the affected area, heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, difficulty urinating due to prostatic hypertrophy, are a nursing mother, or are a child under 7 years of age.

Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When used in children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medication is for anal use only and should not be used for ophthalmic purposes.

If symptoms do not improve after about 1 week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
