#EN

### 💊 벤트플라겔 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of varicose edema, superficial phlebitis, traumatic injuries from accidents, sports injuries (bruises), and tenosynovitis.

### 🕒 벤트플라겔 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply a thin layer to the affected or infected area several times a day, then gently rub it into the skin.

### ⚠️ 벤트플라겔 - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication or salicylates, if you are within 4 weeks of your due date, on mucous membranes, or on open wounds. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a neonate or child, have renal insufficiency, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, or are breastfeeding. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Be careful to avoid contact with eyes and do not apply too broadly. For external use only. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
