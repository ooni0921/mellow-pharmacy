#EN
### 💊 Baikol Syrup - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for the temporary relief of sneezing, runny nose, nasal congestion, and watery eyes caused by common cold, allergic rhinitis, or vasomotor rhinitis, and for the temporary relief of nasal congestion due to sinusitis.

### 🕒 Baikol Syrup - Dosage and Administration
* Children 12 years of age and older and adults: Take 10 mL every 4 hours. Do not exceed 60 mL in 24 hours.

Children 6 years of age and older to under 12 years of age: Take 5 mL every 4 hours. Do not exceed 30 mL in 24 hours.

Children 2 years of age and older to under 6 years of age: Follow physician's instructions.

### ⚠️ Baikol Syrup - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or to sympathomimetic amines or antihistamines, if you have an idiosyncrasy, if you are a nursing mother, or if you have severe hypertension, severe coronary artery disease, narrow-angle glaucoma, urinary retention, peptic ulcer, asthma, emphysema, chronic lung disease, or shortness of breath or dyspnea.

Before taking this medicine, patients with breathing difficulties such as chronic bronchitis, heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid dysfunction, diabetes, glaucoma, or difficulty urinating due to prostatic hypertrophy, and children, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery after taking this medicine.

#CN
### 💊 Baikol Syrup - 功效与作用
* 本药用于暂时缓解感冒、过敏性鼻炎或血管舒缩性鼻炎引起的打喷嚏、流鼻涕、鼻塞、流泪，以及鼻窦炎引起的鼻充血（鼻塞）。

### 🕒 Baikol Syrup - 用法与用量
* 12岁及以上儿童和成人：每4小时服用10毫升，24小时内不要超过60毫升。

6岁及以上至12岁以下儿童：每4小时服用5毫升，24小时内不要超过30毫升。

2岁及以上至6岁以下儿童：请遵医嘱。

### ⚠️ Baikol Syrup - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药或胺类拟交感神经药、抗组胺药过敏者，特异体质者，哺乳期妇女，患有严重高血压、严重冠状动脉疾病、窄角型青光眼、尿潴留、消化性溃疡、哮喘、肺气肿、慢性肺疾病、气短或呼吸困难的患者，请勿服用本药。

在服用本药前，患有慢性支气管炎等呼吸困难、心脏病、高血压、甲状腺功能障碍、糖尿病、青光眼、前列腺肥大引起的排尿困难的患者，以及儿童，应咨询医生或药师。

服药后驾驶或操作机器时请注意安全。
