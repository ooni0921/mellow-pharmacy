#EN
### 💊 비바원정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1 and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, and physical weakness during or after illness; for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders); for beriberi; and for eye strain.

### 🕒 비바원정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 8 years of age and older and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비바원정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age, pregnant or potentially pregnant women, nursing mothers, premature infants, toddlers, and patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 비바원정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠期·哺乳期、病中·病后体力下降时补充维生素B1、B6，以及缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）症状，脚气病，眼睛疲劳。

### 🕒 비바원정 - 用法与用量
* 8岁以上及成人，一次1片，一日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 비바원정 - 注意事项
* 对本药过敏者、未满3个月的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、叶酸缺乏症患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

服用约1个月后症状仍未改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。
