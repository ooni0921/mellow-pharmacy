#EN
### 💊 쎈비백뉴로업정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins (D, B1, B2, B6) during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical decline due to illness or recovery, growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for poor bone and teeth development, prevention of rickets, alleviation of beriberi and eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation of the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, and for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 쎈비백뉴로업정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adolescents aged 12 years and older and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 쎈비백뉴로업정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, infants, individuals with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, those with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
