# EN
### 💊 센쥬씨엘점안액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for eye fatigue, tear supplementation (dry eyes), discomfort when wearing hard or soft contact lenses, and blurred vision (e.g., when there is a lot of eye discharge).

### 🕒 센쥬씨엘점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops 5-6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 센쥬씨엘점안액 - Precautions
* No information available
* Before using this medication, patients with hypersensitivity to eye drops, severe eye pain, or glaucoma should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. Use this medication for instillation only. To prevent contamination, avoid sharing as much as possible.

# CN
### 💊 센쥬씨엘점안액 - 功效与效果
* 本药用于缓解眼睛疲劳、补充泪液（眼睛干涩）、佩戴硬性或软性隐形眼镜时的不适感、以及眼睛模糊（如眼屎多时）。

### 🕒 센쥬씨엘점안액 - 用法用量
* 每日5-6次，每次滴入2-3滴。

### ⚠️ 센쥬씨엘점안액 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 在使用本药前，对眼药水过敏者、眼睛疼痛剧烈者、青光眼患者请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。本药仅限滴眼用。为防止污染，请尽量避免与他人共用。
