#EN
### 💊 니오틴정(비오틴) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for nail, toenail, or hair growth disorders caused by biotin deficiency.

### 🕒 니오틴정(비오틴) - Dosage and Administration
* Take 1 tablet once daily before meals.

### ⚠️ 니오틴정(비오틴) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
