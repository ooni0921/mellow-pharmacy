*   This medicine is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids, and for disinfection.

### 🕒 프라맥스에스좌제 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and individuals aged 15 years or older should insert 1 suppository rectally, up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 프라맥스에스좌제 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not use this medicine if you have heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, or difficulty urinating due to prostate enlargement.

Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medicine.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not use in children.

If the suppository becomes soft, use it after briefly cooling; if it becomes excessively hard, soften it before use.

This medicine is for rectal use only and should not be taken orally.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

*   本藥用於暫時緩解肛裂、痔瘡引起的疼痛、瘙癢、腫脹、出血，並具有消毒作用。

### 🕒 프라맥스에스좌제 - 用法與用量
*   15歲及以上成人，每次1粒，每日最多3次，直腸內給藥。

### ⚠️ 프라맥스에스좌제 - 注意事項
*   無信息。
*   心臟病、高血壓、甲狀腺疾病、糖尿病、因前列腺肥大導致排尿困難的患者請勿使用本藥。

孕婦或可能懷孕的女性在使用本藥前應諮詢醫生或藥劑師。

請嚴格遵守規定的用法和用量。

兒童請勿使用。

如果本藥變軟，請稍作冷卻後使用；如果過於堅硬，請軟化後再使用。

本藥僅供直腸使用，請勿口服。

若使用約7天後症狀仍無改善，請立即停止使用並諮詢醫生或藥劑師。
