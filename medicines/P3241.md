#EN
### 💊 센티렉스실버어드밴스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for vitamin supplementation during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of reduced physical strength during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It is also used to alleviate dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), poor development of bones and teeth, and prevent rickets.

### 🕒 센티렉스실버어드밴스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 1 tablet once daily, with a meal.

### ⚠️ 센티렉스실버어드밴스정 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A daily can cause congenital malformations. Women who are within 3 months of pregnancy or are likely to be pregnant should not take this drug. Overdose in children under 6 years of age can cause fatal iron poisoning, so keep out of reach of children.
*   Patients with hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this drug, infants under 12 months, hyperkalemia, Wilson's disease, hemochromatosis, hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, or severe renal failure should not take this drug. Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are a nursing mother, are a premature infant or infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, are taking cardiac glycosides, have gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, biliary obstruction, copper deficiency, acute or chronic bronchitis, pulmonary edema, alkalosis, peptic ulcer, diabetes, gout, a history of kidney stones, folic acid deficiency, or a thrombotic predisposition. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. The Vitamin A contained in this drug is sufficiently supplied through a normal diet, so the dose for adjunctive therapy should not exceed 5,000 IU per day. This drug may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values, or interfere with blood sugar detection during urine tests.
