#EN
### 💊 Norumo S Granules - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, gastric distension, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour burping, loss of appetite (anorexia), dyspepsia, overeating, and promotion of digestion.

### 🕒 Norumo S Granules - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years or older: 1 sachet per dose.
*   For individuals aged 11 years or older and under 15 years: 2/3 sachet per dose.
*   For individuals aged 8 years or older and under 11 years: 1/2 sachet per dose.
*   For individuals aged 5 years or older and under 8 years: 1/3 sachet per dose.
*   For individuals aged 3 years or older and under 5 years: 1/4 sachet per dose.
*   For individuals aged 1 year or older and under 3 years: 1/5 sachet per dose.
*   Take 3 times a day. Maintain a dosing interval of at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ Norumo S Granules - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you are receiving dialysis therapy, are an infant under 3 months old, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, phosphate deficiency (hypophosphatemia), high blood pressure, heart or kidney disorders, edema, are elderly, or are on a sodium-restricted diet. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
