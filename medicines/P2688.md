* 만 12세 이상 청소년 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

# EN
### 💊 뉴트리-비프리미엄정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness (while sick or after recovery), and old age. It also helps relieve symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lumbago, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis (cheilosis), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, and for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 뉴트리-비프리미엄정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adolescents aged 12 years and older and adults: Take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 뉴트리-비프리미엄정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months old, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, or have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional colitis, cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney (renal) impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), or folic acid deficiency.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 1 month, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

# CN

### 🕒 뉴트리-비프리미엄정 - 用法用量
*   12岁及以上青少年及成人：一次1片，一日1次。

### ⚠️ 뉴트리-비프리미엄정 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本品过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本品。
*   在服用本品前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病、心血管功能障碍、肾脏（肾）功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风、肾结石、有血栓（血管堵塞）倾向的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者请咨询医生或药师。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本品在进行各种尿液检查时可能会干扰血糖检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检测结果。
