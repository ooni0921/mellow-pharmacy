#EN
### 💊 엑세라민엔정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or convalescence (when physical strength is reduced), during growth periods, and in old age.

This medicine, through its contained vitamins, is used to relieve symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, shoulder stiffness, and other joint pains, as well as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

This medicine, through its contained vitamins, is used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 엑세라민엔정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 8 years of age and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 엑세라민엔정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with heart and circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and can affect clinical test values.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN
### 💊 엑세라민엔정 - 功效及作用
* 本药用于补充维生素D、B1、B2、B6，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后体力下降时、发育期、老年期。

本药通过所含维生素等，用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、腰痛或肩颈僵硬等关节痛，以及口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

本药通过所含维生素等，用于骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病预防、脚气病、眼疲劳。

### 🕒 엑세라민엔정 - 用法用量
* 8岁及以上儿童和成人，每次1片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 엑세라민엔정 - 注意事项
* 对本药过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钙血症患者、结节病患者、肾脏疾病患者、肾结石患者、严重肾功能不全患者、患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、经常晒太阳并正常饮食的儿童、心血管功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、有肾结石病史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

服用约1个月后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用本药并咨询医生或药师。

叶酸缺乏症患者请咨询医生或药师。

儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

本药可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检查结果。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
