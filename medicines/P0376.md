### 💊 Tabex Gel - Indications & Effects
* This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammation of bruises and sprains.

### 🕒 Tabex Gel - Dosage & Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area once to several times a day.

### ⚠️ Tabex Gel - Precautions
* No specific information
* Do not use this medicine on wounds, mucous membranes, or areas undergoing radiation therapy.
* Before using this medicine, pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine is for external use only; do not take internally.
* Be careful not to get it in your eyes, and if it does get into your eyes, rinse immediately with water.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
