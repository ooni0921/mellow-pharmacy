#EN
### 💊 바이타민마하정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical debility during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth periods, and old age; prevention of rickets and maldevelopment of bones and teeth; relief of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis; and replenishment of zinc.

### 🕒 바이타민마하정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 바이타민마하정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, hypersensitive to soy or peanuts, have a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, premature infant, infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, etc., cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones or a history thereof, dyslipidemia such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or patients who need careful administration of lipid emulsions, or patients for whom a special risk is anticipated due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
