#EN
### 💊 토마쎈플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for vitamin E supplementation in the elderly, for peripheral circulatory disorders, for the alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness of hands and feet (cold hands/feet) during menopause, and for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 토마쎈플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adolescents aged 12 years and older and adults should take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 토마쎈플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal failure.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, nursing, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, cardiovascular/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or if you are a patient requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient at special risk due to fat overload, or a patient with a thrombotic predisposition.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

* 本药用于老年人补充维生素E，改善末梢血液循环障碍，缓解更年期时出现的肩颈僵硬、手脚发麻、手脚冰凉等症状，以及治疗因镁缺乏引起的肌肉痉挛。

### 🕒 토마쎈플러스연질캡슐 - 用法用量
* 12岁以上青少年及成人，每次1粒，每日服用2次。

### ⚠️ 토마쎈플러스연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 对本药、大豆油、大豆或花生过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、重症肾功能不全患者请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师，如果您是孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性肠炎等胃肠道疾病者、心血管循环系统功能障碍者、肾功能障碍者、低蛋白血症者、高脂蛋白血症者、糖尿病性高血脂症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要谨慎给予脂质乳剂的患者，或因脂肪过载预计有特殊风险的患者，或有血栓形成倾向的患者。
