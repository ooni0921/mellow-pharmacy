#EN
### 💊 기공활혈고(수입명:CHIKUNGHUOSHIUEHKAO) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory (anti-swelling) effects in cases of bruises, stiff shoulders, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 기공활혈고(수입명:CHIKUNGHUOSHIUEHKAO) - Dosage and Administration
* After thoroughly cleaning and drying the affected area (diseased part), apply one patch once daily.
Secure with a bandage or adhesive plaster if necessary.

### ⚠️ 기공활혈고(수입명:CHIKUNGHUOSHIUEHKAO) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer poisoning, or open wounds.
Before using this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity to medicines or cosmetics, those with allergic constitution, patients with severe dampness or erosion, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
For external use only.
If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 기공활혈고(수입명:CHIKUNGHUOSHIUEHKAO) - 注意事项

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
给儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
仅供外用。
