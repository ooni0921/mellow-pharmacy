### 💊 이지콜드노즈연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (rhinorrhea, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 이지콜드노즈연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older and adults, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 이지콜드노즈연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or a history of it, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure, or are in the 30th week of pregnancy or later.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child aged 3 months to under 2 years, an infant or child under 15 years suspected or infected with chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have experienced drug allergies, have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for a prolonged period.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
