### 💊 신콜에프연질캡슐 - Indications and Usage
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 신콜에프연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and adolescents 15 years of age and older: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 신콜에프연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, infants under 3 months of age, or patients with severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, an infant under 2 years of age, a young child, a person prone to allergies, a person with a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), a person with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric or duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, an elderly person, a frail person or a person with high fever, a person with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or a history of ulcers or bleeding problems, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a smoker, a person with asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take this medicine continuously for a long period.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for 5 to 6 times, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
