### 💊 투엑스비콘드로800정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for the supplementation of Vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of low physical strength during or after illness, growth periods, and old age, and for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, and for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation in the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 투엑스비콘드로800정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 19 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 투엑스비콘드로800정 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this drug if you have: hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months, have kidney stones, or have severe renal failure.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug if you are: an infant under 1 year, a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, a young child, a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, a patient currently taking cardiac glycosides, or a patient with a history of kidney stones.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue the drug immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

This drug may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
