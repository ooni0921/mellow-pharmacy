#EN
### 💊 Dentinein Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 Dentinein Capsule - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Dentinein Capsule - Precautions
*   Do not administer this drug to children under 15 years of age.
*   Patients hypersensitive to this drug, those with a history of hypersensitivity (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to cosmetics or eggs, or individuals with an allergic constitution should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
