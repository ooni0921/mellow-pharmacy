#EN
### 💊 마파람과립 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 마파람과립 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 15 years and older: 1 sachet per dose.
*   Aged 11 years and older to under 15 years: 2/3 sachet per dose.
*   Aged 7 years and older to under 11 years: 1/2 sachet per dose.
*   Aged 3 years and older to under 7 years: 1/3 sachet per dose.
*   Aged 2 years and older to under 3 years: 1/4 sachet per dose.
*   Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 마파람과립 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic constitution or have experienced allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), dysuria; are elderly, physically weak, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or are breastfeeding; have a cough (due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, cough accompanied by fever, rash or persistent headache); or are a child under 2 years of age.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
