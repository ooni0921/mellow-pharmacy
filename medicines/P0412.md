#EN
### 💊 알콤정(플루벤다졸) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the treatment of infections caused by roundworm (Ascaris), pinworm (Enterobius), whipworm (Trichuris), hookworm (Ancylostoma/Necator), and mixed infections thereof.

### 🕒 알콤정(플루벤다졸) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children take 1 tablet (500 mg) once.

### ⚠️ 알콤정(플루벤다졸) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it or if you are pregnant. Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age, pregnant or possibly pregnant women, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
