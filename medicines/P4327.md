#EN

### 💊 바스크롱에프캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 바스크롱에프캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 15 years of age and older should take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 바스크롱에프캡슐 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are an infant under 3 months of age, are taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have discontinued them within the last 2 weeks, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicine (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, are elderly, are frail, have a high fever, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, a cough accompanied by fever, rash or persistent headache, have a hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5), or are a child under 2 years of age.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue administration immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* For infants and young children under 7 years of age, administer syrup.
* Drowsiness may occur during administration, so avoid driving vehicles and operating machinery.

#CN

### 💊 바스크롱에프캡슐 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于止咳、化痰。

### 🕒 바스크롱에프캡슐 - 用法与用量
* 15岁及以上成人每次2粒，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 바스크롱에프캡슐 - 注意事项
* 对本药过敏、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、未满3个月的婴儿、正在服用或停用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）后2周内的人，以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，如果您是过敏体质，曾有药物过敏症状（例如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等），患有肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、青光眼（例如：眼睛疼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难、高龄者、身体虚弱者、高烧、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期女性、吸烟者、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽，对日落黄FCF（黄色5号）过敏或有变态反应史，或未满2岁的儿童，请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 服用5-6次后症状仍未改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下进行。
* 对于7岁以下的婴幼儿，请服用糖浆剂。
* 服用期间可能会出现嗜睡，因此请避免驾驶汽车或操作机械。

* 5～6回服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
* 定められた用法・用量を厳守してください。
