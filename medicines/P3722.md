# EN
### 💊 푸레파인연고 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, bleeding, and for disinfection in cases of anal fissures and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 푸레파인연고 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents (15 years of age and older) should apply directly to the affected area up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 푸레파인연고 - Precautions
*   Patients with heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, or difficulty urinating due to prostate enlargement should not use this medication.
*   Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medication.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Do not use in children.
*   This medication is for anal use only and should not be used for ophthalmic purposes.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
