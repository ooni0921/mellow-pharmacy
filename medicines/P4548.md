#EN
### 💊 탁센덱시연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
* This drug is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation after trauma and surgery, and infections (accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever).

### 🕒 탁센덱시연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 300 mg orally 2-4 times a day. However, do not exceed 1,200 mg per day.

### ⚠️ 탁센덱시연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and take this drug or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
    Taking this drug increases the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to it, have current or a history of gastrointestinal ulcers, bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular system or other sites, severe blood disorders, liver impairment, kidney impairment, heart failure, hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, asthma, urticaria or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), are undergoing treatment for pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are pregnant for 6 months or more, have inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous NSAID treatment.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a bleeding tendency, blood disorders, a history of liver impairment, kidney impairment, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, hypersensitivity, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, are a child aged 6 years or older, have stomach cancer, alcohol dependence, are pregnant in the first or second trimester, are a woman who may be pregnant, have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease (hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgical procedures resulting in significant blood loss, have blood coagulation disorders, or are taking low-dose aspirin for the prevention of myocardial infarction or stroke.
Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this drug, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

#CN
### 💊 탁센덱시연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于辅助治疗慢性多发性关节炎、类风湿性关节炎、关节病、强直性脊柱炎、外伤及手术后疼痛性水肿或炎症、感染症（伴有炎症、疼痛及发热）。

### 🕒 탁센덱시연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 用法用量
* 成人每次口服 300 mg，每日 2～4 次。但每日剂量不得超过 1,200 mg。

### ⚠️ 탁센덱시연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 每日定期饮酒三杯以上的患者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能引起胃肠道出血。
    服用本药会增加可能致命的严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死、中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
* 对本药过敏者、胃肠道溃疡患者或有既往史者、胃肠道或脑血管或其他部位出血者、严重血液异常者、肝功能障碍者、肾功能障碍者、心力衰竭者、高血压者、支气管哮喘患者或有既往史者、对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药有哮喘、荨麻疹或过敏史者、冠状动脉旁路移植术前后疼痛治疗的患者、妊娠6个月以上的孕妇、克罗恩病或溃疡性结肠炎等炎症性肠病患者、曾因非甾体抗炎药治疗而发生胃肠道出血或穿孔者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如您有出血倾向、血液异常、肝功能障碍或肾功能障碍史、体液潴留或心力衰竭、高血压、过敏史、系统性红斑狼疮(SLE)及混合性结缔组织病(MCTD)、老年人、6岁以上儿童、胃癌、酒精中毒、妊娠早期或中期孕妇、可能怀孕的女性、缺血性心脏病、外周动脉疾病、脑血管疾病、心血管疾病危险因素患者（高血压、高脂血症、糖尿病、吸烟）、肝卟啉症、因失血过多而进行重大外科手术的患者、凝血功能障碍者、为预防心肌梗死或中风而服用低剂量阿司匹林者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
服用后可能会出现头晕或疲劳，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请小心。

### 🕒 탁센덱시연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 用法及び用量

### ⚠️ 탁센덱시연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事項
