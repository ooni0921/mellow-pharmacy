#EN

### 💊 Jin Nok Cheon Ak (진녹천액) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for nourishing and invigorating, asthenia (weak constitution), physical fatigue, post-illness physical weakness, nutritional disorders, febrile/consumptive diseases, and nutritional supplementation during pregnancy and postpartum.

### 🕒 Jin Nok Cheon Ak (진녹천액) - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 15 years and older, and adults, take 1 bottle once a day.

### ⚠️ Jin Nok Cheon Ak (진녹천액) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to it. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly. This medicine is a herbal extract formulation, so precipitation may occasionally occur, but its efficacy remains unchanged; shake well before taking. This medicine is for oral use only and should not be used for injection. It may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
