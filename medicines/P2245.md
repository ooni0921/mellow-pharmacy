#EN
### 💊 프로펜디연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation after trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 프로펜디연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) per dose, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 capsules (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 프로펜디연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication increases the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulcers (current or history), bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular system, or other areas, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma (current or history), a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), patients undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, pregnant women in the third trimester (6 months or more), nursing mothers, patients with inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous NSAID treatment.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have (or have a history of) blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, hepatic impairment, renal impairment, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, hypersensitivity, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), elderly patients, children aged 6 years or older, gastric cancer, alcohol dependence, pregnant women in the first or second trimester or women who may be pregnant, ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes mellitus, smoking), hepatic porphyria, major surgery resulting in blood loss, or if you have blood coagulation disorders or are receiving anticoagulants.

Considering the importance of drug administration, discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue the drug.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

For acute conditions, long-term use should generally not exceed 5 days.
