### 💊 속엔쿨정 - 효능 및 효과
* 이 약은 소화불량, 식욕감퇴(식욕부진), 과식, 체함, 소화촉진, 소화불량으로 인한 위부(상복부)팽만감에 사용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 속엔쿨정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach (food stagnation/heartburn), promotion of digestion, and gastric distension (upper abdominal bloating) due to indigestion.

### 🕒 속엔쿨정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years and older: Take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 속엔쿨정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a history of or current hypersensitivity to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts.
*   Children aged 7 years or younger should not take this medicine.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, those with an allergic constitution, or patients for whom special risks are anticipated due to fat overload, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 속엔쿨정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于消化不良、食欲减退（食欲不振）、过食、积食、促进消化、因消化不良引起的胃部（上腹部）胀满感。

### 🕒 속엔쿨정 - 用法与用量
*   15岁以上及成人：每次2片，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 속엔쿨정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者或有过敏史者，以及7岁以下的儿童，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，患有高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常的患者、过敏体质者、以及因脂肪超负荷而预计有特殊风险的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
