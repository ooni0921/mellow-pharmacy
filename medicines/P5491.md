#EN
### 💊 메코마그빅정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the supplementation of Vitamins E, B2, and B6 for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, in cases of decreased physical strength during illness or recovery, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness and coldness in hands and feet (peripheral circulation disorder, menopause), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 메코마그빅정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and individuals aged 15 years and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 메코마그빅정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this drug if you are: a patient hypersensitive to this drug, a patient hypersensitive to soy or peanuts, a patient with a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, an infant under 12 months of age, or a patient with severe renal insufficiency.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant or infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with renal impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient for whom lipid emulsions must be administered cautiously, or a patient expected to be at special risk from fat overload, or a patient with folic acid deficiency.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This drug may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 🕒 메코마그빅정 - 用法用量

### ⚠️ 메코마그빅정 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   下列患者请勿服用本药：对本药过敏的患者，对大豆或花生过敏的患者，有大豆油过敏反应史的患者，12个月以下的婴儿，有严重症状的肾功能不全患者。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果您是：孕妇及可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女，早产儿、婴儿，患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病的患者，有心脏·循环系统功能障碍的患者，有肾脏障碍的患者，低蛋白血症患者，患有高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症以及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常的患者，或必须谨慎给予脂质乳剂的患者，或因脂肪过载预计有特殊风险的患者，叶酸缺乏的患者。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

本药可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检查值。

服用1个月左右后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止使用，并咨询医生或药剂师。

### 🕒 메코마그빅정 - 用法・用量
*   15歳以上および成人は、1回1錠、1日1回服用します。

*   情報なし

定められた用法・用量を守ってください。
