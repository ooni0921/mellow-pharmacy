#EN
### 💊 삼남아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for fever and pain (ache) due to colds, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 삼남아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 300-1,000 mg per dose, 3-4 times a day. A maximum of 8 tablets (4,000 mg) can be administered per day. The dosage can be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 삼남아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this medication with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, a history of asthma caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, those who have taken barbiturate-containing drugs or tricyclic antidepressants, and those who have consumed alcohol should not take this medication. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding diathesis, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Do not take this medication for more than 10 days for pain (adults) or 5 days for pain (children), or more than 3 days for fever without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 삼남아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、扭伤痛以及牙痛、关节痛、风湿样疼痛。

### 🕒 삼남아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - 用法与用量
*   成人每次服用300~1,000毫克，每日3~4次。每日最高剂量可达8片（4,000毫克）。可根据年龄和症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 삼남아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上的规律饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能会导致肝损伤。极罕见情况下，曾报告过可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性全身发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量请勿超过最大日剂量（4,000毫克）。这可能会导致肝损伤。请勿与其他含有对乙酰氨基酚的产品同时服用。
*   对本药过敏的患者、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液异常患者、严重肝功能障碍患者、严重肾功能障碍患者、严重心功能低下患者、曾因阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛药引起哮喘的患者、服用过巴比妥类药物或三环抗抑郁药的患者以及饮酒者，请勿服用本药。在服用本药之前，肝功能障碍、肾脏障碍、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向患者、心功能异常、有过敏史、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。未经医生或药师指示，成人止痛服用不超过10天，儿童止痛服用不超过5天，退热服用不超过3天。
