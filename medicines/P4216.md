#EN
### 💊 위앤탁더블액션현탁액 - Efficacy & Effects
*   This medication is used for the symptomatic treatment of gastro-oesophageal reflux (such as acid regurgitation, heartburn, and indigestion caused by reflux of stomach contents, which may occur after meals, during pregnancy, or in conjunction with reflux oesophagitis).

### 🕒 위앤탁더블액션현탁액 - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years and over should take 10-20 mL per dose, four times a day (after meals and at bedtime).

### ⚠️ 위앤탁더블액션현탁액 - Precautions
*   Not specified
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not take it.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient who needs to severely restrict sodium intake (such as those with congestive heart failure or renal impairment), or if you have hypercalcaemia, nephrocalcinosis, recurrent calcium-containing renal stones, or if you are a child under 12 years of age.

If symptoms do not improve after 7 days of treatment, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
