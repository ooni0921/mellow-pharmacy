#EN
### 💊 디카본100정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, as well as for the prevention of bone and teeth developmental disorders and rickets.

### 🕒 디카본100정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 8 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 디카본100정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, an infant under 12 months, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking the medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
