#EN
### 💊 로노펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the treatment of chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation after trauma and surgery, and as an adjunctive treatment for infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 로노펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet (300 mg) per dose, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 tablets (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 로노펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Regular drinkers consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine increases the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and severe cardiovascular adverse reactions may occur.

Taking this medicine may cause serious, potentially fatal gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as bleeding, ulceration, and perforation of the gastrointestinal tract.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulcers or a history thereof, bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular, or other sites, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe heart failure, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, a history of asthma, urticaria or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), for the treatment of peri-operative pain in the setting of coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are pregnant for 6 months or more, have inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to NSAID therapy, or have rare hereditary problems of galactose intolerance, total lactase deficiency or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendencies, liver impairment or a history thereof, kidney impairment or a history thereof, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, a history of hypersensitivity, Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, are a child aged 6 years or older, have stomach cancer, are an alcoholic patient, are a pregnant woman in the first or second trimester or a woman who may be pregnant, have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes mellitus, smoking), have hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss, are receiving diuretics or ACE inhibitors, have blood coagulation disorders or are receiving anticoagulants, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Considering the importance of drug administration, discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue drug administration.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

For acute conditions, long-term use should generally be limited to within 5 days.
