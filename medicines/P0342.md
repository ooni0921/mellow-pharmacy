# EN
### 💊 팜젠아시클로버연고(수출명:파시버연고) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for herpes simplex virus infections (including initial and recurrent genital herpes and labial herpes).

### 🕒 팜젠아시클로버연고(수출명:파시버연고) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply to the affected area (diseased part) 2 to 5 times a day, at 4-hour intervals, for 1 week. If there is no effect after 1 week of use or if symptoms worsen, switch to another treatment method.

### ⚠️ 팜젠아시클로버연고(수출명:파시버연고) - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity or a history of it to this drug, patients with chemical intolerance, breastfeeding mothers, and children should not use this drug. Immunocompromised patients such as those with AIDS or bone marrow transplant recipients, pregnant women, and breastfeeding mothers should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Begin treatment as early as possible, and in recurrent cases, start at the first appearance of prodromal symptoms or lesions (sore areas). Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not use this medication for the purpose of preventing transmission to others or preventing recurrent herpes simplex (blister) infections. Do not dilute this medication or use it as a base to mix with other medications. This medication is for external use only. Do not use for ophthalmic purposes on the cornea or mucous membranes. To prevent this medication from spreading to other parts of the body or infecting others, apply it while wearing a thimble or rubber gloves.

# CN
### 💊 팜젠아시클로버연고(수출명:파시버연고) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于单纯疱疹病毒感染症（包括初期及复发性生殖器疱疹和唇疱疹）。

### 🕒 팜젠아시클로버연고(수출명:파시버연고) - 用法与用量
*   每日2至5次，每隔4小时涂抹于患处（患病部位），连续1周。若使用1周后无效或症状恶化，请改用其他治疗方法。

### ⚠️ 팜젠아시클로버연고(수출명:파시버연고) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏或有过敏史的患者、化学不耐受患者、哺乳期妇女、儿童请勿使用本药。艾滋病患者或骨髓移植患者等免疫缺陷患者、孕妇、哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药师。治疗应尽可能及早开始，对于复发性病例，应在前驱症状或病灶（疼痛部位）初次出现时开始。请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。本药不得用于预防传染他人或预防复发性单纯疱疹（水疱）感染症。请勿稀释本药或将其用作与其他药物混合的基质。本药仅供外用。请勿用于眼科用途，如角膜、粘膜。为防止本药扩散至身体其他部位或传染他人，请戴上指套或橡胶手套涂抹。
