#EN
### 💊 순하나정(데소게스트렐) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for contraception.

### 🕒 순하나정(데소게스트렐) - Dosage and Administration
* Tablets should be taken daily at approximately the same time, with water, and in the order indicated on the package. The first tablet should be taken on the first day of menstruation, and thereafter, one tablet should be taken continuously every day, even if bleeding occurs. After completing the previous pack, open a new pack and continue taking the tablets.

For more details, please refer to the detailed information in the approved prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 순하나정(데소게스트렐) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, have active venous thromboembolic disorder, thromboembolism, VTE, deep vein thrombosis and pulmonary embolism, severe liver disease or have experienced it with liver function values not returning to normal, known or suspected sex steroid-sensitive malignancies, undiagnosed vaginal bleeding, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with or have experienced breast cancer, liver dysfunction, diabetes, hypertension, melasma, depression or have experienced it, are in a state of persistent immobility due to surgery or illness, or are a smoker.
