#EN
### 💊 아프머스엘플라스타(디클로페낙나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for analgesia and anti-inflammation of swelling and pain after trauma (injury), as well as for degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder joint, tendinitis and tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), and myalgia (e.g., muscular, myofascial, low back pain).

### 🕒 아프머스엘플라스타(디클로페낙나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults 15 years of age and older: Apply one patch to the affected area once a day.

### ⚠️ 아프머스엘플라스타(디클로페낙나트륨) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not use this drug if you have a history of hypersensitivity to it, or a history of asthma and hypersensitivity reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs). This drug is also contraindicated for children under 15 years of age, women in their third trimester of pregnancy, and lactating women.
*   Before using this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma, if you are using it for inflammation accompanied by infection and chronic diseases (e.g., degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis)), if you are pregnant (first and second trimesters), if you have difficulty becoming pregnant or are undergoing infertility examinations, or if you are an elderly patient.
*   Do not use on eyes, mucous membranes, damaged skin, or areas with eczema or rash.
*   Do not use with occlusive dressings.
