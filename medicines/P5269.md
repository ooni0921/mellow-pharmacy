#EN
### 💊 투엑스비디큐정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to supplement vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth periods, and old age.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medication is used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (including back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation of the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis. It is also used for the prevention of poor bone and tooth development and rickets.

This medication is used to supplement zinc.

### 🕒 투엑스비디큐정 - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 8 years and older and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 투엑스비디큐정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart/circulatory dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a patient with a history of kidney stones.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medication may interfere with blood sugar detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
