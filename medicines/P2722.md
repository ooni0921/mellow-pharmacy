# EN

### 💊 마이믹스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins D, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical decline during and after illness, growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor bone and teeth development and rickets, alleviation of symptoms such as angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis. Additionally, it helps alleviate hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, and prevents gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds).

### 🕒 마이믹스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years or older should take 1 tablet per dose, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 마이믹스정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and has a normal diet; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a lactating woman; a premature infant or infant; a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine); a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or folic acid deficiency; or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests.

# CN

### 💊 마이믹스정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解身体疲劳，在怀孕、哺乳期，病中、病后体力下降时，发育期，老年期补充维生素 D、B6、C。同时用于预防骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病，缓解口角炎（嘴角炎）、唇炎（嘴唇炎）、口腔炎（口腔内炎症）、舌炎（舌头炎）、湿疹、皮肤炎等症状，缓解因日晒、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑），以及预防牙龈出血和鼻出血（鼻衄）。

### 🕒 마이믹스정 - 用法与用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每次1片，每日3次。

### ⚠️ 마이믹스정 - 注意事项
*   对本药过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态）、结节病（原因不明的全身炎症性疾病）、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   在服用本药前，以下情况者请咨询医生或药师：充分日晒且正常饮食的儿童；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女；早产儿、婴儿；高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）患者；心血管系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风、叶酸缺乏的患者；对日落黄（黄色5号）过敏或有过敏史的患者。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
*   在进行各种尿液检查时，本药可能会干扰血糖的检测。
