#EN
### 💊 콘트라스300연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy/lactation, physical weakness during illness or recovery, and in old age; alleviation of symptoms of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet; alleviation of symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness); and alleviation of symptoms of beriberi, eye strain, angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 콘트라스300연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 8 years and older: Take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 콘트라스300연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: an infant under 1 year of age; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant or breastfeeding; a premature infant; an infant; a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions; a patient for whom a special risk is anticipated due to fat overload; a patient with a thrombotic predisposition; a patient with folic acid deficiency; or a patient hypersensitive to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.
