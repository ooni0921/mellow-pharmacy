##EN
### 💊 VD-Sun Tablet - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy and lactation, growth periods, and old age, as well as for poor development of bones and teeth, and prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 VD-Sun Tablet - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ VD-Sun Tablet - Precautions
*   Patients hypersensitive to this medication, those with hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication.

Before taking this medication, infants under 1 year of age, children who get a lot of sunlight and eat a normal diet, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, nursing mothers, premature infants, toddlers, or patients with a history of kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

##CN
### 💊 브이디썬정 - 功效与作用
*   该药用于妊娠期、哺乳期、发育期和老年期补充维生素D，以及骨骼和牙齿发育不良，预防佝偻病。

### 🕒 브이디썬정 - 用法用量
*   8岁及以上儿童和成人，一次1片，一日1次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 브이디썬정 - 注意事项
*   对本药过敏的患者、高钙血症患者、结节病（不明原因的全身性炎症性疾病）患者、肾病患者、肾结石患者，以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，1岁以下婴儿、经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、肾结石患者或有肾结石病史者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
