# EN
### 💊 탑사인카타플라스마(펠비낙) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammation (anti-inflammatory) in cases of osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia (muscle pain), and swelling/pain after trauma.

### 🕒 탑사인카타플라스마(펠비낙) - Dosage and Administration
*   Remove the release liner from the patch surface, then apply one patch to the affected area twice a day.

### ⚠️ 탑사인카타플라스마(펠비낙) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine in patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, individuals who have experienced or had asthma attacks induced by aspirin or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), individuals who have experienced urticaria or acute rhinitis induced by aspirin or NSAIDs, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women. Patients with bronchial asthma and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not apply to damaged skin, mucous membranes, eczema, or rash areas.
