#EN
### 💊 코투루텐나잘스프레이 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve symptoms such as nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy head caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 코투루텐나잘스프레이 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 7 years and older, spray 1-2 times into each nostril, 1-2 times a day.
*   Administer at intervals of 10-12 hours, and do not exceed 2 doses within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 코투루텐나잘스프레이 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are a child under 7 years of age, or an infant under 2 years of age.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicines including rhinitis sprays, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, have high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, glaucoma, or have a history of propylene glycol hypersensitivity.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts or recurs for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, immediately stop using this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine is for rhinitis spray, meaning it is only for use as a nasal spray, and should not be used in the eyes or ears.
*   Do not share this medicine to prevent contamination.
*   Do not use excessively. It may cause nasal congestion.
*   Drowsiness may occur during use. Be careful when driving a car or operating machinery.
*   Children should use this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   间隔10-12小时喷洒一次，24小时内不要超过2次。

*   儿童应在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
