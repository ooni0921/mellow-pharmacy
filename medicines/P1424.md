#EN
### 💊 디젠정(수출명:Digestal,Dailase) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for excessive stomach acid, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and abdominal bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 디젠정(수출명:Digestal,Dailase) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet per dose, 3 times a day, between meals.

### ⚠️ 디젠정(수출명:Digestal,Dailase) - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you are a lactating woman, a child under 7 years of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine (e.g., rash, redness, itching), have an allergic constitution, have a kidney disorder, are pregnant or may be pregnant, or have hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 (Tartrazine). Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
