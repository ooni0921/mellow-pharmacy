* 이 약은 위·십이지장궤양, 위염, 위산과다, 속쓰림, 구역, 구토, 위통, 신트림의 제산작용 및 증상의 개선에 사용합니다.

### 🕒 파티겔현탁액(알마게이트)(수출명 : 알마게인현탁액, 호메젤현탁액) - 용법 및 용량
* 성인 및 12세 이상의 소아는 1회 1~1.5 g을 1일 3회 식후 30분~1시간에 복용합니다.필요시 취침 전에 1회 더 복용할 수 있습니다.

### ⚠️ 파티겔현탁액(알마게이트)(수출명 : 알마게인현탁액, 호메젤현탁액) - 주의사항
* 정보 없음

#EN
### 💊 파티겔현탁액(알마게이트)(수출명 : 알마게인현탁액, 호메젤현탁액) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for its antacid effects and to improve symptoms such as gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, hyperacidity, heartburn, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and sour belching.

### 🕒 파티겔현탁액(알마게이트)(수출명 : 알마게인현탁액, 호메젤현탁액) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years and older should take 1 to 1.5 g once, three times a day, 30 minutes to 1 hour after meals. If necessary, one additional dose can be taken before bedtime.

### ⚠️ 파티겔현탁액(알마게이트)(수출명 : 알마게인현탁액, 호메젤현탁액) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, Alzheimer's disease, hemorrhoids, fluid retention (edema), pre-eclampsia, diarrhea, or undiagnosed gastrointestinal bleeding. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, are physically weak, are on a low-phosphorus diet, have malabsorption, are elderly, have symptoms of gastrointestinal bleeding such as hematemesis or black stools, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
