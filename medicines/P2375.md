# EN
### 💊 비트리모정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to supply Vitamin B6 during pregnancy and lactation, or when physical strength is low during or after illness, and to relieve symptoms such as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 비트리모정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years and older, take 1 tablet at a time, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 비트리모정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medication, an infant under 3 months of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, infants under 1 year of age or patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
