#EN
### 💊 코비웨이나잘스프레이(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy head sensation caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 코비웨이나잘스프레이(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 7 years and older should spray twice into each nostril per application.

Administer with an interval of at least 3 hours between applications, and do not exceed 6 administrations per day.

### ⚠️ 코비웨이나잘스프레이(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this product or a history thereof, or if you are a child under 7 years of age or an infant under 2 years of age.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicines including nasal sprays for rhinitis, are pregnant or may be pregnant, or have hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, or glaucoma.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting for more than 1 week or recurring, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever and rash or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, immediately discontinue this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine is for use only as a nasal spray for rhinitis (i.e., a medicine to be sprayed into the nose); do not use in the eyes or ears.

Do not share to prevent contamination.

Do not use excessively. It may cause nasal congestion.

Children should use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
