### ⚠️ 타스펜8시간이알서방정650밀리그람(아세트아미노펜)(수출명:도로돈이알정) - Precautions

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorder, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) or for fever for more than 3 days without consulting a doctor or pharmacist. Since this medicine is an extended-release formulation, swallow the tablet whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving it.
