#EN
### 💊 대웅바이오아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the inhibition of thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina pectoris, and for the inhibition of thrombus formation after Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) or Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA). It is also used to reduce cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, and diabetes).

### 🕒 대웅바이오아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 tablet once daily. This medicine is an enteric-coated tablet, so it can be taken before meals with sufficient water.

### ⚠️ 대웅바이오아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, anti-rheumatic drugs, peptic ulcer, aspirin asthma or a history of it, hemophilia, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac insufficiency, bleeding tendency, or if you are concurrently taking methotrexate at doses exceeding 15 mg/week for one week. Pregnant women in the third trimester should not take this medicine. Pregnant women in the first and second trimesters should not take this medicine unless absolutely necessary.
*   Consult your doctor or pharmacist before using this medicine if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (such as renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, fluid depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major hemorrhagic events), hepatic impairment, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are a pre-operative patient, an infant under 3 years old, an elderly patient, a patient with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a patient under 14 years old with chickenpox or influenza infection, or a patient with gout.
*   Due to the risk of bleeding, inform your doctor that you are taking this medicine if you are undergoing surgery (including dental surgery).
*   Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medicine.
