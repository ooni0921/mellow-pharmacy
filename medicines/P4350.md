*   This medication is used for fever, pain associated with the common cold, and relief of headache, toothache, muscle pain, lower back pain, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 타이레펜8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 2 tablets every 8 hours.
    Do not exceed 6 tablets in 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 타이레펜8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other fever-reducing analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
    Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis (Lyell's syndrome), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.
    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, a history of aspirin-induced asthma (asthma attacks triggered by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs), are under 12 years of age, or are taking barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol.
    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have had liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, cardiac dysfunction, hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.
    This medication is an extended-release formulation, so do not crush, chew, or dissolve the tablets; swallow them whole.
