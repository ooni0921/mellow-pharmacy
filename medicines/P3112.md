### 💊 Maxtamin Premium Tablet - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, D, and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth period, and old age; to relieve symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as low back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis; to prevent beriberi, eye strain, poor bone and tooth development, rickets; and to supplement zinc.

### 🕒 Maxtamin Premium Tablet - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ Maxtamin Premium Tablet - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, or kidney stones.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), are pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, premature infant, infant, have cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or have a history of kidney stones, have folic acid deficiency, or have a predisposition to thrombosis (blood clots).

    Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    For administration to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

    If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
