만 8세 이상~만 11세 미만은 1회 1/2병 1일 3회, 만 5세 이상~만 8세 미만은 1회 1/3병 1일 3회, 만 3세 이상~만 5세 미만은 1회 1/4병 1일 3회 식후 복용하며, 복용간격을 4시간 이상으로 합니다.

#EN
### 💊 노루모에프내복액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal distension, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, loss of appetite (anorexia), dyspepsia, and overeating.

### 🕒 노루모에프내복액 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older: Take 1 bottle at a time, 3 times a day.
* For individuals aged 11 years or older and under 15 years: Take 2/3 bottle at a time, 3 times a day.
* For individuals aged 8 years or older and under 11 years: Take 1/2 bottle at a time, 3 times a day.
* For individuals aged 5 years or older and under 8 years: Take 1/3 bottle at a time, 3 times a day.
* For individuals aged 3 years or older and under 5 years: Take 1/4 bottle at a time, 3 times a day.
* Take after meals, ensuring an interval of at least 4 hours between doses.

### ⚠️ 노루모에프내복액 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not administer this medicine to infants under 3 months of age.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, have hypersensitivity to caramel, have kidney impairment, or are on a sodium-restricted diet.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 노루모에프내복액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于胃酸过多、胃灼热、胃部不适、胃部饱胀感、积食、恶心、呕吐、胃痛、嗳酸、食欲不振（食欲减退）、消化不良、过食。

### 🕒 노루모에프내복액 - 用法与用量
* 15岁及以上：一次1瓶，一日3次。
* 11岁及以上至15岁以下：一次2/3瓶，一日3次。
* 8岁及以上至11岁以下：一次1/2瓶，一日3次。
* 5岁及以上至8岁以下：一次1/3瓶，一日3次。
* 3岁及以上至5岁以下：一次1/4瓶，一日3次。
* 饭后服用，服药间隔应至少4小时。

### ⚠️ 노루모에프내복액 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息。
* 未满3个月的婴儿请勿服用本药。
* 在服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、对焦糖过敏者、肾功能障碍患者、正在进行钠限制饮食者，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
* 服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。
