#EN
### 💊 Hyclocream - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the early treatment of recurrent labial herpes (cold sores).

### 🕒 Hyclocream - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should apply an appropriate amount, sufficient to cover the entire lesion including its edges, 5 times a day for 5 days.
Treatment should be started as early as possible, immediately upon the appearance of initial symptoms and signs.

### ⚠️ Hyclocream - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, skin infections (tuberculosis, syphilis, candidiasis, tinea, herpes zoster, chickenpox, vaccinia, scabies, pubic lice), severely compromised immune system (such as bone marrow transplant recipients or AIDS patients), eczematous external otitis with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (excluding Behçet's disease), second-degree deep burns or higher and frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, rosacea, are breastfeeding, or are under 18 years of age.

Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, elderly patients, or individuals with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not breastfeed while using this medication.

Do not use on eyes, nose, inside the mouth, or genitals.

Use only for cold sores on the lips or around the mouth.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not use occlusive dressing therapy without a doctor's supervision.

#CN
### 💊 Hyclocream - 功效与作用
* 本药用于复发性唇疱疹的早期治疗。

### 🕒 Hyclocream - 用法用量
* 成人每日5次，持续5天，每次取适量涂抹于患处，覆盖至边缘。
治疗应在出现初期症状和体征时尽快开始。

### ⚠️ Hyclocream - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者，患有皮肤感染（结核病、梅毒、念珠菌病、癣、带状疱疹、水痘、牛痘、疥疮、阴虱）的患者，免疫系统严重受损的患者（如骨髓移植患者或艾滋病患者），鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳炎患者，溃疡（白塞氏病除外），二度以上深度烧伤和冻伤，口周皮炎，寻常痤疮，酒渣鼻患者，哺乳期妇女，以及18岁以下的儿童，请勿使用本药。

在使用本药之前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，老年人，对丙二醇过敏或有过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

在使用本药期间请勿哺乳。

请勿用于眼睛、鼻子、口腔内及生殖器。

仅用于嘴唇或嘴周的唇疱疹。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

未经医生指导，请勿使用密封敷料法。
