#EN
### 💊 더모메디큐액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for athlete's foot, jock itch (tinea cruris), and ringworm (tinea corporis).

### 🕒 더모메디큐액 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 더모메디큐액 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding. Do not use on infants or children, or on the eyes, around the eyes, mucous membranes (mouth, nose, vagina), genitals, external genitalia, or on diseased areas with severe eczema, dampness, maceration, cracking, or severe wounds. Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, have experienced allergic symptoms, or if the affected area is on the face, extensive, or suppurating. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Clean the affected area and its surroundings before use. Be careful not to get it in your eyes. If it gets into your eyes, rinse immediately with water or lukewarm water. Do not touch your eyes or mucous membranes with hands that have this medicine on them. This medicine is for external use only; do not take internally.
