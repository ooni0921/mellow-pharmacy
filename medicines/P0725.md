#EN
### 💊 포스테리산연고 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 포스테리산연고 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area (diseased part) twice daily, preferably after a bowel movement.

### ⚠️ 포스테리산연고 - Precautions
*   Patients hypersensitive to this medication should not use it.
*   To prevent recurrence, continue applying the medication for a long period even after symptoms such as inflammation, itching, blisters, and burning sensation have disappeared.
*   This medication is for anal use only and should not be used ophthalmically.

#CN

*   本药用于痔疮。

### 🕒 포스테리산연고 - 用法与用量
*   每日2次，尽可能在排便后，取适量涂抹于患处（病患部位）。
