* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 메가원정 - 주의사항
* 정보 없음
* 이 약에 과민증 환자, 고칼슘혈증(혈액 중에 칼슘이 과잉으로 존재하는 상태)환자, 유육종증(원인을 알 수 없는 전신 염증질환), 신장결석, 심한 증상의 신부전 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당(젖당)분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 메가원정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during illness or convalescence (while ill or after recovery), for the supplementation of Vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C in old age, for neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye strain, angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation in the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), relief of eczema and dermatitis, and for the supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 메가원정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 메가원정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition in which an excessive amount of calcium exists in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney stones, severe renal failure, an infant under 12 months of age, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who is exposed to a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition in which an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a patient receiving cardiac glycosides, or a patient with gout or kidney stones.

Please strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
