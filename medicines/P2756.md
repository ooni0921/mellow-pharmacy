#EN
### 💊 사노바겔(리도카인) - Efficacy & Effects
* This medication is used to reduce the tactile sensitivity of the male genital organ.

### 🕒 사노바겔(리도카인) - Dosage & Administration
* Apply 0.25 to 0.66 g of gel (23 to 62 mg as lidocaine) 5 to 15 minutes before sexual intercourse. Dosage and application time vary individually, and the lowest effective dose should be used. Do not exceed a maximum of 2 g (184 mg as lidocaine) within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 사노바겔(리도카인) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug or other amide-type local anesthetics, if there is trauma or inflammation at the application site, or if your partner is pregnant. Before using this medication, patients with liver or kidney disease, or those hypersensitive to propylene glycol, should consult a doctor or pharmacist. For external use only. Do not use in the eyes or nose. It is advisable not to apply continuously to the same area.
