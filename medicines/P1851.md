#EN
### 💊 조인탑200연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamins E, B1, B2, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical decline during or after illness (during illness or recovery), and in old age; for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis; and for the relief of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as stiff shoulders/neck, numbness/coldness in hands and feet.

### 🕒 조인탑200연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 조인탑200연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant (young child), a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic dyslipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient for whom lipid emulsions must be administered cautiously, a patient at special risk due to fat overload, a patient with a predisposition to vascular occlusion, or a patient with folic acid deficiency.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
* When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
