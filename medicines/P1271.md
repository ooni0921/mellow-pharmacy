#EN
### 💊 파이에온정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for hives, eczema, itching, and contact dermatitis (e.g., from poison ivy/oak).

### 🕒 파이에온정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 파이에온정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medication if you are a child under 15 years old, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing mother, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced hypersensitivity reactions to allergy medications, cold medications, antitussives/expectorants, or motion sickness medications; if you have glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, a weak constitution, diarrhea, loose stools, significantly weakened stomach, loss of appetite, nausea, vomiting, or significantly weakened physical strength.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
*   Do not take this medication continuously for a long period.
