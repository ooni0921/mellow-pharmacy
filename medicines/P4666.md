#EN
### 💊 에스빌이부연질캡슐200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain due to the common cold, low back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), post-operative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 에스빌이부연질캡슐200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, in cases of rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, take 1 to 3 capsules (200-600 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times daily, up to a maximum of 16 capsules (3,200 mg) per day. For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3-4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or common cold, adults should take 1 to 2 capsules (200-400 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times daily. For migraine, adults should take 1 to 2 capsules (200-400 mg) per dose, not exceeding 2 capsules within 24 hours.

Adolescents aged 12 years and older should take 1 to 2 capsules (200-400 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times daily.

For individuals weighing less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid taking on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 에스빌이부연질캡슐200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other fever-reducing pain relievers, consult a doctor or pharmacist. This may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforations may also occur.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulcers and/or gastrointestinal bleeding (or a history thereof), severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe heart failure, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma (or a history thereof), asthma, hives, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs (or a history thereof), pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, or are pregnant in the last trimester (last 3 months).

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders (or a history thereof), bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis and/or liver dysfunction (or a history thereof), kidney dysfunction, heart failure or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly, pediatric, pregnant, lactating, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Yellow No. 5.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

When used for the common cold, it should generally be taken within 5 days. For pain, adults should not take it for more than 10 days, and children for more than 5 days, without a doctor's or pharmacist's instructions. For fever, do not take for more than 3 days.

* 本药用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、腰痛、月经痛、类风湿关节炎、幼年型类风湿关节炎、骨关节炎（退行性关节疾病）、术后疼痛、头痛、偏头痛、牙痛、肌肉痛、神经痛、强直性脊柱炎、急性痛风、银屑病关节炎、软组织损伤（扭伤、挫伤）以及非关节风湿病（肌腱炎、腱鞘炎、滑囊炎）。

### 🕒 에스빌이부연질캡슐200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - 用法与用量
* 成人，对于类风湿关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、软组织损伤、非关节风湿病、急性痛风、银屑病关节炎，每次服用1至3粒胶囊（200-600毫克），每日3至4次，每日最高剂量为16粒胶囊（3,200毫克）。对于幼年型类风湿关节炎，每日按体重每公斤30-40毫克，分3至4次服用。对于轻度至中度疼痛或感冒，成人每次服用1至2粒胶囊（200-400毫克），每日3至4次。对于偏头痛，成人每次服用1至2粒胶囊（200-400毫克），24小时内不得超过2粒胶囊。

12岁及以上青少年，每次服用1至2粒胶囊（200-400毫克），每日3至4次。

体重不足30公斤者，每日剂量不应超过500毫克，并建议避免空腹服用。

### ⚠️ 에스빌이부연질캡슐200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 如果您每天定期饮酒三杯或以上，并且正在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能导致胃肠道出血。

服用本药可能会增加严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗塞、中风的风险，这些可能致命。还可能出现严重的胃肠道不良反应，如胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔。
* 以下患者请勿服用本药：对本药过敏者、胃肠道溃疡和/或胃肠道出血患者或有相关病史者、严重血液异常者、严重肝功能障碍者、严重肾功能障碍者、严重心力衰竭者、严重高血压者、支气管哮喘患者或有相关病史者、因阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药引起哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏反应者或有相关病史者、冠状动脉旁路移植术前后发生疼痛的患者、以及妊娠最后三个月的孕妇。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师：有过敏史者、血液异常或有相关病史者、有出血倾向的患者、肝硬化及/或肝功能障碍病史者、肾功能障碍者、心力衰竭或心脏病患者、高血压患者、系统性红斑狼疮及混合性结缔组织病患者、溃疡性结肠炎患者、克罗恩病患者、老年人、儿童、孕妇、哺乳期妇女、以及对黄色5号有过敏或过敏反应史者。

请勿超过推荐剂量服用。

用于感冒时，原则上应在5天内服用。未经医生或药剂师指导，成人疼痛不应服用超过10天，儿童疼痛不应服用超过5天，发热不应服用超过3天。

### 🕒 에스빌이부연질캡슐200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - 用法・用量

### ⚠️ 에스빌이부연질캡슐200밀리그램(이부프로펜) - 注意事項
