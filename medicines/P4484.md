#EN
### 💊 케이파이위보왕산 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for loss of appetite (anorexia), abdominal bloating, indigestion, overeating, upset stomach, nausea, and vomiting.

### 🕒 케이파이위보왕산 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults aged 15 years or older: 1 sachet (3.6 g) per dose, 3 times daily.
* For individuals aged 11 years or older but under 15 years: 2/3 sachet (2.4 g) per dose, 3 times daily.
* For individuals aged 8 years or older but under 11 years: 1/2 sachet (1.8 g) per dose, 3 times daily.
* Take after meals.

### ⚠️ 케이파이위보왕산 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you are an infant under 3 months old, an infant under 1 year old, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year old (in unavoidable cases), pregnant women, or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 케이파이위보왕산 - 功效与作用
* 本药品用于治疗食欲减退（食欲不振）、腹胀、消化不良、过食、积食、恶心、呕吐。

### 🕒 케이파이위보왕산 - 用法用量
* 15岁及以上成人：每次1包（3.6克），每日3次。
* 11岁及以上但未满15岁者：每次2/3包（2.4克），每日3次。
* 8岁及以上但未满11岁者：每次1/2包（1.8克），每日3次。
* 餐后服用。

### ⚠️ 케이파이위보왕산 - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 未满3个月的婴儿及未满1岁的婴儿、以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本药品。
* 在服用本药品之前，未满1岁的婴儿（在不得已的情况下）、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性应咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
* 服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。

* 11歳以上15歳未満：1回2/3包（2.4 g）、1日3回。
* 8歳以上11歳未満：1回1/2包（1.8 g）、1日3回。
