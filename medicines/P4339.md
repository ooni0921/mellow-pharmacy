### 💊 비카인크림(리도카인) - Efficacy and Effects (EN)
*   This medication is used to reduce tactile sensitivity of the male genitalia.

### 🕒 비카인크림(리도카인) - Dosage and Administration (EN)
*   Apply 0.25-0.66 g (23-62 mg as lidocaine) of the cream 5-15 minutes before sexual intercourse. Dosage and application time vary by individual; use the lowest effective dose, but do not exceed a maximum of 2 g (184 mg as lidocaine) within a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 비카인크림(리도카인) - Precautions (EN)
*   No specific information.
*   Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug or other amide-type local anesthetics, if you have trauma or inflammation at the application site, or if your partner is pregnant. Before using this medication, patients with liver or kidney disease or hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. For external use only. Do not use on eyes or nose. It is advisable not to apply continuously to the same area.

### ⚠️ 비카인크림(리도카인) - 注意事项 (CN)
*   无其他具体信息。
*   对本药或其它酰胺类局部麻醉剂过敏者、涂抹部位有外伤或炎症者、以及伴侣怀孕者，请勿使用本药。使用本药前，患有肝肾疾病或对丙二醇过敏的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。仅供外用。请勿用于眼睛或鼻部。建议不要连续涂抹于同一部位。
