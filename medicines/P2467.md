#EN
### 💊 메가600정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, and B6 when physical strength is reduced due to physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, or during/after illness (during illness or after recovery). It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 메가600정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age and older should take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 메가600정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), renal disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant, or a patient with cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, or folic acid deficiency.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

#CN
### 💊 메가600정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时补充维生素B1、B2、B6，并用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩周炎等）症状、脚气病以及眼睛疲劳。

### 🕒 메가600정 - 用法与用量
*   12岁及以上成人，一次1片，一日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 메가600정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的情况）、结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石、症状严重的肾功能衰竭、以及有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，多晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、患有心脏和循环系统功能障碍的患者、低蛋白血症患者、以及叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药可能会使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检测结果。
