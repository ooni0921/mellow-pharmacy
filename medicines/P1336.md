#EN
### 💊 아시콜크림(아시클로버) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for herpes simplex virus infections (including initial and recurrent genital herpes and herpes labialis).

### 🕒 아시콜크림(아시클로버) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply to the affected area 5 times a day at approximately 4-hour intervals. Treatment should be continued for 5 days, and if healing has not occurred after 5 days of use, treatment may be continued for a further 5 days.

### ⚠️ 아시콜크림(아시클로버) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine in patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, breastfeeding women, or children. Before using this medicine, patients with immunodeficiency such as AIDS or bone marrow transplant, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Treatment should be started as early as possible, and in recurrent cases, it should be started at the first sign of prodromal symptoms or lesions. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medicine should not be used to prevent transmission to others or to prevent recurrent herpes simplex virus infections. Do not dilute this medicine or use it as a base for mixing other medicines. This medicine is for external use only. Do not use ophthalmically on the cornea or mucous membranes. To prevent the spread of this medicine to other parts of the body or transmission to others, apply with a thimble or rubber gloves.

#CN
### 💊 아시콜크림(아시클로버) - 功效与作用
*   本品用于单纯疱疹病毒感染症（包括原发性和复发性生殖器疱疹以及唇疱疹）。

### 🕒 아시콜크림(아시클로버) - 用法与用量
*   每日5次，每次间隔约4小时，涂抹于患处（病患部位）。治疗应持续5天，如果使用5天后未愈合，可继续使用5天。

### ⚠️ 아시콜크림(아시클로버) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本品过敏的患者、哺乳期妇女、儿童请勿使用本品。在使用本品前，患有艾滋病或骨髓移植等免疫缺陷的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、对丙二醇有过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。治疗应尽可能早开始，如果是复发性病例，应在出现前驱症状或病变时立即开始。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本品不应用于预防传染给他人或预防复发性单纯疱疹病毒感染。请勿稀释本品或将其用作混合其他药物的基质。本品仅供外用。请勿用于眼科，如角膜、粘膜。为防止本品扩散至身体其他部位或传染给他人，请戴指套或橡胶手套涂抹。

*   情報なし
