#EN
### 💊 에르틴정(엘카르니틴) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and for myocardial metabolic disorders caused by ischemic heart diseases such as angina pectoris and acute myocardial infarction.

### 🕒 에르틴정(엘카르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2-3 g daily, divided into 2-3 doses.

The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 에르틴정(엘카르니틴) - Precautions
* No information available
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not take this drug.

Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.

#CN

### 🕒 에르틴정(엘카르니틴) - 用法与用量

* 无信息
* 对本品过敏者请勿服用。

#JP

### ⚠️ 에르틴정(엘카르니틴) - 注意事項
