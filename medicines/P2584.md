### 💊 쓰리엠애바가드9274외용액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염액) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is an antimicrobial skin cleanser used for general skin and wound cleansing, surgical hand antisepsis, personal hand antisepsis, and preoperative skin antisepsis of the surgical site.

### 🕒 쓰리엠애바가드9274외용액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염액) - Dosage and Administration
*   For general skin and wound cleansing, wipe the area to be cleansed with water, apply only the minimum amount necessary to the skin or wound, then gently wipe. Rinse thoroughly with water.
*   For surgical hand antisepsis, wet hands and forearms with water, apply 5 mL of this product to hands and up to the elbows, and scrub for approximately 3 minutes using a brush or sponge. Rinse thoroughly under running water, repeat the process once more, then dry completely.
*   For personal hand antisepsis, wet hands with water, apply 5 mL of this product to hands, then vigorously rub both hands for 15 seconds. Rinse with water and dry completely.
*   For preoperative skin antisepsis, apply a sufficient amount to the surgical site and rub for at least 2 minutes. Dry with a sterile towel, repeat the process once more, then dry with a sterile towel.

### ⚠️ 쓰리엠애바가드9274외용액(클로르헥시딘글루콘산염액) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this product if you have a history of hypersensitivity to it.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to other drugs, those with allergic diseases such as asthma or a history thereof or a family history, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This product is for external use only; do not administer orally or by injection.
*   Avoid contact with eyes, as it may cause corneal damage. If it gets into the eyes, rinse immediately and thoroughly with clean water.
*   This product is adsorbed by fibers and fabrics (e.g., cotton, gauze, wool, rayon). When using as a soaked solution, if the concentration is not above the effective concentration, provide a new solution.
*   Since insoluble salts may be formed by anions contained in saline solutions, use clean distilled water for dilution.
*   Replace diluted solutions used for hand cleansing, etc., with a fresh solution at least daily.
