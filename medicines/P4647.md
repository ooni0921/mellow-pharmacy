#EN
### 💊 엑세라민프로정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical debility during or after illness, growth period, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), alleviation of symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and the supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 엑세라민프로정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take one tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 엑세라민프로정 - Precautions
* No specific information provided.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, infants under 12 months of age, those with hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is present in excess in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, pregnant women (or women who may be pregnant), lactating women, premature infants, young children, patients with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
