# EN
### 💊 베노론캡슐(디오스민) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the improvement of symptoms related to venous insufficiency (such as leg heaviness, pain), adjunctive treatment of disorders due to capillary fragility, and treatment of signs related to hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 베노론캡슐(디오스민) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 capsule (300 mg) at a time, twice a day (with lunch and dinner).
*   In case of recurrence or worsening of hemorrhoids, up to 4 to 6 capsules (1,200 to 1,800 mg) can be taken per day.
*   Dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 베노론캡슐(디오스민) - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine and breastfeeding women should not take this medicine.
*   Pregnant women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
*   Do not administer for a long period.
*   If symptoms do not improve quickly, an anal examination and a review of treatment are necessary.
