* 이 약은 육체피로, 임신·수유기, 병중·병후(병을 앓는 동안이나 회복 후)의 체력저하시, 발육기, 노년기의 비타민 D, B1, B2, B6의 보급과 뼈, 이의 발육불량, 구루병의 예방, 각기, 눈의피로, 신경통, 근육통, 관절통(요통, 어깨결림 등), 구각염(입꼬리염), 구순염(입술염), 구내염(입안염), 설염(혀염), 습진, 피부염의 완화 및 아연의 보급에 사용합니다.

* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정, 1일 1회 식후 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 비타비케어정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of Vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness (convalescence), during growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for the prevention of bone and tooth underdevelopment, rickets, and for the relief of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain) (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, and for the replenishment of zinc.

### 🕒 비타비케어정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 비타비케어정 - Precautions
*   None
*   Do not take this medicine if you have: hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney (renal) disease, kidney (renal) stones, severe renal insufficiency, a history of soybean oil hypersensitivity or allergic reactions, or hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, an infant under 1 year of age, a child who receives ample sunlight and has a normal diet, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with kidney (renal) impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient with a history of kidney (renal) stones, a patient with hyperlipoproteinemia, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may change urine to a yellow color and affect clinical test results.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
