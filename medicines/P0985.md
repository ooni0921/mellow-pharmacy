#EN

### 💊 Komijung - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for temporary relief of symptoms associated with common cold, allergic rhinitis, or vasomotor rhinitis, such as sneezing, runny nose, nasal congestion, watery eyes, and sinus congestion (nasal blockage) caused by sinusitis.

### 🕒 Komijung - Dosage and Administration
* Children 12 years of age and older and adults: Take 1 tablet every 4 hours. Do not exceed 6 tablets in 24 hours.
* Children 6 to under 12 years of age: Take 1/2 tablet every 4 hours. Do not exceed 3 tablets in 24 hours.
* Children under 6 years of age: Consult a physician.

### ⚠️ Komijung - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, or to amine-sympathomimetics or antihistamines, are a nursing mother, have severe hypertension, severe coronary artery disease, narrow-angle glaucoma, urinary retention, peptic ulcer, asthma, emphysema, chronic lung disease, shortness of breath, respiratory difficulty, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Patients with heart disease, thyroid disorders, diabetes, or difficulty urinating due to prostate enlargement should not take this medication unless directed by a physician. Avoid alcoholic beverages during medication. Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery after taking this medication.

#CN

### 💊 Komijung - 功效与作用
* 此药用于暂时缓解普通感冒、过敏性鼻炎或血管运动性鼻炎引起的打喷嚏、流鼻涕、鼻塞、流泪，以及鼻窦炎引起的鼻充血（鼻塞）等症状。

### 🕒 Komijung - 用法与用量
* 12岁及以上儿童和成人：每4小时服用1片，24小时内不要超过6片。
* 6岁及以上至12岁以下儿童：每4小时服用1/2片，24小时内不要超过3片。
* 6岁以下儿童：遵医嘱。

### ⚠️ Komijung - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药、胺类拟交感神经药或抗组胺药过敏的患者，哺乳期妇女，患有严重高血压、严重冠状动脉疾病、窄角青光眼、尿潴留（尿液滞留）、消化性溃疡、哮喘、肺气肿、慢性肺病、呼吸急促、呼吸困难，或有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者请勿服用本药。患有心脏病、甲状腺功能疾病、糖尿病、前列腺肥大引起的排尿困难的患者，除非有医生的指示，否则请勿服用。服药期间请避免饮酒。服药后驾驶或操作机械时请注意安全。
