#EN
### 💊 람노스과립 (수출명1: Allbio granules, 수출명2: Antibio Forte granules) (락토바실루스카제이변종람노수스) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the regulation of intestinal function and the improvement of diarrhea symptoms by normalizing the intestinal flora.

### 🕒 람노스과립 (수출명1: Allbio granules, 수출명2: Antibio Forte granules) (락토바실루스카제이변종람노수스) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 2-8 sachets (2-8 g) daily. Children aged 2 years and older take 1-4 sachets (1-4 g) daily. Each dose should be taken between meals with a beverage or milk.

### ⚠️ 람노스과립 (수출명1: Allbio granules, 수출명2: Antibio Forte granules) (락토바실루스카제이변종람노수스) - Precautions
*   This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized into phenylalanine in the body. Therefore, patients with phenylketonuria who need to regulate phenylalanine intake should not take this medicine.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine. To compensate for fluid loss due to diarrhea, drink plenty of beverages containing salt or sugar. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   成人每日服用2-8包 (2-8克)。2岁及以上儿童每日服用1-4包 (1-4克)。每次剂量应在餐间与饮料或牛奶一同服用。
