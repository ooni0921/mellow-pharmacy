# EN
### 💊 세라진정(세티리진염산염)[수출명: Cera tablet, Allergen tablet, Unizef tablet, Highcera tablet] - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for seasonal and perennial allergic rhinitis, allergic conjunctivitis, chronic idiopathic urticaria, pruritus, and in combination with topical hydrocortisone for eczema and dermatitis.

### 🕒 세라진정(세티리진염산염)[수출명: Cera tablet, Allergen tablet, Unizef tablet, Highcera tablet] - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 6 years and older should take 1 tablet (10 mg) once daily before bedtime. For patients sensitive to adverse reactions, 1/2 tablet (5 mg) should be taken twice daily, in the morning and evening.

The dosing interval for patients with renal impairment should be adjusted according to their renal function. For pediatric patients with renal impairment, the dose should be individually adjusted considering the patient's renal clearance and body weight. For detailed information, please refer to the product's prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 세라진정(세티리진염산염)[수출명: Cera tablet, Allergen tablet, Unizef tablet, Highcera tablet] - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hydroxyzine, or piperazine derivatives, renal failure, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, are an infant under 6 years of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, hepatic impairment, are elderly, have epilepsy or are at risk of seizures, or have predisposing factors for urinary retention (e.g., spinal cord lesion, prostatic hyperplasia).
