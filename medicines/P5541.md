#EN
### 💊 치킬정(디오스민) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the improvement of symptoms of venous insufficiency (heavy legs, pain), as an adjunctive treatment for disorders caused by capillary fragility, and for the treatment of signs related to hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 치킬정(디오스민) - Dosage and Administration
*   For venous insufficiency, take 600 mg once daily with meals.

    For hemorrhoids, take 600 mg two to three times daily (1,200-1,800 mg) with meals.

### ⚠️ 치킬정(디오스민) - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to it or are breastfeeding.

    Pregnant women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.

    Do not use for a prolonged period.

#JP
