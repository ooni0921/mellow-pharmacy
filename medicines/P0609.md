# EN
### 💊 영풍아시클로버크림 - Indications & Effects
*   This medication is used for herpes simplex virus infections (including initial and recurrent genital herpes (blisters) and labial herpes (blisters)).

### 🕒 영풍아시클로버크림 - Dosage & Administration
*   Apply to the affected area 5 times a day, approximately every 4 hours. Treatment should be continued for 5 days. If the condition does not improve after 5 days of use, it can be used for an additional 5 days.

### ⚠️ 영풍아시클로버크림 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to it, are a breastfeeding mother, or are a child.
*   Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an immunocompromised patient (e.g., with AIDS or bone marrow transplant), pregnant or possibly pregnant, or have a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol.
*   Begin treatment as early as possible. In cases of recurrence, start at the first sign of prodromal symptoms or lesions.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Do not use this medication to prevent infection of others or to prevent recurrent herpes simplex (blister) infections.
*   Do not dilute this medication or use it as a base for mixing other medications.
*   This medication is for external use only.
*   Do not use for ophthalmic purposes on the cornea or mucous membranes.
*   To prevent the spread of this medication to other parts of the body or to others, apply it while wearing a thimble or rubber gloves.

# CN
### 💊 영풍아시클로버크림 - 功效及作用
*   本药用于单纯疱疹病毒感染症（包括初期及复发性生殖器疱疹（水疱）和唇疱疹（水疱））。

### 🕒 영풍아시클로버크림 - 用法及用量
*   每日5次，每次间隔约4小时，涂抹于患处（病患部位）。治疗应持续5天，如果使用5天后仍未见效，可再使用5天。

### ⚠️ 영풍아시클로버크림 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏患者、哺乳期妇女、儿童请勿使用本药。
*   在使用本药前，艾滋病或骨髓移植等免疫缺陷患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、对丙二醇过敏或有相关经验的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   治疗应尽可能早期开始，如果是复发性病例，应在前驱症状或病灶首次出现时开始。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   本药不得用于预防传染他人或预防复发性单纯疱疹（水疱）病毒感染。
*   请勿稀释本药或将其用作混合其他药物的基质。
*   本药仅限外用。
*   请勿用于眼科，包括角膜和粘膜。
*   为防止本药扩散到身体其他部位或传染他人，请在戴指套或橡胶手套的情况下涂抹。
