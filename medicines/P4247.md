#EN
### 💊 삐콤씨파워정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye strain, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis. Additionally, it helps alleviate pigmentation (e.g., chloasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, prevent gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds), prevent poor bone/teeth development and rickets, and relieve symptoms of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as stiff shoulders/neck, numbness in hands/feet, and cold hands/feet.

### 🕒 삐콤씨파워정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 삐콤씨파워정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have kidney stones.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who receives ample sunlight and has a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gout, a patient with a history of kidney stones, or a patient with a thrombogenic predisposition (tendency to form blood clots).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow, affect clinical test results, or interfere with glucose detection during urine tests.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
