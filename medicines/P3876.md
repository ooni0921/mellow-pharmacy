* 정보 없음

#EN
### 💊 케로펜디플라스타(케토프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for analgesia and anti-inflammatory action in degenerative osteoarthritis, periarthritis of shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, humeral epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), muscle pain, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 케로펜디플라스타(케토프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   After removing the release liner from the patch, apply it to the affected area twice a day.

### ⚠️ 케로펜디플라스타(케토프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this drug if you have hypersensitivity or photosensitivity to it, have experienced asthma after using aspirin or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), have hypersensitivity to drugs that cause cross-hypersensitivity reactions with this drug (tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, oxybenzone), are pregnant for 6 months or more, or are a child under 15 years of age. Before using this drug, patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant women, and lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist. While using this drug and for up to 2 weeks after use, regardless of the weather, avoid outdoor activities, and when going out, cover the applied area with clothing, sunglasses, hats, sunscreen, etc., to avoid exposure to ultraviolet rays. Do not use on eyes, mucous membranes, damaged skin, eczema, rash areas, athlete's foot, tinea, etc. Do not use occlusive dressing methods.

*   撕下药面上的离型纸后，每日2次敷于患处（病患部位）。

### ⚠️ 케로펜디플라스타(케토프로펜) - 注意事项
*   信息缺失
