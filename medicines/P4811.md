#EN
### 💊 이지콜드코프연질캡슐 - Indications & Efficacy
* This medicine is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 이지콜드코프연질캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
* Adults and children aged 15 years and older should take 2 capsules once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 이지콜드코프연질캡슐 - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause liver damage.

Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it before, have experienced asthma after taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child aged 3 months to under 2 years, an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have experienced drug-induced allergies, have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, are physically weak or have a high fever, have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal issues such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, or if symptoms such as smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache occur.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not use continuously for a long period.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 对本药有过敏史或曾有过敏反应者、服用其他解热镇痛剂或感冒药时曾引起哮喘者、3个月以下的婴儿、正在服用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）或停用MAO抑制剂后2周内的人，请勿服用本药。

### ⚠️ 이지콜드코프연질캡슐 - 注意事項

定められた用法・用量を守ってください。
