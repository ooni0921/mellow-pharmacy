#EN
### 💊 마그라민액티브맥스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical debility during or after illness (while ill or recovering), growth periods, and old age; for poor bone and tooth development; for the prevention of rickets; for the alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders and symptoms during menopause such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet; for beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness); for the alleviation of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; and for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그라민액티브맥스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Individuals 12 years of age and older and adults: Take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그라민액티브맥스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   None specified
*   Do not take this medicine if you are: a patient hypersensitive to this medicine; a patient hypersensitive to soybeans or peanuts; have experienced an allergic reaction to soybean oil; a patient with hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood); a patient with sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause); a patient with kidney disease or kidney stones; a patient with severe renal failure; or an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a lactating woman; a premature infant or infant; a patient with heart or circulatory system dysfunction; a patient with renal impairment; a patient with hypoproteinemia; a patient with kidney stones or a history of kidney stones; a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient for whom lipid emulsions must be administered carefully, or a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and may affect clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
