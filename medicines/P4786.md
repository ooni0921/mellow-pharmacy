#EN
### 💊 하벤에스액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 하벤에스액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 years and older: 1 pouch (20 mL).
* Aged 11 to under 15 years: 2/3 pouch.
* Aged 7 to under 11 years: 1/2 pouch.
* Aged 3 to under 7 years: 1/3 pouch.
* Aged 2 to under 3 years: 1/4 pouch.
* Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

Do not administer to children under 2 years of age.

### ⚠️ 하벤에스액 - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and take this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized into phenylalanine in the body. Patients with phenylketonuria who need to regulate phenylalanine intake should not take this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it before, have developed asthma after taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age (newborn), or have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child aged 3 months to under 2 years, an infant or child under 15 years suspected of having or infected with chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have experienced drug-induced allergies, have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty with urination, are elderly, are frail or have a high fever, have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that persists or recurs for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for an extended period.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not consume alcohol during medication.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

服药期间请勿饮酒。

服药后可能出现嗜睡，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
