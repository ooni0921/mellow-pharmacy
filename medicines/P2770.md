# EN
### 💊 코키즈펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for antipyresis in case of fever due to acute upper respiratory tract infection.

### 🕒 코키즈펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 6 months or older should take 0.4-0.6 mL/kg (5-7 mg/kg) per dose, as needed, at intervals of 4-6 hours, not exceeding a maximum of 4 doses per day (28 mg/kg).
*   For children weighing 30 kg or less, the maximum daily dose should not exceed 25 mL (300 mg).
*   Refer to the approved product information for recommended single doses by age group.

### ⚠️ 코키즈펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and take this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
*   Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
*   Do not take this medication if you are: hypersensitive to this drug; have peptic ulcer; severe blood disorder; severe liver impairment; severe kidney impairment; severe heart failure; severe hypertension; bronchial asthma or a history thereof; have experienced asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding; a newborn or infant under 6 months of age; or elderly.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have: a history of peptic ulcer; a history or current blood disorder; a bleeding tendency; a history or current liver impairment; a history or current kidney impairment; heart failure; hypertension; a history of hypersensitivity; systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD); ulcerative colitis; Crohn's disease; are elderly; are a child; or have stomach cancer.
*   Considering the importance of drug treatment, either discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue the medication.
*   Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
*   For acute conditions, long-term use should generally not exceed 5 days.

# CN
### 💊 코키즈펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解急性上呼吸道感染引起的发热症状。

### 🕒 코키즈펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 用法与用量
*   生后6个月以上的儿童，每次服用0.4~0.6 mL/kg (5~7 mg/kg)，必要时间隔4~6小时服用，每日最多不超过4次 (28 mg/kg)。
*   体重30kg以下的儿童，每日最大剂量不超过25 mL (300 mg)。
*   各年龄段的单次推荐剂量请参考药品说明书。

### ⚠️ 코키즈펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
*   每天饮酒超过三杯的常规饮酒者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能会引起胃肠道出血。
*   服用本药可能会增加发生可能致命的严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死和中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
*   下列患者请勿服用本药：对本药过敏者；消化性溃疡患者；严重血液异常患者；严重肝功能障碍患者；严重肾功能障碍患者；严重心功能不全患者；严重高血压患者；支气管哮喘患者或有既往史者；对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药（NSAIDs）有过哮喘、荨麻疹或过敏反应史者；冠状动脉（心脏动脉）旁路移植术（CABG）前后疼痛治疗患者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女；新生儿及未满6个月的婴儿；老年人。
*   服用本药前，以下患者请咨询医生或药师：有消化性溃疡病史者；有血液异常或既往史者；有出血倾向的患者；有肝功能障碍或既往史者；有肾功能障碍或既往史者；心功能不全患者；高血压患者；有过敏史者；系统性红斑狼疮（SLE）及混合性结缔组织病（MCTD）患者；溃疡性结肠炎患者；克罗恩病患者；老年人；儿童；胃癌患者。
*   考虑到药物治疗的重要性，请中断哺乳或中断药物治疗。
*   服用本药后可能会出现头晕或疲劳，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
*   对于急性疾病，长期服用原则上应在5天以内。
