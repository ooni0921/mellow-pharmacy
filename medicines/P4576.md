### 💊 타이몰8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Indications and Usage
* This medication is used for the relief of fever, pain due to the common cold, headache, toothache, muscle pain, back pain, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 타이몰8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children 12 years of age and older, and adults should take 2 tablets every 8 hours.
Do not exceed 6 tablets in 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 타이몰8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
Serious skin reactions such as Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS) and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN) have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.
Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin-induced asthma or a history thereof, have taken barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, have consumed alcohol, or are a child under 12 years of age.
Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.
Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist, and do not take for fever for more than 3 days.
This medication is a sustained-release formulation; therefore, swallow the tablet whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving it.
