#EN
### 💊 레티콜연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (symptoms of feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 레티콜연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 15 years and older, and adults: take 2 capsules per dose. For individuals over 7 years old and under 15 years old: take 1 capsule per dose. Administer 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 레티콜연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker who consumes three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you have experienced asthma when taking other fever reducers/pain relievers or cold medicines, if you are an infant under 3 months old, or if you have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant (baby) or child under 15 years old suspected or confirmed to have chickenpox or influenza; a child under 2 years old; if you have symptoms of ischemic colitis (such as sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding); if you have an allergic constitution; if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer/gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty urinating; if you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; if you have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, ulcers, or bleeding problems; if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding; or if you have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, or excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
