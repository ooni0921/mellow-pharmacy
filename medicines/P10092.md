#EN

### 💊 다비우정(방풍통성산건조엑스(4.6→1)) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of accompanying symptoms of hypertension (palpitations, stiff shoulders, flushing), obesity, swelling, and constipation in patients with abundant subcutaneous fat in the abdomen and a tendency towards constipation.

### 🕒 다비우정(방풍통성산건조엑스(4.6→1)) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, typically take 4 tablets once, 3 times a day, before or between meals.

### ⚠️ 다비우정(방풍통성산건조엑스(4.6→1)) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to soybean oil, hypersensitivity to soybeans or peanuts, if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with hypertension, heart or kidney dysfunction, edema, an elderly person, have symptoms of diarrhea or loose stools, have a weak stomach, symptoms of anorexia, nausea, vomiting, are in a weakened state after illness, are significantly debilitated, sweat excessively, have or have experienced circulatory system disorders such as angina pectoris or myocardial infarction, have urinary dysfunction, hyperthyroidism, have experienced rash, redness, or itching due to this medicine, or if you have lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetes, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously.
If there is no improvement in symptoms after taking this medicine for about 1 month (or 1 week in case of constipation), stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* 成人通常一次服用4片，一日3次，饭前或饭间服用。

### ⚠️ 다비우정(방풍통성산건조엑스(4.6→1)) - 注意事项
* 暂无信息。
* 对本药所含大豆油有超敏反应或过敏史者，对大豆或花生有过敏症患者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本药。

### 🕒 다비우정(방풍통성산건조엑스(4.6→1)) - 用法・用量
* 通常、成人1回4錠を1日3回、食前または食間に服用します。
