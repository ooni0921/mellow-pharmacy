# EN
### 💊 코리투살에스코프연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 코리투살에스코프연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 years or older should take 2 capsules per dose.
* Children aged 8 years or older but under 15 years should take 1 capsule per dose.
* Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 코리투살에스코프연질캡슐 - Precautions
* If you are a regular drinker who consumes three or more alcoholic drinks daily, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, stop taking it immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, infants under 3 months of age, or children under 2 years of age.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; children under 2 years of age *only if absolutely necessary*; individuals prone to allergies; those with a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); individuals with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer; elderly individuals; individuals who are frail or have a high fever; individuals with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or those with ulcer or bleeding problems; pregnant women or women who may be pregnant; breastfeeding women; smokers; individuals with asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
