*   This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation after trauma and surgery, and infectious diseases accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 이지엔6프로연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜)(수출명:DAUFENSoftCapsule,SPEEDFEN) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) per dose, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 capsules (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 이지엔6프로연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜)(수출명:DAUFENSoftCapsule,SPEEDFEN) - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma, urticaria or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), gastrointestinal ulcers, bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular or other areas, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, pain before or after coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), are pregnant for 6 months or more, are breastfeeding, have inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or have experienced gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, bleeding tendency, hepatic impairment, renal impairment, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, a child aged 6 years or older, are in early pregnancy or may be pregnant, have stomach cancer, are an alcoholic, have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), hepatic porphyria, blood coagulation disorders or are receiving anticoagulants, or have a history of gastrointestinal ulcers due to NSAIDs.
*   Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
*   For long-term use in acute conditions, it should generally be within 5 days.
