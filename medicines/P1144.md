#EN
### 💊 올바펜정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain due to colds, toothache, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 올바펜정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, avoiding an empty stomach. The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 올바펜정 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity (e.g., rash, redness/congestion, itching, swelling of the larynx, eyelids, lips) to this medicine, or if you have experienced asthma attacks when taking other antipyretic analgesics (Non-Steroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs, NSAIDs) or cold medicines. Do not take if you have peptic ulcers, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, or have consumed barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of liver disorder, kidney disorder, peptic ulcer, blood disorder, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are elderly, are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

#CN
### 💊 올바펜정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、牙痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、关节痛、风湿性疼痛。

### 🕒 올바펜정 - 用法用量
*   成人每次1~2片，每日3次，避免空腹服用。服药间隔应至少4小时。

### ⚠️ 올바펜정 - 注意事项
*   如果每日饮酒三杯或以上并定期饮酒者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师，因为可能引起肝损伤。乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000毫克）请勿超过，否则可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
*   对本药过敏（例如：皮疹、充血发红、瘙痒、喉部、眼睑、嘴唇肿胀）的患者及有过敏史者，服用其他解热镇痛药（非甾体抗炎药）或感冒药时曾出现哮喘发作的患者，患有消化性溃疡、严重血液疾病、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全者，以及服用巴比妥类药物、三环抗抑郁药、酒精者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，患有或曾患有肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、消化性溃疡史、血液疾病或有血液疾病史、有出血倾向的患者、有心功能障碍的患者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。
