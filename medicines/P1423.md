#EN
### 💊 삼메틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for digestive dysfunction in conditions such as gastroesophageal reflux and hiatal hernia, gastritis/duodenitis, and gastroduodenal ulcers, including symptoms like abdominal pain, indigestion, nausea, and vomiting. It is also used for irritable bowel syndrome, spastic colon, habitual vomiting, and non-infectious intestinal transit disorders in pediatric diseases, such as constipation and diarrhea.

### 🕒 삼메틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet (150 mg) once, 3 times a day, before meals.
* The dosage may be adjusted appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 삼메틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - Precautions
* No information.
* Patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication.
* Pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
