#EN

### 💊 Ivedajung - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and periods of physical weakness during or after illness (during or after recovery). It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as angular stomatitis (cheilosis at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 Ivedajung - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet at a time, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Ivedajung - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant/toddler, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gout, kidney stones, or if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
    When administering to children, do so under the supervision and guidance of a guardian.
    This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, which can affect clinical test values.

*   无信息
