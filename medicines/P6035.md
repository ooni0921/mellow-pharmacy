#EN
### 💊 코마키텐큐나잘스프레이 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used to relieve symptoms of stuffy nose, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy head caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 코마키텐큐나잘스프레이 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 7 years and older, spray 1-2 times into each nostril, 1-2 times a day.
    Administer with an interval of 10-12 hours, and do not exceed 2 administrations within a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 코마키텐큐나잘스프레이 - Precautions
*   Do not use this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, are a child under 7 years of age, or an infant under 2 years of age.
    Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medications including nasal sprays for rhinitis, are pregnant or suspect you may be pregnant, have hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, glaucoma, or have a history of propylene glycol hypersensitivity.
    Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts for more than a week or recurs, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever and rash or persistent headache.
    If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
    This medication is for nasal spray use only (sprayed into the nose); do not use it in the eyes or ears.
    To prevent contamination, do not share this medication with others.
    Do not use excessively. It may cause nasal congestion.
    Drowsiness may occur during use. Be careful when driving a car or operating machinery.
    Children should use this under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
    Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.
    Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN
### 💊 코마키텐큐나잘스프레이 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解由普通感冒、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流鼻涕、打喷嚏、头重感等症状。

### 🕒 코마키텐큐나잘스프레이 - 用法与用量
*   7岁及以上儿童和成人，每次向各鼻腔喷射1-2次，每日1-2次。
    给药间隔为10-12小时，24小时内不得超过2次。

### ⚠️ 코마키텐큐나잘스프레이 - 注意事项
*   对本药有过敏史者、7岁以下儿童以及2岁以下婴儿请勿使用本药。
    在使用本药前，如您有过敏体质、曾因包括鼻炎用喷雾剂在内的药物出现过敏症状、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾病、青光眼患者、对丙二醇过敏者或有过敏史者，请咨询医生或药师。
    在使用本药前，如您有吸烟史、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发烧和皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽，请咨询医生或药师。
    如果使用3天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用本药并咨询医生或药师。
    本药仅限于鼻炎用喷雾剂，即只用于喷入鼻腔，请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。
    为防止污染，请勿与他人共用。
    请勿过量使用。过量使用可能会引起鼻塞。
    使用期间可能会出现嗜睡。驾驶汽车或操作机械时请注意。
    儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
    请勿连续使用超过7天。
    请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
