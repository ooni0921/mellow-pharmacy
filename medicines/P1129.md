#EN
### 💊 보두레아크림(우레아)(수출명:EUSOFTYL Cream) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for progressive digital keratoderma (dry type of housewives' eczema), palmar and plantar hyperkeratosis, ichthyosis, senile xerosis, keratosis pilaris, and atopic skin conditions.

### 🕒 보두레아크림(우레아)(수출명:EUSOFTYL Cream) - Dosage and Administration
*   Wash the affected area (diseased part) once to several times daily, then apply this medication and rub it in thoroughly.

### ⚠️ 보두레아크림(우레아)(수출명:EUSOFTYL Cream) - Precautions
*   No specific information provided regarding contraindications/warnings not listed below.
*   Do not use this medication in patients with hypersensitivity to this product, those with acute eczema or inflammatory skin diseases, patients with renal failure, or on mucous membranes such as the ocular mucosa.
*   Before using this medication, patients with inflammation, fissures (cracks), increased sensitivity to skin irritation, those with a history of or current hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, or sulfite-sensitive patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medication is for external use only.
*   Do not apply directly to ulcers, erosions, or open wounds.
*   Use with caution to avoid contact with eyes, nose, mouth, and other mucous membranes. If it accidentally gets into the eyes, rinse thoroughly with a sufficient amount of water.

#CN
### 💊 보두레아크림(우레아)(수출명:EUSOFTYL Cream) - 功效与效果
*   本药用于治疗进行性指掌角皮症（家庭主妇湿疹的干燥型）、手足掌角皮症、鱼鳞病、老年性干皮症、毛囊角化症以及特应性皮肤。

### 🕒 보두레아크림(우레아)(수출명:EUSOFTYL Cream) - 用法与用量
*   每日1至数次，清洗患处（病患部位）后，涂抹本药并充分揉搓。

### ⚠️ 보두레아크림(우레아)(수출명:EUSOFTYL Cream) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏患者、患有急性湿疹或炎症性皮肤病、肾功能不全患者以及眼结膜等粘膜部位，请勿使用本药。
*   在使用本药之前，有炎症、裂口（开裂）、皮肤刺激敏感性增高、对丙二醇有过敏症或曾有过敏史、对亚硫酸盐敏感的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   本药仅供外用。
*   请勿直接用于溃疡、糜烂（溃烂）、伤口部位。
*   请小心使用，避免接触眼睛、鼻子、口腔及其他粘膜。如果不慎入眼，请用足量的水彻底冲洗。

#JP

### ⚠️ 보두레아크림(우레아)(수출명:EUSOFTYL Cream) - 注意事項
