#EN
### 💊 제감콜드캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 제감콜드캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: 2 capsules per dose.
* For individuals aged 11 years and older but under 15 years: 1 and 1/3 capsules per dose.
* For individuals aged 8 years and older but under 11 years: 1 capsule per dose.
* Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 제감콜드캡슐 - Precautions
* Regular drinkers consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are under 2 years of age, are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant or child under 15 years suspected or infected with chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms to medicines (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, edema, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, are elderly, are physically weak or have high fever, have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm, a cough lasting more than 1 week or recurring, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师：患有或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿及15岁以下儿童；过敏体质者；曾对药物有过敏症状（例如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）者；肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、浮肿、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（例如：眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难等患者；老年人；体弱者或高热患者；持续或复发性胃部问题，如胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；伴有吸烟、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、过多痰的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽患者。

请严格遵守用法用量。

儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

服用本药后可能会出现嗜睡，因此驾驶和操作机械时请注意。
