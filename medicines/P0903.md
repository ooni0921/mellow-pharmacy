#EN
### 💊 액소도스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of sneezing, runny nose, nasal congestion, and watery eyes caused by the common cold, allergies, and vasomotor rhinitis.

### 🕒 액소도스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once, children (12-14 years old) take 1/2 tablet once, three times a day after each meal.

### ⚠️ 액소도스정 - Precautions
* If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue administration and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, severe hepatic and renal impairment, severe coronary heart disease, urination disorder, hypertension, coronary artery disease, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, an infant under 2 years old, are taking MAO inhibitors, or have hypersensitivity to methyl parahydroxybenzoate.
* Before taking this medication, patients with asthma, glaucoma, arrhythmia, diabetes, hyperthyroidism, or children under 12 years old should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not take continuously for a long period.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery after taking this medication.

#CN

### 🕒 액소도스정 - 用法用量
* 成人每次1片，儿童（12-14岁）每次1/2片，每日3次，饭后服用。

* 如果出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等严重皮肤不良反应，请停止服药并咨询医生或药师。
* 对本药过敏者、有严重肝肾功能障碍、严重冠心病、排尿障碍、高血压、冠状动脉疾病、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、2岁以下婴儿、正在服用MAO抑制剂的患者、对对羟基苯甲酸甲酯过敏的患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，患有哮喘、青光眼、心律失常、糖尿病、甲状腺功能亢进症或12岁以下儿童应咨询医生或药师。
