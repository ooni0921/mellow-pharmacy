# EN
### 💊 콘티플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms associated with peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause, such as stiff shoulders/neck, numbness/coldness of hands and feet (cold hands and feet), neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., back pain, stiff shoulders), angular cheilitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 콘티플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children/adolescents aged 8 years and older: Take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 콘티플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you have hypersensitivity to soybean oil, soy, or peanuts.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders; if you are a patient requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions; if you are a patient at special risk due to fat overload; if you have hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5); if you are an infant under 1 year of age; if you are pregnant or may be pregnant; if you are breastfeeding; if you are a premature infant; if you are an infant; if you have folic acid deficiency; or if you have a thrombotic predisposition.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and may affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 콘티플러스연질캡슐 - 功效与效果
*   本品用于补充维生素E、B1、B2、B6，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠/哺乳期、病中/病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降、老年期。还用于缓解末梢血液循环障碍和更年期引起的症状，如：肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木/冰冷（手脚冰凉）、神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）、口角炎（口角发炎）、唇炎（嘴唇发炎）、口腔炎（口腔发炎）、舌炎（舌头发炎）、湿疹、皮炎、脚气病以及眼睛疲劳。

### 🕒 콘티플러스연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   成人及8岁以上儿童和青少年：每次1粒，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 콘티플러스연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   对本品有过敏症的患者、3个月以下婴儿、对大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，请勿服用本品。
*   服用本品前，高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症、胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需要慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者、因脂肪过载而有特殊风险的患者、对柠檬黄（黄色5号）过敏的患者、1岁以下婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、叶酸缺乏症患者、有血栓倾向的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本品可能使尿液变为黄色，并可能影响临床检查结果。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
