#EN
### 💊 브렌콜에프연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills (shivering sensation), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 브렌콜에프연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 15 years of age or older should take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 브렌콜에프연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist when regular drinkers consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily take this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, if you have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, an infant or young child under 2 years of age, someone with an allergic constitution, someone who has experienced allergic reactions (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), someone with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric or duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), urinary difficulties, an elderly person, a frail person or a person with high fever, someone with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, gastric discomfort, or stomach pain, or someone with ulcer or bleeding problems, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a smoker, someone with asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache, or someone who is hypersensitive to or has experienced hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
