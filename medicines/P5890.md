##EN
### 💊 비타임트리플엠지정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to supplement vitamins B1, B6, and E in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after recovery from illness when physical strength is low, and in the elderly.

    This medication, through its contained vitamins, is used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, as well as for beriberi and eye fatigue.

    This medication is used for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 비타임트리플엠지정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and adolescents 15 years of age and older, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비타임트리플엠지정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medication, soybeans, or peanuts; a patient with or who has experienced hypersensitivity to soybean oil; an infant under 12 months of age; or a patient with severe renal impairment.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis (Crohn's disease), a patient with cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, a patient who requires cautious administration of lipid emulsions, or a patient with fat overload-related conditions.

    If there is no improvement in symptoms after taking this medication for about 1 month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Patients with a thrombotic predisposition or folate deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
