#EN
### 💊 핫도리정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, loss of appetite (anorexia), dyspepsia, overeating, promotion of digestion, and abdominal bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 핫도리정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older, take 2 tablets once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 핫도리정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a child aged 7 years or younger, a patient undergoing dialysis, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, individuals with kidney impairment, allergic constitution, or those on a sodium-restricted diet should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
