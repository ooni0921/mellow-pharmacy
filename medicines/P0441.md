# EN

### 💊 Zeolacream - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for insect bites, itchy skin (pruritus), eczema, urticaria (hives), dermatitis, heat rash (prickly heat), and chilblains.

### 🕒 Zeolacream - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ Zeolacream - Precautions
*   None provided
*   Do not use this medication on infants under 30 months old, around the eyes, mucous membranes, on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, or on wounded areas.
*   Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced hypersensitivity (e.g., rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances) to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, have severe exudation or erosion, or are a child.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication is for external use only; do not take internally.
*   Be careful not to get it in your eyes. If it gets into your eyes, wash it out immediately with water.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
