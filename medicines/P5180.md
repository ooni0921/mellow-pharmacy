###EN
### 💊 마그윈큐연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and during periods of physical debility during or after illness (while sick or after recovery), for the supplementation of Vitamins E, B1, B6 in old age, for beriberi, eye fatigue, alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders and shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness in hands/feet, cold hands/feet during menopause, relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lumbago, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and for muscle cramps caused by magnesium.

### 🕒 마그윈큐연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adolescents aged 12 years or older and adults: Take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 마그윈큐연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are: hypersensitive to this medicine, hypersensitive to soy or peanuts, have experienced an allergic reaction to soybean oil, have severe renal impairment, or are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: hypersensitive to or have experienced an allergic reaction to Yellow No. 5, pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatic inflammation (pancreatitis) or other lipid metabolism disorders, or if you are a patient who must be administered lipid emulsions cautiously, or if you are a patient with an expected special risk due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
