#EN

### 💊 디케이정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, for impaired development of bones and teeth, and for the prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 디케이정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 8 years of age and older, take 1 tablet at a time, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 디케이정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a patient currently taking cardiotonic glycosides, a patient with gallbladder-related disease, liver disease, or a patient with a history of kidney stones.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

* 本药用于孕期、哺乳期、发育期、老年期维生素D的补充，预防骨骼、牙齿发育不良和佝偻病。

### 🕒 디케이정 - 用法及用量
* 8岁以上儿童及成人，每次1片，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 디케이정 - 注意事项
* 对本药过敏者，高钙血症（血液中钙含量过多的状态）患者，结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病），肾脏疾病，肾结石患者，症状严重的肾功能不全患者，未满12个月的婴儿，以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，以下患者请咨询医生或药师：未满1岁的婴儿，经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女，早产儿，婴幼儿，心脏·循环系统功能障碍患者，肾功能障碍患者，低蛋白血症患者，正在服用强心苷的患者，胆囊相关疾病患者，肝病患者，有肾结石病史或正在患肾结石的患者。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服药并咨询医生或药师。

* 本剤は、妊娠・授乳期、発育期、老年期におけるビタミンDの補給、骨・歯の発育不良、くる病の予防に用いられます。

### 🕒 디케이정 - 用法および用量

定められた用法用量を厳守してください。
