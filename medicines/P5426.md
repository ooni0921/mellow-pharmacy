### 💊 큐어콜연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms such as runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills with cold and trembling symptoms, fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 큐어콜연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 15 years and older should take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 큐어콜연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Regular drinkers who consume 3 or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

    Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or its components, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 2 years of age, an infant or child under 15 years of age who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, experience symptoms of ischemic colitis such as sudden abdominal pain or rectal bleeding related to pseudoephedrine, have a history of allergic symptoms such as drug-induced fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching, are elderly, are frail, or have a high fever.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer, glaucoma, difficulty urinating, if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or if you have ulcer or bleeding problems, or if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that persists or recurs for more than a week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, immediately discontinue this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Do not take continuously for a long period.

    Drowsiness may occur during use. Avoid driving a car or operating machinery.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
