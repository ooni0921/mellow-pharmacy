#EN
### 💊 오라비텐정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness (during illness or recovery), and in old age. It also helps relieve neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as low back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye strain, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, and supplies zinc.

### 🕒 오라비텐정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 2 tablets once a day.

### ⚠️ 오라비텐정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take 오라비텐정 if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant, an infant, or have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, or folate deficiency.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values or interfering with the detection of blood sugar during urine tests.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

*   本品用于补充因身体疲劳、妊娠、哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降以及老年期的维生素 E、B1、B2、B6、C。它还用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩周炎等）、脚气病、眼睛疲劳、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎，并补充锌。

### 🕒 오라비텐정 - 用法用量
*   12岁以上及成人：每次2片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 오라비텐정 - 注意事项
*   无可用信息。
*   对本品过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐受症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题的患者请勿服用本品。
*   服用本品前，如果您患有高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病、心血管功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风或肾结石患者、叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
*   本品可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检测值，或在尿液检查时干扰血糖的检测。
