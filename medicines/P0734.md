# EN

### 💊 멘소래담로션(Mentholatumlotion) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammation of sprains, bruises, muscle pain, joint pain, fracture pain, lower back pain, stiff shoulders, neuralgia, rheumatic pain, skin itching, insect bites, and chilblains.

### 🕒 멘소래담로션(Mentholatumlotion) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 4 times a day.

### ⚠️ 멘소래담로션(Mentholatumlotion) - Precautions
* Do not use this medicine around the eyes, mucous membranes, on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, wound areas, or sensitive skin.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, have severe dampness or erosion, suffer from circulatory disorders or diabetes, are under 12 years of age, are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine is for external use only; do not ingest.

Be careful not to get it in your eyes, and if it does get in, rinse immediately with water.

Do not cover or wrap the application area, and do not expose it to external heat or hot water.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, stop using it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
