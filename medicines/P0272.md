# EN
### 💊 보령아스트릭스캡슐100밀리그람(아스피린) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for inhibiting thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, for inhibiting thrombus formation after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) or percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA), and for reducing cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, diabetes, etc.).

### 🕒 보령아스트릭스캡슐100밀리그람(아스피린) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule once a day.

This drug is an enteric-coated capsule, so it can be taken before meals with plenty of water.

### ⚠️ 보령아스트릭스캡슐100밀리그람(아스피린) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or other salicylate preparations, have or have experienced peptic ulcer, aspirin asthma (induction of asthma attacks by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, etc.), hemophilia, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency, concurrently taking methotrexate in doses exceeding 15 mg per week (15 mg/week), or are in the third trimester of pregnancy.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have kidney dysfunction, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction, liver dysfunction, cardiac dysfunction, blood disorders, bronchial asthma, are a pre-surgical patient, an infant under 3 years of age, have a history of hypersensitivity to analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, antirheumatic drugs, or other allergic diseases, are an elderly person, have glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase deficiency, or are hypersensitive to Yellow No. 5.

Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medicine.

If you are undergoing surgery (including dental surgery), you must inform your doctor that you are taking aspirin preparations and consult them.

# CN
### 💊 보령아스트릭스캡슐100밀리그람(아스피린) - 功效及效果
* 本药用于抑制心肌梗死、脑梗死、不稳定型心绞痛中的血栓形成，以及冠状动脉旁路移植术(CABG)或经皮腔内冠状动脉成形术(PTCA)后抑制血栓形成，并用于降低高风险患者（具有缺血性心脏病家族史、高血压、高胆固醇血症、肥胖、糖尿病等复合危险因素的患者）的心血管风险。

### 🕒 보령아스트릭스캡슐100밀리그람(아스피린) - 用法及用量
* 成人一次1粒，一日1次服用。

本药为肠溶胶囊，因此可在饭前用足量水服用。

### ⚠️ 보령아스트릭스캡슐100밀리그람(아스피린) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药或其他水杨酸制剂过敏的患者、消化性溃疡、阿司匹林哮喘（由非甾体抗炎药等引起的哮喘发作）或有相关病史的患者、血友病、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、心功能不全、有出血倾向的患者、每周服用甲氨蝶呤剂量超过15毫克（15 mg/周）的患者、以及妊娠晚期孕妇，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如果您有肾功能障碍、心血管循环功能异常、肝功能障碍、心功能异常、血液异常、支气管哮喘、手术前患者、3岁以下婴幼儿、对镇痛剂、消炎剂、抗风湿剂过敏以及有其他过敏性疾病史的患者、高龄者（老年人）、葡萄糖-6-磷酸脱氢酶缺乏症患者、对黄色5号过敏的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请注意不要损坏本药的肠溶衣。

如果您将要进行手术（包括牙科手术），请务必告知您的医生您正在服用阿司匹林制剂并进行咨询。
