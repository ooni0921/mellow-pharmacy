# EN
### 💊 티알부틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for digestive dysfunction in conditions such as gastroesophageal reflux and hiatal hernia, gastritis and duodenitis, and gastric and duodenal ulcers, with symptoms including abdominal pain, indigestion, nausea, and vomiting. It is also indicated for irritable bowel syndrome, spastic colon, habitual vomiting, and non-infectious intestinal transit disorders such as constipation and diarrhea, particularly in pediatric diseases.

### 🕒 티알부틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets (100-200 mg) orally three times a day before meals.

Dosage may be adjusted as appropriate according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 티알부틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - Precautions
* No information
* Pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, and lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

# CN
### 💊 티알부틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗食管反流、裂孔疝、胃十二指肠炎、胃十二指肠溃疡等消化功能异常，伴有腹痛、消化不良、恶心、呕吐等症状。同时，也用于治疗儿童非感染性肠道转运障碍，如肠易激综合征、痉挛性结肠、习惯性呕吐、便秘、腹泻等小儿疾病。

### 🕒 티알부틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - 用法用量
* 成人每次口服1~2片（100~200 mg），每日3次，饭前服用。

可根据年龄和症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 티알부틴정(트리메부틴말레산염) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 孕妇、可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女在服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师。
