### 💊 Azeliacream (Azelaic acid (micronized)) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for acne vulgaris (common acne).

### 🕒 Azeliacream (Azelaic acid (micronized)) - Dosage and Administration
* Before applying this medication, thoroughly cleanse and dry the affected area with clean water or a mild facial cleanser. Unless otherwise directed by a doctor or pharmacist, apply a sufficient amount to the affected area twice daily (morning and evening) and rub it in well. The treatment period varies depending on the individual and the severity of the acne, but noticeable therapeutic effects generally appear after about 4 weeks. For optimal therapeutic effects, regular and consistent use for several months is recommended. If skin irritation is severe, reduce the frequency of use to once daily until the irritation subsides, or consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ Azeliacream (Azelaic acid (micronized)) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to it. Before using this medication, pregnant or breastfeeding women, children under 12 years of age, or individuals with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Use consistently throughout the treatment period. Do not use occlusive dressings or wraps. This medication is for external use only. Be careful to avoid contact with eyes, nose, mouth, and other mucous membranes. If it gets into the eyes, rinse thoroughly with a sufficient amount of water. Always wash hands with water after applying the medication.

* 本药用于寻常痤疮（普通痤疮）。

### 🕒 Azeliacream (Azelaic acid (micronized)) - 用法与用量
* 在涂抹本药前，请用清水或温和的洁面乳彻底清洁并擦干患处。除非医生或药剂师另有指示，否则每日两次（早晚）将足够量的本药涂抹于患处并充分揉搓。治疗周期因个体差异和痤疮严重程度而异，但通常在约4周后出现显著疗效，为获得最佳疗效，建议规律并持续使用数月。如果皮肤刺激非常严重，请在使用频率减少至每日一次直至刺激消失，或咨询医生或药剂师。

### ⚠️ Azeliacream (Azelaic acid (micronized)) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者请勿使用本药。在使用本药前，孕妇、哺乳期妇女、12岁以下儿童以及对丙二醇过敏或有相关病史的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。治疗期间请勿间断使用。请勿使用密封绷带法或包扎法。本药仅供外用。请注意避免接触眼睛、鼻子、口腔及其他黏膜。如果不慎入眼，请用足量的水彻底冲洗。涂药后务必用清水洗手。

### 🕒 Azeliacream (Azelaic acid (micronized)) - 用法・用量
* 本剤を塗布する前に、患部をきれいな水または刺激の少ない洗顔料で丁寧に洗浄し、乾燥させてください。医師または薬剤師から特別な指示がない限り、患部に1日2回（朝、夕）十分な量を塗布し、よく擦り込んでください。治療期間は個人差やニキビの程度によって異なりますが、通常、約4週間後から明らかな治療効果が現れ、最適な治療効果を得るためには数ヶ月間、規則的かつ継続的に使用してください。皮膚刺激が非常に強い場合は、刺激がなくなるまで使用回数を1日1回に減らすか、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
