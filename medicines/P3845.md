* 15세 이상 어린이 및 성인 1회 2~3정, 11~14세 어린이는 1회 1~2정, 5~10세 어린이는 1회 1정, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

* 정보 없음

# EN
### 💊 바이코민정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B6, and B12 during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, or physical weakness during or after illness (while sick or recovering), and for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders, frozen shoulder), numbness of hands and feet, and eye strain.

### 🕒 바이코민정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 15 years and older and adults: 2 to 3 tablets, once daily.
*   For children aged 11 to 14 years: 1 to 2 tablets, once daily.
*   For children aged 5 to 10 years: 1 tablet, once daily.

### ⚠️ 바이코민정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to any component of this medicine, if you are a child under 5 years of age, or if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, pregnant or lactating women, patients with a thrombotic predisposition, or patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Do not touch this medicine with wet hands, as the tablets may discolor.

# CN
### 💊 바이코민정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于补充维生素 B1, B6, B12，适用于身体疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时，以及缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬、五十肩等）、手脚麻木、眼睛疲劳等症状。

### 🕒 바이코민정 - 用法与用量
*   15岁以上儿童及成人：一次2~3片，每日一次。
*   11~14岁儿童：一次1~2片，每日一次。
*   5~10岁儿童：一次1片，每日一次。

### ⚠️ 바이코민정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药任何成分过敏者、5岁以下儿童以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   孕妇、哺乳期妇女、有血栓倾向的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者在服用本药前应咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   请勿用湿手触摸本药，因为药片可能会变色。
