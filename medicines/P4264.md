### 💊 오트리빈에스비강분무액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve symptoms of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy head due to common cold (acute rhinitis), allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 오트리빈에스비강분무액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Dosage and Administration
* For adolescents aged 12 years and older and adults, spray 1 to 2 times into each nostril, 1 to 2 times daily.

Do not exceed 2 times a day.

The minimum interval between uses is 8 hours.

Use according to the following steps:

1. Clean your nose.

2. Hold the nozzle between two fingers with your thumb supporting the bottom of the bottle, keeping the bottle upright.

3. Tilt your head slightly forward, insert the nozzle into your nostril, spray inside your nose, and gently inhale simultaneously.

4. After use, clean and dry the nozzle before closing the cap.

### ⚠️ 오트리빈에스비강분무액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug or have a history of hypersensitivity, are under 7 years of age, are taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks, have undergone surgery that exposes the dura mater or transsphenoidal hypophysectomy, have closed-angle glaucoma, or have rhinitis sicca (dry rhinitis) or atrophic rhinitis.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, are a child aged 7 to 11, have an allergic constitution, have a history of drug-induced allergy (fever, rash, redness, swelling, irritation, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have hypertension, cardiovascular disease, hyperthyroidism, diabetes, pheochromocytoma, prostatic hyperplasia, show a strong reaction to adrenergic substances, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

For children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medication is for intranasal spray for rhinitis only; do not use in the eyes or ears.

Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 本药品用于缓解因普通感冒（急性鼻炎）、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流涕、打喷嚏、头重感等症状。

### 🕒 오트리빈에스비강분무액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - 用法用量
* 12岁及以上青少年和成人，每次1~2喷，每日1~2次，喷于各鼻腔。

每日请勿超过2次。

最小使用间隔为8小时。

请按以下顺序使用：

1. 清洁鼻腔。

2. 用两根手指夹住喷嘴，拇指托住瓶底，使瓶身直立。

3. 稍微向前倾斜，将喷嘴伸入鼻腔，喷入鼻腔内，同时轻轻吸气。

4. 使用后，在盖上盖子前，请清洁并擦干喷嘴。

### 🕒 오트리빈에스비강분무액(옥시메タ졸린염산염) - 用法・用量
* 12歳以上の青少年および成人は、1回1～2プッシュを、1日1～2回、各鼻腔に噴霧します。

1日2回を超えて使用しないでください。

最低使用間隔は8時間です。

以下の手順で使用してください。

1. 鼻をきれいにします。

2. ノズルを指2本で挟み、親指でボトルを下から支え、ボトルを立てます。

3. 少し前かがみになり、ノズルを鼻の中に入れ、鼻の中に噴射すると同時に、やさしく呼吸します。

4. 使用後、キャップを閉める前にノズルをきれいにし、乾燥させます。
