#EN
### 💊 다나펜카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory treatment of degenerative osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia, and post-traumatic swelling/pain.

### 🕒 다나펜카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply to the affected area twice a day after peeling off the release liner from the patch.

    Use after wiping off sweat from the application site.

### ⚠️ 다나펜카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have experienced hypersensitivity to this drug, aspirin asthma (asthma attack caused by NSAIDs, etc.) or have a history of it, or on damaged skin and mucous membranes, eczema, or rash areas.

    Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma, hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, or if you are pregnant, a child, or elderly.

#CN

*   本药用于治疗退行性关节炎（骨关节炎）、肩周炎、肌腱炎/腱鞘炎、肱骨外上髁炎（如网球肘）、肌肉痛、外伤后的肿胀和疼痛，具有镇痛消炎作用。

### 🕒 다나펜카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - 用法与用量
*   每日2次，撕下药贴表面的剥离纸，贴于患处。

    请在使用前擦干患处的汗水。

### ⚠️ 다나펜카타플라스마(플루르비프로펜) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药有过敏史者、阿司匹林哮喘（非甾体抗炎药等引起的哮喘发作）或有相关病史者、以及皮肤和黏膜受损部位、湿疹、皮疹部位，请勿使用本药。

    在使用本药前，支气管哮喘患者、对丙二醇有过敏史或有相关病史者、孕妇、儿童、老年人请咨询医生或药师。
