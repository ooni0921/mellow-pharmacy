# EN
### 💊 하로나연고 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the initial treatment of corticosteroid-responsive dermatoses (dermatitis, infected wounds) secondarily infected by neomycin-susceptible bacteria.

### 🕒 하로나연고 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 2 times a day.

### ⚠️ 하로나연고 - Precautions
*   Do not use this drug in patients with hypersensitivity to this drug or aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or bacitracin; patients with skin infections (cutaneous tuberculosis, syphilis, candidiasis, tinea, herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia, scabies, pubic lice); eczematous otitis externa with tympanic membrane perforation; ulcers (except Behcet's disease); second-degree deep or higher burns and frostbite; perioral dermatitis; common acne; or rosacea.
*   Do not use on or around the eyes.
*   Before using this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, an infant or child, or have a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Do not use for ophthalmic purposes. If it gets into the eyes, wash immediately with water, and if symptoms are severe, seek treatment from an ophthalmologist.
*   Do not use for purposes other than treatment, such as after makeup or shaving.
*   Do not use occlusive dressing therapy without a doctor's supervision.

# CN

*   本药用于新霉素敏感细菌继发感染的皮质类固醇反应性皮肤病（皮炎、感染伤口）的初步治疗。

### 🕒 하로나연고 - 用法与用量
*   每日1-2次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ 하로나연고 - 注意事项
*   对本药或氨基糖苷类抗生素（如链霉素、卡那霉素、庆大霉素、新霉素）或杆菌肽过敏的患者，患有皮肤感染症（皮肤结核、梅毒、念珠菌病、癣、带状疱疹、单纯疱疹、水痘、牛痘、疥疮、阴虱）的患者，伴有鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳炎患者，溃疡患者（贝赫切特病除外），二度深层以上烧伤及冻伤患者，口周皮炎、寻常痤疮、酒渣鼻患者，请勿使用本药。
*   请勿用于眼睛或眼睛周围。
*   在使用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性及哺乳期妇女、婴幼儿、对丙二醇过敏或有相关病史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
*   请勿用于眼科。万一进入眼睛，请立即用水冲洗，如症状严重，请接受眼科医生的治疗。
*   请勿用于化妆或剃须后等治疗以外的目的。
*   未经医生指导，请勿使用密封包扎法。
