### 🕒 리버텍트골드연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 리버텍트골드연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information provided
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, have severe biliary obstruction, or are a child under 12 years of age.
* Before taking this medication, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have fat metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or if you are at special risk due to fat overload, or if you are pregnant, may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
