### π’ μ λ½μ‹ μ—°κ³ (ν¬λ΅νƒ€λ―Έν†¤) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve skin itching.

### π•’ μ λ½μ‹ μ—°κ³ (ν¬λ΅νƒ€λ―Έν†¤) - Dosage and Administration
* Once daily after a light bath, apply to the entire well-dried skin, excluding the face and scalp, and gently massage. If necessary, repeat after 24 hours.

### β οΈ μ λ½μ‹ μ—°κ³ (ν¬λ΅νƒ€λ―Έν†¤) - Precautions
* None
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication or a history thereof, or those with acute lupus erythematosus, should not use this medication. Before using this medication, individuals with hypersensitivity to propylene glycol or a history thereof, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, infants, children, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist. For conditions such as purulent dermatitis, weeping lesions, or erosions, treat these conditions first before using this medication. This medication is not for ophthalmic use.
