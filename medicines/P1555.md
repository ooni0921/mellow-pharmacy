### 🕒 코엔큐연질캡슐(수출명 : CAROLBIC SOFT CAPSULE) - 용법 및 용량
* 성인은 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 2회 복용합니다.

# EN
### 💊 코엔큐연질캡슐(수출명 : CAROLBIC SOFT CAPSULE) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, C, E in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during illness or convalescence (during illness or after recovery), and in old age. It also helps alleviate dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, and prevent gum bleeding and nosebleeds (epistaxis). Furthermore, it alleviates symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness in hands/feet, and cold hands/feet due to peripheral circulatory disorders and during menopause, and provides zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 코엔큐연질캡슐(수출명 : CAROLBIC SOFT CAPSULE) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 코엔큐연질캡슐(수출명 : CAROLBIC SOFT CAPSULE) - Precautions
* Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day may cause congenital deformities. Women who are within 3 months of pregnancy or who may be pregnant should not take this medicine.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, peanuts, and infants under 12 months should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, patients with impaired cardiac/circulatory function, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, patients with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or patients who need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously, patients with a thrombotic predisposition, or patients for whom special risks are expected due to lipid overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

It may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after taking it for about 1 month, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 코엔큐연질캡슐(수출명 : CAROLBIC SOFT CAPSULE) - 功效与效果
* 本药用于缓解身体疲劳，妊娠·哺乳期，病中·病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时，以及老年期维生素A、C、E的补充，缓解眼睛干涩，夜盲症（夜晚视力不佳的症状），缓解因阳光·皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑），预防牙龈出血·鼻出血（流鼻血），缓解末梢血液循环障碍及更年期时出现的肩颈僵硬、手足麻木·手足冰冷（手脚冰凉）症状，并补充锌。

### 🕒 코엔큐연질캡슐(수출명 : CAROLBIC SOFT CAPSULE) - 用法与用量
* 成人一次1粒，一日2次服用。

### ⚠️ 코엔큐연질캡슐(수출명 : CAROLBIC SOFT CAPSULE) - 注意事项
* 每日服用维生素A超过5,000 IU可能会导致先天性畸形。妊娠3个月以内或可能怀孕的女性请勿服用本药。
* 对本药或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，以及未满12个月的婴儿请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症(尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态)、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心血管功能障碍患者、肾脏障碍患者、低蛋白血症、痛风或肾结石患者、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需慎重输注脂质乳剂的患者、有血栓（血管堵塞）倾向的患者、因脂肪过负荷预计有特殊风险的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

进行各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测。

服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请咨询医生或药师。
