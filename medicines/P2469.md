#EN
### 💊 한솔이부프로펜연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for fever and pain caused by the common cold, lower back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), post-operative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 한솔이부프로펜연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* **Adults:** For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, take 1 to 3 capsules (200 to 600 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day, up to a maximum of 16 capsules (3,200 mg) per day. For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30 to 40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or the common cold, adults take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day. For migraine, adults take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) at a time, not exceeding 2 capsules in 24 hours.

**Adolescents aged 12 years and older:** Take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day.

If body weight is less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid taking this medicine on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 한솔이부프로펜연질캡슐 - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this drug or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this drug may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to it, have or have a history of gastrointestinal ulcer or gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, or a history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs). Also, patients experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, and pregnant women in the last 3 months of pregnancy should not take this drug.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis or liver impairment or a history thereof, renal impairment or a history thereof, cardiac insufficiency or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) or mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, or if you are elderly, a child, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

When taken for the common cold, it should generally be taken for no more than 5 days. Do not take it for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 한솔이부프로펜연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
* **成人：** 对于类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、软组织损伤、非关节风湿性疾病、急性痛风、银屑病关节炎，每次服用1~3粒软胶囊（200~600毫克），每日3~4次，每日最高剂量不超过16粒软胶囊（3,200毫克）。对于幼年类风湿性关节炎，每日按体重每公斤30~40毫克，分3~4次服用。对于轻度及中度疼痛或感冒，成人每次服用1~2粒软胶囊（200~400毫克），每日3~4次。对于偏头痛，成人每次服用1~2粒软胶囊（200~400毫克），24小时内不得超过2粒软胶囊。

**12岁及以上青少年：** 每次服用1~2粒软胶囊（200~400毫克），每日3~4次。

体重不足30公斤者，每日剂量不得超过500毫克，并且最好避免空腹服用。

### ⚠️ 한솔이부프로펜연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 若每日规律饮酒三杯以上者，在服用本药或其它解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。本药可能引起胃肠道出血。

* 以下患者请勿服用本药：对本药过敏者，有胃肠道溃疡及胃肠道出血或曾有相关病史者，严重血液异常者，严重肝功能障碍者，严重肾功能障碍者，严重心功能不全者，严重高血压者，支气管哮喘或曾有相关病史者，曾因阿司匹林或其它非甾体抗炎镇痛药引起哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏反应等过敏症状者，冠状动脉旁路移植术前后发生疼痛的患者，以及妊娠最后三个月的孕妇。

在服用本药前，若您有以下情况，请咨询医生或药师：有过敏史者，有血液异常或曾有相关病史者，有出血倾向者，肝硬化及肝功能障碍或曾有相关病史者，肾功能障碍或曾有相关病史者，心功能不全或心脏疾病者，高血压者，全身性红斑狼疮及混合性结缔组织病患者，溃疡性结肠炎患者，克罗恩病患者，老年人及儿童，孕妇，哺乳期妇女。

请勿超过推荐剂量服用。

用于感冒时，原则上服用不超过5天。未经医生或药师指示，成人止痛服用不超过10天，儿童止痛服用不超过5天，发热服用不超过3天。

**12歳以上の青少年：** 1回1～2カプセル（200～400mg）を、1日3～4回服用します。
