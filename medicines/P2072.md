#EN
### 💊 Hemoren Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to supplement vitamin C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, and periods of physical debility during or after illness, and for the prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia.

### 🕒 Hemoren Capsule - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ Hemoren Capsule - Precautions
*   Toxicity may occur if administered to children under 6 years of age.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, infants under 12 months, patients with Wilson's disease, hemochromatosis (a disorder of iron metabolism where iron deposits in the liver and pancreas), hemosiderosis, or non-iron deficiency anemia should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Before taking this medication, patients with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, premature infants, infants, gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related diseases, gout or kidney stones, or folate deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests.
*   Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 Hemoren Capsule - 功效与作用
*   本品用于体力疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中病后体力下降时补充维生素C，以及预防和治疗缺铁性贫血。

### 🕒 Hemoren Capsule - 用法用量
*   成人每日1次，每次1粒，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ Hemoren Capsule - 注意事项
*   6岁以下儿童服用可能出现毒性。
*   对本品过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、Wilson病患者、血色素沉着病（铁代谢异常导致铁在肝脏、胰腺沉积的疾病）、含铁血黄素沉着症患者、非缺铁性贫血患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   服用本品前，高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）患者、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心血管功能障碍者、肾功能障碍者、低蛋白血症患者、胆囊相关疾病患者、痛风或肾结石患者、叶酸缺乏患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   本品可能干扰各种尿液检查中的血糖检测。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

### 🕒 ヘモレンカプセル - 用法・用量
*   成人は1日1回、1カプセルを食後に服用してください。
