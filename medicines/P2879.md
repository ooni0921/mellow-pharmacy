#EN
### 💊 미인활명수액 - Efficacy and Effect
* This medicine is used for loss of appetite (anorexia), gastric bloating, indigestion, overeating, upset stomach, nausea, vomiting, bowel regulation, constipation, loose stools, abdominal distension, and abnormal fermentation in the intestines.

### 🕒 미인활명수액 - Dosage and Administration
* For ages 15 and above: 1 bottle (75 mL) per dose.
* For ages 11 and above to under 15: 2/3 bottle (50 mL) per dose.
* For ages 8 and above to under 11: 1/2 bottle (37.5 mL) per dose.
* For ages 5 and above to under 8: 1/3 bottle (25 mL) per dose.
* For ages 3 and above to under 5: 1/4 bottle (18.75 mL) per dose.
* For ages 1 and above to under 3: 1/5 bottle (15 mL) per dose.
* Take 3 times a day after meals.

Ensure a dosing interval of at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 미인활명수액 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not administer this medicine to infants under 3 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, or if you have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to caramel.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, immediately discontinue the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 미인활명수액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于食欲不振、胃部饱胀感、消化不良、暴饮暴食、积食、恶心、呕吐、调理肠胃、便秘、稀便、腹胀感、肠内异常发酵。

### 🕒 미인활명수액 - 用法用量
* 15岁以上：一次1瓶 (75 mL)。
* 11岁以上至未满15岁：一次2/3瓶 (50 mL)。
* 8岁以上至未满11岁：一次1/2瓶 (37.5 mL)。
* 5岁以上至未满8岁：一次1/3瓶 (25 mL)。
* 3岁以上至未满5岁：一次1/4瓶 (18.75 mL)。
* 1岁以上至未满3岁：一次1/5瓶 (15 mL)。
* 每日3次，饭后服用。

服药间隔应为4小时以上。

### ⚠️ 미인활명수액 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 未满3个月的婴儿请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，以及对焦糖有过敏反应或过敏史者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

如果服用约2周后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
