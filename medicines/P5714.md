### 💊 하디코플러스정 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms such as runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 하디코플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 하디코플러스정 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of Acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing Acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or its components, have experienced asthma when taking other fever reducers/pain relievers or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, a child under 2 years of age, or a patient with severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 2 years of age (only if absolutely necessary), an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, experience symptoms of ischemic colitis such as sudden abdominal pain or rectal bleeding related to the pseudoephedrine component, have experienced allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due to medication, are elderly, are physically weak, or have a high fever.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer, glaucoma, difficulty urinating, or if gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain persist or recur, or if you have ulcer or bleeding problems, or if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not continue to take this medicine for a long period.

Drowsiness may occur during administration. Avoid driving a car or operating machinery.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
