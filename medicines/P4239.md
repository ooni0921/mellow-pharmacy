#EN
### 💊 티라노골드플러스츄어블정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, B1, B2, B6, C, D, E in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during and after illness (during illness or after recovery), during growth, and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), poor development of bones and teeth, and prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 티라노골드플러스츄어블정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 8 years or older and adults: Take 2 tablets twice daily. Children aged 36 months or older to under 8 years: Take 1 tablet twice daily.
    Dissolve in the mouth or chew before swallowing.

### ⚠️ 티라노골드플러스츄어블정 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day may cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medication if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy.
    In children under 6 years of age, an overdose may cause fatal iron poisoning, so keep this medication out of reach of children.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this medication, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have hemochromatosis (a disease where iron accumulates in the liver and pancreas due to iron metabolism abnormality), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis (Crohn's disease), a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a history of kidney stones.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

    If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medication, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 티라노골드플러스츄어블정 - 用法用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人：每日2次，每次2片。36个月以上至8岁以下儿童：每日2次，每次1片。
    口服时可含化或咀嚼服用。

### ⚠️ 티라노골드플러스츄어블정 - 注意事项
*   每日服用超过5,000 IU的维生素A可能会导致先天性畸形。妊娠3个月以内或可能怀孕的女性请勿服用本品。
    6岁以下儿童过量服用可能会导致铁中毒性死亡，请将本品放置于儿童无法触及的地方。
*   患有高钙血症（血液中钙质过多的状态）、结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病）、肾脏疾病、对本品过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、血色病（铁代谢异常导致铁在肝脏、胰腺沉积的疾病）、含铁血黄素沉着症、非缺铁性贫血、肾结石、症状严重的肾功能不全患者，以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本品。

    服用本品前，请咨询医生或药剂师：未满1岁的婴儿、经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、高草酸尿症患者（尿液中草酸盐过量排出的状态）、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎（出现在特定部位）等胃肠道疾病的患者、患有心脏循环系统功能障碍、肾脏功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风、有肾结石病史的患者。

    请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

    给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

    本品可能使尿液变黄，并影响临床检查结果。

    如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。
