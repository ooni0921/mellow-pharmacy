#EN
### 💊 엘비페롤정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamin D during pregnancy and lactation, growth period, and old age, as well as for poor development of bones and teeth, and the prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 엘비페롤정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years or older should take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 엘비페롤정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, infants under 12 months of age, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication.
*   Before taking this medication, infants under 1 year of age, children who are exposed to a lot of sunlight and have a normal diet, pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, infants, those with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If there is no improvement in symptoms after taking for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#JP
### 💊 엘비페롤정 - 効能及び効果
*   本剤は、妊娠・授乳期、発育期、老年期におけるビタミンDの補給、骨・歯の発育不良、くる病の予防に用いられます。

### 🕒 엘비페롤정 - 用法及び用量
*   成人及び8歳以上の小児は、1日1回1錠を服用してください。

### ⚠️ 엘비페롤정 - 注意事項
*   情報なし
*   本剤に対し過敏症の既往歴のある患者、高カルシウム血症（血液中にカルシウムが過剰に存在する状態）、サルコイドーシス（原因不明の全身性炎症性疾患）、腎臓疾患、生後12ヵ月未満の乳児、腎結石、重度の腎不全、ガラクトース不耐症、Lappラクターゼ欠損症またはグルコース・ガラクトース吸収不良などの遺伝的問題のある患者は本剤を服用しないでください。

*   1ヶ月程度服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合、服用を直ちに中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
*   小児に服用させる場合、保護者の指導監督のもとに服用させてください。
*   定められた用法・用量を厳守してください。
