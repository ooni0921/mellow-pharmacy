### 💊 트라스트이부플라스타(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), muscle pain, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 트라스트이부플라스타(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply one patch once a day after peeling off the release liner from the medicated surface.

Unless otherwise directed by a doctor, the treatment period should not exceed 14 days, and the patch should be replaced every 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 트라스트이부플라스타(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, aspirin, other analgesics, or NSAIDs (bronchospasm, angioedema, asthma, allergic rhinitis, rash, anaphylactic reactions) or a history thereof, or if you have or have a history of bronchial asthma, gastrointestinal bleeding, gastric/duodenal ulcer or peptic ulcer.
*   Do not use if you have severe heart failure, severe renal or hepatic impairment, are under 12 years of age, are in the third trimester of pregnancy or breastfeeding, have eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer (urushiol), etc., have wounds or inflammatory areas, sensitive skin, hemorrhagic skin diseases, infected skin, or burn areas.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are elderly, have chronic bronchitis, allergic rhinitis, or nasal mucosal inflammation, have a history of gastrointestinal ulcer/bleeding or perforation, ulcerative colitis, or Crohn's disease, have hepatic/renal dysfunction or heart failure, have a history of hypertension or kidney disease, are pregnant (in the 1st and 2nd trimesters when absolutely necessary) or planning pregnancy, have developed intolerance after taking aspirin or ibuprofen, have a history of allergic symptoms (rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.) due to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, severe dampness/erosion, circulatory disorders, diabetes, inflammation accompanied by infection, or chronic diseases (e.g., degenerative arthritis/osteoarthritis).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Clean the affected area thoroughly, and apply after wiping off sweat, etc.

Remove the patch 30 minutes to 1 hour before bathing, and avoid applying immediately after bathing.

This medicine is for external use only; do not ingest.

Do not use on or around the eyes or mucous membranes, and be careful not to get it in the eyes.

Do not use occlusive dressings and avoid exposure to external heat or hot water.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
