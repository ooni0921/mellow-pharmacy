### 💊 MgMax Plus B Soft Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for physical fatigue; during pregnancy and lactation; physical weakness during illness or convalescence (recovery after illness); supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B6 in old age; alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders and shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness of hands and feet (cold hands/feet) during menopause; alleviation of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as low back pain, shoulder stiffness); beriberi; eye fatigue; alleviation of angular stomatitis (cheilosis), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis; and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 MgMax Plus B Soft Capsule - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ MgMax Plus B Soft Capsule - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you have hypersensitivity or allergy experience to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you are a patient with severe renal impairment.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant (general term for young children), a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient with a special risk of fat overload, or a patient with folic acid deficiency.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
