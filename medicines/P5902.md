#EN
### 💊 메가트루육삼삼정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, and D in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or convalescence (recovery after illness) when physical strength is low, during growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 메가트루육삼삼정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age or older: Take 1 tablet per dose, 1 to 2 times daily.

### ⚠️ 메가트루육삼삼정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient hypersensitive to this drug, a patient with hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), renal (kidney) disease, an infant under 12 months of age, a patient with kidney stones, or a patient with severe renal insufficiency.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant, or have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional (appearing in specific parts) colitis, cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal (kidney) impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue administration immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
