#EN
### 💊 콘드로센정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This product is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery). It is also used for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain such as lumbago, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 콘드로센정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 콘드로센정 - Precautions
* **Contraindications:** Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to any component of this product, an infant under 3 months of age, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* **Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are:** an infant under 1 year of age, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant (general term for young child), or a patient with folic acid deficiency.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medication may turn urine yellow and can affect clinical test results.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 콘드로센정 - 功效与效果
* 本品用于身体疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时补充维生素B1、B2、B6，以及缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）、脚气病、眼疲劳。

### 🕒 콘드로센정 - 用法与用量
* 8岁以上及成人，每次1片，每日1次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 콘드로센정 - 注意事项
* **禁忌症:** 对本品成分过敏的患者、3个月以下的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本品。
* **服用本品前请咨询医生或药师，如果您是:** 1岁以下的婴儿、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿（泛指幼童）、或叶酸缺乏症患者。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
* 本品可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检测结果。
* 如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
