# EN
### 💊 아티벡스겔(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Efficacy and Indications
* This medicine is used for the relief of pain and inflammation in traumatic inflammation of tendons, ligaments, muscles, and joints (e.g., sprains, bruises), localized forms of soft tissue rheumatism (e.g., tenosynovitis, shoulder-hand syndrome, bursitis, periarthritis), and localized forms of rheumatic diseases (e.g., osteoarthritis of peripheral joints and spine).

### 🕒 아티벡스겔(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and adolescents 12 years of age and older, apply 2 to 4 g to the affected area 3 to 4 times a day, depending on the size of the painful area, and rub in lightly.

### ⚠️ 아티벡스겔(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, or a history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma attacks, urticaria, or acute rhinitis to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs). Also, do not use in children under 12 years of age, pregnant women, and nursing mothers.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medicine if you have bronchial asthma, are elderly, or have a history of propylene glycol hypersensitivity.

Do not use on skin with lesions (bleeding skin disorders, eczema, infected lesions, burns, wounds), eyes, or mucous membranes.

Wash your hands thoroughly after use. (Except when used on finger joints)

Long-term use over a large area may result in systemic adverse reactions.

Do not use with occlusive bandaging.

# CN
### 💊 아티벡스겔(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - 功效与作用
* 本药品用于缓解肌腱、韧带、肌肉和关节的创伤性炎症（如扭伤、挫伤等）、局部性软组织风湿病（如腱鞘炎、肩手综合征、滑囊炎、关节周围炎等）以及局部性风湿性疾病（如周围关节和脊柱的骨关节病等）引起的疼痛和炎症。

### 🕒 아티벡스겔(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - 用法与用量
* 12岁及以上的成人和青少年，根据疼痛部位的面积，每次取2~4克，每日3~4次，涂抹于患处并轻轻揉搓。

### ⚠️ 아티벡스겔(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药品过敏或有过敏史者，对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎镇痛药有过敏反应（如哮喘发作、荨麻疹或急性鼻炎）者，12岁以下儿童，孕妇和哺乳期妇女请勿使用本药品。

在使用本药品前，支气管哮喘患者、老年人、对丙二醇过敏或有过敏史者应咨询医生或药师。

请勿用于有病变（出血性皮肤病、湿疹、感染病变、烧伤、伤口）的皮肤、眼睛和粘膜部位。

使用后请彻底洗手。（手指关节使用除外）

长期大面积使用可能出现全身性不良反应。

请勿使用密封包扎法。
