### ⚠️ 화이투벤씨플러스캡슐 - 주의사항
* 매일 세잔 이상 정기적 음주자가 이 약 또는 다른 해열진통제를 복용할 때는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오. 간손상을 일으킬 수 있습니다.

매우 드물게 치명적일 수 있는 급성전신발진고름물집증, 스티븐스- 존슨 증후군, 독성표피괴사용해와 같은 중대한 피부반응이 보고되었고 이 약 복용 후 피부발진 또는 다른 과민반응의 징후가 나타나는 경우 즉시 복용을 중단하십시오.

정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

#EN
### 💊 화이투벤씨플러스캡슐 - Indications and Efficacy
* This medication is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 화이투벤씨플러스캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older and adults, take 2 capsules once. For children over 7 years and under 15 years of age, take 1 capsule once. Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 화이투벤씨플러스캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other fever reducers/pain relievers or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age or a child under 2 years of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastric/duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, are elderly, weak, or have high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcers or bleeding problems, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts or recurs for more than a week, chronic cough, a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache, or have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Yellow No. 5.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after taking 5 to 6 doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 对本药有或曾有过敏史者、服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾有过哮喘史者、未满3个月的婴儿及未满2岁的儿童、正在服用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪稳定剂、抗帕金森病药等）或在停药后2周内者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师，若您是感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿或未满15岁儿童、过敏体质者、曾因药物引起过敏症状者（如发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）、患有肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（如眼睛疼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者、高龄者、体弱者或高热者、有持续或复发的胃肠问题（如胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛）或有溃疡、出血问题者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、吸烟者、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续或反复超过一周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽、对黄色5号有过敏症或过敏反应史者。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
