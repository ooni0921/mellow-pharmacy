# EN
### 💊 뉴콘틴연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet during menopause. Additionally, it aids in the relief of symptoms like neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, shoulder pain), beriberi, eye strain, angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 뉴콘틴연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 뉴콘틴연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient at special risk due to fat overload, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition, a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient with a history of or hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Tartrazine).
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

# CN
### 💊 뉴콘틴연질캡슐 - 功效及效果
*   本品用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠及哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时，以及老年期维生素E、B1、B2、B6的补充。并用于缓解末梢血液循环障碍、更年期肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木及手脚冰冷（手脚发凉）症状，以及神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩痛等）症状、脚气病、眼睛疲劳、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

### 🕒 뉴콘틴연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人：每次1粒，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 뉴콘틴연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本品或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿，请勿服用本品。
*   在服用本品前，以下人群请咨询医生或药师：未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性和哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需要慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者、因脂肪过负荷预计有特殊风险的患者、有血栓倾向的患者、叶酸不足的患者、对黄色5号（日落黄）有过敏史或过敏经验的患者。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本品可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检测结果。
