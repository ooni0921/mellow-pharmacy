이 약을 복용하기 전에 흡연자, 고혈압 환자, 비만자, 40세 이상의 여성, 간질, 편두통 환자, 동맥질환의 가족력이 있거나 정맥류 경험자, 전신성 홍반성 루푸스, 유방암의 가족력이 있는 환자, 우울증 환자 및 경험자, 심·신부전, 비전형적 과다증식된 유선병증 환자, 유방결절 경험자, 심장판막질환, 동맥세동 환자 및 경험자, 고지혈증의 가족력이 있는 환자, 담낭질환 및 담석증 경험자, 자궁내막증, 천식 또는 기미, 말초혈액순환장애, 당뇨병, 전조를 수반하지 않는 편두통 환자는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 쎄스콘노아정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for contraception and for the treatment of moderate acne in women for whom this medication is suitable.

### 🕒 쎄스콘노아정 - Dosage and Administration
* Take one tablet daily at the same time for 21 days, followed by a 7-day tablet-free interval.

For further details, refer to the approved prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 쎄스콘노아정 - Precautions
* Smoking increases the risk of serious cardiovascular adverse events, such as thrombosis, in women taking oral contraceptives. Women over 35 years of age who smoke should not use this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have: hypersensitivity to this medicine; estrogen-dependent tumors (e.g., endometrial cancer); breast cancer (patients, suspected cases, or history); thrombophlebitis, thromboembolism (patients or history); deep vein thrombosis; congenital or acquired thrombophilia; cerebrovascular or coronary artery disease (especially myocardial infarction); undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding; moderate to severe hypertension; moderate to severe hepatic impairment, jaundice, hepatitis, and benign or malignant liver tumors (patients or history); severe diabetes with vascular complications; severe metabolic disorders such as severe hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, and porphyria; persistent itching during pregnancy; herpes gestationis; are pregnant or may be pregnant; are breastfeeding; have a pituitary tumor; hyperprolactinemia; valvular heart disease; diabetes mellitus with vascular lesions; pancreatitis; genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption; or are a smoker over 35 years of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker; have hypertension; are obese; are a woman over 40 years of age; have epilepsy; have migraines; have a family history of arterial disease or a history of varicose veins; have systemic lupus erythematosus; have a family history of breast cancer; have or have had depression; have cardiac or renal failure; have atypical proliferative mastopathy; have a history of breast nodules; have valvular heart disease; have or have had atrial fibrillation; have a family history of hyperlipidemia; have a history of gallbladder disease and gallstones; have endometriosis; have asthma or chloasma (melasma); have peripheral circulatory disorders; have diabetes mellitus; or have migraines without aura.

This medicine does not protect against HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases.

In case of severe gastrointestinal disorders, absorption may be incomplete, so use an additional contraceptive method.

This medicine is not indicated for pre-menarcheal or post-menopausal women.

For detailed precautions, refer to the full prescribing information.

服用本药前，吸烟者、高血压患者、肥胖者、40岁以上女性、癫痫患者、偏头痛患者、有动脉疾病家族史或有静脉曲张史者、系统性红斑狼疮患者、有乳腺癌家族史的患者、抑郁症患者及有病史者、心肾功能不全患者、非典型增生性乳腺病患者、有乳腺结节史者、心脏瓣膜病患者、心房颤动患者及有病史者、有高脂血症家族史的患者、有胆囊疾病及胆石症史者、子宫内膜异位症患者、哮喘或黄褐斑患者、周围血液循环障碍患者、糖尿病患者、无先兆偏头痛患者，请咨询医生或药师。
