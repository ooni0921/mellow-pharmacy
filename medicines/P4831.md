#EN
### 💊 비맥스메타비정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 and Zinc during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during and after illness (during illness or recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain, such as lumbago and stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 비맥스메타비정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age or older: Take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 비맥스메타비정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, or a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis (Crohn's disease), heart or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency.
*   Adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and affect clinical test values.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 비맥스메타비정 - 用法用量
*   12岁及以上成人，每次1片，每日1次，饭后服用。

*   对本药物过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高）、结节病（不明原因的全身炎症性疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石、严重肾功能不全患者，请勿服用本药物。
*   服用本药物前，请咨询医生或药师：经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性肠炎等胃肠道疾病者、心脏或循环系统功能障碍者、肾功能障碍者、低蛋白血症者、有肾结石病史者、叶酸缺乏症患者。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人指导下服用。
*   本药物可能使尿液变黄，并影响临床检查结果。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

*   定められた用法・用量を厳守してください。
*   小児に服用させる場合には、保護者の指導監督のもとに服用させてください。
