#EN
### 💊 가네톡액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the adjunctive treatment of liver diseases.

### 🕒 가네톡액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 10-30 mL daily, divided between meals.

Dilute 10 mL of this medication in about half a glass of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 가네톡액 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have kidney disease, myocardial infarction, or a history thereof.

This medication cannot be used as an antidote for alcohol poisoning.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medication is for oral use only; do not use for injection.

If there is no improvement after approximately 1 month of taking the medication, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

* 本药用于肝病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 가네톡액 - 用法与用量
* 成人每日分次服用10-30毫升，两餐之间服用。

将本药10毫升稀释于约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 가네톡액 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 肾脏疾病、心肌梗塞患者及有相关病史者请勿服用本药。

本药不能用作酒精中毒的解毒剂。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

本药仅供口服，请勿用于注射。

服用约1个月后若无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
