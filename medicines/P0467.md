#EN
### 💊 삼익아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain due to colds, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 삼익아세트아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults, take 300 to 1,000 mg per dose, 3 to 4 times a day. A maximum of 8 tablets (4,000 mg) can be administered per day. The dosage can be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 삼익아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
*   Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.
*   Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of 4,000 mg of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorder, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, a history of asthma due to aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics, or if you are taking barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, or have consumed alcohol.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorder, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.
*   Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) or for fever for more than 3 days without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 삼익아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - 功效与作用
*   本品用于感冒引起的发热及疼痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、扭伤痛以及牙痛、关节痛、风湿样疼痛。

### 🕒 삼익아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - 用法用量
*   成人每次服用300~1,000 mg，每日3~4次。每日最高可服用8片（4,000 mg）。可根据年龄、症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 삼익아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上且规律饮酒者，在服用本品或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。
*   极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的急性全身发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症等严重皮肤反应。服用本品后若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。
*   对乙酰氨基酚每日最大剂量（4,000 mg）请勿超量服用。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
*   对本品过敏者、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液异常患者、严重肝功能障碍患者、严重肾功能障碍患者、严重心功能低下患者、曾因阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛剂引发哮喘的患者、正在服用巴比妥类药物或三环抗抑郁药的患者、以及饮酒者请勿服用本品。
*   服用本品前，肝功能障碍、肾功能（肾脏）障碍、消化性溃疡、血液异常、有出血倾向的患者、心功能异常患者、有过敏反应史者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。
*   未经医生或药师指示，止痛不应服用超过10天（成人）或5天（儿童），退烧不应服用超过3天。
