#EN
### 💊 Gokaldijung - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, and to supplement calcium deficiency and other calcium needs.

### 🕒 Gokaldijung - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ Gokaldijung - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take Gokaldijung if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to soybean oil, or hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking Gokaldijung, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to Yellow No. 4, or a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs to administer lipid emulsions cautiously.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
