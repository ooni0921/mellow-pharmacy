#EN
### 💊 하벤목에스캡슐 - Efficacy & Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 하벤목에스캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older and adults, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 하벤목에스캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to it or have a history of such reactions, if you have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, if you are a child under 2 years of age, or if you are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; if you have an allergic constitution or a history of allergic symptoms caused by medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma, urination difficulties; if you are frail or have a high fever; if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or if you have ulcer or bleeding problems; if you have lost a lot of fluid due to vomiting and diarrhea or cannot absorb fluids; if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding; if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts for more than 1 week or recurs, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
