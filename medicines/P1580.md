*   This medication is used for the relief of inflammatory symptoms accompanied by edema, and for post-traumatic or post-surgical edema.

### 🕒 벨라제정45mg(브로멜라인) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age or older should take 1 to 2 tablets (45-90 mg) once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes before meals with water.

Many aspects of its mechanism of action in the body have not yet been elucidated, and the relationship between dosage and effect has not been clarified. Therefore, do not take this medication without a specific purpose.

### ⚠️ 벨라제정45mg(브로멜라인) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients with severe blood coagulation disorders (e.g., hemophilia), severe liver or kidney disease (e.g., hemodialysis patients), patients currently taking anticoagulants or antiplatelet agents, pregnant and breastfeeding women, children under 12 years of age, and patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication.
