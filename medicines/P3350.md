### 💊 Nexcain Cream (Lidocaine) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medication is used for the reduction of tactile sensitivity of the glans penis in men.

### 🕒 Nexcain Cream (Lidocaine) - Dosage & Administration
* Apply 0.25~0.66 g of cream (23~62 mg as lidocaine) 5~15 minutes before intercourse. The dosage and application time vary individually, and the lowest effective dose should be used, but do not exceed a maximum of 2 g (184 mg as lidocaine) within a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ Nexcain Cream (Lidocaine) - Precautions
* No information provided
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication or other amide-type local anesthetics, if there is trauma or inflammation at the application site, or if your partner is pregnant. Before using this medication, patients with liver or kidney disease or hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. For external use only. Do not apply to eyes or nose. It is advisable not to apply continuously to the same area.
