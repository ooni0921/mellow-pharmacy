### 💊 마그벤연질캡슐 - 효능 및 효과
* 이 약은 육체피로, 임신ㆍ수유기, 병중ㆍ병후(병을 앓는 동안이나 회복 후)의 체력 저하 시, 노년기의 비타민 E, B1, B6의 보급에 사용합니다.

이 약은 함유된 비타민 등을 통해 말초혈행장애 및 갱년기시 어깨ㆍ목결림, 수족저림ㆍ수족냉증(손발이 차가움) 증상의 완화에 사용합니다.

이 약은 함유된 비타민 등을 통해 신경통, 근육통, 관절통(요통, 어깨결림 등), 각기, 눈의 피로, 구각염(입꼬리염), 구순염(입술염), 구내염(입안염), 설염(혀염), 습진, 피부염 증상의 완화에 사용합니다.

이 약은 마그네슘결핍으로 인한 근육경련에 사용합니다.

### 🕒 마그벤연질캡슐 - 용법 및 용량
* 만 12세 이상 어린이 및 성인은 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 2회 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 마그벤연질캡슐 - 주의사항
* 정보 없음
* 이 약에 과민증 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 심한 증상의 신부전 환자, 대두유에 과민증 환자 및 경험자, 콩 또는 땅콩에 과민증이 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

#EN
### 💊 마그벤연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins E, B1, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is diminished during or after illness, and in old age.

    This medicine is used to alleviate symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, menopausal shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet, through the vitamins it contains.

    This medicine is used to alleviate symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), beriberi, eye strain, angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation in the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis, through the vitamins it contains.

    This medicine is used for muscle cramps caused by magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그벤연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years and older, and adults, should take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 마그벤연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, an infant under 12 months of age, a patient with severe renal failure, a patient with a history of or current hypersensitivity to soybean oil, or a patient with hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, dyslipidemia such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsion, or a patient with a history of or current hypersensitivity to Allura Red AC (Red No. 40).

    Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    本药通过所含维生素等，用于缓解末梢血液循环障碍以及更年期时的肩颈僵硬、手足发麻・手足发凉（手脚冰冷）等症状。

    本药通过所含维生素等，用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）、脚气病、眼睛疲劳、口角炎（嘴角发炎）、唇炎（嘴唇发炎）、口腔炎（口腔内部发炎）、舌炎（舌头炎症）、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

    本药用于因镁缺乏引起的肌肉痉挛。

### 🕒 마그벤연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   12岁及以上儿童和成人，每次1粒，每日2次。

    服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、心血管系统功能障碍者、肾功能障碍者、低蛋白血症者、高脂蛋白血症者、糖尿病性高脂血症和胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者，对红色40号（诱惑红AC）过敏的患者或有相关经验者，请务必咨询医生或药剂师。

    请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

    给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

    若服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
