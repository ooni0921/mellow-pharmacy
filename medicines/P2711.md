### 💊 하벤큐연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (cold and shivering sensation), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle aches).

### 🕒 하벤큐연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 15 years and over: 2 capsules per dose. For children over 7 years and under 15 years: 1 capsule per dose. Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 하벤큐연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other fever-reducing analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, have experienced asthma when taking other fever-reducing analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 2 years of age, an infant or child under 15 years of age suspected or infected with chickenpox or influenza, or if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) appear in relation to the pseudoephedrine component, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicine (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), dysuria, are elderly, are frail, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as high fever, heartburn, gastric discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
