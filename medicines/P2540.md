### 💊 싸아크쿨로션 - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory action for sprains, bruises, muscle pain, joint pain, fracture pain, lower back pain, stiff shoulders, neuralgia, and rheumatic pain. It is also used for skin itching, insect bites, and chilblains.

### 🕒 싸아크쿨로션 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 4 times a day.

### ⚠️ 싸아크쿨로션 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this product, or on the eyes or around the eyes, mucous membranes, eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, wound areas, or sensitive skin.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash/redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances) due to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, have severe wetness or erosion (soreness), have circulatory disorders or diabetes, are a child under 12 years old, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine is for external use only; do not ingest or inhale it.

Be careful not to let it get into your eyes. If it does get into your eyes, rinse immediately with water.

If hands with the medicine touch mucous membranes such as the eyes, irritation may occur. Therefore, wash your hands thoroughly with soap immediately after touching this medicine.

Do not cover or bandage the applied area, and do not expose it to external heat or hot water.

If water touches the area where this medicine has been applied, you may feel a strong irritation.

Patients with particularly sensitive skin should avoid continuous use of this medicine on the same area.

If a large amount is absorbed through the skin, symptoms of poisoning may occur. Therefore, avoid long-term use over a wide surface area.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
