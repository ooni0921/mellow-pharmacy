#EN
### 💊 베노벡스겔 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory purposes in cases of varicose edema, superficial phlebitis, traumatic injury, sports injuries (contusions), and tenosynovitis.

### 🕒 베노벡스겔 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply a thin layer to the affected or infected area several times a day, then gently rub it into the skin.

### ⚠️ 베노벡스겔 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this drug in patients hypersensitive to this drug or salicylates, patients within 4 weeks of their expected delivery date, on mucous membranes, or on open wounds. Before using this drug, neonates and children, patients with renal insufficiency, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. Be careful not to get it in the eyes and do not apply it too widely. For external use only. When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
