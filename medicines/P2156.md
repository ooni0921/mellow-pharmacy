#EN
### 💊 마이칼디정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy, lactation, periods of growth, and old age, as well as for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, calcium deficiency, and other calcium supplementation.

### 🕒 마이칼디정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents aged 12 years and older should take one tablet at a time, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마이칼디정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney stones, or severe heart failure.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 1 month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
