### 💊 Retico Soft Capsule - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills (shivering and feeling cold), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 Retico Soft Capsule - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, take 2 capsules per dose. For children over 7 years to under 15 years of age, take 1 capsule per dose. Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ Retico Soft Capsule - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if regular drinkers consuming 3 or more alcoholic drinks daily take this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma after taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are: an infant or child under 15 years of age who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; a child under 2 years of age; a person with symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding); a person with an allergic constitution or a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); a person with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or dysuria; an elderly person, a debilitated person, or a person with high fever; a person with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or those with ulcers or bleeding issues; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a lactating woman; a person with a history of smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
