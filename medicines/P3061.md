#EN
### 💊 비알에스락산(바실루스리케니포르미스균) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for acute and chronic enteritis, acute and chronic diarrhea, acute dysentery, and abnormal intestinal fermentation caused by various factors.

### 🕒 비알에스락산(바실루스리케니포르미스균) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2 g per dose, 3 times a day, with an initial dose of 4 g.

Infants and young children should take 1 g per dose, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 비알에스락산(바실루스리케니포르미스균) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.

Infants under 3 months of age should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
