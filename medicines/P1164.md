#EN
### 💊 미투-에스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after illness (during illness or after recovery) when physical strength is reduced, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye strain, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder/neck stiffness during menopause, numbness/tingling and coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet), and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 미투-에스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents aged 12 years and older should take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 미투-에스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant/toddler, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, a patient requiring cautious administration of lipid emulsions, a patient expected to have special risks due to fat overload, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition, or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 and Yellow No. 5.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after about one month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
