#EN

### 💊 메코조인콘드로800정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to alleviate physical fatigue, supplement Vitamin D and B1 during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength declines during or after illness, during growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for the prevention of bone and tooth developmental disorders, rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation of the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 메코조인콘드로800정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 19 years or older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 메코조인콘드로800정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have kidney stones.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have heart or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow, which can affect clinical test values.
