#EN
### 💊 제이웰라민연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical decline during or after illness, and in old age. It is also used to alleviate symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness in hands/feet, and cold hands/feet during menopause.

### 🕒 제이웰라민연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 8 years of age and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 제이웰라민연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or other fat metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or a patient who needs to be administered lipid emulsions cautiously, a patient at special risk due to fat overload, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

本品在进行各种尿液检查时可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
