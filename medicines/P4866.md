#EN
### 💊 Pancrea-Jeong - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach, promoting digestion, and epigastric fullness (upper abdominal distension) due to indigestion.

### 🕒 Pancrea-Jeong - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 15 years or older: Take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Pancrea-Jeong - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take Pancrea-Jeong if you are a child under 7 years of age, a patient with hypersensitivity to soybean oil or who has experienced allergic reactions, or a patient with hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking Pancrea-Jeong, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or if you are a patient who needs to administer lipid emulsions carefully, have an allergic constitution, or are at special risk due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   本品用于消化不良、食欲不振（厌食）、过食、胃胀、促进消化、以及因消化不良引起的上腹部饱胀感。

*   无相关信息
*   7岁以下儿童、对大豆油过敏或有过敏反应史的患者、对大豆或花生过敏的患者请勿服用本品。

请严格遵守用法用量。
服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
