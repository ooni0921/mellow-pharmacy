#EN
### 💊 트라몰8시간서방정650밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve fever, pain associated with colds, headache, toothache, muscle pain, backache, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 트라몰8시간서방정650밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 2 tablets every 8 hours. Do not exceed 6 tablets within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 트라몰8시간서방정650밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, have peptic ulcers, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, a history of asthma caused by aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics, are taking barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, have consumed alcohol, or are a child under 12 years of age. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcers, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), and do not take for fever for more than 3 days, without direction from a doctor or pharmacist. This medicine is an extended-release formulation; therefore, swallow the tablet whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving it.

#CN

### 🕒 트라몰8시간서방정650밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - 用法用量
* 12岁及以上的儿童和成人，每8小时服用2片。24小时内不要超过6片。

### ⚠️ 트라몰8시간서방정650밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯或以上者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。极罕见情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。如果服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000 mg）请勿超过。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有乙酰氨酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡患者、有严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全者、曾因阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛药引起哮喘者、正在服用巴比妥类药物或三环抗抑郁药者、饮酒者、12岁以下儿童请勿服用本药。在服用本药之前，肝功能障碍、肾脏（肾）功能障碍、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、心功能异常、有过敏史者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药师。未经医生或药师指示，成人疼痛服用不超过10天，儿童不超过5天；发热服用不超过3天。本药为缓释制剂，请勿压碎、咀嚼或溶解药片，应整片吞服。
