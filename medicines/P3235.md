#EN
### 💊 엘리스정(엘카르니틴) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medicine is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and myocardial metabolic disorders (angina pectoris, acute myocardial infarction) caused by ischemic heart disease.

### 🕒 엘리스정(엘카르니틴) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults should take 6 to 9 tablets (2 to 3 g) daily, divided into 2 to 3 doses.

The dosage may be appropriately adjusted depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘리스정(엘카르니틴) - Precautions
* No specific information available.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine should not take this medicine.

Pregnant or possibly pregnant women, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
