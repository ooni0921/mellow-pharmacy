#EN
### 💊 엘칸정330밀리그램(엘-카르니틴) - Indications and Effects
* This drug is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and for myocardial metabolic disorders caused by ischemic heart disease such as angina pectoris and acute myocardial infarction.

### 🕒 엘칸정330밀리그램(엘-카르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2-3 g daily, divided into 2-3 doses per day. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘칸정330밀리그램(엘-카르니틴) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, or those with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this drug. Before taking this drug, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 엘칸정330밀리그램(엘-카르니틴) - 功效与作用
* 本品用于原发性、继发性肉碱缺乏症，以及心绞痛、急性心肌梗死等缺血性心脏病引起的心肌代谢障碍。

### 🕒 엘칸정330밀리그램(엘-카르니틴) - 用法用量
* 成人每日服用2~3克，分2~3次服用。可根据年龄、症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 엘칸정330밀리그램(엘-카르니틴) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本品过敏的患者，以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本品。孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女在服用本品前请咨询医生或药师。

#JP
