#EN

### 💊 지엘아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the inhibition of thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, and after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) or percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA), and for the reduction of cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, diabetes, etc.).

### 🕒 지엘아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once daily. As this is an enteric-coated tablet, it can be taken before meals with a sufficient amount of water.

### ⚠️ 지엘아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this medication or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or antirheumatic drugs; have peptic ulcers, aspirin-induced asthma or a history thereof; hemophilia; severe hepatic impairment; severe renal impairment; severe cardiac dysfunction; bleeding tendency; are co-administering methotrexate at a dose of 15 mg/week or more; or are in the third trimester of pregnancy. If you are in the first or second trimester of pregnancy, do not take this medication unless absolutely necessary.
* Before using this medication, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (such as renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, fluid depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major bleeding events), hepatic impairment, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are a pre-operative patient, an infant under 3 years of age, an elderly person, a patient with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a child under 14 years of age with chickenpox or influenza infection, or a patient with gout.
* As there is a risk of bleeding, inform your doctor if you are taking this medication when undergoing surgery (including dental surgery).
* Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medication.

### 🕒 지엘아스피린장용정100밀리그램 - 用法与用量

* 无信息
