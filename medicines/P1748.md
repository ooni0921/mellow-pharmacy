#EN
### 💊 셀지민정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the supplementation of Vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness (during or after recovery from illness), and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 셀지민정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years or older should take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 셀지민정 - Precautions
*   No specific information.
*   Do not administer this drug to patients with hypersensitivity to its ingredients or to infants under 12 months of age.

Before taking this drug, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, premature infants, infants, patients with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, patients with heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, patients with folic acid deficiency, or patients with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This drug may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, which can affect clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after taking the drug for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
