#EN

### 💊 쿨드림연질캡슐(디펜히드라민염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of temporary insomnia.

### 🕒 쿨드림연질캡슐(디펜히드라민염산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 capsule (50 mg) once daily before bedtime.
*   If excessive sedation occurs when taking 1 capsule (50 mg), individuals may take ½ capsule (25 mg) before bedtime.

### ⚠️ 쿨드림연질캡슐(디펜히드라민염산염) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, asthma attack, respiratory distress due to emphysema or chronic bronchitis, glaucoma, lower urinary tract obstructive disease (e.g., prostatic hypertrophy), stenosing peptic ulcer, pyloroduodenal obstruction, are under 15 years of age, have seizure disorders (e.g., epilepsy), pheochromocytoma, are breastfeeding, or experience chronic insomnia or have been diagnosed with insomnia.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have myasthenia gravis, are elderly, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication, or are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 2-3 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   成人每次服用1粒（50 mg），每日1次，睡前服用。
*   根据个人情况，若服用1粒（50 mg）时出现过度镇静作用，可在睡前服用½粒（25 mg）。

### ⚠️ 쿨드림연질캡슐(디펜히드라민염산염) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏者，哮喘发作、肺气肿、慢性支气管炎引起的呼吸困难、青光眼、下尿路梗阻性疾病（如前列腺肥大）、狭窄性消化性溃疡、幽门十二指肠梗阻患者、15岁以下儿童、惊厥性疾病（如癫痫）、嗜铬细胞瘤患者、哺乳期妇女、日常性失眠或已被诊断为失眠症的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，重症肌无力患者、老年人、过敏体质者、有药物过敏史者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   服用本药后可能会出现困倦，因此驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
*   服用2-3次后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
