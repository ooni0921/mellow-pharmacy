#EN
### 💊 나노파모정10밀리그램(파모티딘) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for excessive stomach acid, heartburn, and sour belching.

### 🕒 나노파모정10밀리그램(파모티딘) - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 16 years of age or older, take 1 tablet (10 mg) at a time, up to 2 tablets (20 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 나노파모정10밀리그램(파모티딘) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug should not take it.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you experience difficulty or pain when swallowing food, vomiting blood (hematemesis) or bloody stools, persistent heartburn symptoms for more than 3 months, dizziness or drowsiness accompanied by cold sweats, chest or shoulder pain accompanied by shortness of breath, frequent chest pain, unexpected weight loss, nausea, vomiting, gastrointestinal pain, hypersensitivity to other drugs, kidney disorder, heart disease, liver disorder, if you are elderly, pregnant or possibly pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, or a patient undergoing hemodialysis.
*   If there is no improvement in symptoms, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medication for more than 14 days.

*   对本药过敏的患者请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，如果出现以下情况，请咨询医生或药剂师：吞咽食物困难或吞咽时疼痛，吐血（呕血）或黑便，胃灼热症状持续3个月以上，伴有头晕或嗜睡及冷汗，伴有气短的胸痛或肩痛，频繁出现胸痛，不明原因的体重减轻，恶心、呕吐、胃肠道疼痛，对其他药物过敏，患有肾脏疾病、心脏病、肝脏疾病，老年人，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，接受血液透析的患者。
*   如果症状没有改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
