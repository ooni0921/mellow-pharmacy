# 템페라연질캡슐

##EN
### 💊 템페라연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used to replenish vitamins A, C, B1, and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during illness or recovery, and in old age. It also helps relieve dry eyes, night blindness, pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, prevent gum bleeding and nosebleeds, alleviate shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet due to peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause, and supplement zinc.

### 🕒 템페라연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 2 capsules once a day.

### ⚠️ 템페라연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A daily may cause congenital malformations. Women in the first 3 months of pregnancy or those who may be pregnant should not take this medicine. Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, or have a history of hypersensitivity to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant, an infant, or have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis (colitis appearing in a specific part). Also, consult if you have cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or other conditions requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions, a thrombotic predisposition, or hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests.

##CN
### 💊 템페라연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本产品用于补充体力疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中或病后体力下降、老年期的维生素A、C、B1、E；缓解眼睛干涩、夜盲症（夜间视力不佳）；缓解由阳光、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑）；预防牙龈出血、鼻出血；缓解更年期和末梢血液循环障碍引起的肩颈僵硬、手足发麻、手足冰冷等症状；以及补充锌。

### 🕒 템페라연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   12岁以上儿童及成人：每次2粒，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 템페라연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   每日服用超过5,000 IU的维生素A可能会导致先天性畸形。怀孕前3个月内或可能怀孕的女性请勿服用。对本产品过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、对大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者或有过敏史者请勿服用本产品。在服用本产品前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果您患有高草酸尿症（尿液中排出过量草酸盐）、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、胃肠道疾病（如消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等）、心血管系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风或肾结石、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症和胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常或需要慎重输注脂质乳剂的患者、有血栓形成倾向的患者、对黄色5号过敏的患者。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本产品在进行各种尿液检查时可能会干扰血糖的检测。
