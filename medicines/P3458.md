* 이 약은 육체피로, 임신ㆍ수유기, 병중ㆍ병후(병을 앓는 동안이나 회복 후)의 체력 저하 시, 노년기의 비타민 E, B1, B6의 보급과 말초혈행장애 및 갱년기의 어깨ㆍ목결림, 수족저림ㆍ수족냉증(손발이 차가움) 증상의 완화, 신경통, 근육통, 관절통(요통, 어깨결림 등), 각기, 눈의 피로 증상의 완화, 구각염(입꼬리염), 구순염(입술염), 구내염(입안염), 설염(혀염), 습진, 피부염 증상의 완화와 마그네슘결핍으로 인한 근육경련에 사용합니다.

# EN

### 💊 마그투연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical decline during or after illness (while sick or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness and coldness of hands and feet (cold hands and feet) associated with menopause, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, shoulder stiffness), beriberi, eye fatigue, and for the relief of angular stomatitis (inflammation of the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그투연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adolescents 12 years of age and older and adults should take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그투연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information provided
*   Do not take this medicine if you are allergic to this medicine, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal insufficiency. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment (kidney impairment), hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, dyslipidemia such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs to be carefully administered lipid emulsions, a patient with an expected special risk due to fat overload, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), or a patient with a history of or hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
