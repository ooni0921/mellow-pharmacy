#EN
### 💊 제텐비정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical decline during or after illness (while ill or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, as well as for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 제텐비정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 to 2 tablets once a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 제텐비정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months, have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who is exposed to a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), cardiovascular dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by changing urine color to yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
