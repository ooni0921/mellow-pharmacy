#EN
### 💊 경동아스피린장용정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to inhibit thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, to inhibit thrombus formation after coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG) or percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA), and to reduce cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, and diabetes).

### 🕒 경동아스피린장용정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet once daily. As this is an enteric-coated tablet, it can be taken before meals with plenty of water.

### ⚠️ 경동아스피린장용정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, anti-rheumatic drugs, peptic ulcer, aspirin asthma or a history thereof, hemophilia, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac insufficiency, bleeding tendency, or if you are concomitantly administering methotrexate at doses exceeding 15 mg/week, or if you are in the third trimester of pregnancy. Do not take this medicine during the first and second trimesters of pregnancy unless absolutely necessary. Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (such as renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, volume depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major bleeding events), hepatic impairment, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, if you are a pre-surgical patient, an infant under 3 years old, an elderly patient, a patient with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a patient under 14 years old with chickenpox or influenza infection, or a gout patient. Due to the risk of bleeding, inform your doctor that you are taking this medicine if you are undergoing surgery (including dental surgery). Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medicine.
