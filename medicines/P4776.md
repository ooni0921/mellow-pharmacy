#EN
### 💊 Efcool Syrup - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 Efcool Syrup - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older: 1 sachet (20 mL).
* For individuals aged 11 years or older to under 15 years: 2/3 sachet.
* For individuals aged 7 years or older to under 11 years: 1/2 sachet.
* For individuals aged 3 years or older to under 7 years: 1/3 sachet.
* For individuals aged 2 years or older to under 3 years: 1/4 sachet.
* Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ Efcool Syrup - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and plan to take this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, stop taking it immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body. Therefore, patients with phenylketonuria who need to restrict phenylalanine intake should not take this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity or previous experience with this medicine, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child aged 3 months or older and under 2 years, an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic predisposition or a history of drug allergies, have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty urinating, are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, or experience symptoms such as asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not take for an extended period.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.
* Since drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
