#EN
### 💊 아로나민실버액티브정 - Efficacy and Effects
* 아로나민실버액티브정 is used for the supplementation of Vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical weakness during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It also helps in the poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, relief of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in extremities, cold hands and feet, beriberi, eye fatigue, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 아로나민실버액티브정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years and older: Take 1 tablet once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 아로나민실버액티브정 - Precautions
* Daily intake of Vitamin A exceeding 5,000 IU may cause congenital malformations. Women in the first three months of pregnancy or who may be pregnant should not take this medicine.
* Do not take 아로나민실버액티브정 if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition with excessive calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, are an infant under 12 months of age, have Wilson's disease, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or hereditary problems such as glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, kidney stones or a history thereof, a patient receiving cardiac glycosides, a patient with gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, or gout.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately one month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

* 本药（아로나민실버액티브정）用于身体疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或恢复后）体力下降时、发育期、老年期的维生素A、D、E、B1、B2、B6、C的补充，骨骼、牙齿发育不良，预防佝偻病，缓解末梢血液循环障碍以及更年期时的肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木、手脚冰凉，脚气病，眼睛疲劳，以及锌的补充。

### 🕒 아로나민실버액티브정 - 用法及用量
* 12岁以上及成人：每次1片，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 아로나민실버액티브정 - 注意事项
* 每日服用维生素A超过5,000 IU可能引起先天性畸形。怀孕前3个月内或可能怀孕的女性请勿服用。
* 对本药（아로나민실버액티브정）过敏的患者、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过多的状态）患者、结节病（原因不明的全身炎症性疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石患者、严重肾功能不全患者、不满12个月的婴儿、威尔逊氏病、半乳糖不耐受症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，1岁以下的婴儿、经常晒太阳并正常饮食的儿童、高草酸尿症患者（尿液中草酸盐过量排泄的状态）、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心脏及循环系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症患者、肾结石患者或有肾结石病史者、正在服用洋地黄类药物的患者、胆囊相关疾病、肝脏疾病患者、痛风患者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下进行。

本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液呈黄色，从而影响临床检查结果。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
