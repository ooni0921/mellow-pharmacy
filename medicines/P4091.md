#EN
### 💊 크로씬탑연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of mild to moderate pain such as muscle pain, neuralgia, post-traumatic and post-operative pain, headache, toothache, and earache.

### 🕒 크로씬탑연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 capsules (125-250 mg) three times a day.

### ⚠️ 크로씬탑연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have peptic ulcers, gastrointestinal upset/disturbance caused by drugs, hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma, hives, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other NSAIDs (including COX-2 inhibitors), if you are being treated for pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG), if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, have severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe heart failure, or inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of peptic ulcers, are a child or elderly, have hypertension, fluid retention or heart failure, bronchial asthma, liver impairment or a history of it, or kidney impairment or a history of it.
