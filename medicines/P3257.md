### 💊 프레스탑연고 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids, and for disinfection.

### 🕒 프레스탑연고 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 15 years of age and older, apply directly to the affected area or inject rectally, up to 3 times a day. When injecting rectally, attach the applicator to the tube and insert it into the rectum.

### ⚠️ 프레스탑연고 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, urinary difficulty due to prostate enlargement, or if you are a child. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, or if you are hypersensitive to propylene glycol. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medication is for anal use only and should not be used ophthalmically. If symptoms do not improve after approximately one week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
