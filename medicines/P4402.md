# 투엑스비골드100정

###EN
### 💊 투엑스비골드100정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness (during illness or recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as beriberi, eye strain, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 투엑스비골드100정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 투엑스비골드100정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, an infant under 12 months of age, or a patient with severe renal insufficiency.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or kidney stones.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication may cause urine to turn yellow, affecting clinical test values, or interfere with blood glucose detection during urine tests.

###CN
### 💊 투엑스비골드100정 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于补充维生素 E, B1, B2, B6, C，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、发育期以及老年期。本药还用于缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳、神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩周炎等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状，以及补充锌。

### 🕒 투엑스비골드100정 - 用法及用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每次1片，每日1次。

*   对本药过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、重度肾功能不全患者请勿服用本药。

*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
*   本药可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查值，或在尿液检查时干扰血糖的检测。
