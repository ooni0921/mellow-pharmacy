#EN
### 💊 테라플루나이트타임건조시럽 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain) and various symptoms caused by hay fever (pollen allergy rhinitis) and other upper respiratory allergies (runny nose, sneezing, itchy eyes, nose and throat, watery eyes).

### 🕒 테라플루나이트타임건조시럽 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adolescents and adults aged 12 years and over: Take 1 sachet every 4 to 6 hours as needed. Do not exceed 4 sachets within 24 hours. Dissolve 1 sachet in approximately 250 mL of hot (not boiling) water and consume within 10 to 15 minutes.

### ⚠️ 테라플루나이트타임건조시럽 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. Rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN) have been reported, which can be fatal. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you: have hypersensitivity to this medicine; have experienced or developed asthma attacks due to other antipyretic analgesics (NSAIDs) or cold medicines; have severe cardiac insufficiency, hypertension, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe renal impairment, diabetes, epilepsy, glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase deficiency; are taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have discontinued them within the last 2 weeks; have pheochromocytoma; are an elderly patient with confusion; or have genetic problems such as fructose intolerance, glucose-galactose malabsorption, or sucrase-isomaltase deficiency.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you: have occlusive vascular disease (Raynaud's syndrome); are prone to allergies; have a history of liver impairment, renal impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, or bleeding tendencies; have emphysema, asthma, chronic bronchitis, or a cough with significant phlegm; have thyroid disease or prostatic hypertrophy; are on a sodium-restricted diet; have glaucoma or pre-eclampsia; are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding; are elderly; have a high fever; or have hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Drowsiness, dizziness, blurred vision, and impaired cognitive and psychomotor function may occur after taking this medicine, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
