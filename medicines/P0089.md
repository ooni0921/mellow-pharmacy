#EN
Product Name: 엠코발캡슐(메코발라민)

### 💊 엠코발캡슐(메코발라민) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for peripheral neuropathies.

### 🕒 엠코발캡슐(메코발라민) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 6 capsules (1,500 ㎍) daily, divided into 3 doses.

### ⚠️ 엠코발캡슐(메코발라민) - Precautions
*   No specific information.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, or if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medication, pregnant or breastfeeding individuals, patients on long-term H2 receptor blockers, or those who have consumed excessive alcohol for more than 2 weeks should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
