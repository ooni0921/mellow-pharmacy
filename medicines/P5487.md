#EN
### 💊 맥셀디정 - Efficacy and Effects
* Used for the supplementation of Vitamin D during pregnancy/lactation, growth periods, and old age; for poor development of bones and teeth; for the prevention of rickets; and for the supplementation of selenium.

### 🕒 맥셀디정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 맥셀디정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, a patient with heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a patient with a history of kidney stones.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
