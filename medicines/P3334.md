# EN
### 💊 엘리도스캡슐(에르도스테인) - Efficacy and Effect
* This medicine is used for mucolysis and expectoration in acute and chronic respiratory diseases.

### 🕒 엘리도스캡슐(에르도스테인) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 2-3 times a day. When used for acute respiratory diseases, do not take continuously for more than 10 days.

### ⚠️ 엘리도스캡슐(에르도스테인) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, liver cirrhosis, cystathionine beta-synthase deficiency, peptic ulcer, severe renal impairment (creatinine clearance (sentence incomplete)). Before taking this medicine, patients with mild to moderate hepatic impairment, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, and patients hypersensitive to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
