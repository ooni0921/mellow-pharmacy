#EN
### 💊 Betagenji Cream - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for secondarily infected allergic or inflammatory skin diseases (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis), and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 Betagenji Cream - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ Betagenji Cream - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, a history of or hypersensitivity to aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or bacitracin, skin infections (tuberculosis, syphilis, candidiasis, tinea, herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia, scabies, pediculosis pubis), eczematous external otitis with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (excluding Behçet's disease), second-degree deep burns or higher, frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, or rosacea.
* Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, infants, and young children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
* Do not use for purposes other than treatment after applying makeup or shaving.
* Do not use occlusive dressings without a doctor's supervision.

* 本药用于继发感染的变应性或炎症性皮肤病（湿疹、接触性皮炎、脂溢性皮炎、特应性皮炎、光敏性皮炎、慢性单纯性苔藓、擦烂、剥脱性皮炎、瘙痒、牛皮癣）以及一度烧伤。

### 🕒 베타겐지크림 - 用法用量
* 每日1~3次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ 베타겐지크림 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者、对链霉素、卡那霉素、庆大霉素、新霉素等氨基糖苷类抗生素或杆菌肽有过敏史或过敏反应的患者、患有皮肤感染（结核、梅毒、念珠菌病、癣、带状疱疹、单纯疱疹、水痘、牛痘、疥疮、阴虱病）、伴有鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳道炎、溃疡（白塞病除外）、二度深层及以上烧伤和冻伤、口周皮炎、寻常痤疮、酒渣鼻的患者，请勿使用本药。
* 使用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、婴幼儿应咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
* 请勿用于眼科用途。
* 化妆或刮脸后，请勿用于治疗以外的目的。
* 未经医生指导，请勿使用密封敷料法。

* 本剤は、二次感染したアレルギー性または炎症性の皮膚疾患（湿疹、接触皮膚炎、脂漏性皮膚炎、アトピー性皮膚炎、光線過敏症、慢性単純性苔癬、間擦疹、剥脱性皮膚炎、痒み、乾癬）およびI度熱傷に使用します。

### 🕒 베타겐지크림 - 用法・用量
* 1日1～3回、患部（疾患部位）に適量を塗布します。

* 本剤を使用する前に、妊婦または妊娠している可能性のある女性および授乳婦、乳幼児は医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
