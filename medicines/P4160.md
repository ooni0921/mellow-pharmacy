#EN
### 💊 마더스코연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 마더스코연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 15 years of age and older: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 마더스코연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. Liver damage may occur.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions occur after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). Liver damage may occur. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it before, have a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant (newborn) or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, a child under 2 years of age, experience symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding), have an allergic predisposition or a history of allergic reactions (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric/duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), urination difficulties, are elderly, physically frail, have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or ulcer/bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough with excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
