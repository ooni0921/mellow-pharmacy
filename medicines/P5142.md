#EN
### 💊 마그알파연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins E, B1, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength (during illness or recovery), and in old age.
*   It also helps relieve symptoms of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as stiff shoulders and neck, numbness and coldness in hands and feet, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.
*   Furthermore, it is indicated for beriberi, eye strain, and muscle cramps caused by magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그알파연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그알파연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, have severe renal failure, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to soybean oil, or have hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are currently receiving medical treatment; are pregnant or planning to become pregnant; are breastfeeding; are a premature infant or infant; have heart or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment (kidney disease), hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders; or if you require cautious administration of lipid emulsions, or are at special risk from lipid overload; have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Yellow No. 5; or have folic acid deficiency.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 마그알파연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于补充体力疲劳、妊娠·哺乳期、病中·病后（患病期间或恢复后）体力下降时以及老年期的维生素 E、B1、B6。
*   并用于缓解末梢血液循环障碍及更年期时出现的肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木·手脚冰冷（手脚寒凉）、神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。
*   以及治疗脚气病、眼睛疲劳、镁缺乏引起的肌肉痉挛。

### 🕒 마그알파연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   12 岁以上儿童及成人：每次 1 粒，每日 2 次。

### ⚠️ 마그알파연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   以下患者请勿服用本药：对本药过敏者；未满 12 个月的婴儿；严重肾功能不全患者；对大豆油有过敏症或过敏反应史者；对大豆或花生过敏者。
*   以下情况，请在服用本药前咨询医生或药师：正在接受医生治疗的患者；孕妇及可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；早产儿、婴幼儿；有心脏·循环系统功能障碍者；肾脏功能障碍者；低蛋白血症患者；高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要谨慎给予脂质乳剂的患者；预计因脂肪过量负荷而有特殊风险的患者；对黄色 5 号有过敏症或过敏反应史者；叶酸缺乏的患者。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   如服用约 1 个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
