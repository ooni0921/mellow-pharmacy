* 정보 없음

#EN
### 💊 라니탄큐정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, bloating/feeling of fullness in the stomach, indigestion (due to overeating), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 라니탄큐정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 라니탄큐정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a child under 15 years of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching), are elderly, have high blood pressure, renal impairment, hepatic impairment, cardiac impairment, edema (swelling), or have experienced hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4). Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking the medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 🕒 라니탄큐정 - 用法用量
* 成人每次2片，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 라니탄큐정 - 注意事项
* 信息缺失
* 15岁以下的儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，如您曾出现过敏症状（如皮疹、发红、瘙痒等）、是老年患者、患有高血压、肾功能障碍、肝功能障碍、心功能障碍、水肿（肿胀），或对黄色4号色素（酒石黄）过敏或有经验，请咨询医生或药剂师。请严格遵守规定的用法用量。如服用约2周后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用本药并咨询医生或药剂师。
