# EN

### 💊 큐텐훌연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, E in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is weakened during illness or convalescence, and in the elderly. It is also used for the relief of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as stiff shoulders and neck, numbness and coldness in hands and feet, as well as relief of symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 큐텐훌연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 capsule once daily.

### ⚠️ 큐텐훌연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this drug, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal dysfunction.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are:
    *   pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding.
    *   a premature infant or infant.
    *   a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine).
    *   a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis.
    *   a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction.
    *   a patient with renal dysfunction.
    *   a patient with hypoproteinemia.
    *   a patient with gallbladder-related diseases.
    *   a patient with liver disease.
    *   a patient with gout.
    *   a patient with kidney stones.
    *   a patient with hyperlipoproteinemia.
    *   a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions.
    *   a patient with a specific risk of fat overload.
    *   a patient with thrombogenic tendencies.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with blood sugar detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by causing urine to turn yellow.

# CN

### 💊 큐텐훌연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、老年期维生素B1, B2, B6, C, E的补充，以及缓解末梢血液循环障碍和更年期时的肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木、手脚冰凉（手脚发冷）等症状，缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）、脚气、眼睛疲劳等症状，缓解口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

### 🕒 큐텐훌연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   8岁以上及成人：每次1粒，每日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 큐텐훌연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   对本药或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、有严重症状的肾功能不全患者请勿服用本药。
*   在服用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心血管系统功能障碍患者、肾脏功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、胆囊相关疾病患者、肝病患者、痛风患者、肾结石患者、高脂蛋白血症患者、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需慎重使用脂质乳剂的患者、预计有脂肪超载特殊风险的患者、有血栓形成倾向的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   在各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变为黄色，从而影响临床检查结果。
