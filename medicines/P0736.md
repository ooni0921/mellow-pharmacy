# EN

### 💊 쓸기담50밀리그람연질캅셀 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the improvement of liver function in chronic liver disease and for the improvement of symptoms such as physical fatigue and general malaise caused by impaired liver function.

### 🕒 쓸기담50밀리그람연질캅셀 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule per dose, three times a day.

### ⚠️ 쓸기담50밀리그람연질캅셀 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to it, severe biliary obstruction, fulminant hepatitis, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a child, have large and small intestinal inflammation such as Crohn's disease, radiopaque calcified gallstones, a non-functioning gallbladder, acute cholecystitis, renal disease, or peptic ulcers (acute gastric and duodenal ulcers). Before taking this drug, patients with severe pancreatic disease or cholelithiasis should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
