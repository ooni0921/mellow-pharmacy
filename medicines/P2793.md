# EN

*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and sour belching.

### 🕒 알마돈정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 3 tablets once, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 알마돈정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients undergoing dialysis and children under 7 years of age should not take this medicine. Patients with kidney impairment should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
