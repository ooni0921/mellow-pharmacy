#EN
### 💊 뮤텐캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for difficulty in expectorating sputum in diseases such as acute and chronic bronchitis, bronchial asthma, laryngitis, sinusitis, and cystic fibrosis.

### 🕒 뮤텐캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - Dosage and Administration
* For acute diseases: Adults take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, 3 times a day. Children aged 6-14 years take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, 2 times a day, with a small amount of water before meals.
* For chronic diseases: Adults take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, 2 times a day. Children aged 6-14 years take 1/2 capsule (100 mg) once, 3 times a day, with a small amount of water before meals.
* For cystic fibrosis: Children aged 6 years and older take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, 3 times a day, with a small amount of water before meals.

### ⚠️ 뮤텐캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastric or duodenal ulcer, are an infant under 2 years of age, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this drug, pregnant or potentially pregnant women and nursing mothers should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 뮤텐캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗急慢性支气管炎、支气管哮喘、喉炎、鼻窦炎、囊性纤维化等疾病引起的痰液排出困难。

### 🕒 뮤텐캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - 用法与用量
* 急性疾病：成人每次1粒（200毫克），每日3次；6~14岁儿童每次1粒（200毫克），每日2次。饭前少量水送服。
* 慢性疾病：成人每次1粒（200毫克），每日2次；6~14岁儿童每次1/2粒（100毫克），每日3次。饭前少量水送服。
* 囊性纤维化：6岁及以上儿童每次1粒（200毫克），每日3次。饭前少量水送服。

### ⚠️ 뮤텐캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏者、胃十二指肠溃疡患者、2岁以下婴儿、以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女应咨询医生或药师。
