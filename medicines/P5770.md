#EN

### 💊 아이레쉬점안액(트레할로스수화물)(1회용) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for dry eyes and eye fatigue caused by discomfort, stinging pain, irritation, etc., resulting from wind, smoke, pollution, dust, dry heat, air conditioning, air travel, and prolonged computer use.

### 🕒 아이레쉬점안액(트레할로스수화물)(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 1 drop into each eye as needed, several times a day.
* When instilling, gently pull down the lower eyelid and instill one drop into the lower conjunctival sac while looking upwards.

### ⚠️ 아이레쉬점안액(트레할로스수화물)(1회용) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication or protein-based drugs.
* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you experience severe eye pain, have glaucoma, or have a history of allergic symptoms such as redness, itching, swelling, or rash in the eye due to eye drops.
* Use this medication only for ophthalmic instillation, i.e., for putting into the eyes.
* If symptoms worsen or persist for more than 3 days, immediately discontinue use of this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Wash your hands thoroughly before use.
* If the tip of the container touches the eyelid or eyelashes, the solution may become contaminated or cloudy with eye discharge or fungi. Do not use a cloudy solution.
* To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the medication if possible.
* Use only once after opening; discard any remaining solution and the container after instilling into the eye.
* Discard the first 1-2 drops when using for the first time.
* This medication can be used while wearing contact lenses.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* 滴药时，请轻轻拉下下眼睑，在向上看的同时，将一滴滴入下结膜囊中。

* 使用本药品前，眼睛疼痛严重者、青光眼患者、曾因眼药水引起眼睛充血、瘙痒、浮肿、皮疹等过敏症状者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

* 为防止污染，请尽量避免与他人共用。
* 开封后仅限一次性使用，滴入眼睛后请丢弃剩余药液和容器。
* 首次使用时，请丢弃前1-2滴，不要滴入眼睛。
* 佩戴隐形眼镜时也可使用本药品。
