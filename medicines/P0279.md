#EN
### 💊 복합엘씨500연질캡슐(수출명:MEGA-NAMIC)(수출용) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for indigestion caused by impaired liver function.

### 🕒 복합엘씨500연질캡슐(수출명:MEGA-NAMIC)(수출용) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 capsules daily, before meals or when experiencing indigestion, not exceeding 4 capsules per day.

### ⚠️ 복합엘씨500연질캡슐(수출명:MEGA-NAMIC)(수출용) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, or those with a history of such hypersensitivity, and patients with severe hepatocellular insufficiency or biliary obstruction should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, patients with cystinuria, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders, patients who must be careful when administering lipid emulsions, patients for whom a special risk is expected due to lipid overload, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 and Yellow No. 5 should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

#CN
### 💊 복합엘씨500연질캡슐(수출명:MEGA-NAMIC)(수출용) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于辅助治疗因肝功能下降引起的消化不良。

### 🕒 복합엘씨500연질캡슐(수출명:MEGA-NAMIC)(수출용) - 用法用量
* 成人每日服用1至2粒胶囊，饭前或消化不良时服用，每日不超过4粒。

### ⚠️ 복합엘씨500연질캡슐(수출명:MEGA-NAMIC)(수출용) - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 对大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者或有过敏史者，以及有严重肝细胞功能不全或胆道阻塞的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，患有胱氨酸尿症、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高血脂症以及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常的患者，或需谨慎给予脂肪乳剂的患者，或因脂肪过量而预计有特殊风险的患者，以及对黄色4号和黄色5号有过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
