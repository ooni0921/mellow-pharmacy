# 맥스케어베타정

## EN
### 💊 맥스케어베타정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy/lactation, physical weakness during/after illness, during growth periods, and in old age. It also aids in the alleviation of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), poor bone and teeth development, prevention of rickets, relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, and arthralgia (lumbago, stiff shoulders, etc.), alleviation of beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 맥스케어베타정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 맥스케어베타정 - Precautions
* Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A daily may cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medicine if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or are likely to be pregnant.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 3 months of age, have kidney stones, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or likely to be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant, an infant (young child), have gout, have or have a history of kidney stones, have folic acid deficiency, or have a thrombotic diathesis.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

## CN
### 💊 맥스케어베타정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠・哺乳期、病中・病后体力下降时、发育期、老年期维生素 A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C 的补充，以及缓解眼睛干涩、夜盲症（夜间视力不佳）、骨骼、牙齿发育不良、预防佝偻病，缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩周炎等）症状，缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳、口角炎（嘴角发炎）、唇炎（嘴唇发炎）、口腔炎（口中发炎）、舌炎（舌头炎）、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

### 🕒 맥스케어베타정 - 用法用量
* 8岁以上儿童及成人，一次1片，一日1次。

### ⚠️ 맥스케어베타정 - 注意事项
* 每日服用维生素A超过5,000 IU可能引起先天性畸形。妊娠3个月以内或有怀孕可能性的女性请勿服用。
* 对本药过敏患者、高钙血症（血液中钙质过剩的状态）患者、结节病（原因不明的全身炎症性疾病）患者、肾脏疾病患者、未满3个月的婴儿、肾结石患者、以及有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）患者、孕妇或有怀孕可能性的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、幼儿、痛风患者、肾结石患者或有肾结石史者、叶酸缺乏患者、有血栓倾向的患者，请务必咨询医生或药剂师。
* 服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
* 本药在进行各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变为黄色，从而影响临床检查值。
