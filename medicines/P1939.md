#EN
### 💊 Aimiru Contact Pure Eye Drops - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for eye fatigue, tear supplementation (dry eyes), discomfort when wearing hard or soft contact lenses, and blurred vision (e.g., when there is a lot of eye discharge).

### 🕒 Aimiru Contact Pure Eye Drops - Dosage and Administration
*   Instill 1 to 3 drops, 3 to 6 times a day.

### ⚠️ Aimiru Contact Pure Eye Drops - Precautions
*   No information
*   Before using this medication, patients with hypersensitivity to eye drops, severe eye pain, or glaucoma should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Use this medication only for ophthalmic instillation.
*   To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the medication if possible.

#CN
### 💊 Aimiru Contact Pure Eye Drops - 功效与效果
*   本药用于缓解眼睛疲劳、辅助泪液分泌（眼部干燥）、佩戴硬性或软性隐形眼镜时的不适感、以及视线模糊（如眼屎过多时）。

### 🕒 Aimiru Contact Pure Eye Drops - 用法与用量
*   每日3~6次，每次1~3滴。

### ⚠️ Aimiru Contact Pure Eye Drops - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   在使用本药前，对眼药水有过敏史的患者、眼部疼痛剧烈的患者以及青光眼患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
*   本药仅限滴眼用。
*   为防止污染，请尽量避免与他人共用。

### 💊 アイミールコンタクトピュア点眼液 - 効能・効果
