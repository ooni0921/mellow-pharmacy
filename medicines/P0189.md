#EN
### 💊 판크론정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, stomach upset, promoting digestion, and abdominal distension caused by indigestion.

### 🕒 판크론정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 판크론정 - Precautions
* None specified
* Do not administer this medicine to children aged 7 years or younger, or to patients with hypersensitivity to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, patients with an allergic constitution, dyslipidemia such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or patients who must carefully administer lipid emulsions, or patients for whom special risks are expected due to lipid overload, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 판크론정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于消化不良、食欲减退（食欲不振）、暴饮暴食、积食、促进消化、以及因消化不良引起的胃部胀满感。

### 🕒 판크론정 - 用法与用量
* 15岁以上及成人，一次2片，一日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 판크론정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 7岁以下儿童、对大豆油·大豆·花生过敏的患者请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，过敏体质、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症以及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者，或预计因脂肪过负荷而有特殊风险的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。
