#EN
### 💊 아미펜정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for headaches, toothaches, joint pain, neuralgia, muscle pain, sprain pain, and menstrual pain.

### 🕒 아미펜정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets once, 3 to 4 times a day, avoiding an empty stomach. The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 아미펜정 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen, as it may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to this medicine (e.g., rash, redness, itching, swelling of the larynx, eyelids, lips), or if you have experienced asthma attacks when taking other antipyretic analgesics (NSAIDs) or cold medicines, or if you have peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe heart failure, or have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), infants or children under 15 years old who are infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, physically weak individuals, individuals with high fever, liver dysfunction or history thereof, kidney dysfunction or history thereof, history of peptic ulcer, blood disorders or history thereof, bleeding tendency, heart dysfunction, bronchial asthma, elderly individuals, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, or breastfeeding women.

#CN
### 💊 아미펜정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗头痛、牙痛、关节痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、扭伤痛、月经痛。

### 🕒 아미펜정 - 用法用量
* 成人每次1~2片，每日3~4次，避免空腹服用。服药间隔应为4小时以上。

### ⚠️ 아미펜정 - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯以上的习惯饮酒者，在服用本药或其它解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能导致肝损伤。请勿超过对乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000毫克）。这可能导致肝损伤。请勿将本药与其它含有对乙酰氨基酚的产品一起服用。
* 以下患者请勿服用本药：对本药有过敏症（例如：皮疹、充血发红、瘙痒、喉头、眼睑、嘴唇肿胀）或曾有过敏史者；服用其他解热镇痛药（非甾体抗炎药）、感冒药时曾发生哮喘发作或有相关病史者；患有消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全者；服用过巴比妥类药物、三环抗抑郁药、酒精者。在服用本药前，以下人群请咨询医生或药师：有过敏体质、有过敏症状（例如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）病史者；感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿及15岁以下儿童；患有甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、体弱者、高热者；有肝功能障碍或病史者；有肾功能障碍或病史者；有消化性溃疡病史者；有血液异常或病史者；有出血倾向的患者；有心功能异常的患者；患有支气管哮喘；老年人；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女。
