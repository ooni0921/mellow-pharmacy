# EN
### 💊 엘로틴캡슐(에르도스테인) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for mucolysis and expectoration in acute and chronic respiratory diseases.

### 🕒 엘로틴캡슐(에르도스테인) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 2 to 3 times daily. When used for acute respiratory diseases, do not take continuously for more than 10 days.

### ⚠️ 엘로틴캡슐(에르도스테인) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not administer to patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, liver cirrhosis, cystathionine synthase deficiency, peptic ulcer, or severe renal impairment.
*   Before taking this medication, patients with mild to moderate hepatic impairment, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, and patients hypersensitive to Yellow No. 5 should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN

*   本药用于急慢性呼吸道疾病的黏液溶解和祛痰。

### ⚠️ 엘로틴캡슐(에르도스테인) - 注意事项
*   无信息。

# JP

### ⚠️ 엘로틴캡슐(에르도스테인) - 注意事項
