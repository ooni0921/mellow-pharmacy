### 💊 프리노즈에스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms such as runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills with shivering, fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 프리노즈에스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 15 years of age and older, take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day. For children 11 to under 15 years of age, take 1 tablet once, 2 times a day OR 2 tablets once, 1 time a day. For children 8 to under 11 years of age, take 1 tablet once, 1 time a day. Take 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 프리노즈에스정 - Precautions
*   Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine or its components, or if you have experienced asthma when taking this medicine, other antipyretic analgesics, or cold medicines. This medicine should not be administered to infants under 3 months of age, patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, or patients with severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if:
    *   You are an infant or a child under 15 years of age who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza.
    *   You experience symptoms of ischemic colitis such as sudden abdominal pain or rectal bleeding.
    *   You have an allergic constitution or a history of allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due to medication.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if:
    *   You have a history of liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma, or dysuria.
    *   You have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, gastric discomfort, stomach pain, or a history of ulcers or bleeding problems.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if:
    *   You are pregnant, may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.
    *   You are an infant under 2 years of age, elderly, frail, or have a high fever.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if:
    *   You smoke, or have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that has lasted or recurred for more than a week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, immediately stop taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Avoid driving a car or operating machinery while taking this medicine.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    *   胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果：
    *   孕妇、可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果：

儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

服用期间请避免驾驶汽车或操作机械。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
