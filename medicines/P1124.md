#EN
### 💊 알부라민연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or recovery), and in old age, and for the alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet (acrocyanosis) caused by peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause.

### 🕒 알부라민연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years or older should take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 알부라민연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant, possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gout or kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or patients who need to carefully administer lipid emulsions, patients for whom a special risk of lipid overload is expected, patients with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), patients with folic acid deficiency, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
