#EN
### 💊 차엠지연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or convalescence when physical strength is low, and in old age.
*   Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used for the alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness of hands and feet, and cold sensation in hands and feet caused by peripheral circulatory disorder and during menopause.
*   Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used for the alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and for beriberi and eye fatigue.
*   This medicine is used for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 차엠지연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years or older and adults should take 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 차엠지연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   None
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of hypersensitivity to soybean oil, a hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, pregnant or potentially pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, a young child, a patient with or who has experienced hypersensitivity to Red No. 40, a patient with impaired cardiac or circulatory function, a patient with renal impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions, or a patient with fat overload.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue taking this medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with a thrombotic predisposition or folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
