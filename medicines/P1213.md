#EN
### 💊 포리부틴정150mg(트리메부틴말레산염) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for esophageal reflux and hiatal hernia, gastritis and duodenitis, digestive disorders (abdominal pain, indigestion, nausea, vomiting) in gastric and duodenal ulcers, irritable bowel syndrome and spastic colon, and pediatric disorders (habitual vomiting, non-infectious intestinal transit disorders (constipation, diarrhea)).

### 🕒 포리부틴정150mg(트리메부틴말레산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 100-200 mg once, 3 times a day before meals. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 포리부틴정150mg(트리메부틴말레산염) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine. Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding mothers should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
