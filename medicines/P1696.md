#EN
### 💊 제놀탑에스카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for analgesic and anti-inflammatory effects in cases of osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), muscle pain, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 제놀탑에스카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   After peeling off the release liner from the patch surface, apply to the affected area twice a day.

### ⚠️ 제놀탑에스카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this drug if you have hypersensitivity or photosensitivity to this drug, or hypersensitivity to tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, or oxybenzone. Do not use if you have a history of asthma attacks induced by aspirin asthma or NSAIDs, if you are pregnant for 6 months or more, or if you are under 15 years of age. Patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant or lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this drug. While using this drug and for up to 2 weeks after use, avoid outdoor activities regardless of the weather. When going out for daily errands, cover the application area with clothing, sunglasses, hats, sunscreen, etc., to prevent exposure to ultraviolet rays. Do not apply to eyes and mucous membranes, damaged skin and mucous membranes, areas with eczema or rash, athlete's foot, or ringworm. Do not use occlusive dressing therapy.
