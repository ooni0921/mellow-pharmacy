#EN
### 💊 타이레놀정500밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain due to colds, headache, neuralgia, myalgia, dysmenorrhea, sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 타이레놀정500밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years or older and adults should take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, 3 to 4 times a day (every 4 to 6 hours) as needed.

Take this medicine for the shortest possible duration at the lowest effective dose, and do not exceed a maximum daily dose of 8 tablets (4 g).

### ⚠️ 타이레놀정500밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Individuals who regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported rarely. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, liver impairment, kidney impairment, decreased heart function, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by Nonsteroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs)) or a history of it, have taken barbiturate-containing drugs or tricyclic antidepressants, have consumed alcohol, or are a child under 12 years of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorders or a history of them, bleeding tendencies, decreased heart function, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant or breastfeeding, or have low glutathione reserves.

Do not consume alcohol during medication.

### 🕒 타이레놀정500밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - 用法用量

本药应以最短时间内的最小有效剂量服用，每日最大剂量不得超过8片（4克）。

服用本药前，有过敏史者、肝功能障碍者、肾功能障碍者、消化性溃疡患者、血液异常患者或曾有此病史者、有出血倾向者、心功能异常患者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女以及谷胱甘肽储量低下的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
