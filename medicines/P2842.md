### 💊 유영아스피린장용정100mg - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for inhibiting thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, inhibiting thrombus formation after coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG) or percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA), and reducing cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, diabetes).

### 🕒 유영아스피린장용정100mg - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 tablet once daily. This drug is an enteric-coated tablet and can be taken before meals with a sufficient amount of water.

### ⚠️ 유영아스피린장용정100mg - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this drug if you are hypersensitive to this drug or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or antirheumatic agents, or if you have peptic ulcers, aspirin asthma or a history thereof, hemophilia, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency, or are concurrently taking methotrexate at a dose of 15 mg/week or more. Pregnant women in the third trimester should not take this drug. In the first and second trimesters of pregnancy, do not take this drug unless absolutely necessary. Consult your doctor or pharmacist before using this drug if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (e.g., renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, fluid depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major bleeding events), hepatic impairment, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are a pre-surgical patient, an infant under 3 years old, an elderly person, a patient with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a patient under 14 years old with chickenpox or influenza infection, or a patient with gout. Inform your doctor if you are taking this drug when undergoing surgery (including dental surgery) due to the risk of bleeding. Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this drug.
