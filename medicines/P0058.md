#EN

* This medicine is used for difficulty in expectorating sputum (phlegm) in acute and chronic bronchitis, bronchial asthma, bronchiectasis, and pharyngitis, and for drainage in chronic sinusitis.

* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastric or duodenal ulcer, or are under 2 years of age. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver disorder, heart disorder, a history of gastric or duodenal ulcer, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, or elderly.
