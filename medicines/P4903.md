#EN
### 💊 제트유플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, gastric discomfort (upper abdominal discomfort), gastric distension (upper abdominal bloating), indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomachache, and acid belching.

### 🕒 제트유플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents 15 years of age or older should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, between meals.

### ⚠️ 제트유플러스정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a breastfeeding woman or a child under 7 years of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergies to medicine (such as rash, redness, itching, etc.), have a kidney disorder, have an allergic constitution, are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or are on a sodium-restricted diet.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

*   本药物用于胃酸过多、胃灼热、胃部（上腹部）不适、胃部（上腹部）饱胀感、消化不良、恶心、呕吐、胃痛、酸嗝。

### 🕒 제트유플러스정 - 用法与用量
*   15岁以上及成人，每次2片，每日3次，餐间服用。

### ⚠️ 제트유플러스정 - 注意事项
*   哺乳期妇女、7岁以下儿童请勿服用本药物。

*   请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。
*   服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。

### 🕒 제트유플러스정 - 用法・用量

*   定められた用法・用量を厳守してください。
*   約2週間服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
