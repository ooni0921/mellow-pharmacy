#EN
### 💊 엘탑정(엘카르니틴) - Indications & Effects
* This medication is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and for myocardial metabolic disorders caused by ischemic heart disease such as angina pectoris and acute myocardial infarction.

### 🕒 엘탑정(엘카르니틴) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults should take 2-3 g daily, divided into 2-3 doses.
The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘탑정(엘카르니틴) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug should not take it.
Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
