#EN
### 💊 다이투스정(암브록솔염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for acute and chronic bronchitis and asthmatic bronchitis caused by disorders of mucous secretion.

### 🕒 다이투스정(암브록솔염산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet (30 mg) three times a day, regardless of meals. For continued treatment, take 1 tablet (30 mg) twice a day.

### ⚠️ 다이투스정(암브록솔염산염) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, or if you have severe renal impairment, severe hepatic impairment, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, patients with renal impairment, peptic ulcer, pregnant women, breastfeeding women, or elderly patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 다이투스정(암브록솔염산염) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗由粘液分泌障碍引起的急慢性支气管炎、哮喘性支气管炎。

### 🕒 다이투스정(암브록솔염산염) - 用法用量
* 成人每次1片（30 mg），每日3次，饭前饭后均可服用。持续治疗时，每次1片（30 mg），每日2次。

### ⚠️ 다이투스정(암브록솔염산염) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏者，以及患有严重肾功能障碍、严重肝功能障碍、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，肾功能障碍、消化性溃疡患者、孕妇、哺乳期妇女及老年患者应咨询医生或药剂师。
