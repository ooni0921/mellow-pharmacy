###EN
### 💊 마그비맥스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effect
* This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is low during or after illness (during recovery), for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in old age, for the relief of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet (cold extremities) due to peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause, for neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder pain), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, relief of dermatitis symptoms, beriberi, eye strain, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그비맥스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 12 years and older: Take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그비맥스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are allergic to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months, or if you have severe renal failure.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatic inflammation (pancreatitis) or other lipid metabolism disorders, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously, have folic acid deficiency, are at special risk of fat overload, or have a thrombotic predisposition.
* It may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 1 month, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
