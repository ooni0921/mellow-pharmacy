# 토코탑연질캡슐

## EN

### 💊 토코탑연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is reduced during illness or recovery, and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder/neck stiffness during menopause, numbness/coldness in hands and feet, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 토코탑연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adolescents aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 토코탑연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   None
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal failure. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiovascular dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, dyslipidemia such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or require careful administration of lipid emulsions, or if you are at special risk due to fat overload, or have a thrombotic predisposition, or have a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

## CN

### 💊 토코탑연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于补充维生素E、B1、B6，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠期及哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时，以及老年期。同时，本药还用于缓解末梢血液循环障碍、更年期肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木/冰冷等症状，缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩周炎）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状，以及脚气病、眼睛疲劳和镁缺乏引起的肌肉痉挛。

### 🕒 토코탑연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   12岁及以上青少年及成人，每次1粒，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 토코탑연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无
*   对本药、大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，未满12个月的婴儿，以及症状严重的肾功能不全患者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师：孕妇或可能怀孕的女性及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、心血管系统功能障碍、肾脏（肾）功能障碍、低蛋白血症、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症以及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要谨慎给予脂质乳剂的患者，或因脂肪过负荷预计有特殊风险的患者，有血栓（血管堵塞）倾向的患者，对黄色5号过敏或有相关经验的患者。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。
