# EN
### 💊 비스타치플러스정(글루타티온(환원형)) - Indications & Effects
* This medication is used for drug intoxication and autointoxication.

### 🕒 비스타치플러스정(글루타티온(환원형)) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults take 0.5 to 1 tablet (50 to 100 mg) per dose, 1 to 3 times a day. Dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 비스타치플러스정(글루타티온(환원형)) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication.
