# EN
### 💊 엘도코프캡슐(에르도스테인) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medicine is used for mucolytic and expectorant purposes in acute and chronic respiratory diseases.

### 🕒 엘도코프캡슐(에르도스테인) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 2-3 times a day. For acute respiratory diseases, do not take continuously for more than 10 days.

### ⚠️ 엘도코프캡슐(에르도스테인) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, liver cirrhosis, cystathionine beta-synthase deficiency, peptic ulcer, severe renal impairment (creatinine clearance is [incomplete in original]). Before taking this drug, patients with mild to moderate hepatic impairment, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, and patients hypersensitive to Yellow No. 5 should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
