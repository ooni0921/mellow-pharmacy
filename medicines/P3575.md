# EN
### 💊 쏙코에스연질캡슐 - Indications & Efficacy
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, chills (shivering sensation), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 쏙코에스연질캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
* For adults and children aged 15 years and older, take 2 capsules once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 쏙코에스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported in patients taking acetaminophen. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis appear when taking pseudoephedrine-containing medicines, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, an infant under 2 years of age, an infant (general term for young child), have an allergic predisposition, have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty urinating, are elderly, are frail or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

* 每日饮酒超过三杯的规律饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能导致肝损伤。

服用含有伪麻黄碱的药物时，如果出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等严重皮肤不良反应，请停药并咨询医生或药师。
* 对本药有超敏反应史者、服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾出现哮喘者、未满3个月的婴儿、以及患有严重或未控制的高血压的患者，请勿服用本药。
