정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

복용 후 졸음이 나타날 수 있으므로 운전 및 기계조작 시 주의하십시오.

5~6회 복용하여도 증상의 개선이 없을 경우 복용을 즉각 중지하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 메가클린연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 메가클린연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years or older, take 2 capsules once, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 메가클린연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to it or have experienced it, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age or a child under 2 years of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a child under 2 years of age (only if absolutely necessary), elderly, frail, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision), dysuria, high fever, are a smoker, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache, or if you have a hypersensitivity to or have experienced Yellow No. 5.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, immediately stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
