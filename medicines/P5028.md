#EN

### 💊 판텍큐플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 판텍큐플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day. For individuals over 7 years old and under 15 years old: take 1 capsule per dose, 3 times a day. Take 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 판텍큐플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause liver damage.

    Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it before, have had asthma triggered by other fever reducers/pain relievers or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months old, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years old; an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic predisposition; have experienced drug-induced allergies (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty with urination; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; are breastfeeding; smoke; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema; have a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm; have a cough lasting for more than 1 week or recurring; have a chronic cough; or have a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    Consult a doctor or pharmacist if symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    Do not consume alcohol during the period of taking this medicine.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

    Do not use continuously for a long period.
