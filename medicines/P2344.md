#EN
### 💊 디펜플라스타(디클로페낙나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for analgesic and anti-inflammatory relief of swelling and pain after trauma (injury), and for: degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder joint, tendonitis and tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), and myalgia (e.g., muscle, myofascial, low back pain).

### 🕒 디펜플라스타(디클로페낙나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults 15 years of age and older, apply one patch once a day to the affected area.

### ⚠️ 디펜플라스타(디클로페낙나트륨) - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not use this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID)-induced asthma and hypersensitivity reactions, are under 15 years of age, are in the third trimester of pregnancy, or are a lactating woman.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma, are using it for inflammation accompanied by infection and chronic diseases (e.g., degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis)), are a pregnant woman (first and second trimester), are a woman having difficulty conceiving or undergoing infertility treatment, or are an elderly individual.

Do not use on the eyes and mucous membranes, damaged skin, or areas with eczema or rash.

Do not use with an occlusive dressing.
