#EN
### 💊 하디큐노즈연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills (shivering symptoms), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 하디큐노즈연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults aged 15 years and older: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

For children aged 8 years and older but under 15 years: Take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 하디큐노즈연질캡슐 - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of a hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take this product with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug or its ingredients, are an infant under 3 months of age, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or are in the 30th week of pregnancy or later.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years suspected or confirmed to have chickenpox or influenza, an infant under 2 years, a young child, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating (dysuria), are elderly, physically weak, or have a high fever, have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than one week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

Do not take this medication continuously for a long period.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
