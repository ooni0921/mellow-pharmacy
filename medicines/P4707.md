###EN
### 💊 브로멜장용정45밀리그램(브로멜라인) - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medication is used for the relief of inflammatory symptoms accompanied by edema, and for post-traumatic or post-surgical edema.

### 🕒 브로멜장용정45밀리그램(브로멜라인) - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years and older should take 1 to 2 tablets (45 to 90 mg) once, three times a day, 30 minutes before meals with water. Since many aspects of its mechanism of action in the body are not yet elucidated, and the dose-response relationship is not fully established, do not take it without a clear purpose.

### ⚠️ 브로멜장용정45밀리그램(브로멜라인) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medication if you have severe blood coagulation disorders (e.g., hemophilia), severe liver or severe kidney disease (e.g., hemodialysis patients), are taking anticoagulants or antiplatelet aggregation inhibitors, are pregnant or breastfeeding, are under 12 years of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, patients with a known hypersensitivity or history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

###CN
### 💊 브로멜장용정45밀리그램(브로멜라인) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解伴有水肿的炎症症状，以及创伤或术后水肿。

### 🕒 브로멜장용정45밀리그램(브로멜라인) - 用法用量
*   成人及12岁以上儿童，每次1~2片（45~90毫克），每日3次，饭前30分钟用水送服。由于本药在体内的作用机制尚未明确，且剂量与疗效关系也未完全阐明，因此请勿无目的地服用。

### ⚠️ 브로멜장용정45밀리그램(브로멜라인) - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   以下患者请勿服用本药：严重凝血功能障碍（如血友病等）、严重肝病或严重肾病（如血液透析患者等）、正在服用抗凝剂或血小板聚集抑制剂的患者、孕妇及哺乳期妇女、12岁以下儿童、以及有遗传性问题（如半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等）的患者。在服用本药之前，对日落黄FCF（黄色5号）有过敏症或曾有过敏史的患者应咨询医生或药师。
