#EN

* This medicine is used for eczema, cuts and burns, and erosions and ulcers due to other diseases.

### 🕒 안티렌크림(구아야줄렌) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 안티렌크림(구아야줄렌) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to any of its ingredients.
