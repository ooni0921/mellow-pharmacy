#EN
### 💊 해소코푸에스시럽 - Indications & Effects
* This medicine is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 해소코푸에스시럽 - Dosage & Administration
* Adults take 20 mL per dose, children aged 11 to under 15 years take 13 mL per dose, children aged 8 to under 11 years take 10 mL per dose, children aged 5 to under 8 years take 7 mL per dose, children aged 3 to under 5 years take 5 mL per dose, and children aged 2 to under 3 years take 4 mL per dose. Take 3 to 4 times a day, after meals and at bedtime.

### ⚠️ 해소코푸에스시럽 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, if you are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or a child under 15 years of age.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 2 years of age, have a constitution prone to conditions such as urticaria, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraine, or food allergies, have a history of allergic reactions to medicines, have liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high fever, are frail, have heart disease and high blood pressure, are elderly, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, have glaucoma, urinary retention, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine; exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 해소코푸에스시럽 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息。
* 对本药过敏者、正在服用或停用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪稳定剂、抗帕金森病药等）后2周内的人、3个月以下的婴儿、15岁以下的儿童，请勿服用本药。
