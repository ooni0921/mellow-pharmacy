#EN
### 💊 비타허브정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness leading to physical debility, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis. Furthermore, it helps alleviate beriberi, eye fatigue, peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness and coldness in hands and feet, and provides zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 비타허브정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 12 years and older and adults, take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 비타허브정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have severe renal insufficiency.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis (Crohn's disease), have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, folic acid deficiency, or a thrombotic predisposition.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

#CN
### 💊 비타허브정 - 功效与作用
*   本品用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠·哺乳期、病中·病后体力下降时、老年期维生素E、B1、B2、B6、C的补充，以及神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。还用于缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳、末梢血液循环障碍以及更年期时的肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木·手脚冰凉等症状，并补充锌。

### 🕒 비타허브정 - 用法用量
*   12岁以上儿童及成人，每次1片，每日1次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 비타허브정 - 注意事项
*   对本品过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、肾功能不全症状严重者请勿服用本品。
*   服用本品前，高草酸尿症患者、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠疾病患者、心脏·循环系统功能障碍患者、肾脏功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、痛风患者、肾结石患者、叶酸缺乏患者、有血栓形成倾向的患者请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   若服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
*   在进行各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。
