#EN

### 💊 이지엔6에이스연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for fever and pain associated with the common cold, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 이지엔6에이스연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children 12 years of age and older and adults take 2 capsules (650 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day (every 4 to 6 hours) as needed. Do not exceed 12 capsules (4,000 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 이지엔6에이스연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* If you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (induced by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history of it, have taken barbiturate drugs or tricyclic antidepressants, or have consumed alcohol. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Consult a doctor or pharmacist if pain or fever symptoms persist or worsen, or if new symptoms appear.
