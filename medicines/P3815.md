#EN
### 💊 진한고카타플라스마 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for sprains, bruises, muscle pain, fracture pain, stiff shoulders, frostbite, joint pain, low back pain, and muscle fatigue.

### 🕒 진한고카타플라스마 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply one patch to the affected area once or twice a day.

### ⚠️ 진한고카타플라스마 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not use this medicine around the eyes, on mucous membranes, on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer (urushiol) or similar allergens, or on open wounds.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic reactions to medicines or cosmetics (such as rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.), if you have eczema or severe weeping/maceration, if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or if you are breastfeeding.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine is for external use only.

If symptoms do not improve after several days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
