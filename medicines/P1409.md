# EN
### 💊 신신찜파스 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for shoulder stiffness, lower back pain, muscle pain, muscle fatigue, bruises, sprains, joint pain, and mild frostbite.

### 🕒 신신찜파스 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 신신찜파스 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use around the eyes, mucous membranes, eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, or on wound areas. Before using this medicine, patients with a history of allergy to medicines or cosmetics, those with an allergic constitution, or those with severe wetness or erosion should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully. Use this medicine for external use only. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. Clean the skin of the affected area before application. People with particularly sensitive skin should not apply it continuously to the same spot.
