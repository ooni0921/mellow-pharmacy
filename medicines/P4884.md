#EN
### 💊 트루액티비오정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins D, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during and after illness (while ill or during recovery), growth periods, and old age. It also helps in the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, beriberi, eye strain, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), and provides relief from angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation inside the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis, as well as for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 트루액티비오정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 트루액티비오정 - Precautions
*   No specific information provided.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypersensitivity to soybean oil or a history of it, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium levels in the blood are excessively high), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a patient undergoing medical treatment, a child who receives significant sun exposure and has a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant/young child, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones or a history of them, or a patient with fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

This drug may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning the urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
