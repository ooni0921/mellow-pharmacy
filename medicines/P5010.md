#EN
### 💊 미네서플라이정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for the supply of Vitamin C, Zinc, and Selenium during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and periods of physical debilitation during illness or recovery.

### 🕒 미네서플라이정 - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 12 years or older and adults, take 1 tablet twice daily, or 2 tablets once daily.

### ⚠️ 미네서플라이정 - Precautions
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, Wilson's disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant or infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related disease, liver disease, diabetes, gout, or kidney stones.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

This drug may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, immediately discontinue the drug and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 미네서플라이정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解体力疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期以及病中·病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时的维生素C补充、锌补充和硒补充。

### 🕒 미네서플라이정 - 用法用量
* 12岁及以上儿童和成人，每次1片，每日2次，或每次2片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 미네서플라이정 - 注意事项
* 对本药过敏者、威尔逊病患者、未满12个月的婴儿、或有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，高草酸尿症患者（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心血管功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症患者、胆囊相关疾病、肝病、糖尿病患者、痛风或肾结石患者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守用法用量。

本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
