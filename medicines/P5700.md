#EN
### 💊 위앤프레쉬정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion (food stagnation), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite, overeating, and promotion of digestion.

### 🕒 위앤프레쉬정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times daily, between meals (i.e., between breakfast and lunch, lunch and dinner, etc.).

### ⚠️ 위앤프레쉬정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are: children under 15 years of age, pregnant or possibly pregnant women, breastfeeding women, patients undergoing dialysis, or patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have: a history of allergic symptoms such as rash, redness, or itching; renal impairment, hepatic impairment, cardiac impairment; hypophosphatemia, edema; high blood pressure; elderly individuals; or a history of kidney disease.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, discontinue this medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

### 🕒 위앤프레쉬정 - 用法用量

*   无信息
*   15岁以下儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的妇女、哺乳期妇女、接受透析治疗的患者、以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
