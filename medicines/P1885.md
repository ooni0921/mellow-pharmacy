#EN
### 💊 덴티하이캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 덴티하이캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 덴티하이캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medication if you are under 15 years of age, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching, etc.) to medicines and cosmetics, allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.) to eggs, if you have an allergic constitution, or if you have experienced hypersensitivity to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN

*   本药用于牙周治疗后牙龈炎、轻中度牙周炎的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 덴티하이캡슐 - 用法用量

### ⚠️ 덴티하이캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   15岁以下儿童、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者请勿服用本药。

*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
