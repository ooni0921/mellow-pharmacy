##EN
### 💊 아이티어플러스점안액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for discomfort when wearing soft or hard contact lenses, tear supplementation (dry eyes), eye fatigue, and blurred vision (when there is excessive eye discharge).

### 🕒 아이티어플러스점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2 to 3 drops at a time, 5 to 6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 아이티어플러스점안액 - Precautions
* No information
* Before using this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, allergic constitution, severe eye pain, or glaucoma should consult a doctor or pharmacist. When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Use this medicine only for ophthalmic instillation. Do not share with others. Do not use products past their expiration date, and even if within the expiration date, use promptly after opening.

##CN
### 💊 아이티어플러스점안액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于佩戴软性或硬性隐形眼镜时的不适感、泪液补充（眼睛干燥）、眼疲劳、视力模糊（分泌物过多时）。

### 🕒 아이티어플러스점안액 - 用法用量
* 一次2~3滴，一日5~6次滴眼。

### ⚠️ 아이티어플러스점안액 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 在使用本药前，对本药过敏的患者、过敏体质的患者、眼部疼痛剧烈的患者、青光眼患者，请咨询医生或药师。给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药仅供滴眼用。请勿与他人共用。请勿使用超过有效期的产品，即使在有效期内，开封后也请尽快使用。
