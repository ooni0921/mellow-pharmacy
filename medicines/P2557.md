#EN
### 💊 반테린코와크리미겔 - Indications & Efficacy
* This medication is used for pain relief and anti-inflammation in cases of osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis, tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, humerus epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), muscle pain, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 반테린코와크리미겔 - Dosage & Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 4 times daily.

### ⚠️ 반테린코와크리미겔 - Precautions
* Do not use this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this medication or other indomethacin preparations, have a history of aspirin asthma, or have experienced bronchospasm, rhinitis, and angioedema due to aspirin or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs).
* Do not use this medication for children under 11 years of age, or pregnant women in their third trimester.
* Do not apply this medication to the area around the eyes, mucous membranes, or on skin with lesions (e.g., hemorrhagic skin diseases, eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, infected lesions, burns, wounds, etc.), tinea pedis, tinea corporis/cruris (ringworm), or purulent areas.

* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma, psoriasis, are pregnant (first or second trimester), are breastfeeding, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication, or have asthma.

* If there is skin damage, using this medication may cause temporary irritation and a tingling sensation, so use with caution.

* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

* Be careful not to let it get into your eyes. If it does get into your eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water.

* Wash your hands after using this medication.

* For external use only.

* Do not use more than 50 g per week.

* Do not use occlusive dressings.

* If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 반테린코와크리미겔 - 功效与作用
* 本药物用于缓解和消炎以下症状：退行性关节炎（骨关节炎）、肩周炎、肌腱炎、腱鞘炎、腱周炎、肱骨外上髁炎（如网球肘）、肌肉痛、外伤后肿胀和疼痛。

### 🕒 반테린코와크리미겔 - 用法用量
* 每日1至4次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ 반테린코와크리미겔 - 注意事项
* 请勿在以下情况下使用本药物：对本药物或其他吲哚美辛制剂过敏的患者、有阿司匹林哮喘史或曾因阿司匹林或非甾体抗炎药引起支气管痉挛、鼻炎及血管性水肿的患者。
* 请勿对11岁以下儿童、处于妊娠第三期的孕妇使用本药物。
* 请勿将本药物涂抹于眼周、粘膜、有病变的皮肤（如出血性皮肤病、湿疹、漆树等引起的皮炎、感染性病变、烧伤、伤口等部位）、足癣、体癣/股癣等或化脓部位。

* 在使用本药物前，如有支气管哮喘、银屑病、孕妇（妊娠第一和第二期）、哺乳期妇女、过敏体质者、曾有药物过敏症状者、哮喘患者，请咨询医生或药师。

* 如果皮肤有损伤，使用本药物可能会引起暂时性刺激和刺痛感，使用时请注意。

* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

* 给儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。

* 注意不要进入眼睛。万一进入眼睛，请立即用大量清水冲洗。

* 使用本药物后请洗手。

* 仅限外用。

* 每周使用量请勿超过50克。

* 请勿使用密封包扎法。

* 如果使用5至6天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
