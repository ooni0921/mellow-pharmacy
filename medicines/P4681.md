#EN
### 💊 히알핑점안액(히알루론산나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for ocular moistening in symptoms such as dryness, foreign body sensation, fatigue caused by dust, wind, dry air, prolonged computer use, and discomfort due to contact lens wear.

### 🕒 히알핑점안액(히알루론산나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults may instill 1 drop at a time, up to 6 times a day, as needed.

### ⚠️ 히알핑점안액(히알루론산나트륨) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug or protein-based drugs. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you experience severe eye pain, have a history of allergic reactions to eye drops (such as eye redness, itching, swelling, rash, hyperemia), or have glaucoma. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. Use this medication for ophthalmic instillation only. Do not use simultaneously with other eye drops. If concurrent use is necessary, apply other eye drops first, and then use this medication approximately 30 minutes later. Do not use while wearing soft contact lenses. Lenses may be worn at least 15 minutes after administering this medication. Do not use if the solution appears cloudy. To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the medication if possible.

* 暂无信息
