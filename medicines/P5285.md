#EN
### 💊 탑콘도르600정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and during or after illness when physical strength is decreased. It also helps relieve neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as low back pain and stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 탑콘도르600정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 8 years of age and older, take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 탑콘도르600정 - Precautions
*   **Do not take this medicine if you:**
    *   Have hypersensitivity to this medicine.
    *   Suffer from hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.
    *   Are an infant under 12 months of age.
    *   Have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   **Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are:**
    *   An infant under 1 year of age.
    *   A child who receives abundant sunlight and eats a normal diet.
    *   Pregnant or possibly pregnant.
    *   Breastfeeding.
    *   Premature infant or infant.
    *   Suffering from cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, or hypoproteinemia.
    *   Currently taking cardiac glycosides.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow, which can affect clinical test values.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 탑콘도르600정 - 功效与作用
*   本品用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时的维生素B1、B2、B6补充，以及神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）的缓解、脚气病和眼睛疲劳。

### 🕒 탑콘도르600정 - 用法用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每日1次，每次1片，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 탑콘도르600정 - 注意事项
*   **以下患者请勿服用本品：**
    *   对本品过敏的患者。
    *   患有高钙血症、结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石或严重肾功能不全的患者。
    *   未满12个月的婴儿。
    *   患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者。
*   **服用本品前，请咨询医生或药师：**
    *   未满1岁的婴儿。
    *   经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童。
    *   孕妇或可能怀孕的女性。
    *   哺乳期妇女。
    *   早产儿、婴幼儿。
    *   患有心脏或循环系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症的患者。
    *   正在服用强心苷的患者。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
*   本品可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查值。
*   如服用约1个月后症状无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
