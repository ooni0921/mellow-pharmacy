#EN

### 💊 복합우루사연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to improve physical fatigue, for general invigoration, and to supplement vitamin B1 during and after illness.

### 🕒 복합우루사연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents aged 15 years and older should take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 복합우루사연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No additional general warnings provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, soybean oil, soy, peanuts, or a history of allergic reactions.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis; a patient requiring cautious administration of lipid emulsions; an individual with tartrazine (Yellow No. 5) hypersensitivity or a history of allergic reactions; or a patient at special risk due to fat overload.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
