#EN
### 💊 모드콜코프시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 모드콜코프시럽 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years or older: 20 ml per dose.
*   For individuals aged 11 years or older and under 15 years: 13.3 ml per dose.
*   For individuals aged 8 years or older and under 11 years: 10 ml per dose.
*   For individuals aged 5 years or older and under 8 years: 6.7 ml per dose.
*   For individuals aged 3 years or older and under 5 years: 5 ml per dose.
*   For individuals aged 2 years or older and under 3 years: 4 ml per dose.
*   Take 5-6 times a day, with an interval of approximately 4 hours between doses.

### ⚠️ 모드콜코프시럽 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you are currently taking or have stopped taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) within the last 2 weeks.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution; have experienced drug-induced allergies (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); are an infant or young child aged 3 months or older and under 2 years; have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty with urination; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; are breastfeeding; or if you exhibit symptoms such as smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

*   无信息
*   如果您对此药物有过敏史或过敏经验，3个月以下的婴儿，或者正在服用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）或在停用MAO抑制剂后2周内，请勿服用本药。

*   若服用5-6次后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   服药期间请勿饮酒。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
*   服用后可能会出现嗜睡，因此驾驶车辆或操作机械时请注意。
