# EN
### 💊 리버쿨환 - Efficacy & Effects
* This medicine is used for excessive drinking, nausea (hangover, nausea caused by unpleasant odor), vomiting, loss of appetite, stomach discomfort, gastric weakness, promotion of digestion, overeating, chest heaviness, gastric distension/bloating due to indigestion, and indigestion.

### 🕒 리버쿨환 - Dosage & Administration
* Adults 15 years and older: Take 1 packet (1.2g) per dose, 3 times a day, before or between meals (meaning between mealtimes).

### ⚠️ 리버쿨환 - Precautions
* No information
* Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or those who think they might be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 리버쿨환 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于饮酒过量、恶心（宿醉、因恶臭引起的恶心）、呕吐、食欲不振、胃部不适、胃虚弱、促进消化、暴饮暴食、胸闷、因消化不良引起的胃部胀满感、以及消化不良。

### 🕒 리버쿨환 - 用法与用量
* 15岁以上的成人：每日3次，每次1包(1.2g)，饭前或餐间（两餐之间）服用。

### ⚠️ 리버쿨환 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 服用本药前，孕妇或认为自己可能怀孕的人应咨询医生或药剂师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。

# JP
### 💊 리버쿨환 - 効能・効果
