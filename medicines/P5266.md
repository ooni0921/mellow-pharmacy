### 💊 이부콜벤연질캡슐400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain due to colds, back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), and post-operative pain.

This medicine is used for headaches, migraines, toothaches, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions/bruises), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 이부콜벤연질캡슐400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 1/2 to 1.5 tablets (200-600 mg) 3 to 4 times a day, up to a maximum of 3200 mg per day.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, 30-40 mg per kg of body weight per day is divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain and colds, adults orally take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) 3 to 4 times a day.

The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

For migraines, adults take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) per dose, and should not exceed 400 mg over a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 이부콜벤연질캡슐400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, have current or a history of gastrointestinal ulcers or bleeding, severe blood disorders, liver impairment, kidney impairment, cardiac insufficiency, hypertension, bronchial asthma (current or history), hypersensitivity (such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions) to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors) (current or history), pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, are pregnant in the last three months of pregnancy, or are receiving high-dose methotrexate for chemotherapy.

Do not exceed the recommended dosage.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, a bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis, liver impairment, kidney impairment, cardiac insufficiency or heart disease, hypertension, current or a history of hypersensitivity, Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE), Mixed Connective Tissue Disease (MCTD), ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly or pediatric, pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking low-dose aspirin.
