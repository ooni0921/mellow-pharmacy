#EN
### 💊 다펜나이트연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for acute nocturnal pain accompanied by temporary insomnia due to pain.

### 🕒 다펜나이트연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults, when experiencing difficulty sleeping due to pain, take 1 to 2 capsules once daily at bedtime.

Do not exceed 1,200 mg of ibuprofen and 75 mg of diphenhydramine within 24 hours, including this medication.

Maintain a dosing interval of at least 4 hours.

If the cause of symptoms is not identified, do not take continuously for more than 5 days.

### ⚠️ 다펜나이트연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may occur.
*   Do not take this medication if you are: hypersensitive to this medication; pregnant or potentially pregnant; breastfeeding; under 15 years of age; have or have a history of gastrointestinal ulcers or bleeding; have or have a history of bronchial asthma; have difficulty breathing due to asthma attacks, emphysema, or chronic bronchitis; have or have a history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); have severe blood disorders, liver dysfunction, cardiac insufficiency, or hypertension; have lower urinary tract obstructive diseases such as glaucoma or prostatic hypertrophy; have convulsive disorders such as epilepsy; have pheochromocytoma; experience pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; have insomnia without pain; or have pain without insomnia.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity; have chickenpox; have or have a history of blood disorders, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction, or kidney dysfunction; have a tendency to bleed; have cardiac insufficiency or heart disease, or hypertension; have systemic lupus erythematosus or mixed connective tissue disease; have ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, myasthenia gravis, diabetes, or thyroid disease; are an elderly person aged 65 or over; or are a child.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication for more than 5 days, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not exceed the recommended dosage of this medication.

Breastfeeding women should discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue this medication.

This medication may affect reaction time. Exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not take continuously for a prolonged period.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration instructions.
