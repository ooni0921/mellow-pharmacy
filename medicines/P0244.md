* 이 약에 과민증 환자, 위장관궤양, 위장관출혈, 심한 혈액이상, 심한 간장애, 심한 신장장애, 심한 심장기능부전, 심한 고혈압, 기관지천식, 아스피린 또는 다른 소염진통제에 의한 천식, 두드러기, 알레르기 반응 경험 환자, 심장동맥 우회로술 전후 통증발생 환자, 임신 말기 3개월 기간의 임부, 수유부, 항암요법으로 고용량 메토트렉세이트를 투여 중인 환자, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

이 약을 복용하기 전에 혈액이상, 출혈경향 환자, 간경화, 간장애, 신장장애, 심장기능부전, 심질환, 고혈압, 전신홍반루푸스, 혼합결합조직질환, 궤양성대장염, 크론병, 노인, 만 2세 미만의 소아, 임부, 수두 환자는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

# EN
### 💊 이부날시럽(이부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain associated with the common cold, low back pain, dysmenorrhea (menstrual cramps), post-operative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, strains), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 이부날시럽(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 11-14 years, take 200-250 mg (10-13 mL) per dose; for 7-10 years, take 150-200 mg (8-10 mL) per dose; for 3-6 years, take 100-150 mg (5-8 mL) per dose; and for 1-2 years, take 50-100 mg (3-5 mL) per dose, 3-4 times a day.

The daily dose for children weighing less than 30 kg should not exceed 500 mg (25 mL), and it is advisable to avoid administration on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 이부날시럽(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker (three or more drinks daily) and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid taking high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, or a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs. Also, it should not be taken by patients experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, pregnant women in the last trimester (last 3 months), breastfeeding women, patients receiving high-dose methotrexate for cancer therapy, or patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, a bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, cardiac dysfunction, heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly, a child under 2 years of age, pregnant, or have chickenpox.

When taking this medicine for a common cold, do not take it for more than 5 days in principle. Do not take it for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist, and do not take it for fever for more than 3 days.

感冒时服用，原则上不超过5天；未经医生或药师指示，疼痛用药不超过10天（成人）或5天（儿童），发热用药不超过3天。
