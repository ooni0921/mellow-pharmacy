### 💊 DIPHUTAB Sustained Release Tablet (Ibuprofen) - Efficacy and Indications
* This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to common cold, lower back pain, dysmenorrhea, post-surgical pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, myalgia (muscle pain), neuralgia (nerve pain), acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injury (sprains, contusions), non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 DIPHUTAB Sustained Release Tablet (Ibuprofen) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 1 tablet once, twice daily, and can take up to a maximum of 3200 mg per day.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily is administered in two divided doses. For mild to moderate pain or common cold, adults take 1 tablet once, twice daily.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

### ⚠️ DIPHUTAB Sustained Release Tablet (Ibuprofen) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction (heart attack), and stroke, and may cause serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of or current gastrointestinal ulcer or bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, a history of or current bronchial asthma, a history of or current asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other NSAIDs (including COX-2 inhibitors), patients experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, pregnant women in the last trimester (last 3 months) of pregnancy, and patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders or history thereof, bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis or liver impairment or history thereof, kidney impairment or history thereof, cardiac dysfunction or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly or pediatric, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Do not take this medicine for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or more than 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without the direction of a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 디퓨탭서방정(이부프로펜) - 用法与用量
* 对于风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、软组织损伤、非关节性风湿病、急性痛风、银屑病关节炎，成人每次1片，每日2次，每日最大剂量不超过3200毫克。

对于幼年类风湿性关节炎，每日按体重每公斤30-40毫克，分2次服用。对于轻度及中度疼痛或感冒，成人每次1片，每日2次。

请勿超过推荐剂量。

### ⚠️ 디퓨탭서방정(이부프로펜) - 注意事项
