### 💊 콜드브레이크캡슐 - Indications and Usage
* This medicine is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 콜드브레이크캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 2 capsules once, children aged 8-14 years take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

Do not administer to children under 2 years of age. However, if absolutely necessary, consult a doctor.

### ⚠️ 콜드브레이크캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity or have experienced it before, have had asthma triggered by other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity or have experienced it before to Yellow No. 5, children aged 3 months to under 2 years, infants and children under 15 years suspected or infected with chickenpox or influenza, allergic constitution, history of drug allergy, liver, kidney, heart, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric or duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty with urination, a weak person or one with high fever, a person with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or ulcers and bleeding problems, pregnant or possibly pregnant women, lactating women, smokers, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash or persistent headache.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

Do not take for prolonged periods.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not consume alcohol during administration.

Drowsiness may occur after administration, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
