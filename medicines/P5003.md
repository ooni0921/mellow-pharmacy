#EN
### 💊 아로나민케어싸이정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical decline during or after illness, growth period, and old age.
*   It is also used for the prevention of bone and teeth developmental disorders and rickets.
*   It provides relief of dry eyes, night blindness.
*   It alleviates shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness in extremities, and cold hands/feet due to peripheral circulatory disorders and during menopause.
*   It offers relief of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness).
*   It aids in the relief of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis.
*   It is used for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 아로나민케어싸이정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years and older, and adults: take 2 tablets once daily.

### ⚠️ 아로나민케어싸이정 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A daily can cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medicine if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to any ingredient, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a patient with or a history of kidney stones.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   本品用于补充维生素 A、D、E、B1、B2、B6、C，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠/哺乳期、病中/病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降、发育期、老年期。

*   缓解眼睛干涩、夜盲症。
*   缓解由末梢血液循环障碍和更年期引起的肩颈僵硬、手足麻木、手足冰凉。
*   缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳、神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩周炎）。
*   缓解口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎。
*   补充锌。

### 🕒 아로나민케어싸이정 - 用法及用量
*   12岁以上儿童及成人：每次2片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 아로나민케어싸이정 - 注意事项
*   每日服用超过 5,000 IU 的维生素 A 可能导致先天性畸形。怀孕前3个月内或有怀孕可能性的女性请勿服用。

*   服用本品前，如是未满1岁的婴儿、经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、高草酸尿症患者（尿液中草酸盐过量排出的状态）、孕妇及有怀孕可能性的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病者、心脏/循环系统功能障碍者、肾功能障碍者、低蛋白血症患者、痛风患者、肾结石患者或有肾结石病史者，请咨询医生或药师。

*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。
