# EN
### 💊 영진아스피린장용정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the inhibition of thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, for the inhibition of thrombus formation after Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) or Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA), and for reducing cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, diabetes).

### 🕒 영진아스피린장용정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 tablet once daily.
*   This medication is an enteric-coated tablet, so it can be taken before meals with plenty of water.

### ⚠️ 영진아스피린장용정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or other salicylates, peptic ulcer, aspirin asthma or history of it, hemophilia, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency, or if you are concurrently taking methotrexate at a dose of 15 mg/week or more. Pregnant women in their third trimester should not take this medication. For pregnant women in their first and second trimesters, do not take this medication unless absolutely necessary.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (such as renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, volume depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major bleeding events), hepatic impairment, cardiac dysfunction, blood disorders or history of them, bronchial asthma, are a pre-surgical patient, an infant under 3 years old, have hypersensitivity to analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or anti-rheumatic drugs, are elderly, have glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a child under 14 years old with chickenpox or influenza infection, or have gout.

Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medication.

# CN
### 💊 영진아스피린장용정 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于抑制心肌梗死、脑梗死、不稳定型心绞痛中的血栓形成，以及冠状动脉旁路移植术(CABG)或经皮腔内冠状动脉成形术(PTCA)后抑制血栓形成，并用于降低高危患者（具有缺血性心脏病家族史、高血压、高胆固醇血症、肥胖、糖尿病等复合危险因素的患者）的心血管风险。

### 🕒 영진아스피린장용정 - 用法及用量
*   成人一次1片，一日1次。
*   本药为肠溶片，可与足量水一同在饭前服用。

### ⚠️ 영진아스피린장용정 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药或其他水杨酸制剂过敏、消化性溃疡、阿司匹林哮喘或有其病史者、血友病、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、有出血倾向、每周同时服用甲氨蝶呤15毫克(15 mg/周)以上剂量的患者，以及妊娠晚期（妊娠第3期）的孕妇请勿服用。妊娠早期（妊娠第1期）和中期（妊娠第2期）的孕妇，除非绝对必要，否则请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如果您患有肾功能障碍、心血管循环功能异常（如肾血管疾病、充血性心力衰竭、体液减少、大手术、败血症或主要出血事件等）、肝功能障碍、心功能异常、血液异常或有其病史、支气管哮喘、术前患者、3岁以下婴幼儿、对镇痛药、消炎药、抗风湿药过敏者、老年人、葡萄糖-6-磷酸脱氢酶(G6PD)缺乏症患者、瑞氏综合征、14岁以下患水痘或流感感染的儿童、痛风患者，请咨询医生或药师。

请注意不要损坏本药的肠溶包衣。
