* 이 약에 과민증 환자, 위장관궤양, 위장관출혈, 심한 혈액이상, 심한 간장애, 심한 신장장애, 심한 심장기능부전, 심한 고혈압, 기관지천식, 아스피린 또는 다른 소염진통제에 의한 천식, 두드러기, 알레르기 반응 경험 환자, 심장동맥 우회로술 전후 통증발생 환자, 임신 말기 3개월 기간의 임부, 수유부, 항암요법으로 고용량 메토트렉세이트를 투여 중인 환자, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

권장용량을 초과하여 복용하지 마십시오.

이 약을 복용하기 전에 혈액이상, 출혈경향 환자, 간경화, 간장애, 신장장애, 심장기능부전, 심질환, 고혈압, 전신홍반루푸스, 혼합결합조직질환, 궤양성대장염, 크론병, 노인, 만 2세 미만의 소아, 임부, 수두 환자는 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 이부서스펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to common cold, back pain, dysmenorrhea, post-operative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, myalgia, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injury (sprain, contusion), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 이부서스펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 11-14, take 200-250 mg (10-13 mL) once, 3-4 times a day.
* For children aged 7-10, take 150-200 mg (8-10 mL) once, 3-4 times a day.
* For children aged 3-6, take 100-150 mg (5-8 mL) once, 3-4 times a day.
* For children aged 1-2, take 50-100 mg (3-5 mL) once, 3-4 times a day.

For children weighing less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg (25 mL), and it is advisable to avoid administration on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 이부서스펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, gastrointestinal ulcer, gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, asthma/hives/allergic reactions experienced with aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics, pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are in the last three months of pregnancy, are breastfeeding, are undergoing high-dose methotrexate chemotherapy, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, cirrhosis, liver impairment, kidney impairment, cardiac dysfunction, heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly, are under 2 years of age, are pregnant, or have chickenpox.

When used for a common cold, it should generally be taken for no more than 5 days. For pain, do not take for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist. For fever, do not take for more than 3 days.
