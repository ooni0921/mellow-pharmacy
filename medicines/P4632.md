#EN
### 💊 이브원큐에스연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, low back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), postoperative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injury (sprains, contusions/bruises), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 이브원큐에스연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis: Adults take 1-3 capsules (200-600 mg) once, 3-4 times a day. A maximum of 16 capsules (3200 mg) can be administered per day.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis: Administer 30-40 mg per kg of body weight per day, divided into 3-4 doses.

For mild to moderate pain and common cold: Adults take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) once, 3-4 times a day. Adjust appropriately according to age and symptoms.

For migraine: Adults take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) once. Do not exceed 2 capsules within a 24-hour period.

Adolescents aged 12 years and older take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) once, 3-4 times a day. If the body weight is less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid administration on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 이브원큐에스연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily, consult your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, as well as severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, hypersensitivity (e.g., asthma, urticaria, allergic reactions) to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) or a history thereof, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, or are in the last trimester of pregnancy.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of blood disorders, a tendency to bleed, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction or a history thereof, kidney dysfunction or a history thereof, cardiac dysfunction or heart disease, hypertension, hypersensitivity, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly or a child, pregnant, breastfeeding, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

### 🕒 이브원큐에스연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 用法及用量
* 对于类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、软组织损伤、非关节风湿性疾病、急性痛风、银屑病性关节炎：成人每次服用1-3粒胶囊（200-600毫克），每日3-4次。每日最高剂量可达16粒胶囊（3200毫克）。

对于幼年型类风湿性关节炎：每日按体重每公斤30-40毫克，分3-4次服用。

对于轻度及中度疼痛、感冒：成人每次服用1-2粒胶囊（200-400毫克），每日3-4次。根据年龄和症状适当增减。

对于偏头痛：成人每次服用1-2粒胶囊（200-400毫克）。24小时内不要超过2粒胶囊。

12岁及以上青少年每次服用1-2粒胶囊（200-400毫克），每日3-4次。如果体重低于30公斤，每日剂量不应超过500毫克，建议避免空腹服用。

### ⚠️ 이브원큐에스연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒超过三杯的常客在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能会引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药可能会增加发生致命性严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死、中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡及穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。

患有未受控制的高血压、充血性心力衰竭、已确诊的缺血性心脏病、外周动脉疾病、脑血管疾病的患者应避免服用高剂量布洛芬（每日2,400毫克）。对于有心血管危险因素（高血压、高血脂、糖尿病、吸烟等）的患者，若需要服用高剂量布洛芬（每日2,400毫克），请在服用本药前咨询医生或药师。
* 对本药过敏、有胃肠道溃疡、胃肠道出血或既往史、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、严重高血压患者、支气管哮喘或既往史、对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎镇痛药有过敏反应（如哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏反应）或既往史、冠状动脉搭桥术（CABG）前后发生疼痛的患者、以及妊娠最后三个月的孕妇，请勿服用本药。

在服用本药前，如果存在血液异常或既往史、有出血倾向的患者、肝硬化、肝功能障碍或既往史、肾功能障碍或既往史、心功能不全或心脏病、高血压患者、过敏史者、系统性红斑狼疮及混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、克罗恩病患者、老年人及儿童、孕妇、哺乳期妇女、对日落黄（黄色5号）过敏或有过敏反应史者，请咨询医生或药师。

请勿超过推荐剂量服用。
