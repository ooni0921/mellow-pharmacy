#EN
### 💊 옵티노즈연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 옵티노즈연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 15 years and older, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 옵티노즈연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, stop taking it immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) appear, stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to it or have experienced it before, if you have a history of asthma after taking other fever reducers/pain relievers or cold medications, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years suspected or confirmed to have chickenpox or influenza; if symptoms of ischemic colitis (such as sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) appear; if you are a child under 2 years of age, a pregnant or possibly pregnant woman, or a breastfeeding woman; if you are elderly, frail, have an allergic constitution, or have experienced allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); or if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), dysuria, high fever, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, ulcers, bleeding problems, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take for a prolonged period.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
