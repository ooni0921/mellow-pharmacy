# EN
### 💊 마겐정(알마게이트) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for its antacid action and improvement of symptoms such as gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, hyperacidity, heartburn, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and acid belching.

### 🕒 마겐정(알마게이트) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age and older should take 2 tablets (1 g) at a time, three times a day, 30 minutes to 1 hour after meals, by chewing them.
If necessary, an additional dose may be taken once before bedtime.

### ⚠️ 마겐정(알마게이트) - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, Alzheimer's disease, hemorrhoids, fluid retention (edema), preeclampsia, diarrhea, undiagnosed gastrointestinal bleeding, or hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have kidney impairment, are physically debilitated, are on a low-phosphate diet, have malabsorption disorders, are elderly, have symptoms of gastrointestinal bleeding such as vomiting blood or black stools, or are pregnant, possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medication, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
