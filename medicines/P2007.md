# EN
### 💊 디카맥스디플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supplement vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and in the elderly, to prevent abnormal bone and tooth development, rickets, and to supply calcium for calcium deficiency and other calcium supplementation needs.

### 🕒 디카맥스디플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 8 years of age and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 디카맥스디플러스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to any ingredient in this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium levels in the blood are excessively high), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or have severe heart failure.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiovascular dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
