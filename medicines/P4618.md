#EN
### 💊 멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of inflammatory symptoms accompanied by edema, and for post-traumatic or post-surgical edema.

### 🕒 멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years and older should take 1 tablet (100 mg) once, twice a day, 30 minutes before meals with water. Many aspects of its mechanism of action in the body have not yet been elucidated, and the relationship between dose and effect has not been clarified; therefore, do not take it without a specific purpose.

### ⚠️ 멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - Precautions
* Information Not Available
* Do not take this medication if you have severe blood coagulation disorders (such as hemophilia), severe liver or kidney disease (such as patients on hemodialysis), are taking anticoagulants or antiplatelet agents, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are a child under 12 years of age. Before taking this medication, patients with a history of or hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解伴有水肿的炎症症状，以及外伤或手术后的水肿。

### 🕒 멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - 用法与用量
* 成人及12岁以上儿童，每次1片（100 mg），每日2次，饭前30分钟与水一同服用。本药在体内的作用机制尚有许多未明之处，且剂量与效果的关系也尚未阐明，因此请勿无目的服用。

### ⚠️ 멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 严重凝血功能异常（如血友病等）、严重肝或严重肾脏疾病（如血液透析患者等）、正在服用抗凝剂或抗血小板聚集剂的患者、孕妇及哺乳期妇女、12岁以下儿童请勿服用本药。服用本药前，对日落黄（黄色5号）有过敏史或过敏经验的患者应咨询医生或药师。

### ⚠️ 멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - 注意事項
