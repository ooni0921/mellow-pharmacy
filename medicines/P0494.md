#EN
### 💊 비가졸액(케토코나졸) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the following skin conditions caused by yeast (dandruff, seborrheic dermatitis, tinea versicolor).

### 🕒 비가졸액(케토코나졸) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply this medicine to the infected areas (body, face, scalp, hair), leave it on for 3-5 minutes like a regular shampoo, then rinse thoroughly. For the treatment of dandruff and seborrheic dermatitis, apply twice a week for 2-4 weeks; for prevention of recurrence, apply once every 1-2 weeks. For the treatment of tinea versicolor, apply once daily for up to 5 days; for prevention of recurrence, apply once daily as a single treatment for up to 3 days before sun exposure.

### ⚠️ 비가졸액(케토코나졸) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medicine in patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, on injured or inflamed scalp, or on areas with severe erosion (raw skin). Before using this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, children, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. This medicine is for external use only. This medicine is not for ophthalmic use; apply with caution to avoid contact with eyes, and if it accidentally gets into the eyes, rinse thoroughly with a sufficient amount of water.

* 本药用于治疗酵母菌引起的以下皮肤病（头皮屑、脂溢性皮炎、花斑癣）。

### 🕒 비가졸액(케토코나졸) - 用法与用量
* 将本药涂抹于感染部位（躯干、面部、头皮、头发），像普通洗发水一样作用3-5分钟后冲洗干净。治疗周期为：治疗头皮屑和脂溢性皮炎时，每周2次，持续2-4周；预防复发时，每1-2周使用1次。治疗花斑癣时，每日1次，最多5天；预防复发时，在暴露于阳光前作为单一疗法每日1次，最多3天。

### ⚠️ 비가졸액(케토코나졸) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者，有伤口或炎症的头皮以及严重糜烂（破损）的部位，请勿使用本药。在使用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、儿童、对丙二醇过敏或有过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。本药仅供外用。本药不可用于眼科，使用时请注意避免接触眼睛，如不慎入眼，请用足量的水彻底冲洗。
