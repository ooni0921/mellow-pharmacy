### 💊 옵티제로연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used as an adjunct therapy for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation after trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 옵티제로연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, administer 300 mg orally per dose, 2-4 times daily. Do not exceed 1,200 mg per day.

### ⚠️ 옵티제로연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Warnings and Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this drug or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

When taking this drug, there is an increased risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this drug if you are:
    * Hypersensitive to this drug.
    * Have gastrointestinal ulcers or signs thereof, or a history of recurrence.
    * Have bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular, or other sites.
    * Have severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe heart failure, or severe hypertension.
    * Have bronchial asthma or a history thereof.
    * Have experienced asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors).
    * Experiencing pain pre- or post-coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery.
    * A pregnant woman in the third trimester (6 months or more) or a lactating woman.
    * Have inflammatory bowel diseases such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis.
    * Have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug therapy.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have had:
* Blood disorders or a history thereof.
* Bleeding tendency.
* Hepatic impairment or a history thereof.
* Renal impairment or a history thereof.
* Fluid retention or heart failure.
* Hypertension.
* A history of hypersensitivity.
* Systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD).
* You are an elderly patient or a child aged 6 years or older.
* Gastric cancer or alcoholism.
* You are in the first or second trimester of pregnancy, planning pregnancy, or may be pregnant.
* Ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease (hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking).
* Hepatic porphyria.
* Have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss.
* Blood coagulation disorders.
* A history of gastrointestinal ulcers due to long-term administration of non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs.

* 每日饮酒三杯以上的规律饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能会引起胃肠道出血。

    * 有胃肠道、脑血管或其他部位出血的患者。

    * 有支气管哮喘或其病史的患者。
    * 对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药（包括COX-2抑制剂）有过哮喘、荨麻疹或过敏反应史的患者。
    * 在冠状动脉搭桥术（CABG）前后发生疼痛的患者。
    * 怀孕6个月以上的孕妇及哺乳期妇女。
    * 患有克罗恩病或溃疡性结肠炎等炎症性肠病的患者。
    * 因先前非甾体抗炎药治疗而发生胃肠道出血或穿孔的患者。

服用本药前，如果您有或曾有以下情况，请咨询医生或药剂师：
* 血液异常或其病史。
* 出血倾向。
* 肝功能障碍或其病史。
* 肾功能障碍或其病史。
* 体液潴留或心力衰竭。
