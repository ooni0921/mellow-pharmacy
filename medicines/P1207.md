# EN

### 💊 Niramidesan - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for cuts, scratches, and abrasions.

### 🕒 Niramidesan - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ Niramidesan - Precautions
* No information.
* Before using this medicine, patients who have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicines or cosmetics, patients with extensive affected areas, or patients with severe wounds or burns should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. If symptoms do not improve after several days of administration, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist. This medicine is for external use only; do not take internally.
