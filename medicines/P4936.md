#EN
### 💊 Wienzyme Tablet - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, stomach discomfort, promoting digestion, and gastric distension due to indigestion.

### 🕒 Wienzyme Tablet - Dosage and Administration
* For adults aged 15 years or older, take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Wienzyme Tablet - Precautions
* Do not take this medication if you are a child under 7 years of age or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* If you have an allergic constitution, consult your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately stop taking the medication and consult your doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 Wienzyme Tablet - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗消化不良、食欲不振、过食、胃部不适、促进消化以及由消化不良引起的胃胀感。

### 🕒 Wienzyme Tablet - 用法与用量
* 15岁及以上成人，每次2片，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ Wienzyme Tablet - 注意事项
* 7岁以下儿童，以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，过敏体质者请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守用法用量。
* 若服用约2周后症状无改善，应立即停止服药，并咨询医生或药师。

#JP
