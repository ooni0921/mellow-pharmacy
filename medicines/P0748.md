### 🕒 대웅이부펜시럽(이부프로펜)(수출명:SOTSTOPsyrup) - 용법 및 용량

# EN
### 💊 대웅이부펜시럽(이부프로펜)(수출명:SOTSTOPsyrup) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to common cold, low back pain, dysmenorrhea, post-operative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, strains), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 대웅이부펜시럽(이부프로펜)(수출명:SOTSTOPsyrup) - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 11-14, take 200-250 mg (10-13 mL) once; for 7-10 years, 150-200 mg (8-10 mL) once; for 3-6 years, 100-150 mg (5-8 mL) once; for 1-2 years, 50-100 mg (3-5 mL) once, 3-4 times a day. For children weighing less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg (25 mL), and it is advisable to avoid administration on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 대웅이부펜시럽(이부프로펜)(수출명:SOTSTOPsyrup) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, or if you have gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics. Also, avoid use if you are a patient experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, a pregnant woman in the last trimester of pregnancy, a breastfeeding woman, a patient receiving high-dose methotrexate for anticancer therapy, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Do not exceed the recommended dose. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis, hepatic impairment, renal impairment, cardiac dysfunction, heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly, a child under 2 years of age, a pregnant woman, or a patient with chickenpox. When taking for a cold, it should generally be taken within 5 days. Do not take it for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without the instruction of a doctor or pharmacist, and do not take it for fever for more than 3 days.
