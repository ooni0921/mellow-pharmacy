### 💊 큐립연고 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for chapped lips, macerated lips, cheilitis, and angular cheilitis.

### 🕒 큐립연고 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 큐립연고 - Precautions
*   No information provided
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms to medicines or cosmetics (such as rash, redness, itching), or if you have severe moistness or maceration.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Use this medicine only for application to the lips according to its indicated efficacy and usage.

Be careful not to get it in your eyes. If it gets into your eyes, wash immediately with water or lukewarm water. If symptoms are severe, seek consultation from an ophthalmologist.

Children should use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
