#EN
### 💊 마그토벤연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness (convalescence), and in old age; for the relief of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness and coldness in hands and feet (cold extremities) associated with peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause; for neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, shoulder stiffness); for the relief of symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; for beriberi; for eye fatigue; and for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그토벤연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그토벤연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal dysfunction.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders; or patients who require cautious administration of lipid emulsions; or patients at special risk from fat overload; or patients with thrombotic tendencies (blood clots); or patients with a history of or hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
