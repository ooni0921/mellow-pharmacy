* 만 12세 이상 청소년 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 베라비타정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is reduced during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (back pain, stiff shoulders, etc.), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, and for the supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 베라비타정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adolescents and adults aged 12 years and older, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 베라비타정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant, or a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcers, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart and circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, a thrombotic predisposition, or folic acid deficiency.

Please adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, which can affect clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 베라비타정 - 功效与作用
* 本药物用于补充体力疲劳、妊娠・哺乳期、病中・病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、老年期的维生素E、B1、B2、B6、C，以及缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）症状，脚气病、眼睛疲劳、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎症状，并用于补充锌。

### 🕒 베라비타정 - 用法与用量
* 12岁以上青少年及成人，每次1片，每日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 베라비타정 - 注意事项
* 对本药物过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态）、结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药物。

在服用本药物前，如果儿童经常晒太阳并正常饮食、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病、心血管系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风或肾结石、有血栓倾向的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

本药物可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
