### 🕒 루피엔연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 용법 및 용량

연소성 류마티양 관절염에는 1일 체중 kg당 30~40 mg을 3~4회 분할하여 복용하며, 경증 및 중등도의 동통이나 감기에는 성인 1회 1/2캡슐~1캡슐(200~400 mg), 1일 3~4회 복용합니다.

편두통에 성인은 1회 1/2~1캡슐(200~400 mg)을 복용하고 24시간 동안 1캡슐(400 mg)을 초과하지 않도록 합니다.

연령, 증상에 따라 적절히 증감하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 루피엔연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for fever and pain caused by colds, lower back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), post-operative pain, headaches, migraines, toothaches, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 루피엔연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 1/2 to 1.5 capsules (200-600 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day, with a maximum daily dose of 8 capsules (3,200 mg).
*   For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or colds, adults take 1/2 to 1 capsule (200-400 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day.
*   For migraines, adults take 1/2 to 1 capsule (200-400 mg) once, and should not exceed 1 capsule (400 mg) within 24 hours.
*   Dosage may be adjusted appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 루피엔연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
*   Taking this medication increases the risk of serious and potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulceration, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe heart failure, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other NSAIDs (including COX-2 inhibitors) or a history thereof, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, or if you are in the last trimester (last 3 months) of pregnancy.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction or a history thereof, kidney dysfunction or a history thereof, heart failure or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly or a child, pregnant, or breastfeeding.
*   Do not exceed the recommended dose.
*   Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), and do not take for fever for more than 3 days, without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 루피엔연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 用法与用量

*   对于幼年型风湿性关节炎，每日按体重每公斤30至40 mg，分3至4次服用。对于轻度及中度疼痛或感冒，成人一次服用1/2至1粒胶囊（200至400 mg），每日3至4次。
*   对于偏头痛，成人一次服用1/2至1粒胶囊（200至400 mg），24小时内不得超过1粒胶囊（400 mg）。
*   请根据年龄、症状适当增减剂量。

*   服用本药品前，曾有过敏史者、有血液异常史者、有出血倾向的患者、肝硬化、肝功能障碍史者、肾功能障碍史者、心功能不全或心脏病患者、高血压患者、系统性红斑狼疮和混合性结缔组织病患者、溃疡性结肠炎、克罗恩病患者、老年人及儿童、孕妇、哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药剂师。
