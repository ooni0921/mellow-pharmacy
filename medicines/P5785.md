#EN

### 💊 동성비타비플러스액 - Indications and Effects
*   This drug is used to supplement vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, in old age, or during recovery from illness causing physical debility.
*   Through its contained vitamins, this drug is used to relieve symptoms of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, stiff shoulders (arthralgia), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 동성비타비플러스액 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 bottle per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 동성비타비플러스액 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, patients with severe renal insufficiency, and infants under 12 months should not take this drug.
*   Before using this drug, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, infants, patients with heart and circulatory system dysfunction, patients with renal impairment, and patients with hypoproteinemia should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with a thrombotic predisposition, such as blood vessel blockage, should immediately stop taking this drug and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this drug for about 1 month, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This drug may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
