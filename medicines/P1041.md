#EN
### 💊 파워폴연질캡슐(수출명: POWERPOLL) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins A, D, B1, B2, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of weakened physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth periods, and old age. It also helps alleviate dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), poor development of bones and teeth, and prevents rickets.

### 🕒 파워폴연질캡슐(수출명: POWERPOLL) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once daily.

### ⚠️ 파워폴연질캡슐(수출명: POWERPOLL) - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of vitamin A per day may cause congenital malformations. Women who are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or who may be pregnant should not take this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts; if you are an infant under 3 months of age; or if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, or kidney stones. Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, nursing mothers, premature infants, infants, patients with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), gout, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders, or patients who must cautiously administer lipid emulsions, should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow. If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 파워폴연질캡슐(수출명: POWERPOLL) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、发育期、老年期维生素A、D、B1、B2、C的补充，以及缓解眼睛干涩、夜盲症（夜间视力下降的症状）、骨骼和牙齿发育不良，并预防佝偻病。

### 🕒 파워폴연질캡슐(수출명: POWERPOLL) - 用法用量
*   成人每次1粒，每日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 파워폴연질캡슐(수출명: POWERPOLL) - 注意事项
*   每日服用维生素A超过5,000 IU可能导致先天性畸形。妊娠期前3个月内或可能怀孕的女性请勿服用。
*   对本药或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，未满3个月的婴儿，以及患有高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态）、结节病（原因不明的全身炎症性疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石的患者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、痛风、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药可能会干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查结果。如果服用约1个月后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
