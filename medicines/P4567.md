#EN
### 💊 속시탈정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, gastric distension, indigestion (food stagnation), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and gastric distension due to indigestion.

### 🕒 속시탈정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, after each meal.

### ⚠️ 속시탈정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, or if you are a child (under 15 years old), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a nursing mother.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching), have kidney or liver impairment, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Yellow No. 4 (Tartrazine), have high blood pressure, are elderly, or have heart impairment or edema.

Please strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
