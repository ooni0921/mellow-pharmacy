# EN
### 💊 리게논-큐연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic liver disease and toxic liver disease.

### 🕒 리게논-큐연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once, three times a day. After treatment, reduce the dosage to 1-2 capsules once daily.

### ⚠️ 리게논-큐연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have fat metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, are pregnant, or are hypersensitive to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5). Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

# CN
### 💊 리게논-큐연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于慢性肝病、中毒性肝病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 리게논-큐연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   成人每次1粒，每日3次。治疗后将剂量减少至每日1-2粒。

### ⚠️ 리게논-큐연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对大豆油或大豆、花生过敏的患者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，患有高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常者、孕妇、对日落黄（黄色5号）过敏的患者请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
