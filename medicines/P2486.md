#EN
**Product Name:** 키즈하이츄어블정

### 💊 키즈하이츄어블정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It also helps alleviate dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), poor bone and tooth development, and prevent rickets.

### 🕒 키즈하이츄어블정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years and older: Take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day.
*   Children aged 36 months up to under 8 years old: Take 1 tablet per dose, 3 times a day.
*   Take after meals by dissolving in the mouth or chewing.

### ⚠️ 키즈하이츄어블정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months old, have kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year old, a child with a normal diet and high sunlight exposure, a patient with hyperoxaluria, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, a young child (toddler), a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with kidney impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient with a history of gout or kidney stones, a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient with thrombotic predisposition.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Vitamin A contained in this medicine is sufficiently supplied by a normal diet, so the dose for supplementary therapy should not exceed 5,000 IU per day.
*   If symptoms do not improve after about 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

*   饭后口中含服溶解或咀嚼服用。

*   无信息
*   高钙血症患者、结节病患者、肾脏疾病患者、对本品过敏的患者、12个月以下的婴儿、肾结石患者、重度肾功能不全患者、以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本品。
