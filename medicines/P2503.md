#EN
### 💊 키토실츄어블정 - Indications and Effects
*   This drug is used for the supplementation of Vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of physical weakness (during or after illness), growth periods, and old age, as well as for the relief of dry eyes, night blindness, poor development of bones and teeth, and the prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 키토실츄어블정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times daily. For individuals aged 36 months to under 12 years, take 1 tablet per dose, 3 times daily.
    Take orally by dissolving or chewing in the mouth after meals.

### ⚠️ 키토실츄어블정 - Precautions
*   No information provided
*   Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or genetic problems such as glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant, an infant, have cardiovascular dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, a history of kidney stones, folic acid deficiency, or a predisposition to thrombosis (blood clots).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This drug may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this drug, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
