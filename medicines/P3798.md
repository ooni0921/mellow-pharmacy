#EN
### 💊 하디콜에스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 하디콜에스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 15 years of age or older should take 2 capsules once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 하디콜에스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker who consumes three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: children under 2 years of age; infants or children under 15 years of age who have or are suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) occur in relation to pseudoephedrine; have an allergic constitution; have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicines (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty in urination; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or breastfeeding; have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm; have a cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, chronic cough; or have a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
