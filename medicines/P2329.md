#EN
### 💊 액티몰스액(시트룰린말산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the adjunctive treatment of functional asthenia.

### 🕒 액티몰스액(시트룰린말산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 g once, 3 times a day. Children aged 6 years and older take 1 g once, 2 times a day. Do not take for more than 4 weeks.

### ⚠️ 액티몰스액(시트룰린말산염) - Precautions
* No information.
* Infants under 6 years of age, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should not take this medicine. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
