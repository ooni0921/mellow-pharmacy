#EN
### 💊 프로콘틴600정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the replenishment of vitamins B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, or during periods of physical weakness due to illness or convalescence. It is also used for neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as low back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 프로콘틴600정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 프로콘틴600정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a premature infant, an infant, or a patient with heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, or hypoproteinemia.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medication, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 프로콘틴600정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解体力疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期以及病中或病后体力下降时补充维生素B1、B2、B6，并用于神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）、脚气病、眼睛疲劳。

### 🕒 프로콘틴600정 - 用法用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每日1次，每次1片，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 프로콘틴600정 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏、患有高钙血症、结节病、肾脏疾病、未满12个月的婴儿、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性和哺乳期女性、经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、早产儿、婴幼儿、心血管系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药在各种尿液检查中可能会干扰血糖检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检测值。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。

#JP
### 💊 프로콘틴600정 - 効能・効果
*   本剤は、肉体疲労、妊娠・授乳期、病中病後の体力低下時のビタミンB1、B2、B6の補給、神経痛、筋肉痛、関節痛（腰痛、肩こりなど）、脚気、眼精疲労に用いられます。

### 🕒 프로콘틴600정 - 用法・用量
*   8歳以上および成人の方は、1回1錠を1日1回、食後に服用してください。

*   情報なし

*   約1ヶ月間服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
