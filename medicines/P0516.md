#EN
### 💊 Hubahuba-jeong - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for lower limb heaviness due to varicose veins, phlebitis, and various symptoms associated with hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 Hubahuba-jeong - Dosage and Administration
* For venous diseases, take 1 tablet once, twice daily (morning, evening) during meals. For hemorrhoid symptoms, take 1 tablet once, 3 to 4 times daily during meals.

### ⚠️ Hubahuba-jeong - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hyperthyroidism, are taking MAO inhibitors, have malignant hypertension, severe tachycardia, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are a child. Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you have severe arterial hypertension or a hemorrhagic diathesis. Athletes using this medicine should be aware that it may cause a positive result in doping tests. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 후바후바정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗静脉曲张引起的下肢沉重感、静脉炎以及与痔疮相关的各种症状。

### 🕒 후바후바정 - 用法用量
* 对于静脉疾病，每次1片，每日2次（早、晚）餐中服用；对于痔疮症状，每次1片，每日3至4次餐中服用。

### ⚠️ 후바후바정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 甲状腺功能亢进症患者、正在服用MAO抑制剂的患者、恶性高血压患者、严重心动过速患者、孕妇及哺乳期妇女、儿童请勿服用本药。有严重动脉性高血压、出血倾向的患者在服用本药前应咨询医生或药师。使用本药的运动员请注意，兴奋剂检测中可能会出现阳性反应。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请咨询医生或药师。
