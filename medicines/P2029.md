# EN
### 💊 키페낙에스첩부제(플루르비프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to provide analgesic and anti-inflammatory effects for degenerative osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder joint, tendinitis and tenosynovitis, humeral epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 키페낙에스첩부제(플루르비프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   After peeling off the release liner from the patch surface, apply to the affected area twice a day.
*   Wipe off sweat from the application area before use.

### ⚠️ 키페낙에스첩부제(플루르비프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you have or have experienced aspirin asthma (asthma attacks caused by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, etc.), or on damaged skin and mucous membranes, eczema, or rash areas.
*   Before using this medicine, patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant women, children, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 키페낙에스첩부제(플루르비프로펜) - 功效及效果
*   本药用于缓解退行性关节炎（骨关节炎）、肩周炎、腱炎、腱鞘炎、肱骨外上髁炎（如网球肘）、肌肉痛以及外伤后的肿胀和疼痛，具有镇痛、消炎作用。

### 🕒 키페낙에스첩부제(플루르비프로펜) - 用法及用量
*   撕下药面的离型纸后，每日2次贴于患处（病患部位）。
*   使用前请擦干涂抹部位的汗水。

### ⚠️ 키페낙에스첩부제(플루르비프로펜) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者、阿司匹林哮喘（因非甾体抗炎药等引起的哮喘发作）患者或有此病史者、以及损伤的皮肤和粘膜、湿疹、皮疹部位，请勿使用本药。
*   在使用本药前，支气管哮喘患者、孕妇、儿童、老年人请咨询医生或药师。
