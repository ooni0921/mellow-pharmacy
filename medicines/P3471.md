# EN
### 💊 펜싹코프연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 펜싹코프연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 15 years and older: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 펜싹코프연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* Serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. Discontinue use immediately if skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medicine.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have experienced hypersensitivity to this medicine, asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking or have stopped taking MAO inhibitors (e.g., antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs) within the last 2 weeks.
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; an infant between 3 months and under 2 years old; have an allergic constitution or a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty with urination; are frail or have a high fever; have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have a history of ulcers or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding; smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 펜싹코프연질캡슐 - 用法及用量
* 15岁以上及成人，一次2粒，一日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 펜싹코프연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯以上并定期饮酒者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起肝损伤。
* 曾有报道极少数情况下发生可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后，如果出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。
* 乙酰氨基酚每日最大剂量（4,000 mg）请勿超过。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药有过敏史者、服用其他解热镇痛剂或感冒药时曾有哮喘史者、未满3个月的婴儿、正在服用或停用MAO抑制剂（如抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森药等）后2周内者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，如果婴儿及未满15岁的儿童感染或疑似感染水痘或流感；满3个月以上未满2岁的婴儿、幼儿；过敏体质者、有过敏症状（例如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）者；有肝、肾（肾脏）、心脏、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（例如：眼睛疼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿（小便）困难等症状者；体弱者或高热者；有持续或复发性胃部问题如胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛，或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女；吸烟者、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过量痰液的咳嗽、持续或反复超过1周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

* 服药期间请勿饮酒。
* 服用后可能会出现困倦，因此驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
* 服用5~6次后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药剂师。
