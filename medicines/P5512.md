#EN
### 💊 타세놀이부연질캡슐400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, lower back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), and postoperative pain. It is also used for headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 타세놀이부연질캡슐400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 1/2 to 1.5 tablets (200-600 mg) 3-4 times daily, up to a maximum of 8 tablets (3200 mg) per day.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, 30-40 mg per kg of body weight is taken daily, divided into 3-4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or colds, adults take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) 3-4 times daily.

For migraine, adults take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) orally, and should not exceed 1 tablet (400 mg) within a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 타세놀이부연질캡슐400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Warnings and Precautions
*   Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions, including bleeding, ulceration, and perforation of the gastrointestinal tract, which can be fatal, may occur when taking this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it; a history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); gastrointestinal ulcer or bleeding; severe blood disorders; liver, kidney, or heart failure; hypertension; bronchial asthma; peri-operative pain from coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; are in the last three months of pregnancy; or are undergoing high-dose methotrexate chemotherapy.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity; a history of blood disorders; a bleeding tendency; liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, heart failure, or other heart diseases; hypertension; systemic lupus erythematosus or mixed connective tissue disease; ulcerative colitis or Crohn's disease; are elderly or a child; are pregnant or breastfeeding; or are taking low-dose aspirin to prevent myocardial infarction or stroke.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

### 🕒 타세놀이부연질캡슐400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - 用法・用量
*   リウマチ様関節炎、変形性関節症、強直性脊椎炎、軟部組織損傷、非関節性リウマチ性疾患、急性痛風、乾癬性関節炎の場合、成人は1回1/2錠～1.5錠（200～600 mg）を1日3～4回服用し、1日の最高用量は8錠（3200 mg）までとします。

片頭痛の場合、成人は1回1/2錠～1錠（200～400 mg）を経口投与し、24時間で1錠（400 mg）を超えないようにしてください。

推奨用量を超えて服用しないでください。
