#EN
### 💊 덴드리스캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 덴드리스캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 덴드리스캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not administer this medication to children under 15 years of age or to patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching) to medications or cosmetics, allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to eggs, if you have an allergic constitution, or if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
