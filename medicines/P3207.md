#EN
### 💊 코앤쿨나잘스프레이 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heaviness in the head caused by the common cold (acute rhinitis), allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 코앤쿨나잘스프레이 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, spray once into each nostril, 1 to 3 times daily.

The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms, but do not exceed 3 times a day.

Apply with an interval of at least 8 hours between each dose.

### ⚠️ 코앤쿨나잘스프레이 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication in children under 12 years of age, patients with a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, or individuals who are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the past 2 weeks.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you: have a constitution prone to urticaria, contact dermatitis, migraine, food allergies, etc.; have previously experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash/redness, swelling, irritation, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.) due to medication; have hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, glaucoma; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; are breastfeeding; or have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever/rash or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Use this medication only as a nasal spray for rhinitis, and do not use it in the eyes or ears.

Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.

Drowsiness may occur after use, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, stop using the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 코앤쿨나잘스프레이 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解由普通感冒（急性鼻炎）、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流涕、打喷嚏、头重感。

### 🕒 코앤쿨나잘스프레이 - 用法与用量
* 12岁及以上成人，每日1~3次，每次喷入鼻腔1次。

根据年龄和症状可适当增减剂量，但每日不得超过3次。

每次用药间隔至少8小时。

### ⚠️ 코앤쿨나잘스프레이 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 12岁以下儿童、对本药有过敏史的患者、以及正在服用或停用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）后2周内的人群，请勿使用本药。

在使用本药之前，如果您属于以下情况，请咨询医生或药师：容易引起荨麻疹、接触性皮炎、偏头痛、食物过敏等体质；曾因药物引起过敏症状（如发热、皮疹·发红、水肿、刺激感、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）；患有高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾病、青光眼；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；吸烟、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热·皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

本药仅限作为鼻炎喷雾剂使用，请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。

请勿连续使用超过7天。

使用后可能会出现困倦，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。

如果使用3天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。

年齢および症状に応じて適宜増減しますが、1日3回を超えないようにしてください。

各回、最低8時間以上の間隔を空けて適用してください。

定められた用法用量を厳守してください。

本剤は鼻炎用点鼻薬としてのみ使用し、目や耳には使用しないでください。
