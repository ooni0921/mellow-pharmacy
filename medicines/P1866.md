# EN
### 💊 테르졸겔(테르비나핀) - Indications and Efficacy
* This drug is used for cutaneous fungal infections (tinea pedis, tinea cruris, tinea corporis) caused by dermatophytes and for pityriasis versicolor.

### 🕒 테르졸겔(테르비나핀) - Dosage and Administration
* Before application, thoroughly wash and dry the affected area, then apply a thin layer once daily to the infected area and its surroundings. For intertriginous infections (under the breasts, between fingers and toes, between buttocks, groin), it may also be used with a gauze covering at night. Symptom relief typically appears within 2-3 days, but the usual treatment duration is 1 week.

### ⚠️ 테르졸겔(테르비나핀) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this drug if you are hypersensitive to it. Before using this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of or hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, if you are breastfeeding, or if the patient is a child. This drug is for external use only. Do not use around the eyes.

# CN
### 💊 테르졸겔(테르비나핀) - 功效与作用
* 本药物用于治疗由皮肤真菌引起的皮肤真菌感染（足癣、股癣、体癣）和花斑糠疹。

### 🕒 테르졸겔(테르비나핀) - 用法与用量
* 使用前，将患处彻底清洗并擦干，然后每日一次，薄薄地涂抹于感染部位及其周围。对于间擦性感染（乳房下方、手指和脚趾之间、臀部之间、腹股沟），夜间也可覆盖纱布使用。症状通常在2-3天内缓解，但常规治疗周期为1周。

### ⚠️ 테르졸겔(테르비나핀) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者请勿使用本药。在使用本药前，如您对丙二醇有过敏史或过敏症，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女，以及儿童，请咨询医生或药师。本药仅供外用。请勿在眼周使用。
