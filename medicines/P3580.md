#EN
### 💊 이지콜씨플러스캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 이지콜씨플러스캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and individuals aged 15 years or older, take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 이지콜씨플러스캡슐 - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to it or have experienced it before, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, an infant or young child under 2 years old, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant or is breastfeeding, an elderly person, a frail person, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, edema, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, high fever, gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, ulcers, bleeding problems, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts or recurs for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache, or have a hypersensitivity to or have experienced a reaction to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
