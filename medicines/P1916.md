#EN
### 💊 발무드겔(테르비나핀) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for superficial fungal infections of the skin caused by dermatophytes (tinea pedis, tinea cruris, tinea corporis) and pityriasis versicolor.

### 🕒 발무드겔(테르비나핀) - Dosage and Administration
*   Before application, wash and dry the affected area. Apply a thin layer once daily to the infected area and its surroundings. For intertriginous infections (e.g., under the breasts, between fingers, between toes, between buttocks, in the groin area), a gauze dressing may be applied overnight. Symptom relief usually appears within 2-3 days, but the typical treatment duration is 1 week.

### ⚠️ 발무드겔(테르비나핀) - Precautions
*   No specific information available.
*   Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to it. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity to propylene glycol or have previously experienced it, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, or if the user is a child. This medication is for external use only. Do not use around the eyes.

*   本药品用于治疗由皮肤癣菌引起的皮肤真菌感染症（足癣、股癣、体癣）和花斑癣。

### 🕒 발무드겔(테르비나핀) - 用法与用量
*   使用前，请先清洁并擦干患处（病变部位）。每日一次，将本品薄薄地涂抹于感染部位及其周围。对于擦烂性感染（如乳房下、手指间、脚趾间、臀部间、腹股沟），夜间可以使用纱布覆盖。症状通常在2-3天内缓解，但常规治疗周期为1周。

### ⚠️ 발무드겔(테르비나핀) - 注意事项
*   无相关信息
*   对本品过敏的患者请勿使用本药品。在使用本药品之前，如果对丙二醇过敏或有相关过敏史、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、儿童，请咨询医生或药剂师。本药品仅供外用。请勿在眼部周围使用。
