### 🕒 콜대원키즈이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - 용법 및 용량

체중이 30 kg 미만인 어린이는 1일량이 500 mg (25 mL)을 초과해서는 안되며 공복시 투여는 피하는 것이 바람직합니다.

### ⚠️ 콜대원키즈이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - 주의사항

* 이 약에 과민증 환자, 위장관궤양, 위장관출혈, 심한 혈액이상, 심한 간장애, 심한 신장장애, 심한 심장기능부전, 심한 고혈압, 기관지천식, 아스피린 또는 다른 소염진통제에 의한 천식, 두드러기, 알레르기 반응 경험 환자, 심장동맥 우회로술 전후 통증발생 환자, 임신 말기 3개월 기간의 임부, 수유부, 항암요법으로 고용량 메토트렉세이트를 투여 중인 환자, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 콜대원키즈이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to common cold, lower back pain, dysmenorrhea, post-operative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 콜대원키즈이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 11-14 years, take 200-250 mg (10-13 mL) per dose; for 7-10 years, take 150-200 mg (8-10 mL) per dose; for 3-6 years, take 100-150 mg (5-8 mL) per dose; for 1-2 years, take 50-100 mg (3-5 mL) per dose, 3-4 times a day.

For children weighing less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg (25 mL). It is advisable to avoid administration on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 콜대원키즈이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes mellitus, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, are in the last trimester of pregnancy (last 3 months), are breastfeeding, are undergoing high-dose methotrexate therapy for cancer, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, cardiac dysfunction, heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly, are under 2 years of age, are pregnant, or have chickenpox.

For common cold symptoms, take this medication for a maximum of 5 days. Do not take it for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without a doctor's or pharmacist's instruction.

请勿超过推荐剂量服用。

在服用本药物前，如有血液异常、出血倾向、肝硬化、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全、心脏病、高血压、系统性红斑狼疮、混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、克罗恩病、老年人、2岁以下儿童、孕妇或水痘患者，请咨询医生或药师。

用于感冒时，原则上服用不超过5天。未经医生或药师指示，成人疼痛服用不超过10天，儿童疼痛服用不超过5天，发热服用不超过3天。

本剤を服用する前に、血液異常、出血傾向のある患者、肝硬変、肝障害、腎障害、心機能不全、心疾患、高血圧、全身性エリテマトーデス、混合性結合組織疾患、潰瘍性大腸炎、クローン病、高齢者、2歳未満の小児、妊婦、水痘の患者は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。

風邪で服用する場合は、原則として5日以内に服用し、医師または薬剤師の指示なしに痛みに10日以上（成人）または5日以上（小児）、発熱に3日以上服用しないでください。
