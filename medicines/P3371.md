#EN

### 💊 토마벤연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, decline in physical strength during or after illness, and in old age. It also helps relieve symptoms of peripheral circulatory disorders, menopausal shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lumbago, shoulder stiffness), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis. It is also used for muscle cramps caused by magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 토마벤연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years and older and adults should take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 토마벤연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal insufficiency.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, premature infant, infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatic inflammation, or other lipid metabolism disorders, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously, or if there is a special risk expected due to fat overload, or if you have a thrombotic predisposition.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
