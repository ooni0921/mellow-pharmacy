* 정보 없음
* 이 약 또는 대두유, 콩, 땅콩에 과민증 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 고칼슘혈증(혈액중에 칼슘이 과잉으로 존재하는 상태), 유육종증(원인을 알 수 없는 전신 염증질환), 신장질환, 신장결석, 심한 증상의 신부전 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 본엔디연질캡슐 - Indications and Efficacy
* This medicine is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, and for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, and rickets.

### 🕒 본엔디연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years or older and adults, take 1 capsule once daily.

### ⚠️ 본엔디연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts; if you are an infant under 12 months of age; if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood); sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause); kidney disease; kidney stones; or severe renal insufficiency.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a lactating woman; a premature infant; an infant; a patient with heart or circulatory system dysfunction; a patient with hypoproteinemia; a patient with a history of kidney stones; a patient with hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders; a patient for whom lipid emulsions should be administered cautiously; a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload; or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
