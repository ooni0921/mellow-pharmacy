#EN
### 💊 바실리스산(바실루스리케니포르미스균) - Indications & Effects
*   This drug is used for acute and chronic enteritis, acute and chronic diarrhea, acute dysentery, and abnormal intestinal fermentation caused by various reasons.

### 🕒 바실리스산(바실루스리케니포르미스균) - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults should take 2 g per dose, three times a day, with an initial dose of 4 g.
*   Infants and children should take 1 g per dose, three times a day.

### ⚠️ 바실리스산(바실루스리케니포르미스균) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this drug.
*   Infants under 3 months of age should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
