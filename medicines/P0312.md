### 💊 다이야펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to common cold, low back pain, dysmenorrhea, post-operative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 다이야펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults should take 1/2 to 1.5 tablets (200-600 mg) 3 to 4 times a day. The maximum daily dose should not exceed 3200 mg.

    For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, the dosage is 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or common cold, adults should take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) 3 to 4 times a day.

    For children aged 11-14 years, take 200-250 mg per dose; for 7-10 years, 150-200 mg per dose; for 3-6 years, 100-150 mg per dose; for 1-2 years, 50-100 mg per dose, orally 3 to 4 times a day.

    Children weighing less than 30 kg should not exceed a daily dose of 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid taking this medication on an empty stomach.

    Do not exceed the recommended dose.

### ⚠️ 다이야펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Individuals who regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

    Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.

    Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug; have gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof; severe blood disorders; severe liver impairment; severe kidney impairment; severe heart failure; severe hypertension; bronchial asthma or a history thereof; a history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other NSAIDs (including COX-2 inhibitors); are experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; are in the last trimester of pregnancy (last 3 months); are taking high-dose methotrexate as part of chemotherapy; or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis, liver impairment, kidney impairment, heart failure or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly or a child, are pregnant, breastfeeding, or have a hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

*   对本药过敏的患者；有胃肠道溃疡、胃肠道出血或有相关病史的患者；有严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、严重高血压的患者；有支气管哮喘或有相关病史的患者；对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药（包括COX-2抑制剂）有过敏反应史（如哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏反应）的患者；冠状动脉（心脏动脉）旁路移植术前后发生疼痛的患者；妊娠最后三个月的孕妇；正在接受高剂量甲氨蝶呤化疗的患者；患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。

    服用本药前，患有或曾患有血液异常、出血倾向、肝硬化、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全或心脏病、高血压、系统性红斑狼疮及混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、克罗恩病、老年人、儿童、孕妇、哺乳期妇女、对黄色5号过敏的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
