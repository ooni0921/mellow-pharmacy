#EN
### 💊 맨담이부쿨플라스타(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 맨담이부쿨플라스타(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* After peeling off the release liner from the patch, apply one patch to the affected area once a day.

Unless otherwise directed by a physician, the treatment period should not exceed 14 days, and the patch should be replaced every 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 맨담이부쿨플라스타(이buprofen) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this drug if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to this drug, aspirin, other analgesics, or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), bronchial asthma, gastrointestinal bleeding, gastric or duodenal ulcers, peptic ulcers, severe heart failure, kidney or liver damage, if you are under 12 years of age, in the third trimester of pregnancy, or breastfeeding.

Before using this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are elderly, have chronic bronchitis, allergic rhinitis, inflammation of the nasal mucosa, gastrointestinal disease (gastrointestinal ulcers, bleeding or perforation, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease) or have experienced it, liver or kidney dysfunction, heart failure, hypertension, are pregnant (first or second trimester) or planning a pregnancy, have kidney disease or have experienced it, have experienced intolerance after taking aspirin or ibuprofen, have allergies to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, severe wetness or erosion, circulatory disorders, or diabetes.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Taking a bath with the patch on may cause strong irritation, so remove it 30 minutes to 1 hour before bathing. Also, avoid applying it immediately after bathing.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

This drug is for external use only.

Do not use on or around the eyes or mucous membranes, and be careful not to let it get into the eyes.

Do not use on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer, wounds and inflamed areas, sensitive skin, hemorrhagic skin conditions, infected skin, or burn areas.

Do not use with occlusive dressing methods.

For more detailed information, please refer to the detailed information in the approved package insert.

### ⚠️ 맨담이부쿨플라스타(이부프로펜) - 注意事项

若使用5-6天后症状仍无改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。

贴敷状态下洗澡可能会引起强烈刺激，因此请在洗澡前30分钟至1小时将其揭下。此外，请避免在洗澡后立即贴敷。
