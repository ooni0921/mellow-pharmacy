### 💊 써스펜8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of fever, pain associated with colds, headache, toothache, muscle pain, backache, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 써스펜8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 12 years and older and adults, take 2 tablets every 8 hours. Do not exceed 6 tablets in a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 써스펜8시간이알서방정650밀리그램(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, a history of asthma induced by aspirin or other NSAIDs, are taking barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, have consumed alcohol, or are under 12 years of age. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding tendencies, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without the direction of a doctor or pharmacist. This medication is a sustained-release formulation; therefore, swallow the tablets whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving them.
