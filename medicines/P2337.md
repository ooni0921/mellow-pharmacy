#EN
### 💊 비거스액(시트룰린말산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used as an adjuvant therapy for functional asthenia.

### 🕒 비거스액(시트룰린말산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 g once, three times a day. Children aged 6 years or older should take 1 g once, twice a day. Do not take for more than 4 weeks.

### ⚠️ 비거스액(시트룰린말산염) - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this drug if you are an infant under 6 years of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a lactating woman. Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
