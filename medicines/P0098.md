#EN
### 💊 크린조(염화나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for irrigation, cleansing of skin, wound surfaces, and mucous membranes, wet compresses, gargling, and as an inhalant for mucous membrane cleansing, promotion of sputum expectoration, and cleansing of medical devices.

### 🕒 크린조(염화나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
*   When used as an irrigation solution, use an appropriate amount as directed by a physician. Apply an appropriate amount for cleansing of skin, wound surfaces, and mucous membranes, wet compresses, gargling, and nebulization. It is used for cleansing medical devices.

### ⚠️ 크린조(염화나트륨) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Before using this medicine, patients with severe heart failure or kidney failure should consult a doctor or pharmacist. This medicine should be used topically and not as a parenteral injection. Do not use if systemic absorption may occur. Do not use if there are any foreign particles or if the container is damaged. When used for irrigation, use a freshly opened product and do not reuse any remaining solution.
