#EN
### 💊 한풍제통고온혈플라스타 - Efficacy & Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of pain and inflammation associated with bruises, sprains, muscle pain, joint pain, low back pain, stiff shoulders, neuralgia, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 한풍제통고온혈플라스타 - Dosage & Administration
*   Apply 1 to several patches daily to the affected area as appropriate.

### ⚠️ 한풍제통고온혈플라스타 - Precautions
*   Do not use this medication on infants under 30 months of age, on areas with eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer (urushiol) or similar substances, or on open wounds.
*   Individuals who have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.) due to medicines or cosmetics, those with an allergic constitution, patients with severe moisture accumulation or maceration, children, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before use.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When using for children, use under the supervision of a guardian.
*   For external use only.
*   If the affected area is wet with sweat, etc., wipe it thoroughly before use.
*   Do not warm the affected area with a heating pad, electric blanket, or similar devices.
*   When bathing with this patch applied, you may feel strong irritation, so remove the patch 30 minutes to 1 hour before bathing.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If a large amount is absorbed through the skin, symptoms of poisoning may occur, so avoid long-term use over large surface areas.

*   用于儿童时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
