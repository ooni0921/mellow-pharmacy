#EN
### 💊 모아칼연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supply of Vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, as well as for the prevention of bone and teeth developmental disorders, rickets, and for calcium deficiency and other calcium supplementation.

### 🕒 모아칼연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 8 years and older and adults should take 2 capsules once, 1 to 2 times daily.

### ⚠️ 모아칼연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts; if you are an infant under 12 months of age; if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood); sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause); kidney disease; kidney stones; or severe renal insufficiency.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a lactating woman; a premature infant; an infant; a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction; hypoproteinemia; hyperlipoproteinemia; lipid metabolism disorders such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or patients who require careful administration of lipid emulsions; patients for whom a special risk is anticipated due to fat overload; or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
