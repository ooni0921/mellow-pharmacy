#EN

### 💊 큐어코프연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to relieve various cold symptoms such as sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills with shivering, fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 큐어코프연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 큐어코프연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, its ingredients, or other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, or if you have experienced asthma when taking them. Do not administer to infants under 3 months of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age; an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic constitution; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are breastfeeding; have a history of allergic symptoms such as drug-induced fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching; are elderly; are frail; or have a high fever.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have: liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, or gastroduodenal ulcers; persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have a history of ulcers or bleeding issues; or if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, immediately discontinue this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

### 💊 큐어코프연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解感冒的各种症状，如咽喉痛、咳嗽、痰、寒战（伴有发冷和发抖的症状）、发热、头痛、关节痛和肌肉痛。

### 🕒 큐어코프연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   15岁及以上成人，每次2粒，每日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 큐어코프연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   每日定期饮酒三杯或以上的人士在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。

曾有报告指出，极少数情况下可能发生严重的皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病（AGEP）、皮肤粘膜眼综合征（史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征）和中毒性表皮坏死松解症（莱尔综合征），这些反应可能致命。如果在服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。

请勿超过对乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000毫克）。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与其他含有对乙酰氨基酚的产品同时服用。
*   对本药或其成分有过敏史者，或服用其他解热镇痛剂或感冒药时曾出现哮喘者，以及未满3个月的婴儿（新生儿），请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师，如果您是：2岁以下的儿童；感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿及15岁以下的儿童；过敏体质者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；有过药物引起的过敏症状（如发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）者；老年人；体弱者；或有高热者。

服用本药前，如果您有肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡，或持续或复发性胃肠道问题（如胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛），或有溃疡、出血问题，或吸烟、患有哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续或复发超过一周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、或伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽，请咨询医生或药师。

服用5-6次后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用本药，并咨询医生或药师。

请勿长期连续服用。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
