* 이 약에 과민증 또는 경험자, 다른 해열진통제 또는 감기약 복용 시 천식 경험자, 만3개월 미만의 영아(갓난아기), 중증 또는 조절되지 않는 고혈압 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

5~6회 복용하여도 증상의 개선이 없을 경우 복용을 즉각 중지하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 콜록노즈연질캡슐 - Indications and Efficacy
* This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 콜록노즈연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 15 years of age or older: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

For children 8 to under 15 years of age: Take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 콜록노즈연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, stop taking it immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of Acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing Acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug or have experienced it before, if you have a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic predisposition; have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, physically weak, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are nursing; are a smoker; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough with excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts or recurs for more than a week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache; or are a child under 2 years of age.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

急性全身性発疹性膿疱症などの重篤な皮膚副作用が現れた場合は、服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
* この薬に対し過敏症のある方または経験者、他の解熱鎮痛剤またはかぜ薬服用時に喘息の経験がある方、生後3ヶ月未満の乳児（赤ちゃん）、重症または管理されていない高血圧の患者は、この薬を服用しないでください。

この薬を服用する前に、水痘またはインフルエンザに感染している、またはその疑いのある乳児（赤ちゃん）および15歳未満の小児、アレルギー体質の方、薬によるアレルギー症状（発熱、発疹、関節痛、喘息、かゆみなど）の経験がある方、肝臓疾患、腎臓疾患、心臓疾患、甲状腺疾患、糖尿病、高血圧、胃十二指腸潰瘍、緑内障（目の痛み、目がかすむなど）、排尿困難などのある方、高齢者（老人）、体が弱い方または高熱のある方、胸やけ、胃部不快感、胃痛などの胃腸の問題が持続または再発している方、または潰瘍、出血の問題がある方、妊婦または妊娠している可能性のある女性および授乳婦、喫煙者、喘息、慢性気管支炎、肺気腫、過剰な痰を伴う咳、1週間以上持続または再発する咳、慢性的な咳、発熱・発疹または持続的な頭痛を伴う咳の患者、2歳未満の小児は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
