# EN
### 💊 디큐펜프로연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for fever and pain due to common cold, low back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis (juvenile onset), osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), post-operative pain and headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 디큐펜프로연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   **Adults**:
    *   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, take 1-3 capsules (200-600 mg) per dose, 3-4 times daily, up to a maximum of 16 capsules (3,200 mg) per day.
    *   For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg/kg body weight per day, divided into 3-4 doses.
    *   For mild to moderate pain or common cold, take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) per dose, 3-4 times daily.
    *   For migraine, take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) per dose, not exceeding 2 capsules within 24 hours.

*   **Adolescents 12 years and older**: Take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) per dose, 3-4 times daily.

*   If body weight is less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg. It is advisable to avoid taking this medication on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 디큐펜프로연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
*   Taking this medication increases the risk of serious, potentially fatal, cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
*   This medication should not be taken by patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, those with a history of gastrointestinal ulcers or bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, a history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, patients experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, and pregnant women in the last trimester (last 3 months).
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis or impairment or a history thereof, kidney impairment or a history thereof, cardiac insufficiency or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly or a child, pregnant, or breastfeeding.
*   Do not exceed the recommended dose.
*   When used for the common cold, it should generally be taken for no more than 5 days. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), and do not take for fever for more than 3 days without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 디큐펜프로연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 功效与作用
*   本品用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、腰痛、痛经、类风湿性关节炎、幼年性类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎（退行性关节疾病）、术后疼痛和头痛、偏头痛、牙痛、肌肉痛、神经痛、强直性脊柱炎、急性痛风、银屑病（干鳞症）性关节炎、软组织损伤（扭伤、挫伤）、非关节性风湿病（肌腱炎、腱鞘炎、滑囊炎）。

### 🕒 디큐펜프로연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 用法与用量
*   **成人**:
    *   对于类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、软组织损伤、非关节性风湿病、急性痛风、银屑病性关节炎，每次服用1~3粒胶囊（200~600 mg），每日3~4次，每日最高剂量不超过16粒胶囊（3,200 mg）。
    *   对于幼年性类风湿性关节炎，每日按体重每公斤30~40 mg，分3~4次服用。
    *   对于轻度及中度疼痛或感冒，成人每次服用1~2粒胶囊（200~400 mg），每日3~4次。
    *   对于偏头痛，成人每次服用1~2粒胶囊（200~400 mg），24小时内不超过2粒胶囊。

*   **12岁以上青少年**: 每次服用1~2粒胶囊（200~400 mg），每日3~4次。

*   体重不足30公斤者，每日剂量不应超过500 mg，建议避免空腹服用。

### ⚠️ 디큐펜프로연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯或三杯以上且规律饮酒者，服用本品或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起胃肠道出血。
*   服用本品会增加发生严重且可能致命的心血管血栓事件、心肌梗死、卒中的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡及穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
*   对本品过敏者、有胃肠道溃疡及胃肠道出血史或正在发生者、严重血液异常者、严重肝功能障碍者、严重肾功能障碍者、严重心功能不全者、严重高血压者、支气管哮喘或有哮喘史者、阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎镇痛药引起的哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏反应等过敏史者、冠状动脉旁路移植术前后发生疼痛的患者、以及妊娠末期三个月内的孕妇，请勿服用本品。
*   服用本品前，有过敏史者、有血液异常史或正在发生者、有出血倾向者、肝硬化及肝功能障碍或有肝功能障碍史者、肾功能障碍或有肾功能障碍史者、心功能不全或心脏疾病患者、高血压患者、系统性红斑狼疮及混合性结缔组织病患者、溃疡性结肠炎患者、克罗恩病患者、老年人及儿童、孕妇、哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请勿超过推荐剂量服用。
*   感冒时服用，原则上不超过5天。未经医生或药剂师指导，用于止痛时成人不超过10天，儿童不超过5天；用于退热时不超过3天。
