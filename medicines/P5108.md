#EN
### 💊 락토프린정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for diarrhea, diarrhea accompanied by abdominal pain, indigestion, loose stools, and vomiting.

### 🕒 락토프린정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: Take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times daily, after meals. Maintain a dosing interval of at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 락토프린정 - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not take this medicine if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption; if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant; if you are breastfeeding; if you are a neonate; or if you are a child aged 7 years or younger.
Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergies to medicines, have persistent diarrhea with fever, bloody stools, or mucous stools, are elderly, have gastric or duodenal ulcers, or are hypersensitive to or have experienced allergic reactions to Yellow No. 4 (Tartrazine).
Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not take this medicine continuously for more than 3 days.
