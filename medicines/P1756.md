#EN
### 💊 서근령약교포 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory action in cases of sprains, contusions, muscle pain, joint pain, shoulder pain, and neuralgia.

### 🕒 서근령약교포 - Dosage and Administration
*   Clean and dry the affected area, then apply one patch daily.
*   Secure with bandages or adhesive plaster as needed.

### ⚠️ 서근령약교포 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer (urushiol) or similar allergens, or on open wounds.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar allergens, etc.) to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, suffer from severe moisture accumulation or maceration, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When using for children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Avoid prolonged use over large surface areas, as absorption of a large amount through the skin may lead to symptoms of intoxication.
*   This medicine is for external use only.
