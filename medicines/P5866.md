##EN
### 💊 프렌즈아이드롭아이스점안액 - Efficacy and Effect
* This medication is used for eye fatigue, as a tear supplement for dry eyes, discomfort when wearing hard or soft contact lenses, and blurred vision due to excessive eye discharge (mucus), etc.

### 🕒 프렌즈아이드롭아이스점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops into each eye, 5-6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 프렌즈아이드롭아이스점안액 - Precautions
* No information.
* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you experience severe eye pain, have glaucoma caused by eye drops, or have experienced allergic symptoms such as eye redness, itching, swelling, or rash.
* If your blurred vision does not improve after using this medication, or if symptoms do not improve after about 2 weeks of use, immediately discontinue use of this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* This medication is for ophthalmic use only, meaning it should only be instilled into the eyes.
* Do not let the tip of the container touch your eyelids or eyelashes. If it touches, the solution may become contaminated or cloudy with eye discharge or mold, etc. Also, do not use a cloudy solution.
* To prevent contamination, do not share this medication with others.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

##CN
### 💊 프렌즈아이드롭아이스점안액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解眼睛疲劳、辅助泪液缓解眼干、佩戴硬性或软性隐形眼镜时的不适感，以及因眼屎过多等引起的视力模糊。

### 🕒 프렌즈아이드롭아이스점안액 - 用法用量
* 每次2~3滴，每日5~6次滴入眼中。

### ⚠️ 프렌즈아이드롭아이스점안액 - 注意事项
* 无相关信息。
* 使用本药前，若出现严重眼痛、因眼药而患有青光眼、或曾对眼药产生过敏症状（如眼睛充血、瘙痒、肿胀、皮疹等），请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 如果点眼后视力模糊症状未改善，或使用约2周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止使用本药并咨询医生或药剂师。
* 本药仅供点眼用，即只能滴入眼中。
* 请勿让容器尖端接触眼睑及睫毛。若接触，药液可能因眼屎或霉菌等而受到污染或变浑浊。此外，请勿使用浑浊的药液。
* 为防止污染，请勿与他人共用。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
