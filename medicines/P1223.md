# EN
### 💊 하이비날골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during illness or convalescence (during or after recovery from illness), and in old age. It also aids in the alleviation of neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain) including low back pain and stiff shoulders, angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis symptoms. Furthermore, it is used for beriberi, eye fatigue, alleviation of hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, prevention of gum bleeding and nosebleeds, prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia, and the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 하이비날골드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 하이비날골드정 - Precautions
*   Overdose in children under 6 years of age can cause fatal iron poisoning, so keep out of reach of children.
*   This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body. Therefore, patients with phenylketonuria who need to regulate phenylalanine intake should not take this medicine.
*   Patients hypersensitive to this medicine, infants under 12 months of age, patients with hemochromatosis (a disorder of iron metabolism where iron is deposited in the liver and pancreas), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, or severe renal failure should not take this medicine.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis (colitis appearing in a specific part), cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, folate deficiency, or a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage).
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and can turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.
*   If there is no improvement in symptoms after taking for about 1 month, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN

*   本药用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠ㆍ哺乳期、病中ㆍ病后（患病期间或恢复后）体力下降时以及老年期维生素 E、B1、B2、B6、C的补充；缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎症状；治疗脚气病、眼睛疲劳、缓解因日晒、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑）；预防牙龈出血、鼻出血；预防及治疗缺铁性贫血以及锌的补充。

### 🕒 하이비날골드정 - 用法与用量
*   成人一次1片，一日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 하이비날골드정 - 注意事项
*   6岁以下儿童过量服用可能导致铁中毒性死亡，请置于儿童无法触及之处。
*   本药含有阿斯巴甜，其在体内代谢为苯丙氨酸。因此，苯丙酮尿症患者，需要限制苯丙氨酸摄入者，请勿服用本药。
*   对本药过敏的患者、12个月以下的婴儿、血色病（铁代谢异常导致铁沉积在肝脏、胰腺的疾病）、含铁血黄素沉着症、非缺铁性贫血或症状严重的肾功能不全患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性（特定部分出现的）结肠炎等胃肠疾病患者、心血管功能障碍、肾脏障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风或肾结石、叶酸缺乏症患者、有血栓（血管堵塞）倾向的患者，请务必咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液呈黄色，影响临床检查结果。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。
