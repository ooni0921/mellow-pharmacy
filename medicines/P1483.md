#EN

### 💊 비오알정(엔테로코쿠스페칼리스BIO-4R균)(수출용) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the improvement of conditions resulting from abnormal intestinal flora (e.g., due to antibiotic or chemotherapeutic agent administration), including regularization of bowel function, constipation, loose stools, abdominal bloating, and abnormal intestinal fermentation.

### 🕒 비오알정(엔테로코쿠스페칼리스BIO-4R균)(수출용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 tablet (100 mg) once, 3 times a day. The dosage may be appropriately adjusted depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 비오알정(엔테로코쿠스페칼리스BIO-4R균)(수출용) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Before taking this medicine, infants under 3 months of age, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Please adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after taking the medicine for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
