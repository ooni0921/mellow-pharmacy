#EN
### 💊 가두벌크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for insect bites, itchy skin, eczema, hives, dermatitis, heat rash, and chilblains.

### 🕒 가두벌크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 가두벌크림 - Precautions
* Do not use this medication on infants under 30 months old, around the eyes, on mucous membranes, on dermatitis caused by eczema or lacquer poisoning, or on wounded areas.
* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity to medicines or cosmetics, an allergic constitution, severe wetness or erosion (soreness), are a child, or have experienced hypersensitivity to propylene glycol.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medication is for external use only; do not take internally.
* Be careful not to let it get into your eyes. If it does get into your eyes, rinse immediately with water.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 가두벌크림 - 功效及效果
* 本药用于蚊虫叮咬、皮肤瘙痒、湿疹、荨麻疹、皮炎、痱子、冻疮。

### 🕒 가두벌크림 - 用法及用量
* 每日数次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ 가두벌크림 - 注意事项
* 30个月以下的幼儿、眼睛周围、粘膜、湿疹、漆树等引起的皮炎、伤口部位请勿使用本药。
* 使用本药前，如有对药物或化妆品过敏、过敏体质、严重湿润或糜烂（破溃）的患者、儿童、对丙二醇过敏或有经验的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 给儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
* 本药仅供外用，请勿内服。
* 注意不要进入眼睛，万一进入眼睛，请立即用水冲洗。
* 如果使用5~6天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。

* 定められた用法用量を厳守してください。
* 小児に使用する場合は、保護者の指導監督のもとに使用させてください。
* 本剤は外用のみに使用し、内服しないでください。
* 目に入らないように注意し、万一目に入った場合には、直ちに水で洗い流してください。
