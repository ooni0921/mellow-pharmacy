# EN
### 💊 베스티아제정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach, promoting digestion, and abdominal bloating caused by indigestion.

### 🕒 베스티아제정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 베스티아제정 - Precautions
*   No specific information.
*   Do not administer to patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, patients with hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4), and infants and young children under 7 years of age. Please strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

# CN

*   本药用于消化不良、食欲减退（食欲不振）、暴饮暴食、消化不良引起的胃胀、促进消化。

### 🕒 베스티아제정 - 用法与用量
*   成人一次服用 1~2 片，一日 3 次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 베스티아제정 - 注意事项
*   暂无信息
