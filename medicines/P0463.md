## EN
### 💊 그루타제정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for indigestion, decreased appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach, promoting digestion, and stomach distension due to indigestion.

### 🕒 그루타제정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older should take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 그루타제정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a child aged 7 years or younger, or if you have hypersensitivity or have experienced allergic reactions to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders, or if you are a patient requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions, or if you are a patient at special risk from fat overload.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking the medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

若服用约2周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
