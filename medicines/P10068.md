#EN
### 💊 베라정(방풍통성산건조엑스(4.6→1)) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for accompanying symptoms of hypertension (palpitations, stiff shoulders, hot flashes), obesity, swelling, and constipation in patients with abundant subcutaneous fat in the abdomen and a tendency for constipation.

### 🕒 베라정(방풍통성산건조엑스(4.6→1)) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, before or between meals.

### ⚠️ 베라정(방풍통성산건조엑스(4.6→1)) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypertension, cardiac or renal impairment, edema, are elderly, have symptoms of diarrhea, loose stools, anorexia, nausea, or vomiting, have a weak gastrointestinal system, are in a weakened state after illness, are significantly debilitated, sweat excessively, have circulatory disorders such as angina pectoris or myocardial infarction, urinary disorders, hyperthyroidism, have a history of rash, redness, or itching caused by this medication, or have diabetes.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medication, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
