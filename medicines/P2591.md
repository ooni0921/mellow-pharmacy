#EN
### 💊 래피콜데이앤나잇연질캡슐 - Indications and Efficacy
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shaky), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 래피콜데이앤나잇연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* There are daytime and nighttime formulations.

For the daytime formulation, adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older should take 2 capsules once, twice daily, 30 minutes after meals.

For the nighttime formulation, adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older should take 2 capsules once, once daily, 30 minutes after meals, 6 hours after taking the daytime formulation.

### ⚠️ 래피콜데이앤나잇연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker of three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age or a child under 2 years of age, are taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks, or are a breastfeeding woman (for nighttime formulation).

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty with urination, are frail or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery. (Nighttime formulation)

夜用型：在服用日用型6小时后，15岁及以上成人，每次2粒，每日1次，饭后30分钟服用。
