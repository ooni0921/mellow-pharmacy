* No information
* Do not use this product if you are hypersensitive to it.
* Before using this product, patients with severe eye pain or those who have experienced allergic reactions to eye drops (such as eye redness, itching, swelling, or conjunctival hyperemia) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This product is for ophthalmic use only.
* Do not use this product as a contact lens wetting solution, nor while wearing soft contact lenses.
* Contact lenses should be removed before using this product and can be reinserted 15 minutes after instillation.
