#EN
### 💊 렉센에스연고 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 렉센에스연고 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply directly to the affected area up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 렉센에스연고 - Precautions
* Do not use this medicine if the affected area is purulent (suppurating).
* Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medicine.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, use under the supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine is for anal use only and should not be used ophthalmically.
