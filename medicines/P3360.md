# EN
### 💊 이엑스로이드액(시트룰린말산염) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for functional asthenia.

### 🕒 이엑스로이드액(시트룰린말산염) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults: Take 1g per dose, three times a day.

Children aged 6 years and above: Take 1g per dose, twice a day.

Do not administer for more than 4 weeks.

### ⚠️ 이엑스로이드액(시트룰린말산염) - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not administer this medicine to children under 6 years of age.

Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
