#EN
### 💊 노즈원큐나잘스프레이(무향,멘톨향) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of nasal congestion and runny nose caused by colds, rhinitis, sinusitis, and nasal inflammation due to surgery.

### 🕒 노즈원큐나잘스프레이(무향,멘톨향) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 12 years and older, spray once into each nostril 3 times a day.

This medication is used in the following sequence:

Remove the protective cap.

If using this medication for the first time, press the pump several times to ensure the solution is dispensed uniformly.

Insert the spray into the nostril, and press the spray firmly to administer a single dose (one spray).

When spraying, breathe gently through the nose to allow the liquid to be well distributed.

Remove the spray before the pressure decreases.

After use, close the protective cap for storage.

### ⚠️ 노즈원큐나잘스프레이(무향,멘톨향) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to it, are under 6 years of age, have undergone surgery exposing the cerebral dura mater or transsphenoidal hypophysectomy, have closed-angle glaucoma, dry rhinitis or atrophic rhinitis, or are taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within 2 weeks.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medications (including nasal sprays for rhinitis) (e.g., fever, rash/redness, swelling, irritation, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), high blood pressure, cardiovascular disease, hyperthyroidism, diabetes, pheochromocytoma, prostatic hypertrophy, insomnia, dizziness, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When used for children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medication is for use as a nasal spray for rhinitis only; do not use in the eyes or ears.

Do not use continuously for more than 1 week.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, stop use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 12岁以上及成人，每日3次，每次向各鼻腔喷射1次。

本药物按以下顺序使用：

取下保护盖。

首次使用本药物时，请反复按压泵数次，直至药液均匀喷出。

将喷雾器插入鼻腔，用力按压喷雾器，喷射一次用量（1次）。

喷射时，请轻轻通过鼻子呼吸，以确保药液充分分布。

使用后，请盖好保护盖并妥善保管。

* 本剤は、かぜ、鼻炎、副鼻腔炎、手術による鼻腔の炎症による鼻づまり、鼻水の緩和に用います。

### 🕒 노즈원큐나잘스프레이(무향,멘톨향) - 用法・用量
* 12歳以上および成人は、1回1回ずつ、1日3回、各鼻腔（鼻の中）に噴霧します（スプレーします）。

本剤は以下の手順で使用します。

保護キャップを取り外します。

本剤を初めて使用する場合は、ポンプを数回押して薬液が均一に噴射されることを確認します。

鼻腔にスプレーを挿入し（入れ）、スプレーを強く押して1回投与量（1回）噴霧します（スプレーします）。

噴霧時（スプレーする時）は、液剤が良く分布するように鼻から軽く呼吸します。

圧力が低下する前にスプレーを取り除きます。

使用後は保護キャップを閉めて保管します。
