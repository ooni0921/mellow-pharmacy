#EN
### 💊 알리코비오틴정5밀리그램(비오틴) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for nail, toenail, or hair growth disorders caused by biotin deficiency.

### 🕒 알리코비오틴정5밀리그램(비오틴) - Dosage and Administration
* Take 1 tablet once daily before meals.

### ⚠️ 알리코비오틴정5밀리그램(비오틴) - Precautions
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
