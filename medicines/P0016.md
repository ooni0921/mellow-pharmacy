5~6회 복용하여도 증상이 좋아지지 않을 경우 복용을 즉각 중지하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 코푸시럽에스 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 코푸시럽에스 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: Take 20 mL once.
* Children aged 11 to under 15: Take 13 mL once.
* Children aged 8 to under 11: Take 10 mL once.
* Children aged 5 to under 8: Take 7 mL once.
* Children aged 3 to under 5: Take 5 mL once.
* Children aged 2 to under 3: Take 4 mL once.
* Administer 3-4 times a day, after meals and at bedtime.

### ⚠️ 코푸시럽에스 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug or have experienced it before, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or a child under 2 years of age.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: prone to allergies, have a caramel hypersensitivity or have experienced it before, have liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high fever, are frail, have heart disease and high blood pressure, are elderly, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, have glaucoma, have urination disorders, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, cough that lasts for more than 1 week or recurs, chronic cough, cough accompanied by fever, rash or persistent headache, or if you are a child under 2 years of age for whom this medication is absolutely necessary.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

* 3岁以上至5岁以下儿童一次5毫升。
* 2岁以上至3岁以下儿童一次4毫升。
* 每日3-4次，饭后及睡前服用。

### ⚠️ 코푸시럽에스 - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 对本药过敏或有过敏史的患者、3个月以下婴儿及2岁以下儿童请勿服用本药。

* 如果服用5-6次后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

* 儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
* 服用后可能会出现嗜睡，因此驾驶及操作机械时请注意。
