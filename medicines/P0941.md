### ⚠️ 기가에이연질캡슐 - 주의사항
* 정보 없음

정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

복용 중에는 음주하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 Giga A Soft Capsule - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 Giga A Soft Capsule - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 15 years and older, take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ Giga A Soft Capsule - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or toddler aged 3 months to under 2 years, a breastfeeding woman, an elderly person, have an allergic predisposition, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicine (fever, rash, joint pain, itching, etc.), have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), dysuria, have a high fever or are physically weak, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts for more than 1 week or recurs, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache, or if you are sensitive to or have a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 기가에이연질캡슐 - 用法与用量

* 无信息
* 对本药过敏者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、未满3个月的婴儿、正在服用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪稳定剂、抗帕金森病药等）或停药后2周内的人群，请勿服用本药。

如果服用5~6次后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服药并咨询医生或药师。

* 情報なし
* 本剤に過敏症の患者、妊婦または妊娠している可能性のある女性、生後3ヶ月未満の乳児、MAO阻害剤（抗うつ剤、抗精神病薬、気分安定剤、抗パーキンソン病薬など）を服用しているか、服用中止後2週間以内の人は、本剤を服用しないでください。

本剤を服用する前に、生後3ヶ月以上2歳未満の乳幼児、授乳婦、高齢者、アレルギー体質の人、薬によるアレルギー症状（発熱、発疹、関節痛、かゆみなど）を経験したことがある人、肝臓病、腎臓病、心臓病、甲状腺疾患、糖尿病、高血圧、緑内障（目の痛み、目のかすみなど）、排尿困難、高熱がある人や体が弱い人、喫煙者、喘息、慢性気管支炎、肺気腫、過剰な痰を伴う咳、1週間以上続くまたは再発する咳、慢性的な咳、発熱・発疹または持続的な頭痛を伴う咳の患者、黄色5号に過敏症のある人またはその既往歴のある人は、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
