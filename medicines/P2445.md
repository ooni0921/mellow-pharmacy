### 💊 테라플루콜드앤코프나이트타임건조시럽 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat (throat pain), chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 테라플루콜드앤코프나이트타임건조시럽 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 sachet three times a day, with at least 4 hours between doses, adhering to the prescribed minimum amount. Dissolve 1 sachet in approximately 250 mL of hot water (not boiling) and consume within 10-15 minutes.

### ⚠️ 테라플루콜드앤코프나이트타임건조시럽 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson Syndrome, and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma due to other cold medicines or antipyretic analgesics, are taking or have taken MAO inhibitors within the last 2 weeks, have breathing difficulties due to asthma attacks, emphysema, or chronic bronchitis, have glaucoma, prostate hypertrophy, lower urinary tract obstructive disease, stenosing peptic ulcer, pyloroduodenal obstruction, hypertension, severe blood disorder, severe liver impairment, severe heart dysfunction, epilepsy, pheochromocytoma, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history thereof, diabetes, are taking barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol, are under 18 years of age, are on a sodium-restricted diet, are breastfeeding, have glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase deficiency, sucrose-isomaltase deficiency or hereditary problems such as fructose intolerance and glucose-galactose malabsorption, have phenylketonuria (as it contains aspartame which is metabolized to phenylalanine and requires dietary restriction), or are elderly patients with confusion. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are prone to allergies, have Raynaud's syndrome, myasthenia gravis, liver impairment, kidney impairment, a history of peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendencies (platelet dysfunction), heart dysfunction, thyroid disease, porphyria, a history of pre-eclampsia, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are elderly, have a cough with a lot of phlegm, have obstructive vascular disease, angle-closure glaucoma, bronchitis and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), or are hypersensitive to Red 40. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully. Avoid driving or operating machinery as drowsiness, dizziness, blurred vision, and cognitive and psychomotor impairment may occur.

### 🕒 테라플루콜드앤코프나이트타임건조시럽 - 用法・用量
*   成人の方は1日3回、1回1包を、少なくとも4時間以上の間隔をあけて、定められた最小量を服用してください。1回1包を沸騰させない程度の熱いお湯約250 mLに溶かし、10〜15分以内に服用してください。

### ⚠️ 테라플루콜드앤코프나이트타임건조시럽 - 使用上の注意
*   毎日3杯以上定期的に飲酒する方が、この薬または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する際は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。非常にまれに、致死的となる可能性のある急性汎発性発疹性膿疱症、スティーブンス・ジョンソン症候群、中毒性表皮壊死融解症などの重篤な皮膚反応が報告されています。この薬の服用後に皮膚発疹または他の過敏反応の兆候が現れた場合は、直ちに服用を中止してください。アセトアミノフェンの一日最大量（4,000 mg）を超えて服用しないでください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。アセトアミノフェンを含む他の製品と併用しないでください。
