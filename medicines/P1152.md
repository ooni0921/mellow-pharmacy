# EN
### 💊 Densroll Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 Densroll Capsule - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Densroll Capsule - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Children under 15 years of age should not take this medicine.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, those with a history of hypersensitivity (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to cosmetics or eggs, or those with an allergic constitution should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Please strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
