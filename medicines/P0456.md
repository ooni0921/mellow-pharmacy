# EN
### 💊 넬슨이부프로펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to colds, low back pain, dysmenorrhea, post-operative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 넬슨이부프로펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults should take 1/2 to 1.5 tablets (200-600 mg) per dose, 3-4 times daily, with a maximum daily dose of 3200 mg.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3-4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or cold symptoms, adults should take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) per dose, 3-4 times daily.

Do not exceed the recommended dosage.

Adjust the dosage appropriately based on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 넬슨이부프로펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* Individuals who regularly consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid taking high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
* Do not take this medication if you have: hypersensitivity to this drug, current or history of gastrointestinal ulceration or bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, current or history of bronchial asthma, current or history of hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors), pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are in the last trimester (last 3 months) of pregnancy, are receiving high-dose methotrexate for chemotherapy, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you have: current or history of blood disorders, a tendency to bleed, current or history of liver cirrhosis or hepatic impairment, current or history of renal impairment, cardiac dysfunction or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly or a child, are pregnant, are breastfeeding, or have hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

制御されていない高血圧、うっ血性心不全、確立された虚血性心疾患、末梢動脈疾患、脳血管疾患を持つ患者は、高用量イブプロフェン（1日2,400 mg）の服用を避けるべきであり、心血管リスク因子（高血圧、高脂血症、糖尿病、喫煙など）を持つ患者が、高用量イブプロフェン（1日2,400 mg）の服用が必要な場合は、本剤を服用する前に医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
