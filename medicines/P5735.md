* 이 약은 육체피로, 임신 및 수유기, 병을 앓는 동안이나 회복 후의 체력저하 시, 노년기의 비타민 E, B2, B6의 보급에 사용합니다.

이 약은 함유된 비타민 등을 통해 말초혈행장애 및 갱년기시 어깨 및 목결림, 수족저림 및 수족냉증 증상의 완화에 사용합니다.

이 약은 함유된 비타민 등을 통해 구각염, 구순염, 구내염, 설염, 습진, 피부염 증상의 완화에 사용합니다.

### 🕒 투웰스정 - 용법 및 용량
* 만 15세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

* 정보 없음
* 이 약 및 콩 또는 땅콩에 과민증 환자, 대두유 과민증 환자 또는 경험자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 심한 증상의 신부전 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 투웰스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supplement vitamins E, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or convalescence when physical strength is low, and in old age.
* Through its contained vitamins, this medicine is used for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet.
* Through its contained vitamins, this medicine is used for the relief of symptoms such as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 투웰스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and individuals aged 15 years and older should take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 투웰스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybeans, or peanuts; if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to soybean oil; if you are an infant under 12 months of age; or if you have severe renal impairment.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have hypoproteinemia, heart and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, or patients who require careful administration of lipid emulsions, or patients related to fat overload.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Patients with a thrombotic predisposition or folate deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* 本剤は、含有されているビタミン等により、口角炎、口唇炎、口内炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮膚炎などの症状の緩和に用います。

### 🕒 투웰스정 - 用法及び用量
* 15歳以上及び成人は、1回1錠を1日1回服用します。

* 情報なし
* 本剤及び大豆又はピーナッツに過敏症の患者、大豆油過敏症の患者又は経験者、生後12ヶ月未満の乳児、重度の腎不全患者は、本剤を服用しないでください。
