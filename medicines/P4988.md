#EN
### 💊 더블비정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during or after recovery), growth period, and old age; for poor development of bones and teeth; prevention of rickets; relief of peripheral circulatory disorders and climacteric symptoms such as stiff shoulders, neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet; relief of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis; and for the supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 더블비정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years or older and adults, take 2 tablets once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 더블비정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.

*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant/toddler, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a history of or current kidney stones.

*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

*   May interfere with the detection of glucose in various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
