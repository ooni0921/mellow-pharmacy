#EN
### 💊 비맥스메타정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of rickets and poor development of bones and teeth, beriberi, eye fatigue, and for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, and for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 비맥스메타정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ 비맥스메타정 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or severe renal failure. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with heart or circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with renal impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient with a history of kidney stones, or a patient with folate deficiency. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
