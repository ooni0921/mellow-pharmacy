#EN

### 💊 코비코나잘액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy head symptoms caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 코비코나잘액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals 7 years of age and older, and adults: spray twice into each nostril per administration.
*   Administer with an interval of at least 3 hours between doses, and do not exceed 6 administrations per day.

### ⚠️ 코비코나잘액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not use this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are a child under 7 years of age, or an infant under 2 years of age.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, a history of allergic symptoms caused by medicines including nasal sprays for rhinitis, a history of propylene glycol hypersensitivity, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, or if you have hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, or glaucoma.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts for more than a week or recurs, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever and rash or persistent headache.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine is for nasal spray use only (sprayed into the nose); do not use in the eyes or ears.
*   Do not share with others to prevent contamination.
*   Do not overuse. It may cause nasal congestion.
*   Children should use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN

*   本药用于缓解普通感冒、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流鼻涕、打喷嚏、头部沉重感等症状。

### 🕒 코비코나잘액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - 用法与用量
*   7岁及以上儿童和成人：每次向鼻腔内喷2次。
*   每次给药间隔应至少3小时，每日给药次数不得超过6次。

### ⚠️ 코비코나잘액(옥시메타졸린염산염) - 注意事项
*   无相关信息。
*   对本药有过敏史者、7岁以下儿童以及2岁以下婴儿禁用本药。
*   使用本药前，如有过敏体质、曾因包括鼻炎用喷雾剂在内的药物引起过敏症状者、对丙二醇过敏史者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾病、青光眼患者，请咨询医生或药师。

*   如使用3天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用本药并咨询医生或药师。
*   本药仅用于鼻炎喷雾剂，即喷入鼻腔内使用，请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。
*   为防止污染，请勿与他人共用。
*   请勿过量使用。过量使用可能引起鼻塞。
*   儿童请在监护人的指导下使用。
*   请勿连续使用超过7天。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。
