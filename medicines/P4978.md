#EN
### 💊 푸레파인겔 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids, and for disinfection.

### 🕒 푸레파인겔 - Dosage and Administration
* Adolescents aged 15 years and older and adults should apply directly to the affected area up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 푸레파인겔 - Precautions
* No specific information
* Patients with heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, or urination difficulty due to prostatic hypertrophy should not use this medication.
* Patients currently receiving medical treatment should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medication.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not use in children.
* This medication is for anal use only; do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
