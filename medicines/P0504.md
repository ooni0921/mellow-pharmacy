# EN
### 💊 Hwangnyeok-aek - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for nutritional supplementation in cases of general debility, constitutional debility, physical fatigue, during and after illness (during or after recovery), loss of appetite, nutritional disorders, febrile and debilitating diseases, and during pregnancy and lactation.

### 🕒 Hwangnyeok-aek - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years and older should take 1 bottle once daily after meals. This medicine is for oral use only and should not be used for injection.

### ⚠️ Hwangnyeok-aek - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine or those with a history of allergies should consult a doctor or pharmacist. As this medicine is a herbal extract formulation, precipitation may sometimes occur, but the efficacy remains unchanged. Shake well before taking. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medicine may turn urine yellow and can affect clinical test values.

# CN

*   本药用于滋养强壮、体质虚弱、身体疲劳、病中病后（患病期间或恢复后）、食欲不振、营养不良、发热性、消耗性疾病、妊娠期、哺乳期等情况的营养补充。

### ⚠️ 황력액 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏者或有药物过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。本药为生药提取物配方制剂，偶有沉淀产生，但药效不变，请摇匀后服用。请严格遵守规定的用法用量。本药可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检测值。
