# EN

### 💊 아스피린프로텍트정100밀리그람 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to inhibit thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, to inhibit thrombus formation after Coronary Artery Bypass Graft (CABG) or Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA), and to reduce cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, and diabetes).

### 🕒 아스피린프로텍트정100밀리그람 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 tablet once daily.

This medicine is an enteric-coated tablet, so it can be taken before meals with sufficient water.

### ⚠️ 아스피린프로텍트정100밀리그람 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or other salicylates, peptic ulcer, aspirin asthma or a history thereof, hemophilia, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency, are concomitantly administered methotrexate at a dose of 15 mg/week or more, or are pregnant in the third trimester.

Do not take this medicine during the first and second trimesters of pregnancy unless absolutely necessary.

Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (such as renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, volume depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major bleeding events), hepatic impairment or a history thereof, cardiac dysfunction, blood disorders or a history thereof, bronchial asthma, are a pre-operative patient, an infant under 3 years old, have hypersensitivity and allergic diseases to analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or anti-rheumatic drugs, are elderly, have Glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, are under 14 years old with chickenpox or influenza infection, or have gout.

There is a risk of bleeding, so if you are undergoing surgery (including dental surgery), inform your doctor that you are taking this medicine.

Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medicine.

# CN

### 💊 아스피린프로텍트정100밀리그람 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于抑制心肌梗死、脑梗死、不稳定型心绞痛的血栓形成，冠状动脉旁路移植术(CABG)或经皮腔内冠状动脉成形术(PTCA)后抑制血栓形成，以及在高危患者（有缺血性心脏病家族史、高血压、高胆固醇血症、肥胖、糖尿病等复合危险因素的患者）中降低心血管风险。

### 🕒 아스피린프로텍트정100밀리그람 - 用法与用量
*   成人每次1片，每日1次服用。

本药为肠溶片，可饭前与足量水一同服用。

### ⚠️ 아스피린프로텍트정100밀리그람 - 注意事项
*   对本药或其他水杨酸制剂过敏的患者、消化性溃疡、阿司匹林哮喘或有此病史者、血友病、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、有出血倾向者、每周同时服用15毫克（15 mg/周）以上甲氨蝶呤的患者、以及妊娠第三期的孕妇，请勿服用本药。

妊娠第一和第二期，除非绝对必要，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如果您有肾功能障碍、心血管循环功能异常（如肾血管疾病、充血性心力衰竭、体液不足、重大手术、脓毒症或重大出血事件等）、肝功能障碍或有此病史者、心功能异常、血液异常或有此病史者、支气管哮喘、术前患者、3岁以下婴幼儿、对镇痛剂、消炎剂、抗风湿剂过敏或有其他过敏性疾病的患者、老年人、葡萄糖-6-磷酸脱氢酶(G6PD)缺乏症患者、瑞氏综合征、14岁以下患水痘或流感感染的患者、痛风患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

有出血风险，因此如果进行手术（包括牙科手术），请告知医生您正在服用本药。

请注意不要损坏本药的肠溶衣。
