* 정보 없음

#EN
### 💊 케토진첩부제(케토프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory treatment of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder joint, tendinitis, tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia (muscle pain), and swelling and pain after trauma (injury).

### 🕒 케토진첩부제(케토프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Remove the release liner from the patch and apply it to the affected area twice a day.

### ⚠️ 케토진첩부제(케토프로펜) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this drug in patients with hypersensitivity and photosensitivity to this drug, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks caused by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs), or hypersensitivity to ingredients that may cause cross-hypersensitivity reactions (tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, oxybenzone), pregnant women in the last 6 months of pregnancy, children under 15 years of age, or on eyes, mucous membranes, athlete's foot, or tinea.Consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this drug if you have bronchial asthma, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or have damaged skin/mucous membranes, eczema, or rashes.While using this drug and for up to 2 weeks after use, avoid outdoor activities regardless of the weather, and when going out, cover the applied area with clothing, sunglasses, hats, sunscreen, etc., to prevent exposure to ultraviolet rays.Do not use occlusive dressings.
