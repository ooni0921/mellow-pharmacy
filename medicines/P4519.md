#EN
### 💊 미가펜후레쉬연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
* This drug is used for fever and pain due to the common cold, lower back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), post-operative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 미가펜후레쉬연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 0.5 to 1.5 capsules (200 to 600 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times a day, up to a maximum of 8 capsules (3,200 mg) per day.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30 to 40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or cold, adults take 0.5 to 1 capsule (200 to 400 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times a day.

For migraine, adults take 0.5 to 1 capsule (200 to 400 mg) per dose, not exceeding 1 capsule (400 mg) over a 24-hour period.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Adjust appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 미가펜후레쉬연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* If you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this drug or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this drug may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
* Do not take this drug if you have a hypersensitivity to it, active gastrointestinal ulceration or bleeding, or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, hepatic impairment, renal impairment, heart failure, hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, allergy to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors) or a history thereof, or if you are experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, or if you are pregnant in your last trimester (last 3 months).

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis and/or hepatic impairment or a history thereof, renal impairment or a history thereof, heart failure or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly or a child, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.

* 对于类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、软组织损伤、非关节风湿性疾病、急性痛风、银屑病性关节炎，成人每次服用0.5至1.5粒胶囊（200至600毫克），每日3至4次，每日最高可服用8粒胶囊（3,200毫克）。

对于幼年性类风湿性关节炎，每日按体重每公斤30至40毫克，分3至4次服用。对于轻度及中度疼痛或感冒，成人每次服用0.5至1粒胶囊（200至400毫克），每日3至4次。

对于偏头痛，成人每次服用0.5至1粒胶囊（200至400毫克），24小时内不得超过1粒胶囊（400毫克）。

请勿超过推荐剂量。

请根据年龄、症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 미가펜후레쉬연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 注意事项

若年性関節リウマチには、1日体重1kgあたり30〜40 mgを3〜4回に分けて服用し、軽度及び中等度の疼痛や感冒には、成人で1回0.5カプセル〜1カプセル（200〜400 mg）を、1日3〜4回服用します。

片頭痛には、成人で1回0.5〜1カプセル（200〜400 mg）を服用し、24時間で1カプセル（400 mg）を超えないようにしてください。

推奨用量を超えて服用しないでください。

年齢、症状に応じて適切に増減してください。

### ⚠️ 미가펜후레쉬연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 注意事項
* 毎日3杯以上のアルコールを常飲している方が、本剤または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する場合は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。胃腸出血を引き起こす可能性があります。

本剤を服用する前に、以下の場合は医師または薬剤師に相談してください：過敏症の既往歴のある患者、血液疾患又はその既往歴のある患者、出血傾向のある患者、肝硬変及び／又は肝機能障害又はその既往歴のある患者、腎機能障害又はその既往歴のある患者、心機能不全又は心疾患、高血圧、全身性エリテマトーデス及び混合性結合組織病患者、潰瘍性大腸炎、クローン病、高齢者及び小児、妊婦、授乳婦。

推奨用量を超えて服用しないでください。

医師または薬剤師の指示なしに、疼痛には10日以上（成人）または5日以上（小児）、発熱には3日以上服用しないでください。
