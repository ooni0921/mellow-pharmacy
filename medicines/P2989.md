# EN
### 💊 테르미나겔(테르비나핀) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for superficial fungal infections of the skin caused by dermatophytes (tinea pedis, tinea cruris, tinea corporis) and tinea versicolor.

### 🕒 테르미나겔(테르비나핀) - Dosage and Administration
* Before application, thoroughly clean and dry the affected area (diseased part), then apply a thin layer once daily to the infected area and its surroundings. In cases of intertriginous infections (under the breasts, between fingers and toes, between buttocks, in the groin), gauze may be applied overnight. Symptom relief typically appears within 2-3 days, but the usual treatment period is 1 week.

### ⚠️ 테르미나겔(테르비나핀) - Precautions
* No information.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not use it. Before using this medication, patients with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist. This medication is for external use only. Do not use around the eyes.

# CN
### 💊 테르미나겔(테르비나핀) - 功效与作用
* 本品用于由皮肤癣菌引起的皮肤真菌感染（足癣、股癣、体癣）和花斑癣。

### 🕒 테르미나겔(테르비나핀) - 用法与用量
* 给药前，彻底清洁并擦干患处（病患部位），然后将本品薄薄地涂抹于感染部位及其周围，每日一次。对于擦烂性感染（乳房下、手指之间、脚趾之间、臀部之间、腹股沟），夜间可覆盖纱布使用。症状通常在2-3天内缓解，但常规治疗周期为1周。

### ⚠️ 테르미나겔(테르비나핀) - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 对本品过敏的患者请勿使用。在使用本品前，对丙二醇有过敏史的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、儿童应咨询医生或药剂师。本品仅供外用。请勿用于眼睛周围。
