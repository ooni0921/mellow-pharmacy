# EN

* This medication is used for pain relief and anti-inflammation for sprains, bruises, muscle pain, joint pain, fracture pain, back pain, stiff shoulders, neuralgia, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 제일핫파프(군납명:노닐산바닐아미드카타플라스마제) - Dosage and Administration
* Remove the polyester film from the medication surface and apply it to the affected area once or twice daily.

### ⚠️ 제일핫파프(군납명:노닐산바닐아미드카타플라스마제) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, or on wounded areas.
* Before using this medication, patients with hypersensitivity to medications or cosmetics, individuals with allergic constitutions, patients with severe wet eczema (weeping) or erosion, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* This medication is for external use only. Do not use for an extended period.
* Do not warm the application area (affected area) with a heating pad, electric blanket, or similar devices.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of administration, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。

* 请勿用加热垫、电热毯等加热敷贴患处（患病部位）。
