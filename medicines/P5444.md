### 💊 하벤파워골드캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 하벤파워골드캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: 2 capsules per dose. For individuals aged 8 years and older but under 15 years: 1 capsule per dose. Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 하벤파워골드캡슐 - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of a hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, stop taking it immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis appear, stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you: have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine or its ingredients; have experienced asthma when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines; are an infant under 3 months of age; or have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age; an infant (newborn baby) or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding, etc.) related to pseudoephedrine; have experienced hives, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraine, food allergies, or allergic reactions (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty urinating; are elderly; are frail or have a high fever; have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have a history of ulcers or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are breastfeeding; are currently receiving treatment from a doctor or dentist (e.g., taking other medications such as antidiabetic drugs, gout medications, arthritis medications, anticoagulants, steroids); or have a cough (cough due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be cautious when driving or operating machinery.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
