# EN
### 💊 마스원큐페이스트(트리암시놀론아세토니드) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of inflammation caused by chronic desquamative gingivitis, and refractory stomatitis and glossitis accompanied by erosion or ulceration.

### 🕒 마스원큐페이스트(트리암시놀론아세토니드) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to several times a day. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 마스원큐페이스트(트리암시놀론아세토니드) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this drug, or if you have tuberculous, viral, or other purulent infections of the oral cavity, syphilis, chickenpox, mycosis, bacterial skin infections, perioral dermatitis, rosacea, or gingivitis due to vitamin C deficiency. Before using this medicine, patients with or with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, infants and children, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not eat food immediately after use. Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.

# CN
### 💊 마스원큐페이스트(트리암시놀론아세토니드) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解慢性剥脱性龈炎、伴有糜烂或溃疡的难治性口腔炎和舌炎所致的炎症。

### 🕒 마스원큐페이스트(트리암시놀론아세토니드) - 用法用量
*   每日1至数次，取适量涂于患处。可根据年龄和症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 마스원큐페이스트(트리암시놀론아세토니드) - 注意事项
*   暂无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者，患有口腔结核性、病毒性、或其他化脓性感染、梅毒、水痘、真菌病、细菌性皮肤感染、口周皮炎、酒渣鼻，以及维生素C缺乏引起的龈炎患者，请勿使用本药。使用本药前，对丙二醇过敏或有相关病史的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、婴幼儿及儿童以及老年人，请咨询医生或药师。使用后请勿立即进食。请勿用于眼科用途。
