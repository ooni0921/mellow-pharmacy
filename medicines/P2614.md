### 💊 Senslieve Tab. - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for contraception.

### 🕒 Senslieve Tab. - Dosage and Administration
*   Take one tablet daily for 21 days according to the indicated sequence, followed by a 7-day tablet-free interval. It is advisable to take the tablet at the same time each day if possible. Menstruation usually occurs during the 7-day tablet-free interval, typically starting 2-3 days after taking the last tablet. If you continue to take this medicine, after a 7-day tablet-free interval, start taking tablets from a new pack; menstruation may not have ended before starting the new pack. For detailed information, refer to the full prescribing information.

### ⚠️ Senslieve Tab. - Warnings and Precautions
*   Oral contraceptive users should refrain from smoking, as it may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular events such as thrombosis. Smokers over 35 years of age should not take this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, tumors (e.g., breast cancer, endometrial cancer, pituitary tumor), thrombophlebitis, thromboembolism, cerebrovascular or coronary artery disease, undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding, severe hypertension, severe liver impairment, Dubin-Johnson syndrome, Rotor syndrome, jaundice, cholestasis, hepatitis, sickle cell anemia, severe diabetes mellitus, hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, porphyria, metabolic disorders, persistent pruritus during pregnancy, herpes gestationis, pemphigoid, otosclerosis, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, galactorrhea, hyperprolactinemia, migraine with aura such as scotoma or scintillating scotoma, pancreatitis, headaches with focal neurological symptoms such as aura, hepatitis C, congenital or acquired hypercoagulability, are a smoker over 35 years of age, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker, have hypertension, obesity, are a woman over 40 years of age, have epilepsy, migraine, a history of arterial disease or varicose veins, systemic lupus erythematosus, a history of breast cancer, depression or a history of depression, heart or renal failure, atypical hyperplasia of the mammary gland, a history of breast nodules, valvular heart disease, atrial fibrillation, a history of hyperlipidemia, gallbladder disease or gallstones, endometriosis, asthma or chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorders, diabetes mellitus, or migraine without aura. This medicine does not protect against HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases. If you have severe gastrointestinal disorders, absorption may be incomplete, so use additional contraceptive methods. This medicine is not applicable to pre-menarcheal and post-menopausal women. For detailed warnings and precautions, refer to the full prescribing information.

### 🕒 센스리베정 - 用法与用量
