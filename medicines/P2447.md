# EN
### 💊 알부골드연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, B12, C, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of reduced physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for peripheral circulatory disorders, relief of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, cold hands and feet during menopause, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder pain), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 알부골드연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years or older: Take 1 capsule per dose, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 알부골드연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, and infants under 3 months of age should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age; have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine); are pregnant, potentially pregnant, or breastfeeding; are a premature infant or infant; have gout or kidney stones; have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or require cautious administration of lipid emulsions; are at special risk due to fat overload; have a thrombotic diathesis (predisposition to thrombosis); have folic acid deficiency; or have hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) or a history of such hypersensitivity.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 알부골드연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本品用于缓解体力疲劳、妊娠哺乳期、病中病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、老年期维生素B1、B2、B6、B12、C、E的补充以及末梢血液循环障碍、更年期肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木、手脚冰凉（手脚发冷）、神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩痛等）的症状，以及脚气、眼睛疲劳。

### 🕒 알부골드연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   成人及8岁以上儿童，每次1粒，每日3次。

### ⚠️ 알부골드연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   对本品或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿请勿服用本品。

在服用本品前，以下人群请咨询医生或药师：未满1岁的婴儿；高草酸尿症患者（尿液中草酸盐排出过多的状态）；孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女；早产儿、婴幼儿；痛风或肾结石患者；高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需要慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者；因脂肪过载预计有特殊风险的患者；有血栓（血管堵塞）倾向的患者；叶酸缺乏症患者；对日落黄FCF（黄色5号）过敏或有相关过敏史的患者。

请严格遵守用法用量。

儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

本品可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
