#EN
### 💊 디센플러스카타플라스마(디클로페낙나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis and tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, elbow pain (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 디센플러스카타플라스마(디클로페낙나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply one patch to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 디센플러스카타플라스마(디클로페낙나트륨) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not use this medication in children under 18 years of age, pregnant women in their third trimester or who have passed 5 months of pregnancy, breastfeeding women, individuals with a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, patients with or a history of aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) induced asthma and hypersensitivity reactions, or patients with peptic ulcers.
*   Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman (first and second trimesters), a woman who has difficulty conceiving or is undergoing infertility testing, an elderly person, a patient with bronchial asthma, or an individual with or a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol and sodium pyrosulfite (sulfite sensitivity).
*   Do not use continuously for more than 2 weeks.
*   Do not apply to eyes and mucous membranes, or to lesioned skin (e.g., exudative dermatitis, eczema, infected lesions, burns, wounds).
*   Do not use with occlusive dressings without medical supervision.

#CN

### ⚠️ 디센플러스카타플라스마(디클로페낙나트륨) - 注意事项
*   无可用信息
*   18岁以下儿童、妊娠3期及妊娠5个月以上的孕妇、哺乳期妇女、对本药有过敏史者、阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药(NSAID)诱发的哮喘及过敏反应患者或有病史者、消化性溃疡患者请勿使用本药。
*   使用本药前，妊娠期妇女（妊娠1期和2期）、备孕困难或正在接受不孕检查的女性、老年人、支气管哮喘患者、对丙二醇和焦亚硫酸钠（亚硫酸盐敏感性）有过敏史或过敏反应者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请勿连续使用2周以上。
*   请勿用于眼睛、粘膜及有病变的皮肤（渗出性皮炎、湿疹、感染病灶、烧伤、伤口等）。
*   未经医生指导，请勿采用密闭敷料法使用。
