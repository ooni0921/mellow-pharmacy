# EN

### 💊 제뉴원트리메부틴말레산염정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for digestive functional disorders (abdominal pain, indigestion, nausea, vomiting) in cases of gastroesophageal reflux and hiatal hernia, gastritis and duodenitis, gastric and duodenal ulcers, as well as irritable bowel syndrome and spastic colon, and pediatric conditions (habitual vomiting, non-infectious intestinal transit disorders (constipation, diarrhea)).

### 🕒 제뉴원트리메부틴말레산염정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets (100 to 200 mg) three times a day, before meals. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 제뉴원트리메부틴말레산염정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine. Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
