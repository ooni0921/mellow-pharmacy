#EN
### 💊 임팩타민프리미엄정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supply of vitamins B1, B2, B6, E, and C for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness (during illness or after recovery) when physical strength is depleted, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 임팩타민프리미엄정 - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years and older and adults take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, once daily, after a meal.

### ⚠️ 임팩타민프리미엄정 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug and infants under 12 months should not take this drug.
* Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, a thrombotic predisposition, or a folic acid deficiency.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* It may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test results by changing the urine to a yellow color.
* If symptoms do not improve after taking this drug for about 1 month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 임팩타민프리미엄정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于补充身体疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时以及老年期的维生素B1、B2、B6、E、C。同时，还用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状，以及用于脚气病、眼睛疲劳和补充锌。

### 🕒 임팩타민프리미엄정 - 用法用量
* 12岁以上儿童及成人，每次1~2片，每日1次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 임팩타민프리미엄정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者以及未满12个月的婴儿不应服用本药。
* 服用本药前，患有高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、心血管功能障碍、肾脏障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风或肾结石、有血栓倾向的患者以及叶酸不足的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
* 在进行各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变为黄色，从而影响临床检查结果。
* 如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
