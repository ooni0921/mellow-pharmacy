#EN
### 💊 내간애액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for liver diseases.

### 🕒 내간애액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 10-30 mL daily, divided between meals.
* Dilute 10 mL of this medicine in about half a glass of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 내간애액 - Precautions
* Patients with kidney disease, myocardial infarction, or those with a history of such conditions, should not take this medicine.
* Patients with a history of hypersensitivity to caramel should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medicine is for oral use only; do not use for injection.
* Do not use as an antidote for alcohol poisoning.
* If there is no improvement after approximately one month of taking the medicine, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 내간애액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于肝脏疾病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 내간애액 - 用法与用量
* 成人每日分次服用10~30毫升，餐间服用。
* 将本药10毫升稀释（变淡）在约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 내간애액 - 注意事项
* 肾脏疾病、心肌梗塞患者及有相关病史者请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，对焦糖过敏的患者或有相关病史者请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 本药仅供内服，不可用于注射。
* 请勿用作酒精中毒的解毒剂。
* 服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。

* 本剤は内服用のみに使用し、注射用としては使用しないでください。
