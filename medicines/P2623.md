# EN
### 💊 노즈케어점비액 - Efficacy and Effect
*   This medicine is used to relieve nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy head caused by acute rhinitis, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 노즈케어점비액 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 7 years and older should spray 1 to 2 times into each nostril per dose. A dosing interval of at least 3 hours should be maintained, and the medicine should not be used more than 6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 노즈케어점비액 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to it, are an infant under 7 years of age, or are currently taking (using) MAO inhibitors (such as antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have discontinued their use within the last 2 weeks. Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, have high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, glaucoma, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache. Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration. This medicine is for intranasal use only for rhinitis; do not use it in the eyes or ears. Do not use continuously for more than 7 days. Drowsiness may occur after use, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

# CN
### 💊 노즈케어점비액 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解由急性鼻炎、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流涕、打喷嚏、头重感。

### 🕒 노즈케어점비액 - 用法用量
*   成人及7岁以上儿童，每次向鼻腔内喷雾1至2次。两次给药间隔应至少3小时，每日不超过6次。

### ⚠️ 노즈케어점비액 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   如果您对此药过敏、是未满7岁的幼儿，或正在服用（使用）MAO抑制剂（如抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪稳定剂、抗帕金森病药等）或在停用MAO抑制剂后2周内，请勿使用本药。在使用本药前，如果您有过敏体质、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾病、青光眼、吸烟、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过量痰液的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热·皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药仅限用于鼻炎喷雾剂，请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。请勿连续使用超过7天。使用后可能会出现嗜睡，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
