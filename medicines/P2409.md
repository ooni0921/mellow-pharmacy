#EN
### 💊 덱시탑시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for fever reduction in cases of fever caused by acute upper respiratory tract infection.

### 🕒 덱시탑시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 6 months and older should take 0.4-0.6 mL/kg (5-7 mg/kg) per dose, as needed, every 4-6 hours, not exceeding a maximum of 4 doses (28 mg/kg) per day.

For children weighing 30 kg or less, do not exceed a maximum of 25 mL (300 mg) per day.

Refer to the approved product information for recommended dosages by age group.

### ⚠️ 덱시탑시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication increases the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, heart failure, hypertension, bronchial asthma, asthma, hives, or allergies caused by aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics (or those with a history thereof), patients undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding mothers should not take this medication.

Before taking this medication, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, cardiac dysfunction, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, gastric cancer, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly, a child, or taking low-dose aspirin for the prevention of myocardial infarction or stroke.

#CN
### 💊 덱시탑시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 功效与作用
*   本药品用于治疗急性上呼吸道感染引起的发热，作为退烧剂使用。

### 🕒 덱시탑시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 用法用量
*   6个月及以上儿童，每次按0.4~0.6 mL/kg (5~7 mg/kg)服用，必要时每隔4~6小时服用一次，每日最多不超过4次 (28 mg/kg)。

体重30公斤及以下的儿童，每日最大剂量不超过25毫升 (300毫克)。

各年龄段的推荐剂量请参考批准的产品说明书。

### ⚠️ 덱시탑시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯或以上者，在服用本药品或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药品会增加可能致命的严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死、中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
*   对本药品过敏的患者，患有消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心力衰竭、高血压的患者，支气管哮喘、因阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛药引起哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏的患者或有过敏史者，冠状动脉旁路移植术前后疼痛治疗的患者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，请勿服用本药品。

在服用本药品之前，患有过敏症、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全、高血压、系统性红斑狼疮、混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、胃癌、克罗恩病、老年人、儿童，以及为预防心肌梗死或中风而服用低剂量阿司匹林的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
