#EN
### 💊 파워콜캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, muscle pain, joint pain).

### 🕒 파워콜캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults (15 years and older) should take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 파워콜캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can rarely be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months old, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

* Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, a child under 2 years old, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), urination difficulty, are elderly, weak or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or ulcers, bleeding problems, are pregnant or likely to be pregnant, breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts more than 1 week or recurs, chronic cough, a cough accompanied by fever, rash or persistent headache, or have experienced hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

#CN
### 💊 파워콜캡슐 - 功效与效果
* 本药用于缓解普通感冒的各种症状（流鼻涕、鼻塞、打喷嚏、喉咙痛、咳嗽、咳痰、寒战、发热、头痛、肌肉痛、关节痛）。

### 🕒 파워콜캡슐 - 用法与用量
* 成人（15岁以上）每次服用2粒胶囊，每日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 파워콜캡슐 - 注意事项
* 如果您每日饮酒超过三杯，并且正在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能会导致肝损伤。
* 曾有报告指出，极少数情况下可能发生致命的严重皮肤反应，例如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病（AGEP）、皮肤粘膜眼综合征（史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征）和中毒性表皮坏死松解症（莱尔综合征）。若服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状，请立即停止服用。
* 每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量请勿超过最大剂量（4,000 mg）。过量服用可能导致肝损伤。请勿与其他含有对乙酰氨基酚的产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏者，服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾出现哮喘者，3个月以下婴儿，以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

* 服用本药前，如果婴儿或15岁以下儿童感染或疑似感染水痘或流感，2岁以下儿童，过敏体质者，曾出现过敏症状（发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等），肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者，老年人，体弱者或高烧患者，胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，吸烟者，哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽，持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽，慢性咳嗽，伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽患者，或对黄色5号有过敏史或过敏者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

* 如果服用5～6次后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。

* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

* 服用后可能会出现困倦，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

* 毎日3杯以上のアルコール飲料を定期的に摂取している方が本剤または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する際は、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。
