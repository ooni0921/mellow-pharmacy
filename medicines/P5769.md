#EN
### 💊 인코드아이연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins A, B1, and B2 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after recovery from illness when physical strength is low, and in old age.
*   Through the vitamins it contains, this medicine is used to alleviate night blindness, beriberi, eye fatigue, dry eyes, as well as neuralgia, myalgia, back pain, shoulder stiffness, joint pain, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 인코드아이연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years and older, take 2 capsules once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 인코드아이연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Pregnant women taking more than 5,000 IU of vitamin A per day may cause congenital malformations. Women who are within the first three months of pregnancy or who may be pregnant should not take this medicine.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, patients with a history of hypersensitivity to soybean oil, patients with hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, and infants under 3 months of age should not take this medicine.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year old, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, premature infant, young child, patient with a history of hypersensitivity to Red No. 40, patient with fat metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsions, or patient with fat overload syndrome.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of use, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   The vitamin A contained in this medicine is sufficiently supplied through a normal diet, so the dose for adjunctive therapy should not exceed 5,000 IU per day.
*   Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.
*   Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   本品通过所含维生素，用于缓解夜盲症、脚气病、眼睛疲劳、眼睛干涩，以及神经痛、肌肉痛、腰痛、肩周炎等关节痛，口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

### 🕒 인코드아이연질캡슐 - 用法用量

### ⚠️ 인코드아이연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   孕妇每日服用维生素A超过5,000 IU可能引起先天性畸形。妊娠3个月以内或有可能怀孕的女性请勿服用本品。
*   对本品过敏者、对大豆油过敏史者、对大豆或花生过敏者、3个月以下婴儿请勿服用本品。
*   服用本品前，以下人群请咨询医生或药师：1岁以下婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、对红色40号成分过敏史者、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症、胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者、需慎重输注脂肪乳剂的患者、脂肪超负荷相关患者。
*   若服用约1个月后症状无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
*   本品所含维生素A通过正常饮食已充足供给，因此辅助治疗剂量每日不应超过5,000 IU。
*   儿童应在监护人的指导和监督下服用本品。
*   本品可使尿液呈黄色，可能影响临床检查结果。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
