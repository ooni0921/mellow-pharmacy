#EN

### 💊 마그벤맥스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of declining physical strength during or after illness (while suffering from illness or during recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, menopausal shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness and coldness in hands and feet, as well as for neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그벤맥스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years and older and adults should take 1 capsule at a time, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그벤맥스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are allergic to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal dysfunction.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders (such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis), or are a patient who needs cautious administration of lipid emulsions, are hypersensitive to Red No. 40, have folate deficiency, or are a patient for whom a special risk is anticipated due to fat overload.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
