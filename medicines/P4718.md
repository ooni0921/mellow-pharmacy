* This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for hair loss.

### 🕒 다모판텐정100밀리그램(덱스판테놀) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day.

The treatment period is 6 weeks.

### ⚠️ 다모판텐정100밀리그램(덱스판테놀) - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, are under 19 years of age, have conditions other than alopecia, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, and those who have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

If symptoms do not improve after 6 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

To avoid drug interactions, inform your doctor or pharmacist about your current treatment status.

If any adverse reactions occur while taking this medication, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
