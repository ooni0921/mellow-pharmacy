#EN
### 💊 씨엠지뷰플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins A, E, B1, B2 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery) in old age, alleviation of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), peripheral circulatory disorders and alleviation of symptoms such as stiff shoulders and neck, numbness and coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet) during menopause, alleviation of symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (lumbago, stiff shoulders, etc.), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation in the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 씨엠지뷰플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 2 capsules once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 씨엠지뷰플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day may cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medicine if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or are likely to be pregnant.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, a nursing infant under 3 months of age, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergy to soybean oil, or are hypersensitive to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a nursing infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs to be administered lipid emulsions carefully, or a patient hypersensitive to Yellow No. 5 and Yellow No. 203.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

The Vitamin A contained in this medicine is sufficiently supplied through a normal diet, so the dosage for adjunctive therapy should not exceed 5,000 IU per day.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

Patients with thrombogenic predisposition should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
