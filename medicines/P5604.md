#EN
### 💊 툴스속편정 - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and sour belching.

### 🕒 툴스속편정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 툴스속편정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, undergoing dialysis, have hypermagnesemia, hyperphosphatemia, constipation, intestinal stricture, acute appendicitis, are a woman in the first trimester of pregnancy, or a child under 7 years of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, have experienced hypermagnesemia or magnesium deficiency, or have kidney impairment or a history of kidney disease.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 2 weeks, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Swallow quickly when taking.

Be aware of aluminum accumulation in nerve and bone tissues.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
