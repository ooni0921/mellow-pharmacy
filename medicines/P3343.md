#EN
### 💊 하벤프레쉬나잘스프레이 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for moisturizing and protecting the nasal mucosa.

### 🕒 하벤프레쉬나잘스프레이 - Dosage and Administration
* For each use, spray 1-2 times into each nostril (left and right), 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 하벤프레쉬나잘스프레이 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to it.
* If you have any other medical conditions, consult your doctor or pharmacist before using this medication.
* This medication is for nasal use only; do not ingest it or use it in the eyes or ears.
* If using other products applied to the nose concurrently, allow an interval of at least 30 minutes, and use this medication last.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
