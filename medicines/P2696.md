#EN
### 💊 성광아시클로버크림 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for herpes simplex virus infections (including initial and recurrent genital herpes and labial herpes).

### 🕒 성광아시클로버크림 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply to the affected area 5 times a day at approximately 4-hour intervals. Treatment should be continued for 5 days, and if not cured after 5 days of use, it may be used for an additional 5 days.

### ⚠️ 성광아시클로버크림 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are a lactating woman, or are a child.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an immunodeficient patient (e.g., AIDS or bone marrow transplant), pregnant or possibly pregnant, or have known hypersensitivity to propylene glycol.
*   Treatment should be started as early as possible, and in recurrent cases, it should be started at the first appearance of prodromal symptoms or lesions.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Do not use this medicine for preventing infection to others or preventing recurrent herpes simplex virus infection.
*   Do not dilute this medicine or use it as a base for mixing with other drugs.
*   This medicine is for external use only.
*   Do not use for ophthalmic purposes, on the cornea, or mucous membranes.
*   To prevent this medicine from spreading to other parts of your body or infecting others, apply it while wearing a thimble or rubber gloves.

#CN
### 💊 성광아시클로버크림 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于单纯疱疹病毒感染症（包括初期和复发性生殖器疱疹以及唇疱疹）。

### 🕒 성광아시클로버크림 - 用法及用量
*   每日5次，每次间隔约4小时，涂抹于患处（病患部位）。治疗应持续5天，如果使用5天后仍未痊愈，可以再使用5天。

### ⚠️ 성광아시클로버크림 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者、哺乳期妇女、儿童请勿使用本药。
*   在使用本药之前，免疫缺陷患者（如艾滋病或骨髓移植等）、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、对丙二醇有过敏史或过敏反应者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   治疗应尽早开始，对于复发性病例，应在出现前驱症状或病灶首次出现时开始。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   请勿将本药用于预防他人感染或预防复发性单纯疱疹病毒感染。
*   请勿稀释本药或将其用作与其他药物混合的基质。
*   本药仅限外用。
*   请勿用于眼科用途、角膜或黏膜。
*   为防止本药扩散到身体其他部位或传染给他人，请戴指套或橡胶手套涂抹。
