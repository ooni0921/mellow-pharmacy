#EN
### 💊 아드레큐에이정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the supply of Vitamin C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery); for the alleviation of pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight, skin diseases, etc.; for the prevention of gum bleeding and nosebleeds (epistaxis); for the alleviation of symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; and as a nutritional tonic.

### 🕒 아드레큐에이정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years and older and adults should take 1 tablet per dose, 1 to 2 times a day.

### ⚠️ 아드레큐에이정 - Precautions
*   No specific information.
*   Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (a state where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, or hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who receives a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), pregnant or possibly pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant/young child, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gout, or folic acid deficiency.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   May interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests.
