# 벤포비파워정

##EN
### 💊 벤포비파워정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during illness or convalescence (during or after illness), during growth, and in old age for supplementation of vitamins D, B1, B2, B6. It is also used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain) (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), relief of angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 벤포비파워정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents 12 years of age and older: take 1 tablet once a day after a meal.

### ⚠️ 벤포비파워정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, a patient with hypersensitivity to soybeans or peanuts, a person with a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, an infant under 12 months of age, a patient with hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), a patient with sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), a patient with kidney disease, or a patient with severe renal insufficiency.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcers, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with kidney impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient with or a history of kidney stones, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient for whom lipid emulsions should be administered cautiously, a patient with special risks due to fat overload, an infant under 1 year of age, a child who receives a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, or a patient with folic acid deficiency.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
