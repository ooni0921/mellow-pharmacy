#EN
### 💊 콜대원콜드에스시럽 - Indications and Efficacy
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 콜대원콜드에스시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older: 1 sachet (20 mL) per dose.
* For individuals aged 11 years or older but under 15 years: 2/3 sachet per dose.
* For individuals aged 7 years or older but under 11 years: 1/2 sachet per dose.
* For individuals aged 3 years or older but under 7 years: 1/3 sachet per dose.
* For individuals aged 2 years or older but under 3 years: 1/4 sachet per dose.
* Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 콜대원콜드에스시럽 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you: are hypersensitive to this medicine or its components; are an infant under 3 months of age; have experienced asthma after taking this medicine, its components, other antipyretic analgesics, or cold medicines; or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: an infant or young child under 2 years of age; an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; someone with a predisposition to conditions such as hives, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraine, or food allergies; someone with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma, or difficulty urinating; an elderly person; a physically weak person; someone with a high fever; someone with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or with ulcers or bleeding issues; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a nursing mother; or someone with a cough (due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take this medicine continuously for a long period.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine; therefore, exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
