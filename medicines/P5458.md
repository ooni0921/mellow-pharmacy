#EN
### 💊 쏙콜에이연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 쏙콜에이연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years and over: Take 2 capsules, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 쏙콜에이연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming 3 or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
*   Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.
*   Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or its ingredients, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age; an infant (newborn) or child under 15 years of age who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) related to pseudoephedrine; have a history of hives, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraine, food allergies, or allergic reactions (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric/duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are a nursing mother; or have a cough (due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
