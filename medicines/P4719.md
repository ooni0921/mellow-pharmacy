### 💊 엘앤씨메가펜정(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 엘앤씨메가펜정(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - Dosage and Administration
*   Take 1 to 3 tablets (0.3 to 1.0 g) orally, 3 to 4 times a day.

A maximum of 12 tablets (4 g) can be administered per day.

The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age, disease, and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘앤씨메가펜정(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history of it, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, if you are elderly, pregnant or breastfeeding, or if you are on long-term warfarin therapy.

*   一次服用1至3片 (0.3至1.0 克)，每日3至4次，口服。

可根据年龄、疾病和症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 엘앤씨메가펜정(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒量超过三杯的规律饮酒者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能会导致肝损伤。

极少数情况下报告有致命性的严重皮肤反应，如急性全身性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。如果服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状，请立即停止服用。

每日乙酰氨基酚服用量请勿超过最大剂量 (4,000 mg)。这可能会导致肝损伤。请勿与含有乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
*   对本药过敏、患有消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全的患者，或患有阿司匹林哮喘（由非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘发作）或有相关病史的患者，以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传问题的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，患有或有肝功能障碍病史、肾功能障碍病史、消化性溃疡病史、血液异常病史、出血倾向、心功能异常、过敏史、支气管哮喘的患者，以及老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女、长期服用华法林的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
