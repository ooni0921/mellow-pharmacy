#EN
### 💊 잇케어페이스트 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the alleviation of various symptoms caused by gingivitis (gum inflammation) and alveolar pyorrhea (periodontitis), such as gum redness (congestion and reddening), swelling, pus, and bad breath.

### 🕒 잇케어페이스트 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount, approximately 0.3 g (about 0.5 cm), onto a toothbrush and massage the gums twice daily (morning and evening). After use, you may rinse lightly with water.

### ⚠️ 잇케어페이스트 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Before using this medication, individuals with a history of allergies to medicines should consult a doctor or pharmacist. When administering to children, administer under the supervision and guidance of a guardian. For oral use only.

#CN
### 💊 잇케어페이스트 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于缓解牙龈炎、牙槽脓漏引起的各种症状（牙龈发红（充血变红）、肿胀、化脓、口臭）。

### 🕒 잇케어페이스트 - 用法及用量
*   每次取适量约0.3克（约0.5厘米）涂抹于牙刷上，每日2次（早、晚）按摩牙龈。使用后可用水轻轻漱口。

### ⚠️ 잇케어페이스트 - 注意事项
*   暂无信息。
*   在使用本药前，对药物有过敏史者请咨询医生或药剂师。儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。仅供口腔使用。
