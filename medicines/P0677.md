# EN
### 💊 헤모케어정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for various symptoms associated with varicose veins (such as heaviness in the lower limbs), phlebitis, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 헤모케어정 - Dosage and Administration
* For venous disorders, take 1 tablet once, twice daily (morning, evening) during meals. For hemorrhoid symptoms, take 1 tablet once, 3 to 4 times daily during meals.

### ⚠️ 헤모케어정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hyperthyroidism, are taking MAO inhibitors, have malignant hypertension, severe tachycardia, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are a child. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe arterial hypertension, a bleeding diathesis, or a history of hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4). Athletes using this medication should be aware that it may result in a positive doping test. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of use, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 헤모케어정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗由静脉曲张引起的下肢沉重感、静脉炎以及痔疮相关的各种症状。

### 🕒 헤모케어정 - 用法与用量
* 静脉疾病患者，一次1片，一日2次（早、晚），餐中服用。痔疮症状患者，一次1片，一日3~4次，餐中服用。

### ⚠️ 헤모케어정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 患有甲状腺功能亢进症、正在服用MAO抑制剂的患者、恶性高血压患者、严重心动过速患者、孕妇和哺乳期妇女以及儿童请勿服用本药。服用本药前，患有严重动脉性高血压、有出血倾向的患者、对黄色4号（柠檬黄）过敏或有过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。使用本药的运动员请注意，兴奋剂检测中可能会出现阳性反应。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请咨询医生或药师。

# JP

### 🕒 헤모케어정 - 用法及び用量
