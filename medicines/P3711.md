# EN
### 💊 솔루샷노즈연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 솔루샷노즈연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older and adults, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 솔루샷노즈연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of a hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine or its ingredients, have experienced asthma when taking other fever reducers/pain relievers or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 2 years of age; an infant or child under 15 years of age who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) occur in relation to pseudoephedrine; have an allergic constitution; have a history of drug-induced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, physically weak, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or breastfeeding; or are a smoker, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 对本药或其成分有过敏史或过敏经历者、服用其他解热镇痛剂或感冒药时曾发生哮喘者、未满3个月的婴儿、重度或未受控制的高血压患者请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师：不满2岁的儿童；感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿及不满15岁的儿童；如果出现与伪麻黄碱成分相关的缺血性结肠炎症状（如：剧烈腹痛、直肠出血等）；过敏体质者；有药物过敏症状（例如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）史者；有肝脏疾病、肾脏疾病、心脏疾病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（例如：眼睛疼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难等病症者；老年人；体弱或高热者；胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠道问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女；吸烟者；患有哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过量痰液的咳嗽、持续或反复超过1周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽患者。
