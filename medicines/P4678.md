* 이 약은 육체피로, 임신·수유기, 병중·병후(병을 앓는 동안이나 회복 후)의 체력 저하 시, 발육기, 노년기의 비타민A, D, B1, B2, C의 보급과 눈의 건조감의 완화, 야맹증(밤에 잘 못보는 증상), 뼈, 이의 발육 불량, 구루병의 예방에 사용합니다.

### 🕒 쎈파워비타연질캡슐 - 용법 및 용량
* 만 8세 이상 또는 성인은 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 쎈파워비타연질캡슐 - 주의사항
* 정보 없음
* 이 약 또는 대두유, 콩, 땅콩에 과민증 환자, 만 3개월 미만의 젖먹이, 고칼슘혈증(혈액중에 칼슘이 과잉으로 존재하는 상태), 유육종증(원인을 알 수 없는 전신 염증질환), 신장결석 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 쎈파워비타연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamins A, D, B1, B2, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness (during illness or recovery), growth periods, and old age. It also helps relieve dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), poor development of bones and teeth, and prevents rickets.

### 🕒 쎈파워비타연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 8 years of age or older, or adults, take 1 capsule once daily.

### ⚠️ 쎈파워비타연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts; if you are an infant under 3 months of age; if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), or kidney stones.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet; pregnant or possibly pregnant women; lactating women; premature infants or infants; patients with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine); patients with or those who have experienced gout or kidney stones; patients with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or patients who should be administered lipid emulsions cautiously; patients with or those who have experienced hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4) and Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5); or patients who are expected to have a special risk due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 쎈파워비타연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解体力疲劳，妊娠期、哺乳期，病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时，发育期，老年期补充维生素A、D、B1、B2、C，并可缓解眼睛干燥感、夜盲症（夜间视力不佳的症状），预防骨骼、牙齿发育不良和佝偻病。

### 🕒 쎈파워비타연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
* 8岁以上或成人，每次1粒，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 쎈파워비타연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者，未满3个月的婴儿，高钙血症（血液中钙质过量存在的状态），肉样瘤病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病），肾结石患者请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，以下人群请咨询医生或药师：经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女，早产儿、婴儿，高草酸尿症（尿液中草酸盐排出过量的状态），痛风或有肾结石病史的患者，高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症、胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需慎重使用脂质乳剂的患者，对黄色4号及黄色5号过敏或有相关病史的患者，因脂肪过载预计有特殊风险的患者。

请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。

儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。

若服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停药并咨询医生或药师。
