# EN
### 💊 속콜플러스연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 속콜플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 속콜플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are taking MAO inhibitors (e.g., antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic constitution; have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicine (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty with urination; are physically weak or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are breastfeeding; smoke; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
