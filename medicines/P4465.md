#EN
### 💊 센스지크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin conditions (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis) and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 센스지크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 센스지크림 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, bacterial skin infections (such as tuberculosis, syphilis), fungal infections (such as candidiasis, tinea), spirochetal infections, yeast infections, viral infections (such as herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia), parasitic skin infections (such as scabies, pubic lice), eczematous otitis externa with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (excluding Behçet's disease), second-degree deep burns or higher / frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, rosacea, or a history of hypersensitivity to aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or bacitracin.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medication if you are pregnant, may be pregnant, breastfeeding, or are an infant/child.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not use for an extended period.

When used in children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

Do not use occlusive dressing therapy without a doctor's supervision.

Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.

Do not use for non-therapeutic purposes such as after makeup or shaving.
