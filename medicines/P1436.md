#EN
### 💊 다우제큐정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach, promoting digestion, and abdominal bloating caused by indigestion.

### 🕒 다우제큐정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 15 years and older: 2 tablets per dose.
*   Children aged 11 years and older to under 15 years: 1 and 1/3 tablets per dose.
*   Children aged 8 years and older to under 11 years: 1 tablet per dose.
*   Take 3 times a day after meals.
*   Ensure an interval of at least 4 hours between doses.

### ⚠️ 다우제큐정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a child under 7 years of age, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant, may be pregnant, or have an allergic constitution.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 2 weeks, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
