#EN
### 💊 Duoclok Cream - Efficacy & Effects
* This medication is used for the early treatment of recurrent labial herpes (cold sores).

### 🕒 Duoclok Cream - Dosage & Administration
* Adults should apply an appropriate amount, sufficient to cover the affected area up to its edges, 5 times a day for 5 days. The treatment period for this medication is limited to within 5 days. Treatment should be initiated as soon as possible after the first signs and symptoms appear.

### ⚠️ Duoclok Cream - Warnings & Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, skin infections (tuberculosis, syphilis, candidiasis, tinea, herpes zoster, chickenpox, vaccinia, scabies, pubic lice), impaired immune system (bone marrow transplant or AIDS), eczematous external otitis with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (except Behcet's disease), second-degree deep burns or frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, rosacea, are a nursing mother, a child under 18 years of age, or have had or experienced myocardial infarction.
* Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Use only for cold sores on the lips or around the mouth; do not use on eyes, nose, inside the mouth, or genitals.
* Do not use on individuals without signs or symptoms.
* Do not use for the purpose of preventing transmission to others or preventing recurrent herpes simplex infections.
* Do not dilute this medication or use it as a base to mix with other drugs.
