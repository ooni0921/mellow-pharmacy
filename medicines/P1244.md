### 💊 Tera 400 mg Tablets - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, or physical debility during or after illness (during or after recovery from an illness). It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 Tera 400 mg Tablets - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 8 years or older and adults, take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ Tera 400 mg Tablets - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not administer this product to patients with hypersensitivity to this product or to infants under 3 months of age.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child exposed to a lot of sunlight and eating a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, or someone with a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to Yellow No. 5.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication may turn urine yellow, which could affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
