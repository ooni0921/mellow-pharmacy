#EN
### 💊 마그포스맥스연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used to supply vitamins B1, B2, B6, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, peripheral circulatory disorders, and shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet) during menopause, as well as for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그포스맥스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 12 years of age or older: Take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그포스맥스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have severe renal failure, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to soybean oil, or have hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5), or have folic acid deficiency.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after taking the medicine for about 1 month, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
