* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 본디칼맥스정 - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used to supplement Vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, to prevent bone and teeth developmental disorders (such as rickets), and to supplement calcium deficiency and other calcium requirements.

### 🕒 본디칼맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 본디칼맥스정 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this product, an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is excessive calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
