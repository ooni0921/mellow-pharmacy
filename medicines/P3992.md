#EN
### 💊 휴텍스아스피린장용정100mg(아스피린) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the inhibition of thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, for the inhibition of thrombus formation after Coronary Artery Bypass Graft (CABG) or Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA), and for the reduction of cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, diabetes, etc.).

### 🕒 휴텍스아스피린장용정100mg(아스피린) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet once daily. This medicine is an enteric-coated tablet, so it can be taken before meals with plenty of water.

### ⚠️ 휴텍스아스피린장용정100mg(아스피린) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or antirheumatic drugs; peptic ulcer; aspirin asthma or a history of it; hemophilia; severe hepatic impairment; severe renal impairment; severe cardiac dysfunction; bleeding tendency; concomitant administration of methotrexate at a dose exceeding 15 mg/week; or if you are a pregnant woman in the third trimester. Do not take this medicine during the first and second trimesters of pregnancy unless absolutely necessary. Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment; cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (e.g., renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, fluid depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major hemorrhagic events); hepatic impairment; cardiac dysfunction; bronchial asthma; are a patient prior to surgery; are an infant aged 3 years or younger; are elderly; have glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency; have Reye's syndrome; are a child aged 14 years or younger with chickenpox or influenza infection; or have gout. Inform your doctor that you are taking this medicine if you are undergoing surgery (including dental surgery) due to the risk of bleeding. Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medicine.
