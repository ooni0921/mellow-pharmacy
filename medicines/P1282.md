정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

#EN
### 💊 소하자임플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, gastric discomfort, gastric bloating, indigestion (gastric stasis), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite, overeating, promotion of digestion, and gastric bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 소하자임플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after each meal.

### ⚠️ 소하자임플러스정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this drug if you are:
    *   A patient undergoing dialysis.
    *   A patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
    *   A child (under 15 years old).
    *   A pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a breastfeeding woman.

    Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug if you are:
    *   Hypersensitive to this drug.
    *   A patient with renal impairment, hepatic impairment, or hypophosphatemia.
    *   A patient with a history of tartrazine (Yellow No. 4) hypersensitivity or allergic reactions.
    *   A patient with high blood pressure.
    *   An elderly patient.
    *   A patient with cardiac impairment, renal impairment, or edema.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN

*   本品用于胃酸过多、胃灼热、胃部不适、胃胀感、食滞（胃滞）、恶心、呕吐、胃痛、嗳酸、消化不良、食欲不振、过食、促进消化、因消化不良引起的胃胀感。

### 🕒 소하자임플러스정 - 用法与用量

*   以下患者请勿服用本品：

    服用本品前，以下患者请咨询医生或药师：
    *   对本品过敏的患者。
    *   肾功能障碍、肝功能障碍、磷酸盐水平低下的患者。
    *   对柠檬黄（黄色4号）过敏或有过敏反应史的患者。
    *   血压高的患者。
    *   老年患者。
    *   心脏功能障碍、肾功能障碍、水肿患者。
