# EN
### 💊 스카이정 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for paroxysmal pain of gastrointestinal diseases or functional disorders and spasmodic pain of the biliary system, urinary system, and female reproductive system, such as dysmenorrhea.

### 🕒 스카이정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 tablets once, three times a day. Do not exceed a maximum daily dose of 6 tablets.

### ⚠️ 스카이정 - Precautions
* If you are a regular drinker who consumes three or more alcoholic beverages daily, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* Do not take 스카이정 if you have experienced hypersensitivity (e.g., rash, red spots, itching, swelling of the larynx, eyelids, lips) to this medicine, experienced asthma due to other cold medicines or antipyretic analgesics, have severe kidney impairment, glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase deficiency, glaucoma, urination disorders due to benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), functional strictures of the gastrointestinal tract such as pyloric stenosis (narrowing of the pylorus), paralytic or obstructive ileus (bowel obstruction), severe myasthenia gravis, megacolon, tachycardia and rapid arrhythmia, severe liver impairment, severe heart disease, hemorrhagic colitis, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, or have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol. Before taking 스카이정, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bacterial diarrhea, liver impairment or a history thereof, Gilbert's syndrome (Meulengracht disease), abnormal heart function (coronary insufficiency, heart failure, arrhythmia, etc.), kidney impairment or a history thereof, a history of peptic ulcer, blood disorders or a history thereof, a tendency to bleed, bronchial asthma, ulcerative colitis, hyperthyroidism, fever, prostatic hypertrophy, are elderly, pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, at risk of narrow-angle glaucoma, susceptible to intestinal or ureteral outlet obstruction, or prone to rapid arrhythmia. Since visual accommodation disorders may occur, exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

# CN
### 💊 스카이정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于胃肠道疾病的发作性疼痛或胆道系统、泌尿系统、女性生殖系统（如痛经）的功能障碍和痉挛性疼痛。

### 🕒 스카이정 - 用法用量
* 成人每次服用1~2片，每日3次。每日最大剂量不得超过6片。

### ⚠️ 스카이정 - 注意事项
* 每日饮用三杯以上酒精饮品者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能导致肝损伤。
* 对本药有过敏史（如皮疹、红斑、瘙痒、喉部、眼睑、嘴唇等肿胀）者，因其他感冒药或解热镇痛药引起哮喘者，严重肾功能障碍患者，葡萄糖-6-磷酸脱氢酶缺乏症患者，青光眼患者，前列腺肥大引起的排尿（小便）障碍者，幽门狭窄等胃肠道功能性狭窄者，麻痹性或阻塞性肠梗阻患者，严重重症肌无力患者，巨结肠患者，心动过速及快速型心律不齐患者，严重肝功能障碍患者，严重心脏病患者，出血性结肠炎患者，消化性溃疡患者，严重血液异常患者，以及服用巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药、酒精者，请勿服用本药。在服用本药之前，细菌性腹泻患者，肝功能障碍或有其病史者，吉尔伯特综合征（莫伊伦格拉赫特病）患者，心脏功能异常（冠状动脉供血不足、心力衰竭、心律不齐等）患者，肾功能障碍或有其病史者，消化性溃疡病史者，血液异常或有其病史者，有出血倾向的患者，支气管哮喘患者，溃疡性结肠炎患者，甲状腺功能亢进症患者，发热患者，前列腺肥大患者，老年人，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，有闭角型青光眼风险的患者，容易发生肠道或输尿管出口阻塞的患者以及容易发生快速型心律不齐的患者，请咨询医生或药师。由于可能出现眼睛调节障碍，驾驶和操作机械时请注意。
