* This medication is used for neuralgia, lower back pain, and shoulder stiffness.

### 🕒 엠피스에프정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, twice daily, when experiencing pain or an attack.

### ⚠️ 엠피스에프정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due to medication, or if you are an infant, toddler, or child (under 14 years old) who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, are physically weak, have a high fever, are elderly, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

If symptoms do not improve after several doses, immediately stop taking this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
