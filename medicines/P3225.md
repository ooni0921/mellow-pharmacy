# EN
### 💊 니코에이패취(니코틴) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjunctive therapy for smoking cessation.

### 🕒 니코에이패취(니코틴) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply one patch daily immediately upon waking in the morning and remove it at bedtime. With clean hands, peel off one side of the release liner from the patch and apply it to a dry, intact, hairless area of the skin (such as the buttocks, upper arm, or chest). Then, peel off the remaining release liner and firmly adhere the patch completely. For recommended dosage and duration, please refer to the approved product information.

### ⚠️ 니코에이패취(니코틴) - Precautions
*   If you continue to smoke while using this medication, cardiovascular side effects due to nicotine may occur more frequently and distinctly. Therefore, use this medication only if you have completely stopped smoking.
*   Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, chronic skin conditions (psoriasis, chronic dermatitis, urticaria), systemic skin diseases, hypersensitivity dermatitis, unstable angina, worsening angina, myocardial infarction within the last 3 months, late stage of acute infarction, severe cardiac arrhythmia, systemic hypertension, peripheral vascular disease, cerebrovascular disease (acute stroke, cerebrovascular abnormalities), are pregnant or breastfeeding, are under 18 years of age (children and adolescents), are a non-smoker, or an occasional smoker. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have gastritis, peptic ulcer, occlusive arterial disease, stable angina, heart failure, decompensated heart failure, myocardial infarction, cardiac arrhythmia, vasospasm, uncontrolled hypertension, history of arterial bypass graft or angioplasty, renal failure, hepatic failure, diabetes mellitus, hyperthyroidism, or pheochromocytoma.

*   本药用作戒烟时的辅助疗法。

### 🕒 니코에이패취(니코틴) - 用法用量
*   每日早晨起床后立即贴敷一片贴剂，并在就寝时取下。用干净的双手撕下贴剂一侧的剥离纸，将其贴敷于无毛发、干燥且未受损的皮肤区域（如臀部、上臂、胸部等），然后撕下剩余的剥离纸，完全贴紧。有关推荐剂量和推荐疗程，请参阅批准的药品说明。

### ⚠️ 니코에이패취(니코틴) - 注意事项
*   若在使用本药期间继续吸烟，尼古丁可能导致心血管副作用更频繁、更显著地发生，因此仅限已完全戒烟的患者使用。
*   以下患者请勿使用本药：对本药过敏者、患有慢性皮肤病（如银屑病、慢性皮炎、荨麻疹）、全身性皮肤病、过敏性皮炎、不稳定型心绞痛、心绞痛恶化、近3个月内发生过心肌梗死、急性梗死后期、严重心律失常、全身性高血压、周围血管疾病、脑血管疾病（急性脑卒中、脑血管异常）、孕妇及哺乳期妇女、18岁以下儿童及青少年、非吸烟者及偶尔吸烟者。在使用本药前，患有胃炎、消化性溃疡、闭塞性动脉疾病、稳定型心绞痛、心力衰竭、失代偿性心力衰竭、心肌梗死、心律失常、血管痉挛、未控制的高血压、动脉血管旁路移植术或血管成形术史患者、肾功能不全、肝功能不全、糖尿病、甲状腺功能亢进症、嗜铬细胞瘤的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
