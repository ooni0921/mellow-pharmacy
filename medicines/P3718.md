# EN
### 💊 앤티팻정(엘카르니틴) - Efficacy and Indications
* This drug is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and for myocardial metabolic disorders caused by ischemic heart diseases such as angina pectoris and acute myocardial infarction.

### 🕒 앤티팻정(엘카르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2-3 g daily, divided into 2-3 doses. The dosage may be appropriately adjusted depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 앤티팻정(엘카르니틴) - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have fat metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or if you are a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsions, or if you are a patient expected to have special risks due to fat overload, or if you are pregnant, possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding.

# CN
### 💊 앤티팻정(엘카르니틴) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗原发性、继发性肉碱缺乏症，以及由心绞痛、急性心肌梗死等缺血性心脏病引起的心肌代谢障碍。

### 🕒 앤티팻정(엘카르니틴) - 用法用量
* 成人每日服用2-3克，分2-3次服用。可根据年龄和症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 앤티팻정(엘카르니틴) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药或大豆油、大豆、花生过敏的患者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，如有高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者，或因脂肪过载预计有特殊风险的患者，或孕妇、可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期女性，请咨询医生或药剂师。
