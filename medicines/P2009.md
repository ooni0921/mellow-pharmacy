# EN
### 💊 훼리폴캡슐 - Indications and Efficacy
* This medication is used for the prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia.

### 🕒 훼리폴캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule once a day.

### ⚠️ 훼리폴캡슐 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hemochromatosis (a disorder where iron deposition occurs in the liver and pancreas due to abnormal iron metabolism), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis; cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction; renal impairment; hypoproteinemia; folic acid deficiency; or if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medication, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 훼리폴캡슐 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于预防及治疗缺铁性贫血。

### 🕒 훼리폴캡슐 - 用法与用量
* 成人每次1粒，每日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 훼리폴캡슐 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿（包括母乳喂养儿、新生儿）、血色素沉着病（因铁代谢异常导致铁沉积于肝脏、胰腺的疾病）、含铁血黄素沉着症、非缺铁性贫血、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病、心血管系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、叶酸缺乏的患者、孕妇及哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
