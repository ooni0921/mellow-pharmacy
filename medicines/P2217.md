* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful edema or inflammation following trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 에스빈연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule (300 mg) per dose, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 capsules (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 에스빈연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other fever-reducing analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal, cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose dexibuprofen (1,200 mg per day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose dexibuprofen (1,200 mg per day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulceration or a history thereof, bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular system, or other sites, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other NSAIDs, patients undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, pregnant women in the last trimester (6 months or more), breastfeeding women, patients with inflammatory bowel diseases such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or patients with a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous NSAID treatment.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have experienced blood disorders, have a bleeding tendency, have or have experienced hepatic impairment, have or have experienced renal impairment, have fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, a history of hypersensitivity, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, are a child aged 6 years or older, have stomach cancer, are an alcoholic, are in the first or second trimester of pregnancy, or are a woman who may be pregnant, have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), have hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss, have blood coagulation disorders or are taking anticoagulants, or have or have experienced hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF).

Considering the importance of drug administration, either discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue drug administration.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

For acute conditions, long-term administration should generally be limited to within 5 days.

本剤を服用する前に、血液異常またはその既往歴のある患者、出血傾向のある患者、肝障害またはその既往歴のある患者、腎障害またはその既往歴のある患者、体液貯留または心不全患者、高血圧、過敏症の既往歴のある患者、全身性エリテマトーデス（SLE）および混合性結合組織病（MCTD）患者、高齢者、6歳以上の小児、胃癌、アルコール中毒患者、妊娠初期・中期の妊婦および妊娠している可能性のある女性、虚血性心疾患、末梢動脈疾患、脳血管疾患、心血管疾患のリスク因子のある患者（例：高血圧、高脂血症、糖尿病、喫煙）、肝性ポルフィリン症、失血を伴う重大な外科手術を受けた患者、血液凝固障害があるまたは抗凝固剤を投与されている患者、黄色5号（サンセットイエローFCF）に過敏症の患者またはその既往歴のある患者は、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。

薬物投与の重要性を考慮し、授乳を中止するか、薬物投与を中止してください。

本剤服用後、めまいや疲労が現れることがあるため、車の運転や機械操作には注意してください。

急性疾患に対して長期服用する場合は、原則として5日以内とします。
