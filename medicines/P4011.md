#EN
### 💊 알코펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Indications and Usage
* This medication is used for fever and pain due to colds, back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), post-operative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 알코펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults with rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, or psoriatic arthritis, take 1 to 3 capsules (200 to 600 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times a day, up to a maximum of 16 capsules (3,200 mg) per day.
* For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30 to 40 mg per kg of body weight per day, divided into 3 to 4 doses.
* For mild to moderate pain or colds, adults should take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times a day.
* For migraines, adults should take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) per dose, not exceeding 2 capsules within a 24-hour period.

* Adolescents aged 12 years and older should take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) per dose, 3 to 4 times a day.

* For individuals weighing less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid taking this medication on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 알코펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* Individuals who regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

* Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to it, a history of gastrointestinal ulceration or bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, or a history of hypersensitivity reactions (such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions) to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs). Also, it should not be taken by patients experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, or by pregnant women in the last trimester (final 3 months) of pregnancy.

* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis or hepatic impairment, renal impairment, cardiac dysfunction or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus or mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, or if you are elderly, a child, pregnant, breastfeeding, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

* Do not exceed the recommended dose.

* When taken for colds, this medication should generally be used for no more than 5 days. Do not take it for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without a doctor's or pharmacist's instruction, and do not take it for fever for more than 3 days.

* 軽度から中等度の疼痛または風邪の場合、成人は1回1～2カプセル（200～400 mg）を1日3～4回服用します。
* 片頭痛の場合、成人は1回1～2カプセル（200～400 mg）を服用し、24時間で2カプセルを超えないようにします。
