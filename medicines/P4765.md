#EN
### 💊 바르지오모두크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for tinea pedis (athlete's foot), tinea cruris (jock itch), and tinea corporis.

### 🕒 바르지오모두크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 바르지오모두크림 - Precautions
* No information
* This medication should not be used by patients with hypersensitivity to this drug or those with a history of such reactions, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, and infants/children.

Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug or those with a history of allergies, individuals with an allergic constitution, those with affected areas on the face or extensive affected areas, those with suppurative affected areas, or those whose distinction between "eczema" and "tinea pedis • tinea cruris (jock itch), tinea corporis" is unclear, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not use on or around the eyes, mucous membranes (e.g., inside the mouth, nose, vagina), genital area, external genitalia, eczema, areas with excessive moisture, maceration, cracking, severely injured affected areas, fingernails, or scalp.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 2 weeks of use, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Clean the affected area and its surroundings before use.

Be careful not to get it in your eyes. If it gets into your eyes, rinse immediately with water or warm water, and seek immediate medical attention from an ophthalmologist.

Do not touch your eyes or mucous membranes with hands that have applied this medication.

This medication is for external use only.
