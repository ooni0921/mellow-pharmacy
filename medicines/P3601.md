# EN
### 💊 혈앤보알파연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamins E, B1, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical decline during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and in old age; for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet; for the relief of symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; for beriberi; for eye fatigue; and for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 혈앤보알파연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years or older and adults: Take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 혈앤보알파연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months old, or if you have severe renal insufficiency.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously, or if you are at special risk due to lipid overload, or have a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), or have hypersensitivity to or experience with Yellow No. 5.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
