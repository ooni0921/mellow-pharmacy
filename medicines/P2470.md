#EN
### 💊 미그펜400연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, lower back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), postoperative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 미그펜400연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 1/2 to 1.5 capsules (200-600 mg) once, 3-4 times a day, up to a maximum of 8 capsules (3,200 mg) per day.

    For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight per day, divided into 3-4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or colds, adults take 1/2 to 1 capsule (200-400 mg) once, 3-4 times a day.

    For migraine, adults take 1/2 to 1 capsule (200-400 mg) once, ensuring not to exceed 1 capsule (400 mg) within 24 hours.

    Do not exceed the recommended dose.

    Adjust the dosage appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 미그펜400연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

    Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of gastrointestinal ulcers or bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs (non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history thereof, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, or if you are in the last trimester of pregnancy.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis or liver impairment, kidney impairment, cardiac dysfunction or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus or mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly, a child, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

    Do not exceed the recommended dose.

    When taken for a cold, generally do not take for more than 5 days. For pain, do not take for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without a doctor's or pharmacist's instruction. For fever, do not take for more than 3 days.
