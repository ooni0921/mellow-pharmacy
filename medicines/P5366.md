#EN

### 💊 타세놀콜드캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (rhinorrhea, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, muscle pain, joint pain).

### 🕒 타세놀콜드캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older, take 2 capsules once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 타세놀콜드캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and plan to take this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* In very rare cases, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN) have been reported, which can be fatal. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma, have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, or are an infant under 3 months of age.

* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic constitution; have a history of allergic reactions (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or are breastfeeding; have a cough (due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, accompanied by excessive phlegm, lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache); have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5); are taking other medications such as antidiabetics, antigout medications, arthritis medications, anticoagulants, or steroids; or have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* For administration to children under 2 years of age, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

### 💊 타세놀콜드캡슐 - 功效与作用
* 本药品用于缓解感冒的各种症状（流鼻涕、鼻塞、打喷嚏、喉咙痛、咳嗽、咳痰、发冷、发热、头痛、肌肉痛、关节痛）。

### 🕒 타세놀콜드캡슐 - 用法用量
* 15岁以上成人，每次服用2粒胶囊，每日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 타세놀콜드캡슐 - 注意事项
* 每天饮酒超过三杯的常规饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能导致肝损伤。
* 极少数情况下，曾报告过可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、皮肤黏膜眼综合征（史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征）和中毒性表皮坏死松解症（莱尔综合征）。服用本药后，如果出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。
* 每日扑热息痛（对乙酰氨基酚）的最大剂量不应超过4,000毫克。这可能导致肝损伤。请勿与含有扑热息痛的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药有过敏史或过敏经验者、有哮喘史者、有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿，请勿服用本药。

* 服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师，如果您是感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿或15岁以下儿童；有过敏体质；有皮疹、发烧、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等过敏反应史；有肝病、肾病、心脏病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（如眼睛疼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难等；是老年人、体弱者或有高烧；有持续或复发的胃肠道问题，如胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛，或有溃疡、出血问题；是孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女；有咳嗽（吸烟、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续或复发超过1周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发烧、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽）；有日落黄（黄色5号）过敏史或过敏反应经验；正在服用其他药物，如降糖药、抗痛风药、关节炎药、抗凝血剂、类固醇等；或曾服用过巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药和酒精。
* 服用5-6次后症状仍未改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
* 给2岁以下儿童服用时，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 服药期间请勿饮酒。
* 服用后可能会出现嗜睡，因此驾驶和操作机械时请注意。
