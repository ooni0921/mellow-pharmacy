# EN
### 💊 가네진정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjunctive therapy for chronic hepatitis and toxic liver disease.

### 🕒 가네진정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 tablet per dose, 2 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 가네진정 - Precautions
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug should not take it.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medication may turn urine yellow during various urine tests and may affect clinical test results.

# CN

*   本药物用于慢性肝炎、中毒性肝病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 가네진정 - 用法与用量
*   成人每次1片，每日2～3次服用。

*   对本药过敏的患者请勿服用本药。
*   叶酸缺乏症患者在服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   本药在进行各种尿液检查时，可能使尿液变为黄色，从而影响临床检查结果。
