#EN
### 💊 Hepatos Syrup - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the adjunctive treatment of liver diseases.

### 🕒 Hepatos Syrup - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 10-30 mL daily, divided into doses between meals.
* Take 10 mL of this medicine diluted in about half a glass of water.

### ⚠️ Hepatos Syrup - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have kidney disease, myocardial infarction, or a history of such conditions.
* This medicine cannot be used as an antidote for alcohol poisoning.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of caramel hypersensitivity or allergic reactions.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* This medicine is for oral use only and should not be used for injection.

#CN
### 💊 헤파토스시럽 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于肝脏疾病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 헤파토스시럽 - 用法与用量
* 成人每日服用10~30毫升，分次于两餐之间服用。
* 将本药10毫升稀释于约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 헤파토스시럽 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 肾脏疾病、心肌梗塞患者及有相关病史者请勿服用本药。
* 本药不能用作酒精中毒的解毒剂。
* 服用本药前，对焦糖过敏或有过敏反应史者请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
* 服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
* 本药仅限口服，请勿用于注射。
