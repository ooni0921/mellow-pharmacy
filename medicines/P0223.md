#EN
### 💊 오디스겔(케토프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), muscle pain, and swelling/pain after trauma.

### 🕒 오디스겔(케토프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 4 times a day, depending on symptoms, and gently rub it in until well absorbed.

### ⚠️ 오디스겔(케토프로펜) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, photosensitivity, asthma experienced due to aspirin or other NSAIDs, hypersensitivity to tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, or oxybenzone, if you are pregnant (6 months or more into pregnancy), or if you are a child under 15 years of age. Before using this medicine, patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant women, or nursing mothers should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Photosensitivity may occur; therefore, avoid ultraviolet light during use and for up to 2 weeks after using this medicine. Do not use on eyes, mucous membranes, athlete's foot, or ringworm. Exercise caution when using on damaged skin or mucous membranes, eczema, or rashes, as it may cause temporary irritation and sharp pain. Do not use occlusive dressing methods. If symptoms do not improve after about 1 week of use, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 오디스겔(케토프로펜) - 用法用量
* 根据症状，取适量每日1至4次涂抹于患处，并轻轻揉搓使其充分吸收。

### ⚠️ 오디스겔(케토프로펜) - 注意事项
* 暂无其他信息。
