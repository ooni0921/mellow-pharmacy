# EN
### 💊 콜드펜노즈연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve various symptoms of a cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (shivering symptoms), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 콜드펜노즈연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older (adults), take 2 capsules once. For individuals aged 7 years and older but under 15 years, take 1 capsule once. Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 콜드펜노즈연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; an infant aged 3 months or older but under 2 years; a toddler; have an allergic constitution; have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.); have difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are breastfeeding; smoke; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough that lasts or recurs for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

Do not take this medication continuously for a prolonged period.

If symptoms do not improve after taking the medication about 5-6 times, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 콜드펜노즈연질캡슐 - 用法用量
* 15岁以上及成人，每次2粒；7岁以上15岁以下儿童，每次1粒；每日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 콜드펜노즈연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯以上者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。

请勿超过对乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000毫克）。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。

如果出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等重症皮肤不良反应，请停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
* 对本药有过敏史者、服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾发生哮喘者、未满3个月的婴儿、重度或未控制的高血压患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师：感染水痘或流感或疑似感染的婴儿及未满15岁的儿童；3个月以上未满2岁的婴儿、幼儿；过敏体质者；有过敏症状（如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）经验者；肝病、肾病、心脏病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（如：眼睛疼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者；老年人、体弱者或高热者；胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；吸烟者；哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续或反复发作超过一周的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽患者。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

服药期间请勿饮酒。

服用后可能出现嗜睡，驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

请勿长期持续服用。

如果服用5-6次后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

* 毎日3杯以上のアルコール飲料を定期的に摂取する方が本剤または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する際は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。

アセトアミノフェンとして1日最大用量（4,000 mg）を超えて服用しないでください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。アセトアミノフェンを含む他の製品と併用しないでください。

急性汎発性発疹性膿疱症のような重症皮膚異常反応が現れた場合は、服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。

定められた用法・用量を守ってください。
