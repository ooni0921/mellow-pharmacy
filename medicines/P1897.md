### 🕒 덴드로프-쿨액(시클로피록스올아민) - Dosage and Administration
* After wetting the hair with water, apply a sufficient amount of this medication to create lather. Massage thoroughly with fingertips. After 3 to 5 minutes, rinse the hair thoroughly with water several times to completely wash it off. Use 2 to 3 times a week, or as needed for the treatment of scalp conditions.

### ⚠️ 덴드로프-쿨액(시클로피록스올아민) - Precautions
* Information not available
* Do not use this medication in patients with hypersensitivity to this product, or on the eyes, mucous membranes (oral cavity, nasal cavity, vagina), pubic region, vulva, eczema, wet lesions, erosions, fissures, or severely traumatized areas. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by poison ivy, etc.), if the affected area is on the face, extensive, or purulent, if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, or if the patient is a child under 12 years of age. Apply this medication externally only to the scalp and adjacent areas. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not use this medication immediately if the affected area or its surroundings are dirty. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

* 本药用于治疗头皮疾病时，每周使用2~3次或根据需要使用。先用水润湿头发，然后取足量本药，揉搓至起泡，用指尖充分按摩。3~5分钟后，用清水反复冲洗头发，直至完全洗净。

### ⚠️ 덴드로프-쿨액(시클로피록스올아민) - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者、眼周、黏膜（口腔、鼻腔、阴道）、阴部、外阴、湿疹、湿润、糜烂、皲裂或严重外伤（伤口）的患部，请勿使用本药。使用本药前，如出现过敏症状（如皮疹、红斑、瘙痒、漆树等引起的皮炎等），患部位于面部、范围广泛或有化脓性感染的患者，孕妇及哺乳期妇女，以及12岁以下的儿童，请咨询医生或药师。本药仅限外用于头皮及邻近部位。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。患部（疾病部位）或其周围脏污时，请勿立即使用。给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
