#EN
### 💊 파프케이플러스카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory relief of degenerative arthritis (osteoarthritis), periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis and tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia (muscle pain), and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 파프케이플러스카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* After removing the release liner from the medication surface, apply to the affected area twice daily.

### ⚠️ 파프케이플러스카타플라스마(케토프로펜) - Precautions
* No information provided
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity or photosensitivity to this medication, or to tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, or oxybenzone; if you have or have experienced aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by NSAIDs, etc.); if you are pregnant and 6 months or more into your pregnancy; or if you are a child under 15 years of age.

Before using this medication, patients with bronchial asthma, pregnant women, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Avoid outdoor activities regardless of the weather while using this medication and for up to 2 weeks after use. When going out daily, cover the application site with clothing, sunglasses, a hat, sunscreen, etc., to avoid exposure to ultraviolet rays.

Do not use on the eyes and mucous membranes, damaged skin and mucous membranes, areas with eczema or rash, athlete's foot, or tinea.

Do not use occlusive dressing.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
