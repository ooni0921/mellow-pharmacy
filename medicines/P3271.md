#EN
### 💊 미놀노즈점비액(자일로메타졸린염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy head sensation caused by the common cold (acute rhinitis), allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 미놀노즈점비액(자일로메타졸린염산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 12 years and older, spray once into each nostril, up to 3 times a day.

The interval between applications should be at least 8 hours.

### ⚠️ 미놀노즈점비액(자일로메타졸린염산염) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or a history of it, are a child under 12 years of age, have undergone surgery that exposed the dura mater or transsphenoidal hypophysectomy, have closed-angle glaucoma, have rhinitis sicca (dry rhinitis) or atrophic rhinitis, or have pheochromocytoma or benign prostatic hyperplasia.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash/redness, swelling, irritation, joint pain, asthma, itching), have hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, glaucoma, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, cough accompanied by fever/rash or persistent headache, or show a strong reaction to adrenergic substances such as insomnia, dizziness, tremor, cardiac arrhythmia, or increased blood pressure.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Excessive use may paradoxically cause nasal congestion, so do not use excessively.

Do not use in the eyes or ears.

To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the product if possible.

Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, stop use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   12歳以上及び成人は、1回1噴霧ずつ、1日3回を限度として、各鼻腔内に噴霧します。

使用間隔は8時間以上とします。

### ⚠️ 미놀노즈점비액(자일로메타졸린염산염) - 注意事項
*   情報なし
*   本剤に対し過敏症のある患者又は経験者、12歳未満の小児、脳硬膜が露出する手術や経蝶形骨洞下垂体摘出術を受けた人、閉塞隅角緑内障患者、乾燥性鼻炎又は萎縮性鼻炎患者、褐色細胞腫又は前立腺肥大症患者は、本剤を使用しないでください。

本剤を使用する前に、妊婦又は妊娠している可能性のある女性及び授乳婦、アレルギー体質、アレルギー症状（例：発熱、発疹・発赤、浮腫、刺激感、関節痛、喘息、かゆみ等）の経験者、高血圧、心臓病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾患、緑内障、喫煙者、喘息、慢性気管支炎、肺気腫、過剰な痰を伴う咳、1週間以上続く又は再発する咳、慢性咳嗽、発熱・発疹や持続的な頭痛を伴う咳患者、不眠症、めまい、ふるえ、心臓不整脈又は血圧上昇のようにアドレナリン作動物質に対し強い反応を示す患者は、医師又は薬剤師に相談してください。

定められた用法・用量を厳守してください。

過度に使用すると、かえって鼻づまりを引き起こすことがあるため、過度に使用しないでください。
