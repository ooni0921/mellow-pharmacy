##EN

### 💊 메가콘티800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, and D in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or periods of physical weakness after recovery, during growth periods, and in old age.
*   This medication is used to alleviate symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, stiff shoulders, and other joint pains, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, through the vitamins it contains.
*   This medication is used for poor bone and teeth development, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue, through the vitamins it contains.

### 🕒 메가콘티800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults aged 19 years and older, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 메가콘티800정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this medication, soy or peanuts, have a history of hypersensitivity to soybean oil, are an infant under 12 months, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have heart and circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, a history of kidney stones, hypoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions carefully, or have conditions related to fat overload.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
