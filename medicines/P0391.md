#EN
### 💊 알파아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain due to the common cold, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 알파아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 300 to 1,000 mg per dose, 3 to 4 times a day. A maximum of 8 tablets (4,000 mg) can be administered per day. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 알파아세트아미노펜정500밀리그람 - Precautions
* None
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorder, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, a history of asthma caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, are taking barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, or have consumed alcohol. Before taking this medicine, patients with liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorder, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, the elderly, pregnant or lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Without instructions from a doctor or pharmacist, do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), and do not take for fever for more than 3 days.
