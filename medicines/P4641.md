#EN
### 💊 미안점안액(순), 미안점안액(쿨), 미안점안액(쿨하이) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for discomfort when wearing soft or hard contact lenses, tear supplementation (dry eyes), eye fatigue, and blurred vision (when there is a lot of eye discharge).

### 🕒 미안점안액(순), 미안점안액(쿨), 미안점안액(쿨하이) - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops at a time, 5-6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 미안점안액(순), 미안점안액(쿨), 미안점안액(쿨하이) - Precautions
* No information
* Before using this medication, patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, allergic constitution, severe eye pain, or glaucoma should consult a doctor or pharmacist. When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully. Use this medication only for ophthalmic administration. Do not share with others. Do not use products past their expiration date, and even if within the expiration date, use promptly after opening.
