#EN
### 💊 조인액트800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, B6, and D in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or physical decline after recovery, during growth periods, and in old age.
*   This medicine, through its contained vitamins, is used to relieve symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, shoulder stiffness, and other joint pains, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.
*   This medicine, through its contained vitamins, is used for poor bone and teeth development, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 조인액트800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 조인액트800정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypersensitivity to soybean oil or have experienced it, hypersensitivity to soybeans or peanuts, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, premature infant, infant, have heart and circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or patients requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions, or patients with lipid overload.
*   If symptoms do not improve after about 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
