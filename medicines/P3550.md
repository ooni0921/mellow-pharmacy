# EN
### 💊 기가프신연질캡슐 - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medication is used for phlegm or phlegm that causes coughing.

### 🕒 기가프신연질캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults and children aged 15 years or older: 2 capsules per dose. Children aged 8-14 years: 1 capsule per dose. Take 3 times daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 기가프신연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastric/duodenal ulcers, or are a child under 8 years of age. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, elderly, have a predisposition to conditions such as urticaria, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis (hay fever), migraine, or food allergies, have a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have a high fever, heart disease, liver disease, or severe kidney (renal) disease, or a history of gastric/duodenal ulcers. Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

# CN
