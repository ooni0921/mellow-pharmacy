#EN
### 💊 케이파이콜싹과립 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 케이파이콜싹과립 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 15 years or older: 1 sachet (3 g) per dose.
*   Children aged 11 years or older and under 15 years: 2/3 sachet (2 g) per dose.
*   Children aged 7 years or older and under 11 years: 1/2 sachet (1.5 g) per dose.
*   Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 케이파이콜싹과립 - Precautions
*   Patients who regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported in patients taking acetaminophen. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen, as it may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing acetaminophen.

This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body. Therefore, patients with phenylketonuria who need to regulate phenylalanine intake should not take this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma after taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, a child under 2 years old, pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, elderly, frail, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, edema, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), dysuria, high fever, gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, ulcers, bleeding problems, or if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough with excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   7岁以上至11岁以下：一次1/2包（1.5克）。
*   每日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

本品含有阿斯巴甜，其在体内代谢为苯丙氨酸。因此，需要限制苯丙氨酸摄入的苯丙酮尿症患者不应服用本品。
