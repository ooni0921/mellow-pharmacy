#EN
### 💊 렛잇비프로정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, physical decline during illness or recovery, growth period, and old age; prevention of poor bone and tooth development, rickets; relief of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis; and supply of zinc.

### 🕒 렛잇비프로정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 렛잇비프로정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, a history of allergic reactions to soybean oil, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, infants under 12 months of age, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant or young child, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, etc., a patient with heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, a patient with or a history of kidney stones, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsions, or a patient for whom a special risk is anticipated due to fat overload.

*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking the medicine for about 1 month, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
