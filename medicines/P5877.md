#EN
### 💊 통케어정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for neuralgia, lower back pain, and stiff shoulders.

### 🕒 통케어정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, twice daily, when experiencing pain or an attack.

### ⚠️ 통케어정 - Precautions
* No information
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due to medication, or if you are an infant, toddler, or child (under 14 years old) infected with or suspected of being infected with chickenpox or influenza.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver, kidney, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, or if you are physically weak, have a high fever, are elderly, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

If symptoms do not improve after several doses, immediately stop taking this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN
### 💊 통케어정 - 功效与作用
* 本药品用于神经痛、腰痛、肩部僵硬。

### 🕒 통케어정 - 用法用量
* 成人每次服用1至2片，每日2次，于疼痛或发作时服用。

### ⚠️ 통케어정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 服用本药品前，有过敏体质、曾因药物引起发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等过敏症状者，或感染水痘或流感，或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴幼儿及儿童（14岁以下）应咨询医生或药师。

服用本药品前，患有肝脏、肾脏、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压等疾病者，体弱者，高热患者，老年人，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女应咨询医生或药师。

服用多次后症状仍未改善时，请立即停止服用本药品，并咨询医生或药师。

儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
