###EN
### 💊 Seinesium Soft Capsule - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for vitamin E supplementation in the elderly, alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness and coldness in the extremities during peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 Seinesium Soft Capsule - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ Seinesium Soft Capsule - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or if you have severe renal failure.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or if you are a patient who needs careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload, or a patient with a predisposition to thrombosis (blood clots).
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
