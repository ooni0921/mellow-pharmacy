#EN
### 💊 노즈그린연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, watery eyes, sore throat, and heavy head caused by common cold (acute rhinitis), allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 노즈그린연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and adolescents aged 15 years and older, take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times a day, after each meal.

### ⚠️ 노즈그린연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity or a history of allergic reactions to this medicine or its components, if you are taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if: you or your parents or siblings have a predisposition to develop conditions such as hives, contact dermatitis, migraines, or food allergies; you have a history of allergic reactions to medicines (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); you have high blood pressure, heart disease, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), urination difficulties, thyroid disease, diabetes, etc.; you are physically weak or have a high fever; you are elderly; you are currently receiving treatment from a doctor; or you have a cough (cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm; cough lasting for more than 1 week or recurring; chronic cough; cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Since drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
*   Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

#CN
### 💊 노즈그린연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解普通感冒（急性鼻炎）、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流鼻涕、打喷嚏、流泪、喉咙痛、头重感。

### 🕒 노즈그린연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   15岁以上成人及青少年，每次1粒，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 노즈그린연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   以下情况请勿服用本药：对本药及其成分有过敏史或过敏经验者；正在服用MAO抑制剂（如抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）或在停用MAO抑制剂后2周内者；未满3个月的婴儿。
*   服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师：本人、父母或兄弟姐妹有易患荨麻疹、接触性皮炎、偏头痛、食物过敏等体质者；曾因药物引起过敏反应（如发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）者；患有高血压、心脏病、青光眼（如眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病等疾病者；身体虚弱或有高热者；老年人；正在接受医生治疗者；患有咳嗽（吸烟引起的咳嗽、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽）者。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   服用后可能会出现睡意，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
*   服药期间请勿饮酒。
