#EN
### 💊 세피덤지엔크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin diseases (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis), and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 세피덤지엔크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 세피덤지엔크림 - Precautions
* None
* Do not use this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug or have experienced it before; if you have skin infections caused by bacteria (e.g., tuberculosis, syphilis), fungi (e.g., candidiasis, tinea), spirochetes, yeast, viruses (e.g., herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia), or animals (e.g., scabies, pubic lice); if you have eczematous external otitis with tympanic membrane perforation; if you have ulcers (except Behçet's disease); if you have second-degree deep burns or frostbite or worse; if you have perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, or rosacea; or if you have a hypersensitivity to aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or bacitracin, or have experienced it before.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a hypersensitivity to propylene glycol or have experienced it before, if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, if you are breastfeeding, or if you are an infant or child.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When using for children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not use continuously for a long period.

Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.

Do not use for non-therapeutic purposes such as after makeup or shaving.

Do not use occlusive dressing methods.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 3 to 4 weeks of use, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

For more details, refer to the detailed information in the approved product labeling.
