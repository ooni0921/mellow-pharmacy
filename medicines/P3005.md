#EN

* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 덴퍼스골드캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 덴퍼스골드캡슐 - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not take this medicine if you are a child under 15 years of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., rash, redness, itching) to medicines or cosmetics, experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to eggs, or have an allergic constitution.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
