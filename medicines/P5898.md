#EN
### 💊 Klidam-Jung - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for neuralgia, lumbago (lower back pain), and stiff shoulders.

### 🕒 Klidam-Jung - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults, take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, twice daily, during pain or an attack.

### ⚠️ Klidam-Jung - Precautions
*   No information
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you: have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), are an infant (nursing baby) infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, are an infant or child aged 14 or younger, have liver, kidney, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, are physically weak, have a high fever, are elderly, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after several doses, immediately stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
