#EN
### 💊 판제스트에프정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach, promoting digestion, and abdominal distension due to indigestion.

### 🕒 판제스트에프정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: Take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 판제스트에프정 - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not administer this medication to children under 7 years of age.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Yellow No. 4 (Tartrazine).
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 판제스트에프정 - 功效与作用
* 本药物用于消化不良、食欲不振、过食、积食、促进消化以及因消化不良引起的胃部胀满感。

### 🕒 판제스트에프정 - 用法与用量
* 成人：每次服用1至2片，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 판제제스트에프정 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 7岁以下儿童请勿服用本药物。
* 服用本药物前，有过敏体质、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、对黄色4号(柠檬黄)过敏或有其他过敏反应史者，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

#JP
