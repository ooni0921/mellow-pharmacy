#EN
### 💊 코코페디시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medication is used for fever reduction in cases of acute upper respiratory tract infections.

### 🕒 코코페디시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage & Administration
* For children aged 6 months and older, administer 0.4 to 0.6 mL/kg (5 to 7 mg/kg) per dose as needed, every 4 to 6 hours. Do not exceed a maximum of 4 doses per day (28 mg/kg).
* For children weighing 30 kg or less, do not exceed a maximum daily dose of 25 mL (300 mg).
* Refer to the approved product information for recommended dosages by age group.

### ⚠️ 코코페디시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
* Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, liver impairment, kidney impairment, heart failure, high blood pressure, bronchial asthma, asthma, urticaria, or allergies caused by aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics (or have a history thereof), patients undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and nursing mothers.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver impairment, kidney impairment, heart failure, high blood pressure, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, stomach cancer, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly, a child, or if you are taking low-dose aspirin for the prevention of myocardial infarction or stroke.

#CN
### 💊 코코페디시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药品用于治疗急性上呼吸道感染引起的退烧。

### 🕒 코코페디시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 用法用量
* 对于6个月及以上的儿童，按需服用，每次剂量为0.4~0.6 mL/kg（5~7 mg/kg），间隔4~6小时服用，每日最多不超过4次（28 mg/kg）。
* 对于体重在30kg及以下的儿童，每日最大剂量不超过25 mL（300 mg）。
* 请参考获批的产品说明书，了解各年龄段的推荐剂量。

### ⚠️ 코코페디시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 如果您每日定期饮酒三杯或以上，在服用本药品或其他解热镇痛药前，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能会引起胃肠道出血。
* 服用本药品可能会增加严重心血管血栓事件、心肌梗死、中风的风险，这些情况可能致命。此外，还可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
* 以下患者请勿服用本药品：对本药品过敏者、消化性溃疡、严重血液疾病、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全、高血压患者、支气管哮喘、因阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛药引起的哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏症患者或有相关病史者、接受冠状动脉旁路移植术前后疼痛治疗的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女。
* 在服用本药品前，如果您有过敏症、消化性溃疡、血液疾病、出血倾向、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全、高血压、系统性红斑狼疮、混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、胃癌、克罗恩病，或您是老年人、儿童，或您正在服用低剂量阿司匹林以预防心肌梗死或中风，请咨询医生或药剂师。
