#EN
### 💊 디크로닉정250밀리그램(클로닉신리시네이트)(수출용) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of mild or moderate pain such as muscle pain, neuralgia, post-traumatic and post-operative pain, headache, toothache, and earache.

### 🕒 디크로닉정250밀리그램(클로닉신리시네이트)(수출용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1/2 to 1 tablet (125-250 mg) per dose, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 디크로닉정250밀리그램(클로닉신리시네이트)(수출용) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have: peptic ulcers; gastric or duodenal disturbance/upset caused by drugs; hypersensitivity to this medicine; a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors); are undergoing treatment for pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; are pregnant; have severe hepatic impairment; severe renal impairment; severe heart failure; or inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have: a history of peptic ulcer; are a child or elderly; have hypertension; fluid retention or heart failure; bronchial asthma; hepatic impairment or a history thereof; renal impairment or a history thereof; or are a lactating woman.
