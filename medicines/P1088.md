#EN
### 💊 리버올라액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjuvant treatment for liver diseases.

### 🕒 리버올라액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 10-30 mL daily, divided into doses between meals. Dilute 10 mL of this medication in about half a glass of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 리버올라액 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have or have experienced kidney disease or myocardial infarction.
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to caramel.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medication is for oral use only; do not use for injection.
* Do not use as an antidote for alcohol poisoning.
* If there is no improvement after approximately one month of use, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 리버올라액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于肝病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 리버올라액 - 用法用量
* 成人每日分次在两餐之间服用10-30 mL。将本药10 mL稀释（变淡）于约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 리버올라액 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 肾病、心肌梗塞患者及有相关病史者请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，对焦糖过敏的患者或有过敏史者，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
* 本药仅供内服，不可用于注射。
* 请勿将其用作酒精中毒的解毒剂。
* 服用约1个月后若无改善，请停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
