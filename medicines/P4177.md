#EN
### 💊 디뉴펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medication is used as an adjunct for the treatment of chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful edema or inflammation following trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 디뉴펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults should orally administer 300 mg once, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 1,200 mg per day.

### ⚠️ 디뉴펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Individuals who regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication increases the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* This medication should not be taken by patients with hypersensitivity to this drug; patients with existing gastrointestinal ulcers or signs thereof, or those with a history of recurrence; patients with bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular, or other sites; severe blood disorders; severe liver dysfunction; severe renal dysfunction; severe heart failure; severe hypertension; bronchial asthma or a history thereof; patients who have experienced asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors); patients experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; pregnant women beyond 6 months of gestation and breastfeeding mothers; patients with inflammatory bowel diseases such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis; patients with a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug treatment; and patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver dysfunction, renal dysfunction, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, hypersensitivity; if you have systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, are a child over 6 years old, have stomach cancer, are an alcoholic; if you are in early or mid-pregnancy, planning pregnancy, or may be pregnant; if you have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or cardiovascular risk factors (hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking); if you have hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss, have blood coagulation disorders, or have a history of gastrointestinal ulcers due to long-term use of non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs.

#CN
### 💊 디뉴펜정(덱시부프로펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于辅助治疗慢性多发性关节炎、类风湿性关节炎、关节病、强直性脊柱炎、外伤及手术后疼痛性水肿或炎症，以及伴有炎症、疼痛和发热的感染症。

### 🕒 디뉴펜정(덱시부프로펜) - 用法与用量
* 成人每次口服300毫克，每日2至4次。但每日总量不得超过1,200毫克。

### ⚠️ 디뉴펜정(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 每天定期饮酒三杯以上的患者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药时，发生可能致命的严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死、中风的风险增加，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
* 对本药过敏的患者、有胃肠道溃疡或其征兆的患者、或有复发史的患者；胃肠道、脑血管或其他部位出血、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心力衰竭、严重高血压、支气管哮喘或有哮喘病史的患者；对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药（包括COX-2抑制剂）有过哮喘、荨麻疹或过敏反应史的患者；冠状动脉旁路移植术(CABG)前后出现疼痛的患者；怀孕6个月以上的孕妇及哺乳期妇女；克罗恩病或溃疡性结肠炎等炎症性肠病患者；曾因非甾体抗炎药治疗而出现胃肠道出血或穿孔的患者；以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。

在服用本药前，如果您有或曾有血液异常、出血倾向、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、体液潴留或心力衰竭、高血压、过敏史；如果您患有系统性红斑狼疮(SLE)和混合性结缔组织病(MCTD)，是老年人，是6岁以上儿童，患有胃癌，酒精中毒；如果您处于妊娠早期或中期，计划怀孕，或可能怀孕；如果您患有缺血性心脏病、周围动脉疾病、脑血管疾病或有心血管疾病风险因素（高血压、高血脂、糖尿病、吸烟）；如果您患有肝性卟啉病，曾接受导致失血的重大外科手术，有血液凝固障碍，或曾因长期服用非甾体抗炎药而导致消化道溃疡，请咨询医生或药剂师。
