#EN
### 💊 젤콤정(플루벤다졸) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the treatment of infections by roundworm, pinworm, whipworm, and hookworm, as well as mixed infections thereof.

### 🕒 젤콤정(플루벤다졸) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children should take 1 tablet (500 mg) as a single dose.

### ⚠️ 젤콤정(플루벤다졸) - Precautions
*   Information not available.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine and pregnant women should not take this medicine.
*   Infants under 1 year of age, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
