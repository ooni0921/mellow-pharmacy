* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1일 1회, 1회 1정을 식후 복용합니다.

* 정보 없음
* 고칼슘혈증(혈액중에 칼슘이 과잉으로 존재하는 상태), 유육종증(원인을 알 수 없는 전신 염증질환), 신장질환, 이 약에 과민증이 있는 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 신장결석, 심한 증상의 신부전, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

각종 요검사시에 혈당의 검출을 방해할 수 있으며, 요를 황색으로 변하게 하여 임상검사치에 영향을 줄 수 있습니다.

#EN
### 💊 파워비큐액티브정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, D, and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, and for the alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 파워비큐액티브정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years and older: take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 파워비큐액티브정 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are an infant under 12 months, or if you have kidney stones, severe renal failure, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, glucose-galactose malabsorption, or other genetic problems.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are currently receiving medical treatment, if you are a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), if you are pregnant, may be pregnant, or breastfeeding, if you are a premature infant, an infant, if you have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, if you are taking cardiac glycosides, or if you have gout or kidney stones.
* This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar in various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN

* 本品用于补充体力疲劳、妊娠哺乳期、病中病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降、发育期、老年期的维生素B1、B2、B6、C、D、E；预防骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病；以及缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩周炎等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎、脚气、眼睛疲劳等症状。

### 🕒 파워비큐액티브정 - 用法与用量
* 12岁以上儿童及成人：每日1次，每次1片，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 파워비큐액티브정 - 注意事项
* 高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高）、结节病（不明原因的全身炎症性疾病）、肾脏疾病、对本品过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本品。
* 服用本品前，请咨询医生或药剂师：正在接受医生治疗的患者、经常晒太阳并正常饮食的儿童、高草酸尿症（尿液中草酸盐排出过多的状态）、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性和哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、心血管系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、正在服用强心苷的人、痛风患者或肾结石患者。
* 本品可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检测结果。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
