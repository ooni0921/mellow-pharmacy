# EN

### 💊 미니메이드정(수출용) - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjunctive therapy for weight loss by reducing food intake.

### 🕒 미니메이드정(수출용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day before meals. If necessary, an additional 2 tablets may be taken before bedtime.
*   It is recommended to take with a sufficient amount of water.
*   It is recommended to take the recommended dose for at least 3 to 4 weeks.

### ⚠️ 미니메이드정(수출용) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, infants, and children should not take this medication.
*   Pregnant or breastfeeding women, and patients with digestive disorders should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication for more than 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# JP

### 💊 미니메이드정(수출용) - 効能及び効果
*   本剤は、食物摂取量の減少による体重減少の補助療法として使用します。

*   推奨量を最低3〜4週間服用することが推奨されます。

*   情報なし。

*   本剤を服用する前に、妊婦および授乳婦、消化器障害の患者は医師または薬剤師に相談してください。

*   1ヶ月以上服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
