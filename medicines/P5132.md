# 티아셀포르테액

##EN
### 💊 티아셀포르테액 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamin B₁ during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, and periods of physical debility during or after illness, as well as for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, and for the supplementation of selenium.

### 🕒 티아셀포르테액 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older and adults, take 1 sachet twice daily.

### ⚠️ 티아셀포르테액 - Precautions
* Information not available
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or any of its ingredients, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
* Before taking this medicine, patients receiving medical treatment, patients with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcers, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, patients with cardiovascular dysfunction, patients with renal impairment, and patients with hypoproteinemia should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
