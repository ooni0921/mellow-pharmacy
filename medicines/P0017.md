#EN
### 💊 Aronalmin Gold Tablet - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, E, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during illness or convalescence (during illness or after recovery), and in old age (when containing cod liver oil, vitamin D, E). It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 Aronalmin Gold Tablet - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ Aronalmin Gold Tablet - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have gout or kidney stones, have a folic acid deficiency, have a thrombotic predisposition, or have hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning the urine yellow.
