#EN
### 💊 새로덱스정100밀리그램(덱스판테놀) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for hair loss.

### 🕒 새로덱스정100밀리그램(덱스판테놀) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day.

The treatment period is 6 weeks.

### ⚠️ 새로덱스정100밀리그램(덱스판테놀) - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are under 19 years of age, have conditions other than alopecia, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, or have a history of allergic reactions to medications.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 6 weeks of use, immediately discontinue the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

To avoid drug interactions, inform your doctor or pharmacist of your current treatment status.

If any adverse reactions occur while taking this medication, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
