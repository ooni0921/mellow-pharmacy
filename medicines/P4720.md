#EN
### 💊 이부스타플라스타(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory relief of osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia, and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 이부스타플라스타(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   After peeling off the release liner from the medication surface, apply it to the affected area once a day.

### ⚠️ 이부스타플라스타(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to it, are a child under 12 years of age, are in the third trimester of pregnancy or breastfeeding, have experienced hypersensitivity (bronchospasm, angioedema, asthma, allergic rhinitis, rash, anaphylactic reaction) due to aspirin or other NSAIDs, have bronchial asthma, have or have experienced gastrointestinal bleeding, have or have experienced gastric/duodenal ulcers or peptic ulcers, have severe heart failure, or have severe kidney or liver damage.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medication if you are elderly, have chronic bronchitis, allergic rhinitis or inflammation of the nasal mucosa (more frequent asthma attacks or severe inflammatory reactions of the skin and mucous membranes), have a history of gastrointestinal diseases (e.g., gastrointestinal ulcers, bleeding or perforation, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease),
*   have liver or kidney impairment or kidney failure, have high blood pressure, are pregnant or planning to become pregnant, have kidney disease, have allergic symptoms to drugs or cosmetics (e.g., rash, redness, itching, dermatitis due to poison ivy, etc.), have an allergic constitution, have severe wetness (moisture) or erosion, or have circulatory disorders or diabetes.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medication is for external use only and should not be taken orally.
*   Do not use on or around the eyes or mucous membranes, and be careful not to let it get into your eyes.
*   Do not use occlusive dressings.
