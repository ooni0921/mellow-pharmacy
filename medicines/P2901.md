#EN
### 💊 신일겔정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and sour belching.

### 🕒 신일겔정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: Take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day. For individuals aged 8 to under 15 years: Take 1 tablet at a time, 3 times a day. Take between meals (between mealtimes) and at bedtime. The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 신일겔정 - Precautions
* No information provided
* Do not take this medicine if you are undergoing dialysis therapy or are a child under 7 years of age. Before taking this medicine, patients with kidney impairment should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
