### 💊 트라몰정160밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain (ache) due to colds, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, joint pain, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 트라몰정160밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years and younger should take the recommended single dose every 4-6 hours as needed. This medicine should be taken at the lowest effective dose for the shortest possible duration, and should not exceed 5 doses (75 mg/kg) per day. If the child's weight is known, taking the dose according to weight (10-15 mg/kg) is more appropriate. Refer to the approved product information for recommended doses by age group.

### ⚠️ 트라몰정160밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker of three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body, so it should not be taken by patients with phenylketonuria who need to regulate phenylalanine intake. Rarely, severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (induced asthma attack by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history thereof, patients taking barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, people who have consumed alcohol, patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, a child under 2 years of age, pregnant or breastfeeding.

### ⚠️ 트라몰정160밀리그람(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项

* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液异常患者、严重肝功能障碍患者、严重肾功能（肾脏）障碍患者、严重心功能低下患者、阿司匹林哮喘（由非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘发作）患者或有相关病史者、正在服用巴比妥类药物或三环类抗抑郁药的患者、饮酒者、有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，肝功能障碍或有相关病史者、肾功能（肾脏）障碍或有相关病史者、有消化性溃疡病史者、血液异常或有相关病史者、有出血倾向的患者、有心功能异常的患者、有过敏史者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、2岁以下儿童、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药剂师。
