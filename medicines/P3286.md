#EN
### 💊 엘코스텐캡슐(에르도스테인) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for mucolytic and expectorant purposes in acute and chronic respiratory diseases.

### 🕒 엘코스텐캡슐(에르도스테인) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 2 to 3 times daily. For acute respiratory diseases, do not take continuously for more than 10 days.

### ⚠️ 엘코스텐캡슐(에르도스테인) - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have:
    * Hypersensitivity to this drug.
    * Liver cirrhosis.
    * Cystathionine synthase deficiency.
    * Peptic ulcer.
    * Severe renal impairment (creatinine clearance [information missing]).
* Consult your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you have:
    * Mild to moderate hepatic impairment.
    * Are pregnant or may be pregnant.
    * Are breastfeeding.
    * Are hypersensitive to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

#CN
### 💊 엘코스텐캡슐(에르도스테인) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于急慢性呼吸道疾病的黏液溶解和祛痰。

### 🕒 엘코스텐캡슐(에르도스테인) - 用法用量
* 成人一次1粒（300毫克），每日2~3次服用。用于急性呼吸道疾病时，请勿连续服用超过10天。

### ⚠️ 엘코스텐캡슐(에르도스테인) - 注意事项
* 若有以下情况，请勿服用本药：
    * 对本药过敏的患者。
    * 肝硬化。
    * 胱硫醚-β-合成酶缺乏症。
    * 消化性溃疡。
    * 严重肾功能障碍（肌酐清除率 [信息缺失]）。
* 若有以下情况，请在服用本药前咨询医生或药师：
    * 轻度及中度肝功能障碍患者。
    * 孕妇或可能怀孕的女性及哺乳期妇女。
    * 对日落黄（黄色5号）过敏的患者。
