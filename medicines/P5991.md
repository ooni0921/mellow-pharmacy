#EN
### 💊 제스그린정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, gastric discomfort, bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite, overeating, and promotion of digestion.

### 🕒 제스그린정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, with or between meals.

### ⚠️ 제스그린정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a child under 15 years of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing woman, a patient undergoing dialysis, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of allergic reactions such as rash, redness, or itching, or if you are a patient with kidney disorder, liver disorder, heart disorder, hypophosphatemia, edema, high blood pressure, an elderly patient, or a patient with a history of kidney disease.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately stop taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
