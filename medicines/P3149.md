#EN
### 💊 지아이스피드더블현탁액 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to improve symptoms such as acid reflux, heartburn, and indigestion caused by reflux of stomach contents (e.g., after meals, during pregnancy, or when accompanied by reflux esophagitis).

### 🕒 지아이스피드더블현탁액 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age and older should take 10-20 mL once, four times a day (after meals and at bedtime).

### ⚠️ 지아이스피드더블현탁액 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to it. Before taking this medication, patients with congestive heart failure, renal impairment, hypercalcemia, nephrocalcinosis, recurrent calcium-containing kidney stones, and children under 12 years of age should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 지아이스피드더블현탁액 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于改善胃酸反流、胃灼热以及因胃内容物反流引起的消化不良（如餐后、妊娠期间、伴有反流性食管炎等）症状。

### 🕒 지아이스피드더블현탁액 - 用法与用量
*   成人及12岁以上儿童，每次服用10~20 mL，每日4次（餐后及睡前）。

### ⚠️ 지아이스피드더블현탁액 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药过敏的患者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，患有充血性心力衰竭、肾功能障碍、高钙血症、肾钙沉着症和复发性含钙肾结石的患者，以及12岁以下儿童，请咨询医生或药师。
