#EN
### 💊 비케어정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, B6 and zinc during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness, growth period, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, and to relieve eczema and dermatitis.

### 🕒 비케어정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children over 12 years of age: Take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 비케어정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, a history of soy oil hypersensitivity or allergic reactions, or soy or peanut hypersensitivity.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant, an infant, an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sun exposure and has a normal diet, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or require cautious administration of lipid emulsions.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

This drug may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.
