#EN
### 💊 덴스트롱캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the adjunctive treatment of gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 덴스트롱캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 덴스트롱캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this drug if you are a child under 15 years of age, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching) to medicines or cosmetics, allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to eggs, if you have an allergic constitution, or if you have hypersensitivity or have experienced it to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
