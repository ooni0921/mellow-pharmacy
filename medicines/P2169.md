### 🕒 Sokkurinsan - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older and adults, take 1 sachet per dose. For individuals aged 8 to 14 years, take 1/2 sachet per dose. Administer 3 times a day, after meals or between meals.

### ⚠️ Sokkurinsan - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient undergoing dialysis, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, patients with kidney disorders should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. Do not take long-term. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
