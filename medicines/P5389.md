#EN
### 💊 화이투벤큐플러스노즈연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 화이투벤큐플러스노즈연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 15 years or older: take 2 capsules once, 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.
* For children aged 8 years to under 15 years: take 1 capsule once, 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 화이투벤큐플러스노즈연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker of three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this medication with other products containing acetaminophen.
* If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold/expectorant medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age; an infant (baby) or child under 15 years of age suspected of or infected with chickenpox or influenza; experiencing symptoms of ischemic colitis (such as sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) related to pseudoephedrine; have an allergic constitution; have a history of allergic reactions (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.); have difficulty with urination; are elderly; are frail or have a high fever; have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; are breastfeeding; or have a cough (due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting more than 1 week or recurring, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).
* If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
