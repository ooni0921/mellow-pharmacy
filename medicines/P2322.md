# EN
### 💊 번세이프크림(구아야줄렌) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for eczema, burns, erosions, and ulcers caused by other diseases.

### 🕒 번세이프크림(구아야줄렌) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 번세이프크림(구아야줄렌) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it. Before using this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity to propylene glycol or those who have experienced it should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
