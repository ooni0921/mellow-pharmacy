#EN
### 💊 쎈비큐콘드로정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength declines during or after illness (during illness or recovery), during growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation inside the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis. Furthermore, it is used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 쎈비큐콘드로정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 19 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 쎈비큐콘드로정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (excess calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, an infant under 12 months, kidney stones, severe renal failure, a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to soybean oil, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, premature infants, infants, patients with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders, or patients who require careful administration of lipid emulsions should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medication may turn urine yellow and may affect clinical test results.

Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
