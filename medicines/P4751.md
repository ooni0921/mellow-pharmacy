#EN
### 💊 벤포액티브프리미엄정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for neurological disorders caused by vitamin B1 and B6 deficiencies.

### 🕒 벤포액티브프리미엄정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 벤포액티브프리미엄정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication.
* Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
* If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication for about 1 month, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

#CN
### 💊 벤포액티브프리미엄정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗维生素B1和B6缺乏引起的神经系统疾病。

### 🕒 벤포액티브프리미엄정 - 用法用量
* 成人每次1片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 벤포액티브프리미엄정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者不应服用本药。
* 孕妇及哺乳期妇女在服用本药前应咨询医生或药剂师。
* 如服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停药并咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

#JP
