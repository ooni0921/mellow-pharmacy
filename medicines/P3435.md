#EN
### 💊 사이프록실액(시클로피록스올아민) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for seborrheic dermatitis.

### 🕒 사이프록실액(시클로피록스올아민) - Dosage and Administration
* Wet the hair with water, then apply a sufficient amount of this medication to create lather. Massage thoroughly with fingertips. After 3-5 minutes, rinse the hair several times with water to wash it off completely. Use 2-3 times a week or as needed for the treatment of scalp conditions.

### ⚠️ 사이프록실액(시클로피록스올아민) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to it, or on areas around the eyes, mucous membranes (oral cavity, nasal cavity, vagina), pubic area, vulva, eczema, wet lesions, erosions (ulcerations), cracks, or severely traumatized lesions (diseased areas). Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.), if the affected area is on the face or is extensive, if it is purulent (suppurating), if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, or if you are a child under 12 years of age. This medication should only be applied externally to the scalp and adjacent areas. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not use immediately if the affected area or its surroundings are dirty. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

* 本剤は、週に2〜3回、または頭皮疾患の治療時に必要な場合に使用します。髪を水で濡らした後、泡立つ程度の十分な量を塗布し、指先で十分にマッサージします。3〜5分後、髪を水で数回すすぎ、完全に洗い流してください。
