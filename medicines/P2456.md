#EN
### 💊 미로틱필름코팅정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for vitamin B6 supplementation during periods of physical decline, such as pregnancy, lactation, or during/after illness. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 미로틱필름코팅정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 미로틱필름코팅정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before administering this medicine to infants under 1 year of age, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 미로틱필름코팅정 - 功效與作用
*   本藥用於懷孕、哺乳期或病中、病後體力下降時補充維生素B6，並緩解口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、濕疹、皮膚炎等症狀。

### 🕒 미로틱필름코팅정 - 用法用量
*   8歲以上兒童及成人：每次1片，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 미로틱필름코팅정 - 注意事項
*   無可用資訊。
*   對本藥過敏的患者、未滿3個月的嬰兒、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遺傳性問題的患者，請勿服用本藥。
*   在給未滿1歲的嬰兒服用本藥前，請諮詢醫生或藥劑師。
*   請嚴格遵守規定的用法用量。
*   給兒童服用時，請在監護人的指導和監督下服用。
*   若服用約1個月後症狀仍無改善，請立即停止服用並諮詢醫生或藥劑師。
