#EN
### 💊 위엔자임듀얼정 - Effects and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach, promoting digestion, and abdominal bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 위엔자임듀얼정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and over, take 2 tablets once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 위엔자임듀얼정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, or if you are a child aged 7 years or younger.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or have experienced hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4).
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
