#EN
### 💊 팜플루콜드연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 팜플루콜드연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 15 years of age or older: Take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 팜플루콜드연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of Acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing Acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medication or its ingredients, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; an infant or toddler under 2 years of age; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a nursing mother; an elderly person; a frail individual; someone with an allergic constitution; or a history of allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); or have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric/duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, high fever, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain or other gastrointestinal problems, ulcers, bleeding problems; are a smoker; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting for more than one week or recurring, a chronic cough; or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
