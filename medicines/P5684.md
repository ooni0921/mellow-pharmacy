###EN
### 💊 이지클린장용연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of constipation and related symptoms such as loss of appetite, abdominal distension, abnormal fermentation in the intestines, and hemorrhoid symptoms.

### 🕒 이지클린장용연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 years or older: Take 2 capsules once daily at bedtime.
* For the initial dose, take the minimum amount. Observe the shape and condition of the stool, then gradually increase or decrease the dosage.

### ⚠️ 이지클린장용연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have acute abdominal diseases such as appendicitis, intestinal hemorrhage, or ulcerative colitis, intestinal obstruction, hypersensitivity to soybean oil or a history of it, or hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 10 years of age, a pregnant or possibly pregnant woman, a patient with severe abdominal pain, nausea, or vomiting, a person on a sodium-restricted diet, someone with a history of rectal bleeding or intestinal dysfunction, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient with fat overload, or a patient with hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) or a history of it.
* If constipation does not improve after taking this medicine for about 1 week, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not continue to take it for more than 1 week.
* Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

* 无信息
* 以下患者请勿服用本药：阑尾炎、肠出血、溃疡性结肠炎等急性腹部疾病患者；肠梗阻患者；对大豆油过敏或有过敏史的患者；对大豆或花生过敏的患者。
