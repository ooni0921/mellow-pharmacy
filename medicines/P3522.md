#EN
### 💊 아이수점안액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for conjunctivitis (epidemic ophthalmia), stye, blepharitis (inflammation of the eyelids), and eye itching.

### 🕒 아이수점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 1 to 3 drops, 3 to 6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 아이수점안액 - Precautions
* No specific additional information available.
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are hypersensitive to this drug or sulfonamides, or if you have severe eye pain.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
* Do not use continuously for a long period.
* This medicine is for ophthalmic use only.
* Do not use as a soft contact lens wetting solution or while wearing soft contact lenses.
* To prevent contamination, avoid sharing with others if possible.
* Be careful not to let the tip of the container touch your eyelids or eyelashes, as eye discharge or mold can contaminate or cloud the solution.
* Do not use if the solution is turbid (cloudy).
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if symptoms do not improve after 3-4 days of administration.

#CN
### 💊 아이수점안액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于结膜炎（流行性眼炎）、麦粒肿、睑缘炎（眼睑糜烂）和眼睛瘙痒。

### 🕒 아이수점안액 - 用法用量
* 每日3～6次，每次1～3滴。

### ⚠️ 아이수점안액 - 注意事项
* 暂无额外信息。
* 在使用本药前，如果您对此药或磺胺类药物过敏，或有严重的眼部疼痛，请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 请勿长期连续使用。
* 本药仅供滴眼用。
* 请勿用作软性隐形眼镜的润湿液或在佩戴软性隐形眼镜时使用。
* 为防止污染，请尽量避免与他人共用。
* 容器尖端接触眼睑及睫毛时，可能会被眼屎或霉菌等污染或混浊（浑浊），因此请注意不要直接接触眼睛。
* 如果药液混浊（浑浊），请勿使用。
* 如果使用3～4天后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药剂师。
