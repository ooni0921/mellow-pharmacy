#EN
### 💊 풀믹신연고 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the prevention of infection and pain relief caused by cuts, wounds, and burns.

### 🕒 풀믹신연고 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 2 years of age or older, apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 풀믹신연고 - Precautions
* Do not use this medicine in patients with hypersensitivity to aminoglycoside antibiotics and bacitracin, or in patients with tympanic membrane perforation.
* For external use only.
* Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
* If symptoms do not improve after using for more than 2 weeks, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 풀믹신연고 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于预防和缓解刀伤、创伤、烧伤引起的感染及疼痛。

### 🕒 풀믹신연고 - 用法用量
* 2岁以上患者，每日1～3次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ 풀믹신연고 - 注意事项
* 对氨基糖苷类抗生素及杆菌肽过敏的患者，以及鼓膜穿孔的患者请勿使用本药。
* 仅限外用。
* 请勿用于眼科。
* 如果使用2周以上症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
