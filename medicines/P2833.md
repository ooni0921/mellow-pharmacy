#EN

### 💊 맥스케어프리미엄정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and during or after illness (while sick or after recovery) when physical strength is low. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye strain, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 맥스케어프리미엄정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years and older, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 맥스케어프리미엄정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age and patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
*   May cause urine to turn yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 💊 맥스케어프리미엄정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解体力疲劳、妊娠哺乳期、病中病后（患病期间或恢复后）体力下降时维生素B1、B2、B6的补充，以及神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬等）的症状，同时缓解脚气、眼睛疲劳、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。

### 🕒 맥스케어프리미엄정 - 用法与用量
*   8岁以上及成人，一次1片，一日1次。

### ⚠️ 맥스케어프리미엄정 - 注意事项
*   无可用信息。
*   对本药过敏的患者、未满3个月的婴儿、以及患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、叶酸缺乏症患者请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药可能使尿液变黄，并影响临床检查值。
*   如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
