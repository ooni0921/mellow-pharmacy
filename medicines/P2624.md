# EN
### 💊 벤포메가정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, D, E and zinc, and for the relief of symptoms such as physical fatigue, physical debility during pregnancy and lactation, illness or convalescence (while sick or after recovery), growth period, and old age. It also helps alleviate neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilosis), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, poor development of bones and teeth, and prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 벤포메가정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years or older and adults, take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 벤포메가정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, or have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, or kidney stones.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and has a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with impaired cardiac or circulatory system function, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or a history of kidney stones, a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about one month, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
