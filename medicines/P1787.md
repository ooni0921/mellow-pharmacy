#EN
### 💊 칼시롤정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, to prevent bone and teeth underdevelopment and rickets, and to supplement calcium in cases of calcium deficiency and other calcium supplementation needs.

### 🕒 칼시롤정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years or older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 칼시롤정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, an infant under 12 months, a patient with hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, or a patient with or experienced hypersensitivity to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 칼시롤정 - 功效与作用
*   本药品用于补充妊娠期、哺乳期、发育期、老年期的维生素D，预防骨骼、牙齿发育不良和佝偻病，以及补充钙缺乏症和其他需要补充钙的情况。

### 🕒 칼시롤정 - 用法与用量
*   成人及8岁以上的儿童，每次1片，每日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 칼시롤정 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药品过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态）、结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本药品。
*   在服用本药品之前，如果儿童经常晒太阳并正常饮食，或者您是孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、心血管系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、有肾结石病史者、对日落黄F.C.F. (黄色5号) 过敏或有过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下进行。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。

### 🕒 칼시롤정 - 用法・用量

*   小児に服用させる場合には、保護者の指導監督のもとに服用させてください。
*   約1ヶ月服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
