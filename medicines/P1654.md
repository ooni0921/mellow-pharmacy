# EN
### 💊 리포직점안겔(카보머) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to relieve symptoms of dry eyes.

### 🕒 리포직점안겔(카보머) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should instill 1 drop into the conjunctival sac 2 to 5 times daily, depending on the severity of symptoms. When instilling at bedtime, do so approximately 30 minutes before sleep.

Generally, the treatment of dry keratoconjunctivitis requires long-term or continuous therapy, so you should consult an ophthalmologist.

### ⚠️ 리포직점안겔(카보머) - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to it.

Before using this medication, patients with severe eye pain, those who have experienced allergic symptoms due to eye drops (e.g., eye hyperemia, itching, swelling, redness), pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, nursing mothers, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Use this medication for ophthalmic instillation only.

Remove contact lenses before using this medication and reinsert them 30 minutes after use.

If other eye drops are used concurrently, administer them at least 15 minutes apart, and always use this medication last.

Blurred vision may occur after using this medication; therefore, exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

Close the cap after use.

Administer in small drops to avoid stickiness on the eyelids.

Be careful not to let the tip of the container touch the eyeball. If the tip touches the eyelids or eyelashes, the solution may become contaminated or cloudy with eye discharge or fungi. Therefore, use caution and do not use if the solution is cloudy.

To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the medication if possible.

# CN
### 💊 리포직점안겔(카보머) - 功效与效果
*   本药品用于缓解眼睛干涩症状。

### 🕒 리포직점안겔(카보머) - 用法与用量
*   成人根据症状严重程度，每次1滴，每日2~5次滴入结膜囊内。睡前滴药时，应在睡前约30分钟滴入。

通常，干性角结膜炎的治疗需要长期或持续性治疗，因此应咨询眼科医生。

### ⚠️ 리포직점안겔(카보머) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药品过敏的患者请勿使用。

在使用本药品前，患有严重眼痛的患者、曾因眼药水出现过敏症状（如眼睛充血、瘙痒、肿胀、红肿等）者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、儿童，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

本药品仅供滴眼使用。

使用本药品前应摘除隐形眼镜，并在使用本药品30分钟后重新佩戴。

如果同时使用其他滴眼剂，请至少间隔15分钟使用，并始终最后使用本药品。

使用本药品后可能会出现视力模糊，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

使用后请盖紧瓶盖。

为避免眼睑粘连，请以小滴方式给药。

请注意避免容器尖端接触眼球。如果容器尖端接触到眼睑或睫毛，药液可能会被眼屎或霉菌等污染或混浊，因此请注意，请勿使用已混浊的药液。

为防止污染，请尽量避免共用。

一般的に、乾燥性角結膜炎の治療は長期間または継続的な治療が必要なため、眼科医にご相談ください。
