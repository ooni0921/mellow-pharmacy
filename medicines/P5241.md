### 🕒 위제로알파정 - 용법 및 용량
* 만 15세 이상 소아 및 성인은 1회 3정씩, 만 8세 이상~만 15세 미만은 1회 2정씩, 1일 3회 식후 또는 식간(식사때와 식사때 사이에)에 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 위제로알파정 - 주의사항
* 정보 없음
* 투석요법을 받고 있는 환자, 만 7세 이하의 어린이, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당(젖당) 분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 위제로알파정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, gastric bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and gastric bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 위제로알파정 - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 15 years and older and adults, take 3 tablets per dose. For children aged 8 years and older but under 15 years, take 2 tablets per dose. Administer 3 times a day, after meals or between meals (between mealtimes).

### ⚠️ 위제로알파정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are a patient undergoing dialysis therapy, a child aged 7 years or younger, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have kidney (renal) impairment, an allergic constitution, are pregnant or may be pregnant, have phosphate deficiency, have tartrazine (Yellow No. 4) hypersensitivity or a history of allergic reactions, or are on a sodium-restricted diet.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.
