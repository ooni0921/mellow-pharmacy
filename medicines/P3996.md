#EN
### 💊 코감노즈정 - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 코감노즈정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 코감노즈정 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker who consumes three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, stop taking it immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant (newborn, baby) or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, a child under 2 years old, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic reactions (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, edema (swelling), gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, experience heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, ulcers, bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding, or if you have a history of smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts for more than a week or recurs, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after taking 5 to 6 doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
