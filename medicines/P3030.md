#EN
### 💊 트릴로신정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of inflammatory edema after surgery and trauma, and in cases of galactostasis.

### 🕒 트릴로신정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2 tablets per dose, four times daily on the first day, and then 1 tablet per dose, four times daily as a maintenance dose. The mechanism of action in the body is still largely unknown, and the dose-effect relationship has not been clarified. Therefore, do not administer without a specific purpose.

### ⚠️ 트릴로신정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine. Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, patients with blood coagulation disorders, severe liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, or those with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 (Tartrazine) should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
