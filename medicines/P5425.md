### 💊 맥스타민메가맥스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used to supplement vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, illness and convalescence, growth periods, and old age. It is also used for poor bone and tooth development, prevention of rickets, relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilosis), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 맥스타민메가맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take one tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 맥스타민메가맥스정 - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to it, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, a toddler, or if you have hyperoxaluria, gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis (Crohn's disease), cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, are receiving cardiac glycosides, have gout or kidney stones, or have a history of kidney stones.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This drug may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
