# EN
### 💊 로페랄연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of Vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness (during illness or after recovery) when physical strength is depleted, and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in the hands and feet, and cold hands and feet (peripheral circulatory disorder) during menopause.

### 🕒 로페랄연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age or older should take 1 capsule per dose, 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 로페랄연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, a young child, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or other lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or a patient who requires cautious administration of lipid emulsions, a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient with or who has experienced hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 로페랄연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本品用于缓解体力疲劳，妊娠期、哺乳期、患病期间或病后恢复期体力下降时，以及老年期维生素E、B1、B2、B6、C的补充。同时，也可用于缓解更年期时由末梢血液循环障碍引起的肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木和手脚冰凉等症状。

### 🕒 로페랄연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   8岁以上及成人，每次1粒，每日3次。

### ⚠️ 로페랄연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本品或大豆油、大豆、花生有过敏症的患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿，请勿服用本品。

服用本品前，请咨询医生或药师：未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期女性、早产儿、幼儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）患者、高脂蛋白血症患者、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需谨慎使用脂质乳剂的患者、预计因脂肪超负荷有特殊风险的患者、有血栓（血管堵塞）性素因的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者、对黄色5号有过敏症或曾有过敏史的患者。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

本品在进行各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。
