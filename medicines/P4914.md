#EN
### 💊 액티브메가비타정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the following: supplementation of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness, growth period, and old age; poor development of bones and teeth; prevention of rickets; relief of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (inflammation of the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation in the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis; and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 액티브메가비타정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years or older and adults, take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 액티브메가비타정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to any component of this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, are an infant under 12 months old, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, are a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout or kidney stones, or a history of kidney stones.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking the medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
