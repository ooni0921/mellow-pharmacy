#EN
### 💊 디클로펜24첩부제(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory treatment of osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), swelling and pain after trauma (e.g., contusion, sprain), and muscle pain.

### 🕒 디클로펜24첩부제(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area once a day.

### ⚠️ 디클로펜24첩부제(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you have experienced hypersensitivity to this drug, if you have or have experienced aspirin asthma (asthmatic attack due to NSAIDs, etc.), or if you are in the third trimester of pregnancy (late stage).

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma, hypersensitivity to propylene glycol (or a history thereof), are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are having difficulty conceiving or undergoing fertility tests, are breastfeeding, are a child, or are elderly.

Do not use on lesioned skin (exudative dermatitis, eczema, infected lesions, burns, wounds, etc.), eyes, or mucous membranes.

#CN
### 💊 디클로펜24첩부제(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - 功效与效果
* 本药用于治疗变形性关节炎、肩周炎、腱鞘炎、腱周炎、肱骨外上髁炎（如网球肘）、外伤后肿胀和疼痛（如挫伤、扭伤）、肌肉痛的镇痛和消炎。

### 🕒 디클로펜24첩부제(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - 用法与用量
* 每日1次贴于患处（疾病部位）。

### ⚠️ 디클로펜24첩부제(디클로페낙디에틸암모늄) - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 对本药有过敏史者、患有或曾患有阿司匹林哮喘（因非甾体抗炎药等引起的哮喘发作）的患者、妊娠第三期（晚期）的女性，请勿使用本药。

在使用本药前，支气管哮喘患者、对丙二醇过敏或有相关病史的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、难以怀孕或正在接受不孕检查的女性、哺乳期妇女、儿童、老年人应咨询医生或药师。

请勿用于有病变的皮肤（渗出性皮炎、湿疹、感染病变、烧伤、伤口等）、眼睛和粘膜。

#JP
