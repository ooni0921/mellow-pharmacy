### 💊 나르펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜)(수출명:PANALTab.) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to cold, low back pain, dysmenorrhea, postoperative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injury (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 나르펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜)(수출명:PANALTab.) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults with rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, take 1/2 to 1.5 tablets (200-600 mg) per dose, 3-4 times daily. The maximum daily dose is 3200 mg. For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3-4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or cold, adults take 1/2 to 1 tablet (200-400 mg) per dose, 3-4 times daily. Children aged 11-14 years take 200-250 mg per dose, 7-10 years take 150-200 mg per dose, 3-6 years take 100-150 mg per dose, and 1-2 years take 50-100 mg per dose, orally 3-4 times daily. Children weighing less than 30 kg should not exceed a daily dose of 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid taking this medicine on an empty stomach. Do not exceed the recommended dose.

### ⚠️ 나르펜정400밀리그램(이부프로펜)(수출명:PANALTab.) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulcer, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors) or a history thereof, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), are in the last trimester of pregnancy, are receiving high-dose methotrexate for chemotherapy, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders or a history thereof, a bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis, hepatic impairment or a history thereof, renal impairment or a history thereof, cardiac dysfunction or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly or a child, are pregnant, breastfeeding, or have hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
