#EN
### 💊 더게인정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 더게인정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adolescents aged 12 years or older and adults should take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 더게인정 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients hypersensitive to this medicine, infants under 12 months, and patients with severe renal insufficiency should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, patients with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, or pregnant women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
