# EN
### 💊 신신티눈밴드(수출명:PARCHEparaCALLOSGHLInternacionalS.A) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for corns, calluses, and warts.

### 🕒 신신티눈밴드(수출명:PARCHEparaCALLOSGHLInternacionalS.A) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply this medicine so that its central part covers the affected area (diseased part), and usually replace it every 2 to 5 days.

### ⚠️ 신신티눈밴드(수출명:PARCHEparaCALLOSGHLInternacionalS.A) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine in patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, diabetes, circulatory disorders, around the eyes and mucous membranes, infected, inflamed, reddened (hyperemic and red), irritated areas, moles, congenital moles or warts, hairy warts, and warts in the genital area. Patients who have experienced allergic symptoms (rash, redness, itching, etc.) to drugs or cosmetics, infants and toddlers, those with renal failure, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant and nursing mothers, patients with purulent dermatitis, moist lesions, or erosion should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. Use this medicine only for external application. If you enter water with this medicine applied, the adhesive may melt and disappear, so be careful. Apply carefully, avoiding contact with eyes, nose, mouth, and other mucous membranes. If it gets into the eyes, rinse thoroughly with a sufficient amount of water. Be careful not to let it touch skin other than the affected area (diseased part).
