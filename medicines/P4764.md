#EN
### 💊 마이드롭점안액(폴리데옥시리보뉴클레오티드나트륨)(1회용) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for nutritional supply in ulcerative diseases of the cornea and conjunctiva caused by nutritional deficiency, and for micro-damage to the cornea and conjunctiva resulting from contact lens wear, etc.

### 🕒 마이드롭점안액(폴리데옥시리보뉴클레오티드나트륨)(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Instill 2-3 drops per application, 2-4 times daily.

Discard any remaining solution and the container after use.

### ⚠️ 마이드롭점안액(폴리데옥시리보뉴클레오티드나트륨)(1회용) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine should not use it.

Patients with significant corneal damage should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medicine.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration instructions.

When administering to children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine is for ophthalmic use only.

When using for the first time, discard the first 1-2 drops without instilling them.

This medicine is for single-use only after opening.

Be careful that if the tip of the container touches the eyelid or eyelashes, the solution may become contaminated or turbid (cloudy) by eye discharge or fungi, etc. Do not use turbid (cloudy) solution.

To prevent contamination, do not share this product if possible.

#CN

### ⚠️ 마이드롭점안액(폴리데옥시리보뉴클레오티드나트륨)(1회용) - 注意事项

若容器尖端接触眼睑或睫毛，药液可能会被眼屎或霉菌等污染或变得浑浊（模糊），请注意。此外，请勿使用浑浊（模糊）的药液。

为防止污染，请尽量避免与他人共用。
