#EN
### 💊 마이펜이부400연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), postoperative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 마이펜이부400연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic disease, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, take 200-600 mg once, 3-4 times a day. The maximum daily dose is 3,200 mg.

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3-4 doses.

For mild to moderate pain and colds, adults should take 200-400 mg once, 3-4 times a day. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

For migraines, adults should take 200-400 mg once. Do not exceed 400 mg in a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 마이펜이부400연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg/day) should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, have or have experienced gastrointestinal ulcers or bleeding, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, a history of bronchial asthma, hypersensitivity to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors), pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are in the last trimester of pregnancy or breastfeeding, or are receiving high-dose methotrexate for chemotherapy.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of blood abnormalities, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis or liver impairment, kidney impairment, cardiac dysfunction or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) or mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly or a child, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are taking low-dose aspirin for the prevention of myocardial infarction or stroke.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
