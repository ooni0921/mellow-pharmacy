#EN
### 💊 마그라민트리플액션정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for:
    * Supplementation of Vitamins D, E, B1, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness (during illness or after recovery) when physical strength is depleted, during growth, and in old age.
    * Prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, and rickets.
    * Alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders, and shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet) during menopause.
    * Alleviation of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as lower back pain, shoulder stiffness).
    * Alleviation of beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation in the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, and dermatitis.
    * Muscle spasms due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그라민트리플액션정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 마그라민트리플액션정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, or have severe renal insufficiency.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, or have or have had kidney stones.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
