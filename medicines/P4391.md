#EN
### 💊 키도멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of inflammatory symptoms accompanied by edema, and for edema following trauma or surgery.

### 🕒 키도멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years or older should take 1 tablet (100 mg) once, twice daily, 30 minutes before meals with water. Since many aspects of its mechanism of action in the body have not yet been elucidated, and the dose-effect relationship has not been fully established, do not take this medication without a clear purpose.

### ⚠️ 키도멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with severe blood coagulation disorders (e.g., hemophilia), severe liver or severe kidney disease (e.g., hemodialysis patients), patients receiving anticoagulants or antiplatelet agents, pregnant and lactating women, and children under 12 years of age should not take this medicine. Before taking this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 키도멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解伴有水肿的炎症症状，以及外伤或手术后的水肿。

### 🕒 키도멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - 用法与用量
*   成人及12岁以上儿童，每次1片（100毫克），每日2次，餐前30分钟与水一同服用。由于本品在体内的作用机制尚未完全阐明，且剂量与效果的关系也未明确，请勿无目的地服用。

### ⚠️ 키도멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   有严重凝血功能障碍（如血友病）、严重肝脏或严重肾脏疾病（如血液透析患者）、正在服用抗凝剂或抗血小板聚集剂的患者、孕妇及哺乳期妇女、以及12岁以下儿童，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，对黄色5号（日落黄FCF）过敏或有相关过敏史的患者应咨询医生或药师。

#JP

### 🕒 키도멜라인장용정(브로멜라인) - 用法・用量

*   情報なし
