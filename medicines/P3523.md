# EN

### 💊 오케이원스외용액(테르비나핀염산염) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for superficial fungal infections of the skin caused by dermatophytes (tinea pedis (athlete's foot)).

### 🕒 오케이원스외용액(테르비나핀염산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Before use, thoroughly wash and completely dry the affected area (diseased part). Apply a sufficient amount once to adequately cover the affected area and its surroundings. Even if no lesions (painful areas) are apparent on one foot, apply a thin layer to all spaces between the toes on both feet, to the entire sole, and up to approximately 1.5 cm above the sole. Allow it to dry for 1-2 minutes until a film forms. Do not rub the medication in when applying. To maximize the effect of this medication, it should not be washed off for 24 hours after application. Therefore, it is recommended to apply it after a shower or bath, and then wait until the same time the next day before washing your feet again.

### ⚠️ 오케이원스외용액(테르비나핀염산염) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, nursing mothers, and children under 15 years of age should not use this medication.
* Patients with lesions (body changes due to disease) that may be irritated by alcohol should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medication.
* Do not use occlusive dressings or wraps unless directed by a doctor.
* Apply this medication for external use only.
* Do not use for ophthalmic purposes on the cornea or conjunctiva. If it gets into the eyes, thoroughly rinse with a sufficient amount of water.
* Do not use on the face, nails, scalp, around the mouth, vaginal area, on fissures (cracks), or eroded (raw) areas.

# CN

### 💊 오케이원스외용액(테르비나핀염산염) - 功效及作用
* 本药用于由皮肤癣菌引起的皮肤真菌感染（足癣（脚癣，香港脚））。

### 🕒 오케이원스외용액(테르비나핀염산염) - 用法用量
* 使用前，请彻底清洗并完全干燥患处（病变部位）。仅一次，足量涂抹于患处及其周围，使其充分湿润。即使另一只脚没有出现病变（疼痛部位），也要在双脚脚趾间全部薄薄地涂抹，并涂抹于整个脚底和脚底以上约1.5厘米的高度，然后让其干燥1至2分钟，直至形成薄膜。涂抹本药时请勿揉搓。为提高本药的效果，涂抹后24小时内不应清洗，因此建议在淋浴或沐浴后涂抹，并等到次日同一时间后再次清洗双脚。

### ⚠️ 오케이원스외용액(테르비나핀염산염) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、15岁以下儿童请勿使用本药。
* 使用本药前，患有酒精可能刺激的病变（因疾病引起的身体变化）的患者应咨询医生或药师。
* 除非医生指示，否则请勿使用密封包扎法或敷贴法。
* 本药仅供外用。
* 请勿用于眼科，勿用于角膜、结膜。若不慎入眼，请用足量的水彻底冲洗。
* 请勿用于面部、指甲、头皮、口腔周围、阴道区域、裂伤（开裂）、糜烂（溃烂）部位。
