# EN
### 💊 도미다프리미엄정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for:
    *   Supplementing vitamins E, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness (while suffering from illness or after recovery), and in old age.
    *   Alleviating symptoms of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as stiff shoulders, stiff neck, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet.
    *   Alleviating symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.
    *   Alleviating hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight, skin diseases, etc.
    *   Preventing gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds).

### 🕒 도미다프리미엄정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 도미다프리미엄정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you:
    *   have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybeans, or peanuts.
    *   have or have experienced hypersensitivity to soybean oil.
    *   are an infant under 3 months of age.
    *   have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood).
    *   have sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause).
    *   have kidney disease.
    *   have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are:
*   an infant under 1 year of age.
*   a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet.
*   a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a lactating woman.
*   a premature infant or an infant.
*   a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine).
*   a patient with gout or kidney stones.
*   a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or patients who need careful administration of lipid emulsions, or patients for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload.
*   a patient with a thrombotic predisposition.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

# CN
### 💊 도미다프리미엄정 - 功效及效果
*   本药用于：
    *   补充维生素E、B2、B6、C，适用于身体疲劳、妊娠及哺乳期、病中及病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、老年期。
    *   缓解末梢血液循环障碍及更年期时的肩周炎、颈部僵硬、手足发麻、手足冰冷等症状。
    *   缓解口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状。
    *   缓解因阳光、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑）。
    *   预防牙龈出血、鼻出血（鼻血）。

### 🕒 도미다프리미엄정 - 用法及用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每次1片，每日2次。

### ⚠️ 도미다프리미엄정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   以下患者请勿服用本药：
    *   对本药或大豆、花生过敏的患者。
    *   对大豆油过敏或有相关经历的患者。
    *   未满3个月的婴儿。
    *   高钙血症患者（血液中钙含量过高的状态）。
    *   结节病患者（原因不明的全身炎症性疾病）。
    *   肾脏疾病患者。
    *   患有半乳糖不耐受症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者。

在服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师：
*   未满1岁的婴儿。
*   经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童。
*   孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女。
*   早产儿、婴幼儿。
*   高草酸尿症患者（尿液中草酸盐过量排出的状态）。
*   痛风患者、肾结石患者。
*   高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要慎重给予脂质乳剂的患者，或因脂肪过量而预期有特殊风险的患者。
*   有血栓形成倾向的患者。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

本药在进行各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。

    *   肉体疲労時、妊娠・授乳期、病中病後（病気を患っている間や回復後）の体力低下時、老年期におけるビタミンE、B2、B6、Cの補給。
