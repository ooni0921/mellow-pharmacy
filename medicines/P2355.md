#EN
### 💊 Kerasyl Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for damaged hair, non-infectious nail dystrophy, and as an adjunctive treatment for hair loss.

### 🕒 Kerasyl Capsule - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 capsule three times a day. Children aged 12 years and older should take 1 capsule once or twice a day after meals. The average treatment period is 3 to 6 months.

### ⚠️ Kerasyl Capsule - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, are under 12 years of age, have hair loss due to scarring, androgenetic alopecia, or male-pattern baldness. Pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

#CN
### 💊 케라시딜캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于受损毛发、非感染性指甲发育不良以及脱发的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 케라시딜캡슐 - 用法与用量

### ⚠️ 케라시딜캡슐 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏者、12岁以下儿童、因疤痕引起的脱发、雄激素性脱发、男性型秃发患者请勿服用本药。孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女在服用本药前应咨询医生或药剂师。
