### 💊 엘도스트캡슐300밀리그램(에르도스테인) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for mucolysis and expectoration in acute and chronic respiratory diseases.

### 🕒 엘도스트캡슐300밀리그램(에르도스테인) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) per dose, 2-3 times a day. When taken for acute respiratory diseases, do not take continuously for more than 10 days.

### ⚠️ 엘도스트캡슐300밀리그램(에르도스테인) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   This medicine should not be administered to patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, hepatic cirrhosis, cystathionine synthase deficiency, peptic ulcer, or severe renal impairment (creatinine clearance [original text cut off here]).
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with mild to moderate hepatic impairment, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant and breastfeeding women, and patients with hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
