#EN
### 💊 락토폴정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for diarrhea, diarrhea accompanied by abdominal pain, indigestion, loose stools, and vomiting.

### 🕒 락토폴정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 15 years of age and older should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.
* The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 락토폴정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a neonate, a child aged 7 years or younger, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4), a history of drug allergies (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), gastric or duodenal ulcers, diarrhea accompanied by fever, are an elderly patient, or have bloody stools or persistent mucous stools.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for more than 3 days.

Do not consume alcohol during medication.

If symptoms do not improve after several doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 락토폴정 - 用法用量
* 15岁及以上成人，每次2片，每日3次，饭后服用。

* 孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、新生儿、7岁以下儿童，以及患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。
