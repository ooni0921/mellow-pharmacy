#EN
### 💊 파비스암브록솔염산염정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for acute and chronic bronchitis and asthmatic bronchitis caused by mucus secretion disorders.

### 🕒 파비스암브록솔염산염정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: Take 1 tablet (30 mg) three times daily, regardless of meals. For continued treatment, take 1 tablet (30 mg) twice daily.

### ⚠️ 파비스암브록솔염산염정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, severe renal impairment, severe hepatic impairment, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, peptic ulcer, are pregnant, breastfeeding, or are elderly.

#CN
### 💊 파비스암브록솔염산염정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗由粘液分泌障碍引起的急慢性支气管炎、哮喘性支气管炎。

### 🕒 파비스암브록솔염산염정 - 用法用量
* 成人：每次1片（30 mg），每日3次，饭前饭后均可服用。持续治疗时，每次1片（30 mg），每日2次。

### ⚠️ 파비스암브록솔염산염정 - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 对本药过敏者、严重肾功能障碍、严重肝功能障碍、半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，肾功能障碍、消化性溃疡、孕妇、哺乳期妇女、老年患者应咨询医生或药师。
