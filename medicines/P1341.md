# EN
### 💊 댄스탑외용액(케토코나졸) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the following skin conditions caused by yeast (dandruff, seborrheic dermatitis, tinea versicolor).

### 🕒 댄스탑외용액(케토코나졸) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply this medication to the affected area (body, face, scalp, hair) and leave it on for 3-5 minutes, similar to a regular shampoo, then rinse off. For the treatment of dandruff and seborrheic dermatitis, apply twice a week for 2-4 weeks; for recurrence prevention, apply once every 1-2 weeks. For the treatment of tinea versicolor, apply once daily for up to 5 days; for recurrence prevention, apply once daily for up to 3 days as a single therapy before sun exposure.

### ⚠️ 댄스탑외용액(케토코나졸) - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this product, or on an injured or inflamed scalp, or severe erosions (soreness).
*   Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, children, and individuals with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medication is for external use only.
*   Do not use this medication for ophthalmic purposes. Apply it carefully to avoid contact with the eyes. If it accidentally gets into the eyes, rinse thoroughly with a sufficient amount of water.

# CN
### 💊 댄스탑외용액(케토코나졸) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于治疗酵母菌引起的以下皮肤病（头皮屑、脂溢性皮炎、花斑癣）。

### 🕒 댄스탑외용액(케토코나졸) - 用法用量
*   将本药涂抹于感染部位（身体、面部、头皮、头发），像普通洗发水一样作用3~5分钟后冲洗干净。治疗周期方面，治疗头皮屑和脂溢性皮炎时，每周2次，持续2~4周；预防复发时，每1~2周使用1次。治疗花斑癣时，每日1次，最多5天；预防复发时，在暴露于阳光前作为单一疗法每日1次，最多3天。

### ⚠️ 댄스탑외용액(케토코나졸) - 注意事项
*   无可用信息
*   对本药过敏的患者，以及头皮有伤口或炎症、严重糜烂（溃烂）的部位，请勿使用本药。
*   在使用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、儿童以及对丙二醇有过敏史或过敏经验者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   本药仅限外用。
*   本药不可用于眼科用途，请注意避免接触眼睛，如不慎入眼，请用足量的水彻底冲洗。
