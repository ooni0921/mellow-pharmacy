#EN
### 💊 마그엠정(알마게이트) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for its antacid action and improvement of symptoms such as gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, hyperacidity, heartburn, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and acid regurgitation.

### 🕒 마그엠정(알마게이트) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years or older should chew and take 2 tablets (1 g) at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes to 1 hour after meals.
* If necessary, an additional dose can be taken once before bedtime.

### ⚠️ 마그엠정(알마게이트) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug, Alzheimer's disease, hemorrhoids, fluid retention, preeclampsia (toxemia of pregnancy), diarrhea, undiagnosed gastrointestinal bleeding, or hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, are physically debilitated, are on a low-phosphate diet, have digestive absorption disorders, are elderly, have symptoms of gastrointestinal bleeding such as hematemesis (vomiting blood) or melena (black, tarry stools), or are pregnant, possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
