* This medicine is used for the improvement of dyspeptic symptoms (abdominal distension, delayed digestion, belching, nausea).

### 🕒 가레오연질캡슐500밀리그램(디히드록시디부틸에테르) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 to 2 capsules (0.5 to 1.0 g) once, 1 to 3 times a day, before meals, swallowing whole without chewing.

### ⚠️ 가레오연질캡슐500밀리그램(디히드록시디부틸에테르) - Precautions
* Not available
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, glaucoma, urinary tract, prostate, or biliary tract obstruction, cholelithiasis (gallstones), or acute/chronic liver disease. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
