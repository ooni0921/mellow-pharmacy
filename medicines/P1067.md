#EN
### 💊 피지오머비강세척액 스프레이 노즐(멸균등장해수)/피지오머비강세척액 젯 노즐(멸균등장해수) - Efficacy and Effects
* This product is used for nasal irrigation in cases of nasal congestion caused by nasal mucosal secretions or purulent discharge.

### 🕒 피지오머비강세척액 스프레이 노즐(멸균등장해수)/피지오머비강세척액 젯 노즐(멸균등장해수) - Dosage and Administration
* The spray nozzle is for infants aged 1 to 24 months, and the jet nozzle is for children aged 6 years and older and adults. Administer two sprays into each nostril in the morning and evening. Administer by lying on your side or standing, with your head tilted, to prevent the liquid from flowing into the larynx or pharynx.

### ⚠️ 피지오머비강세척액 스프레이 노즐(멸균등장해수)/피지오머비강세척액 젯 노즐(멸균등장해수) - Precautions
* When the jet nozzle is used by infants and young children, there is a risk of the liquid entering the wrong place or causing laryngospasm due to its high spray pressure.
* The spray nozzle should not be used for infants under 1 month of age. The jet nozzle should not be used for infants and young children under 6 years of age. Sharing the product among multiple people may spread infection.
