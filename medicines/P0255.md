#EN
### 💊 다제스캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, gastric distress due to overeating (food stagnation), promoting digestion, gastric bloating due to indigestion, liver diseases caused by impaired bile secretion, and as an adjunctive treatment for biliary tract (bile duct, gallbladder) diseases.

### 🕒 다제스캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 1 to 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 다제스캡슐 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have: hypersensitivity to this drug, acute hepatitis, severe biliary obstruction, radiopaque calcified gallstones, a non-functioning gallbladder, acute cholecystitis, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, a child, kidney disease, peptic ulcer, colitis and enteritis such as Crohn's disease, or hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have: an allergic constitution, severe pancreatic disease, gallstones, variceal bleeding, hepatic coma, ascites, a condition requiring liver transplantation, a history of or hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5, or are elderly.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
