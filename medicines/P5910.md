#EN
### 💊 코앤쿨에스나잘스프레이 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy-headedness caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 코앤쿨에스나잘스프레이 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 7 years or older, spray 1 to 2 times into each nostril, 1 to 2 times a day.
*   Administer with an interval of 10 to 12 hours, and do not exceed 2 administrations within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 코앤쿨에스나잘스프레이 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, if you are a child under 7 years of age, or an infant under 2 years of age.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicines including rhinitis sprays, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or have hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, or glaucoma.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm, a cough lasting for more than a week or recurring, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, stop using this medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine is a rhinitis spray, meaning it is only for use inside the nose. Do not use it in the eyes or ears.
*   Do not share to prevent contamination.
*   Drowsiness may occur during use. Be careful when driving a car or operating machinery.
*   Do not use excessively. It may cause nasal congestion.
*   Children should use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

### ⚠️ 코앤쿨에스나잘스프레이 - 注意事项

*   对本品过敏或有过敏史者，7岁以下儿童以及2岁以下婴儿请勿使用本品。

*   本品为鼻炎喷雾剂，仅用于鼻腔内，请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。

*   使用期间可能会出现嗜睡。驾驶汽车或操作机械时请注意。

*   儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
*   请勿连续使用7天以上。
