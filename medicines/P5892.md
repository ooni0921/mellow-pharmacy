* Adults take 1 to 2 tablets per dose, twice a day, when pain or symptoms occur.

### ⚠️ 프리스담정 - Precautions
* No information available
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you: have an allergic constitution; have experienced allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due to medication; are an infant, young child, or child (under 14 years old) infected with or suspected of being infected with chickenpox or influenza; are pregnant or may be pregnant; are a breastfeeding woman; are elderly; have a weakened constitution or high fever; or have liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, or hypertension.
