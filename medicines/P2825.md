#EN
### 💊 이지펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, post-traumatic and post-surgical painful swelling or inflammation, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 이지펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults, take 1 capsule (300 mg) at a time, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 capsules (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 이지펜연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

There is an increased risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal, when taking this medicine. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, have a history of gastrointestinal ulcers, bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular, or other sites, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, have experienced asthma, hives or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), are undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are in the last trimester of pregnancy (6 months or more), are breastfeeding, have inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous NSAID treatment.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of blood disorders, a tendency to bleed, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, a history of hypersensitivity, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, a child aged 6 years or older, have stomach cancer, are an alcoholic, are in the first or second trimester of pregnancy or may be pregnant, have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss, or have blood clotting disorders or are receiving anticoagulants.

Considering the importance of drug administration, discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue drug administration.

Since dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medicine, be careful when driving or operating machinery.

For acute conditions, long-term administration should generally be limited to within 5 days.
