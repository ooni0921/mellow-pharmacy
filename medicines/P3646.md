#EN

### 💊 코르텍골드구강붕해필름 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, watery eyes, sore throat, and heaviness in the head caused by the common cold (acute rhinitis), allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 코르텍골드구강붕해필름 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and individuals 15 years and older: Take 1 film at a time, 3 times a day, by dissolving it in the mouth. The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours. Take this medicine (film) in the following order: Open the aluminum sheet, slowly peel it away from the mouth, and take out only the medicine (film). (Do not swallow the entire aluminum sheet. This could lead to unexpected accidents such as it getting stuck in the esophageal mucosa.) Place this medicine (film) on the tongue and dissolve it with saliva. This medicine (film) is easily soluble in water, so do not handle it with wet hands. This medicine (film) dissolves immediately in the mouth, but if it sticks to your throat, drink water to swallow it. Also, if you have little saliva and find it difficult to take, put water in your mouth and dissolve it while taking it.

### ⚠️ 코르텍골드구강붕해필름 - Precautions
*   No additional information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, are under 15 years of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, are elderly, have high fever, difficulty urinating, dry mouth, glaucoma, diabetes, thyroid dysfunction, heart disease, high blood pressure, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever/rash or persistent headache.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of taking the medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
