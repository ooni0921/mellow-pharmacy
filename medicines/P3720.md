#EN
### 💊 일성아세트아미노펜정325밀리그램 - Efficacy and Indications
* This medicine is used for fever and pain due to common cold, headache, neuralgia, muscle pain, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia (joint pain), and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 일성아세트아미미노펜정325밀리그램 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, take 300-1,000 mg per dose, 3-4 times a day. A maximum of 4,000 mg can be administered per day. The dosage can be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 일성아세트아미노펜정325밀리그램 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* This medicine contains aspartame, which is metabolized to phenylalanine in the body. Patients with phenylketonuria who need to restrict phenylalanine intake should not take this medicine.
* Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac insufficiency, aspirin asthma (asthma exacerbation caused by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history thereof, or if you have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, if you are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

#CN
### 💊 일성아세트아미노펜정325밀리그램 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、扭伤痛、牙痛、关节痛以及风湿痛。

### 🕒 일성아세트아미노펜정325밀리그램 - 用法用量
* 成人每次口服300～1,000 mg，每日3～4次。每日最高给药量可达4,000 mg。可根据年龄、症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 일성아세트아미노펜정325밀리그램 - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒量超过三杯的规律饮酒者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起肝损伤。
* 本药含有阿斯巴甜，其在体内代谢为苯丙氨酸，因此需要限制苯丙氨酸摄入量的苯丙酮尿症患者请勿服用。
* 极罕见情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性全身性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。若服药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。
* 对乙酰氨基酚每日最大剂量（4,000 mg）请勿超量服用。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液疾病患者、严重肝功能障碍者、严重肾功能障碍者、严重心功能不全者、阿司匹林哮喘（由非甾体抗炎药引起的哮喘发作）或有其病史者、服用巴比妥类药物、三环抗抑郁药或酒精者请勿服用本药。
* 在服用本药前，有肝功能障碍或病史者、肾功能障碍或病史者、消化性溃疡病史者、血液疾病或病史者、有出血倾向的患者、有心功能异常的患者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药剂师。
