정해진 용법과 용량을 잘 지키십시오.

장기간 계속하여 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 킥코프에스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold, including sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 킥코프에스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 15 years and older should take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 킥코프에스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months old, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of drug allergies, are a child aged 3 months to under 2 years, an infant or child under 15 years suspected or confirmed to have chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, are elderly, physically weak, or have a high fever, experience persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or suspect you may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts or recurs for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not use continuously for a prolonged period.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medication.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 킥코프에스연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
* 15岁以上及成人，每次2粒胶囊，每日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 킥코프에스연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 每天定期饮酒三杯以上者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起肝损伤。

曾有报告称，极少数情况下可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性全身发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后，如果出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。

每日醋氨酚最大剂量（4,000 mg）请勿超量服用。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有醋氨酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药有过敏史或曾有过敏反应者、服用其他解热镇痛剂或感冒药时曾引起哮喘者、未满3个月的婴儿、正在服用或停用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）后2周内的人，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如有以下情况，请咨询医生或药剂师：有药物过敏史者、3个月以上未满2岁的儿童、患有或疑似水痘或流感的婴儿及未满15岁的儿童、过敏体质者、肝脏、肾脏、心脏、甲状腺疾病患者、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡患者、老年人、体弱者或高热者、持续或反复出现胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题或有溃疡、出血问题者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、吸烟者、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿患者、伴有过多痰的咳嗽、持续或反复1周以上的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽等症状出现时。

服用期间请勿饮酒。

服用5~6次后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药剂师。

### 🕒 킥코프에스연질캡슐 - 用法・用量

### ⚠️ 킥코프에스연질캡슐 - 注意事項

定められた用法・用量を守ってください。

長期間続けて服用しないでください。

服用中に飲酒しないでください。
