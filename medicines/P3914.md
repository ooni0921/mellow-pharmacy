#EN
### 💊 액티진정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C and zinc in cases of physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness, growth period, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, and for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (lower back pain, stiff shoulders, etc.), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 액티진정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 액티진정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are premature infants, infants, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcers, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones or a history thereof, folic acid deficiency, or a thrombotic predisposition.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values, or interfere with glucose detection during urine tests.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 액티진정 - 注意事项
*   对本品过敏者、高钙血症、结节病、肾脏疾病患者、未满12个月的婴儿、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本品。

本品可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查值，或在尿液检查时干扰血糖检测。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
