#EN
### 💊 뉴먼트에이스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the supplementation of Vitamins A, C, D, E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of physical decline during or after illness (convalescence), growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the alleviation of dry eyes, nyctalopia (difficulty seeing at night), prevention of poor bone and teeth development, rickets, alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders, and menopausal symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness of hands and feet. Furthermore, it helps alleviate symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 뉴먼트에이스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 capsule once a day.

### ⚠️ 뉴먼트에이스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Daily intake of Vitamin A exceeding 5,000 IU may cause congenital malformations. Do not take this drug if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or are likely to be pregnant. In case of overdose, this drug may cause fatal iron poisoning in children under 6 years of age; therefore, keep it out of reach of children.
*   Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypercalcemia (excess calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months, have Wilson's disease, hemochromatosis (a disorder of iron metabolism where iron accumulates in the liver and pancreas), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or hypersensitivity to soybean oil, soy, or peanuts.
*   Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (excessive excretion of oxalate in urine), are pregnant or likely to be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or localized colitis, have cardiac/circulatory dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, a history of gout or kidney stones, folic acid deficiency, a thrombotic predisposition (tendency to form blood clots), or lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This drug may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.
