* 이 약은 육체피로, 임신·수유기, 병중·병후(병을 앓는 동안이나 회복 후)의 체력 저하 시, 노년기의 비타민 A, C, E의 보급과 눈의 건조감의 완화, 야맹증(밤에 잘 못보는 증상), 말초혈행장애 및 갱년기 시 어깨·목결림, 수족저림·수족냉증(손발이 차가움) 증상의 완화, 햇빛·피부병 등에 의한 색소침착(기미, 주근깨)의 완화, 잇몸 출혈·비출혈(코피) 예방에 사용합니다.

### 🕒 알피렌연질캡슐 - 용법 및 용량
* 성인은 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 1회 식후에 복용합니다.

* 이 약 또는 대두유, 콩, 땅콩에 과민증 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

# EN
### 💊 알피렌연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used to supplement vitamins A, C, and E for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during periods of decreased physical strength due to illness or convalescence (during illness or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, relief of symptoms such as numbness and coldness in the hands and feet (cold hands and feet), alleviation of pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, and prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds).

### 🕒 알피렌연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 알피렌연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A daily can cause congenital malformations. Do not take this drug if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy.
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or other patients with abnormal lipid metabolism, or patients who must carefully administer lipid emulsions, patients with a thrombotic predisposition, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 (Tartrazine).

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

This drug may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue the drug immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
