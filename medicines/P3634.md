* 15세 이상 및 성인은 1회 2정씩, 1일 3회 식후 30분에 복용합니다.

아세트아미노펜으로 일일 최대 용량(4,000 mg)을 초과하여 복용하지 마십시오. 간손상을 일으킬 수 있습니다. 아세트아미노펜을 포함하는 다른 제품과 함께 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 에페젠정 - Efficacy & Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 에페젠정 - Dosage & Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years or older and adults, take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 에페젠정 - Warnings & Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause liver damage.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.

    Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old suspected of or infected with chickenpox or influenza; an infant or toddler aged 3 months to under 2 years; have an allergic constitution or a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, edema, gastroduodenal ulcer; are elderly, physically weak, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or potentially pregnant; or are breastfeeding.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    Do not use continuously for a long period.

    Do not consume alcohol during medication.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 에페젠정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解多种感冒症状（咽喉痛、咳嗽、痰、发冷、发热、头痛、关节痛、肌肉痛）。

### 🕒 에페젠정 - 用法与用量
*   15岁以上及成人，一次2片，一日3次，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 에페젠정 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能导致肝损伤。

    如果出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等严重皮肤不良反应，请停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

    每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量不得超过最大日剂量（4,000毫克）。可能导致肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
*   对本药有过敏史者、服用其他解热镇痛药或感冒药时曾发生哮喘者、未满3个月的婴儿、正在服用MAO抑制剂（抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪稳定剂、抗帕金森病药等）或停用后2周内的人，请勿服用本药。

    服用本药前，如果婴儿及未满15岁的儿童感染或疑似感染水痘或流感；满3个月以上及未满2岁的婴幼儿；过敏体质者；有过敏症状（例如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）史者；患有肝脏、肾脏、心脏、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、浮肿、胃十二指肠溃疡者；老年人；体弱者或高热者；胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠问题持续或反复出现，或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。

    请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

    给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

    请勿长期连续使用。

    服药期间请勿饮酒。

    服用5-6次后症状仍未改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

### ⚠️ 에페젠정 - 注意事項

    急性汎発性発疹性膿疱症のような重篤な皮膚の副作用が現れた場合は、服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。

    小児に投与する場合は、保護者の指導監督のもとに服用させてください。

    長期間続けて使用しないでください。

    服用中は飲酒しないでください。

    5～6回服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
