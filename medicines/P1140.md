# EN
### 💊 Asno Capsule - Indications and Efficacy
* This medicine is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 Asno Capsule - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ Asno Capsule - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or a history thereof, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, an infant under 3 months of age, or if you have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age, have an allergic constitution, have a history of allergic symptoms caused by medicines (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), dysuria, are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are nursing, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache, or have tartrazine (Yellow No. 5) hypersensitivity or a history of allergic reactions.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

For infants and young children under 7 years of age, administer a syrup formulation.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue administration immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Drowsiness may occur after administration, so be cautious when driving or operating machinery.

* 对本药有过敏症或过敏史的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、未满3个月的婴儿、以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。
