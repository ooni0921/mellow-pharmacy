#EN
### 💊 헤모맥스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia.

### 🕒 헤모맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 8 years and older and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 헤모맥스정 - Precautions
*   Keep out of reach of children. Iron-containing preparations can cause fatal poisoning if accidentally overdosed by children aged 6 years and under.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, an infant under 12 months of age, or have hemochromatosis (iron deposition in the liver, pancreas, or other organs due to abnormal iron metabolism), hemosiderosis, or non-iron deficiency anemia.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, or a patient with hypoproteinemia.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medication, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN
### 💊 헤모맥스정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于预防和治疗缺铁性贫血。

### 🕒 헤모맥스정 - 用法与用量
*   8岁及以上儿童和成人，每次1片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 헤모맥스정 - 注意事项
*   请置于儿童接触不到的地方。含铁制剂在6岁及以下儿童意外过量服用时，可能导致中毒性死亡。
*   对本药过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、因铁代谢异常导致铁沉积在肝脏、胰脏或胰腺的血色素沉着症患者、含铁血黄素沉着症患者、非缺铁性贫血患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、心脏或循环系统功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病的患者、以及低蛋白血症患者，请咨询医生或药师。

如果服用本药约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

*   本剤に過敏症の患者、生後12ヶ月未満の乳児、鉄代謝異常により鉄が肝臓、膵臓などに沈着するヘモクロマトーシス、ヘモジデリン沈着症、非鉄欠乏性貧血の患者は、本剤を服用しないでください。
