# EN
### 💊 젯트콤연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the replenishment of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical debility during or after illness, growth periods, and old age; for the prevention of maldevelopment of bones and teeth and rickets; for the relief of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness and coldness in hands and feet associated with peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause; for the relief of symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; for the relief of pigmentation (chloasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases; and for the prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds).

### 🕒 젯트콤연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 12 years or older, take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 젯트콤연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts; if you are an infant under 12 months of age; if you have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood); if you have sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause); if you have kidney disease or kidney stones. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding; a premature infant or infant; a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient requiring careful administration of lipid emulsions; a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine); a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis; a patient with impaired cardiac or circulatory system function; a patient with hypoproteinemia; a patient with gout; a patient for whom special risk is anticipated due to fat overload; or a patient with thrombotic diathesis (a predisposition to blood clots). Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medication may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests, and may cause urine to turn yellow, affecting clinical test values.
