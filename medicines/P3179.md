#EN
### 💊 파라멜산(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴수화물) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for detoxification in severe liver diseases such as cirrhosis.

### 🕒 파라멜산(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴수화물) - Dosage and Administration
*   Take 2 sachets (6 g) once, twice a day, dissolved in a large amount of water, after meals.

### ⚠️ 파라멜산(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴수화물) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with severe renal impairment (serum creatinine level ≥ 3 mg/100 mL) should not take this medication. Before taking this medication, patients with hypersensitivity to acetylsalicylic acid (aspirin) or Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 파라멜산(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴수화물) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于肝硬化等重症肝病解毒的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 파라멜산(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴수화물) - 用法与用量
*   每次2包(6 g)，每日2次，溶于大量水中，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 파라멜산(L-아스파르트산-L-오르니틴수화물) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   严重肾功能不全患者（血清肌酐水平≥3 mg/100 mL）请勿服用本药。在服用本药前，对乙酰水杨酸（阿司匹林）或日落黄（黄色5号）过敏的患者应咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。如服用约2周后症状无改善，请停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
