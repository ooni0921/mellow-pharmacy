###EN
### 💊 Bontop 600 Gold Tablets - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medication is used to supplement vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical decline during or after illness, growth periods, and in old age.
*   It also helps alleviate symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (such as low back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 Bontop 600 Gold Tablets - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 8 years and older, and adults, should take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ Bontop 600 Gold Tablets - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 3 months old, have hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, or kidney stones.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year old, a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with or a history of kidney stones, or a patient with folic acid deficiency.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
