# EN

### 💊 부스코판당의정(부틸스코폴라민브롬화물) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for gastric/duodenal ulcer, esophageal spasm, pylorospasm, gastritis, enteritis, intestinal colic, spasmodic constipation, functional diarrhea, cholecystitis, cholangitis, cholelithiasis, biliary dyskinesia, sequelae after cholecystectomy, urolithiasis, cystitis, and dysmenorrhea.

### 🕒 부스코판당의정(부틸스코폴라민브롬화물) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 tablets (10 to 20 mg) once, 3 to 5 times a day. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 부스코판당의정(부틸스코폴라민브롬화물) - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, glaucoma, functional stenosis of the gastrointestinal tract such as pyloric stenosis, paralytic or obstructive ileus, myasthenia gravis, megacolon, tachyarrhythmia, severe heart disease, hemorrhagic colitis, micturition disorder due to prostatic hyperplasia, or if you are pregnant, possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bacterial diarrhea, congestive heart failure, arrhythmia, ulcerative colitis, hyperthyroidism, are in a high-temperature environment, have prostatic hyperplasia, or are elderly.
* After taking this medicine, visual accommodation disorder and dizziness may occur, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

# CN

### 💊 부스코판당의정(부틸스코폴라민브롬화물) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于胃/十二指肠溃疡、食道痉挛、幽门痉挛、胃炎、肠炎、肠绞痛、痉挛性便秘、功能性腹泻、胆囊炎、胆管炎、胆结石、胆道运动障碍、胆囊切除术后遗症、尿路结石、膀胱炎、月经困难。

### 🕒 부스코판당의정(부틸스코폴라민브롬화물) - 用法与用量
* 成人一次服用1~2片（10~20 mg），一日3~5次。可根据年龄、症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 부스코판당의정(부틸스코폴라민브롬화물) - 注意事项
* 对本药过敏者、青光眼、幽门狭窄等胃肠道功能性狭窄患者、麻痹性或阻塞性肠梗阻、重症肌无力、巨结肠、心动过速性心律失常、严重心脏病、出血性结肠炎、前列腺肥大引起的排尿障碍患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，细菌性腹泻、充血性心力衰竭、心律失常、溃疡性结肠炎、甲状腺功能亢进症、处于高温环境的患者、前列腺肥大患者、老年人请务必咨询医生或药师。
* 服用本药后可能会出现视调节障碍、头晕，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
