### 💊 하이립클로버크림 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the early treatment of recurrent labial herpes (cold sores).

### 🕒 하이립클로버크림 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount, sufficient to cover the entire affected area up to its edges, 5 times a day for 5 days.

Treatment should be started as soon as possible, immediately upon the appearance of initial symptoms and signs.

### ⚠️ 하이립클로버크림 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug; bacterial (e.g., tuberculosis, syphilis), fungal (e.g., candidiasis, tinea), viral (e.g., shingles, chickenpox, vaccinia, *excluding* herpes simplex), or parasitic (e.g., scabies, pubic lice) skin infections; severely impaired immune systems such as bone marrow transplant recipients or AIDS patients; eczematous otitis externa with tympanic membrane perforation; ulcers (excluding Behcet's disease), or second-degree deep burns or frostbite; perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, or rosacea; if you are a breastfeeding woman, a child under 18 years of age, or a patient with or with a history of myocardial infarction.

Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and elderly patients, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not use on the eyes, nose, inside of the mouth, or genitals.

Do not use this medicine for the purpose of preventing transmission to others or preventing recurrent herpes simplex (blister) infections.

Do not dilute this medicine or use it as a base for mixing with other drugs.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration instructions.

Use only for cold sores on the lips or around the mouth.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 5 days of use, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
