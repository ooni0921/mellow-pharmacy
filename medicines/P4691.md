#EN
### 💊 벤포스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for neurological disorders caused by vitamin B1 and B6 deficiency.

### 🕒 벤포스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 벤포스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not take it.

Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
