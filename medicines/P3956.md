#EN

### 💊 피엠쿨에어로솔 - Efficacy & Effects
* This medicine is used for athlete's foot, tinea cruris (jock itch), and tinea corporis (ringworm).

### 🕒 피엠쿨에어로솔 - Dosage & Administration
* Before application, thoroughly wash and completely dry the affected area, then spray an appropriate amount once daily.

### ⚠️ 피엠쿨에어로솔 - Precautions
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant or breastfeeding, on infants and children, on or around the eyes, mucous membranes (e.g., mouth, nose, vagina), scrotum, vulva, eczema, wet areas (due to moisture), erosions (skin breakdown), severely injured affected areas, fingernails, or scalp.
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, if the affected area is on the face or extensive, or if there is pus, or if the distinction between "eczema" and "athlete's foot, tinea cruris (jock itch), tinea corporis" is unclear.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
* Do not use on an affected area or its surroundings if they are contaminated.
* Be careful not to get it in your eyes. If it gets into your eyes, rinse immediately with water or lukewarm water.
* Use only for external application; do not ingest.
* Shake well before use.
* Spray from a distance of 5 cm to the affected area, ensure the spray nozzle is well-directed, and do not spray towards the face, especially the eyes, or inhale.
* There is a risk of frostbite, etc., so do not spray continuously on the same area for more than 1 second.
* Do not touch your eyes or mucous membranes with hands that have this medicine on them.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, or if symptoms worsen due to the use of this medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
