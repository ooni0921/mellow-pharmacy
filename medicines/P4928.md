#EN
### 💊 소가프텐정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of heartburn associated with hyperacidity and gastric discomfort.

### 🕒 소가프텐정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and individuals aged 15 years and older should take 1 tablet at a time.
*   Do not exceed 2 tablets within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 소가프텐정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, patients experiencing difficulty swallowing the drug, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and nursing mothers should not take this drug.
*   Before taking this drug, children under 15 years of age, patients with unexplained weight loss, persistent abdominal pain, kidney disease, or patients undergoing hemodialysis should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
