* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 2회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 비맥스엠지플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after illness (during illness or recovery) when physical strength is low, for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, E in old age, for beriberi, eye fatigue, peripheral circulatory disorder, and to relieve shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet during menopause, as well as for neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 비맥스엠지플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children over 12 years of age should take 1 capsule per dose, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 비맥스엠지플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, hypersensitive to soy or peanuts, have experienced allergic reactions to soybean oil, have severe renal impairment, or are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are hypersensitive to or have experienced hypersensitivity to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or are a patient who should administer lipid emulsions cautiously, or a patient at special risk due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

May turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after taking for about 1 month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
