#EN
### 💊 마그큐엔연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamins E, B1, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after illness when physical strength is low, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness and coldness in the extremities (cold hands and feet), neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그큐엔연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age or older: Take 1 capsule twice a day.

### ⚠️ 마그큐엔연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, an infant under 12 months of age, a patient with severe renal insufficiency, an individual with a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to soybean oil, or a patient with hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with renal impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient with hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or a patient who should be administered lipid emulsions cautiously.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
