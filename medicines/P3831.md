# EN
### 💊 Excophedi Syrup - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medication is used for cough, phlegm, runny nose, nasal congestion, and sneezing.

### 🕒 Excophedi Syrup - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 2 to 6 years: 5 mL per dose.
*   Children aged 6 to 12 years: 10 mL per dose.
*   Take every 4 to 6 hours.
*   Do not exceed 4 doses within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ Excophedi Syrup - Precautions
*   If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue administration and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with severe or uncontrolled hypertension should not take this medication.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, itching, etc.), have liver disorder, kidney disorder, thyroid disease, diabetes, high fever, are debilitated, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, have heart disorder and hypertension, have a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, or have persistent or chronic cough such as asthma.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after several doses, discontinue administration immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 엑스코프디시럽 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于治疗咳嗽、咳痰、流鼻涕、鼻塞和打喷嚏。

### 🕒 엑스코프디시럽 - 用法用量
*   2至6岁儿童：每次5毫升。
*   6至12岁儿童：每次10毫升。
*   每4至6小时服用一次。
*   24小时内不得服用超过4次。

### ⚠️ 엑스코프디시럽 - 注意事项
*   如果出现急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病等严重皮肤不良反应，请停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
*   患有严重或未受控制的高血压患者请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果您是：2岁以下儿童、过敏体质、曾因药物引起过敏症状（例如：发热、皮疹、关节痛、瘙痒等）、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高热、体弱者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、心脏病及高血压患者、伴有过多痰的咳嗽、或有哮喘等持续性或慢性咳嗽者。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

服用数次后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。

*   重度または管理されていない高血圧の患者は、本剤を服用しないでください。

定められた用法・用量を厳守してください。

小児に服用させる場合は、保護者の指導監督のもとに服用させてください。

数回服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
