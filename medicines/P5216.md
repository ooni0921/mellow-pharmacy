#EN
### 💊 마크롱연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for vitamin E supplementation in the elderly, alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in the hands and feet (cold extremities) during menopause, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마크롱연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 12 years or older, take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마크롱연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, have severe renal failure, a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to soybean oil, or hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5), hyperlipoproteinemia, abnormal lipid metabolism such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or require careful administration of lipid emulsions.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
