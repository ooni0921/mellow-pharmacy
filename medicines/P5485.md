##EN

### 💊 위싱유정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, indigestion, stomach discomfort, gastric distension, upset stomach from overeating, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, overeating, promoting digestion, and loss of appetite.

### 🕒 위싱유정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 15 years or older should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 위싱유정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient undergoing dialysis or a child under 7 years of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with renal impairment, individuals with allergies, or those on a sodium-restricted diet should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
