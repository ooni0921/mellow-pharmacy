#EN
### 💊 아론정(독시라민숙신산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the adjunctive treatment of insomnia and for sedation.

### 🕒 아론정(독시라민숙신산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet (25 mg) once a day, strictly 30 minutes before sleep. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 아론정(독시라민숙신산염) - Precautions
* No information provided
* Do not take this medication if you have respiratory disorders such as emphysema, chronic bronchitis, asthma, glaucoma, prostatic hypertrophy (prostate enlargement), if you are breastfeeding, are a child under 15 years of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Excessive sedation may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery. Do not administer for more than 2 weeks without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.
