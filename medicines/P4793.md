#EN

### 💊 DicatheoLC Tab - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for vitamin D supplementation during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, as well as for poor bone and tooth development and the prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 DicatheoLC Tab - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ DicatheoLC Tab - Precautions
*   Do not take DicatheoLC Tab if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, an infant under 12 months of age, a patient with kidney stones, or a patient with severe renal insufficiency.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiovascular system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a patient receiving cardiac glycosides, or a patient with or a history of kidney stones.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking the medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
