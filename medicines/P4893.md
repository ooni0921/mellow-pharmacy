* 만 8세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 비타씬맥스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C in old age, beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), relief of angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 비타씬맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 8 years and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비타씬맥스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have severe renal failure, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or kidney stones.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This drug may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue the drug immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
