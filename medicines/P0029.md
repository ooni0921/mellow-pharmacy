#EN
### 💊 겔포스현탁액(인산알루미늄겔) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the antacid action and improvement of symptoms such as gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity (heartburn, stomach pain, nausea, vomiting).

### 🕒 겔포스현탁액(인산알루미늄겔) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 pouch, 3 to 4 times a day, between meals and at bedtime.

The dosage may be adjusted appropriately according to age or symptoms.

### ⚠️ 겔포스현탁액(인산알루미늄겔) - Precautions
* No information
* Before taking this medication, patients with kidney disorders or constipation should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
