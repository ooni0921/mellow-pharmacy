#EN
### 💊 비스콘틴800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins D, B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during and after illness (while sick or after recovery) when physical strength is low, during growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, rickets, and for the relief of beriberi, eye strain, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular cheilitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 비스콘틴800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years or older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비스콘틴800정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 3 months of age, have kidney stones, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, infants, patients with a history of kidney stones, or patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and may affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 💊 비스콘틴800정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于补充维生素D、B1、B2、B6，适用于身体疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、发育期以及老年期。它还用于预防骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病，并缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳、神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩颈僵硬）、口角炎（口角发炎）、唇炎（嘴唇发炎）、口腔炎（口腔发炎）、舌炎（舌头发炎）、湿疹和皮炎。

### 🕒 비스콘틴800정 - 用法与用量
*   19岁及以上成人，每次1片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 비스콘틴800정 - 注意事项
*   对本药过敏者、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高）患者、结节病（原因不明的全身炎症性疾病）患者、肾病患者、未满3个月的婴儿、肾结石患者，以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题者，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、曾患肾结石或有肾结石病史的患者、叶酸缺乏症患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   本药可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检测结果。
*   若服用本药约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。
