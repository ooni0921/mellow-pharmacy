# EN
### 💊 베나치오액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for loss of appetite (anorexia), stomach bloating, indigestion, overeating, upset stomach, nausea, and vomiting.

### 🕒 베나치오액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 15 years and older: 1 bottle per dose.
* Children aged 11 years and older to under 15 years: 2/3 bottle per dose.
* Children aged 8 years and older to under 11 years: 1/2 bottle per dose.
* Children aged 5 years and older to under 8 years: 1/3 bottle per dose.
* Children aged 3 years and older to under 5 years: 1/4 bottle per dose.
* Children aged 1 year and older to under 3 years: 1/5 bottle per dose.
* Take 3 times daily after meals.

Ensure a minimum dosing interval of 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 베나치오액 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not administer this medicine to infants under 3 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, infants under 1 year of age, and individuals on a sodium-restricted diet should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 베나치오액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于食欲不振（食欲减退）、胃胀、消化不良、暴饮暴食、积食、恶心、呕吐。

### 🕒 베나치오액 - 用法用量
* 15岁及以上成人：每次1瓶。
* 11岁及以上至未满15岁儿童：每次2/3瓶。
* 8岁及以上至未满11岁儿童：每次1/2瓶。
* 5岁及以上至未满8岁儿童：每次1/3瓶。
* 3岁及以上至未满5岁儿童：每次1/4瓶。
* 1岁及以上至未满3岁儿童：每次1/5瓶。
* 每日3次，饭后服用。

服药间隔应至少4小时。

### ⚠️ 베나치오액 - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 未满3个月的婴儿请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、未满1岁的婴儿、以及进行钠限制饮食的人，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

若服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
