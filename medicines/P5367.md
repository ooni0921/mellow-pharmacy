### 💊 Carolnight Soft Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for acute nocturnal pain accompanied by temporary insomnia due to pain.

### 🕒 Carolnight Soft Capsule - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: If difficulty sleeping due to pain, take 1 to 2 capsules once daily before bedtime.
* Do not exceed 1,200 mg of ibuprofen and 75 mg of diphenhydramine within 24 hours, including this medication.
* The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours after taking ibuprofen and/or diphenhydramine components.
* If the cause of symptoms is not identified, do not take continuously for more than 5 days.

### ⚠️ Carolnight Soft Capsule - Precautions
* Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
* When taking this medication, there is an increased risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, gastrointestinal ulceration, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, asthma attack, emphysema, dyspnea due to chronic bronchitis, hypersensitivity reactions such as asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors) or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, glaucoma, obstructive lower urinary tract diseases such as prostatic hypertrophy, children under 15 years of age, convulsive disorders such as epilepsy, pheochromocytoma, insomnia without pain, pain without insomnia, women who are pregnant or may be pregnant, and breastfeeding mothers.

* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
