# EN
### 💊 콜대원코프에스시럽 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, cough, phlegm, sore throat, chills (shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 콜대원코프에스시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 15 years of age or older: 1 pouch (20 mL) per dose.
* For individuals 11 years to under 15 years of age: 2/3 pouch per dose.
* For individuals 7 years to under 11 years of age: 1/2 pouch per dose.
* For individuals 3 years to under 7 years of age: 1/3 pouch per dose.
* For individuals 2 years to under 3 years of age: 1/4 pouch per dose.
* Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 콜대원코프에스시럽 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or its components, have experienced asthma when taking this medicine or its components, other antipyretic analgesics, or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age; an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; a person with a constitution prone to developing hives, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraines, or food allergies; a person with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, or are elderly; a person with a gastroduodenal ulcer, who is physically weak, or has a high fever; a person with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or who has a history of ulcers or bleeding; a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant; a breastfeeding woman; or a person with a cough (cough due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).

* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not take this medicine continuously for a long period.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after taking 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 本药用于缓解感冒的多种症状（流鼻涕、鼻塞、打喷嚏、咳嗽、咳痰、咽喉痛、恶寒（发冷发抖的症状）、发热、头痛、关节痛、肌肉痛）。

* 15岁及以上：每次1包（20毫升）。
* 11岁及以上至未满15岁：每次2/3包。
* 7岁及以上至未满11岁：每次1/2包。

* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

* 11歳以上15歳未満：1回2/3包。
* 7歳以上11歳未満：1回1/2包。
* 3歳以上7歳未満：1回1/3包。
