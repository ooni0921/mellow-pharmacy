#EN

### 💊 Enerity Solution - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for drowsiness and fatigue relief.

### 🕒 Enerity Solution - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 15 years and older, and adults: Take 1 bottle once daily. Do not take more than twice a day.

### ⚠️ Enerity Solution - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are a child under 15 years of age, or have symptoms of excessive stomach acid, heart disease, or stomach ulcers. Pregnant or potentially pregnant women, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not take continuously for a long period.
