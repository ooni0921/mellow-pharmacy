#EN
### 💊 Kwangdong Jagangryeok Yeonjo-Ex (Ssanghwa-tang Yeonjo-Ex) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for frail constitution, fatigue recovery, overwork, spontaneous sweating (a symptom where one sweats naturally even when mentally alert and not physically active), and during and after illness.

### 🕒 Kwangdong Jagangryeok Yeonjo-Ex (Ssanghwa-tang Yeonjo-Ex) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 sachet per dose, 3 times a day, before or between meals.

### ⚠️ Kwangdong Jagangryeok Yeonjo-Ex (Ssanghwa-tang Yeonjo-Ex) - Precautions
* No other specific information is provided.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with hypertension, heart or kidney disease, edema, are elderly, have markedly weak gastrointestinal function, experience symptoms of anorexia, nausea, or vomiting, or are a child.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

If symptoms do not improve after several days of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
