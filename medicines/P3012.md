#EN
### 💊 마그맥스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for physical fatigue, physical weakness during pregnancy and lactation or during/after illness (during illness or recovery), supplementation of vitamins E, B1, and B6 in old age, alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorder, alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder and neck stiffness, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet (chilly hands and feet) during menopause, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (lumbago, shoulder stiffness, etc.), alleviation of symptoms of angular stomatitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (inflammation of the lips), stomatitis (inflammation of the mouth), glossitis (inflammation of the tongue), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그맥스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adolescents aged 12 years and older and adults: Take 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그맥스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this medication, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age, or a patient with severe renal failure.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant (young child), or if you have cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment (kidney disorder), hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders (such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis), require cautious administration of lipid emulsions, are at special risk due to fat overload, have a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), or have a history of hypersensitivity or current hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF).
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
