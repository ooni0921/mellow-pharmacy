### 💊 리렉스담정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for neuralgia, back pain, and stiff shoulders.

### 🕒 리렉스담정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1-2 tablets per dose, twice daily, when experiencing pain or seizures.

### ⚠️ 리렉스담정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due to this medicine; or if you have hives, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraine, food allergies; or if you are an infant, toddler, or child under 14 suspected or infected with chickenpox or influenza; or if you are elderly, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, physically weak, have a high fever, or have liver, kidney, thyroid disease, diabetes, or hypertension.

If symptoms do not improve after several doses, immediately stop taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
