#EN
### 💊 조인트콘친600골드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of physical fatigue, vitamin B1, B2, B6 supplementation during pregnancy/lactation or during/after illness (during illness or after recovery) when physical strength is decreased, neuralgia, muscle and joint pain (such as lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 조인트콘친600골드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years and older, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 조인트콘친600골드정 - Precautions
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, and infants under 3 months of age should not take this drug.
*   Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, premature infants, and young children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 1 month, immediately stop use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
