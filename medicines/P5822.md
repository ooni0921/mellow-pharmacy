#EN
### 💊 나들이시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the prevention and relief of dizziness, vomiting, and headache caused by motion sickness.

### 🕒 나들이시럽 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, take 1/2 pouch (15 ml) per dose. For individuals aged 11 years and older but under 15 years, take 1 pouch (10 ml) per dose. For individuals aged 7 years and older but under 11 years, take 3/4 pouch (7.5 ml) per dose. For individuals aged 3 years and older but under 7 years, take 1/2 pouch (5 ml) per dose. When preventing motion sickness, take 30 minutes before boarding a vehicle or vessel.

If additional doses are needed, take each dose at an interval of at least 4 hours.

Do not exceed 3 doses per day.

### ⚠️ 나들이시럽 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Breastfeeding mothers and infants/young children under 3 years of age should not take this medication.

Before taking this medication, individuals who have experienced allergic symptoms such as rash, redness/congestion, or itching due to medication, pregnant women or those who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, glaucoma patients, patients with urinary dysfunction, and patients with respiratory diseases such as emphysema or chronic bronchitis should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

If a pouch is divided and the remaining amount is to be taken, seal the opening tightly, store it, and consume within 2 days.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
