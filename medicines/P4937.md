#EN
### 💊 위엔자임유디정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for indigestion, abdominal bloating due to indigestion, loss of appetite, overeating, feeling stuffed/heavy, and to aid digestion.

### 🕒 위엔자임유디정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 위엔자임유디정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you are a child under 7 years of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, are pregnant, or may be pregnant.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medication, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
