* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

# EN
### 💊 우루큐백정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness (while sick or after recovery), growth period, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor bone and teeth development, rickets, and the alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (lumbago, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye fatigue, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 우루큐백정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age or older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 우루큐백정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months, have hypercalcemia (a condition characterized by an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 and Yellow No. 5, hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, have kidney stones or a history thereof, or have folic acid deficiency.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN

### 🕒 우루큐백정 - 用法用量

### ⚠️ 우루큐백정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本品过敏者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钙血症（血液中钙质过量存在的状态）、结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病）、肾脏疾病、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本品。

在各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检测值。

服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。
