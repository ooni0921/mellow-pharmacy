# EN
### 💊 네오콜코프캡슐 - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 네오콜코프캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
*   For adults and adolescents aged 15 years and older: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 네오콜코프캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are pregnant or suspect you might be pregnant, are an infant under 3 months of age, are taking MAO inhibitors (e.g., antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are breastfeeding, an infant or young child aged 3 months to under 2 years, elderly, frail, have a high fever, dysuria (difficulty urinating), have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, edema (swelling), glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough that lasts for more than 1 week or recurs, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN

### 🕒 네오콜코프캡슐 - 用法用量
*   15岁以上及成人：一次2粒，一日3次，饭后服用。

*   无信息
*   以下患者请勿服用本药：对本药过敏者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，3个月以下的婴儿，正在服用或停用MAO抑制剂（如抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）不足2周者，以及有半乳糖不耐受症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者。
*   服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师：哺乳期妇女，3个月以上2岁以下的婴幼儿，老年人，体弱者，或伴有高热、排尿困难等症状者，过敏体质者，曾有过敏症状（发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）者，有肝脏、肾脏、心脏、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、水肿（肿胀）、青光眼（眼痛、视力模糊等）者，吸烟者，患有哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过量痰液的咳嗽，持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽，慢性咳嗽，或伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽患者。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   服用后可能会出现嗜睡，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。
*   如服用5~6次后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
