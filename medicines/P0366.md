# EN
### 💊 Ijirong Naebok-aek - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the prevention and relief of motion sickness symptoms such as dizziness, nausea/vomiting, and headache.

### 🕒 Ijirong Naebok-aek - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older: 1 bottle (30 mL) per dose. For individuals aged 11 years or older but under 15 years: 2/3 bottle (20 mL) per dose. For individuals aged 7 years or older but under 11 years: 1/2 bottle (15 mL) per dose. For individuals aged 3 years or older but under 7 years: 1/3 bottle (10 mL) per dose. For the prevention of motion sickness, take one dose 30 minutes before boarding. If additional doses are required, take one dose with an interval of at least 4 hours. The maximum daily dosage is limited to 3 times per day.

### ⚠️ Ijirong Naebok-aek - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a nursing mother or an infant/child under 3 years of age. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness/congestion, itching, etc.), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are a nursing mother, have glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), urinary dysfunction, or respiratory diseases such as emphysema or chronic bronchitis. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* 本药用于预防和缓解晕车引起的头晕、呕吐、头痛等症状。

### 🕒 이지롱내복액 - 用法用量
* 15岁以上成人：一次1瓶 (30 mL)。11岁以上至未满15岁儿童：一次2/3瓶 (20 mL)。7岁以上至未满11岁儿童：一次1/2瓶 (15 mL)。3岁以上至未满7岁儿童：一次1/3瓶 (10 mL)。为预防晕车，请在乘车前30分钟服用一次。如需追加服用，请间隔4小时以上服用一次。每日服用次数最多不超过3次。

### ⚠️ 이지롱내복액 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 哺乳期妇女、3岁以下婴幼儿请勿服用本药。服用本药前，如您有以下情况，请咨询医生或药剂师：有过敏症状（如皮疹、充血发红、瘙痒等）史者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，哺乳期妇女，青光眼患者（眼痛、视力模糊等），排尿障碍者，患有肺气肿或慢性支气管炎等呼吸系统疾病的患者。给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下进行。服用本药后可能会出现困倦，因此驾驶或操作机械时请注意。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
