#EN

### 💊 마하펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain due to the common cold, headache, neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), dysmenorrhea (menstrual pain), sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia (joint pain), and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 마하펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For children aged 12 years and over, and adults: Take 2 capsules (650 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day (every 4 to 6 hours) as needed. Do not exceed a maximum daily dose of 12 capsules (4,000 mg).

### ⚠️ 마하펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker who consumes three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. Rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen, as it may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history of it, have taken barbiturate drugs or tricyclic antidepressants, or have consumed alcohol. Before taking this medicine, patients with hepatic impairment, renal impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, the elderly, pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist. If pain or fever symptoms persist or worsen, or if new symptoms appear, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 마하펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 用法与用量
* 12岁以上儿童及成人：每次服用2粒胶囊 (650 mg)，每日3至4次 (每4至6小时一次)，必要时服用。每日最大剂量不超过12粒胶囊 (4,000 mg)。

### ⚠️ 마하펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒超过三杯的常饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起肝损伤。极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的急性全身发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症等严重皮肤反应。服用本药后，如果出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状，请立即停止服用。醋氨酚每日最大剂量 (4,000 mg) 不得超过。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有醋氨酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏患者、消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全患者、阿司匹林哮喘 (由非甾体抗炎药引起哮喘发作) 或有相关病史者、服用巴比妥类药物或三环类抗抑郁药的患者、饮酒者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，患有肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、心功能异常的患者、有过敏史者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药剂师。如果疼痛或发热症状持续或恶化，或出现新症状，请咨询医生或药剂师。
