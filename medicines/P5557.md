#EN
### 💊 카복텔라점안액(카르복시메틸셀룰로오스나트륨)(1회용) - Efficacy and Effect
*   This medication is used for the temporary relief of burning, irritation, and discomfort due to dryness of the eye or exposure to wind or sun.

### 🕒 카복텔라점안액(카르복시메틸셀룰로오스나트륨)(1회용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Instill 1 to 2 drops into the affected eye(s) as needed. Discard any remaining solution and the container after use.

### ⚠️ 카복텔라점안액(카르복시메틸셀룰로오스나트륨)(1회용) - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not use it.

Before using this medication, patients experiencing severe eye pain, those with a history of allergic reactions (e.g., eye redness, itching, swelling, hyperemia), or those currently receiving medical treatment should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Use this medication for ophthalmic instillation only.

Do not use as a wetting solution for soft contact lenses or while wearing soft contact lenses.

Contact lenses should be removed before using this medication and can be reinserted 15 minutes after instillation.

If using concurrently with other ophthalmic preparations, allow at least a 15-minute interval between their respective instillations.

Due to its high viscosity, temporary blurred vision may occur after instillation of this medication; exercise caution.
