#EN
### 💊 마그스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for physical fatigue; during pregnancy and lactation; physical weakness during illness or convalescence (recovery period); supplementation of vitamins B1, B6, and E in old age; relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorder, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness and coldness in hands and feet; relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, and arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness); beriberi; eye fatigue; relief of symptoms such as angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 12 years and older and adults, take 1 capsule twice daily, or 2 capsules once daily.

### ⚠️ 마그스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have severe renal failure, have hypersensitivity to soybean oil, or have hypersensitivity to soybeans or peanuts. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or if you require careful administration of lipid emulsions; if you are expected to have special risks due to fat overload; if you are an infant under 1 year of age; if you are pregnant or may be pregnant; if you are lactating; if you are a premature infant or young child; if you have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction; if you have renal impairment; if you have hypoproteinemia; or if you have a thrombotic predisposition. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
