#EN
### 💊 아이클리어점안액 - Efficacy and Effect
* This medication is used for eye fatigue, conjunctival hyperemia, discomfort in the eyes after swimming or when dust or sweat enters the eyes, inflammation of the eyes caused by ultraviolet rays and other light, blepharitis (soreness of the eyelids), discomfort when wearing hard contact lenses, itchy eyes, and blurred vision (e.g., when there is excessive eye discharge).

### 🕒 아이클리어점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops at a time, 5-6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 아이클리어점안액 - Precautions
* Before using this medication, patients with severe eye pain, those who have experienced allergic symptoms to eye drops (e.g., conjunctival hyperemia, itching, swelling, rash, redness due to congestion), patients with glaucoma, or those with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medication is for ophthalmic use only. Do not use as a soft contact lens solution or while wearing soft contact lenses. To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the product if possible. Do not use expired medication, and use opened containers as soon as possible.

#CN

* 本药用于缓解眼睛疲劳、结膜充血、游泳后或灰尘汗水进入眼睛时的不适感、紫外线及其他光线引起的眼部炎症、眼睑溃烂、佩戴硬性隐形眼镜时的不适感、眼睛瘙痒、以及视物模糊（如眼屎过多时等）。

* 在使用本药前，眼睛疼痛剧烈的患者、对眼药水有过敏症状（例：眼睛充血、瘙痒、肿胀、皮疹、充血发红等）的患者、青光眼患者、或对丙二醇过敏的患者或有过敏史者，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药仅供滴眼用。请勿用作软性隐形眼镜的佩戴液或在佩戴软性隐形眼镜时使用。为防止污染，请尽量避免与他人共用。请勿使用超过有效期的药物，已开封的药物请尽快使用。
