# EN
### 💊 리버베타액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for liver diseases.

### 🕒 리버베타액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 10-30 mL daily, divided between meals. Dilute 10 mL of this medicine in about half a cup of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 리버베타액 - Precautions
* No specific information.
* Do not take this medicine if you have kidney disease, myocardial infarction, or a history thereof. This medicine cannot be used as an antidote for alcohol poisoning. If there is no improvement after approximately one month of taking this medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medicine is for oral use only and should not be used for injection.

# CN
### 💊 리버베타액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于肝脏疾病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 리버베타액 - 用法与用量
* 成人每日分次在两餐之间服用10~30毫升。将本药10毫升稀释于约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 리버베타액 - 注意事项
* 无特别信息。
* 患有肾脏疾病、心肌梗死或有其病史者请勿服用本药。本药不能用作酒精中毒的解毒剂。如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药仅供口服，不可用于注射。
