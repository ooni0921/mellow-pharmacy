#EN

### 💊 오네론액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the prevention and relief of dizziness, vomiting, and headache caused by motion sickness.

### 🕒 오네론액 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older: take 1 sachet.
* For individuals aged 11 to under 15 years: take 2/3 sachet.
* For individuals aged 7 to under 11 years: take 1/2 sachet.
* For individuals aged 3 to under 7 years: take 1/3 sachet.
* Take the prescribed dose once, 30 minutes before boarding.

If additional doses are needed, take each subsequent dose with an interval of at least 4 hours.

The maximum daily dosage frequency for this medication is 2 times.

### ⚠️ 오네론액 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medication if you are a breastfeeding mother or an infant/young child under 3 years of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms such as rash, redness, or itching due to medication, if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, have glaucoma, urination disorders, heart problems, respiratory diseases such as emphysema or chronic bronchitis, are frail, elderly, or have a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF).

If you use a partial sachet, seal the opening well, store it, and use the remaining portion within 2 days.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN

### 💊 오네론액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于预防和缓解因晕车、晕船、晕机等引起的头晕、呕吐、头痛。

### 🕒 오네론액 - 用法用量
* 15岁及以上：每次1袋。
* 11岁及以上至15岁以下：每次2/3袋。
* 7岁及以上至11岁以下：每次1/2袋。
* 3岁及以上至7岁以下：每次1/3袋。
* 在乘车（船、机）前30分钟服用一次。

如果需要额外服用，请在每次服用之间间隔至少4小时。

本药每日最大服用次数为2次。

### ⚠️ 오네론액 - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 哺乳期妇女、3岁以下的婴幼儿请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如您曾因药物出现皮疹、充血发红、瘙痒等过敏症状，或您是孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、青光眼患者、排尿障碍患者、心脏疾病患者、肺气肿或慢性支气管炎等呼吸系统疾病患者、体弱者、老年人、对黄色5号·日落黄FCF过敏者或有相关经历者，请咨询医生或药师。

如果分割药袋后使用剩余部分，请将开口密封保存，并在2天内使用。

服用本药后可能会出现嗜睡，因此驾驶和操作机械时请注意。

儿童请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

### 🕒 오네론액 - 用法・用量
