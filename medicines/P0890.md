# EN
### 💊 베타엠크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for secondarily infected allergic or inflammatory skin diseases (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis) and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 베타엠크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area (diseased area) 1 to 3 times daily.

### ⚠️ 베타엠크림 - Precautions
* No information available
* Patients with a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to this drug, aminoglycoside antibiotics (such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin), or bacitracin, those with eczematous external otitis with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (excluding Behçet's disease), second-degree deep burns or higher, frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, and rosacea should not use this drug.
* Before using this medication, patients with a history of propylene glycol hypersensitivity or allergic reactions, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, infants, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
* Do not use for purposes other than treatment after applying makeup or shaving.
* Do not use occlusive dressings without medical supervision.

# CN
### 💊 베타엠크림 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于继发感染的过敏性或炎症性皮肤病（湿疹、接触性皮炎、脂溢性皮炎、特应性皮炎、光敏性皮炎、慢性单纯性苔藓、擦烂、剥脱性皮炎、瘙痒、银屑病）和一度烧伤。

### 🕒 베타엠크림 - 用法用量
* 每日1~3次，取适量涂抹于患处（病患部位）。

### ⚠️ 베타엠크림 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药或链霉素、卡那霉素、庆大霉素、新霉素等氨基糖苷类抗生素或杆菌肽有超敏反应或过敏史者，伴有鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳道炎，溃疡（白塞氏病除外），二度深层及以上烧伤·冻伤，口周皮炎，寻常痤疮，酒渣鼻患者，请勿使用本药。
* 在使用本药前，对丙二醇有超敏反应或过敏史者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，婴幼儿应咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。
* 请勿用于眼科用途。
* 化妆或剃须后，请勿用于治疗以外的目的。

# JP

### 🕒 베타엠크림 - 用法・用量
* 1日1～3回、患部（疾患部位）に適切な量を塗布します。
