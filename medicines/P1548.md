# EN
### 💊 안국아시클로버크림 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for herpes simplex virus infections (including initial and recurrent genital herpes and labial herpes).

### 🕒 안국아시클로버크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area 5 times a day, at approximately 4-hour intervals. Treatment should be continued for 5 days, and if the condition does not improve after 5 days of use, it can be used for an additional 5 days.

### ⚠️ 안국아시클로버크림 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, are breastfeeding, or are a child. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an immunocompromised patient (e.g., with AIDS or bone marrow transplant), pregnant or potentially pregnant, or have a history of propylene glycol hypersensitivity. Start treatment as early as possible; in cases of recurrence, begin at the first appearance of prodromal symptoms or lesions. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not use this medication for the prevention of transmission to others or for the prevention of recurrent herpes simplex virus infections. Do not dilute this medication or use it as a base to mix with other drugs. This medication is for external use only. Do not use it for ophthalmic purposes on the cornea or mucous membranes. To prevent the spread of this medication to other parts of the body or transmission to others, apply it while wearing a finger cot or rubber gloves.

* 本药品用于单纯疱疹病毒感染症（包括初期和复发性生殖器疱疹和唇疱疹）。

### 🕒 안국아시클로버크림 - 用法与用量

* 无信息
