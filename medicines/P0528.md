#EN
### 💊 무스판정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for paroxysmal pain in gastrointestinal disorders, or functional disorders and spasmodic pain in the biliary system, urinary system, and female reproductive system, such as dysmenorrhea.

### 🕒 무스판정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets once, 3 times a day. Do not exceed a maximum daily dose of 6 tablets.

### ⚠️ 무스판정 - Precautions
*   If you are a regular drinker of three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this drug or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause liver damage.
*   Do not take this drug if you have experienced hypersensitivity reactions (rash, red spots, itching, swelling of the larynx, eyelids, lips, etc.) to this drug, or asthma due to other cold medicines or antipyretic analgesics, severe renal impairment, glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase deficiency, glaucoma, urination disorders (difficulty urinating) due to prostatic hypertrophy, functional stenosis of the gastrointestinal tract such as pyloric stenosis (narrowing of the pylorus), paralytic or obstructive ileus (intestinal obstruction), severe myasthenia gravis, megacolon, tachycardia and rapid arrhythmia, severe hepatic impairment, severe heart disease, hemorrhagic colitis, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, or if you have taken barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol.
*   Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bacterial diarrhea, liver impairment or a history thereof, Gilbert's syndrome (Meulengracht's syndrome), cardiac dysfunction (coronary artery insufficiency, heart failure, arrhythmia, etc.), renal impairment or a history thereof, a history of peptic ulcer, blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendencies, bronchial asthma, ulcerative colitis, hyperthyroidism, fever, prostatic hypertrophy, are elderly, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, at risk of narrow-angle glaucoma, susceptible to intestinal or ureteral outlet obstruction, or prone to rapid arrhythmia.
*   As visual accommodation disorders may occur, exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

*   本药用于胃肠道疾病的阵发性疼痛，或胆道系统、泌尿系统、女性生殖系统（如痛经）的功能障碍和痉挛性疼痛。

### 🕒 무스판정 - 用法及用量
*   成人一次服用1至2片，一日3次。一日最大剂量不得超过6片。

### ⚠️ 무스판정 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上且定期饮酒者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。
*   有本药过敏史（皮疹、红斑、瘙痒、喉头、眼睑、唇部等浮肿），或其他感冒药或解热镇痛药引起的哮喘史，严重肾功能障碍，葡萄糖-6-磷酸脱氢酶缺乏症患者，青光眼，前列腺肥大引起的排尿（排小便）障碍，幽门狭窄（幽门变窄）等胃肠道功能性狭窄，麻痹性或闭塞性肠梗阻（肠道堵塞）患者，严重肌无力症，巨结肠患者，心动过速及快速型心律失常患者，严重肝功能障碍，严重心脏病，出血性结肠炎，消化性溃疡，严重血液异常患者，以及服用巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药、酒精者，请勿服用本药。
