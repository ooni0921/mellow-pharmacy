#EN
### 💊 투스딘골드캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for gingivitis and mild to moderate periodontitis after periodontal treatment.

### 🕒 투스딘골드캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: Take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day after meals.

### ⚠️ 투스딘골드캡슐 - Precautions
* No specific information provided.
* Do not administer this medicine to children under 15 years of age, or to patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., rash, redness, itching) to medicines or cosmetics, allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) to eggs, if you have an allergic constitution, or if you have or have experienced hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5). Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
