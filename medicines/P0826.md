#EN
### 💊 복합쓸기담연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the improvement of physical fatigue, as a nutritional tonic, and for vitamin B1 supplementation during and after illness.

### 🕒 복합쓸기담연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 15 years of age and older should take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 복합쓸기담연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No other specific information.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine should not take it. Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
