#EN
### 💊 소니펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medication is used for fever reduction in acute upper respiratory tract infections.

### 🕒 소니펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage & Administration
*   For children aged 6 months and older, take 0.4-0.6 mL/kg (5-7 mg/kg) per dose, as needed, every 4-6 hours, not exceeding a maximum of 4 doses per day (28 mg/kg).

For children weighing 30 kg or less, do not exceed a maximum of 25 mL (300 mg) per day.

For recommended dosages by age group, please refer to the approved product information.

### ⚠️ 소니펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication may increase the risk of serious and potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, as well as severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, liver impairment, kidney impairment, heart failure, hypertension, bronchial asthma, asthma, hives, or allergies caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, or if you have a history of such reactions. It should also not be taken by patients undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding mothers.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding tendency, liver impairment, kidney impairment, heart failure, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, gastric cancer, Crohn's disease, or if you are elderly, a child, or taking low-dose aspirin for the prevention of myocardial infarction or stroke.

#CN
### 💊 소니펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于急性上呼吸道感染引起的发热退烧。

### 🕒 소니펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 用法用量
*   6个月以上的儿童，每次服用0.4-0.6 mL/kg (5-7 mg/kg)，必要时每隔4-6小时服用，每日最多不超过4次 (28 mg/kg)。

对于体重30 kg以下的儿童，每日最大剂量不超过25 mL (300 mg)。

各年龄段的推荐剂量请参考批准的说明书。

### ⚠️ 소니펜시럽(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上的定期饮酒者，在服用本药或其他解热镇痛剂时，请咨询医生或药剂师。这可能会引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药可能会增加可能致命的严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死和中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
*   对本药过敏者，患有消化性溃疡、严重血液异常、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心力衰竭、高血压、支气管哮喘、阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛剂引起的哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏症患者或有相关病史者，以及进行冠状动脉旁路移植术前后疼痛治疗的患者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，请勿服用本药。

在服用本药之前，如果您有过敏症、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心力衰竭、高血压、系统性红斑狼疮、混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、胃癌、克罗恩病，或者您是老年人、儿童，或正在服用低剂量阿司匹林以预防心肌梗死或中风，请咨询医生或药剂师。
