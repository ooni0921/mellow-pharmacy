### 💊 Tavazagel - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of pain and inflammation in cases of varicose edema, superficial phlebitis, traumatic injuries, sports injuries (bruises), and tenosynovitis.

### 🕒 Tavazagel - Dosage and Administration
* Apply a thin layer several times a day to the affected or infected area, then gently rub it into the skin.

### ⚠️ Tavazagel - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug or salicylates, if you are within 4 weeks of your expected delivery date, or on mucous membranes or open wounds. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a neonate or child, have renal (kidney) insufficiency, are pregnant or likely to be pregnant, or are breastfeeding. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Be careful to avoid contact with eyes and do not apply too widely. For external use only. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
