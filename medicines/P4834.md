#EN
### 💊 아로나민골드프리미엄정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness (병중·병후), growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the prevention of poor bone and tooth development, rickets, beriberi, and for the relief of symptoms such as eye fatigue, neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (perleche), cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 아로나민골드프리미엄정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet per dose, 1 to 2 times daily.

### ⚠️ 아로나민골드프리미엄정 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or potentially pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiovascular dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, a history of kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow, which can affect clinical test values.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
