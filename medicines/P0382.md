#EN
### 💊 셀톤크림 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin diseases (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo (skin chafing), exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis (scaly skin)), and 1st-degree burns.

### 🕒 셀톤크림 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 셀톤크림 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, or have a history of hypersensitivity to aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or bacitracin, or have skin infections (tuberculosis, syphilis, candidiasis, tinea, herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia, scabies, pubic lice), eczematous otitis externa with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (except Behcet's disease), 2nd-degree deep burns or higher and frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, or rosacea.
*   Before using this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, nursing mothers, infants, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
*   Do not use for purposes other than treatment after cosmetic application or shaving.
*   Do not use occlusive dressing therapy without a doctor's supervision.

#CN
### 💊 셀톤크림 - 功效及效果
*   本药物用于继发感染的过敏性或炎症性皮肤病（湿疹、接触性皮炎、脂溢性皮炎、特应性皮炎、光敏性皮炎、慢性单纯性苔藓、擦烂（皮肤擦伤）、剥脱性皮炎、瘙痒、牛皮癣（银屑病）），以及一度烧伤。

### 🕒 셀톤크림 - 用法及用量
*   每日1至3次，取适量涂抹于患处（疾病部位）。

### ⚠️ 셀톤크림 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   以下患者请勿使用本药物：对本药物过敏者；对链霉素、卡那霉素、庆大霉素、新霉素等氨基糖苷类抗生素或杆菌肽有过敏史或过敏者；皮肤感染（结核病、梅毒、念珠菌病、癣、带状疱疹、单纯疱疹、水痘、牛痘、疥疮、阴虱）；鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳道炎；溃疡（除白塞病外）；二度深层以上烧伤及冻伤；口周皮炎；寻常痤疮；酒渣鼻患者。
*   在使用本药物前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、婴幼儿应咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
*   请勿用于眼科。
*   请勿在化妆或剃须后用于治疗以外的目的。
*   请勿在没有医生指导的情况下使用密封包扎法。
