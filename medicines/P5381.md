#EN
### 💊 화이투벤큐플러스코프연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 화이투벤큐플러스코프연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older but under 15 years: 1 capsule per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 화이투벤큐플러스코프연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker consuming more than three alcoholic beverages daily and take this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
*   Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can very rarely be fatal, have been reported. Discontinue this medicine immediately if skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking it.
*   Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines (expectorants), or are an infant under 3 months of age.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic constitution; have experienced allergic reactions (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty with urination; are frail or have a high fever; have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are breastfeeding; are undergoing treatment from a doctor or dentist (e.g., taking other medications such as diabetes medication, gout medication, arthritis medication, anticoagulants, steroids); have a cough (cough due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache); or are taking barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, and alcohol.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   For administration to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

#CN
### 💊 화이투벤큐플러스코프연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解感冒的各种症状（流鼻涕、鼻塞、打喷嚏、咽喉痛、咳嗽、咳痰、发冷（寒战）、发热、头痛、关节痛、肌肉痛）。

### 🕒 화이투벤큐플러스코프연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   15岁及以上成人，每日3次，每次2粒，饭后30分钟服用。
*   8岁以上未满15岁者，每日3次，每次1粒，饭后30分钟服用。

### ⚠️ 화이투벤큐플러스코프연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒超过三杯的长期饮酒者，服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起肝损伤。
*   曾有报告，非常罕见地可能发生危及生命的重度皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。
*   每日服用对乙酰氨基酚的剂量请勿超过最大推荐剂量（4,000 mg）。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与其他含有对乙酰氨基酚的产品同时服用。
*   对本药过敏者，服用其他解热镇痛药、感冒药（化痰药）时曾有哮喘经历者，以及未满3个月的婴儿，请勿服用本药。
*   在服用本药前，若您是感染或疑似感染水痘或流感的婴儿或未满15岁的儿童；过敏体质者；有过敏反应（如发热、皮疹、关节痛、哮喘、瘙痒等）经历者；患有肝病、肾病、心脏病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、胃十二指肠溃疡、青光眼（眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者；体弱者或高热者；胃灼热、胃部不适、胃痛等胃肠道问题持续或反复发作，或有溃疡、出血问题者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性；哺乳期妇女；正在接受医生或牙医治疗的人（正在服用糖尿病药、痛风药、关节炎药、抗凝剂、类固醇等其他药物的人）；咳嗽患者（吸烟、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰液的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽）；正在服用巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药和酒精者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   服用后可能出现困倦，因此驾驶和操作机械时请注意。
