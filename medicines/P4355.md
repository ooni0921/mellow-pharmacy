#EN

### 💊 헤모엔크림(프라목신염산염) - Indications & Efficacy
* This medicine is used for the temporary relief of discomfort, burning, and itching associated with hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 헤모엔크림(프라목신염산염) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults should apply directly to the affected area up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 헤모엔크림(프라목신염산염) - Precautions
* Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to it. Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are under 12 years of age, pregnant or likely to be pregnant, or have experienced hypersensitivity to propylene glycol. Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. This medicine is for external use in the anal area only and should not be used ophthalmically. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 🕒 헤모엔크림(프라목신염산염) - 用法用量

* 对本药过敏的患者请勿使用本药。在使用本药之前，12岁以下的儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、对丙二醇过敏或有相关病史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法用量。给儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。本药仅用于肛门部位，不可用于眼科。如果使用约7天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
