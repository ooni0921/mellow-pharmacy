* 8세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 센도스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A1, B1, B2, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness, growth period, and old age, as well as for the relief of dry eyes, night blindness, poor development of bones and teeth, and prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 센도스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age or older: Take one capsule once daily.

### ⚠️ 센도스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, soybean oil, soy, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, have a history of allergies, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), or kidney disease.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with or a history of gout or kidney stones, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient with folic acid deficiency, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage), or a patient at special risk due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may cause urine to turn yellow, affecting clinical test values.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

若服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
