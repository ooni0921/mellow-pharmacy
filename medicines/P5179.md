#EN
### 💊 벤포마그빅액티브에스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness (during convalescence), vitamin E, B1, B2, B6 supplementation in old age, beriberi, eye strain, alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as stiff shoulders/neck, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet. It is also used for neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), alleviation of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, and muscle cramps caused by magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 벤포마그빅액티브에스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 12 years and older, take 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 벤포마그빅액티브에스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Information not available.
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this medication, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, have experienced allergic reactions to soybean oil, have severe renal insufficiency, or are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity or have experienced hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant or infant/young child, have cardiac or circulatory dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or if you require careful administration of lipid emulsions, or if you are expected to have a particular risk due to fat overload.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This medication may cause urine to turn yellow and affect clinical laboratory test results.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
