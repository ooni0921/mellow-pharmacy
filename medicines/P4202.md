### 🕒 릴리애정 - 용법 및 용량
* 1일 1정씩 21일간 복용하고, 7일간 휴약한 후 새로운 포장을 복용합니다.정제는 매일 대략 같은 시간에, 물과 함께, 포장에 표시된 순서대로 복용합니다.7일의 휴약기간 동안 소퇴성 출혈이 나타나는데, 대개 정제의 마지막 복용 후 2~3일 내에 시작되며, 새로운 포장을 시작하기 전에 월경이 끝나지 않을 수도 있습니다.자세한 사항은 허가사항 상세정보를 참고하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 릴리애정 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for contraception.

### 🕒 릴리애정 - Dosage and Administration
* Take one tablet daily for 21 days, then take a 7-day tablet-free interval before starting a new pack. Tablets should be taken at approximately the same time each day, with water, and in the order indicated on the pack. Withdrawal bleeding usually occurs during the 7-day tablet-free interval, typically starting 2-3 days after the last tablet was taken, and may not have finished before the new pack is started. For detailed information, refer to the full prescribing information.

### ⚠️ 릴리애정 - Warnings and Precautions
* Smoking increases the risk of serious cardiovascular side effects from oral contraceptive use, including thrombosis. Women who use oral contraceptives should be strongly advised not to smoke. Women over 35 who smoke should not use this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have: hypersensitivity to this medicine, venous thrombosis, arterial thrombosis, prodromal symptoms of thrombosis, predisposition to venous or arterial thrombosis such as activated protein C (APC) resistance, antithrombin-III deficiency, protein C deficiency, protein S deficiency, hyperhomocysteinemia, antiphospholipid antibodies, migraine, diabetes, severe hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, and severe metabolic disorders such as porphyria, major surgery with prolonged immobilization, pancreatitis due to severe hypertriglyceridemia, severe liver disease, tumors (liver tumors, pituitary tumors), malignant tumors due to sex steroids (in the reproductive organs or breasts), undiagnosed vaginal bleeding, pregnant or possibly pregnant women, persistent itching during pregnancy, gestational herpes, pemphigoid gestationis or otosclerosis, patients receiving direct-acting antiviral agents for hepatitis C, patients with congenital or acquired hypercoagulopathy, smokers aged 35 years or older, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker, have high blood pressure, are obese, are over 40 years old, have liver disease, arterial disease, systemic lupus erythematosus, a history of breast cancer, depression, heart or kidney failure, atypical hyperplastic mammary disease, a history of breast nodules, heart valve disease, atrial fibrillation, a history of hyperlipidemia, gallbladder disease and gallstones, endometriosis, asthma, or chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorders, or diabetes.
* This medicine does not protect against HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases.
* If you have severe gastrointestinal disorders, absorption may be incomplete, so use additional contraceptive methods.

### 🕒 릴리애정 - 用法用量

* 以下患者请勿服用本药：对此药过敏的患者，静脉血栓形成，动脉血栓，血栓形成前驱症状，有静脉或动脉血栓形成倾向的患者，例如活化蛋白C（APC）抵抗、抗凝血酶-III缺乏、蛋白C缺乏、蛋白S缺乏、高同型半胱氨酸血症、抗磷脂抗体，偏头痛，糖尿病，重度高脂血症，高脂蛋白血症以及卟啉症等重度代谢障碍患者，长期不动的大手术，重度高甘油三酯血症引起的胰腺炎，重度肝病，肿瘤（肝肿瘤，垂体肿瘤），性类固醇引起的恶性肿瘤（生殖器或乳房），原因不明的阴道出血，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，怀孕期间持续瘙痒，妊娠疱疹，妊娠天疱疮或耳硬化症，正在接受丙型肝炎直接作用抗病毒药物治疗的患者，先天性或获得性高凝血病患者，35岁以上吸烟者，以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者。服用本药前，如果您是吸烟者、高血压患者、肥胖者、40岁以上女性、肝病患者、动脉疾病患者、系统性红斑狼疮患者、有乳腺癌病史、抑郁症患者、心肾功能不全患者、非典型增生性乳腺病患者、有乳腺结节病史、心脏瓣膜疾病、心房颤动患者、有高脂血症病史、胆囊疾病和胆石症病史、子宫内膜异位症、哮喘或黄褐斑患者、周围血液循环障碍患者、糖尿病患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
