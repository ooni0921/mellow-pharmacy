# EN
### 💊 비올씨정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamins B1, B2, B6, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, and during periods of physical weakness (during or after illness). It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder pain), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis; for beriberi, eye fatigue; for the alleviation of pigmentation (e.g., melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases; and for the prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds).

### 🕒 비올씨정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 8 years and older, and adults, should take 1 tablet per dose, twice daily (morning and evening).

### ⚠️ 비올씨정 - Precautions
*   None specified
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, and infants under 3 months of age, should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), a patient with folic acid deficiency, a patient with gout or kidney stones, or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision and direction of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 비올씨정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解体力疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时补充维生素B1、B2、B6、C，以及缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩痛等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状，治疗脚气病、眼疲劳，减轻由阳光或皮肤病引起的色素沉着（如黄褐斑、雀斑），并预防牙龈出血和鼻出血（流鼻血）。

### 🕒 비올씨정 - 用法用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每次1片，每日2次（早晨、晚上）服用。

### ⚠️ 비올씨정 - 注意事项
*   无相关信息
*   对本药过敏的患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿，请勿服用本药。

在服用本药前，以下人群请咨询医生或药师：未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症患者（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、叶酸缺乏患者、痛风或肾结石患者、对日落黄（黄色5号）过敏或有相关病史的患者。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变为黄色，从而影响临床检查值。

如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
