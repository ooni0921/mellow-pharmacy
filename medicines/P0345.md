#EN
### 💊 메디락-베베산 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for regulating bowel movements, constipation, loose stools, abdominal bloating, and abnormal intestinal fermentation.

### 🕒 메디락-베베산 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 3 years and older: 1.5 g per dose.
* For individuals aged 1 to 2 years: 1 g per dose.
* For infants aged 3 months to under 1 year: 0.5 g per dose.
* Take 1 to 3 times a day, at intervals of 4 hours, after or between meals. It can be mixed with water, milk, baby food, etc., or taken as is.

### ⚠️ 메디락-베베산 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medication.
* For infants aged 3 months to under 1 year, consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medication, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 3个月以上~1岁以下的婴儿在服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
