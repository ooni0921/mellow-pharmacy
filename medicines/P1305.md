# EN
### 💊 마로엘정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for loss of appetite (anorexia), abdominal distension, abnormal intestinal fermentation, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 마로엘정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults (15 years or older) take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day. Individuals aged 11 years or older but under 15 years take 1 tablet once, 2 times a day. Take before meals, between meals, or after meals. Maintain a dosing interval of at least 4 hours. For the initial dose, take the minimum amount and gradually increase or decrease the dose while observing the shape and condition of your stool.

### ⚠️ 마로엘정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you have an acute abdominal disease (such as appendicitis, intestinal bleeding, ulcerative colitis), intestinal obstruction, or if you are a child under 7 years of age. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you experience severe abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, have kidney dysfunction, rectal bleeding, or a history of bowel dysfunction. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

### ⚠️ 마로엘정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
