#EN
### 💊 휴니즈아세틸시스테인캡슐200mg - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for difficulty in expectoration in diseases such as acute and chronic bronchitis, bronchial asthma, laryngitis, sinusitis, and cystic fibrosis.

### 🕒 휴니즈아세틸시스테인캡슐200mg - Dosage and Administration
* **For acute conditions:** Adults should take 1 capsule (200 mg) per dose, 3 times a day. Children aged 6 to 14 years should take 1 capsule (200 mg) per dose, 2 times a day. Take before meals with a small amount of water.
* **For chronic conditions:** Adults should take 1 capsule (200 mg) per dose, 2 times a day. Children aged 6 to 14 years should take 1/2 capsule (100 mg) per dose, 3 times a day. Take before meals with a small amount of water.
* **For cystic fibrosis:** Individuals aged 6 years and older should take 1 capsule (200 mg) per dose, 3 times a day. Take before meals with a small amount of water.

### ⚠️ 휴니즈아세틸시스테인캡슐200mg - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastric or duodenal ulcers, infants under 2 years of age, or those with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.
* Before taking this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 휴니즈아세틸시스테인캡슐200mg - 功效与作用
* 本药用于急慢性支气管炎、支气管哮喘、喉炎、鼻窦炎、囊性纤维化等疾病引起的痰液排出困难。

### 🕒 휴니즈아세틸시스테인캡슐200mg - 用法与用量
* **急性疾病患者：** 成人每次1粒（200毫克），每日3次；6-14岁儿童每次1粒（200毫克），每日2次。均应饭前用少量水服用。
* **慢性疾病患者：** 成人每次1粒（200毫克），每日2次；6-14岁儿童每次1/2粒（100毫克），每日3次。均应饭前用少量水服用。
* **囊性纤维化患者：** 6岁以上儿童每次1粒（200毫克），每日3次。饭前用少量水服用。

### ⚠️ 휴니즈아세틸시스테인캡슐200mg - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者、胃或十二指肠溃疡患者、2岁以下婴儿，以及患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期女性，请咨询医生或药师。
