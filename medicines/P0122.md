### 🕒 제텐씨정 - 용법 및 용량
* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 식후에 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 제텐씨정 - 주의사항

#EN
### 💊 제텐씨정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of reduced physical strength during or after illness (while sick or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; alleviation of pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight, skin diseases, etc.; prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds); and supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 제텐씨정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age or older: Take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 제텐씨정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or potentially pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, or a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, thrombotic predisposition, folate deficiency, or a history of hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4).
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

#CN
### 💊 제텐씨정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解身体疲劳，妊娠、哺乳期，疾病期间或康复后体力下降时以及老年人维生素B1、B2、B6、C、E的补充；缓解口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状；缓解因阳光、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑）；预防牙龈出血、鼻出血（鼻衄）以及补充锌。

### 🕒 제텐씨정 - 用法用量
*   12岁以上及成人，一次1片，一日1次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 제텐씨정 - 注意事项
*   对本药过敏的患者，以及未满12个月的婴儿请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、心脏、循环系统功能障碍、肾脏功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风、肾结石、有血栓倾向的患者、缺乏叶酸的患者、对酒石黄（黄色4号）过敏或有相关经历的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查结果。

*   本剤は、肉体疲労、妊娠・授乳期、病中・病後（病気中または回復後）の体力低下時、老年期におけるビタミンB1、B2、B6、C、Eの補給、口角炎、口唇炎、口内炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮膚炎などの症状緩和、日焼け、皮膚病などによる色素沈着（しみ、そばかす）の緩和、歯ぐきからの出血・鼻出血（鼻血）の予防、および亜鉛の補給に用いられます。

### 🕒 제텐씨정 - 用法・用量
