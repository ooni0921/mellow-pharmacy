#EN
### 💊 화콜키즈콜드시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 화콜키즈콜드시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals 15 years and older, take 20 mL (4 sachets) per dose. For individuals 11 years to under 15 years, take 13 mL per dose. For individuals 7 years to under 11 years, take 10 mL (2 sachets) per dose. For individuals 3 years to under 7 years, take 6.5 mL per dose. For individuals 2 years to under 3 years, take 5 mL (1 sachet) per dose. Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.
* The dosing interval should be at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 화콜키즈콜드시럽 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker (three or more drinks daily) and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.
* Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, Parkinson's disease medications, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic constitution or a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastro-duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcers or bleeding issues; are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding; are a child under 2 years of age; are a smoker; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* Do not continue taking this medication for a prolonged period.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
