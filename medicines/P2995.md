#EN
### 💊 빅콘에스600정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins B1, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy or lactation, and physical weakness during or after illness, as well as for the relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, stiff shoulders), beriberi, and eye strain.

### 🕒 빅콘에스600정 - Dosage and Administration
*   No information

### ⚠️ 빅콘에스600정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug, an infant under 12 months of age, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to soy oil, are hypersensitive to soy or peanuts, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac or circulatory dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, disorders of fat metabolism such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or a patient for whom lipid emulsions should be administered cautiously.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

This medicine may turn urine yellow and may affect clinical test values.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after taking the medicine for about 1 month, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
