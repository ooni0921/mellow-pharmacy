#EN
### 💊 요모겐트로키(플루르비프로펜) - Indications & Effects
* This medicine is used for the short-term symptomatic relief of sore throat.

### 🕒 요모겐트로키(플루르비프로펜) - Dosage & Administration
* Adults, children and adolescents 12 years of age and over should slowly dissolve one lozenge in the mouth.

Move the lozenge around in the mouth while dissolving to avoid localized irritation.

If needed, take a maximum of 5 lozenges per day, with an interval of 3 to 6 hours between doses, and use for a maximum of 3 days.

### ⚠️ 요모겐트로키(플루르비프로펜) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); peptic ulcer, bleeding; asthma (including asthmatic attacks), urticaria, bronchospasm, rhinitis caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, or a history thereof; severe cardiac insufficiency; gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation caused by NSAIDs or antirheumatic drugs; severe colitis; severe blood disorders; unclear hematopoietic disorders; severe hepatic impairment; severe renal impairment; are in the last three months of pregnancy; are breastfeeding; have hereditary fructose intolerance, glucose-galactose malabsorption, or sucrose-isomaltase deficiency; or are a child under 12 years of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have a history of bronchial asthma; cardiac insufficiency, renal impairment, hepatic impairment, hypertension, blood disorders; bleeding tendency; are elderly; have peripheral arterial occlusive disease, cerebrovascular disease, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, are a smoker; have hay fever, nasal polyps, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease; are in the first or second trimester of pregnancy; have fluid retention, or heart failure.

Dizziness and visual disturbances may occur after taking this medicine, so be cautious when driving or operating machinery.
