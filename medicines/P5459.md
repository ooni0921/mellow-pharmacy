#EN
### 💊 알파엠지정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the supply of vitamins (E, B2, B6) during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical debility during or after illness (while sick or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms (stiff shoulders/neck, numbness/coldness in hands and feet), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 알파엠지정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and individuals 15 years of age or older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 알파엠지정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have severe renal failure, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to soybean oil, or have hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient currently receiving medical treatment, pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatic inflammation, or other lipid metabolism disorders, or if you are a patient who must be administered lipid emulsions cautiously.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
