#EN
### 💊 쏘큐정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for loss of appetite (anorexia), abdominal bloating, indigestion, overeating, upset stomach, nausea, vomiting, promotion of digestion, and abdominal bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 쏘큐정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 쏘큐정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not administer this medication to children under 7 years of age or patients undergoing dialysis therapy.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you have an allergic constitution, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or have phosphate deficiency.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

#CN

*   本药用于食欲减退（食欲不振）、胃部胀满感、消化不良、过食、积食、恶心、呕吐、促进消化、以及因消化不良引起的胃部胀满感。

*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
