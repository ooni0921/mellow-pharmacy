#EN
### 💊 아노덱스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, B1, B2, B6, C, D, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, periods of physical weakness (during or after illness), growth periods, and old age.
* It is also used for the relief of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), impaired development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia, and supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 아노덱스정 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 8 years and older, and adults: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 아노덱스정 - Precautions
* Taking more than 5,000 IU of vitamin A daily can cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medicine if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy.

* Accidental overdose in children under 6 years of age can lead to fatal iron poisoning. Keep this medicine out of reach of children.
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, infants under 12 months, hyperkalemia, hemochromatosis (a disorder of iron metabolism where iron is deposited in the liver and pancreas), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year old, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalate is excreted in the urine), pregnant or possibly pregnant women, lactating women, premature infants, infants (young children), patients with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, patients with cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, acute or chronic bronchitis, pulmonary edema, alkalosis, peptic ulcer, a history of gout or kidney stones, patients with folic acid deficiency, or patients with a predisposition to thrombosis (blood clots).

* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

* This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

* If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 아노덱스정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于补充维生素A、B1、B2、B6、C、D、E，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中及病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时、发育期、老年期。
* 此外，还用于缓解眼睛干涩、夜盲症（夜间视力不佳的症状）、骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病的预防，以及缺铁性贫血的预防和治疗，并补充锌。

### 🕒 아노덱스정 - 用法与用量
* 8岁以上及成人，每次1片，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 아노덱스정 - 注意事项
* 每日服用维生素A超过5,000 IU可能导致先天性畸形。怀孕前3个月内或可能怀孕的女性请勿服用本药。

* 6岁以下儿童过量服用可能导致铁中毒性死亡，请将本药放置在儿童接触不到的地方。
* 对本药过敏的患者、高钙血症（血液中钙质过多的状态）患者、结节病（病因不明的全身炎症性疾病）患者、肾脏疾病患者、未满12个月的婴儿、高钾血症患者、血色素沉着症（铁代谢异常导致铁沉积在肝脏、胰腺的疾病）、含铁血黄素沉着症、非缺铁性贫血、肾结石、严重肾功能衰竭、以及患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。

* 服用本药前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果您是：未满1岁的婴儿、经常晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、高草酸尿症患者（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病的患者、心血管功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、胆囊相关疾病、肝脏疾病、急慢性支气管炎、肺水肿、碱中毒、消化性溃疡、痛风或有肾结石病史者、叶酸缺乏的患者、有血栓（血管阻塞）倾向的患者。

* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

* 在进行各种尿液检查时，本药可能干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。

* 服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
