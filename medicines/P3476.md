# EN
### 💊 가스제로구강필름(시메치콘) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of abdominal pressure, abdominal distension (bloating), and fullness caused by gas in the gastrointestinal tract.

### 🕒 가스제로구강필름(시메치콘) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2-4 sheets per dose, as needed, after meals or before bedtime, by placing them on the tongue and allowing them to dissolve. Do not exceed 8 sheets per day. After dissolving, swallow immediately without holding it in the mouth.
