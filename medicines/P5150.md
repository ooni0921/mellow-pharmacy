#EN
### 💊 바이캅정(탄산수소나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the antacid action and symptom improvement of gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity, as well as for the improvement of acidosis, promotion of uric acid excretion, and prevention of gout attacks.

### 🕒 바이캅정(탄산수소나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets (0.5 to 1 g) per dose, 6 to 10 tablets (3 to 5 g) daily.

Dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 바이캅정(탄산수소나트륨) - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient requiring sodium intake restriction (e.g., hypernatremia, edema, preeclampsia, bladder calculus, hypertension), or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, patients with severe gastrointestinal ulcers, renal impairment, cardiac dysfunction, pulmonary dysfunction, electrolyte imbalance such as hypochloremic alkalosis, congestive heart failure, edema, sodium retention, oliguria, or anuria should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 바이캅정(탄산수소나트륨) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于胃·十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多的制酸作用及症状改善，以及改善酸中毒、促进尿酸排泄和预防痛风发作。

### 🕒 바이캅정(탄산수소나트륨) - 用法用量
* 成人每次服用1~2片(0.5~1 g)，每日6~10片(3~5 g)。

可根据年龄和症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 바이캅정(탄산수소나트륨) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 需要限制钠摄入的患者（如高钠血症、水肿、妊娠中毒症、膀胱结石、高血压患者等），以及有遗传性问题（如半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良）的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，患有严重消化道溃疡、肾功能障碍、心功能障碍、肺功能障碍、低氯性碱中毒等电解质失调、充血性心力衰竭、水肿、钠潴留、少尿症、无尿症的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
