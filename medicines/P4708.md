#EN
### 💊 판덱스정(덱스판테놀) - Efficacy and Effect
* This medicine is used as an adjuvant treatment for hair loss.

### 🕒 판덱스정(덱스판테놀) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults: Take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day.

The treatment period is 6 weeks.

### ⚠️ 판덱스정(덱스판테놀) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are under 19 years of age, are not suffering from alopecia, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant, may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, or have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 6 weeks of taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

Inform your doctor or pharmacist about your recent treatment status to avoid drug interactions.
