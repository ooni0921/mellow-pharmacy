#EN
### 💊 씨큐어리버액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supportive treatment of liver disease.

### 🕒 씨큐어리버액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 10-30 mL daily, divided between meals. Dilute 10 mL of this medication in about half a glass of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 씨큐어리버액 - Precautions
* Do not take this medication if you have kidney disease, myocardial infarction, or a history thereof.
* This medication cannot be used as an antidote for alcohol poisoning.
* If there is no improvement after approximately one month of use, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medication is for oral use only; do not use it for injection.

#CN
### 💊 씨큐어리버액 - 功效与作用
* 本药物用于肝脏疾病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 씨큐어리버액 - 用法用量
* 成人每日服用10~30毫升，分次于两餐之间服用。将本药10毫升稀释于约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 씨큐어리버액 - 注意事项
* 肾脏疾病、心肌梗死及有相关病史者请勿服用本药。
* 本药不能用作酒精中毒时的解毒剂。
* 若服用约一个月后仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 本药仅供内服，不可注射使用。
