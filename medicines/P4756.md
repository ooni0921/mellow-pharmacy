#EN
### 💊 포지넨크림 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the early treatment of recurrent labial herpes (cold sores).

### 🕒 포지넨크림 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should apply an appropriate amount, sufficient to cover the affected area up to its edges, 5 times a day for 5 days.
*   Treatment should be started as soon as possible, immediately upon the appearance of initial symptoms and signs.

### ⚠️ 포지넨크림 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, a patient with bacterial (e.g., tuberculosis, syphilis), fungal (e.g., candidiasis, tinea), or viral (excluding herpes simplex, e.g., herpes zoster, varicella, vaccinia) or parasitic (e.g., scabies, crab louse) skin infections, a patient with severely compromised immune system such as bone marrow transplant recipients or AIDS patients, a patient with eczematous otitis externa with tympanic membrane perforation, a patient with ulcers (excluding Behçet's disease), a patient with second-degree deep or more severe burns/frostbite, a patient with perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, or rosacea, a lactating woman, a child under 18 years of age, or a patient with a history of myocardial infarction.
*   Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and elderly patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not use on eyes, nose, mouth, or genitals.
*   This medicine should not be used for the prevention of infection to others or for the prevention of recurrent herpes simplex (blister) infections.
*   Do not dilute this medicine or use it as a base to mix with other medicines.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Use only for labial herpes on or around the lips.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 5 days of use, discontinue administration immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

*   本品用于复发性唇疱疹的早期治疗。

### 🕒 포지넨크림 - 用法与用量
*   成人每次取适量，足以充分覆盖患处至边缘，每日5次，连续使用5天。
*   治疗应在出现初期症状和体征时尽快开始。

### ⚠️ 포지넨크림 - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   以下患者请勿使用本品：对本品过敏的患者；患有细菌性（如结核、梅毒等）、真菌性（如念珠菌病、癣等）、除单纯疱疹外的病毒性（如带状疱疹、水痘、牛痘等）或动物性（如疥疮、阴虱等）皮肤感染的患者；骨髓移植患者或艾滋病患者等免疫系统严重受损的患者；伴有鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳道炎患者；溃疡患者（贝赫切特病除外）；二度深层以上烧伤或冻伤患者；口周皮炎、寻常痤疮、酒渣鼻患者；哺乳期妇女；未满18周岁的儿童；心肌梗死患者或有既往史者。
*   孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、老年患者请咨询医生或药师。
*   请勿用于眼睛、鼻子、口腔内及生殖器。
*   本品不应用于预防他人感染或预防复发性单纯疱疹（水泡）感染。
*   请勿稀释本药或将其用作与其他药物混合的基质。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   仅用于唇部或唇周的唇疱疹。
*   如使用约5天后症状仍无改善，请立即停止用药并咨询医生或药师。
