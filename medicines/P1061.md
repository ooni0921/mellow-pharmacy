### 💊 Hemorex Cream (Pramoxine HCl) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the temporary relief of discomfort, burning, and itching associated with hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 Hemorex Cream (Pramoxine HCl) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should apply directly to the affected area up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ Hemorex Cream (Pramoxine HCl) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not use this medication if you are hypersensitive to it. Before using this medication, children under 12 years of age, pregnant women, or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. This medication is for anal use only and should not be used ophthalmically.
