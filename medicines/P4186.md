#EN
### 💊 치치엔더블유크림 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 치치엔더블유크림 - Dosage and Administration
*   **For topical application:** Apply directly to the affected area, up to 3 times a day.
*   **For rectal insertion:** Adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older should insert 2 g per dose rectally, up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 치치엔더블유크림 - Precautions
*   Do not use this medication if the affected area is suppurating, or for children.
*   Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of use, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medication is for anal use only and should not be used for ophthalmic purposes.

#CN
### 💊 치치엔더블유크림 - 功效与作用
*   本药物用于暂时缓解肛裂和痔疮引起的疼痛、瘙痒、肿胀（浮肿）和出血。

### 🕒 치치엔더블유크림 - 用法与用量
*   **涂抹时：** 每日3次，直接涂抹于患处。
*   **注入时：** 成人及15岁以上青少年，每次2克，每日3次，通过直肠内插入注入。

### ⚠️ 치치엔더블유크림 - 注意事项
*   患处化脓者和儿童请勿使用本药物。
*   在使用本药物前，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性应咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   若使用约7天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
*   本药物仅限用于肛门部位，不得用于眼科。

*   本剤は肛門部位にのみ使用し、眼科用としては使用しないでください。
