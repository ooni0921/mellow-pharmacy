### 💊 Karol B Cold Soft Capsule - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 Karol B Cold Soft Capsule - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years and older: Take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ Karol B Cold Soft Capsule - Precautions
*   Consult your doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker of three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take this medicine with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it before, if you have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, if you have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or if you are after 30 weeks of pregnancy.

Consult your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, an infant under 2 years old, a toddler, someone with an allergic constitution or a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), someone with liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric/duodenal ulcers, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or difficulty urinating. Also consult if you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, smoking, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough that lasts or recurs for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not use continuously for a long period.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult your doctor or pharmacist.
