#EN
### 💊 알레지엔정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Indications and Usage
*   This medication is used for the temporary relief of symptoms due to hay fever or other upper respiratory allergies, such as runny nose, sneezing, itchy and watery eyes, and itching of the nose or throat.

### 🕒 알레지엔정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents 12 years of age and older should take 1 tablet with water every 12 hours.

Do not take more than 2 tablets within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 알레지엔정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have experienced hypersensitivity to it.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 12 years of age, an elderly person aged 65 or older, someone unsure whether symptoms are due to allergic rhinitis, a cold, or another cause, someone previously diagnosed with other allergic diseases, someone with severe nasal congestion, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, someone who has experienced allergic symptoms to drugs, a patient with renal impairment, or a patient with cardiovascular disease.

Avoid breastfeeding while taking this medication.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.
