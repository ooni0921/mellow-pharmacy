#EN
### 💊 비타메드레모나산 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of Vitamins B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, or physical weakness during or after illness. It also helps alleviate pigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, and prevents gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds).

### 🕒 비타메드레모나산 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 8 years and older, take 1 sachet once daily.

### ⚠️ 비타메드레모나산 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this product, or hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year old, pregnant or potentially pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant/young child, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in urine), gout, or kidney stones.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian or parent.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning the urine yellow.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 1 month, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 비타메드레모나산 - 功效与作用
*   本药品用于缓解体力疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期以及病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时补充维生素B2、B6、C，减轻因日晒、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑），以及预防牙龈出血和鼻出血（流鼻血）。

### 🕒 비타메드레모나산 - 用法与用量
*   8岁及以上成人，每次1袋，每日1次。

### ⚠️ 비타메드레모나산 - 注意事项
*   对本药品过敏的患者，以及患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药品。
*   在服用本药品前，1岁以下婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症患者（尿液中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、痛风患者、肾结石患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药品在进行各种尿液检查时，可能会干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查值。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。
*   如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
