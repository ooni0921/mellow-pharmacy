# EN
### 💊 엘도브론캡슐300밀리그램(에르도스테인) - Efficacy and Effect
* This drug is used for mucolysis and expectoration in acute and chronic respiratory diseases.

### 🕒 엘도브론캡슐300밀리그램(에르도스테인) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) once, 2-3 times a day.

When taken for acute respiratory diseases, do not take continuously for more than 10 days.

* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, liver cirrhosis, cystathionine beta-synthase deficiency, peptic ulcer, severe renal impairment (creatinine clearance... [information incomplete in original])
