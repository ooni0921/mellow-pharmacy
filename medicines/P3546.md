#EN
### 💊 벤포비타100정 - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for the supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, and C when experiencing physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, or when physical strength is diminished during or after illness. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye strain, angular stomatitis (cheilosis), cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis, and for the supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 벤포비타100정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 8 years and older, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 벤포비타100정 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, have experienced allergic reactions to soy milk, are hypersensitive to soy or peanuts, have hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months old, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or genetic problems such as glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, premature infant, infant, a patient with impaired cardiac or circulatory function, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, dyslipidemia such as diabetic hyperlipidemia and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs to be carefully administered lipid emulsions, a patient currently taking cardiotonic glycosides, a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient expected to have a special risk due to fat overload.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medication may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
