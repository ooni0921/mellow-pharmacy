#EN
### 💊 브로아제장용정(브로멜라인) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of inflammatory symptoms accompanied by edema, and for edema after trauma or surgery.

### 🕒 브로아제장용정(브로멜라인) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age and older should take 1 tablet (100 mg) once, twice daily, 30 minutes before meals with water. The mechanism of action in the body is still largely unknown, and the dose-response relationship has not been clarified, so do not take it without a specific purpose.

### ⚠️ 브로아제장용정(브로멜라인) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have severe blood coagulation disorders (e.g., hemophilia), severe liver or kidney disease (e.g., hemodialysis patients), are taking anticoagulants or antiplatelet agents, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are under 12 years of age. Before taking this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity to sunset yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5) or those with a history of such hypersensitivity should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 브로아제장용정(브로멜라인) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于缓解伴有水肿的炎症症状，以及外伤或手术后的水肿。

### 🕒 브로아제장용정(브로멜라인) - 用法及用量
*   成人及12岁以上儿童，一次1片（100毫克），一日2次，饭前30分钟用水送服。本药在体内的作用机制尚有许多未明之处，且剂量与效果的关系也未阐明，因此请勿无目的服用。

### ⚠️ 브로아제장용정(브로멜라인) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   有严重血液凝血异常（如血友病等）、严重肝病或严重肾病（如血液透析患者等）、正在服用抗凝剂或血小板聚集抑制剂的患者、孕妇及哺乳期妇女、以及12岁以下儿童请勿服用本药。服用本药前，对日落黄FCF（黄色5号）有过敏症或有相关经验的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

### 🕒 브로아제장용정(브로멜라인) - 用法用量

### ⚠️ 브로아제장용정(브로멜라인) - 注意事項
*   情報なし
