#EN
### 💊 하드칼씨플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is depleted during or after illness, for vitamin D and C supplementation during growth periods and in old age, for poor development of bones and teeth, and for the prevention of rickets.

### 🕒 하드칼씨플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 8 years of age or older should take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 하드칼씨플러스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excess of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a patient with hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with heart/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, a patient currently taking cardiac glycosides, a patient with gout, or a patient with or a history of kidney stones.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

It may interfere with blood glucose detection during various urine tests.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 1 month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
