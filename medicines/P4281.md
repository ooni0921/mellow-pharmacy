#EN

* This medicine is used for zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 징키스틴캡슐(히스티딘아연이수화물) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule once daily, 1 hour before a meal. If you have difficulty swallowing the capsule, its contents can be mixed with water or fruit juice and taken.

### ⚠️ 징키스틴캡슐(히스티딘아연이수화물) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients hypersensitive to this medicine should not take it. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
