* This medicine is used for sterilization and disinfection of scratches, cuts, and wound surfaces.

### 🕒 솔트스틱액 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area once to several times a day.

### ⚠️ 솔트스틱액 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (such as rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.) to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, or have a widespread and severe wound or severe burn. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. If it gets into the eyes, wash immediately with cold water and consult a doctor. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. This medicine is for external use only; do not ingest. If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
