# EN
### 💊 케펨플라스타(케토프로펜) - Indications & Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for the analgesic and anti-inflammatory treatment of degenerative osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, epicondylitis of the humerus (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia (muscle pain), and post-traumatic swelling/pain.

### 🕒 케펨플라스타(케토프로펜) - Dosage & Administration
*   Remove the release liner from the patch and apply it to the affected area once a day.

### ⚠️ 케펨플라스타(케토프로펜) - Warnings & Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity or photosensitivity to this medicine, a history of aspirin asthma (asthma attack caused by NSAIDs), hypersensitivity to ingredients that can cause cross-hypersensitivity reactions (tiaprofenic acid, fenofibrate, bezafibrate, ciprofibrate, oxybenzone), are pregnant for 6 months or more, are a child under 15 years old, or on the eyes, mucous membranes, tinea pedis (athlete's foot), or tinea corporis (ringworm).
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have bronchial asthma, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or plan to use it on damaged skin, eczema, or rash areas.
*   During use and for up to 2 weeks after use, regardless of weather, avoid outdoor activities. When going out daily, cover the application area of this medicine with clothes, sunglasses, hats, sunscreen, etc., to avoid exposure to ultraviolet light.
*   Do not use occlusive dressing.
*   If symptoms do not improve after about one week of use, immediately stop use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 케펨플라스타(케토프로펜) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于治疗退行性关节炎（骨关节炎）、肩周炎、腱炎·腱鞘炎、腱周炎、肱骨上髁炎（如：网球肘）、肌肉痛、外伤后的肿胀·疼痛的镇痛·消炎。

### 🕒 케펨플라스타(케토프로펜) - 用法与用量
*   撕下药面的剥离纸后，每日1次贴于患部（患病部位）。

### ⚠️ 케펨플라스타(케토프로펜) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药有过敏症及光过敏症患者、阿司匹林哮喘（非甾体抗炎镇痛药等引起的哮喘发作）或有经验者、对可能引起交叉过敏反应的成分（硫普罗芬酸、非诺贝特、苯扎贝特、西普贝特、氧苯酮）过敏的患者、怀孕6个月以上的孕妇、15岁以下的儿童、以及眼睛、粘膜、脚癣、体癣部位，请勿使用本药。
*   在使用本药之前，支气管哮喘患者、孕妇或哺乳期妇女、皮肤受损、湿疹、皮疹部位的使用，请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   在使用本药期间及使用后2周内，无论天气如何，请避免户外活动，日常外出时，请用衣服、太阳镜、帽子、防晒霜等遮盖本药涂抹部位，以免暴露于紫外线。
*   请勿使用密封敷料法。
*   使用约1周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药剂师。
