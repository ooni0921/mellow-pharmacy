#EN
### 💊 팜편안디정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion (food stagnation), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and abdominal bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 팜편안디정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 팜편안디정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption; if you are pregnant or may be pregnant; if you are breastfeeding; or if you are under 15 years of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an allergy patient, a patient with or who has experienced hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 4 (Tartrazine), a patient with kidney or liver impairment, a patient with high blood pressure or an elderly person, a patient with heart or kidney impairment, or a patient with edema.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
