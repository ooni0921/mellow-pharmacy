#EN
### 💊 Hemofomin Q Capsule - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia.

### 🕒 Hemofomin Q Capsule - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule once daily.

### ⚠️ Hemofomin Q Capsule - Precautions
* Keep out of reach of children, as accidental overdose of iron-containing products is a leading cause of fatal poisoning in children under 6.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months, or have hereditary problems such as hemochromatosis, hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis; heart or circulatory system dysfunction; kidney impairment; hypoproteinemia; are pregnant; or are breastfeeding.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration instructions carefully.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of use, discontinue the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 Hemofomin Q Capsule - 功效及效果
* 本药用于预防和治疗缺铁性贫血。

### 🕒 Hemofomin Q Capsule - 用法及用量
* 成人每日服用1次，每次1粒胶囊。

### ⚠️ Hemofomin Q Capsule - 注意事项
* 6岁以下儿童过量服用可能导致铁中毒性死亡，请将本药放置在儿童触及不到的地方。
* 对本药过敏的患者、12个月以下的婴儿、血色素沉着症、血铁黄素沉着症、非缺铁性贫血、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，患有消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性肠炎等胃肠道疾病、心血管功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、孕妇、哺乳期妇女应咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

* 約1ヶ月服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
