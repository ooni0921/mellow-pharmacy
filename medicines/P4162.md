* 이 약에 과민증 환자, 다른 해열진통제 또는 감기약 복용 시에 천식 경험자, 만3개월 미만의 영아(갓난아기)는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 마더스코프연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 마더스코프연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older and adults, take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 마더스코프연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause liver damage.

*   Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

*   Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

*   If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, or are an infant under 3 months of age.

*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, a child under 2 years of age, a person experiencing symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding), a person with an allergic constitution, a person who has experienced allergic reactions (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), a person with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastric/duodenal ulcer, an elderly person, a person with a weakened body or high fever, a person with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or ulcers or bleeding problems, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a smoker, a person with asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   如果出现急性全身发疹性脓疱病等严重皮肤不良反应，请立即停药并咨询医生或药师。
