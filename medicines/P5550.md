#EN
### 💊 코드비타연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during illness or recovery, supplementation of Vitamins A, B1, B2 in old age, relief of dry eyes, nyctalopia (difficulty seeing at night), neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), relief of angular cheilitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (oral inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, and for beriberi and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 코드비타연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years and older: Take 2 capsules twice daily.

### ⚠️ 코드비타연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A daily may cause congenital malformations. Do not take if you are within 3 months of pregnancy or if there is a possibility of pregnancy.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, an infant under 3 months old, have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to soybean oil, or are hypersensitive to soy or peanuts.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year old, pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient who needs to carefully administer lipid emulsions, or if you have experienced hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Red No. 40.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   Since Vitamin A contained in this medicine is sufficiently supplied through a normal diet, be careful not to exceed 5,000 IU per day for supplementary therapy.
*   May cause urine to turn yellow and affect clinical test results.

#CN

### 🕒 코드비타연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   8岁以上儿童及成人，每日2次，每次2粒胶囊。

*   每日服用维生素 A 超过 5,000 IU 可能导致先天性畸形。妊娠3个月内或可能怀孕的女性请勿服用。
*   对本药品过敏者、未满3个月的婴儿、有大豆油过敏史或过敏反应者、对大豆或花生过敏的患者请勿服用本药品。
*   服用本药品前，以下人群请咨询医生或药剂师：未满1岁的婴儿、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者或需要谨慎给予脂质乳剂的患者、对红色40号有超敏反应或过敏史者。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药品中含有的维生素 A 可通过正常饮食充分摄取，因此作为辅助疗法时，请注意每日用量不要超过 5,000 IU。
*   可能导致尿液变黄，影响临床检查结果。
