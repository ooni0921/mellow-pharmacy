#EN
### 💊 엘칸액(엘-카르니틴) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and myocardial metabolic disorders caused by ischemic heart diseases such as angina pectoris and acute myocardial infarction.

### 🕒 엘칸액(엘-카르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 2-3 g daily, divided into 2-3 doses. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘칸액(엘-카르니틴) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients hypersensitive to this medicine should not take it. Pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine. Dilute before oral administration.

#JP
