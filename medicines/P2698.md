#EN
### 💊 비타라민정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamin B6 during periods of pregnancy and lactation, or physical exhaustion during or after illness (during illness or recovery), and for the alleviation of symptoms such as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 비타라민정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 8 years of age or older: take 1 tablet per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 비타라민정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age or have a folic acid deficiency.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
