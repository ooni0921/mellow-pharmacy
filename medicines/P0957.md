# EN
### 💊 타스나정(탄산수소나트륨) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the antacid action and improvement of symptoms in gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity, improvement of acidosis, promotion of uric acid excretion, and prevention of gout attacks.

### 🕒 타스나정(탄산수소나트륨) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 to 2 tablets (0.5 to 1 g) per dose, with a total daily dose of 3 to 5 g. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 타스나정(탄산수소나트륨) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients requiring sodium intake restriction (e.g., hypernatremia, edema, preeclampsia, bladder calculus, hypertensive patients) should not take this medication. Before taking this medication, patients with severe gastrointestinal ulcers, renal impairment, cardiac dysfunction, pulmonary dysfunction, electrolyte imbalance such as hypochloremic alkalosis, congestive heart failure, edema, or sodium retention, as well as oliguria or anuria, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 타스나정(탄산수소나트륨) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于胃十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多的制酸作用及症状改善，酸中毒的改善，促进尿酸排泄和预防痛风发作。

### 🕒 타스나정(탄산수소나트륨) - 用法用量
*   成人一次服用1~2片(0.5~1 g)，每日3~5 g。根据年龄、症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 타스나정(탄산수소나트륨) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   需要限制钠摄入的患者（如高钠血症、水肿、妊娠中毒症、膀胱结石、高血压患者等）请勿服用本药。服用本药前，患有严重消化道溃疡、肾功能障碍、心功能障碍、肺功能障碍、低氯性碱中毒等电解质紊乱患者、充血性心力衰竭、水肿或钠潴留以及少尿或无尿患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
