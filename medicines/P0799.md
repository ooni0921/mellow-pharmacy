#EN
### 💊 가레오액(디히드록시디부틸에테르) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the improvement of dyspeptic symptoms (abdominal bloating, delayed digestion, belching, nausea).

### 🕒 가레오액(디히드록시디부틸에테르) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 0.5~1.0 g per dose, 1~3 times a day, before meals.

### ⚠️ 가레오액(디히드록시디부틸에테르) - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, glaucoma, urinary tract obstruction, prostate obstruction, biliary tract obstruction, cholelithiasis, or acute/chronic liver disease. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of use, immediately discontinue taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
