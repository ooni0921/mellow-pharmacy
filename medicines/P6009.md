* This medicine is used for neuralgia, back pain, and stiff shoulders.

### 🕒 제놀담푸러정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 tablets at a time, twice a day, when experiencing pain or an attack.

### ⚠️ 제놀담푸러정 - Precautions
* No information
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due due to medicine, are an infant, toddler, or child (under 14 years old) infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, are a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, an elderly person, a person with a weak constitution or high fever, or a person with liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, etc.

If symptoms do not improve after several doses, immediately stop taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
