#EN
### 💊 알레톡정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the temporary relief of symptoms such as runny nose, sneezing, itchy and watery eyes, and itching of the nose or throat caused by hay fever or other upper respiratory allergies.

### 🕒 알레톡정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 12 years of age and older should take 1 tablet with water every 12 hours.

Do not take more than 2 tablets within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 알레톡정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or are breastfeeding.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 12 years of age, a patient with kidney impairment, an elderly person (65 years or older), someone who does not know if their symptoms are caused by allergic rhinitis, a cold, or other causes, someone with a history of allergic disease diagnosis (such as bronchial asthma, atopic dermatitis), someone with severe nasal congestion, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, someone with a history of allergies to medicines, someone with kidney impairment (renal impairment), or someone with cardiovascular disease.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN

* 本药用于暂时缓解由花粉过敏或其他上呼吸道过敏引起的流鼻涕、打喷嚏、眼睛瘙痒和流泪、鼻子或喉咙发痒等症状。

### 🕒 알레톡정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - 用法用量
* 成人及12岁以上青少年，每次1片，每隔12小时用水服用。

24小时内服用不得超过2片。

### ⚠️ 알레톡정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏者、哺乳期妇女请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，12岁以下儿童、肾功能障碍患者、65岁以上老年人、不确定症状是否由过敏性鼻炎或感冒等其他原因引起者、有过敏性疾病诊断史（如支气管哮喘、特应性皮炎等）者、鼻塞症状严重者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、有药物过敏史者、肾功能障碍（肾损伤）患者、心血管疾病患者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
