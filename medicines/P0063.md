#EN
### 💊 마스질정(클레마스틴푸마르산염) - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for allergic rhinitis, hay fever, urticaria, pruritic skin conditions (pruritus, eczema, dermatitis), angioneurotic edema, and sneezing, runny nose, and cough due to the common cold.

### 🕒 마스질정(클레마스틴푸마르산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 tablet (1 mg) twice daily, in the morning and evening. Do not exceed 2 tablets (2 mg) per day. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 마스질정(클레마스틴푸마르산염) - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a nursing mother, premature infant or infant under 1 year of age, have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine or other antihistamines, have lower respiratory tract diseases such as asthma attacks, are currently taking MAO inhibitors, have lower urinary tract obstructive diseases such as prostatic hypertrophy, have glaucoma, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have stenosing peptic ulcer or pyloroduodenal obstruction, hypertension, a history of bronchial asthma, hyperthyroidism, cardiovascular disease, polyuria, convulsive disorders such as epilepsy or a history thereof, tartrazine (Yellow No. 4) hypersensitivity, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are a nursing mother.
*   Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

*   成人每次服用1片（1毫克），每日2次，早晚各一次。每日请勿超过2片（2毫克）。可根据年龄和症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 마스질정(클레마스틴푸마르산염) - 注意事项
*   哺乳期妇女、早产儿及1岁以下婴儿、对本品或其他抗组胺药有过敏史者、患有哮喘发作等下呼吸道疾病者、正在服用MAO抑制剂者、患有前列腺肥大等下尿路阻塞性疾病者、青光眼患者、以及患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本品。
