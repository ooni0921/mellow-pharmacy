# EN
### 💊 뮤코반캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for difficulty in expectoration in acute and chronic bronchitis, bronchial asthma, laryngitis, sinusitis, and cystic fibrosis.

### 🕒 뮤코반캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - Dosage and Administration
* For acute conditions, adults take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, three times daily. Children aged 6-14 years take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, twice daily, before meals with a small amount of water.
* For chronic conditions, adults take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, twice daily. Children aged 6-14 years take 1/2 capsule (100 mg) once, three times daily, before meals with a small amount of water.
* For cystic fibrosis, individuals aged 6 years and older take 1 capsule (200 mg) once, three times daily, before meals with a small amount of water.

### ⚠️ 뮤코반캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this drug, gastric or duodenal ulcer, infants under 2 years of age, or patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.
* Pregnant or possibly pregnant women, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

# CN
### 💊 뮤코반캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于急慢性支气管炎、支气管哮喘、喉炎、鼻窦炎、囊性纤维化引起的痰液排出困难。

### 🕒 뮤코반캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - 用法用量
* 急性疾病：成人每次1粒（200毫克），每日3次；6~14岁儿童每次1粒（200毫克），每日2次，饭前与少量水一同服用。
* 慢性疾病：成人每次1粒（200毫克），每日2次；6~14岁儿童每次1/2粒（100毫克），每日3次，饭前与少量水一同服用。
* 囊性纤维化：6岁及以上每次1粒（200毫克），每日3次，饭前与少量水一同服用。

### ⚠️ 뮤코반캡슐200mg(아세틸시스테인) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏者、胃/十二指肠溃疡患者、2岁以下婴儿、以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性问题的患者请勿服用本药。
* 孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女在服用本药前应咨询医生或药师。
