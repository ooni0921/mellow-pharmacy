#EN
### 💊 솔루아펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by cold, headache, neuralgia, myalgia, dysmenorrhea, sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 솔루아펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 12 years and older and adults take 2 capsules (650 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day (every 4 to 6 hours) as needed. Do not exceed 12 capsules (4,000 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 솔루아펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, aspirin asthma (induced asthmatic attacks by NSAIDs) or a history thereof, have taken barbiturates or tricyclic antidepressants, or have consumed alcohol. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. If pain or fever symptoms persist or worsen, or if new symptoms appear, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 💊 솔루아펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、扭伤痛、牙痛、关节痛、风湿性疼痛。

### 🕒 솔루아펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 用法与用量
*   12岁以上儿童及成人，每次2粒(650 mg)，每日3~4次(每4~6小时一次)，必要时服用。每日最大服用量不得超过12粒(4,000 mg)。

### ⚠️ 솔루아펜연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上且正在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药的患者，请咨询医生或药师。本药可能引起肝损伤。极罕见情况下，曾报告过可致命的急性全身发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症等严重皮肤反应。服用本药后如出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。醋氨酚每日最大剂量不得超过4,000 mg。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与其他含醋氨酚的产品同时服用。
*   对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡、严重血液病、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全患者、阿司匹林哮喘(由非甾体抗炎药诱发的哮喘发作)或有此病史者、服用巴比妥类药物或三环类抗抑郁药的患者、饮酒者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、消化性溃疡、血液病、出血倾向、心功能异常患者、有过敏史者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。如果疼痛或发热症状持续或恶化，或出现新症状，请咨询医生或药师。
