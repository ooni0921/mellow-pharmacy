*   This medication is used for the pain relief and anti-inflammatory treatment of bruises, sprains, lower back pain, stiff shoulders, muscle pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 맨담듀오임팩카타플라스마 - Dosage and Administration
*   Remove the release liner from the medication surface and apply to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 맨담듀오임팩카타플라스마 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not use this medication on infants under 30 months of age, on skin affected by eczema, contact dermatitis (e.g., from lacquer trees), or on open wounds.
*   Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms to medications or cosmetics (e.g., rash/redness, itching, lacquer allergy), have an allergic constitution, suffer from severe moisture retention or maceration, are a child, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a breastfeeding woman.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Avoid prolonged use over large skin areas, as symptoms of poisoning may occur if a large amount is absorbed through the skin.
*   This medication is for external use only.
*   Do not warm the affected area with heating pads, electric blankets, or similar devices.
