### 💊 조인본콘드로800정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins B1, B2, B6, and D in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or recovery from illness when physical strength is reduced, during growth periods, and in old age.
*   This medicine is used to relieve joint pain symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, back pain, or stiff shoulders through the vitamins it contains.
*   This medicine is used to prevent beriberi, eye strain, poor bone and tooth development, and rickets through the vitamins it contains.

### 🕒 조인본콘드로800정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 19 years and older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 조인본콘드로800정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, an infant under 12 months, a patient with hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, severe renal insufficiency, or kidney stones.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, a breastfeeding mother, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient with renal impairment, or a patient with a history of kidney stones.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
