#EN
### 💊 글루타에스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite, overeating, upset stomach (food stagnation), promoting digestion, and stomach bloating caused by indigestion.

### 🕒 글루타에스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 15 years of age and older: Take 2 tablets at a time, 3 times a day, regardless of meals.

Maintain a dosing interval of at least 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 글루타에스정 - Precautions
*   No specific information provided.
*   Do not administer this medicine to children 7 years of age and under.

Individuals with an allergic predisposition should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue taking this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medicine under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
