# EN

### 💊 Norumo Hyun-Tak-Aek - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the antacid effect and improvement of symptoms associated with gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity.

### 🕒 Norumo Hyun-Tak-Aek - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults, take 1 sachet (1.212 g) per dose, 3 times daily between meals, orally. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ Norumo Hyun-Tak-Aek - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients undergoing dialysis therapy or those with phosphate deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
