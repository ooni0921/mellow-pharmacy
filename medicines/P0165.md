#EN
### 💊 하이플포비돈요오드액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This product is used for antiseptic disinfection of lacerations, burns, wounds, pressure ulcers, ulcers, abscesses, disinfection of infected skin areas, antiseptic disinfection of surgical sites, and disinfection of injection and catheter sites.

### 🕒 하이플포비돈요오드액 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 하이플포비돈요오드액 - Precautions
* No information provided
* Do not use this product if you are hypersensitive to this drug, have thyroid dysfunction (especially nodular goiter, endemic goiter, Hashimoto's thyroiditis, etc.), renal failure, are a neonate or infant under 6 months of age, have herpetiform dermatitis, or are a patient before or after radioactive iodine therapy.
* Before using this product, patients with severe conditions (e.g., burn patients), a history of thyroid disease, a history of renal failure, pregnant women, or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not use for ophthalmic or internal use.
* Be careful not to get it in your eyes; if it gets into your eyes, rinse immediately with clean water.
* Do not mix with other preparations.
* Clean and disinfect the affected area.
* For severe wounds, use sterile distilled water for injection or sterile purified water as a diluent; do not use tap water or purified water.
* Soaps may weaken the bactericidal action of this product, so wash them off thoroughly before use.
