#EN
### 💊 콜콜코프캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 콜콜코프캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 콜콜코프캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug; if you are pregnant or may be pregnant; if you are an infant under 3 months of age; if you are currently taking or have stopped taking MAO inhibitors (such as antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) within the last 2 weeks; or if you have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5; if you are an infant or toddler aged 3 months to under 2 years; if you have an allergic predisposition or a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, itching, etc.); if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or dysuria; if you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; if you are breastfeeding; if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema; if you have a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine; exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

#CN
### 💊 콜콜코프캡슐 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于治疗咳嗽、咳痰。

### 🕒 콜콜코프캡슐 - 用法与用量
* 成人每次2粒，每日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 콜콜코프캡슐 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性，未满3个月的婴儿，正在服用MAO抑制剂（如抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）或停用MAO抑制剂后2周内的人，以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，若对黄色5号过敏者，3个月以上未满2岁的婴幼儿，易过敏体质者，有皮疹、发热、关节痛、瘙痒等过敏症状史者，肝病、肾病、心脏病、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、高血压、青光眼（如眼痛、视力模糊等）、排尿困难者，高龄者、体弱者或高热者，哺乳期妇女，吸烟者，患有哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿者，伴有过多痰液的咳嗽，持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽，慢性咳嗽，伴有发热、皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。
* 服用5-6次后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。
* 服用后可能会出现困倦，驾驶或操作机器时请注意。
