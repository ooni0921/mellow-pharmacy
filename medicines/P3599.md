# EN
### 💊 세다큐콜드연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 세다큐콜드연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and individuals aged 15 years or older, take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 세다큐콜드연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Individuals who regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take this medication with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medication, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe and uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: infants and children under 15 years old who are infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; infants under 2 years old; toddlers; individuals with an allergic constitution; individuals with a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); individuals with liver, kidney, heart, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty urinating; elderly individuals; frail individuals or those with high fever; individuals with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or those with ulcers or bleeding problems; pregnant women or women who may be pregnant; breastfeeding women; individuals with smoking habits, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not use continuously for a prolonged period.

Do not consume alcohol during medication.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
