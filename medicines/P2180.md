#EN
### 💊 메디파프쿨카타플라스마(펠비낙) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for analgesia and anti-inflammation (anti-inflammatory) for degenerative osteoarthritis, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, humeral epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), myalgia (muscle pain), and post-traumatic swelling and pain.

### 🕒 메디파프쿨카타플라스마(펠비낙) - Dosage and Administration
* After peeling off the release liner from the patch surface, apply one patch to the affected area twice a day.

### ⚠️ 메디파프쿨카타플라스마(펠비낙) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, have experienced or had asthma attacks induced by aspirin or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), have experienced urticaria or acute rhinitis induced by aspirin or NSAIDs, or if you are pregnant, possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding. Patients with bronchial asthma and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not use on damaged skin or mucous membranes, eczema, or rash areas.

#CN
### 💊 메디파프쿨카타플라스마(펠비낙) - 功效与作用
* 本品用于退行性关节炎（骨关节炎）、肩周炎、腱炎/腱鞘炎、腱周炎、肱骨外上髁炎（如网球肘）、肌肉痛、外伤后肿胀和疼痛的镇痛、消炎（抗炎）治疗。

### 🕒 메디파프쿨카타플라스마(펠비낙) - 用法与用量
* 揭去药面上的离型纸后，每次1贴，每日2次，贴于患处。

### ⚠️ 메디파프쿨카타플라스마(펠비낙) - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 对本品过敏的患者，曾因阿司匹林或非甾体抗炎药等引起哮喘发作的患者，曾因阿司匹林或非甾体抗炎药等引起荨麻疹或急性鼻炎的患者，孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女请勿使用本品。支气管哮喘患者、儿童请咨询医生或药师。请勿用于受损皮肤、粘膜、湿疹或皮疹部位。
