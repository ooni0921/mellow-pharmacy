* 만 12세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN

### 💊 멀티브큐골드정 - Efficacy & Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical debility during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It is also used for the alleviation of dry eyes, night blindness, prevention of underdeveloped bones/teeth and rickets, alleviation of hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sun exposure or skin diseases, prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds), prevention and treatment of iron deficiency anemia, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 멀티브큐골드정 - Dosage & Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 멀티브큐골드정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium levels in the blood are excessively high), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hyperkalemia, hemochromatosis (a disorder of iron metabolism where iron is deposited in the liver and pancreas), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

*   Consult your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and has a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding mother, a premature infant, an infant (young child), have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, cardiac or circulatory dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, alkalosis, gout, a thrombotic predisposition, folic acid deficiency, or a history of kidney stones.

*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision and guidance of a guardian.

*   This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test values.

*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 💊 멀티브큐골드정 - 功效与效果
*   本药用于缓解体力疲劳、妊娠期、哺乳期、病中或病后体力下降、发育期、老年期的维生素 A、D、E、B1、B2、B6、C 的补充，以及缓解眼睛干涩、夜盲症（夜间视力不佳的症状）、骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病的预防，缓解因日晒、皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑），预防牙龈出血、鼻出血（流鼻血），预防及治疗缺铁性贫血，以及补充锌。

### 🕒 멀티브큐골드정 - 用法与用量
*   12岁以上儿童及成人：每日一次，每次1片。

### ⚠️ 멀티브큐골드정 - 注意事项
*   以下患者禁止服用本药：对本药过敏者、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态）患者、结节病（病因不明的全身性炎症疾病）、肾脏疾病、未满12个月的婴儿、高钾血症患者、血色病（铁代谢异常导致铁在肝脏、胰脏中沉积的疾病）、含铁血黄素沉着症、非缺铁性贫血、肾结石、严重肾功能不全、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者。

*   服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师，如果您是：多晒太阳且饮食正常的儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性及哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病、心脏或循环系统功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、碱中毒、痛风、有血栓形成倾向的患者、叶酸缺乏症患者、有肾结石病史者。

*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

*   儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。

*   进行各种尿液检查时，本药可能会干扰血糖检测，并可能使尿液呈黄色，从而影响临床检查值。

*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。

*   12歳以上および成人は、1回1錠、1日1回服用してください。

*   定められた用法・用量を守ってください。

*   約1ヶ月間服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。
