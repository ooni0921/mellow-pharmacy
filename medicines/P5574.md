#EN
### 💊 속펜하겐정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion (gastric dyspepsia), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and abdominal bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 속펜하겐정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, between meals.

### ⚠️ 속펜하겐정 - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a patient undergoing dialysis, a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, a child (under 15 years of age), a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a breastfeeding woman.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of allergic reactions (e.g., rash, redness, itching), are currently taking other medications, have kidney impairment, liver impairment, are under medical treatment, have phosphate deficiency, have high blood pressure or are elderly, have heart or kidney disorders, have edema (swelling), or have a history of kidney disease.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, discontinue use and consult a pharmacist or doctor.
