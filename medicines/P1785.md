#EN
### 💊 터나빈겔(테르비나핀) - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used for cutaneous fungal infections caused by dermatophytes (tinea pedis, tinea cruris, tinea corporis) and pityriasis versicolor.

### 🕒 터나빈겔(테르비나핀) - Dosage and Administration
*   Before application, wash and dry the affected area thoroughly, then apply a thin layer to the infected area and its surroundings once daily. For intertriginous infections (under the breasts, between fingers, between toes, between buttocks, groin area), the product may also be used covered with gauze at night. Symptom relief usually appears within 2-3 days, but the usual treatment period is 1 week.

### ⚠️ 터나빈겔(테르비나핀) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to it. Before using this medication, patients with or with a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist. This medication is for external use only. Do not use around the eyes.

#CN
### 💊 터나빈겔(테르비나핀) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于由皮肤癣菌引起的皮肤真菌感染（足癣、股癣、体癣）和花斑糠疹。

### 🕒 터나빈겔(테르비나핀) - 用法与用量
*   使用前，请彻底清洗并擦干患处，然后每日一次，将薄层药膏涂抹于感染区域及周围。对于摩擦部位感染（如乳房下、手指间、脚趾间、臀部间、腹股沟），夜间可覆盖纱布使用。症状通常在2-3天内缓解，但常规治疗周期为1周。

### ⚠️ 터나빈겔(테르비나핀) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者请勿使用本药。使用本药前，对丙二醇过敏或有过敏史的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、儿童应咨询医生或药剂师。本药仅供外用。请勿用于眼睛周围。
