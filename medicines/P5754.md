#EN
### 💊 티엔코드아이연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamins A, B1, B2 in cases of physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (back pain, stiff shoulders, etc.), angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corner of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, relief of dry eye symptoms, night blindness, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 티엔코드아이연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years and older, take 2 capsules once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 티엔코드아이연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day may cause congenital malformations. Women who are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or who may be pregnant should not take this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, an infant under 3 months old, have a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to soybean oil, or are hypersensitive to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year old, pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, premature infant, young child, have hyperlipidemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis, or other fat metabolism disorders, or if you need to carefully administer lipid emulsions, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to Red No. 40.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about one month, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may affect clinical test results by turning urine yellow.

The Vitamin A contained in this medicine is sufficiently supplied through a normal diet, so the dose for supplemental therapy should not exceed 5,000 IU per day.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
