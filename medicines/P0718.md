#EN
### 💊 류마스탑플라스타(디클로페낙디에틸아민)(수출용) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for osteoarthritis deformans, periarthritis of the shoulder, tendinitis/tenosynovitis, peritendinitis, humerus epicondylitis (e.g., tennis elbow), post-traumatic swelling/pain (e.g., contusion, strain, sprain), and muscle pain.

### 🕒 류마스탑플라스타(디클로페낙디에틸아민)(수출용) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ 류마스탑플라스타(디클로페낙디에틸아민)(수출용) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not use this medication in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, patients with or a history of aspirin asthma (induction of asthma attacks by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, etc.), or during the third trimester of pregnancy.

Before using this medication, pregnant women (first and second trimesters), women having difficulty conceiving or undergoing infertility testing, women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, children, the elderly, and patients with bronchial asthma should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not use on lesioned skin (e.g., exudative dermatitis, eczema, infected lesions, burn wounds), eyes, or mucous membranes.
