# EN
### 💊 클리마일드연고 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for eczema, prickly heat (heat rash), skin itching, allergic skin diseases, burns, and frostbite.

### 🕒 클리마일드연고 - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area (diseased part) 2-3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 클리마일드연고 - Precautions
*   Do not use on areas affected by chickenpox, athlete's foot, ringworm, or suppuration, on mucous membranes such as the conjunctiva, or on large areas of the face.
*   Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergies to medications or cosmetics, if the affected area is extensive, wet, or severely festering, or if you are pregnant or may be pregnant.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medication is for external use only and should not be taken internally.
