# EN
### 💊 나리스타에스점비액(수출명:듀라존점비액,나소존점비액) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and head heaviness caused by acute rhinitis, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 나리스타에스점비액(수출명:듀라존점비액,나소존점비액) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 7 years or older should spray 1 to 2 times into each nostril per dose. Maintain a dosing interval of at least 3 hours, and do not exceed 6 doses per day.

### ⚠️ 나리스타에스점비액(수출명:듀라존점비액,나소존점비액) - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, are an infant under 7 years of age, or are taking (using) or have stopped taking (using) MAO inhibitors (such as antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) within the past 2 weeks. Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, are pregnant or may be pregnant, have hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, glaucoma, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Use this medicine only as a nasal spray for rhinitis; do not use it in the eyes or ears. Do not use continuously for more than 7 days. Drowsiness may occur after use, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery. When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

# CN

* 此药用于缓解因急性鼻炎、过敏性鼻炎或鼻窦炎引起的鼻塞、流鼻涕、打喷嚏、头重感。

### 🕒 나리스타에스점비액(수출명:듀라존점비액,나소존점비액) - 用法与用量
* 成人及7岁以上儿童，每次向鼻腔内喷雾1至2次。给药间隔应至少3小时，每日不超过6次。

### ⚠️ 나리스타에스점비액(수출명:듀라존점비액,나소존점비액) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者、未满7岁的幼儿以及正在服用（使用）或停用MAO抑制剂（如抗抑郁药、抗精神病药、情绪调节剂、抗帕金森病药等）后2周内的人群请勿使用本药。在使用本药之前，有过敏体质、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、高血压、心脏病、糖尿病、甲状腺疾病、青光眼、吸烟、哮喘、慢性支气管炎、肺气肿、伴有过多痰的咳嗽、持续1周以上或反复发作的咳嗽、慢性咳嗽、伴有发热·皮疹或持续性头痛的咳嗽患者，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药仅限用作鼻炎喷雾剂，请勿用于眼睛和耳朵。请勿连续使用7天以上。使用后可能会出现嗜睡，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
