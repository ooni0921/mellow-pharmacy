#EN

### 💊 모물린키드크림 - Efficacy and Effect
* This medication is used for itching, dermatitis, eczema, hives, prickly heat, frostbite, insect bites, and chafing.

### 🕒 모물린키드크림 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 모물린키드크림 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication in infants under 1 month of age. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, hypersensitivity to medicines or cosmetics, severe wetness or inflammation, or if you are an infant or child under 6 years of age. Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. This medication is for external use only; do not take internally. Be careful not to let it get into your eyes, and if it does, rinse immediately with water.
