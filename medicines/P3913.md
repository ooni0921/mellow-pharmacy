#EN

### 💊 우루리버정100mg(우르소데옥시콜산) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used as an adjunctive treatment for liver diseases caused by insufficient bile secretion, biliary tract (bile duct, gallbladder) system diseases, improvement of liver function in chronic liver diseases, and indigestion in post-small bowel resection sequelae and inflammatory small bowel diseases.

### 🕒 우루리버정100mg(우르소데옥시콜산) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 0.5 to 1 tablet (50 to 100 mg) once, three times a day.

### ⚠️ 우루리버정100mg(우르소데옥시콜산) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, severe biliary obstruction, fulminant hepatitis, radiopaque calcified gallstones, non-functional gallbladder, acute cholecystitis, are pregnant or potentially pregnant or breastfeeding, are a child, have kidney disease, peptic ulcers (acute gastric or duodenal ulcers), large and small bowel inflammation such as Crohn's disease, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, patients with severe pancreatic disease, cholelithiasis, variceal bleeding, hepatic coma, ascites, or those requiring a liver transplant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
