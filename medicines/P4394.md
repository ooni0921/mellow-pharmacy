### 💊 로나콜연질캡슐 - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve the various symptoms of cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 로나콜연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older and adults, take 2 capsules once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals. For individuals aged 8 years and older but under 15 years, take 1 capsule once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 로나콜연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

    Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis (Lyell's syndrome), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity or a history of it, have experienced asthma when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months old, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old who is infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) appear in relation to pseudoephedrine; if you have an allergic constitution or a history of allergic reactions to medicines (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty urinating; if you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding; or if you are a child under 2 years old.

    Before taking this medicine, patients with cough (cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm; cough lasting or recurring for more than one week; chronic cough; cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

    When used in children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
