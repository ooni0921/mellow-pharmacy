#EN
### 💊 미안블루점안액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for eye fatigue, conjunctival hyperemia, discomfort in the eyes after swimming or when dust or sweat enters the eyes, inflammation of the eyes caused by ultraviolet rays and other light, irritation of the eyelids, discomfort when wearing hard contact lenses, itchy eyes, blurred vision (e.g., when there is a lot of eye discharge).

### 🕒 미안블루점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops at a time, 5-6 times daily.

### ⚠️ 미안블루점안액 - Precautions
* No information
* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe eye pain, have experienced allergic symptoms due to eye drops (e.g., eye hyperemia, itching, swelling, rash, redness due to congestion, etc.), or have glaucoma. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Use this medication only for ophthalmic instillation. Do not use as a solution for soft contact lenses or while wearing soft contact lenses. Do not allow the tip of the container to touch the eyelids or eyelashes, and do not use if the solution is turbid. Do not use with others. If symptoms do not improve after several days of use, stop use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 无信息
* 在使用本药物之前，有严重眼痛者、有过眼药水过敏症状（例：眼睛充血、瘙痒、肿胀、皮疹、充血发红等）者、青光眼患者请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。本药物仅供滴眼用。请勿用作软性隐形眼镜的润湿液或在佩戴软性隐形眼镜时使用。请勿让容器尖端接触眼睑、睫毛，浑浊的液体请勿使用。请勿与他人共用。使用数日后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
