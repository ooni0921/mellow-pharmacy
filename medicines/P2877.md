아세트아미노펜으로 일일 최대 용량(4,000 mg)을 초과하여 복용하지 마십시오. 간손상을 일으킬 수 있습니다. 아세트아미노펜을 포함하는 다른 제품과 함께 복용하지 마십시오.

급성전신발진고름물집증과 같은 중증 피부 이상반응이 나타나는 경우 복용을 중단하고 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN

### 💊 비비코에스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 비비코에스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older: Take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 비비코에스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

    Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. Discontinue use immediately if skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medicine.

    Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine or have a history of it, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or are pregnant beyond 30 weeks.

    Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are a child under 2 years of age, an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, experience symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) related to pseudoephedrine, have an allergic constitution, have a history of allergic symptoms due to medicines (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastric or duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), dysuria, are elderly, are frail or have high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting more than 1 week or recurrent cough, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, stop taking immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 비비코에스연질캡슐 - 使用上の注意
*   毎日3杯以上アルコールを摂取する習慣のある方は、本剤または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する前に、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。

    非常にまれに、致死的な重篤な皮膚反応（急性汎発性発疹性膿疱症、スティーブンス・ジョンソン症候群、中毒性表皮壊死融解症など）が報告されており、本剤服用後に皮疹または他の過敏症の兆候が現れた場合は、直ちに服用を中止してください。

    急性汎発性発疹性膿疱症のような重篤な皮膚の副作用が現れた場合は、服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
*   以下の場合は本剤を服用しないでください：本剤に対して過敏症の既往歴がある方、他の解熱鎮痛剤またはかぜ薬の服用時にぜんそくを経験したことがある方、生後3ヶ月未満の乳児、重度または未管理の高血圧患者、妊娠30週以降の妊婦。

    本剤を服用する前に、以下の場合は医師または薬剤師にご相談ください：2歳未満の小児、水痘またはインフルエンザに感染しているかその疑いのある乳児および15歳未満の小児、プソイドエフェドリン成分に関連して虚血性大腸炎の症状（例：急激な腹痛、直腸出血など）が現れた場合、アレルギー体質の方、薬によるアレルギー症状（例：発熱、発疹、関節痛、ぜんそく、かゆみなど）の経験がある方、肝臓病、腎臓病、心臓病、甲状腺疾患、糖尿病、高血圧、胃・十二指腸潰瘍、緑内障（例：眼の痛み、目のかすみなど）、排尿困難の方、高齢者、体が弱い方または高熱のある方、胸やけ、胃部不快感、胃痛などの胃腸症状が持続または再発している方、または潰瘍、出血の問題がある方、妊婦または妊娠している可能性のある女性、授乳婦、喫煙者、ぜんそく、慢性気管支炎、肺気腫、過剰なたんを伴う咳、1週間以上続くまたは再発する咳、慢性的な咳、発熱・発疹または持続する頭痛を伴う咳の患者。

    定められた用法・用量を守って服用してください。

    服用後に眠気が現れることがありますので、車の運転や機械の操作には注意してください。

    5～6回服用しても症状の改善が見られない場合は、直ちに服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
