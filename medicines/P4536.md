#EN
### 💊 타이렌연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Indications and Efficacy
* This medicine is used for fever and pain (ache) due to common cold, headache, neuralgia, myalgia, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia, and rheumatic pain (ache).

### 🕒 타이렌연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 12 years and older, take 2 capsules at a time, 3-4 times a day, every 4-6 hours as needed.

Do not exceed a maximum of 12 capsules (4 g) per day.

Take this medicine at the lowest effective dose for the shortest possible duration.

### ⚠️ 타이렌연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the daily maximum dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood abnormalities, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe heart failure, aspirin asthma (asthma attack induced by non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs) or a history thereof, or if you consume alcohol.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver dysfunction or a history thereof, kidney dysfunction or a history thereof, a history of peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities or a history thereof, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant or breastfeeding, or have concomitant infections.

Do not take for more than 10 days for pain (adults) or more than 5 days (children), or more than 3 days for fever without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.

* 12岁以上成人及儿童，每次2粒胶囊，每日3~4次，每4~6小时一次，必要时服用。

每日服用量请勿超过12粒胶囊（4克）。

请以最短时间内最小有效剂量服用本药品。

### ⚠️ 타이렌연질캡슐(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
* 若每日饮酒三杯以上的规律饮酒者服用本药品或其它解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药剂师。可能引起肝损伤。

极少数情况下报告了可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药品后，若出现皮疹或其它过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。

服用本药品前，若有肝功能障碍或有此病史者、肾功能障碍或有此病史者、有消化性溃疡病史者、有血液异常或有此病史者、有出血倾向者、有心功能异常患者、过敏症患者、支气管哮喘患者、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女、合并感染症患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

未经医生或药剂师指示，请勿连续服用本药品用于疼痛超过10天（成人）或超过5天（儿童），用于发热超过3天。
