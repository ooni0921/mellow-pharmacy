#EN
### 💊 씨큐맥스정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamins C, D, E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, physical debilitation during and after illness, growth period, and geriatric age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lumbago, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, poor bone and tooth development, prevention of rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, relief of pigmentation (e.g., melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, and prevention of gum bleeding and nose bleeding.

### 🕒 씨큐맥스정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years and older: Take 1 to 2 tablets once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 씨큐맥스정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months, have kidney stones, or have severe renal failure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, have cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a history of kidney stones.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage) or folic acid deficiency.

When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

This medicine may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

### 💊 씨큐맥스정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解身体疲劳、怀孕期和哺乳期、病中和病后体力下降、发育期、老年期的维生素C、D、E、B1、B2、B6补充；缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩周炎等）、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状；预防骨骼和牙齿发育不良、佝偻病；缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳；缓解因日晒或皮肤病引起色素沉着（如黄褐斑、雀斑）；预防牙龈出血和鼻出血（流鼻血）。

### 🕒 씨큐맥스정 - 用法用量
* 12岁以上及成人：每日一次，每次1~2片，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 씨큐맥스정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者、高钙血症（血液中钙含量过高的状态）、结节病（原因不明的全身炎症性疾病）、肾脏疾病、12个月以下婴儿、肾结石、严重肾功能不全患者请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，高草酸尿症患者（尿液中草酸盐排出过多的状态）、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心脏·循环系统功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、痛风患者、肾结石或有肾结石病史的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

有血栓形成倾向（血管阻塞）的患者、叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。

服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。

本药可能会干扰各种尿液检查中的血糖检测，并可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查值。

请严格遵守用法用量。
