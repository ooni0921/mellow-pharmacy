#EN
### 💊 암포젤정(건조수산화알루미늄겔) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for antacid action and improvement of symptoms in gastric and duodenal ulcers, gastritis, and hyperacidity.

### 🕒 암포젤정(건조수산화알루미늄겔) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 tablets (300-600 mg) once, 3 times daily between meals. Dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 암포젤정(건조수산화알루미늄겔) - Precautions
* No information
* Patients undergoing dialysis therapy should not take this medication. Before taking this medication, patients with phosphate deficiency, renal impairment, or a history of kidney disease should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not use for prolonged periods.

#CN
### 💊 암포젤정(건조수산화알루미늄겔) - 功效及作用
* 本药用于胃、十二指肠溃疡、胃炎、胃酸过多症的制酸作用及症状改善。

### 🕒 암포젤정(건조수산화알루미늄겔) - 用法及用量
* 成人每次1~2片（300~600 mg），每日3次，饭间服用。可根据年龄、症状适当增减。

### ⚠️ 암포젤정(건조수산화알루미늄겔) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 正在接受透析治疗的患者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，患有磷酸盐缺乏症、肾功能障碍、有肾病史的患者请咨询医生或药剂师。请勿长期连续服用。

#JP
