#EN

### 💊 속콜시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 속콜시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older: 1 sachet (20 mL) once.
* For individuals aged 11 years or older to under 15 years: 2/3 sachet (13.3 mL) once.
* For individuals aged 7 years or older to under 11 years: 1/2 sachet (10 mL) once.
* For individuals aged 3 years or older to under 7 years: 1/3 sachet (6.7 mL) once.
* For individuals aged 2 years or older to under 3 years: 1/4 sachet (5 mL) once.
* Take 3 times daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 속콜시럽 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.
* Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis (Lyell's syndrome), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, stop taking it immediately.
* Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to it, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, or are an infant under 3 months of age.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic predisposition or a history of allergic reactions (fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty urinating; are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding; have gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain; have ulcer or bleeding problems; are currently taking diabetes medications, gout medications, arthritis medications, anticoagulants, or steroids; or if you experience symptoms of cough (cough associated with smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, excessive phlegm; cough lasting or recurring for more than a week; chronic cough; cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache); or if you are taking barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, or alcohol.
* For children under 2 years of age, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
* If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, stop taking the medication immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
