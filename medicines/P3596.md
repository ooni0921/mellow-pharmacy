#EN
### 💊 삐콤씨액티브정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and in old age, for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (back pain, stiff shoulders, etc.), relief of symptoms of beriberi, eye strain, angular cheilitis (inflammation at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis, relief of hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, prevention of gum bleeding, nosebleeds, and for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, stiff shoulders and neck during menopause, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet.

### 🕒 삐콤씨액티브정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years and older: Take 1 tablet once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 삐콤씨액티브정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant, are an infant, have gout or kidney stones, or have a folic acid deficiency.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow, which can affect clinical test values or interfere with glucose detection during urine tests.

#CN
### 💊 삐콤씨액티브정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于在身体疲劳、妊娠、哺乳期、病中・病后（患病期间或恢复后）体力下降时，以及老年期补充维生素E、B1、B2、B6、C，缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（腰痛、肩颈僵硬等症状），缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳、口角炎（嘴角发炎）、唇炎（嘴唇发炎）、口腔炎（口腔发炎）、舌炎（舌头发炎）、湿疹、皮炎症状，缓解因日晒・皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑），预防牙龈出血、鼻出血（鼻血），以及用于缓解末梢血液循环障碍、更年期时的肩颈僵硬、手脚发麻・手脚冰凉（手脚冰冷）等症状。

### 🕒 삐콤씨액티브정 - 用法与用量
*   8岁以上及成人：一次1片，一日2次。

### ⚠️ 삐콤씨액티브정 - 注意事项
*   对本药过敏的患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，未满1岁的婴儿、高草酸尿症患者、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、痛风患者或有肾结石的患者、叶酸不足的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查值，或在尿液检查时干扰血糖的检测。
