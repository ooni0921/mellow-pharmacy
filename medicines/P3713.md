# EN

### 💊 솔루샷쿨연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 솔루샷쿨연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: Take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 솔루샷쿨연질캡슐 - Precautions
* If you are a regular drinker of three or more alcoholic beverages daily, consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 2 years of age; an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) appear related to pseudoephedrine; if you have an allergic constitution or have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), or difficulty urinating; if you are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding; if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, stop taking the medicine immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
