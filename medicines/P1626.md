#EN
### 💊 시너스비액(옥시메타졸린염산염)(수출명:오리지날릴리프비액,엑스트라모이스처라이징비액,사이너스비액) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of nasal congestion, runny nose, sneezing, and heavy head caused by the common cold, allergic rhinitis, or sinusitis.

### 🕒 시너스비액(옥시메타졸린염산염)(수출명:오리지날릴리프비액,엑스트라모이스처라이징비액,사이너스비액) - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 7 years and older and adults: 2-3 drops per dose, administered into each nostril twice a day.
* For individuals aged 2 years to under 7 years: 1 drop per dose, administered into each nostril twice a day.
* When used consecutively, maintain an interval of at least 3 hours between doses.

### ⚠️ 시너스비액(옥시메타졸린염산염)(수출명:오리지날릴리프비액,엑스트라모이스처라이징비액,사이너스비액) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 2 years of age, or are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks.

Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medication (e.g., fever, rash, redness, swelling, irritation, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.), have high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, glaucoma, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Excessive use of this medication may actually cause nasal congestion, so do not use it excessively.

Do not use in the eyes or ears.

To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the product if possible.

Do not use continuously for more than 7 days.

When using for children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
