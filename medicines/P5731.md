#EN
### 💊 팩트라민투엑스정 - Indications and Effects
*   This medication is used to supplement vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, and C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during or after illness when physical strength is low, during growth, and in old age.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medication is used to alleviate symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, shoulder stiffness, other joint pain, beriberi, eye strain, angular cheilitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

Through the vitamins it contains, this medication is used to prevent poor bone and tooth development and rickets.

This medication is used to supplement zinc.

### 🕒 팩트라민투엑스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For children aged 8 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 팩트라민투엑스정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, or suffer from hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, or kidney stones.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets plenty of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, or a patient with hyperoxaluria, gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, heart and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or a history of kidney stones.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of taking this medication, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Patients with a thrombotic predisposition should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medication may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
