#EN
### 💊 엠로딘액(L-카르니틴) - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and myocardial metabolic disorders caused by ischemic heart disease (angina pectoris, acute myocardial infarction).

### 🕒 엠로딘액(L-카르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2-3 g orally per day, divided into 2-3 doses.

Adjust the dosage appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엠로딘액(L-카르니틴) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine should not take it.

Pregnant women, breastfeeding women, and uremic patients should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

Dilute before oral administration.

#CN

*   本药用于治疗原发性、继发性肉碱缺乏症，以及缺血性心脏病引起的心肌代谢障碍（心绞痛、急性心肌梗死）。

### 🕒 엠로딘액(L-카르니틴) - 用法用量
*   成人每日口服2~3克，分2~3次服用。

请根据年龄和症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 엠로딘액(L-카르니틴) - 注意事项
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者请勿服用本药。

口服前请稀释后服用。

#JP
