#EN
### 💊 그날엔노즈에이연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used to relieve various cold symptoms such as runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle aches.

### 🕒 그날엔노즈에이연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults, take 2 capsules per dose. For individuals aged over 7 years and under 15 years, take 1 capsule per dose. Administer 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 그날엔노즈에이연질캡슐 - Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day, consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of or current hypersensitivity to this medication or its components, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, a child under 2 years of age, a patient with severe or uncontrolled hypertension, or are pregnant after 30 weeks.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 2 years of age (only if absolutely necessary), an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, experience symptoms of ischemic colitis such as sudden abdominal pain or rectal bleeding in relation to pseudoephedrine, have experienced allergic symptoms such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, or itching due to medication, are elderly, frail, or have a high fever.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer, glaucoma, difficulty urinating, persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have a history of ulcers or bleeding problems, or if you smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than one week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, immediately stop taking this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not take this medication continuously for a prolonged period.

Drowsiness may occur during administration. Avoid driving a car or operating machinery.

Please strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

アセトアミノフェンとして1日最大用量（4,000 mg）を超えて服用しないでください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。アセトアミノフェンを含む他の製品と併用しないでください。

急性汎発性発疹性膿疱症のような重症皮膚異常反応が現れた場合は、服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
* 本剤に過敏症のある患者または経験者、本剤および本剤の構成成分、他の解熱鎮痛剤、風邪薬服用時に喘息を経験したことがある方、生後3ヶ月未満の乳児および2歳未満の小児、重症または管理されていない高血圧患者、妊娠30週以降の方は、本剤を服用しないでください。

本剤を服用する前に、以下に該当する方は医師または薬剤師にご相談ください：2歳未満の小児（必要不可欠な場合に限る）、水痘またはインフルエンザに感染しているかまたはその疑いのある乳幼児および15歳未満の小児、アレルギー体質の方、妊婦または妊娠している可能性のある女性、授乳婦、プソイドエフェドリン成分に関連して急激な腹痛、直腸出血などの虚血性大腸炎の症状が発現した場合、薬による発熱、発疹、関節痛、喘息、かゆみなどのアレルギー症状を経験したことがある方、高齢者、体が弱い方または高熱のある方。

本剤を服用する前に、以下に該当する方は医師または薬剤師にご相談ください：肝臓疾患、腎臓疾患、心臓疾患、甲状腺疾患、糖尿病、高血圧、胃・十二指腸潰瘍、緑内障、排尿困難などがある方、胸やけ、胃部不快感、胃痛といった胃腸の問題が持続または再発している方、あるいは潰瘍、出血問題を抱えている方、喫煙者、喘息、慢性気管支炎、肺気腫、過剰な痰を伴う咳、1週間以上持続または再発する咳、慢性的な咳、発熱・発疹または持続的な頭痛を伴う咳がある方。

5～6回服用しても症状が良くならない場合は、本剤の服用を直ちに中止し、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。

小児は保護者の指導および監督のもとに服用させてください。

長期間続けて服用しないでください。

服用中に眠気が来ることがあります。自動車の運転または機械類の運転操作は避けてください。

定められた用法用量をよく守ってください。
