#EN
### 💊 엘카렉정(엘카르니틴) - Indications and Uses
*   This medication is used for primary and secondary carnitine deficiency, and for myocardial metabolic disorders caused by ischemic heart diseases such as angina pectoris and acute myocardial infarction.

### 🕒 엘카렉정(엘카르니틴) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2-3 g per day, divided into 2-3 doses. The dose can be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘카렉정(엘카르니틴) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not take it. Pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, and lactating women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
