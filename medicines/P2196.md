#EN
### 💊 제일한방파프수플러스카타플라스마 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for sprains, contusions, muscle pain, fracture pain, stiff shoulders, frostbite, joint pain, back pain, and muscle fatigue.

### 🕒 제일한방파프수플러스카타플라스마 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply one patch at a time, once or twice daily, to the affected area.

### ⚠️ 제일한방파프수플러스카타플라스마 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medication around the eyes, on mucous membranes, on moist skin, on areas with dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances, or on wounds.
* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer or similar substances) due to medications or cosmetics, if you have moist skin or severe excoriation, or if you are pregnant, possibly pregnant, or breastfeeding.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.
* This medication is for external use only; do not ingest.
* If a large amount is absorbed through the skin, symptoms of poisoning may occur, so avoid long-term use over large areas.
