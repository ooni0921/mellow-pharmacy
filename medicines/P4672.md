###EN
### 💊 뉴락스에프정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of constipation and symptoms associated with constipation such as loss of appetite (anorexia), abdominal distension, abnormal intestinal fermentation, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 뉴락스에프정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 to 2 tablets once daily at bedtime (or on an empty stomach).
    However, for the initial dose, take the minimum amount and gradually increase or decrease the dosage while observing the shape and condition of the stool.

### ⚠️ 뉴락스에프정 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medication if you have acute abdominal diseases (such as appendicitis, intestinal hemorrhage, ulcerative colitis), intestinal obstruction, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, are a child under 10 years of age, have an allergic constitution, severe abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, or have experienced rectal bleeding or intestinal failure.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not chew the tablets when taking them.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one week of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

###CN
### 💊 뉴락스에프정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解便秘，以及便秘引起的食欲不振（食欲减退）、腹胀、肠内异常发酵、痔疮。

### 🕒 뉴락스에프정 - 用法用量
* 成人每次服用1~2片，每日1次，睡前（或空腹时）服用。
    初次服用时请服用最小量，并根据大便的形状和状态，逐渐增量或减量。

### ⚠️ 뉴락스에프정 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 急性腹部疾病（如阑尾炎、肠出血、溃疡性结肠炎等）、肠梗阻、半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如果您是孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、10岁以下儿童、过敏体质者、有严重腹痛、恶心、呕吐症状者，或有直肠出血或肠功能不全史者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

服用时请勿咀嚼药片。

服用约一周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
