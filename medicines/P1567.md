# EN
### 💊 투헬스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness (during or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., low back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, peripheral circulatory disorders, stiff shoulders and neck during menopause, numbness and coldness in hands and feet.

### 🕒 투헬스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule once, twice a day.

### ⚠️ 투헬스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Do not take this medicine if you are allergic to this medicine, soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, if you are an infant under 12 months old, or if you have severe renal insufficiency.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, hyperlipidemia due to diabetes, pancreatitis, or other fat metabolism disorders, or if you need to administer lipid emulsions cautiously, or if special risks are expected due to fat overload, or if you have a thrombotic predisposition (blood vessel blockage).
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN
### 💊 투헬스연질캡슐 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于补充维生素E、B1、B2、B6，适用于体力疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中病后(患病期间或康复后)体力下降时、老年期。同时用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛(如腰痛、肩颈僵硬等)、口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮炎等症状，以及缓解脚气病、眼睛疲劳、末梢血液循环障碍、更年期肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木ㆍ手脚冰冷等症状。

### 🕒 투헬스연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
* 12岁以上及成人，一次1粒，一日2次服用。

### ⚠️ 투헬스연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 对本药、大豆油、大豆、花生过敏者，未满12个月的婴儿，以及症状严重的肾功能不全患者，请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、心血管功能障碍者、肾功能障碍者、低蛋白血症、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症以及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者，或需要谨慎给予脂质乳剂的患者，或因脂肪过载预计有特殊风险的患者，或有血栓形成倾向(血管堵塞)的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
* 本药可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检查结果。
* 服用约1个月后症状仍未改善时，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药剂师。
