### 💊 이지엔6애니연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for fever and pain caused by cold, back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), postoperative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injury (sprain, contusion), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 이지엔6애니연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults with rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, or psoriatic arthritis, take 1-3 capsules (200-600 mg) at a time, 3-4 times a day, up to a maximum of 16 capsules (3,200 mg) per day. For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3-4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or cold, adults take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) at a time, 3-4 times a day. For migraine, adults take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) at a time, not exceeding 2 capsules within 24 hours.

Adolescents aged 12 years and older take 1-2 capsules (200-400 mg) at a time, 3-4 times a day.

If body weight is less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg, and it is advisable to avoid taking the drug on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 이지엔6애니연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
*   If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this drug or other antipyretic analgesics, consult a doctor or pharmacist. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this drug may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Severe gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcer, and perforation may also occur.

Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day). If patients with cardiovascular risk factors (hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking, etc.) require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg per day), they should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug.
*   Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other NSAIDs, pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are in the last trimester of pregnancy, are breastfeeding, or are receiving high-dose methotrexate for chemotherapy.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, cardiac dysfunction, heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly, are a child, are pregnant, are breastfeeding, or are taking low-dose aspirin.

When taking for a cold, in principle, do not take for more than 5 days. For pain, do not take for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without doctor or pharmacist's instructions. For fever, do not take for more than 3 days.
