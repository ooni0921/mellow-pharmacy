# EN

### 💊 신신파스아렉스마일드카타플라스마 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory (anti-inflammation) effects in cases of contusions, sprains, lower back pain, stiff shoulders, muscle pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 신신파스아렉스마일드카타플라스마 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area (diseased part) 1 to 3 times a day, after peeling off the release liner from the medication surface.

### ⚠️ 신신파스아렉스마일드카타플라스마 - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not use this medicine in infants under 30 months, on skin inflammations caused by eczema or lacquer, or on wounded areas.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergies (e.g., rash, redness, itching, skin inflammation caused by lacquer, etc.), if you or your family are prone to allergies, if you have severe moistness or erosion, if you are undergoing treatment by a doctor, if you are a child, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a nursing mother.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not warm the application site (diseased area) with a heater, electric blanket, or similar devices.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When using for children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine is for external use only.

### ⚠️ 신신파스아렉스마일드카타플라스마 - 注意事项
