# EN

### 💊 타미스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for fever, pain associated with colds, and to relieve headaches, toothaches, muscle aches, backaches, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 타미스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years and older should take 2 tablets every 8 hours.

Do not exceed 6 tablets in a 24-hour period.

### ⚠️ 타미스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day and are taking this medication or other fever-reducing analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, a history of asthma induced by aspirin or other NSAIDs, or if you are a child under 12 years of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver impairment, kidney impairment, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or for fever for more than 3 days without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.

This medication is an extended-release formulation, so swallow the tablet whole without crushing, chewing, or dissolving it.

# CN

### 💊 타미스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于退烧、缓解感冒引起的疼痛以及头痛、牙痛、肌肉痛、腰痛、月经痛、关节痛。

### 🕒 타미스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 用法与用量
*   12岁以上成人每次服用2片，每8小时一次。

24小时内不要超过6片。

### ⚠️ 타미스펜8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上的规律饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能会导致肝损伤。

极少数情况下，曾报告可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性全身发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征和中毒性表皮坏死松解症。服用本药后若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应症状，请立即停止服用。

乙酰氨基酚的每日最大剂量（4,000 毫克）不可超量服用。可能会导致肝损伤。请勿与其他含有乙酰氨基酚的产品同时服用。
*   对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡、严重血液病、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、有阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛药引起哮喘史者、12岁以下儿童请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，患有肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、消化性溃疡、血液病、出血倾向、心功能异常、有过敏史、支气管哮喘、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女请咨询医生或药师。

未经医生或药师指导，请勿用于止痛超过10天（成人），或退烧超过3天。

本药为缓释制剂，因此请勿压碎、咀嚼或溶解药片，应整片吞服。
