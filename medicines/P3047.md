# EN

### 💊 비알이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Indications and Usage
* This medicine is used for rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), fever and pain due to the common cold, low back pain, dysmenorrhea, post-operative pain, ankylosing spondylitis, headache, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injury (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 비알이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 11-14 years: Take 200-250 mg (10-13 mL) per dose, 3-4 times daily.
* Children aged 7-10 years: Take 150-200 mg (8-10 mL) per dose, 3-4 times daily.
* Children aged 3-6 years: Take 100-150 mg (5-8 mL) per dose, 3-4 times daily.
* Children aged 1-2 years: Take 50-100 mg (3-5 mL) per dose, 3-4 times daily.

For children weighing less than 30 kg, the total daily dose should not exceed 500 mg (25 mL). It is advisable to avoid administering this medicine on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 비알이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - Warnings and Precautions
* If you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily, consult your doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.
* Taking this medicine increases the risk of serious, potentially fatal, cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, or cerebrovascular disease should avoid high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg daily). Patients with cardiovascular risk factors (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes mellitus, smoking) who require high-dose ibuprofen (2,400 mg daily) should consult their doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulcer, gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics, pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, are in the last trimester of pregnancy, are breastfeeding, are receiving high-dose methotrexate for chemotherapy, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
* Do not exceed the recommended dose.
* Before taking this medicine, consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis, liver impairment, kidney impairment, cardiac insufficiency, heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly, are a child under 2 years of age, are pregnant, or have chickenpox.
* When taking for the common cold, generally take for no more than 5 days. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), and do not take for fever for more than 3 days without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN

* 此药用于类风湿性关节炎、青少年类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎（退行性关节疾病）、感冒引起的发热及疼痛、腰痛、痛经、术后疼痛、强直性脊柱炎、头痛、牙痛、肌肉痛、神经痛、急性痛风、银屑病关节炎、软组织损伤（扭伤、挫伤）、非关节风湿性疾病（肌腱炎、腱鞘炎、滑囊炎）。

### 🕒 비알이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - 用法与用量
* 11~14岁儿童：每次服用 200~250 毫克 (10~13 毫升)，每日 3~4 次。

### ⚠️ 비알이부펜시럽(이부프로펜) - 注意事项

* 以下患者请勿服用此药：对此药过敏者，有胃肠道溃疡、胃肠道出血、严重血液异常、严重肝功能障碍、严重肾功能障碍、严重心功能不全、严重高血压、支气管哮喘、因阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛药引起的哮喘、荨麻疹、过敏反应史的患者，冠状动脉搭桥手术前后出现疼痛的患者，妊娠最后三个月期的孕妇，哺乳期妇女，正在接受高剂量甲氨蝶呤化疗的患者，以及有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者。
* 请勿超过推荐剂量服用。
* 服用此药前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果您有血液异常、出血倾向、肝硬化、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全、心脏病、高血压、系统性红斑狼疮、混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、克罗恩病、老年人、2岁以下儿童、孕妇或水痘患者。
* 感冒时服用，原则上不超过5天。未经医生或药剂师指导，疼痛服用不超过10天（成人）或5天（儿童），发热服用不超过3天。

* 11〜14歳は1回200〜250 mg（10〜13 mL）を1日3〜4回服用します。

* 毎日3杯以上アルコールを定期的に摂取する方が、本剤または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する場合は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。胃腸出血を引き起こす可能性があります。
