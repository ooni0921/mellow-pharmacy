#EN
### 💊 Turbincool Plus Cream - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for cutaneous fungal infections (tinea) caused by dermatophytes (athlete's foot, tinea cruris (jock itch), ringworm).

### 🕒 Turbincool Plus Cream - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area once daily.

### ⚠️ Turbincool Plus Cream - Precautions
*   Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, or an infant or child.
*   Do not use on or around the eyes, mucous membranes (mouth, nose, vagina), scrotum, vulva, eczema, wet/weeping lesions, chafing, cracks, severely traumatized affected areas, nails, or scalp.
*   Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have an allergic predisposition, if the affected area is on the face, extensive, or purulent (festering), or if you have or have had hypersensitivity to propylene glycol.
*   Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   Do not apply to dirty affected areas or surrounding skin.
*   Be careful not to get it in the eyes. If it gets into the eyes, rinse immediately with cold or lukewarm water.
*   This medicine is for external use only.

### 🕒 Turbincool Plus Cream - 用法・用量
*   1日1回、適量を患部（病変部位）に塗布してください。

### ⚠️ Turbincool Plus Cream - 使用上の注意
*   本剤に対し過敏症のある患者、妊婦または妊娠している可能性のある女性および授乳婦、乳幼児および小児は本剤を使用しないでください。

*   目に入らないように注意し、万一目に入った場合には、すぐに冷水またはぬるま湯で洗い流してください。
*   本剤は外用にのみ使用してください。
