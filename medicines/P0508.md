# EN

### 💊 아스타펜정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain due to cold, headache, neuralgia, myalgia, menstrual pain, sprain pain, toothache, arthralgia, and rheumatic pain.

### 🕒 아스타펜정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 300~1,000 mg once, 3~4 times a day. The maximum daily dose is 4,000 mg. The dosage may be adjusted appropriately according to age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 아스타펜정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks per day while taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage. Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, liver disorder, kidney disorder, cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency, aspirin asthma (asthma attacks induced by NSAIDs) or a history thereof, have taken barbiturate drugs, tricyclic antidepressants, alcohol, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver disorder, kidney disorder, peptic ulcer, blood abnormalities, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without a doctor's or pharmacist's instruction, and do not take for fever for more than 3 days.

* 本药用于感冒引起的发热和疼痛、头痛、神经痛、肌肉痛、月经痛、扭伤痛、牙痛、关节痛、风湿样疼痛。

### 🕒 아스타펜정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - 用法与用量
* 成人每次服用 300~1,000 mg，每日 3~4 次。每日最高剂量可达 4,000 mg。可根据年龄、症状适当增减剂量。

### ⚠️ 아스타펜정325밀리그람(아세트아미노펜제피세립) - 注意事项
* 每日饮酒三杯以上且经常饮酒者在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。可能引起肝损伤。极罕见情况下，曾报告可能致命的急性全身发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征、中毒性表皮坏死松解症等严重皮肤反应。服用本药后若出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停药。对乙酰氨基酚每日最大剂量（4,000 mg）请勿超量服用。可能引起肝损伤。请勿与含有对乙酰氨基酚的其他产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡、血液异常、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心脏功能低下、出血倾向、阿司匹林哮喘（非甾体抗炎药诱发的哮喘发作）或有此病史者、服用巴比妥类药物、三环类抗抑郁药、酒精者、以及有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，若有肝功能障碍、肾脏（肾）功能障碍、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、心脏功能异常、有过敏史、支气管哮喘、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。未经医生或药师指示，疼痛服用时间不得超过10天（成人）或5天（儿童），发热服用时间不得超过3天。
