#EN

### 💊 모닝톡에프장용연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used to relieve constipation and symptoms associated with constipation, such as loss of appetite, abdominal bloating, abnormal intestinal fermentation, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 모닝톡에프장용연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults aged 15 years or older, take 2 capsules once daily at bedtime.
*   For the initial dose, take the minimum amount, and gradually increase or decrease the dosage while observing the shape and condition of the stool.

### ⚠️ 모닝톡에프장용연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information provided
*   Do not take this medication if you have acute abdominal diseases such as appendicitis, intestinal bleeding, or ulcerative colitis, if you have intestinal obstruction, or if you have a history of or are hypersensitive to soybean oil, soy, or peanuts.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 10 years of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a patient experiencing severe abdominal pain, nausea, or vomiting, a person on a sodium-restricted diet, someone with a history of rectal bleeding or intestinal dysfunction, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient prone to fat overload, or a patient with a history of hypersensitivity reaction to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF).
*   If constipation does not improve after about 1 week of taking this medication, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not continue to take this medication for more than 1 week.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
