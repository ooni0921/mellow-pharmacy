# EN
### 💊 메가골드비타정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of vitamins B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, and physical weakness during or after illness (while sick or after recovery). It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (lip corner inflammation), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue, as well as for the supply of zinc.

### 🕒 메가골드비타정 - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years and older, and adults: take 1 tablet once daily, after meals.

### ⚠️ 메가골드비타정 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it, are an infant under 12 months, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant or infant, have cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney problems, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, or folic acid deficiency. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian. If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist. This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, which can affect clinical test results.

# CN
### 💊 메가골드비타정 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解身体疲劳、妊娠期和哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时，补充维生素B1、B2、B6、C以及锌。同时，还用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、关节痛（如腰痛、肩膀僵硬等）、口角炎（嘴角发炎）、唇炎（嘴唇发炎）、口腔炎（口腔发炎）、舌炎（舌头发炎）、湿疹、皮炎症状、脚气病以及眼睛疲劳。

### 🕒 메가골드비타정 - 用法用量
* 12岁及以上儿童和成人：每日1次，每次1片，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 메가골드비타정 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性问题的患者请勿服用本药。服用本药前，高草酸尿症患者、孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、心脏ㆍ循环系统功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、痛风患者、肾结石患者、叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用，并咨询医生或药师。本药可能在各种尿液检查中干扰血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查结果。

### 🕒 메가골드비타정 - 用法及び用量
