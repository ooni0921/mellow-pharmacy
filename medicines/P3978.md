#EN
### 💊 마그포스액티브연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for:
    * Supplementation of Vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, when physical strength is low during and after illness (while sick or after recovery), and in old age.
    * Alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet (cold extremities) due to peripheral circulatory disorders and during menopause.
    * Alleviation of symptoms of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis (cheilitis), labial stomatitis (lip inflammation), oral stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, and dermatitis.
    * Beriberi, eye fatigue, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그포스액티브연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults: Take 1 capsule per dose, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그포스액티브연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are: hypersensitive to this medicine or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts; an infant under 12 months of age; or a patient with severe renal failure.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: pregnant or possibly pregnant; breastfeeding; a premature infant; an infant; a patient with impaired cardiac or circulatory function; a patient with kidney (renal) impairment; a patient with hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic dyslipidemia, pancreatitis, or other lipid metabolism disorders; a patient who needs to be carefully administered lipid emulsions; a patient for whom a specific risk is expected due to lipid overload; a patient with a thrombotic predisposition (blood clot blockage); or a patient with folic acid deficiency.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medicine may turn urine yellow, which could affect clinical test results.
