#EN
### 💊 덱스녹실정(덱스판테놀) - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for hair loss.

### 🕒 덱스녹실정(덱스판테놀) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet at a time, 3 times a day.

The treatment period is 6 weeks.

### ⚠️ 덱스녹실정(덱스판테놀) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are under 19 years of age, have conditions other than alopecia, or have hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Before taking this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, and individuals with a history of allergic reactions to medications should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 6 weeks of taking the medication, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

To avoid drug interactions, inform your doctor or pharmacist of your current treatment status.

If any abnormalities occur while taking this medication, stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
