# EN

### 💊 디카본500정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamin D during pregnancy, lactation, growth periods, and old age, for the prevention of poor development of bones and teeth and rickets, and for calcium deficiency and other calcium supplementation.

### 🕒 디카본500정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 8 years or older: Take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 디카본500정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, infants under 12 months of age, hypercalcemia (a condition in which there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age, children who get a lot of sunlight and have a normal diet, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant and lactating women, premature infants, toddlers, patients with cardiovascular system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, or a history of kidney stones should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
