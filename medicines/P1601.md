#EN
### 💊 Sialdin Tab. - Indications and Efficacy
* This medication is used for stomach pain, heartburn, indigestion, and nausea.

### 🕒 Sialdin Tab. - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 to under 80 years should take 2 tablets at a time. If necessary, 2 tablets can be taken at 4-hour intervals, up to 3 times a day. However, do not exceed 3 doses (6 tablets) per day.

### ⚠️ Sialdin Tab. - Precautions
* Do not take this medication if you are: hypersensitive to histamine H2-receptor antagonists; undergoing dialysis; suffering from blood disorders, kidney/liver diseases, gastric/duodenal diseases, asthma, or immune system disorders such as rheumatism; have a history of blood abnormalities such as erythrocytopenia, thrombocytopenia, or leukocytopenia; are pregnant or potentially pregnant, or breastfeeding; are under 15 years of age or over 80 years of age; or are currently taking steroids, antibiotics, anticancer drugs, or azole antifungals.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: an elderly person aged 65 or older, have a history of drug hypersensitivity, experience sore throat, cough, high fever, unexplained weight loss, abdominal pain, or have a history of kidney disease.
* Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* If symptoms do not improve after 3 days of taking the medication, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Drowsiness, dizziness, or confusion may occur after taking this medication; therefore, avoid driving or operating machinery.

* 本药品用于胃痛、胃灼热、消化不良和恶心。

### 🕒 시알딘정 - 用法用量
* 15岁以上至80岁以下成人，一次服用2片。必要时，可每隔4小时服用2片，每日最多3次。但是，每日服用次数不得超过3次（6片）。

### ⚠️ 시알딘정 - 注意事项
* 禁忌人群：对组胺H2受体拮抗剂过敏者；正在接受透析治疗的患者；患有血液疾病、肾脏/肝脏疾病、胃/十二指肠疾病、哮喘、风湿等免疫系统疾病的患者；有红细胞减少症、血小板减少症、白细胞减少症等血液异常史者；孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女；15岁以下儿童及80岁以上老年人；正在服用类固醇、抗生素、抗癌药、唑类抗真菌药的患者。
* 服用本药前，请咨询医生或药师：65岁以上老年人；有药物过敏史者；出现咽喉痛、咳嗽、高烧、原因不明的体重减轻、腹痛患者；有肾脏病史者。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

### 🕒 シアルディン錠 - 用法・用量
* 15歳以上80歳未満の成人は、1回2錠服用してください。必要に応じて、4時間間隔で1回2錠ずつ、1日3回まで服用できます。ただし、1日3回（6錠）を超えて服用しないでください。
