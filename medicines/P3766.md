# EN
### 💊 아스팜장용정(아스피린) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the inhibition of thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, for the inhibition of thrombus formation after Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) or Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA), and for the reduction of cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, diabetes).

### 🕒 아스팜장용정(아스피린) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once a day. Since this is an enteric-coated tablet, it can be taken before meals with plenty of water.

### ⚠️ 아스팜장용정(아스피린) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or antirheumatic drugs, peptic ulcer, aspirin asthma or a history thereof, hemophilia, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency, or if you are co-administering methotrexate at a dose exceeding 15 mg/week for a week, or if you are a pregnant woman in the third trimester. In the first and second trimesters of pregnancy, do not take this drug unless absolutely necessary. Before using this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (e.g., renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, volume depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major hemorrhagic events), hepatic impairment, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, if you are a pre-operative patient, an infant under 3 years of age, an elderly patient, a patient with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a patient under 14 years of age with chickenpox or influenza infection, or a gout patient. Since there is a risk of bleeding, inform your doctor that you are taking this drug if you are undergoing surgery (including dental surgery). Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this drug.

* 本品用于心肌梗死、脑梗死、不稳定型心绞痛中抑制血栓形成；冠状动脉旁路移植术(CABG)或经皮冠状动脉腔内成形术(PTCA)术后抑制血栓形成；以及降低高风险患者（具有缺血性心脏病家族史、高血压、高胆固醇血症、肥胖、糖尿病等多种危险因素的患者）的心血管风险。

### 🕒 아스팜장용정(아스피린) - 用法用量
* 成人每次1片，每日1次服用。本品为肠溶片，可与足量水一同在饭前服用。

### ⚠️ 아스팜장용정(아스피린) - 注意事项
* 无信息
