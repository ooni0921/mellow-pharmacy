### 💊 라니아정 - Effects and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for contraception and for the treatment of moderate acne in women who can take this medicine.

### 🕒 라니아정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Take 1 tablet daily for 21 days in the indicated order, followed by a 7-day break. It is preferable to take the medicine at the same time each day if possible. Menstruation usually occurs during the 7-day break, typically starting within 2-3 days after the last tablet is taken. If you continue to take this medicine, begin a new pack of tablets after the 7-day break; menstruation may not have finished before starting the new pack. For more details, refer to the detailed information in the marketing authorization.

### ⚠️ 라니아정 - Precautions
*   Women taking oral contraceptives should refrain from smoking, as there may be an increased risk of serious cardiovascular events such as thrombosis. Smokers aged 35 or older should not take this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, tumors (e.g., breast cancer, endometrial cancer, pituitary tumor), thrombophlebitis, thromboembolism, cerebrovascular or coronary artery disease, undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding, severe hypertension, severe liver impairment, Dubin-Johnson syndrome, Rotor syndrome, jaundice, cholestasis (bile retention), hepatitis, sickle cell anemia, severe diabetes mellitus, hyperlipidemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, porphyria, metabolic disorders, persistent pruritus during pregnancy, herpes gestationis, pemphigoid gestationis, otosclerosis, if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are a nursing mother, have a pituitary tumor, galactorrhea, hyperprolactinemia, migraine with aura such as scotoma or scintillating scotoma, valvular heart disease complicated with pulmonary hypertension or atrial fibrillation, pancreatitis, valvular heart disease with a history of subacute bacterial endocarditis, diabetes mellitus with vascular lesions, headache with focal neurological symptoms such as aura, are taking direct-acting antiviral agents for hepatitis C, have congenital or acquired hypercoagulability, are a smoker aged 35 or older, or have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a smoker, have hypertension, are obese, are a woman over 40 years old, have epilepsy, migraine, a history of arterial disease or varicose veins, systemic lupus erythematosus, a history of breast cancer, depression or a history of depression, heart or kidney failure, atypical mammary epithelial hyperplasia, a history of breast nodules, valvular heart disease, atrial fibrillation, a history of hyperlipidemia, gallbladder disease and a history of gallstones, endometriosis, asthma or chloasma, peripheral circulatory disorder, diabetes mellitus, or migraine without aura. This medicine does not prevent HIV infection (AIDS) or other sexually transmitted diseases. If you have a severe gastrointestinal disorder, absorption may be incomplete, so use an additional contraceptive method. This medicine is not indicated for pre-menarcheal women and post-menopausal women. For detailed precautions, refer to the detailed information in the marketing authorization.
