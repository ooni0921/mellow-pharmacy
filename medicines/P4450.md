### 💊 하이카디정 - 효능 및 효과
* 이 약은 임신ㆍ수유기, 발육기, 노년기의 비타민 D의 보급과 뼈, 이의 발육불량, 구루병의 예방과 칼슘결핍 및 기타 칼슘의 보급에 사용합니다.

### 🕒 하이카디정 - 용법 및 용량
* 성인 및 만 8세 이상의 어린이는 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

### ⚠️ 하이카디정 - 주의사항
* 정보 없음
* 이 약에 과민증 환자, 만 12개월 미만의 젖먹이, 고칼슘혈증(혈액중에 칼슘이 과잉으로 존재하는 상태), 유육종증(원인을 알 수 없는 전신 염증질환), 신장질환, 신장결석, 심한 증상의 신부전, 갈락토오스 불내성, Lapp 유당분해효소 결핍증 또는 포도당-갈락토오스 흡수장애 등의 유전적인 문제가 있는 환자는 이 약을 복용하지 마십시오.

#EN
### 💊 Haicardi Tab. - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supply of Vitamin D during pregnancy and lactation, growth period, and old age; prevention of poor development of bones and teeth and rickets; and the supply of calcium for calcium deficiency and other calcium needs.

### 🕒 Haicardi Tab. - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 8 years or older should take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ Haicardi Tab. - Precautions
* No information available
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, infants under 12 months, patients with hypercalcemia (a state where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal failure, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.

Before taking this medicine, children who get plenty of sunlight and have normal diets, pregnant women and women who may be pregnant, nursing mothers, premature infants, infants, patients with cardiovascular dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, individuals with a history of kidney stones, or patients or individuals with a history of hypersensitivity to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for more than 1 month, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
