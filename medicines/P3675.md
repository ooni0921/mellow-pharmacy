# EN

### 💊 코스빌연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used as an adjunct in the treatment of chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation after trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 코스빌연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 capsule (300 mg) at a time, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 capsules (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 코스빌연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

When taking this medicine, there is an increased risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medicine, a patient with or a history of gastrointestinal ulceration, a patient with gastrointestinal, cerebrovascular, or other bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, a patient with or a history of bronchial asthma, a patient with a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), a patient undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, a pregnant woman in the third trimester (6 months or more), a breastfeeding woman, a patient with inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or a patient with a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous NSAID therapy.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders or a history thereof, a bleeding tendency, hepatic impairment or a history thereof, renal impairment or a history thereof, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, a history of hypersensitivity, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), if you are elderly, a child over 6 years old, a patient with gastric cancer, an alcoholic, a pregnant woman in the first or second trimester, or a woman who may be pregnant, a patient with ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgical operations resulting in blood loss, have coagulation disorders or are receiving anticoagulants, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5).

Considering the importance of drug administration, either discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue drug administration.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medicine, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

In principle, for acute diseases, long-term use should be limited to within 5 days.

服用本药前，以下患者请咨询医生或药剂师：血液异常患者或有血液异常史者，有出血倾向的患者，肝功能障碍患者或有肝功能障碍史者，肾功能障碍患者或有肾功能障碍史者，体液潴留或心力衰竭患者，高血压患者，有过敏史者，系统性红斑狼疮(SLE)及混合性结缔组织病(MCTD)患者，老年患者，6岁以上儿童，胃癌患者，酒精中毒患者，妊娠初期、中期孕妇及可能怀孕的女性，缺血性心脏病患者，外周动脉疾病患者，脑血管疾病患者，有心血管疾病风险因素的患者（例如高血压、高脂血症、糖尿病、吸烟），肝性卟啉病患者，导致失血的重大外科手术患者，有凝血障碍或正在接受抗凝剂治疗的患者，或对黄色5号（日落黄）有过敏或过敏反应史者。
