#EN
### 💊 치스민캡슐(디오스민) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the improvement of symptoms related to venous insufficiency (such as leg heaviness, pain), as an adjuvant treatment for disorders caused by capillary fragility, and for the treatment of signs associated with hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 치스민캡슐(디오스민) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 1 capsule (300 mg) twice daily (with lunch and dinner).

In cases of recurrence or exacerbation of hemorrhoids, up to 4 to 6 capsules (1,200 to 1,800 mg) per day can be taken.

The dosage can be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 치스민캡슐(디오스민) - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to it or if you are a nursing mother.

Pregnant women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.

Do not administer for a prolonged period.

If symptoms do not improve quickly, a proctological examination and reconsideration of treatment are necessary.

*   本药用于改善与静脉功能不全相关的症状（如腿部沉重感、疼痛等）、作为毛细血管脆弱引起的疾病的辅助治疗，以及治疗与痔疮相关的症状。

### 🕒 치스민캡슐(디오스민) - 用法及用量
*   成人每次1粒（300毫克），每日2次（午餐、晚餐时）服用。

痔疮复发或恶化时，每日可服用4至6粒（1,200至1,800毫克）。

可根据年龄、症状适当增减。

*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者、哺乳期妇女请勿服用本药。

孕妇在服用本药前应咨询医生或药剂师。

请勿长期给药。

如果症状未迅速改善，则需要进行肛门检查并重新考虑治疗方案。
