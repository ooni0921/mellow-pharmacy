#EN
### 💊 에치시드정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and acid belching.

### 🕒 에치시드정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 3 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 에치시드정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you are undergoing dialysis therapy or are a child under 7 years old.
*   Patients with kidney impairment should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
*   Follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN
### 💊 에치시드정 - 功效与作用
*   本药品用于缓解胃酸过多、胃灼热、胃部不适、胃部饱胀感、消化不良、恶心、呕吐、胃痛、酸嗝。

### 🕒 에치시드정 - 用法与用量
*   成人：每次3片，每日3次，饭后服用。

*   接受透析治疗的患者、7岁以下的儿童请勿服用本药品。
*   肾功能障碍患者在服用本药品前应咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法与用量。
