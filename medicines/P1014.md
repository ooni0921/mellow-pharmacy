# EN
### 💊 화인코프에이캡슐 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for cough and phlegm.

### 🕒 화인코프에이캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 and over: Take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.
* Children aged 8-14: Take 1 capsule per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 화인코프에이캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient with or have experienced hypersensitivity to this medicine, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, an infant under 3 months of age, or a patient with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are a lactating woman, a child under 2 years of age, an elderly person, a frail individual, a person with an allergic constitution, a person who has experienced allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), a patient with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty urinating, high fever, a smoker, or a patient with asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* 对本药有过敏史或过敏经验的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、未满3个月的婴儿、患有半乳糖不耐症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收障碍等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

如果服用5-6次后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
