### 💊 Cureco Plus Soft Capsule - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms such as runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, and muscle pain.

### 🕒 Cureco Plus Soft Capsule - Dosage and Administration
* For adults aged 15 years and older, take 1 capsule at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ Cureco Plus Soft Capsule - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if a regular drinker (consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily) takes this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. If a skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP) occur, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine or its components, if you have experienced asthma after taking this medicine, its components, other antipyretic analgesics, or cold medicines, if you are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 2 years of age; an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; have an allergic constitution; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are breastfeeding; experience symptoms of ischemic colitis such as sudden abdominal pain or rectal bleeding related to pseudoephedrine; have experienced allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching) due to medication; are elderly; are frail or have a high fever; or have a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF).

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have: liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, peptic ulcer, glaucoma, or difficulty urinating; if you have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; or if you are a smoker, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting more than one week or that recurs, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not take for a prolonged period.

Drowsiness may occur during use. Avoid driving a car or operating machinery.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

アセトアミノフェンで1日最大用量（4,000 mg）を超えて服用しないでください。肝障害を引き起こす可能性があります。アセトアミノフェンを含む他の製品と併用しないでください。

急性汎発性発疹性膿疱症のような重症皮膚異常反応が現れた場合は、服用を中止し、医師または薬剤師に相談してください。

本剤を服用する前に、以下の場合は医師または薬剤師に相談してください：肝臓疾患、腎臓疾患、心臓疾患、甲状腺疾患、糖尿病、高血圧、胃十二指腸潰瘍、緑内障、排尿困難などがある方；胸焼け、胃部不快感、胃痛のような胃腸の問題が持続または再発している方、または潰瘍、出血の問題を抱えている方；喫煙者、喘息、慢性気管支炎、肺気腫、過剰な痰を伴う咳、1週間以上続くまたは再発する咳、慢性的な咳、発熱および発疹または持続的な頭痛を伴う咳がある方。
