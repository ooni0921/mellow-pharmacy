#EN
### 💊 임팩셀연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of physical fatigue; during pregnancy and lactation; for physical weakness during or after illness (during or after recovery); for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, and B6 in the elderly; for the alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/tingling in hands and feet, and coldness in hands and feet (cold hands and feet) associated with peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause; for neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness); for the alleviation of symptoms of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis; for beriberi; for eye fatigue; for muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency; and for selenium supplementation.

### 🕒 임팩셀연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 12 years and over: Take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 임팩셀연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medication or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts; an infant under 12 months of age; or a patient with severe renal insufficiency.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a pregnant or potentially pregnant woman, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis (appearing in specific parts); a patient with cardiovascular or circulatory system dysfunction; a patient with renal impairment; a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis; a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions; a patient for whom special risks are expected due to fat overload; or a patient with a predisposition to thrombosis.

Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
