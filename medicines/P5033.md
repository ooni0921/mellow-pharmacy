# 비오라민골드정

### 💊 효능 및 효과 (Indications and Effects)

**EN**
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins E, B1, B2, B6, C in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, during illness or convalescence after illness, and in old age.
*   This medicine, through its contained vitamins, is used for the relief of joint pain symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, lower back pain, or stiff shoulders, as well as for beriberi and eye fatigue.

**CN**
*   本药用于补充维生素 E、B1、B2、B6、C，适用于身体疲劳、妊娠及哺乳期、疾病期间或病后恢复期的体力下降，以及老年期。
*   本药通过所含维生素等，用于缓解神经痛、肌肉痛、腰痛或肩周炎等关节疼痛症状，以及脚气病、眼睛疲劳。

**JP**
*   本剤は、肉体疲労、妊娠授乳期、病中病後の体力低下時、老年期におけるビタミンE、B1、B2、B6、Cの補給に用います。
*   本剤は、含有されるビタミン等を通じて、神経痛、筋肉痛、腰痛や肩こりなどの関節痛症状の緩和、および脚気、眼精疲労に用います。

### ⚠️ 주의사항 (Precautions)

**EN**
*   No information
*   Do not administer this medicine to patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine or to infants under 3 months of age.
*   Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, lactating women, premature infants, infants, patients with hyperoxaluria, gout, or kidney stones, or patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF) should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after about 1 month of administration, immediately discontinue this medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Patients with a thrombotic predisposition or folic acid deficiency should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection in various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

**CN**
*   无信息
*   对本药过敏的患者，以及未满3个月的婴儿请勿服用本药。
*   孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、高草酸尿症患者、痛风或肾结石患者，以及对黄色5号（日落黄FCF）有过敏史或过敏经验的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   服用1个月左右后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用本药并咨询医生或药师。
*   有血栓倾向的患者，以及叶酸缺乏的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测，并可能使尿液变黄，从而影响临床检查结果。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
