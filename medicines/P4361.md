#EN
### 💊 팜페인이부연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for fever and pain due to the common cold, back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), post-operative pain, headache, migraine, toothache, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 팜페인이부연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, for rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, take 1 to 3 capsules (200-600 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day, up to a maximum of 16 capsules (3,200 mg) per day. For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or common cold, adults take 1 to 2 capsules (200-400 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day. For migraine, adults take 1 to 2 capsules (200-400 mg) at a time, not exceeding 2 capsules within a 24-hour period.

Adolescents 12 years of age and older take 1 to 2 capsules (200-400 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day.

If body weight is less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg. It is advisable to avoid taking this medication on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 팜페인이부연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulcer, gastrointestinal bleeding, severe blood disorder, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma, a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other NSAIDs, a patient experiencing pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, a pregnant woman in the last trimester (last 3 months), a nursing mother, or a patient receiving high-dose methotrexate as chemotherapy.
Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders, a bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, cardiac insufficiency, heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly, a child, pregnant, nursing, or taking low-dose aspirin.

When taking for a common cold, generally do not take for more than 5 days. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children) without a doctor's or pharmacist's instruction, and do not take for fever for more than 3 days.

### 🕒 팜페인이부연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 用法与用量
* 成人用于类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、软组织损伤、非关节风湿性疾病、急性痛风、银屑病性关节炎时，一次服用1~3粒（200~600 mg），每日3~4次，每日最高剂量可达16粒（3,200 mg）。用于幼年（儿童或青少年期发生的）类风湿性关节炎时，每日按体重每公斤30~40 mg，分3~4次服用。轻度至中度疼痛或感冒，成人一次服用1~2粒（200~400 mg），每日3~4次。偏头痛，成人一次服用1~2粒（200~400 mg），24小时内不得超过2粒。

12岁及以上青少年，一次服用1~2粒（200~400 mg），每日3~4次。

体重低于30 kg者，每日剂量不得超过500 mg。建议避免空腹服用。

### ⚠️ 팜페인이부연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 注意事项

感冒时服用，原则上不超过5天。未经医生或药师指导，成人疼痛服用不得超过10天，儿童不得超过5天；发热服用不得超过3天。

### 🕒 팜페인이부연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 用法・用量
* 成人は、リウマチ性関節炎、変形性関節症、強直性脊椎炎、軟部組織損傷、非関節リウマチ性疾患、急性痛風、乾癬性関節炎には、1回1～3カプセル（200～600 mg）を1日3～4回服用し、1日最高16カプセル（3,200 mg）まで服用できます。若年性（幼少期または若い年齢で発症する）リウマチ性関節炎には、1日体重1kgあたり30～40 mgを3～4回に分けて服用します。軽度および中程度の疼痛または風邪には、成人1回1～2カプセル（200～400 mg）を1日3～4回服用します。片頭痛には、成人1回1～2カプセル（200～400 mg）を服用し、24時間で2カプセルを超えないようにします。

12歳以上の青少年は、1回1～2カプセル（200～400 mg）を1日3～4回服用します。

体重が30 kg未満の場合、1日量が500 mgを超えないようにし、空腹時の服用は避けることが望ましいです。
