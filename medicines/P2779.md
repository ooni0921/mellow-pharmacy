#EN

### 💊 나오덤크림(구아야줄렌) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for eczema, burns (thermal injury), and erosion and ulcers caused by other diseases.

### 🕒 나오덤크림(구아야줄렌) - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area several times a day.

### ⚠️ 나오덤크림(구아야줄렌) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to it. Before using this medicine, patients with hypersensitivity or a history of hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
