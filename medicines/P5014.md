#EN
### 💊 신카인겔(수출명: 태그가드(TAGuard)) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for the temporary relief and disinfection of pain, itching, swelling (edema), and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 신카인겔(수출명: 태그가드(TAGuard)) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents aged 15 years and older should apply directly to the affected area 1 to 3 times daily.

### ⚠️ 신카인겔(수출명: 태그가드(TAGuard)) - Precautions
* Patients with heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, or urination difficulty due to prostatic hyperplasia should not use this drug.
* Before using this drug, patients with a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not use in children.
* This drug is for anal use only; do not use for ophthalmic purposes.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of use, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
