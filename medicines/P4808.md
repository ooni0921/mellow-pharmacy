#EN

### 💊 세비타비겔(니코틴산아미드) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the topical treatment of mild to moderate inflammatory acne.

### 🕒 세비타비겔(니코틴산아미드) - Dosage and Administration
*   Cleanse the affected area thoroughly, then apply a sufficient amount to the affected area twice daily, in the morning and evening.

### ⚠️ 세비타비겔(니코틴산아미드) - Precautions
*   No information available
*   Do not use this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to it, or if you have severe acne.

Pregnant women, women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medication.

Do not use around the eyes or on mucous membranes.

This medication is for external use only.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If excessive dryness, irritation, or skin peeling occurs, reduce the application to once daily or once every two days.

After thoroughly washing the skin with warm water and soap, apply to the affected area.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve within 12 weeks, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### ⚠️ 세비타비겔(니코틴산아미드) - 注意事項
*   情報なし
*   本剤に対し過敏症の患者または既往のある患者、重度のざ瘡患者は本剤を使用しないでください。

本剤を使用する前に、妊婦および妊娠している可能性のある女性、授乳婦は医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。

本剤は外用のみに使用してください。
