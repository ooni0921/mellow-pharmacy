## EN
### 💊 Wonbi Red Liquid - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the replenishment of vitamins B2 and B6, and for general tonic effects (restorative and invigorating), in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy or lactation, and when physical strength is weakened during or after illness (while suffering from an illness or after recovery).

## CN
### 💊 원비레드액 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于身体疲劳、妊娠或哺乳期、病中或病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时，补充维生素 B2、B6，以及滋养强壮。

## JP
### 💊 원비레드액 - 効能及び効果
*   本剤は、肉体疲労、妊娠・授乳期、病中・病後（病気を患っている間や回復後）の体力低下時に、ビタミンB2、B6の補給と滋養強壮に用いられます。

# 원비레드액 - 용법 및 용량

## EN
### 🕒 Wonbi Red Liquid - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children aged 8 years or older: take 1 bottle per dose.
*   For children aged 24 months or older to under 8 years: take 1/2 bottle per dose.
*   Administer twice daily.

## CN
### 🕒 원비레드액 - 用法用量
*   8岁以上及成人：每次1瓶。
*   24个月以上至8岁以下：每次1/2瓶。
*   每日服用2次。

## JP
### 🕒 원비레드액 - 用法及び用量
*   8歳以上および成人は1回1瓶、生後24ヶ月以上8歳未満は1回1/2瓶を、1日2回服用してください。

# 원비레드액 - 주의사항

## EN
### ⚠️ Wonbi Red Liquid - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not administer this medicine to patients with hypersensitivity to this product, or to infants under 3 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding women, premature infants, and young children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and may affect clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
