### 💊 엠피록스액(시클로피록스올아민) - Indications and Effects
*   This medicine is used for seborrheic dermatitis.

### 🕒 엠피록스액(시클로피록스올아민) - Dosage and Administration
*   This medicine can be used 2-3 times a week or as needed for the treatment of scalp conditions.

After wetting the hair with water, apply a sufficient amount to create lather, and massage thoroughly with fingertips.

After 3-5 minutes, rinse the hair thoroughly with water several times to completely wash it off.

### ⚠️ 엠피록스액(시클로피록스올아민) - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine or any of its ingredients should not use this medicine.

Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you: have a history of allergic reactions (e.g., rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by poison ivy, etc.) to this medicine or its ingredients; have an allergic predisposition yourself or in your family; have affected areas on the face or extensive affected areas; have purulent affected areas; are pregnant or breastfeeding; are under 12 years of age; or are currently receiving medical treatment.

This medicine is for external use only on the scalp and adjacent areas.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

Do not apply to the following areas. If it accidentally gets into these areas, rinse thoroughly with plenty of water and consult a doctor or pharmacist if irritation persists:
Eyes or around the eyes, mucous membranes (e.g., oral cavity, nasal cavity, vagina, etc.), genitals, vulva, etc.
Affected areas with severe eczema, wetness, erosion, cracks, or trauma (wounds).
Do not use if the affected area or its surroundings are dirty. If it accidentally gets into these areas, wash thoroughly with plenty of water and consult a doctor or pharmacist if irritation persists.

When using for children, use under the supervision of a guardian.

Keep away from fire.

### 💊 엠피록스액(시클로피록스올아민) - 功效与作用
*   本品用于治疗脂溢性皮炎。

### 🕒 엠피록스액(시클로피록스올아민) - 用法用量
*   本品可每周使用2-3次，或在治疗头皮疾病时按需使用。

用水润湿头发后，取足量本品至产生泡沫，用指尖充分按摩。

3-5分钟后，用清水多次冲洗头发，直至完全洗净。

### ⚠️ 엠피록스액(시클로피록스올아민) - 注意事项
*   无可用信息。
*   对本品或其任何成分过敏的患者禁用本品。

在使用本品前，请咨询医生或药剂师，如果您：对本品或其成分有药物过敏史（例如皮疹、发红、瘙痒、漆树皮炎等）；本人或家族有过敏体质；患处在面部或范围广泛；患处有化脓；孕妇及哺乳期妇女；12岁以下儿童；或正在接受医生治疗。

本品仅限外用于头皮及邻近部位。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

请勿用于以下部位。如果不慎进入这些部位，请用大量清水彻底冲洗，如果刺激持续，请咨询医生或药剂师：
眼睛或眼周、黏膜（例如口腔、鼻腔、阴道等）、阴部、外阴等。
患有严重湿疹、湿润、糜烂、皲裂或外伤（伤口）的患处。
如果患处或其周围脏污，请勿使用。如果不慎进入这些部位，请用大量清水彻底冲洗，如果刺激持续，请咨询医生或药剂师。

儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。

请远离火源。
