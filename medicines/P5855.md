#EN
### 💊 쎌레타민정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins D, B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during and after illness, growth period, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, muscle pain, joint pain (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, beriberi, eye fatigue, and zinc supplementation.

### 🕒 쎌레타민정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 쎌레타민정 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia (a condition where there is an excessive amount of calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, are an infant under 12 months of age, have kidney stones, severe renal failure, a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to soybean oil, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, or genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalate is excreted in the urine), are an infant under 1 year of age, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, cardiovascular dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, or kidney stones or a history of them.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have folic acid deficiency.

When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may affect clinical test values by turning urine yellow.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
