#EN
### 💊 헬봄액 - Indications & Effects
* This medication is used as an adjuvant treatment for liver diseases.

### 🕒 헬봄액 - Dosage & Administration
* Adults should take 10-30 mL daily, divided into doses between meals.
Dilute 10 mL of this medication in about half a glass of water before taking.

### ⚠️ 헬봄액 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with kidney disease, myocardial infarction, or those with a history of myocardial infarction should not take this medication.
Before taking this medication, individuals with caramel hypersensitivity or a history of allergic reactions should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
This medication cannot be used as an antidote for alcohol poisoning.
Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
This medication is for oral use only; do not use it for injection.
If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of use, discontinue immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 헬봄액 - 功效与作用
* 本药物用于肝脏疾病的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 헬봄액 - 用法用量
* 成人每日分次服用10-30毫升，餐间服用。
将本药物10毫升稀释在大约半杯水中服用。

### ⚠️ 헬봄액 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 肾病患者、心肌梗塞患者及曾有心肌梗塞病史者请勿服用本药物。
服用本药物前，对焦糖过敏或有过敏反应史者请咨询医生或药剂师。
本药物不能用作酒精中毒的解毒剂。
请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
本药物仅供口服，不可用于注射。
如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
