#EN
### 💊 이부뉴펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis (occurring in young age), osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), post-operative pain, headaches, migraines, toothaches, muscle pain, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis (dry scaly disease), soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 이부뉴펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults:
    *   For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injury, non-articular rheumatic disease, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, take 1 to 3 capsules (200 to 600 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day, up to a maximum of 16 capsules (3,200 mg) per day.
    *   For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30 to 40 mg per kg of body weight per day, divided into 3 to 4 doses.
    *   For mild to moderate pain or cold symptoms, adults take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day.
    *   For migraines, adults take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) at a time, not exceeding 2 capsules within a 24-hour period.

    Adolescents aged 12 years and older take 1 to 2 capsules (200 to 400 mg) at a time, 3 to 4 times a day.

    If body weight is less than 30 kg, the daily dose should not exceed 500 mg. It is advisable to avoid taking this medicine on an empty stomach.

### ⚠️ 이부뉴펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily and are taking this medicine or other fever reducers/pain relievers. This may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

    Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, which can be fatal. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulcers, and perforation may also occur.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of gastrointestinal ulcers or bleeding, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac insufficiency, severe high blood pressure, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions caused by aspirin or other anti-inflammatory pain relievers, have pain before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, or are in the last three months of pregnancy.

    Do not exceed the recommended dose.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders, bleeding tendencies, liver cirrhosis, liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, cardiac insufficiency, heart disease, high blood pressure, systemic lupus erythematosus, mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, are elderly, a child, pregnant, or breastfeeding.

    When taking for cold symptoms, it should generally be taken within 5 days. Do not take for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), and do not take for fever for more than 3 days without the advice of a doctor or pharmacist.

* 本药用于治疗感冒引起的发热及疼痛、腰痛、痛经、风湿性关节炎、幼年（儿童或青少年期发生的）风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎（退行性关节疾病）、术后疼痛、头痛、偏头痛、牙痛、肌肉痛、神经痛、强直性脊柱炎、急性痛风、银屑病（干性鳞屑病）性关节炎、软组织损伤（扭伤、挫伤）以及非关节性风湿病（肌腱炎、腱鞘炎、滑囊炎）。

### 🕒 이부뉴펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 用法用量
* 成人用量：
    *   风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、软组织损伤、非关节性风湿病、急性痛风、银屑病性关节炎，一次1至3粒（200至600毫克），每日3至4次，每日最高剂量不超过16粒（3,200毫克）。
    *   幼年风湿性关节炎，每日按体重每公斤30至40毫克，分3至4次服用。
    *   轻度及中度疼痛或感冒，成人一次1至2粒（200至400毫克），每日3至4次。
    *   偏头痛，成人一次1至2粒（200至400毫克），24小时内不超过2粒。

    12岁以上青少年，一次1至2粒（200至400毫克），每日3至4次。

    体重低于30公斤者，每日剂量不应超过500毫克。建议避免空腹服用。

### ⚠️ 이부뉴펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 注意事项
* 若每日饮酒三杯以上且正在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能引起胃肠道出血。

    服用本药可能增加致命性心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死、中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡及穿孔等严重胃肠道不良反应。

    请勿超过推荐剂量服用。

    服用本药前，对本药过敏者、有血液异常、出血倾向者、肝硬化、肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、心功能不全、心脏病、高血压、系统性红斑狼疮、混合性结缔组织病、溃疡性结肠炎、克罗恩病、老年人、儿童、孕妇、哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。

    用于感冒时，原则上应在5天内服用。未经医生或药师指导，用于疼痛时不应超过10天（成人）或5天（儿童），用于发热时不应超过3天。

### 🕒 이부뉴펜연질캡슐(이부프로펜) - 用法及び用量

    *   軽度および中等度の疼痛または感冒には、成人1回1～2カプセル（200～400 mg）を1日3～4回服用します。
    *   片頭痛には、成人1回1～2カプセル（200～400 mg）を服用し、24時間で2カプセルを超えないようにします。

    12歳以上の青少年は、1回1～2カプセル（200～400 mg）を1日3～4回服用します。

    体重が30 kg未満の場合、1日量が500 mgを超えてはならず、空腹時の服用は避けることが望ましいです。

* 毎日3杯以上のアルコールを定期的に摂取している方が本薬または他の解熱鎮痛剤を服用する場合は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。胃腸出血を引き起こす可能性があります。

    本薬を服用する前に、過敏症の既往歴のある方、血液異常、出血傾向のある患者、肝硬変、肝機能障害、腎機能障害、心機能不全、心疾患、高血圧、全身性エリテマトーデス、混合性結合組織病、潰瘍性大腸炎、クローン病、高齢者、小児、妊婦、授乳婦は医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。

    感冒に服用する場合は、原則として5日以内に服用し、医師または薬剤師の指示なしに、疼痛には10日以上（成人）または5日以上（小児）、発熱には3日以上服用しないでください。
