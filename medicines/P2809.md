### 💊 명문아스피린장용정100mg - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the inhibition of thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina; inhibition of thrombus formation after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) or percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA); and reduction of cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, diabetes, etc.).

### 🕒 명문아스피린장용정100mg - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 tablet once daily. Since this medication is an enteric-coated tablet, it can be taken before meals with a sufficient amount of water.

### ⚠️ 명문아스피린장용정100mg - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this medication or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, or antirheumatic drugs; have peptic ulcer; have or have experienced aspirin asthma; have hemophilia; have severe liver impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency; are co-administering methotrexate at a dose exceeding 15 mg/week; or are in the third trimester of pregnancy. During the first and second trimesters of pregnancy, do not take this medication unless absolutely necessary. Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (such as renovascular disease, congestive heart failure, fluid depletion, major surgery, sepsis, or major hemorrhagic events), liver impairment, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are a pre-surgical patient, are an infant under 3 years of age, are elderly, have glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, have chickenpox or influenza infection under 14 years of age, or have gout. Due to the risk of bleeding, inform your doctor that you are taking this medication if you are undergoing surgery (including dental surgery). Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this medication.
