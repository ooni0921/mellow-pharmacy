# EN
### 💊 에스부펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used as an adjunct to treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, post-traumatic and post-operative painful swelling or inflammation, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 에스부펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take one tablet (300 mg) per dose, 2 to 4 times a day. However, do not exceed 4 tablets (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 에스부펜정(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
*   Regular drinkers consuming three or more alcoholic drinks daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this drug or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

When taking this drug, the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic events, myocardial infarction, and stroke is increased. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation may also occur.
*   Do not take this drug if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this drug; have or have a history of gastrointestinal ulcers; have bleeding in the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular system, or other sites; severe blood disorders; severe hepatic impairment; severe renal impairment; severe cardiac insufficiency; severe hypertension; bronchial asthma or a history thereof; a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery; are pregnant in the last trimester (6 months or more); are breastfeeding; have inflammatory bowel diseases such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis; or have a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous NSAID treatment.

Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with or a history of blood disorders; have a tendency to bleed; have or have a history of hepatic impairment; have or have a history of renal impairment; have fluid retention or heart failure; have hypertension; have a history of hypersensitivity; have systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD); are elderly; are a child aged 6 years or older; have gastric cancer; are an alcoholic; are pregnant in the first or second trimester or a woman who may be pregnant; have ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease; have risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes mellitus, smoking); have hepatic porphyria; have undergone major surgery resulting in blood loss; or have blood coagulation disorders or are receiving anticoagulants.

Considering the importance of drug administration, either discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue drug administration.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this drug, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

For acute conditions, long-term administration should generally be limited to within 5 days.

# CN

*   本药用于辅助治疗慢性多发性关节炎、类风湿性关节炎、骨关节炎、强直性脊柱炎、外伤及术后疼痛性肿胀或炎症，以及伴有炎症、疼痛和发热的感染症。

### 🕒 에스부펜정(덱시부프로펜) - 用法与用量
*   成人每次服用1片(300毫克)，每日2~4次。但每日不得超过4片(1,200毫克)。

### ⚠️ 에스부펜정(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项
*   每日饮酒三杯以上的定期饮酒者，在服用本药或其它解热镇痛药时，请咨询医生或药师。这可能会引起胃肠道出血。

服用本药时，可能会增加致命性严重心血管血栓反应、心肌梗死和中风的风险，并可能出现胃肠道出血、溃疡和穿孔等严重的胃肠道不良反应。
*   对本药过敏的患者；有胃肠道溃疡或病史者；胃肠道、脑血管或其他部位出血者；严重血液异常者；严重肝功能障碍者；严重肾功能障碍者；严重心功能不全者；严重高血压者；有支气管哮喘或病史者；对阿司匹林或其他非甾体抗炎药有哮喘、荨麻疹或过敏反应史者；冠状动脉（心脏动脉）旁路移植术（CABG）前后疼痛治疗患者；妊娠6个月以上的孕妇；哺乳期妇女；患有克罗恩病或溃疡性结肠炎等炎症性肠病患者；以及因以前非甾体抗炎药治疗而有胃肠道出血或穿孔史者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，如您有血液异常或病史；有出血倾向；有肝功能障碍或病史；有肾功能障碍或病史；有体液潴留或心力衰竭；高血压；有过敏史；有系统性红斑狼疮（SLE）和混合性结缔组织病（MCTD）；老年人；6岁以上儿童；胃癌；酒精中毒患者；妊娠早期或中期孕妇及可能怀孕的女性；缺血性心脏病、外周动脉疾病、脑血管疾病；有心血管疾病危险因素的患者（例如，高血压、高脂血症、糖尿病、吸烟）；肝性卟啉病；因失血导致严重外科手术者；有凝血功能障碍或正在接受抗凝剂治疗的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

考虑到药物治疗的重要性，应停止哺乳或停止用药。

服用本药后可能会出现头晕或疲劳，因此在驾驶或操作机械时请注意。

用于急性疾病时，长期服用原则上应在5天以内。
