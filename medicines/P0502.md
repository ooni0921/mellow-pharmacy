# EN

### 💊 하이라제정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, dyspepsia (stomach upset due to overeating), promoting digestion, and abdominal distension due to indigestion.

### 🕒 하이라제정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 15 years and older: Take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 하이라제정 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are a child under 7 years of age, or a patient with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption.
*   Before taking this medicine, patients with allergic predispositions, pregnant women, or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

# CN

### 💊 하이라제정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于消化不良、食欲不振（厌食）、过量饮食、积食（因过食引起的胃部不适）、促进消化、以及因消化不良引起的胃胀。

### 🕒 하이라제정 - 用法用量
*   成人及15岁以上儿童：一次2片，一日3次，饭后服用。

### ⚠️ 하이라제정 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   7岁以下儿童、患有半乳糖不耐受症、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，过敏体质患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性应咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   如果服用本药约2周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
