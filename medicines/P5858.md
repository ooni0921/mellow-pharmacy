#EN
### 💊 Taejeon Wissan - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, acid belching, loss of appetite (anorexia), dyspepsia, overeating, promotion of digestion, and abdominal bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 Taejeon Wissan - Dosage and Administration
* For adults 15 years of age or older, take 1 packet per dose, 3 times a day, after meals or between meals.

### ⚠️ Taejeon Wissan - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient undergoing dialysis therapy or an infant under 3 months of age.

Before taking this medicine, infants under 1 year of age, patients with kidney disorders, individuals with allergic constitution, or those on a sodium-restricted diet should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Please adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
