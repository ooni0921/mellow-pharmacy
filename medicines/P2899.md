#EN
### 💊 티나청포르테점안액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for eye fatigue, conjunctival hyperemia (redness), discomfort after swimming or when dust or sweat enters the eye, eye inflammation caused by ultraviolet rays and other light, eyelid irritation, discomfort when wearing hard contact lenses, itchy eyes, and blurred vision (e.g., when there is excessive eye discharge).

### 🕒 티나청포르테점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops at a time, 5-6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 티나청포르테점안액 - Precautions
* No information
* Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe eye pain, have experienced allergic symptoms to eye drops (e.g., eye redness, itching, swelling, rash, intense hyperemia), or have glaucoma.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Use this medication for ophthalmic instillation only.

Do not use as a contact lens wetting solution for soft contact lenses, nor while wearing soft contact lenses.

Be careful not to let the tip of the container touch your eyelids or eyelashes, as this may cause contamination or cloudiness of the solution by eye discharge or fungi. Do not use if the solution becomes cloudy.

To prevent contamination, avoid sharing the medication with others as much as possible.

If there is no improvement in symptoms after several days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 티나청포르테점안액 - 功效与效果
* 本药用于缓解眼疲劳、结膜充血、游泳后眼部不适或当灰尘或汗水进入眼睛时引起的紫外线及其他光线引起的眼部炎症、眼睑溃烂、佩戴硬性隐形眼镜时的不适感、眼睛瘙痒、视物模糊（如眼屎过多时）。

### 🕒 티나청포르테점안액 - 用法用量
* 一次滴2～3滴，一日5～6次。

### ⚠️ 티나청포르테점안액 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 在使用本药前，严重眼痛者、有过眼药水过敏症状（例：眼部充血、瘙痒、肿胀、皮疹、充血发红等）者、青光眼患者请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法用量。

本药仅供滴眼使用。

请勿用作软性隐形眼镜的润湿液，也请勿在佩戴软性隐形眼镜时使用。

请注意，如果容器尖端触及眼睑及睫毛，药液可能会被眼屎或霉菌等污染或变得浑浊（模糊），一旦浑浊请勿使用。

为防止污染，请尽量避免与他人共用。

若使用数日后症状仍无改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
