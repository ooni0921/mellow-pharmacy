#EN
### 💊 자하보액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for general tonic, weak constitution, physical fatigue, post-illness physical weakness, loss of appetite, nutritional disorders, and febrile/wasting diseases.

### 🕒 자하보액 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 bottle per dose, once daily.

### ⚠️ 자하보액 - Precautions
* As this product is manufactured using human placenta as a raw material, take it only when its benefits outweigh the risks.
* Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not take it.

Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.

This medication is for oral use only; do not use it for injection.

If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication for a certain period, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

It may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

#CN
### 💊 자하보액 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于滋养强壮、体质虚弱、身体疲劳、病后体力下降、食欲不振、营养障碍、发热性·消耗性疾病。

### 🕒 자하보액 - 用法用量
* 成人一次服用1瓶，一日1次。

### ⚠️ 자하보액 - 注意事项
* 本品以人胎盘为原料制造，因此仅在其益处大于风险时方可服用。
* 对本药过敏的患者请勿服用。

孕妇、哺乳期妇女在服用本药前请咨询医生或药师。

本药仅供口服，请勿用于注射。

若服用一定期间后症状未见改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。

本药可能使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检查结果。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
