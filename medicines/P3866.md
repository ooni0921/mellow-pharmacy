### 💊 치클리스연고 - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids, and for disinfection.

### 🕒 치클리스연고 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, apply directly to the affected area or inject rectally, up to 3 times a day.
* When using for injection, attach the applicator to the tube and insert it into the rectum.

### ⚠️ 치클리스연고 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not use this medicine if you have heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, difficulty urinating due to prostate enlargement, or if you are a child.
* Before using this medicine, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and patients hypersensitive to propylene glycol, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.
* This medicine is for anal use only and should not be used ophthalmically.
* If symptoms do not improve after about 1 week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
