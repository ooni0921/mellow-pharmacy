*   This medication is used for pain accompanied by sudden muscle spasms.

### 🕒 다푸러스액(작약감초탕) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 packet (single dose) at a time, 3 times a day, before meals or between meals.

### ⚠️ 다푸러스액(작약감초탕) - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you have a heart condition or a history of heart disease, or if you are an infant under 1 year of age.

Before taking this medication, patients with high blood pressure, kidney disorders, or edema, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, children, and the elderly should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Take this medication only when symptoms are present, and do not continue to take it.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
