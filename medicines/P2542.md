### 🕒 자임큐텐연질캡슐 - 용법 및 용량
* 12세 이상 및 성인 1회 1캡슐씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

* 비타민 A를 1일 5,000 IU 이상 복용 시 선천성 기형을 일으킬 수 있습니다. 임신 3개월 이내 또는 임신하고 있을 가능성이 있는 여성은 복용하지 마십시오.

# EN
### 💊 자임큐텐연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins A, C, B1, and E during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of reduced physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), and in old age. It is also used for the alleviation of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), hyperpigmentation (melasma, freckles) caused by sunlight or skin diseases, prevention of gum bleeding and epistaxis (nosebleeds), and the alleviation of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness in hands/feet, and cold hands/feet due to peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause.

### 🕒 자임큐텐연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and children 12 years of age and older, take 1 capsule once daily.

### ⚠️ 자임큐텐연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day can cause congenital malformations. Women who are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or who may be pregnant should not take this medicine.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, an infant under 12 months of age, or hypersensitive to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in urine), are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, are a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcers, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, heart/circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones, hyperlipoproteinemia, hyperlipidemia in diabetic patients, and lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, patients with a predisposition to thrombosis, or patients hypersensitive to Yellow No. 5. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood sugar during various urine tests.

# CN
### 💊 자임큐텐연질캡슐 - 功效与效果
*   本药用于补充体力疲劳、妊娠ㆍ哺乳期、病中ㆍ病后（患病期间或康复后）体力下降时以及老年期的维生素A、C、B1、E，并缓解眼睛干燥感、夜盲症（夜间视力不佳的症状）、因阳光ㆍ皮肤病等引起的色素沉着（黄褐斑、雀斑），预防牙龈出血ㆍ鼻出血（鼻血），以及缓解末梢血液循环障碍和更年期时出现的肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木ㆍ手脚冰凉等症状。

### 🕒 자임큐텐연질캡슐 - 用法与用量
*   12岁以上及成人，一次1粒，一日1次。

### ⚠️ 자임큐텐연질캡슐 - 注意事项
*   每日服用超过5,000 IU的维生素A可能导致先天性畸形。妊娠3个月以内或可能怀孕的女性请勿服用本药。
*   对本药过敏的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、对大豆油或大豆或花生过敏的患者请勿服用本药。在服用本药前，如有高草酸尿症（尿中排出过量草酸盐的状态）、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病、心血管功能障碍、肾功能障碍、低蛋白血症、痛风、肾结石、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者、有血栓形成倾向的患者、对黄色5号过敏的患者，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。本药可能干扰各种尿液检查中血糖的检测。
