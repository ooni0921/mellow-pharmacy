### 💊 이지콜드플러스연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 이지콜드플러스연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years or older and adults, take 2 capsules at a time, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 이지콜드플러스연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker (three or more drinks daily) and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

    Serious skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

    Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

    If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a hypersensitivity to this medicine or have experienced it, have had asthma triggered by other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months old, have severe or uncontrolled high blood pressure, or are in the 30th week of pregnancy or later.

    Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant aged 3 months or older and under 2 years, an infant or child under 15 years old infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, have an allergic constitution, have experienced drug-induced allergies, have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastric/duodenal ulcer, glaucoma (eye pain, blurred vision, etc.), difficulty urinating, are elderly, weak, or have a high fever, have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough with excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

    Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

    Do not take continuously for a long period.

    Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

    Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

    If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.
