### 💊 Skibibackjeong - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used to supplement vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, and C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of decreased physical strength during or after illness (during illness or after recovery), growth periods, and old age. It is also used for poor development of bones and teeth, prevention of rickets, relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), angular stomatitis (cheilitis at the corners of the mouth), cheilitis (lip inflammation), stomatitis (mouth inflammation), glossitis (tongue inflammation), eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, eye fatigue, and supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 Skibibackjeong - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years and older: Take 1 tablet once daily after a meal.

### ⚠️ Skibibackjeong - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have hypercalcemia (a condition where calcium is excessively present in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, or kidney stones.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets plenty of sunlight and has normal meals, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing mother, a premature infant, an infant/toddler, or if you have hyperoxaluria (a condition where an excessive amount of oxalates are excreted in the urine), cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, hypoproteinemia, gout, a thrombotic predisposition, or folate deficiency.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with glucose detection during various urine tests and may affect clinical test results by turning the urine yellow.
