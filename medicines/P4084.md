#EN
### 💊 속이쿨정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach bloating, indigestion (food stagnation), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, dyspepsia, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and abdominal distension due to indigestion.

### 🕒 속이쿨정 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times daily, after each meal.

### ⚠️ 속이쿨정 - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not administer this medication to patients with hereditary problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, children (under 15 years of age), pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and nursing mothers.

Before administering this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of allergic reactions, renal impairment, hepatic impairment, hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to Tartrazine (Yellow No. 4), high blood pressure, are elderly, or have cardiac or renal disorders, or edema.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
