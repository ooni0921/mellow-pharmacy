# EN
### 💊 콜대원콜드시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, cough, phlegm, sore throat, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 콜대원콜드시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older: 1 sachet (20 mL) per dose. For individuals aged 11 years or older and under 15 years: 2/3 sachet per dose. For individuals aged 7 years or older and under 11 years: 1/2 sachet per dose. For individuals aged 3 years or older and under 7 years: 1/3 sachet per dose. For individuals aged 2 years or older and under 3 years: 1/4 sachet per dose. Take 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 콜대원콜드시럽 - Precautions
* Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN) have been reported very rarely and can be fatal in patients taking acetaminophen. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have experienced hypersensitivity to this medicine, have a history of asthma caused by other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or a child under 2 years of age.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: an infant or child under 15 years of age infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; a child under 2 years of age (only if absolutely necessary); individuals with a predisposition to urticaria, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migraine, food allergies, etc.; individuals with liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, or are elderly; individuals with gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty urinating, or are frail; individuals with high fever; individuals with persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or with ulcer or bleeding problems; pregnant women or women who may be pregnant; nursing mothers; or individuals with a cough (due to smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not take continuously for an extended period.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.

* 对本药有过敏史或过敏经验者，服用其他解热镇痛剂或感冒药时曾引发哮喘者，以及3个月以下的婴儿和2岁以下的儿童，请勿服用本药。
