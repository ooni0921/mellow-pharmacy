#EN
### 💊 Sokpyeonhwan - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach pain, sour belching, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, loss of appetite (anorexia), indigestion, overeating, upset stomach, nausea, and vomiting.

### 🕒 Sokpyeonhwan - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years or older: Take 1 packet (20 pills) once.
* For individuals aged 11 years or older and under 15 years: Take 2/3 packet (13 pills) once.
* For individuals aged 8 years or older and under 11 years: Take 1/2 packet (10 pills) once.
* All age groups should take it 3 times a day.
* Maintain an interval of at least 4 hours between doses.

### ⚠️ Sokpyeonhwan - Precautions
* No information provided
* Do not administer this medicine to children under 7 years of age.
* Before taking this medicine, patients with renal impairment, pregnant women, or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Please strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
