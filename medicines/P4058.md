#EN
### 💊 네오크로신연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of mild to moderate pain such as muscle pain, neuralgia, post-traumatic and post-operative pain, headache, toothache, and earache.

### 🕒 네오크로신연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults take 1 to 2 capsules (125-250 mg) once, three times a day.

### ⚠️ 네오크로신연질캡슐(클로닉신리시네이트) - Precautions
* No information available
* Do not take this medication if you have peptic ulcers, gastric/duodenal irritation caused by medication, hypersensitivity to this medication, a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors), are undergoing treatment for pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, have severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe heart failure, or inflammatory bowel diseases such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis.
* Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of peptic ulcers, are a child or an elderly person, have hypertension, fluid retention, or heart failure, have bronchial asthma, have liver impairment or a history thereof, or have kidney impairment or a history thereof.
