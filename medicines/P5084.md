#EN

### 💊 렉센엔좌제 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids, and for disinfection.

### 🕒 렉센엔좌제 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 15 years of age or older should insert 1 suppository rectally, up to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 렉센엔좌제 - Precautions
* No information
* Patients with heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, or difficulty urinating due to prostatic hypertrophy should not use this medication.
* Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medication.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Do not use in children.
* This medication is for rectal use only and should not be taken orally.
* If the suppository becomes soft, use it after cooling it briefly. If it becomes too hard, soften it before use.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 💊 렉센엔좌제 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于暂时缓解肛裂和痔疮引起的疼痛、瘙痒、肿胀和出血，并用于消毒。

### 🕒 렉센엔좌제 - 用法用量
* 15岁及以上的成人，每次1枚，每日不超过3次，插入直肠。

### ⚠️ 렉센엔좌제 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 心脏病、高血压、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、前列腺肥大引起的排尿困难患者，请勿使用本药。
* 孕妇或可能怀孕的女性在使用本药前，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守用法用量。
* 请勿用于儿童。
* 本药仅供直肠使用，不可内服。
* 如果本药变软，请稍作冷却后再使用；如果过硬，请软化后使用。
* 使用约7天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。

* 本剤が軟らかくなった場合は、一時的に冷却してから使用し、また、硬すぎる場合は、軟らかくしてから使用してください。
