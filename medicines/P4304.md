* This medicine is used for shoulder pain associated with stiff shoulders, low back pain, joint pain, muscle pain, tendinitis (pain and swelling in hands, wrists, and ankles), elbow pain (such as tennis elbow), bruises, and sprains.

### 🕒 펠비고쿨플라스타 - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area 1 to 2 times a day.

### ⚠️ 펠비고쿨플라스타 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, have asthma (induced by NSAIDs, etc.) or a history of asthma due to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), have experienced urticaria or acute rhinitis due to aspirin or other NSAIDs, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, or are under 15 years of age.
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist before using this medicine if you are currently receiving medical treatment or have a history of allergic reactions.
* When bathing after applying this medicine, you may experience strong irritation, so remove it at least 30 minutes before bathing or use it at least 30 minutes after bathing.
* Do not warm the application site with a hot pack or electric blanket, etc.
* Do not use continuously for 2 weeks.
* People with sensitive skin should not apply it continuously to the same area.
* If there is no improvement in symptoms after 5-6 days of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Use only when symptoms are present.
* If you are sweating or the affected area is wet, wipe it thoroughly before use.
* People with sensitive skin should, before use, apply a small piece (about 1-2 cm) to a sensitive skin area on the inner arm for at least half a day to confirm that symptoms such as rash, redness, itching, or inflammation do not occur, then proceed with use.
* Do not use around the eyes or on mucous membranes.
* Do not use on eczema, inflammation, athlete's foot, insect bites, purulent areas, or wounds.
