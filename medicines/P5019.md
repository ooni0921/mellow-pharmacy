#EN
### 💊 마그로디안연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Indications
*   This drug is used for:
    *   Supplementation of vitamins B1, B2, B6, E during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of physical decline during or after illness (병중․병후), and in old age.
    *   Relief of beriberi, eye fatigue, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders).
    *   Relief of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.
    *   Relief of peripheral circulatory disorders and symptoms such as stiff shoulders/neck, numbness and coldness in hands and feet (수족저림․수족냉증) during menopause.
    *   Muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그로디안연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 12 years of age and older: Take 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그로디안연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not take this drug if you have or have a history of hypersensitivity to this drug, are an infant under 12 months of age, have severe renal insufficiency, or have or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this drug if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, are lactating, are a premature infant, an infant, have cardiac or circulatory dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, or require careful administration of lipid emulsions, or if a special risk due to fat overload is anticipated.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

This drug may cause urine to turn yellow, which may affect clinical test results.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, discontinue administration immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

    *   缓解末梢血液循环障碍以及更年期时的肩颈僵硬、手脚麻木冰冷（手脚发凉）。
    *   缓解镁缺乏引起的肌肉痉挛。

### 🕒 마그로디안연질캡슐 - 用法用量
*   12岁以上及成人：每次1粒，每日2次。

*   无信息
*   对本药品有过敏症或曾有过敏史的患者、未满12个月的婴儿、重症肾功能不全患者、对大豆油、大豆、花生有过敏症或曾有过敏史的患者，请勿服用本药品。

本药品可能使尿液变黄，影响临床检查结果。

若服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
