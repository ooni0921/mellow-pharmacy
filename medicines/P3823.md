* 1회 2~3방울, 1일 5~6회 점안합니다.

#EN
### 💊 일양투아이블루점안액 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for eye fatigue, conjunctival hyperemia, discomfort after swimming or when dust or sweat enters the eye, eye inflammation caused by ultraviolet and other light rays, blepharitis (soreness of eyelids), discomfort when wearing hard contact lenses, itchy eyes, and blurred vision (e.g., when there is a lot of eye discharge).

### 🕒 일양투아이블루점안액 - Dosage and Administration
* Instill 2-3 drops at a time, 5-6 times a day.

### ⚠️ 일양투아이블루점안액 - Precautions
* Information not available
* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have severe eye pain, have experienced allergic symptoms due to eye drops (e.g., eye redness, itching, swelling, rash, severe conjunctival hyperemia), or have glaucoma.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* Use this medicine for ophthalmic administration only.
* Do not use as a solution for inserting soft contact lenses or while wearing soft contact lenses.
* Ensure the tip of the container does not touch your eyelids or eyelashes, and do not use if the solution is cloudy.
* Do not share this medicine with others.
* If symptoms do not improve after several days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not administer medicine past its expiration date, and use opened containers as soon as possible.

* 本药用于缓解眼部疲劳、结膜充血、游泳后眼部不适或异物（如灰尘、汗水）进入眼睛时引起的不适、紫外线及其他光线引起的眼部炎症、眼睑溃烂（眼睑糜烂）、佩戴硬性隐形眼镜时的不适感、眼睛瘙痒、以及视物模糊（如眼屎过多时）。

### 🕒 일양투아이블루점안액 - 用法与用量
* 每次2~3滴，每日滴眼5~6次。

### ⚠️ 일양투아이블루점안액 - 注意事项
* 暂无信息
* 在使用本药前，如果眼部剧烈疼痛，曾对眼药水有过敏症状（例如：眼部充血、瘙痒、肿胀、皮疹、充血发红等），或患有青光眼，请咨询医生或药师。
* 请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
* 本药仅用于滴眼。
* 请勿用作软性隐形眼镜的润滑液，或在佩戴软性隐形眼镜时使用。
* 容器尖端请勿触及眼睑、睫毛，混浊的药液请勿使用。
* 请勿与他人共用。
* 若使用数日后症状仍无改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
