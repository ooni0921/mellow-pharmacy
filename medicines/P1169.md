#EN
### 💊 티메롤8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Indications and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of fever, pain (ache) due to common cold, headache, toothache, muscle pain, backache, menstrual pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 티메롤8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Dosage and Administration
* Children aged 12 years and older, and adults should take 2 tablets every 8 hours. Do not exceed 6 tablets within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 티메롤8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a regular drinker (three or more alcoholic drinks daily) and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage. Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately. Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, peptic ulcer, severe blood disorders, severe liver dysfunction, severe kidney dysfunction, severe cardiac dysfunction, history of asthma due to aspirin or other anti-inflammatory analgesics, or if you are taking barbiturates, tricyclic antidepressants, or have consumed alcohol, or are under 12 years of age. Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have liver dysfunction, kidney dysfunction, peptic ulcer, blood disorders, bleeding tendency, cardiac dysfunction, a history of hypersensitivity, bronchial asthma, are elderly, pregnant, or breastfeeding. Do not take this medicine for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without the direction of a doctor or pharmacist. This medicine is an extended-release formulation, so do not crush, chew, or dissolve the tablet; swallow it whole.

#CN
### 💊 티메롤8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解发热、感冒引起的疼痛、头痛、牙痛、肌肉痛、腰痛、月经痛和关节痛。

### 🕒 티메롤8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 用法用量
* 12岁及以上儿童和成人每8小时服用2片。24小时内不要超过6片。

### ⚠️ 티메롤8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事项
* 如果您每天饮酒三杯或以上，并且正在服用本药或其他解热镇痛药，请咨询医生或药师。这可能会导致肝损伤。极少数情况下，曾报告过可能致命的严重皮肤反应，如急性泛发性发疹性脓疱病、史蒂文斯-约翰逊综合征和中毒性表皮坏死松解症。如果在服用本药后出现皮疹或其他过敏反应迹象，请立即停止服用。扑热息痛每日最大剂量（4,000毫克）请勿超量服用。这可能会导致肝损伤。请勿与其他含有扑热息痛的产品同时服用。
* 对本药过敏者、消化性溃疡患者、严重血液异常患者、严重肝功能障碍患者、严重肾功能障碍患者、严重心功能不全患者、曾因阿司匹林或其他消炎镇痛药引起哮喘的患者、正在服用巴比妥类药物或三环类抗抑郁药的患者、饮酒者、以及12岁以下儿童，请勿服用本药。在服用本药之前，如果您有肝功能障碍、肾功能障碍、消化性溃疡、血液异常、出血倾向、心功能异常、曾有过敏史、支气管哮喘、老年人、孕妇或哺乳期妇女，请咨询医生或药师。未经医生或药师指示，成人止痛服用不超过10天，儿童止痛不超过5天，退烧服用不超过3天。本药为缓释制剂，请勿压碎、咀嚼或溶解药片，应整片吞服。

### ⚠️ 티메롤8시간이알서방정(아세트아미노펜) - 注意事項
