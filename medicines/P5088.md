### 💊 Metazic Cream - Indications and Effects
* This medication is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin conditions (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, chronic lichen simplex, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis), and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 Metazic Cream - Dosage and Administration
* Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ Metazic Cream - Precautions
* Do not use this medication if you have a hypersensitivity to this drug; bacterial (e.g., tuberculosis, syphilis), fungal (e.g., candidiasis, tinea), spirochete, yeast, viral (e.g., herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia), or animal (e.g., scabies, pubic lice) skin infections; eczematous external otitis with a perforated eardrum; ulcers (excluding Behçet's disease); second-degree deep or higher burns/frostbite; perioral dermatitis; acne vulgaris; rosacea; or a history of hypersensitivity to aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or to bacitracin.

Before using this medication, individuals with a history of or current hypersensitivity to propylene glycol, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, breastfeeding mothers, infants, and children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.

Do not use for purposes other than treatment after applying makeup or shaving.

Do not use occlusive dressing methods unless directed by a doctor.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Do not use continuously for a long period.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 3 to 4 weeks of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 💊 Metazic Cream - 功效与作用
* 本药用于继发感染的过敏性或炎症性皮肤病（湿疹、接触性皮炎、脂溢性皮炎、特应性皮炎、光敏性皮炎、慢性单纯性苔藓、擦烂、剥脱性皮炎、瘙痒、银屑病）和一度烧伤。

### 🕒 Metazic Cream - 用法用量
* 每日1～3次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ Metazic Cream - 注意事项
* 对本药过敏的患者，细菌性（如结核、梅毒等）、真菌性（如念珠菌病、癣等）、螺旋体属、酵母菌、病毒性（如带状疱疹、单纯疱疹、水痘、牛痘等）、动物性（如疥疮、阴虱等）皮肤感染患者，鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳道炎患者，溃疡（白塞病除外），二度深层以上烧伤或冻伤患者，口周皮炎、寻常痤疮、酒渣鼻患者，以及对链霉素、卡那霉素、庆大霉素、新霉素等氨基糖苷类抗生素或杆菌肽有过敏史的患者，请勿使用本药。

在使用本药之前，对丙二醇有过敏史或当前过敏的患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的妇女以及哺乳期妇女、婴幼儿应咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

请勿用于眼科用途。

请勿在化妆或剃须后用于治疗以外的目的。

除非医生指示，请勿使用密封包扎法或敷裹法。

给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导和监督下使用。

请勿长期连续使用。

如果使用3～4周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
