5~6회 복용하여도 증상이 좋아지지 않을 경우 의사 또는 약사와 상의하십시오.

#EN
### 💊 쌩코에스연질캡슐 - Indications & Efficacy
* This medication is used for the relief of various symptoms of the common cold (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, chills [shivering symptoms], fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 쌩코에스연질캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
* Adults and adolescents aged 15 years and older should take 1 capsule per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 쌩코에스연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Regular drinkers who consume three or more alcoholic beverages daily should consult a doctor or pharmacist when taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS), and Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported very rarely. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medication, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg), as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe cutaneous adverse reactions such as Acute Generalized Exanthematous Pustulosis (AGEP) appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity to it, have experienced asthma attacks when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medications, are an infant under 3 months of age, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant/toddler under 2 years of age, an infant infected with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza, a child under 15 years of age, have an allergic predisposition, have a history of drug allergies (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver, kidney, heart, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastrointestinal/duodenal ulcers, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), dysuria (difficulty urinating), are elderly, frail, or have a high fever, have persistent or recurring gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems, are pregnant or may be pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough with excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache, or have a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Carefully follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not consume alcohol during use.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

As drowsiness may occur after taking this medication, exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.
