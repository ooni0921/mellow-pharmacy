# EN
### 💊 조인스탑연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 during physical fatigue, pregnancy and lactation, physical weakness during or after illness (during illness or recovery), and old age. It is also used for the relief of symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness and coldness in hands and feet, neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, shoulder stiffness), angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, beriberi, and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 조인스탑연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children 8 years of age and older: Take 1 capsule once, twice daily.

### ⚠️ 조인스탑연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this drug or to soybean oil, soybeans, or peanuts, or if you are an infant under 3 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant/young child, a patient with hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or lipid metabolism disorders such as pancreatitis, a patient who requires careful administration of lipid emulsions, a patient with special risks expected from fat overload, a patient with a thrombotic predisposition, a patient with folic acid deficiency, or a patient with a history of or current hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   May turn urine yellow and affect clinical test results.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking for about 1 month, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

### 🕒 조인스탑연질캡슐 - 用法及用量

*   给儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导监督下服用。
*   本药可能会使尿液变黄，并可能影响临床检查结果。
*   如果服用约1个月后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
