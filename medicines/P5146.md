#EN
### 💊 마그랑비연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B1, B2, B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, weakened physical strength during or after illness, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of peripheral circulatory disorders and menopausal symptoms such as stiff shoulders/neck, numbness/coldness in hands/feet, relief of neuralgia, myalgia, arthralgia (e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), relief of beriberi and eye fatigue, relief of angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, dermatitis, supplementation of zinc, and muscle cramps due to magnesium deficiency.

### 🕒 마그랑비연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children aged 12 years or older: Take 1 capsule twice daily.

### ⚠️ 마그랑비연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you are a patient hypersensitive to this medicine or its components, an infant under 12 months of age, a patient with severe renal impairment, a patient with a history of hypersensitivity or allergic reaction to soybean oil, or a patient with hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are an infant under 1 year of age, a patient undergoing medical treatment, pregnant or possibly pregnant, a lactating woman, a premature infant, an infant, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with cardiac or circulatory dysfunction, a patient with renal impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a pregnant woman, or a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or a patient requiring cautious administration of lipid emulsions.

Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medicine may cause urine to turn yellow and affect clinical test values.
