#EN
### 💊 디제스콘더블액션현탁액(수출용) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the improvement of acid reflux, heartburn, and indigestion due to reflux of stomach contents (after meals, during pregnancy, or when accompanied by reflux esophagitis).

### 🕒 디제스콘더블액션현탁액(수출용) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and children 12 years of age and older should take 10-20 mL (1-2 sachets) once, four times a day (after meals and at bedtime).

### ⚠️ 디제스콘더블액션현탁액(수출용) - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to it.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient who needs to severely restrict sodium (such as those with congestive heart failure or renal impairment), a patient with hypercalcemia, nephrocalcinosis, recurrent calcium-containing kidney stones, or a child under 12 years of age.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 7 days of administration, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 디제스콘더블액션현탁액(수출용) - 功效与作用
* 本药用于改善胃酸反流、胃灼热以及胃内容物反流引起的消化不良（餐后、妊娠期间、伴有反流性食管炎时）。

* 成人及12岁以上儿童，一次服用10～20 mL（1～2袋），一日4次（饭后及睡前）。

### ⚠️ 디제스콘더블액션현탁액(수출용) - 注意事项
* 信息缺失
* 对本药过敏的患者请勿服用本药。
* 服用本药前，如患有充血性心力衰竭、肾功能障碍等需严格限制钠摄入量的患者，高钙血症、肾钙沉着症、复发性含钙肾结石患者，以及12岁以下儿童，请务必咨询医生或药师。
* 服用约7天后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
