### 💊 현대아라미정 - 효능 및 효과
* 이 약은 육체피로, 임신 및 수유기, 병을 앓는 동안이나 회복 후의 체력 저하, 노년기의 비타민 E, B2, B6의 보급에 사용합니다.

이 약은 함유된 비타민 등을 통해 말초혈행장애 및 갱년기 시 어깨 및 목결림, 수족저림 및 수족냉증 증상의 완화에 사용합니다.

이 약은 함유된 비타민 등을 통해 구각염, 구순염, 구내염, 설염, 습진, 피부염 증상의 완화에 사용합니다.

### 🕒 현대아라미정 - 용법 및 용량
* 만 15세 이상 및 성인은 1회 1정씩, 1일 1회 복용합니다.

#EN
### 💊 현대아라미정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of vitamins E, B2, and B6 in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, decreased physical strength during or after illness recovery, and in old age.
*   This medicine, through its contained vitamins, is used to relieve symptoms such as peripheral circulatory disorders, shoulder and neck stiffness during menopause, numbness in hands and feet, and cold hands and feet.
*   This medicine, through its contained vitamins, is used to relieve symptoms such as angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis.

### 🕒 현대아라미정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults and individuals aged 15 years or older, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ 현대아라미정 - Precautions
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypersensitivity to or experience with soybean oil, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, severe renal insufficiency, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.
*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant, have gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, patients for whom lipid emulsions should be administered cautiously, or patients with fat overload.
*   Patients with a thrombotic predisposition such as vascular obstruction, or those with folic acid deficiency, should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If there is no improvement in symptoms after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   This medicine may turn urine yellow and affect clinical test values.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

### 💊 현대아라미정 - 功效与作用
*   本药用于补充维生素E、B2、B6，适用于身体疲劳、妊娠及哺乳期、患病期间或病后恢复期的体力下降、老年期。
*   本药通过所含维生素等，用于缓解末梢血液循环障碍以及更年期时的肩颈僵硬、手足发麻及手足冰冷等症状。
*   本药通过所含维生素等，用于缓解口角炎、唇炎、口腔炎、舌炎、湿疹、皮肤炎等症状。

### 🕒 현대아라미정 - 用法与用量
*   15岁以上及成人，一次1片，一日1次服用。

### ⚠️ 현대아라미정 - 注意事项
*   对本药、大豆油过敏或有过敏史者、对大豆或花生过敏者、严重肾功能不全患者、未满12个月的婴儿，请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，孕妇及可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、早产儿、婴幼儿、消化性溃疡、慢性溃疡性结肠炎、局限性结肠炎等胃肠道疾病患者、心脏及循环系统功能障碍患者、肾功能障碍患者、低蛋白血症患者、高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症及胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常患者、应慎重给予脂质性乳剂的患者、脂肪超负荷相关患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   有血管堵塞等血栓性素因的患者、叶酸不足的患者，请咨询医生或药师。
*   服用约1个月后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
*   本药可能使尿液呈黄色，并可能影响临床检查结果。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

*   15歳以上および成人は、1回1錠を、1日1回服用してください。
