#EN

### 💊 비맥스에버플러스정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the supplementation of Vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy, lactation, periods of physical decline during or after illness (convalescence), growth periods, and in old age. It is also used for the relief of dry eyes, night blindness (difficulty seeing at night), prevention of poor development of bones and teeth, and rickets. Furthermore, it helps alleviate peripheral circulatory disorders and climacteric symptoms such as stiff shoulders/neck, numbness and coldness in hands/feet. It provides relief from neuralgia, myalgia (muscle pain), arthralgia (joint pain, e.g., lower back pain, stiff shoulders), beriberi, eye strain, and for the supplementation of zinc.

### 🕒 비맥스에버플러스정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Children aged 15 years or older and adults should take 1 tablet once daily after meals.

### ⚠️ 비맥스에버플러스정 - Precautions
*   Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A daily may cause congenital malformations. Do not take this medicine if you are within the first 3 months of pregnancy or are likely to be pregnant.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney disease, kidney stones, severe renal insufficiency, or if you are an infant under 12 months old.

*   Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant under 1 year old, a child who gets a lot of sunlight and has a normal diet, pregnant or possibly pregnant, lactating, a premature infant, an infant/toddler, a patient with hyperoxaluria, a patient with gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with cardiac or circulatory system dysfunction, a patient with renal impairment, a patient with hypoproteinemia, a patient receiving cardiac glycosides, a patient with gout or kidney stones, an individual with a history of kidney stones, a patient with lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, or pancreatitis, or a patient for whom lipid emulsions must be administered cautiously, pregnant, possibly pregnant, lactating, or elderly.

*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, administer under the supervision of a guardian.
*   This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.
