#EN

### 💊 치젤연고 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the temporary relief of pain, itching, swelling, and bleeding associated with anal fissures and hemorrhoids, as well as for disinfection.

### 🕒 치젤연고 - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 15 years and older, apply directly to the affected area or inject rectally, up to 3 times a day. When injecting rectally, attach the applicator to the tube and insert it into the rectum.

### ⚠️ 치젤연고 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not use this medication if you have heart disease, high blood pressure, thyroid disease, diabetes, or difficulty urinating due to prostate enlargement, or if you are a child. Before using this medication, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. This medication is for anal use only and should not be used for ophthalmic purposes. If symptoms do not improve after approximately one week of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 💊 치젤연고 - 功效及效果
* 本药用于暂时缓解肛裂、痔疮引起的疼痛、瘙痒、肿胀、出血以及消毒。

### 🕒 치젤연고 - 用法用量
* 15岁及以上成人，每日最多3次，直接涂抹于患处，或注入直肠内使用。若需直肠内注入，请将注入器安装到软管上，然后插入直肠。

### ⚠️ 치젤연고 - 注意事项
* 无信息。
* 有心脏病、高血压、甲状腺疾病、糖尿病、因前列腺肥大导致排尿困难的患者以及儿童请勿使用本药。孕妇或可能怀孕的女性在使用本药前应咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法用量。本药仅供肛门部位使用，请勿用于眼科。使用约一周后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
