### 🕒 Do Saeng Wu Kong Kao Gyung Go Je - Dosage and Administration
* Clean and dry the affected area, then apply one patch once a day.
* If necessary, secure with a bandage or adhesive plaster.

### ⚠️ Do Saeng Wu Kong Kao Gyung Go Je - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer (urushiol) or other substances, or on wounded areas.

* Before using this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms (e.g., rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.) due to medicines or cosmetics, have an allergic constitution, have severe wetness (moisture retention) or erosion (soreness), are pregnant or possibly pregnant, or are breastfeeding.

* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* When using for children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

* If there is no improvement in symptoms after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

* Avoid long-term use over a wide surface area, as a large amount absorbed through the skin may lead to symptoms of intoxication.

* For external use only.
