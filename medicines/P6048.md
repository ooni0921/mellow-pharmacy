#EN
### 💊 듀얼싹플러스장용연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to relieve constipation and symptoms associated with constipation such as loss of appetite, abdominal distension, abnormal intestinal fermentation, and hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 듀얼싹플러스장용연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* Adults aged 15 years and older should take 1 to 2 capsules once daily at bedtime.

Start with the minimum dose and gradually increase or decrease the amount while observing the shape and condition of the stool.

### ⚠️ 듀얼싹플러스장용연질캡슐 - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this medicine if you have acute abdominal diseases such as appendicitis, intestinal bleeding, or ulcerative colitis, intestinal obstruction, hypersensitivity to soybean oil or a history thereof, or hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child under 10 years old, pregnant or possibly pregnant, experiencing severe abdominal pain or nausea/vomiting, on a sodium-restricted diet, have a history of rectal bleeding or intestinal dysfunction, have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, or patients who need careful administration of lipid emulsions, patients with fat overload, or patients with hypersensitivity to or a history of hypersensitivity to Yellow No. 5 (Sunset Yellow FCF) or Yellow No. 203 (Quinoline Yellow WS).

If constipation does not improve after about 1 week of taking this medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist. Do not take continuously for more than 1 week.

Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.

* 本药用于缓解便秘以及由便秘引起的食欲不振、腹胀、肠道异常发酵、痔疮等症状。

### 🕒 듀얼싹플러스장용연질캡슐 - 用法与用量

初次服用请从最小剂量开始，根据大便的形状和状态，逐渐增减用量。

### ⚠️ 듀얼싹플러스장용연질캡슐 - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 患有急性腹部疾病（如阑尾炎、肠出血、溃疡性结肠炎）、肠梗阻、对大豆油过敏或有过敏史、对大豆或花生过敏的患者，请勿服用本药。

服用本药前，若您是10岁以下儿童、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、有严重腹痛或恶心呕吐症状的患者、正在进行钠限制饮食者、有直肠出血或肠功能不全史者、有高脂蛋白血症、糖尿病性高脂血症和胰腺炎等脂肪代谢异常的患者、需谨慎使用脂质乳剂的患者、脂肪超负荷相关患者、对黄色5号(Sunset Yellow FCF)或黄色203号(Quinoline Yellow WS)过敏或有过敏史的患者，请咨询医生或药师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

* 本剤は、便秘および便秘に伴う食欲不振、腹部膨満、腸内異常発酵、痔の症状緩和に用いられます。

### 🕒 듀얼싹플러스장용연질캡슐 - 用法・用量

初回は少量から服用を開始し、便の形状と状態を見ながら徐々に量を増減してください。
