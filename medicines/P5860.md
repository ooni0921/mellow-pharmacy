#EN
### 💊 담엔쿨정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for neuralgia, lower back pain, and stiff shoulders.

### 🕒 담엔쿨정 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 to 2 tablets at a time, twice daily, when experiencing pain or an attack.

### ⚠️ 담엔쿨정 - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant suspected of having or infected with chickenpox or influenza, an infant or child aged 14 or younger, someone with an allergic constitution, someone who has experienced allergic reactions (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), someone with liver disease, kidney disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, a frail person, someone with a high fever, an elderly person, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a breastfeeding woman.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medication several times, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
