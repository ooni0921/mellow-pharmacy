### 💊 엘씨펜연질캡슐400mg(이부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for fever and pain caused by colds, back pain, menstrual pain, rheumatoid arthritis, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis (degenerative joint disease), postoperative pain, headaches, migraines, toothaches, muscle aches, neuralgia, ankylosing spondylitis, acute gout, psoriatic arthritis, soft tissue injuries (sprains, contusions), and non-articular rheumatic diseases (tendinitis, tenosynovitis, bursitis).

### 🕒 엘씨펜연질캡슐400mg(이부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, soft tissue injuries, non-articular rheumatic diseases, acute gout, and psoriatic arthritis, adults take 1/2 to 1.5 capsules (200-600 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day, with a maximum daily dose of 8 capsules (3,200 mg).

For juvenile rheumatoid arthritis, take 30-40 mg per kg of body weight daily, divided into 3 to 4 doses. For mild to moderate pain or colds, adults take 1/2 to 1 capsule (200-400 mg) once, 3 to 4 times a day.

For migraines, adults take 1/2 to 1 capsule (200-400 mg) once, not exceeding 1 capsule (400 mg) within 24 hours.

Dosage may be adjusted appropriately depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 엘씨펜연질캡슐400mg(이부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medicine may increase the risk of serious, potentially fatal, cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke, and may cause serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions such as gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to this medicine, gastrointestinal ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding or a history thereof, severe blood disorders, severe liver impairment, severe kidney impairment, severe heart failure, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, hypersensitivity (such as asthma, hives, or allergic reactions) to aspirin or other non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (including COX-2 inhibitors) or a history thereof, pain occurring before or after coronary artery bypass graft surgery, or if you are in the last three months of pregnancy.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of hypersensitivity, blood disorders or a history thereof, bleeding tendency, liver cirrhosis and liver impairment or a history thereof, kidney impairment or a history thereof, heart failure or heart disease, hypertension, systemic lupus erythematosus and mixed connective tissue disease, ulcerative colitis, Crohn's disease, if you are elderly or a child, pregnant or breastfeeding, or have a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to Yellow No. 5.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Do not take this medicine for pain for more than 10 days (adults) or 5 days (children), or for fever for more than 3 days, without the advice of a doctor or pharmacist.
