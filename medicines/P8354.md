#EN
### 💊 에프코프시럽 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for cough, phlegm, and asthma.

### 🕒 에프코프시럽 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 15 years of age and older: 10 mL per dose.
* For children 11 years of age and older but under 15 years of age: 6.67 mL per dose.
* For children 8 years of age and older but under 11 years of age: 5 mL per dose.
* For children 5 years of age and older but under 8 years of age: 3.33 mL per dose.
* For children 3 years of age and older but under 5 years of age: 2.5 mL per dose.
* For children 2 years of age and older but under 3 years of age: 2 mL per dose.
* Take 5-6 times a day.

Administer with an interval of approximately 4 hours.

### ⚠️ 에프코프시럽 - Precautions
* No information provided.
* Do not administer this medicine to patients with hypersensitivity to this product or to infants under 3 months of age.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are pregnant or possibly pregnant, breastfeeding, a child aged 3 months or older but under 2 years of age, elderly, frail, have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms (such as fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, high fever, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the supervision of a guardian.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
