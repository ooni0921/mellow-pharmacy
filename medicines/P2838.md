#EN

### 💊 유한아스피린장용정(아스피린) - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used to inhibit thrombus formation in myocardial infarction, cerebral infarction, and unstable angina, to inhibit thrombus formation after Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG) or Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty (PTCA), and to reduce cardiovascular risk in high-risk patients (patients with multiple risk factors such as a family history of ischemic heart disease, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, obesity, diabetes, etc.).

### 🕒 유한아스피린장용정(아스피린) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults should take 1 tablet once a day. As this drug is an enteric-coated tablet, it can be taken before meals with a sufficient amount of water.

### ⚠️ 유한아스피린장용정(아스피린) - Precautions
* No information
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or other salicylates, analgesics, anti-inflammatory drugs, anti-rheumatic drugs, peptic ulcer, aspirin asthma or history thereof, hemophilia, severe liver impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, bleeding tendency, concomitant use of methotrexate at a dose of 15 mg/week or more for one week, or are in the third trimester of pregnancy. If you are in the first or second trimester of pregnancy, do not take this drug unless absolutely necessary. Before using this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have renal impairment, cardiovascular circulatory dysfunction (renal vascular disease, congestive heart failure, fluid depletion, major surgery, sepsis or major bleeding incidents, etc.), liver impairment, cardiac dysfunction, bronchial asthma, are a pre-surgical patient, an infant under 3 years old, an elderly person, a patient with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase (G6PD) deficiency, Reye's syndrome, a patient under 14 years old with chickenpox or influenza infection, or a patient with gout. Inform your doctor if you are taking this drug when undergoing surgery (including dental surgery) due to the risk of bleeding. Be careful not to damage the enteric coating of this drug.

#CN

### 💊 유한아스피린장용정(아스피린) - 功效与效果
* 本药用于抑制心肌梗死、脑梗死、不稳定型心绞痛中的血栓形成，以及冠状动脉旁路移植术 (CABG) 或经皮腔内冠状动脉成形术 (PTCA) 后血栓形成的抑制，并用于减少高危患者（具有缺血性心脏病家族史、高血压、高胆固醇血症、肥胖、糖尿病等复合危险因素的患者）的心血管风险。

### 🕒 유한아스피린장용정(아스피린) - 用法与用量
* 成人每次1片，每日1次。本药为肠溶片，可饭前用足量水送服。

### ⚠️ 유한아스피린장용정(아스피린) - 注意事项
* 无信息
* 对本药或其他水杨酸类制剂、镇痛剂、消炎剂、抗风湿剂有过敏史的患者，消化性溃疡、阿司匹林哮喘或有其病史者，血友病，严重肝功能障碍，严重肾功能障碍，严重心功能不全，有出血倾向者，一周内同时服用15毫克(15 mg/周)以上甲氨蝶呤的患者，以及妊娠3期的孕妇请勿服用。妊娠1期和2期，除非绝对必要，请勿服用本药。在使用本药前，如果您有肾功能障碍、心血管循环功能异常（肾血管疾病、充血性心力衰竭、体液不足、重大手术、败血症或重大出血事件等）、肝功能障碍、心功能异常、支气管哮喘、手术前患者、3岁以下婴幼儿、老年人、葡萄糖-6-磷酸脱氢酶(G6PD)缺乏症患者、瑞氏综合征、14岁以下水痘或流感感染患者、痛风患者，请咨询医生或药师。由于存在出血风险，若进行手术（包括牙科手术），请告知医生您正在服用本药。请注意不要损坏本药的肠溶包衣。
