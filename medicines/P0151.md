#EN
### 💊 베아제정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, upset stomach (indigestion), promoting digestion, and stomach bloating caused by indigestion.

### 🕒 베아제정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults, take 1 tablet once, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 베아제정 - Precautions
*   Do not administer this medication to children under 7 years of age.
*   Before taking this medication, patients with an allergic constitution, pregnant women, or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

*   7岁以下的儿童请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，过敏体质患者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性请咨询医生或药剂师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   如果服用约2周后症状仍无改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药剂师。
