#EN
### 💊 액티브원액(시트룰린말산염) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This drug is used for the adjunctive treatment of functional asthenia.

### 🕒 액티브원액(시트룰린말산염) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults: Take 1 sachet (1 g) diluted in water, 3 times daily. Children aged 6 years and older: Take 1 sachet (1 g) diluted in water, 2 times daily. Do not administer for more than 4 weeks.

### ⚠️ 액티브원액(시트룰린말산염) - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Infants under 6 years of age, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant, and breastfeeding mothers should not take this drug. When administering to children, administer under the guidance and supervision of a guardian. Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration. If symptoms do not improve after approximately one month of administration, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 액티브원액(시트룰린말산염) - 功效与作用
*   本药用于功能性虚弱的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 액티브원액(시트룰린말산염) - 用法与用量
*   成人：一次1袋（1克），每日3次，用水稀释后服用。6岁以上儿童：一次1袋（1克），每日2次，用水稀释后服用。请勿给药超过4周。

### ⚠️ 액티브원액(시트룰린말산염) - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   6岁以下婴幼儿、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女请勿服用本药。儿童服用时，请在监护人的指导和监督下服用。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。如果服用约1个月后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药剂师。

#JP

*   情報なし
