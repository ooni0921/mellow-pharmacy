#EN
### 💊 에프벡스한방카타플라스마 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the relief of pain and inflammation associated with sprains, contusions, muscle pain, joint pain, lumbago (lower back pain), stiff shoulders, and chilblains (frostbite).

### 🕒 에프벡스한방카타플라스마 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, apply to the affected area 1-2 times a day after removing the film from the plaster.

### ⚠️ 에프벡스한방카타플라스마 - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not use this medication on infants under 30 months of age, on areas with eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer (urushiol) or similar substances, or on wounded skin.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a patient with hypersensitivity to this medication, or have a history of allergic reactions to cosmetics or similar products (such as rash, redness (hyperemia and flushing), itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.), if you have an allergic constitution, if you have severe moist lesions (wetness) or severe erosion (soreness), or if you are a child.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

For external use only.

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 applications, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 에프벡스한방카타플라스마 - 功效与作用
* 本药用于缓解扭伤、挫伤、肌肉痛、关节痛、腰痛、肩膀僵硬、冻疮（冻伤）的疼痛和炎症。

### 🕒 에프벡스한방카타플라스마 - 用法与用量
* 成人：每日1～2次，撕下药片表面的薄膜后，贴于患处。

### ⚠️ 에프벡스한방카타플라스마 - 注意事项
* 无可用信息。
* 30个月以下的幼儿、湿疹、漆树等引起的皮炎、伤口部位请勿使用本药。

对本药过敏的患者，或有过敏症状（皮疹、发红（充血发红）、瘙痒、漆树等引起的皮炎等）经验者，过敏体质的患者，湿润（潮湿）或严重糜烂（溃烂）的患者，以及儿童，请咨询医生或药剂师。

请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

给儿童使用时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。

仅供外用。

若使用5-6次后症状仍未改善，请咨询医生或药剂师。
