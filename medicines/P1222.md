#EN
### 💊 Jincelmon SE Tab. - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used to supplement vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, C during physical fatigue, pregnancy/lactation, periods of physical weakness during or after illness, growth periods, and old age. It also helps alleviate dry eyes, night blindness, prevent poor bone and tooth development, rickets, and prevent and treat iron deficiency anemia, as well as provide zinc.

### 🕒 Jincelmon SE Tab. - Dosage and Administration
* For individuals aged 12 years and older, and adults, take 1 tablet once daily.

### ⚠️ Jincelmon SE Tab. - Precautions
* Taking more than 5,000 IU of Vitamin A per day may cause congenital malformations. Women who are within the first three months of pregnancy or who may be pregnant should not take this medicine. If children under 6 years old take an overdose, it can cause iron poisoning-related death, so keep it out of reach of children.
* Do not take this medicine if you have hypersensitivity to any component of this medicine, are an infant under 12 months of age, have hypercalcemia (a condition with excessive calcium in the blood), sarcoidosis (a systemic inflammatory disease of unknown cause), kidney disease, hyperkalemia, Wilson's disease, hemochromatosis (a disease involving iron deposition in the liver and pancreas due to iron metabolism abnormalities), hemosiderosis, non-iron deficiency anemia, kidney stones, or severe renal failure.
* Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are a child who gets sufficient sunlight and eats a normal diet, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a breastfeeding woman, a premature infant, an infant, have hyperoxaluria (a condition where excessive oxalates are excreted in the urine), gastrointestinal diseases such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis, heart or circulatory system dysfunction, kidney impairment, hypoproteinemia, gallbladder-related diseases, liver disease, acute or chronic bronchitis, pulmonary edema, alkalosis, peptic ulcer, gout, kidney stone patients or those with a history, patients with thrombotic predisposition, or patients with folic acid deficiency.
* Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
* This medicine may interfere with the detection of blood glucose in various urine tests.
* If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medicine, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
