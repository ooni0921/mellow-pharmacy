#EN
### 💊 수렌유환 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for excessive drinking, nausea (hangover, nausea due to bad odor), vomiting, loss of appetite, stomach discomfort, weak stomach, promotion of digestion, overeating, heartburn, abdominal bloating due to indigestion, and indigestion.

### 🕒 수렌유환 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults aged 15 years or older should take 1 sachet (10 pills) per dose, 3 times a day, before or between meals.

### ⚠️ 수렌유환 - Precautions
*   Patients with genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption should not take this medicine.
*   Pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after taking this medicine for about 2 weeks, immediately stop taking it and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 🕒 수렌유환 - 用法与用量

*   患有半乳糖不耐受、Lapp乳糖酶缺乏症或葡萄糖-半乳糖吸收不良等遗传性疾病的患者，请勿服用本药。

*   请严格遵守规定的用法用量。
*   服用约2周后症状仍无改善时，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
