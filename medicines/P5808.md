#EN
### 💊 현대아라비정 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the supplementation of Vitamins D, E, B1, B2, B6, C, and Zinc in cases of physical fatigue, during pregnancy and lactation, physical debility during or after illness, during growth periods, and in old age.

Through its contained vitamins, etc., this medication is used for the relief of symptoms such as neuralgia, myalgia, lumbago, stiff shoulders, and other joint pains, angular stomatitis, cheilitis, stomatitis, glossitis, eczema, and dermatitis. It is also used for poor development of bones and teeth, beriberi, and eye strain.

### 🕒 현대아라비정 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children 12 years of age and older, take 1 tablet once a day.

### ⚠️ 현대아라비정 - Precautions
* No information.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, a history of or hypersensitivity to soybean oil, hypercalcemia, sarcoidosis, kidney stones, kidney disease, severe renal insufficiency, or if you are an infant under 12 months of age.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have gastrointestinal disorders such as hyperoxaluria, peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, regional enteritis (Crohn's disease), cardiac and circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, gout, kidney stones (or a history thereof), if you are pregnant, possibly pregnant, or lactating, if you are a premature infant or infant, if you have lipid metabolism disorders such as hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, and pancreatitis, if you are a patient who should be administered lipid emulsions cautiously, or if you have conditions related to fat overload.

Patients with thrombotic predisposition such as vascular occlusion, or patients with folic acid deficiency, should immediately discontinue taking this medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 1 month of taking this medication, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Children should take this medication under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

This medication may interfere with the detection of glucose during various urine tests and may turn urine yellow, affecting clinical test results.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
