# EN
### 💊 노펜파워핫카타플라스마 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for pain relief and anti-inflammation in cases of bruises, sprains, back pain, stiff shoulders, muscle pain, and joint pain.

### 🕒 노펜파워핫카타플라스마 - Dosage and Administration
*   Remove the release liner from the patch surface and apply it to the affected area 1 to 3 times a day.

### ⚠️ 노펜파워핫카타플라스마 - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Do not use this medication in infants under 30 months, on eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer (urushiol) or similar substances, or on wounded areas.
*   Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have experienced allergic symptoms to medications or cosmetics (e.g., rash, redness, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.), have an allergic constitution, have severe dampness or maceration, are a child, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a breastfeeding woman.
*   Strictly adhere to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When using for children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Since symptoms of poisoning may appear if a large amount is absorbed, avoid long-term use over a wide area.
*   This medication is for external use only.
*   Do not warm the affected area with heating pads or electric blankets.
