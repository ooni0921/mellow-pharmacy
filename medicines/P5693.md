#EN
### 💊 알러진정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used for the temporary relief of symptoms due to hay fever or other upper respiratory allergies, such as runny nose, sneezing, itchy and watery eyes, and itching of the nose or throat.

### 🕒 알러진정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Dosage and Administration
* Adults and adolescents 12 years of age and older should take 1 tablet with water every 12 hours.

Do not exceed 2 tablets within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 알러진정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - Precautions
* No information available.
* Do not take this medication if you have experienced hypersensitivity to it.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are: a child under 12 years of age, an elderly person aged 65 or older, someone unsure if their symptoms are caused by allergic rhinitis or a cold, someone previously diagnosed with other allergic diseases, someone with severe nasal congestion, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, a nursing mother, someone who has experienced allergic symptoms due to drugs, a patient with renal impairment, or a patient with cardiovascular disease.

Avoid breastfeeding while taking this medication.

Do not exceed the recommended dose.

#CN

* 本药用于暂时缓解花粉症或其他上呼吸道过敏引起的流鼻涕、打喷嚏、眼睛发痒和流泪、鼻或喉咙发痒等症状。

### 🕒 알러진정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - 用法用量
* 成人及12岁以上青少年，每次1片，每隔12小时用水送服。

24小时内服用量请勿超过2片。

### ⚠️ 알러진정60밀리그램(펙소페나딘염산염) - 注意事项
* 无可用信息。
* 对本药有过敏史者请勿服用本药。

在服用本药前，如果您是：12岁以下儿童、65岁以上老年人、不确定症状是否由过敏性鼻炎或感冒等其他原因引起者、曾被诊断出患有其他过敏性疾病者、鼻塞症状严重者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性、哺乳期妇女、曾因药物等原因出现过敏症状者、肾功能障碍患者、心血管疾病患者，请咨询医生或药师。

服用本药期间请避免哺乳。

请勿超过推荐剂量。
