#EN

### 💊 Gaviscon Double Action Liquid - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the improvement of acid reflux, heartburn, and indigestion caused by the regurgitation of stomach contents (e.g., after meals, during pregnancy, or accompanied by reflux esophagitis).

### 🕒 Gaviscon Double Action Liquid - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 12 years and older should take 10-20 mL per dose, four times a day (after meals and at bedtime).

### ⚠️ Gaviscon Double Action Liquid - Precautions
*   No information.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medication should not take it.
*   Before taking this medication, patients with congestive heart failure, renal impairment, hypercalcemia, nephrocalcinosis, recurrent calcium-containing kidney stones, and children under 12 years of age should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately one week of administration, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

### 💊 Gaviscon Double Action Liquid - 功效与作用
*   本药用于改善胃酸反流、胃灼热以及胃内容物反流引起的消化不良（例如餐后、妊娠期间或伴有反流性食管炎的情况）。

### 🕒 Gaviscon Double Action Liquid - 用法用量
*   成人及12岁以上儿童，每次服用10~20毫升，每日4次（饭后及睡前）。

### ⚠️ Gaviscon Double Action Liquid - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药过敏的患者请勿服用本药。
*   服用本药前，患有充血性心力衰竭、肾功能不全、高钙血症、肾钙质沉着症、复发性含钙肾结石的患者以及12岁以下儿童请咨询医生或药师。
*   服用约一周后症状仍未改善，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
