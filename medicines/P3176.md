#EN
### 💊 리오칸크림(리도카인) - Efficacy and Effect
*   This medication is used to reduce the sensitivity of the male genital tactile sensation.

### 🕒 리오칸크림(리도카인) - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply 0.25~0.66 g (23~62 mg as lidocaine) of the cream 5~15 minutes before sexual intercourse.

Dosage and application time vary by individual; use the lowest effective dose and do not exceed a maximum of 2 g (184 mg as lidocaine) within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ 리오칸크림(리도카인) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug or other amide-type local anesthetics, if there is trauma or inflammation at the application site, or if your partner is pregnant.

Before using this medication, patients with liver or kidney disease or hypersensitivity to propylene glycol should consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not use this medication for more than 3 months unless directed by a doctor.

For external use only.

Do not use on eyes or nose.

It is advisable not to apply continuously to the same area.

#JP

*   本剤は、男性性器の触覚過敏症の軽減に用いられます。

### 🕒 리오칸크림(리도카인) - 用法・用量

本剤を使用する前に、肝・腎疾患のある方、プロピレングリコールに過敏症のある方は、医師または薬剤師にご相談ください。
