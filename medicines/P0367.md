### 💊 Taeguk Acyclovir Cream - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for herpes simplex virus infections (including initial and recurrent genital herpes and labial herpes).

### 🕒 Taeguk Acyclovir Cream - Dosage and Administration
* Apply to the affected area 5 times a day, at approximately 4-hour intervals. Treatment should be continued for 5 days, and if the condition does not improve after 5 days of use, it may be used for an additional 5 days.

### ⚠️ Taeguk Acyclovir Cream - Precautions
* No information
* Do not use this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine, are breastfeeding, or are a child. Before using this medicine, patients with immunodeficiency such as AIDS or bone marrow transplant recipients, pregnant women or women who may be pregnant should consult a doctor or pharmacist. Treatment should be started as early as possible, and in recurrent cases, it should be started at the first sign of prodromal symptoms or lesions. Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration. Do not use this medicine for the purpose of preventing infection to others or recurrent herpes simplex virus infections. Do not dilute this medicine or use it as a base for mixing with other medicines. This medicine is for external use only. Do not use for ophthalmic purposes on the cornea or mucous membranes. To prevent this medicine from spreading to other parts of the body or infecting others, apply it while wearing a thimble or rubber gloves.

* 本药品用于单纯疱疹病毒感染症（包括初期和复发性生殖器疱疹和唇疱疹）。

### 🕒 태극아시클로버크림 - 用法与用量

### ⚠️ 태극아시클로버크림 - 注意事项
* 无信息
