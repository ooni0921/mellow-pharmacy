# EN
### 💊 유에츄데이앤나이트연질캡슐 - Indications & Efficacy
* This medication is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 유에츄데이앤나이트연질캡슐 - Dosage & Administration
* Daytime and nighttime versions are available.

For the daytime version, individuals aged 15 years and older (including adults) should take 2 capsules per dose, twice daily, 30 minutes after meals.

For the nighttime version, individuals aged 15 years and older (including adults) should take 2 capsules per dose, once daily, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 유에츄데이앤나이트연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can rarely be fatal, have been reported. Discontinue use immediately if skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity appear after taking this medication.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose of acetaminophen (4,000 mg). This may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medication if you have a history of hypersensitivity or allergy to this medication or its components, have experienced asthma after taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months, have severe or uncontrolled hypertension (daytime version), or are a lactating woman (nighttime version).

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication if you are: a child under 2 years; an infant or child under 15 years with or suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; experiencing symptoms of pseudoephedrine-related ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding); have an allergic constitution; have a history of allergic symptoms to medication (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching); have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, hypertension, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty with urination; are elderly; are physically frail; have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have a history of ulcers or bleeding; are pregnant or possibly pregnant; are a lactating woman; smoke; have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, a cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, a cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medication; exercise caution when driving or operating machinery (nighttime version).

If you are breastfeeding, do not take this medication or discontinue breastfeeding (nighttime version).

If symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
