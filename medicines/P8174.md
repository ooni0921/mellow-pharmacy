### 💊 Pandital Tab. - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal bloating, indigestion (gastric stagnation), nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, sour belching, indigestion, loss of appetite (anorexia), overeating, promotion of digestion, and abdominal bloating due to indigestion.

### 🕒 Pandital Tab. - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults should take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, between meals.

### ⚠️ Pandital Tab. - Precautions
*   No information available.
*   Do not take this medicine if you are undergoing dialysis, have genetic problems such as galactose intolerance, Lapp lactase deficiency, or glucose-galactose malabsorption, are a child under 15 years of age, a pregnant woman or a woman who may be pregnant, or a lactating woman.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have or have experienced allergies (e.g., rash, redness, itching), kidney impairment, liver impairment, hypophosphatemia, high blood pressure, are elderly, have heart or kidney disorders, or have edema (swelling).

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Do not use continuously for a long period.
