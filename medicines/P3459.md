##EN

### 💊 제뉴파마한방만통고카타플라스마 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for pain relief and anti-inflammatory (antiphlogistic) purposes in cases of sprains, bruises, muscle pain, joint pain, back pain (lumbago), stiff shoulders, and chilblains (frostbite).

### 🕒 제뉴파마한방만통고카타플라스마 - Dosage and Administration
*   For adults, apply to the affected area (diseased part) 1 to 2 times a day after peeling off the film from the patch surface.

### ⚠️ 제뉴파마한방만통고카타플라스마 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not use this medicine in infants under 30 months of age, on skin affected by eczema, dermatitis caused by lacquer (urushiol) or similar allergens, or on injured areas.
*   Patients with hypersensitivity to this medicine, or those with allergic symptoms due to cosmetics (e.g., rash, redness/flushing, itching, dermatitis caused by lacquer, etc.), allergic constitution, moist (damp) or eroded (chafed) skin, or children should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 5 to 6 days of use, discontinue use immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

##CN

### 💊 제뉴파마한방만통고카타플라스마 - 功效及效果
*   本药物用于扭伤、挫伤、肌肉痛、关节痛、腰痛、肩颈僵硬、冻疮（冻伤）的止痛·消炎（抗炎）。

### 🕒 제뉴파마한방만통고카타플라스마 - 用法及用量
*   成人每日1~2次，撕掉药面薄膜后贴于患处（病患部位）。

### ⚠️ 제뉴파마한방만통고카타플라스마 - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   30个月以下的幼儿、湿疹、漆树等引起的皮炎、伤口部位请勿使用本药物。
*   对本药物过敏的患者，或因化妆品等引起过敏症状（皮疹、发红（充血发红）、瘙痒、漆树等引起的皮炎等）的患者，过敏体质者，湿润（潮湿）或糜烂（溃烂）患者，儿童，请咨询医生或药师。
*   请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。
*   给儿童用药时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。
*   如果使用约5~6天后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
