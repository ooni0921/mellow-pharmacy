#EN

### 💊 위앤정 - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for hyperacidity, heartburn, stomach discomfort, abdominal distension, indigestion, nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, and acid regurgitation.

### 🕒 위앤정 - Dosage and Administration
*   For individuals aged 15 years and older, and adults: take 2 tablets per dose, 3 times a day, after meals.

### ⚠️ 위앤정 - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not administer this medication to children under 7 years of age or patients undergoing dialysis therapy.
*   Patients with renal impairment should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.
*   Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.
*   If symptoms do not improve after approximately 2 weeks of administration, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
