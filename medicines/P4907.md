#EN
### 💊 치오스캡슐(디오스민) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for the improvement of symptoms related to venous insufficiency (such as leg heaviness, pain), adjunctive treatment for disorders caused by capillary fragility, and the treatment of signs related to hemorrhoids.

### 🕒 치오스캡슐(디오스민) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 capsule (300 mg) per dose, twice daily (at lunch and dinner).

In case of recurrence or worsening of hemorrhoids, up to 4 to 6 capsules (1,200 to 1,800 mg) can be taken daily.

Dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age and symptoms.

### ⚠️ 치오스캡슐(디오스민) - Precautions
*   No specific information provided.
*   Patients hypersensitive to this medication and nursing mothers should not take this medication.

Pregnant women should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medication.

Do not administer for a long period.

If symptoms do not improve quickly, a proctological examination and re-evaluation of treatment are necessary.
