#EN
### 💊 펜싹콜연질캡슐 - Indications and Efficacy
*   This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills (feeling cold and shivering), fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 펜싹콜연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults and children aged 15 years and older: Take 2 capsules per dose, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 펜싹콜연질캡슐 - Precautions
*   Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics, as it may cause liver damage.

Very rarely, severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome (SJS), and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal, have been reported. Discontinue use immediately if skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reactions appear after taking this medicine.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen, as it may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

Discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist if severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis occur.
*   Do not take this medicine if you have a history of hypersensitivity to this medicine, a history of asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months of age, or if you are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, antiparkinsonian drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within 2 weeks.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine if you are: an infant or child under 15 years of age who has or is suspected of having chickenpox or influenza; an infant aged 3 months or older and under 2 years, or a toddler; have an allergic constitution, or a history of allergic symptoms (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching, etc.); have liver, kidney, heart, or thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), or dysuria (difficulty urinating); are elderly, frail, or have a high fever; have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, or stomach pain, or have ulcer or bleeding problems; are pregnant or may be pregnant, or are breastfeeding; have a cough accompanied by smoking, asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, or excessive phlegm; have a cough lasting or recurring for more than 1 week, a chronic cough, or a cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine.

Do not take continuously for a long period.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be cautious when driving or operating machinery.

Consult a doctor or pharmacist if symptoms do not improve after 5-6 doses.
