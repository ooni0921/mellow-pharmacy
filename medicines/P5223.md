#EN

### 💊 토탈플루연질캡슐 - Efficacy and Effects
* This medicine is used for the relief of various cold symptoms (runny nose, nasal congestion, sneezing, sore throat, cough, phlegm, chills, fever, headache, joint pain, muscle pain).

### 🕒 토탈플루연질캡슐 - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 15 years and older, take 2 capsules once, 3 times a day, 30 minutes after meals.

### ⚠️ 토탈플루연질캡슐 - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medicine or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause liver damage.

Severe skin reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP), Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN), which can be fatal in very rare cases, have been reported. If skin rash or other signs of hypersensitivity reaction appear after taking this medicine, discontinue use immediately.

Do not exceed the maximum daily dose (4,000 mg) of acetaminophen. It may cause liver damage. Do not take with other products containing acetaminophen.

If severe skin adverse reactions such as acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis appear, discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.
* Do not take this medicine if you are hypersensitive to this medicine or its components, have experienced asthma when taking other antipyretic analgesics or cold medicines, are an infant under 3 months or a child under 2 years of age, are currently taking MAO inhibitors (antidepressants, antipsychotics, mood stabilizers, anti-Parkinson's drugs, etc.) or have stopped taking them within the last 2 weeks, or have severe or uncontrolled hypertension.

Before taking this medicine, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are an infant (newborn) or child under 15 years of age suspected or confirmed to have chickenpox or influenza, if symptoms of ischemic colitis (e.g., sudden abdominal pain, rectal bleeding) occur in relation to pseudoephedrine, if you have an allergic constitution, have experienced allergic symptoms due to medicine (e.g., fever, rash, joint pain, asthma, itching), have liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, thyroid disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, gastroduodenal ulcer, glaucoma (e.g., eye pain, blurred vision), difficulty urinating, are elderly, are frail or have a high fever, have persistent or recurrent gastrointestinal problems such as heartburn, stomach discomfort, stomach pain, or have a history of ulcers or bleeding problems, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, smoke, have asthma, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, cough accompanied by excessive phlegm, cough lasting or recurring for more than a week, chronic cough, or cough accompanied by fever, rash, or persistent headache.

If symptoms do not improve after 5 to 6 doses, immediately stop taking the medicine and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Follow the prescribed dosage and administration strictly.

When administering to children, do so under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

Drowsiness may occur after taking this medicine, so be careful when driving or operating machinery.
