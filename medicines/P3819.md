#EN
### 💊 Magne-B Tablet - Efficacy and Effects
* This drug is used for muscle cramps and physical fatigue due to magnesium deficiency; decreased physical strength during pregnancy, lactation, illness, or convalescence; supplementation of vitamins E and B1 in old age; relief of symptoms such as shoulder/neck stiffness, numbness/coldness in hands and feet due to peripheral circulatory disorders and menopause; relief of neuralgia, myalgia, and arthralgia (e.g., back pain, shoulder stiffness); beriberi; and eye fatigue.

### 🕒 Magne-B Tablet - Dosage and Administration
* For adults and children aged 12 years and older: Take 2 tablets once daily, OR 1 tablet twice daily.

### ⚠️ Magne-B Tablet - Precautions
* Do not take this drug if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, hypersensitivity to soybean oil or a history thereof, hypersensitivity to soy or peanuts, are an infant under 12 months of age, or have severe renal failure.

* Before taking this drug, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you are pregnant or may be pregnant, breastfeeding, a premature infant, an infant/young child, a patient with gastrointestinal disorders such as peptic ulcer, chronic ulcerative colitis, or regional enteritis, a patient with cardiac/circulatory system dysfunction, renal impairment, hypoproteinemia, diabetes mellitus, a thrombotic predisposition, lipid metabolism disorders (e.g., hyperlipoproteinemia, diabetic hyperlipidemia, pancreatitis), or if you need cautious administration of lipid emulsions, or if special risks due to fat overload are anticipated.

* Follow the prescribed dosage and administration carefully.
