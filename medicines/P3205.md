#EN
### 💊 Metagen Cream - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used for secondary infected allergic or inflammatory skin conditions (eczema, contact dermatitis, seborrheic dermatitis, atopic dermatitis, photodermatitis, lichen simplex chronicus, intertrigo, exfoliative dermatitis, pruritus, psoriasis) and first-degree burns.

### 🕒 Metagen Cream - Dosage and Administration
*   Apply an appropriate amount to the affected area 1 to 3 times daily.

### ⚠️ Metagen Cream - Precautions
*   Do not use this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, bacterial skin infections (such as tuberculosis, syphilis), fungal skin infections (such as candidiasis, tinea), spirochetal, yeast, viral skin infections (such as herpes zoster, herpes simplex, chickenpox, vaccinia), parasitic skin infections (such as scabies, pubic lice), eczematous external otitis with tympanic membrane perforation, ulcers (except Behcet's disease), second-degree deep burns or worse, frostbite, perioral dermatitis, acne vulgaris, rosacea, or hypersensitivity to aminoglycoside antibiotics such as streptomycin, kanamycin, gentamicin, neomycin, or to bacitracin.

    Before using this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have a history of propylene glycol hypersensitivity or allergic reactions, are pregnant or potentially pregnant, are breastfeeding, or are an infant or child.

    Adhere strictly to the prescribed dosage and administration.

    When using for children, use under the guidance and supervision of a guardian.

    Avoid long-term and large-quantity use, and occlusive dressing therapy.

    Do not use for ophthalmic purposes.

    Do not use for purposes other than treatment, such as after makeup or shaving.

    If symptoms do not improve after 3 to 4 weeks of treatment, immediately discontinue use and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN
### 💊 Metagen Cream - 功效与作用
*   本药用于继发感染的过敏性或炎症性皮肤病（湿疹、接触性皮炎、脂溢性皮炎、特应性皮炎、光皮炎、慢性单纯性苔藓、擦烂、剥脱性皮炎、瘙痒、银屑病）和一度烧伤。

### 🕒 Metagen Cream - 用法与用量
*   每日1~3次，取适量涂抹于患处。

### ⚠️ Metagen Cream - 注意事项
*   对本药过敏者，或患有细菌（如结核、梅毒）、真菌（如念珠菌病、癣）、螺旋体、酵母菌、病毒（如带状疱疹、单纯疱疹、水痘、牛痘）或动物（如疥疮、阴虱）性皮肤感染、伴有鼓膜穿孔的湿疹性外耳炎、溃疡（白塞病除外）、二度深层以上烧伤或冻伤、口周皮炎、寻常痤疮、酒渣鼻，以及对链霉素、卡那霉素、庆大霉素、新霉素等氨基糖苷类抗生素或杆菌肽过敏的患者，请勿使用本药。

    使用本药前，对丙二醇过敏或有其他过敏反应史者、孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女、婴幼儿应咨询医生或药师。

    请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。

    用于儿童时，请在监护人的指导监督下使用。

    避免长期大量使用及密封包扎法。

    请勿用于眼科。

    请勿用于治疗以外的目的，如化妆后或剃须后。

    如果治疗3~4周后症状仍未改善，请立即停止使用并咨询医生或药师。
