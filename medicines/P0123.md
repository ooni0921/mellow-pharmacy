# EN
### 💊 한미유리아크림200밀리그램(우레아) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This product is used for progressive palmar and plantar hyperkeratosis (dry type of housewives' eczema), hyperkeratosis of hands and feet, ichthyosis, senile xerosis, lichen pilaris (keratosis pilaris), and atopic skin.

### 🕒 한미유리아크림200밀리그램(우레아) - Dosage and Administration
*   After washing the affected area, apply this product 1 to several times a day and rub it in thoroughly.

### ⚠️ 한미유리아크림200밀리그램(우레아) - Precautions
*   Do not use this product in patients with hypersensitivity to this product, acute eczema or inflammatory skin diseases, renal impairment, or on mucous membranes such as the ocular conjunctiva.
*   Before using this product, patients with inflammation, fissures (cracked skin), or increased sensitivity to skin irritation should consult a doctor or pharmacist.
*   For external use only.
*   Do not apply directly to ulcers, erosions (raw areas), or wound sites.
*   Use with caution to avoid contact with eyes, nose, mouth, and other mucous membranes.
*   If it accidentally gets into the eyes, wash thoroughly with a sufficient amount of water.
