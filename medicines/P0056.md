# EN
### 💊 일양노이시린에이정(규산알루민산마그네슘) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medicine is used for the antacid action and improvement of symptoms in hyperacidity, gastritis, and duodenitis.

### 🕒 일양노이시린에이정(규산알루민산마그네슘) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adults take 1 to 2 tablets (0.5 to 1 g) once, 3 to 4 times a day, on an empty stomach. The dosage may be appropriately increased or decreased depending on age or symptoms.

### ⚠️ 일양노이시린에이정(규산알루민산마그네슘) - Precautions
*   No information
*   Patients with hepatic impairment, cardiac dysfunction, or hypermagnesemia should consult a doctor or pharmacist before taking this medicine.
