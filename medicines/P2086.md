#EN

### 💊 덱시원연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Efficacy and Effects
* This medication is used as an adjunctive treatment for chronic polyarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, painful swelling or inflammation after trauma and surgery, and infections accompanied by inflammation, pain, and fever.

### 🕒 덱시원연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Dosage and Administration
* For adults, take 1 capsule (300 mg) at a time, 2 to 4 times daily. Do not exceed 4 capsules (1,200 mg) per day.

### ⚠️ 덱시원연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - Precautions
* Consult a doctor or pharmacist if you regularly consume three or more alcoholic drinks daily and are taking this medication or other antipyretic analgesics. It may cause gastrointestinal bleeding.

Taking this medication increases the risk of serious, potentially fatal cardiovascular thrombotic reactions, myocardial infarction, and stroke. Serious gastrointestinal adverse reactions, including gastrointestinal bleeding, ulceration, and perforation, may also occur.
* Do not take this medication if you have hypersensitivity to this drug, gastrointestinal ulcers or a history thereof, bleeding from the gastrointestinal tract, cerebrovascular, or other sites, severe blood disorders, severe hepatic impairment, severe renal impairment, severe cardiac dysfunction, severe hypertension, bronchial asthma or a history thereof, a history of asthma, urticaria, or allergic reactions to aspirin or other nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), patients undergoing pain treatment before or after coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery, pregnant women in the third trimester (6 months or more), lactating women, patients with inflammatory bowel disease such as Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, or patients with a history of gastrointestinal bleeding or perforation due to previous NSAID treatment.

Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have blood disorders or a history thereof, a bleeding tendency, hepatic impairment or a history thereof, renal impairment or a history thereof, fluid retention or heart failure, hypertension, a history of hypersensitivity, systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD), are elderly, a child over 6 years of age, a patient with gastric cancer, an alcoholic, a pregnant woman in the first or second trimester or a woman who may be pregnant, a patient with ischemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease, cerebrovascular disease, or risk factors for cardiovascular disease (e.g., hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking), hepatic porphyria, have undergone major surgical procedures resulting in hypovolemia, or have blood coagulation disorders or are receiving anticoagulants.

Considering the importance of drug administration, either discontinue breastfeeding or discontinue the medication.

Dizziness or fatigue may occur after taking this medication, so exercise caution when driving or operating machinery.

For acute conditions, long-term administration should generally not exceed 5 days.

### 🕒 덱시원연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 用法与用量

### ⚠️ 덱시원연질캡슐(덱시부프로펜) - 注意事项

服用本药前，有血液异常或病史者，有出血倾向的患者，有肝功能障碍或病史者，有肾功能障碍或病史者，有体液潴留或心力衰竭的患者，高血压患者，有过敏史者，系统性红斑狼疮（SLE）和混合结缔组织病（MCTD）患者，老年人，6岁以上儿童，胃癌患者，酒精中毒患者，妊娠初期、中期的孕妇及可能怀孕的女性，缺血性心脏病、周围动脉疾病、脑血管疾病、有心血管疾病风险因素的患者（例如，高血压、高脂血症、糖尿病、吸烟），肝性卟啉病患者，因失血导致重大外科手术的患者，有凝血障碍或正在接受抗凝剂治疗的患者，请咨询医生或药剂师。

考虑到药物治疗的重要性，请停止哺乳或停止用药。
