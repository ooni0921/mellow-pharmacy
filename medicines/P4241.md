#EN
### 💊 히미팔팔액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - Efficacy and Effects
*   This medication is used as an adjuvant therapy for symptoms of mental and physical asthenia, and as an adjuvant therapy during the recovery period (amino acid deficiency).

### 🕒 히미팔팔액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - Dosage and Administration
*   Adolescents aged 12 years and older and adults should take 5 g once daily. It is best to take it in the morning, but it can also be taken before bedtime. It can be taken with a small amount of water.

### ⚠️ 히미팔팔액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - Precautions
*   No information provided.
*   Do not take this medication if you are hypersensitive to this drug, have severe renal or hepatic impairment, or have experienced myocardial infarction. Before taking this medication, consult a doctor or pharmacist if you have urinary retention issues, are pregnant or possibly pregnant, are breastfeeding, plan to take it continuously for more than 15 days, or have a history of hypersensitivity to Sunset Yellow FCF (Yellow No. 5). Strictly follow the prescribed dosage and administration. If any unusual symptoms occur while taking this medication, stop taking it immediately and consult a doctor or pharmacist.

#CN

*   本药用于精神和身体虚弱症状的辅助治疗，以及恢复期（氨基酸缺乏状态）的辅助治疗。

### 🕒 히미팔팔액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - 用法与用量

### ⚠️ 히미팔팔액(L-아스파르트산-L-아르기닌수화물) - 注意事项
*   无信息。
*   对本药过敏者，患有严重肾功能障碍或肝功能障碍者，或有心肌梗死史者，请勿服用本药。服用本药前，如果您有尿潴留问题，是孕妇或可能怀孕的女性以及哺乳期妇女，计划连续服用15天以上，或对日落黄（黄色5号）过敏者，请咨询医生或药师。请严格遵守规定的用法和用量。服用本药后若出现异常症状，请立即停止服用并咨询医生或药师。
